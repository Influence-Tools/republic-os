# 27 U.S.C. § 202c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act June 26, 1936, ch. 830, title V, § 503, 49 Stat. 1965, which provided for repeal of former laws, was to have taken effect when a majority of the members authorized to be appointed took office under section 202b of this title. The members, however, were never appointed.
