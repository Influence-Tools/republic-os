# 54 U.S.C. § 303102 - Funding

## Text

For each of fiscal years 2012 to 2023,11 See Application of Section note below. $150,000,000 shall be deposited in the Historic Preservation Fund from revenues due and payable to the United States under section 9 of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act (43 U.S.C. 1338), section 8733(b) of title 10, or both, notwithstanding any provision of law that those proceeds shall be credited to miscellaneous receipts of the Treasury.

(Pub. L. 113–287, § 3, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3206; Pub. L. 114–289, title VIII, § 802(a), (c)(1), Dec. 16, 2016, 130 Stat. 1494, 1495; Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 809(s), Aug. 13, 2018, 132 Stat. 1844.)
