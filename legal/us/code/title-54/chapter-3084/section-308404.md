# 54 U.S.C. § 308404 - Sunset

## Text

This program shall expire on the date that is 7 years after the date of enactment of this chapter.

(Added Pub. L. 115–104, § 3(a), Jan. 8, 2018, 131 Stat. 2261.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextThe date of enactment of this chapter, referred to in text, is the date of enactment of Pub. L. 115–104, which was approved Jan. 8, 2018.
