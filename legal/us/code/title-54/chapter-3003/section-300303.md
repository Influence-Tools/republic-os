# 54 U.S.C. § 300303 - Council

## Text

In this division, the term “Council” means the Advisory Council on Historic Preservation established by section 304101 of this title.

(Pub. L. 113–287, § 3, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3188.)
