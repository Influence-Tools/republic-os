# 54 U.S.C. § 101119 - Liability of United States

## Text

The United States shall not be liable for any debts, defaults, acts, or omissions of the National Park Foundation.

(Pub. L. 113–287, § 3, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3122.)
