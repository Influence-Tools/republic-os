# 19 U.S.C. § 1525 - Repealed. Pub. L. 89–762, § 2, Nov. 5, 1966, 80 Stat. 1312

## Notes

Section, act June 17, 1930, ch. 497, title IV, § 525, 46 Stat. 741, authorized the Secretary of the Treasury to employ not more than ten persons in the District of Columbia who have been detailed from the field force of the Customs Service.
