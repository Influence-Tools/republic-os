# 19 U.S.C. § 2494 - Progress reports

## Text

The President shall include as a part of the annual report required under section 2291h of title 22 an evaluation of progress that each major drug producing country and each major drug-transit country has made during the reporting period in achieving the objectives set forth in section 2492(b) of this title.

(Pub. L. 93–618, title VIII, § 804, as added Pub. L. 99–570, title IX, § 9001, Oct. 27, 1986, 100 Stat. 3207–166; amended Pub. L. 106–36, title I, § 1001(a)(9), June 25, 1999, 113 Stat. 131.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Amendments1999—Pub. L. 106–36 substituted “section 2291h of title 22” for “section 2291(e)(1) of title 22”.
