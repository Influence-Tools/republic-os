# 19 U.S.C. § 96 to 98 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 96, act Sept. 8, 1916, ch. 463, § 702, 39 Stat. 796, related to investigation of administration and fiscal and industrial effects of the customs laws. See section 1332(a) of this title. Section 97, act Sept. 8, 1916, ch. 463, § 703, 39 Stat. 796, related to conveyance of information to committees of Congress. See section 1332(g) of this title. Section 98, act Sept. 8, 1916, ch. 463, § 704, 39 Stat. 796, related to investigative powers of the Commission over commercial transactions and relations with foreign countries. See section 1332(b) of this title.
