# 41 U.S.C. § 1301 - Definition

## Text

In this subchapter, the term “Council” means the Federal Acquisition Regulatory Council established under section 1302(a) of this title.

(Pub. L. 111–350, § 3, Jan. 4, 2011, 124 Stat. 3691.)
