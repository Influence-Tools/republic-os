# 41 U.S.C. § 115 - Supplies

## Text

In this subtitle, the term “supplies” has the same meaning as the terms “item” and “item of supply”.

(Pub. L. 111–350, § 3, Jan. 4, 2011, 124 Stat. 3681.)
