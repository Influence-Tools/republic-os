# 33 U.S.C. § 24 - Big Tarkio River, Missouri

## Text

The Big Tarkio River, in the counties of Holt and Atchison, in the State of Missouri, is declared to be not a navigable water of the United States within the meaning of the laws enacted by Congress for the preservation and protection of such waters. The right to alter, amend, or repeal this section is expressly reserved.

(Feb. 15, 1910, ch. 33, §§ 1, 2, 36 Stat. 194.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification The first sentence hereof is section 1 and the last sentence section 2 of act Feb. 15, 1910, entitled “An Act to declare Big Tarkio River, in Holt and Atchison counties, Missouri, nonnavigable”.
