# 33 U.S.C. § 4211 to 4219 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections 111 to 119 of Pub. L. 116–224, formerly classified to sections 4211 to 4219, respectively, of this title, were renumbered sections 111 to 119 of Pub. L. 109–449 by Pub. L. 119–65, § 3(a), Dec. 26, 2025, 139 Stat. 1987, and transferred to sections 1961 to 1969, respectively, of this title.
