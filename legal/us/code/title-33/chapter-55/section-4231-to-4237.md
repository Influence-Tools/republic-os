# 33 U.S.C. § 4231 to 4237 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections 121 to 127 of Pub. L. 116–224, formerly classified to sections 4231 to 4237, respectively, of this title, were renumbered sections 121 to 127 of Pub. L. 109–449 by Pub. L. 119–65, § 4(a), Dec. 26, 2025, 139 Stat. 1989, and transferred to sections 1971 to 1977, respectively, of this title.
