# 33 U.S.C. § 1912 - International law

## Text

Any action taken under this chapter shall be taken in accordance with international law.

(Pub. L. 96–478, § 17, as added Pub. L. 100–220, title II, § 2108, Dec. 29, 1987, 101 Stat. 1464.)
