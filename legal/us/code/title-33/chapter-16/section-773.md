# 33 U.S.C. § 773 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 19, 1950, ch. 761, § 3, 64 Stat. 466, which related to application for benefits for surviving spouses of Lighthouse Service employees and employee death benefits, was transferred to former section 2534(a) of Title 14, Coast Guard. Pub. L. 117–263, div. K, title CXVIII, § 11808(a)(18), Dec. 23, 2022, 136 Stat. 4166, repealed section 2534 of Title 14.
