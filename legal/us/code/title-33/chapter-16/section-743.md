# 33 U.S.C. § 743 - Repealed. Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV [LXXXV], § 8512(a)(5), Jan. 1, 2021, 134 Stat. 4759

## Notes

Section, acts June 17, 1910, ch. 301, § 11, 36 Stat. 538; May 17, 1932, ch. 190, 47 Stat. 158, related to lighthouse districts. Section 11 of act June 17, 1910, also enacted former section 717 of this title.

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsPrior provisions for the arrangement of the various lighthouse districts were made by R.S. § 4670; act July 26, 1886, ch. 779, 24 Stat. 148, which were repealed by section 13 of act June 17, 1910.
