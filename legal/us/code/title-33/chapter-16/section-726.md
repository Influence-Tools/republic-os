# 33 U.S.C. § 726 - Repealed. Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20, 63 Stat. 561

## Notes

Section, act Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 168, 37 Stat. 1018, related to procurement of supplies and equipment for special works of Lighthouse Service. See section 940 of Title 14, Coast Guard.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective first day of third month after approval by the President [Aug. 4, 1949], see section 19 of act Aug. 19, 1949, set out as an Effective Date note preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
