# 33 U.S.C. § 3903 - Applications

## Text

(a) In general To receive assistance under this chapter, an eligible entity shall submit to the Secretary or the Administrator, as applicable, an application at such time, in such manner, and containing such information as the Secretary or the Administrator may require.

(b) Combined projects In the case of an eligible project described in paragraph (9) or (10) of section 3905 of this title, the Secretary or the Administrator, as applicable, shall require the eligible entity to submit a single application for the combined group of projects.

(Pub. L. 113–121, title V, § 5024, June 10, 2014, 128 Stat. 1333; Pub. L. 114–322, title IV, § 5008(b)(2)(B), Dec. 16, 2016, 130 Stat. 1897.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries
