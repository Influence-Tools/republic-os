# 10 U.S.C. § 6151 - Authority to conduct program relating to fissile materials

## Text

The Secretary of Energy may conduct programs designed to improve the protection, control, and accountability of fissile materials in Russia.

(Added Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(a), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1372.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 6151 was renumbered section 8262 of this title. Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in section 2565 of Title 50, War and National Defense, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 119–60, § 3111(b)(1).
