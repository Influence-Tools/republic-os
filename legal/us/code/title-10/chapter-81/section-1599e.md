# 10 U.S.C. § 1599e - Payment of retention bonuses to DOD civilian employees in Guam.

## Text

Notwithstanding subsection (f) of section 5754 of title 5, the Secretary of Defense or Secretary of a military department may waive the limit established under subsection (e)(1) of such section and pay an otherwise eligible employee or category of employees in the territory of Guam retention bonuses of up to 50 percent of basic pay, based on a critical agency need.

(Added Pub. L. 119–60, div. A, title XI, § 1109, Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1075.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 1599e, Pub. L. 114–92, div. A, title XI, § 1105(a)(1), Nov. 25, 2015, 129 Stat. 1023, related to probationary period for employees, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title XI, § 1106(a)(1), Dec. 27, 2021, 135 Stat. 1950, effective Dec. 31, 2022.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Conforming amendments by section 1106 of Pub. L. 117–81 (amending sections 3321, 3393, 7501, 7511, and 7541 of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees) were executed as if effective at the same time as the repeal, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.

ApplicationPub. L. 117–81, div.
