# 10 U.S.C. § 672 - Reference to chapter 1209

## Text

Provisions of law relating to service of members of reserve components on active duty are set forth in chapter 1209 of this title (beginning with section 12301).

(Added Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1662(e)(4), Oct. 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 2992.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 672 was renumbered section 12301 of this title.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective DateSection effective Dec. 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see section 1691 of Pub. L. 103–337, set out as a note under section 10001 of this title.
