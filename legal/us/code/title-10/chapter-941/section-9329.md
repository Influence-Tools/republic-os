# 10 U.S.C. § 9329 - Computation of retired pay: law applicable

## Text

A member of the Air Force or the Space Force retired under this chapter is entitled to retired pay computed under chapter 945 of this title.

(Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, 70A Stat. 554, § 8929; renumbered § 9329 and amended Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, §§ 806(b)(13), 809(a), Aug. 13, 2018, 132 Stat. 1833, 1840; Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title IX, § 923(c)(20), Jan. 1, 2021, 134 Stat. 3812.)
