# 10 U.S.C. § 2200d - Regulations

## Text

The Secretary of Defense shall prescribe regulations for the administration of this chapter.

(Added Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [[div. A], title IX, § 922(a)(1)], Oct. 30, 2000, 114 Stat. 1654, 1654A–236.)
