# 10 U.S.C. § 12101 - Definition

## Text

In this chapter, the term “enlistment” means original enlistment or reenlistment.

(Added Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1662(b)(1), Oct. 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 2988.)
