# 10 U.S.C. § 195 - Defense Automated Printing Service: applicability of Federal printing requirements

## Text

The Defense Automated Printing Service shall comply fully with the requirements of section 501 of title 44 relating to the production and procurement of printing, binding, and blank-book work.

(Added Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title III, § 383(a), Nov. 18, 1997, 111 Stat. 1711.)

## Notes

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Authority To Procure Services From Government Publishing OfficePub. L. 105–85, div. A, title III, § 387(c), Nov. 18, 1997, 111 Stat. 1713, as amended by Pub. L. 113–235, div. H, title I, § 1301(b), Dec. 16, 2014, 128 Stat. 2537, provided that: “Consistent with section 501 of title 44, United States Code, the Secretary of a military department or head of a Defense Agency may contract directly with the Government Publishing Office for printing and duplication services otherwise available through the Defense Automated Printing Service.”
