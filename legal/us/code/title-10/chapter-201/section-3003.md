## Text

(Added Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1806(a)(1), Jan. 1, 2021, 134 Stat. 4152; amended Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title XVII, § 1701(i)(1)(B), Dec. 27, 2021, 135 Stat. 2140.)
