# 10 U.S.C. § 3678 - Repealed. Pub. L. 119–60, div. A, title VIII, § 811(a)(6), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 948]

## Notes

Section, added and amended Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1825(a), (j), Jan. 1, 2021, 134 Stat. 4206, 4208, related to contracts with terms of 18 months or more: limitation.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Leases for Tanker Aircraft Under Multiyear Aircraft-Lease Pilot ProgramPub. L. 107–314, div. A, title I, § 133, Dec. 2, 2002, 116 Stat. 2477, which provided that the Secretary of the Air Force could not enter into a lease for the acquisition of tanker aircraft for the Air Force under section 8159 of div. A of Pub. L. 107–117 (10 U.S.C. 3671 note) until certain requirements were met, was repealed by Pub. L. 119–60, div. A, title VIII, § 811(b)(19)(A), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 949.
