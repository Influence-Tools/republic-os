# 28 U.S.C. § 1393 - Repealed. Pub. L. 100–702, title X, § 1001(a), Nov. 19, 1988, 102 Stat. 4664]

## Notes

Section, act June 25, 1948, ch. 646, 62 Stat. 935, related to divisional venue in civil cases of a single defendant or defendants in different divisions.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealPub. L. 100–702, title X, § 1001(b), Nov. 19, 1988, 102 Stat. 4664, provided that: “The amendments made by this section [repealing this section] take effect 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act [Nov. 19, 1988].”
