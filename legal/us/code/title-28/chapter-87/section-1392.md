# 28 U.S.C. § 1392 - Repealed. Pub. L. 112–63, § 203, Dec. 7, 2011, 125 Stat. 764]

## Notes

Section, act June 25, 1948, ch. 646, 62 Stat. 935; Pub. L. 104–220, § 1, Oct. 1, 1996, 110 Stat. 3023, related to defendants or property in different districts in the same State.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal by Pub. L. 112–63 effective upon the expiration of the 30-day period beginning on Dec. 7, 2011, and applicable to any action commenced in a United States district court on or after such effective date, and to any action removed from a State court to a United States district court that had been commenced, within the meaning of State law, on or after such effective date, see section 205 of Pub. L. 112–63, set out as an Effective Date note under section 1390 of this title.
