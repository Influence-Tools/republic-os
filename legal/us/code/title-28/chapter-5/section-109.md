# 28 U.S.C. § 109 - New Hampshire

## Text

New Hampshire constitutes one judicial district. Court shall be held at Concord and Littleton.

(June 25, 1948, ch. 646, 62 Stat. 885.)

## Notes

Historical and Revision NotesBased on title 28, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 175 (Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231, § 95, 36 Stat. 1119; Aug. 23, 1912, ch. 344, 37 Stat. 357; Feb. 20, 1926, ch. 23, 44 Stat. 8). Changes in arrangement and phraseology were made.
