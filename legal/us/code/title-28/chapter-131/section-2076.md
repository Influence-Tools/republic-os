# 28 U.S.C. § 2076 - Repealed. Pub. L. 100–702, title IV, § 401(c), Nov. 19, 1988, 102 Stat. 4650]

## Notes

Section, added Pub. L. 93–595, § 2(a)(1), Jan. 2, 1975, 88 Stat. 1948; amended Pub. L. 94–149, § 2, Dec. 12, 1975, 89 Stat. 806, authorized the Supreme Court to prescribe amendments to Federal Rules of Evidence. See sections 2072 to 2074 of this title.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective Dec. 1, 1988, see section 407 of Pub. L. 100–702, set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under section 2071 of this title.
