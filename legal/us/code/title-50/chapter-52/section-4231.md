# 50 U.S.C. § 4231 - Short title

## Text

This subchapter may be cited as the “Aleutian and Pribilof Islands Restitution Act”.

(Pub. L. 100–383, title II, § 201, Aug. 10, 1988, 102 Stat. 911.)
