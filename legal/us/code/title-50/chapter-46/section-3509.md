# 50 U.S.C. § 3509 - Repealed. Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1208, title VI, § 601(b), 68 Stat. 1115

## Notes

Section, acts June 20, 1949, ch. 227, § 9, 63 Stat. 212; Aug. 16, 1950, ch. 719, 64 Stat. 450, related to establishment of positions in the professional and scientific field.

Section was formerly classified to section 403i of this title and repealed prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
