# 50 U.S.C. § 4301 - Designation of chapter

## Text

This chapter shall be known as the “Trading with the enemy 11 So in original. Act.”

(Oct. 6, 1917, ch. 106, § 1, 40 Stat. 411.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextThis chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning act Oct. 6, 1917, ch. 106, 40 Stat. 411, known as the Trading with the enemy Act, also known as the Trading with the Enemy Act, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 1 of the former Appendix to this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
