# 50 U.S.C. § 2575 to 2577 - Repealed. Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(b)(1), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1458

## Notes

Section 2575, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLIII, § 4309, as added Pub. L. 114–92, div. C, title XXXI, § 3132(a)(1), Nov. 25, 2015, 129 Stat. 1202; amended Pub. L. 115–91, div. C, title XXXI, §§ 3114(b), 3133(f), Dec. 12, 2017, 131 Stat. 1885, 1897; Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title X, § 1081(e)(3), Aug. 13, 2018, 132 Stat. 1986; Pub. L. 118–31, div. C, title XXXI, § 3118(c), Dec. 22, 2023, 137 Stat. 791, required development and annual updating of a five-year defense nuclear nonproliferation management plan. See section 6159 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2576, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLIII, § 4310, as added Pub. L. 115–91, div. C, title XXXI, § 3114(a), Dec. 12, 2017, 131 Stat. 1885, related to documentation and assessment of certain defense nuclear nonproliferation programs. See section 6160 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2577, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLIII, § 4311, as added Pub. L. 115–91, div. C, title XXXI, § 3131(a), Dec. 12, 2017, 131 Stat. 1894; amended Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title XVII, § 1702(k)(3)(A), Dec. 27, 2021, 135 Stat. 2160; Pub. L. 117–263, div. A, title VIII, § 809(c)(2), Dec. 23, 2022, 136 Stat. 2706, required submission of annual Selected Acquisition Reports on certain hardware relating to defense nuclear nonproliferation. See section 6161 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
