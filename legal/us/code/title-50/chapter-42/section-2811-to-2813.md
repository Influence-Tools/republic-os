# 50 U.S.C. § 2811 to 2813 - Repealed. Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(b)(1), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1458

## Notes

Section 2811, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4831, formerly Pub. L. 105–85, div. C, title XXXI, § 3158, Nov. 18, 1997, 111 Stat. 2046; Pub. L. 108–7, div. D, title V, § 506, Feb. 20, 2003, 117 Stat. 158; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4831, by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(k)(11), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1785; Pub. L. 108–137, title V, § 504(a), Dec. 1, 2003, 117 Stat. 1868; Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146(a)(2)(K), (i)(6), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1073, 1082, related to transfers of real property at certain Department of Energy facilities and indemnification. See section 6351 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2812, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4832, formerly Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [div. C, title XXXI, § 3156], Oct. 30, 2000, 114 Stat. 1654, 1654A–467; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4832, by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(k)(12), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1785; Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3131(z), Jan. 2, 2013, 126 Stat. 2185; Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146(i)(7)(A), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1083; Pub. L. 113–291, div. C, title XXXI, § 3142(u), Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3902, authorized creation of engineering and manufacturing research, development, and demonstration programs by managers of certain nuclear weapons production facilities. See section 6352 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2813, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4833, formerly Pub. L. 105–85, div. C, title XXXI, § 3138, Nov. 18, 1997, 111 Stat. 2039; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4833, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(k)(13), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1786; Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3131(aa), Jan. 2, 2013, 126 Stat. 2185, authorized a pilot program relating to the use of proceeds of disposal or utilization of certain Department of Energy assets. See section 6354 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Activities at Covered Nuclear Weapons FacilitiesPub. L. 108–447, div. C, title III, § 308, Dec. 8, 2004, 118 Stat. 2959, which was set out as a note under section 2812 of this title and authorized engagement in research, development, and demonstration activities at certain nuclear weapons facilities, was repealed by Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(b)(20), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1459. See section 6353 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
