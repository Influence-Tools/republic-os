# 50 U.S.C. § 2621 to 2623 - Repealed. Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(b)(1), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1458

## Notes

Section 2621, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLIV, § 4441, formerly Pub. L. 101–510, div. C, title XXXI, § 3137, Nov. 5, 1990, 104 Stat. 1833; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLIV, § 4441, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(g)(16), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1767; Pub. L. 113–291, div. C, title XXXI, § 3142(h), Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3900, provided for safety measures for high-level nuclear waste tanks at Hanford Nuclear Reservation. See section 6201 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2622, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLIV, § 4442, formerly Pub. L. 105–261, div. C, title XXXI, § 3139, Oct. 17, 1998, 112 Stat. 2250; Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(b)–(d)], Oct. 30, 2000, 114 Stat. 1654, 1654A–463; Pub. L. 107–107, div. C, title XXXI, § 3135, Dec. 28, 2001, 115 Stat. 1368; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLIV, § 4442, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(g)(17), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1767; Pub. L. 112–81, div. C, title XXXI, § 3113, Dec. 31, 2011, 125 Stat. 1709; Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146(e)(13), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1078; Pub. L. 115–232, div. C, title XXXI, § 3118, Aug. 13, 2018, 132 Stat. 2292, related to establishment of the Office of River Protection at the Hanford Reservation. See section 6202 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2623, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLIV, § 4443, formerly Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(a)], Oct. 30, 2000, 114 Stat. 1654, 1654A–462; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLIV, § 4443, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(g)(18), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1768, designated the tank waste remediation system environmental project in Richland, Washington as the River Protection Project. See section 6203 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
