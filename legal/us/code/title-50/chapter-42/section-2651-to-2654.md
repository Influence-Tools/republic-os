# 50 U.S.C. § 2651 to 2654 - Repealed. Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(b)(1), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1458

## Notes

Section 2651, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLV, § 4501, formerly Pub. L. 104–106, div. C, title XXXI, § 3154, Feb. 10, 1996, 110 Stat. 624; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLV, § 4501, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(h)(2), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1771; Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3131(j), Jan. 2, 2013, 126 Stat. 2182; Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146(a)(2)(F), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1073; Pub. L. 113–291, div. C, title XXXI, § 3142(j), Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3900, prohibited international inspections of Department of Energy facilities unless protection of Restricted Data was certified. See section 6221 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2652, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLV, § 4502, formerly Pub. L. 106–65, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146, Oct. 5, 1999, 113 Stat. 935; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLV, § 4502, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(h)(3), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1771; Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3131(k)(1), (bb)(1)(D), Jan. 2, 2013, 126 Stat. 2182, 2185; Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146(f)(1), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1079; Pub. L. 118–159, div. C, title XXXI, § 3112, Dec. 23, 2024, 138 Stat. 2296, restricted access to national security laboratories by foreign visitors from certain sensitive countries. See section 6222 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2653, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLV, § 4503, formerly Pub. L. 106–65, div. C, title XXXI, § 3143, Oct. 5, 1999, 113 Stat. 934; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLV, § 4503, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(h)(4), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1772; Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3131(l), Jan. 2, 2013, 126 Stat. 2182, required background investigations for personnel at Department of Energy facilities working around Restricted Data. See section 6223 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2654, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLV, § 4504, formerly Pub. L. 107–107, div. C, title XXXI, § 3152, Dec. 28, 2001, 115 Stat. 1376; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLV, § 4504, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(h)(5)(A), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1772; Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146(f)(2), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1079; Pub. L. 115–232, div. C, title XXXI, § 3112, Aug. 13, 2018, 132 Stat. 2290, related to a new counterintelligence polygraph program for the Department of Energy. See section 6224 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Pilot Program on Conduct by Department of Energy of Background Reviews for Access by Certain Individuals to National Security LaboratoriesPub. L. 115–232, div. C, title XXXI, § 3121, Aug. 13, 2018, 132 Stat. 2295, as amended by Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(c)(2)(M), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1461, which was set out as a note under section 2652 of this title and established a pilot program to assess the feasibility and advisability of conducting certain background reviews, was transferred and is set out as a note under section 6222 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
