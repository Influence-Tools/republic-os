# 50 U.S.C. § 2781 to 2787 - Repealed. Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(b)(1), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1458

## Notes

Section 2781, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4801, formerly Pub. L. 99–145, title XV, § 1534, Nov. 8, 1985, 99 Stat. 774; Pub. L. 100–180, div. C, title I, § 3131(a), Dec. 4, 1987, 101 Stat. 1238; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4801, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(k)(2), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1783; Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146(i)(1), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1081, set out various costs not allowed under covered contracts. See section 6321 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2782, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4802, formerly Pub. L. 101–189, div. C, title XXXI, § 3151, Nov. 29, 1989, 103 Stat. 1682; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4802, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(k)(3), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1783; Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3131(v), Jan. 2, 2013, 126 Stat. 2184; Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146(a)(2)(J), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1073, established a prohibition on bonuses to contractors operating defense nuclear facilities. See section 6322 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2782a, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4802A, as added Pub. L. 114–92, div. C, title XXXI, § 3134(a), Nov. 25, 2015, 129 Stat. 1207, required biennial assessments of emergency preparedness of defense nuclear facilities. See section 6323 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2783, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4803, formerly Pub. L. 101–510, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141, Nov. 5, 1990, 104 Stat. 1837; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4803, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(k)(4), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1783; Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146(i)(2), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1081, known as the Atomic Testing Liability Act, related to contractor liability for injury or loss of property arising out of atomic weapons testing programs. See section 6324 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2784, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4804, as added Pub. L. 109–364, div. C, title XXXI, § 3118, Oct. 17, 2006, 120 Stat. 2509, established a notice-and-wait requirement applicable to certain third-party financing arrangements. See section 6325 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2785, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4805, as added Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3117(a)(1), Jan. 2, 2013, 126 Stat. 2173, related to publication of contractor performance evaluations leading to award fees. See section 6326 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2786, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4806, as added Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3113(a), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1053; amended Pub. L. 113–291, div. C, title XXXI, § 3142(s), Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3901; Pub. L. 115–232, div. C, title XXXI, § 3117, Aug. 13, 2018, 132 Stat. 2292; Pub. L. 116–92, div. C, title XXXI, § 3133, Dec. 20, 2019, 133 Stat. 1958; Pub. L. 116–283, div. C, title XXXI, § 3161, Jan. 1, 2021, 134 Stat. 4391; Pub. L. 117–81, div. C, title XXXI, § 3119, Dec. 27, 2021, 135 Stat. 2228, provided enhanced procurement authority to the Secretary of Energy to manage supply chain risk. See section 6327 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2787, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVIII, § 4807, as added Pub. L. 115–232, div. C, title XXXI, § 3131(a), Aug. 13, 2018, 132 Stat. 2298; amended Pub. L. 118–31, div. C, title XXXI, § 3119, Dec. 22, 2023, 137 Stat. 791; Pub. L. 118–159, div. C, title XXXI, § 3122, Dec. 23, 2024, 138 Stat. 2300, related to cost-benefit analyses for competition of management and operating contracts. See section 6328 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
