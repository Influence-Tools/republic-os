# 50 U.S.C. § 2511 to 2513 - Repealed. Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(b)(1), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1458

## Notes

Section 2511, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLI, § 4101, formerly Pub. L. 98–525, title XVI, § 1634, Oct. 19, 1984, 98 Stat. 2649; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLI, § 4101, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(d)(2), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1757, related to the continuation in force of Executive Order 12344 pertaining to the Naval Nuclear Propulsion Program. See section 6102 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2512, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLI, § 4102, formerly Pub. L. 104–201, div. C, title XXXI, § 3140, Sept. 23, 1996, 110 Stat. 2833; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLI, § 4102, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(d)(3), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1757; Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3113(a), Jan. 2, 2013, 126 Stat. 2169; Pub. L. 113–291, div. C, title XXXI, § 3142(b), Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3900, related to establishment of a management structure for the nuclear security enterprise and of the National Nuclear Security Administration Council. See section 6103 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 2513, Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLI, § 4103, formerly Pub. L. 96–540, title II, § 210, Dec. 17, 1980, 94 Stat. 3202; renumbered Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLI, § 4103, and amended Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(d)(4), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1757; Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146(b), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1073, restricted use of funds for licensing of certain activities or facilities. See section 6106 of Title 10, Armed Forces. Provisions similar to those in section 2513 were contained in Pub. L. 96–164, title II, § 210, Dec. 29, 1979, 93 Stat. 1264.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Monitoring of Industrial Base for Nuclear Weapons Components, Subsystems, and MaterialsPub. L. 116–283, div. C, title XXXI, § 3113, Jan. 1, 2021, 134 Stat. 4378, as amended by Pub. L. 117–81, div. C, title XXXI, § 3135, Dec. 27, 2021, 135 Stat. 2231, which related to monitoring the industrial base that supports the nuclear weapons components, subsystems, and materials of the Administration, including required consultations and briefings, was repealed by Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(b)(8), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1459. See section 6104 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Common Financial Reporting System for the Nuclear Security EnterprisePub. L. 114–328, div. C, title XXXI, § 3113, Dec. 23, 2016, 130 Stat. 2757, as amended by Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(c)(2)(E), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1460, which related to implementation of a common financial reporting system for the nuclear security enterprise, was repealed by Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(b)(9), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1459. See Section 6105 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Governance and Management of Nuclear Security EnterprisePub. L. 114–92, div. C, title XXXI, § 3137, Nov. 25, 2015, 129 Stat. 1213, which was set out as a note under section 2512 of this title and related to development of an implementation plan to reform the governance and management of the nuclear security enterprise, was transferred and is set out as a note under section 6103 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Clarification of Role of Secretary of EnergyPub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141, Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1069, which related to continued authority of the Secretary of Energy to carry out certain activities or to delegate authority, was repealed by Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(b)(2), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1458.

Executive Documents

Executive Order No. 12344 Ex. Ord. No. 12344, Feb. 1, 1982, 47 F.R. 4979, which related to the Naval Nuclear Propulsion Program, was transferred and is set out as a note under section 6102 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
