# 50 U.S.C. § 415a–2 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

C, and also of the Consolidated Security, Disaster Assistance, and Continuing Appropriations Act, 2009, Pub. L. 110–329, was editorially reclassified as section 3305 of this title.
