# 50 U.S.C. § 541 to 546 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections 541 to 546, act Aug. 10, 1950, ch. 673, §§ 2–7, 64 Stat. 435–437, terminated not later than ten years after Apr. 1, 1950. See Effective and Termination Date note below. Section 541 related to Congressional declaration of policy. Section 542 related to production program of abacá. Section 543 related to administration. Section 544 related to financing. Section 545 related to disposal of property. Section 546 related to reports to Congress.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective and Termination DatesAct Aug. 10, 1950, ch. 673, § 8, 64 Stat. 437, provided that this chapter become effective Apr. 1, 1950, and remain effective for ten years thereafter, unless Congress or President direct earlier termination of operations, and for such further period as necessary to earliest practicable liquidation of operations under this chapter.
