# 14 U.S.C. § 3905 - Disenrollment

## Text

Members of the Auxiliary may be disenrolled pursuant to applicable regulations.

(Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, 63 Stat. 555, § 824; renumbered § 3905, Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 119(b), Dec. 4, 2018, 132 Stat. 4236.)

## Notes

Historical and Revision NotesExperience has shown that it is desirable to have a statute definitely providing for separation of Auxiliarists from the organization. 81st Congress, House Report No. 557.

Editorial Notes

Amendments2018—Pub. L. 115–282 renumbered section 824 of this title as this section.
