# 14 U.S.C. § 2114 - United States Deputy Marshals in Alaska

## Text

Commissioned officers may be appointed as United States Deputy Marshals in Alaska.

(Added Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 112(c)(3), Dec. 4, 2018, 132 Stat. 4221.)
