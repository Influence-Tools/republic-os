# 14 U.S.C. § 3756 - Grade on entry upon active duty

## Text

A Reserve officer ordered to active duty or active duty for training shall be ordered in the grade held; except that the Secretary may authorize a higher grade.

(Added Pub. L. 96–322, § 1, Aug. 4, 1980, 94 Stat. 1015, § 745; renumbered § 3756, Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 118(b), Dec. 4, 2018, 132 Stat. 4233.)
