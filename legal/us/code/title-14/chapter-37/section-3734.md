# 14 U.S.C. § 3734 - Effect of this subchapter on retirement and retired pay

## Text

Except as provided in subsection 3757(b) 11 So in original. Probably should be “section 3757(b)”. of this title, nothing in this subchapter authorizes the retirement of a Reserve officer or the payment of retired, retainer, or severance pay to a Reserve officer; or affects in any manner the law relating to the retirement of, or the granting of retired or retainer pay or other benefits to a Reserve officer.

(Added Pub. L. 96–322, § 1, Aug. 4, 1980, 94 Stat. 1007, § 723; renumbered § 3734 and amended Pub. L. 115–282, title I, §§ 118(b), 123(b)(2), Dec. 4, 2018, 132 Stat. 4233, 4240.)
