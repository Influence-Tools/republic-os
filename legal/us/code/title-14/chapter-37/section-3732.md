# 14 U.S.C. § 3732 - Applicability of this subchapter

## Text

This subchapter applies only to the Reserve; except that it does not apply to a temporary member of the Reserve.

(Added Pub. L. 96–322, § 1, Aug. 4, 1980, 94 Stat. 1007, § 721; renumbered § 3732, Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 118(b), Dec. 4, 2018, 132 Stat. 4233.)
