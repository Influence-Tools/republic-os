# 36 U.S.C. § 50305 - Exclusive right to name

## Text

The corporation and its State and local subdivisions have the exclusive right to use the name “Disabled American Veterans”.

(Pub. L. 105–225, Aug. 12, 1998, 112 Stat. 1344.)
