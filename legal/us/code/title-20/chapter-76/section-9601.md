# 20 U.S.C. § 9601 - Definitions

## Text

In this subchapter:

(1) In general The terms “local educational agency” and “State educational agency” have the meanings given those terms in section 7801 of this title.

(2) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Education.

(Pub. L. 107–279, title II, § 202, Nov. 5, 2002, 116 Stat. 1975; Pub. L. 114–95, title IX, § 9215(gg)(1), Dec. 10, 2015, 129 Stat. 2174.)
