# 20 U.S.C. § 9582 - Copyright

## Text

Nothing in this Act shall be construed to affect the rights, remedies, limitations, or defense under title 17.

(Pub. L. 107–279, title I, § 192, Nov. 5, 2002, 116 Stat. 1974.)
