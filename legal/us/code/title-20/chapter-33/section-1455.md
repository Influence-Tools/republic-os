# 20 U.S.C. § 1455 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 2005 through 2010.

(Pub. L. 91–230, title VI, § 655, as added Pub. L. 108–446, title I, § 101, Dec. 3, 2004, 118 Stat. 2774.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 1455, Pub. L. 91–230, title VI, § 655, as added Pub. L. 105–17, title I, § 101, June 4, 1997, 111 Stat. 129, set out minimum State grant amounts, prior to the general amendment of subchapters I to IV of this chapter by Pub. L. 108–446. A prior section 1456, Pub. L. 91–230, title VI, § 656, as added Pub. L. 105–17, title I, § 101, June 4, 1997, 111 Stat. 129, which authorized appropriations, was omitted in the general amendment of subchapters I to IV of this chapter by Pub. L. 108–446.
