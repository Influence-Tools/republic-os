# 20 U.S.C. § 6121 to 6127 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections 6121 to 6127 were omitted pursuant to section 6251 of this title which provided that the authority under this chapter terminated on Oct. 1, 2001. Section 6121, Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 201, May 4, 1994, 108 Stat. 578, stated purpose of this part. Section 6122, Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 202, May 4, 1994, 108 Stat. 578, authorized grants to States and territories. Section 6123, Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 203, May 4, 1994, 108 Stat. 579, related to application for grant. Section 6124, Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 204, May 4, 1994, 108 Stat. 580, related to approval of application. Section 6125, Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 205, May 4, 1994, 108 Stat. 580, related to use of amounts from grant. Section 6126, Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 206, May 4, 1994, 108 Stat. 581, related to maintenance of effort by State. Section 6127, Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 207, May 4, 1994, 108 Stat. 582, required reports by State.
