# 20 U.S.C. § 8701 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–10, title XIII, § 13401, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3886, which authorized technology-based technical assistance, was renumbered section 1031 of the Educational Research, Development, Dissemination, and Improvement Act of 1994 by Pub. L. 107–110, title X, § 1024(a), (b), Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1987, 1988, transferred to section 6056 of this title, and subsequently repealed.
