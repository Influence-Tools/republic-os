# 20 U.S.C. § 7151 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 7151, Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 4141, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title IV, § 401, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1762, which related to gun-free requirements, was renumbered section 8561 of Pub. L. 89–10 by Pub. L. 114–95, title IV, § 4001(a)(2)(D), title VIII, § 8001(a)(8), Dec. 10, 2015, 129 Stat. 1966, 2088, 2089, and transferred to section 7961 of this title.
