# 20 U.S.C. § 9241 to 9243 - Repealed. Pub. L. 113–128, title V, § 511(a), July 22, 2014, 128 Stat. 1705

## Notes

Section 9241, Pub. L. 105–220, title II, § 231, Aug. 7, 1998, 112 Stat. 1070, related to grants and contracts for eligible providers.

Section 9242, Pub. L. 105–220, title II, § 232, Aug. 7, 1998, 112 Stat. 1072, related to submission of applications by eligible providers.

Section 9243, Pub. L. 105–220, title II, § 233, Aug. 7, 1998, 112 Stat. 1072, related to local administrative cost limits.
