# 20 U.S.C. § 1865 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–380, title IV, § 406, Aug. 21, 1974, 88 Stat. 551; Pub. L. 94–273, § 2(15), Apr. 21, 1976, 90 Stat. 375; Pub. L. 94–482, title V, § 501(a)(6), (7), Oct. 12, 1976, 90 Stat. 2235; Pub. L. 95–207, § 13(e), Dec. 13, 1977, 91 Stat. 1472; Pub. L. 95–561, title III, § 301(b)(1)(E), (3), Nov. 1, 1978, 92 Stat. 2228, which related to career education and establishment of Office of Career Education and National Advisory Council for Career Education, was transferred to section 2612a of this title and subsequently omitted from the Code.
