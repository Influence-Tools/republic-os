# 20 U.S.C. § 75g - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 87–443, § 8, Apr. 27, 1962, 76 Stat. 63.)
