# 20 U.S.C. § 80r–7 - Congressional Budget Act compliance

## Text

Authority under this subchapter to enter into contracts or to make payments shall be effective in any fiscal year only to the extent provided in advance in an appropriations Act, except as provided under section 80r–9(b) of this title.

(Pub. L. 108–184, § 9, Dec. 16, 2003, 117 Stat. 2682.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextThe Congressional Budget Act, referred to in section catchline, probably means the Congressional Budget Act of 1974, which is titles I through IX of Pub. L. 93–344, July 12, 1974, 88 Stat. 297. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under section 621 of Title 2, The Congress, and Tables.
