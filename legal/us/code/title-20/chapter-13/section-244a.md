# 20 U.S.C. § 244a - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 16, 1940, ch. 629, 54 Stat. 761, which related to school facilities for children of Government employees and other residents in Indian reservations, national parks, and national monuments, was transferred to section 7713a of this title.
