# 20 U.S.C. § 245 - Repealed. Pub. L. 85–620, title II, § 206, Aug. 12, 1958, 72 Stat. 561

## Notes

Section, act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, § 10, as added Aug. 8, 1953, ch. 402, § 11, 67 Stat. 536; amended Aug. 12, 1955, ch. 868, § 1, 69 Stat. 713; Aug. 3, 1956, ch. 915, title II, § 212, 70 Stat. 972, related to election by States to receive payments with respect to education of Indian children.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealPub. L. 85–620, title II, § 207, Aug. 12, 1958, 72 Stat. 561, provided that: “The amendments made by this title [amending sections 237, 238, 239, 243, 244 of this title and repealing this section] shall be effective for the period beginning July 1, 1958.”
