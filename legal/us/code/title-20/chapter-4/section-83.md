# 20 U.S.C. § 83 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 18, 1894, ch. 301, § 1, 28 Stat. 384, which required that a detailed report of expenses on account of the National Zoological Park be made to Congress at the beginning of each regular session, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66, as amended, set out as a note under section 1113 of Title 31, Money and Finance. See, also, page 192 of House Document No. 103–7.
