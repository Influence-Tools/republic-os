# 20 U.S.C. § 881 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 801, formerly title VI, § 601, Apr. 11, 1965, 79 Stat. 55, renumbered title VII, § 701, Pub. L. 89–750, title I, § 161, Nov. 3, 1966, 80 Stat. 1204, renumbered title VIII, § 801, and amended Pub. L. 90–247, title I, §§ 142(b), 152(c), title VII, §§ 702, 703, Jan. 2, 1968, 81 Stat. 799, 803, 816, 819; Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 162, Apr. 13, 1970, 84 Stat. 152; Pub. L. 94–193, § 1(c), Dec. 31, 1975, 89 Stat. 1102; Pub. L. 94–482, title V, § 501(l), Oct. 12, 1976, 90 Stat. 2237, which related to definitions, was renumbered Pub. L. 89–10, title X, § 1001 by Pub. L. 95–561, title VIII, § 801(1), (2), Nov. 1, 1978, 92 Stat. 2284, and transferred to section 3381 of this title, and was subsequently omitted in the general amendment of Pub. L. 89–10 by Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3519.
