# 20 U.S.C. § 887e - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 812, as added Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 110, Aug. 21, 1974, 88 Stat. 513, which provided for open meetings of educational agencies, was renumbered Pub. L. 89–10, title X, § 1006, by Pub. L. 95–561, title VIII, § 801(1), (2), Nov. 1, 1978, 92 Stat. 2284, and transferred to section 3386 of this title, and was subsequently omitted in the general amendment of Pub. L. 89–10 by Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3519.
