# 20 U.S.C. § 3423b - Office of Non-Public Education

## Text

There shall be in the Department an Office of Non-Public Education to ensure the maximum potential participation of non-public school students in all Federal educational programs for which such students are eligible.

(Pub. L. 96–88, title II, § 214, as added Pub. L. 103–382, title II, § 271(c), Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3929.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 214 of Pub. L. 96–88 was renumbered section 212 and is classified to section 3423a of this title. Another prior section 214 of Pub. L. 96–88 was renumbered section 217 and is classified to section 3424 of this title.
