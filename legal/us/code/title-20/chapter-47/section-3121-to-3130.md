# 20 U.S.C. § 3121 to 3130 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, Pub. L. 89–10, by Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101, Oct. 20, 1994, 108 Stat. 3519.
