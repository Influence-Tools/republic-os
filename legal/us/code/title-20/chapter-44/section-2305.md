# 20 U.S.C. § 2305 - Limitation

## Text

All of the funds made available under this chapter shall be used in accordance with the requirements of this chapter.

(Pub. L. 88–210, § 6, as added Pub. L. 109–270, § 1(b), Aug. 12, 2006, 120 Stat. 690.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 2305, Pub. L. 88–210, § 6, as added Pub. L. 105–332, § 1(b), Oct. 31, 1998, 112 Stat. 3082, related to limitation on use of funds, prior to the general amendment of this chapter by Pub. L. 109–270.
