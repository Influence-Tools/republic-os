# 12 U.S.C. § 4212 - Protection for declarants

## Text

A declarant under this subchapter shall enjoy the protections of section 3059A(e) 11 See References in Text note below. of title 18.

(Pub. L. 101–647, title XXV, § 2572, Nov. 29, 1990, 104 Stat. 4899.)
