# 12 U.S.C. § 1430a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 22, 1932, ch. 522, § 10a, as added June 27, 1934, ch. 847, § 502, 48 Stat. 1261, provided for advances by Federal Home Loan Banks to finance home repairs, improvements, and alterations until July 1, 1936.
