# 12 U.S.C. § 5328 - Council funding

## Text

Any expenses of the Council shall be treated as expenses of, and paid by, the Office of Financial Research.

(Pub. L. 111–203, title I, § 118, July 21, 2010, 124 Stat. 1408.)
