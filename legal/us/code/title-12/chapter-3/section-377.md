# 12 U.S.C. § 377 - Repealed. Pub. L. 106–102, title I, § 101(a), Nov. 12, 1999, 113 Stat. 1341

## Notes

Section, acts June 16, 1933, ch. 89, § 20, 48 Stat. 188; Aug. 23, 1935, ch. 614, title II, § 203(a), title III, § 302, 49 Stat. 704, 707, prohibited member banks from affiliating with organizations dealing in securities and provided for penalties.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective 120 days after Nov. 12, 1999, see section 161 of Pub. L. 106–102, set out as an Effective Date of 1999 Amendment note under section 24 of this title.
