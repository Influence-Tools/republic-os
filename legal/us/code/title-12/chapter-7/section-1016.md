# 12 U.S.C. § 1016 - Repealed and Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section (except subsec. (h)), acts May 12, 1933, ch. 25, title II, § 32, 48 Stat. 48; June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VIII, § 80(a), 48 Stat. 273; Jan. 31, 1934, ch. 7, §§ 9, 10, 48 Stat. 347; June 11, 1934, ch. 446, 48 Stat. 929; May 28, 1935, ch. 150, § 32, 49 Stat. 300; June 3, 1935, ch. 164, § 2, 49 Stat. 313; July 22, 1937, ch. 516, § 2, 50 Stat. 521; Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, §§ 13, 14, 50 Stat. 708; June 16, 1938, ch. 462, § 2, 52 Stat. 709; Feb. 1, 1940, ch. 19, 54 Stat. 19; June 29, 1940, ch. 441, § 1, 54 Stat. 684; June 3, 1942, ch. 321, 56 Stat. 306; June 27, 1942, ch. 449, § 2, 56 Stat. 392; June 26, 1943, ch. 146, 57 Stat. 196; June 30, 1944, ch. 329, 58 Stat. 646; June 30, 1945, ch. 204, §§ 11–13, 59 Stat. 269; July 12, 1946, ch. 570, § 1, 60 Stat. 532; June 30, 1947, ch. 166, title II, § 206(h), 61 Stat. 208; Aug. 18, 1959, Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h), 73 Stat. 387; Oct. 4, 1961, Pub. L. 87–353, § 3(b), 75 Stat. 773, related to general provisions governing loans to farmers by Land Bank Commission, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 87–353, § 3(b), Oct. 4, 1961, 75 Stat. 773. Subsec. (h) provided: “Any instrument heretofore or hereafter executed on behalf of the Land Bank Commissioner and/or the Federal Farm Mortgage Corporation by a Federal land bank, through its duly authorized officers, shall be conclusively presumed to have been duly authorized by the Land Bank Commissioner and the Federal Farm Mortgage Corporation.”
