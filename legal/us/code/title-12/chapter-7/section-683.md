# 12 U.S.C. § 683 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, acts July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 4, 39 Stat. 363; Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252, title III, § 304, 42 Stat. 1474; June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VII, § 70, title VIII, § 80a, 48 Stat. 269, 273, related to compensation and allowances of directors, officers, and employees, and was stricken by act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 7(b), 50 Stat. 707. Section 70 of act June 16, 1933, ch. 98, was repealed by Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 648. See section 2226 of this title.
