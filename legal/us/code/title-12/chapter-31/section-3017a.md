# 12 U.S.C. § 3017a - Class A notes as paid-in capital of the Bank

## Text

Only for purposes of section 3017(a) of this title, class A notes shall be deemed to be paid-in capital of the Bank.

(Pub. L. 97–35, title III, § 391(b)(1), Aug. 13, 1981, 95 Stat. 434.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was enacted as part of the National Consumer Cooperative Bank Act Amendments of 1981 and also as part of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1981, and not as part of the National Consumer Cooperative Bank Act which comprises this chapter.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries
