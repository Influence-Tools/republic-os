# 12 U.S.C. § 2214a - “Bank” defined

## Text

In this part, the term “bank” includes each association operating under subchapter II.

(Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4.28A, as added Pub. L. 104–105, title II, § 209, Feb. 10, 1996, 110 Stat. 174.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 4.28A of title IV of Pub. L. 92–181, which provided for chartering of Farm Credit System Capital Corporation by Farm Credit Administration and revoked charter of Farm Credit System Capital Corporation which had been issued under part D of this subchapter, was classified to section 2216 of this title, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 100–233, title II, § 207(a)(3), Jan. 6, 1988, 101 Stat. 1607.
