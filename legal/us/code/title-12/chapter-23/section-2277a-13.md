# 12 U.S.C. § 2277a–13 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5.64, as added Pub. L. 100–233, title III, § 302, Jan. 6, 1988, 101 Stat. 1619, which required the Farm Credit System Insurance Corporation to submit an annual report to Congress on the operations of the Corporation, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66, as amended, set out as a note under section 1113 of Title 31, Money and Finance. See, also, page 167 of House Document No. 103–7.
