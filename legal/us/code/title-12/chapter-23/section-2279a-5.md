# 12 U.S.C. § 2279a–5 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–181, title VII, § 7.5, as added Pub. L. 100–233, title IV, § 416, Jan. 6, 1988, 101 Stat. 1646, which required reports by merged banks for cooperatives, was renumbered section 3.29 of title III of Pub. L. 92–181 by Pub. L. 100–399, title IV, § 408(e), Aug. 17, 1988, 102 Stat. 1001, and was classified to section 2149a of this title, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 115–334, title V, § 5411(16), Dec. 20, 2018, 132 Stat. 4680.
