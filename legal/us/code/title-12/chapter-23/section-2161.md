# 12 U.S.C. § 2161 - Repealed. Pub. L. 100–399, title I, § 101(a), Aug. 17, 1988, 102 Stat. 989

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4.9A, as added Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 105, Dec. 23, 1985, 99 Stat. 1687, authorized a central reserve for Farm Credit System.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective immediately after enactment of Pub. L. 100–233, approved Jan. 6, 1988, see section 1001(a) of Pub. L. 100–399, set out as a note under section 2002 of this title.
