# 12 U.S.C. § 162 - Repealed. Pub. L. 86–671, § 6, July 14, 1960, 74 Stat. 552.

## Notes

Section, act Feb. 26, 1881, ch. 82, 21 Stat. 352, prescribed the manner of verification of reports of condition of national banks. See section 1817 of this title.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective Jan. 1, 1961, see section 7 of Pub. L. 86–671, set out as an Effective Date of 1960 Amendment note under section 1817 of this title.
