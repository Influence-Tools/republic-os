# 12 U.S.C. § 28 - Repealed. Pub. L. 103–325, title VI, § 602(e)(1), Sept. 23, 1994, 108 Stat. 2291

## Notes

Section, R.S. § 5170, required publication of certificate of authority to commence banking for 60 days after issuance.

Editorial Notes

Codification R.S. § 5170 derived from act June 3, 1864, ch. 106, § 18, 13 Stat. 104, which was the National Bank Act. See section 38 of this title.
