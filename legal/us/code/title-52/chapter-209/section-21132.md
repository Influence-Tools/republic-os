# 52 U.S.C. § 21132 - Transfer of functions

## Text

There are transferred to the Election Assistance Commission established under section 20921 of this title all functions which the Federal Election Commission exercised under section 20508(a) of this title before October 29, 2002.

(Pub. L. 107–252, title VIII, § 802(a), Oct. 29, 2002, 116 Stat. 1726.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 15532 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
