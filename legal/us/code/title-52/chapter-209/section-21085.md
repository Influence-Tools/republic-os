# 52 U.S.C. § 21085 - Methods of implementation left to discretion of State

## Text

The specific choices on the methods of complying with the requirements of this subchapter shall be left to the discretion of the State.

(Pub. L. 107–252, title III, § 306, formerly § 305, Oct. 29, 2002, 116 Stat. 1714; renumbered § 306, Pub. L. 118–106, § 2(a)(1), Oct. 4, 2024, 138 Stat. 1591.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextThis subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title III of Pub. L. 107–252, Oct. 29, 2002, 116 Stat. 1704, which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title III to the Code, see Tables.

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 15485 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
