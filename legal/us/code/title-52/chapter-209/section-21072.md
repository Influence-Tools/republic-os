# 52 U.S.C. § 21072 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out the provisions of this part $200,000 for fiscal year 2003 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the 6 succeeding fiscal years.

(Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 296, Oct. 29, 2002, 116 Stat. 1704.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 15472 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
