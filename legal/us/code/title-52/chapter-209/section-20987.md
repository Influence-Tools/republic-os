# 52 U.S.C. § 20987 - Consultation with Standards Board and Board of Advisors

## Text

The Commission shall carry out its duties under this part in consultation with the Standards Board and the Board of Advisors.

(Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 247, Oct. 29, 2002, 116 Stat. 1692.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 15387 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
