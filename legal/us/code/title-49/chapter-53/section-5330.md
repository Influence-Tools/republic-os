# 49 U.S.C. § 5330 - Repealed. Pub. L. 112–141, div. B, § 20030(e), July 6, 2012, 126 Stat. 731]

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 103–272, § 1(d), July 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 831; Pub. L. 109–59, title III, §§ 3002(b)(4), 3029(a), Aug. 10, 2005, 119 Stat. 1545, 1625, related to State safety oversight of certain rail fixed guideway public transportation systems.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealPub. L. 112–141, div. B, § 20030(e), July 6, 2012, 126 Stat. 731, provided that the repeal of this section is effective 3 years after the effective date of the final rules issued by the Secretary of Transportation under section 5329(e) of this title. Such effective date is Apr. 15, 2016, see 81 F.R. 14230.
