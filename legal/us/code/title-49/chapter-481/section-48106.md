# 49 U.S.C. § 48106 - Airway science curriculum grants

## Text

Amounts are available from the Airport and Airway Trust Fund established under section 9502 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (26 U.S.C. 9502) to carry out section 44510 11 See References in Text note below. of this title. The amounts remain available until expended.

(Pub. L. 103–272, § 1(e), July 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 1296.)
