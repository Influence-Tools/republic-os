# 49 U.S.C. § 15103 - Remedies as cumulative

## Text

Except as otherwise provided in this part, the remedies provided under this part are in addition to remedies existing under another law or common law.

(Added Pub. L. 104–88, title I, § 106(a), Dec. 29, 1995, 109 Stat. 922.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsProvisions similar to those in this section were contained in section 10103 of this title prior to the general amendment of this subtitle by Pub. L. 104–88, § 102(a).
