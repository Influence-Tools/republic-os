# 49 U.S.C. § 47132 - Repealed. Pub. L. 106–181, title I, § 123(a)(1), Apr. 5, 2000, 114 Stat. 74]

## Notes

Section, added Pub. L. 104–264, title I, § 142(a), Oct. 9, 1996, 110 Stat. 3221, temporarily directed the Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration to issue guidelines to carry out not more than 10 pavement maintenance pilot projects.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal applicable only to fiscal years beginning after Sept. 30, 1999, see section 3 of Pub. L. 106–181, set out as an Effective Date of 2000 Amendments note under section 106 of this title.
