# 42 U.S.C. § 7381g - Definitions

## Text

In this part:

(1) Director The term “Director” means the Director of Science, Engineering, and Mathematics Education.

(2) National Laboratory The term “National Laboratory” has the meaning given the term in section 15801 of this title.

(Pub. L. 101–510, div. C, title XXXI, § 3170, as added Pub. L. 110–69, title V, § 5003(d)(3), Aug. 9, 2007, 121 Stat. 603.)
