# 42 U.S.C. § 7274m to 7274o - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 7274m, Pub. L. 103–337, div. C, title XXXI, § 3163, Oct. 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 3097, which related to safety oversight and enforcement at defense nuclear facilities, was renumbered section 4642 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(i)(13)(A)–(C), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1779, and was formerly classified to section 2732 of Title 50, War and National Defense. See section 6262 of Title 10, Armed Forces. Section 7274n, Pub. L. 104–201, div. C, title XXXI, § 3143, Sept. 23, 1996, 110 Stat. 2836, which related to projects to accelerate closure activities at defense nuclear facilities, was renumbered section 4421 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(g)(11)(A)–(C), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1766, and was classified to former section 2601 of Title 50, War and National Defense, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 113–66, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146(e)(10), Dec. 26, 2013, 127 Stat. 1077. Section 7274o, Pub. L. 104–201, div. C, title XXXI, § 3159, Sept. 23, 1996, 110 Stat. 2842; Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title XIII, § 1305(c), (d), Nov. 18, 1997, 111 Stat. 1954; Pub. L. 106–65, div. C, title XXXI, § 3163(f), Oct. 5, 1999, 113 Stat. 946, which related to reports on critical difficulties at nuclear weapons laboratories and nuclear weapons production plants, was renumbered section 4213 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(e)(14), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1760, and was formerly classified to section 2533 of Title 50, War and National Defense. See section 6124 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
