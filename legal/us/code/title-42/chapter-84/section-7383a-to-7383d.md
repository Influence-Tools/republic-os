# 42 U.S.C. § 7383a to 7383d - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 7383a, Pub. L. 106–65, div. C, title XXXI, § 3143, Oct. 5, 1999, 113 Stat. 934, which related to background investigations of certain personnel at Department of Energy facilities, was renumbered section 4503 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(h)(4)(A)–(C), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1772, and was formerly classified to section 2653 of Title 50, War and National Defense. See section 6223 of Title 10, Armed Forces. Section 7383b, Pub. L. 106–65, div. C, title XXXI, § 3145, Oct. 5, 1999, 113 Stat. 935, which related to protection of classified information during laboratory-to-laboratory exchanges, was renumbered section 4524 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(h)(14), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1775, and was formerly classified to section 2674 of Title 50, War and National Defense. See section 6234 of Title 10, Armed Forces. Section 7383c, Pub. L. 106–65, div. C, title XXXI, § 3146, Oct. 5, 1999, 113 Stat. 935, which related to restrictions on access to national laboratories by foreign visitors from sensitive countries, was renumbered section 4502 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(h)(3)(A)–(C), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1771, and was formerly classified to section 2652 of Title 50, War and National Defense. See section 6222 of Title 10, Armed Forces. Section 7383d, Pub. L. 106–65, div. C, title XXXI, § 3150, Oct. 5, 1999, 113 Stat. 939, which related to notice to congressional committees of certain security and counterintelligence failures within nuclear energy defense programs, was renumbered section 4505 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(h)(6), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1773, and was formerly classified to section 2656 of Title 50, War and National Defense. See section 6225 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
