# 42 U.S.C. § 7271d to 7273a - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 7271d, Pub. L. 107–314, div. C, title XXXI, § 3143, Dec. 2, 2002, 116 Stat. 2733, which related to requirements for specific funding requests for new or modified nuclear weapons, was renumbered section 4209 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(e)(10), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1759, and was formerly classified to section 2529 of Title 50, War and National Defense. See section 6120 of Title 10, Armed Forces. Section 7272, Pub. L. 96–540, title II, § 210, Dec. 17, 1980, 94 Stat. 3202, which prohibited the use of funds for purposes related to the licensing of defense activities or facilities of the Department of Energy by the Nuclear Regulatory Commission, was renumbered section 4103 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(d)(4)(A)–(C)(ii), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1757, and was formerly classified to section 2513 of Title 50, War and National Defense. See section 6106 of Title 10, Armed Forces. Section 7273, Pub. L. 96–540, title II, § 211, Dec. 17, 1980, 94 Stat. 3203, which related to restrictions on the use of funds to pay penalties under the Clean Air Act, was renumbered section 4722 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(j)(5)(A)–(C)(ii), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1781, 1782, and was formerly classified to section 2762 of Title 50, War and National Defense. See section 6302 of Title 10, Armed Forces. Section 7273a, Pub. L. 99–661, div. C, title I, § 3132, Nov. 14, 1986, 100 Stat. 4063, which related to restrictions on the use of funds to pay penalties under environmental laws, was renumbered section 4721 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(j)(4)(A)–(C), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1781, and was formerly classified to section 2761 of Title 50, War and National Defense. See section 6301 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
