# 42 U.S.C. § 7274f - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 102–190, div. C, title XXXI, § 3134, Dec. 5, 1991, 105 Stat. 1575, which related to the Defense Environmental Restoration and Waste Management Account, was renumbered section 4401 of Pub. L. 107–314 by Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(g)(2), Nov. 24, 2003, 117 Stat. 1764, and was formerly classified to section 2581 of Title 50, War and National Defense. See section 6171 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
