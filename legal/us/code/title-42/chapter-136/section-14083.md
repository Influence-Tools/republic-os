# 42 U.S.C. § 14083 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–322, title XVIII, § 180104, Sept. 13, 1994, 108 Stat. 2046, which authorized appropriations for the hiring of additional Drug Enforcement Administration agents for fiscal years 1996 to 2000, was omitted as obsolete.
