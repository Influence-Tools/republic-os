# 42 U.S.C. § 14015 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–322, title IV, § 40509, Sept. 13, 1994, 108 Stat. 1950, which related to a report on recordkeeping relating to domestic violence, to be submitted to Congress not later than one year after Sept. 13, 1994, was omitted as obsolete.
