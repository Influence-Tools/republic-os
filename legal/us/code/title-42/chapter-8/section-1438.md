# 42 U.S.C. § 1438 - Repealed. Pub. L. 105–276, title V, § 582(a)(15), Oct. 21, 1998, 112 Stat. 2644

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 93–383, title II, § 209, Aug. 22, 1974, 88 Stat. 669; Pub. L. 98–479, title II, § 201(g), Oct. 17, 1984, 98 Stat. 2228; Pub. L. 102–550, title VI, § 625(b), Oct. 28, 1992, 106 Stat. 3820, related to special low-income housing projects for elderly or disabled families.
