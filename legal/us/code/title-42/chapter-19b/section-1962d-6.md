# 42 U.S.C. § 1962d–6 - Feasibility studies; acceleration; advancement of costs by non-Federal sources

## Text

The Secretary may accelerate feasibility studies authorized by law when and to the extent that the costs of such studies shall have been advanced by non-Federal sources.

(Pub. L. 89–561, § 5, Sept. 7, 1966, 80 Stat. 714.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was not enacted as part of the Water Resources Planning Act which comprises this chapter.
