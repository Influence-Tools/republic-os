# 42 U.S.C. § 10103 - Territories and possessions

## Text

Nothing in this chapter shall be deemed to repeal, modify, or amend the provisions of section 1491 of title 48.

(Pub. L. 97–425, § 4, Jan. 7, 1983, 96 Stat. 2205.)
