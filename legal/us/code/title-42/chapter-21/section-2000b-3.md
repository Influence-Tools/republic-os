# 42 U.S.C. § 2000b–3 - “Complaint” defined

## Text

A complaint as used in this subchapter is a writing or document within the meaning of section 1001, title 18.

(Pub. L. 88–352, title III, § 304, July 2, 1964, 78 Stat. 246.)
