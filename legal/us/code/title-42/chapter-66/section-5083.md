# 42 U.S.C. § 5083 - Repealed. Pub. L. 95–510, § 102(b), Oct. 24, 1978, 92 Stat. 1781

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 93–113, title V, § 503, Oct. 1, 1973, 87 Stat. 415; Pub. L. 94–293, § 6(b), May 27, 1976, 90 Stat. 526, authorized appropriations for national volunteer programs to assist small businesses and promote volunteer service by persons with business experience.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective Oct. 1, 1979, see section 105 of Pub. L. 95–510, set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under section 634 of Title 15, Commerce and Trade.
