# 42 U.S.C. § 1961 - Repealed. Pub. L. 95–467, title IV, § 410(a), Oct. 17, 1978, 92 Stat. 1316

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 88–379, § 1(b), July 17, 1964, 78 Stat. 329, set out Congressional purpose in enacting water resources research program legislation. See section 7802 of this title.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 88–379, § 1(a), July 17, 1964, 78 Stat. 329, provided that Pub. L. 88–379, which enacted sections 1961 to 1961c–8, may be cited as the “Water Resources Research Act of 1964”, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 95–467, title IV, § 410(a), Oct. 17, 1978, 92 Stat. 1316.
