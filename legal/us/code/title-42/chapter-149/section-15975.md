# 42 U.S.C. § 15975 - Petroleum coke gasification

## Text

The Secretary is authorized to provide loan guarantees for at least 5 petroleum coke gasification projects.

(Pub. L. 109–58, title IV, § 415, Aug. 8, 2005, 119 Stat. 756.)
