# 42 U.S.C. § 2000ff–10 - Regulations

## Text

Not later than 1 year after May 21, 2008, the Commission shall issue final regulations to carry out this chapter.

(Pub. L. 110–233, title II, § 211, May 21, 2008, 122 Stat. 920.)

## Notes

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective DateSection effective 18 months after May 21, 2008, see section 213 of Pub. L. 110–233, set out as a note under section 2000ff of this title.
