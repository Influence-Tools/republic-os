# 42 U.S.C. § 2000ff–11 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this chapter (except for section 2000ff–7 of this title).

(Pub. L. 110–233, title II, § 212, May 21, 2008, 122 Stat. 920.)

## Notes

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective DateSection effective 18 months after May 21, 2008, see section 213 of Pub. L. 110–233, set out as a note under section 2000ff of this title.
