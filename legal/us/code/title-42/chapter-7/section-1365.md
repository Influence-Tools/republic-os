# 42 U.S.C. § 1365 - Repealed. Pub. L. 86–442, § 1, Apr. 22, 1960, 74 Stat. 81

## Notes

Section, act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XV, § 1505, as added Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1212, § 4(a), 68 Stat. 1133, related to status of a Federal employee who was performing Federal service at time of separation from employment by the United States.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective only with respect to benefit years which began more than thirty days after Apr. 22, 1960, see Pub. L. 86–442, § 1, Apr. 22, 1960, 74 Stat. 81.
