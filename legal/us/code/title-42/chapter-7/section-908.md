# 42 U.S.C. § 908 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VII, § 707, as added Jan. 2, 1968, Pub. L. 90–248, title IV, § 401, 81 Stat. 930, related to grants for expansion and development of undergraduate and graduate programs in the fiscal year ending June 30, 1969, and each of the three succeeding fiscal years.
