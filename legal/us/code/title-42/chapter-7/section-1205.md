# 42 U.S.C. § 1205 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title X, § 1005, 49 Stat. 647, made available $30,000 for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1936, for expenses in administering sections 1201 to 1204 of this title.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

RepealsPub. L. 92–603, title III, § 303(a), (b), Oct. 30, 1972, 86 Stat. 1484, provided that this section was repealed effective Jan. 1, 1974, except with respect to Puerto Rico, Guam, and the Virgin Islands.
