# 42 U.S.C. § 1396r–7 - Repealed. Pub. L. 105–33, title IV, § 4713(a), Aug. 5, 1997, 111 Stat. 509

## Notes

Section, act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XIX, § 1926, as added Dec. 19, 1989, Pub. L. 101–239, title VI, § 6402(b), 103 Stat. 2260, related to adequate payment levels for obstetrical and pediatric services.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealPub. L. 105–33, title IV, § 4713(b), Aug. 5, 1997, 111 Stat. 509, provided that: “The repeal made by subsection (a) [repealing this section] shall apply to services furnished on or after October 1, 1997.”
