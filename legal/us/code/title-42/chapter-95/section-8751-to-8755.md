# 42 U.S.C. § 8751 to 8755 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections 8751 to 8755 were omitted from the Code in view of termination of United States Synthetic Fuels Corporation. See note set out under section 8791 of this title. Section 8751, Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 151, June 30, 1980, 94 Stat. 667, related to issue by Corporation of obligations purchasable by United States only. Section 8752, Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 152, June 30, 1980, 94 Stat. 668, related to limitations on total amount of obligational authority. Section 8753, Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 153, June 30, 1980, 94 Stat. 669, related to budgetary treatment. Section 8754, Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 154, June 30, 1980, 94 Stat. 669, related to receipts of Corporation. Section 8755, Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 155, June 30, 1980, 94 Stat. 669, related to tax status of Corporation.
