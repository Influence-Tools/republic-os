# 42 U.S.C. § 5714–1 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 5714–1 was editorially reclassified as section 11221 of Title 34, Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Study of Housing Services and StrategiesPub. L. 108–96, title I, § 119, Oct. 10, 2003, 117 Stat. 1170, provided that the Secretary of Health and Human Services was to conduct a study of programs funded under part B of the Runaway and Homeless Youth Act (42 U.S.C. 5714–1 et seq.) to report on long-term housing outcomes for youth after exiting the program.
