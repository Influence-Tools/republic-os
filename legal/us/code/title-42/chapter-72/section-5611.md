# 42 U.S.C. § 5611 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 5611 was editorially reclassified as section 11111 of Title 34, Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Education and Outreach to Trafficking SurvivorsPub. L. 114–22, title I, § 119, May 29, 2015, 129 Stat. 247, which required the Attorney General to make available, on the website of the Office of Juvenile Justice and Delinquency Prevention, educational and outreach information for trafficking victim advocates, crisis hotline personnel, foster parents, law enforcement personnel, and crime survivors, was editorially reclassified as section 20710 of Title 34, Crime Control and Law Enforcement.
