# 42 U.S.C. § 6508 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 96–514, title I, Dec. 12, 1980, 94 Stat. 2964, as amended, which related to competitive leasing of oil and gas, was renumbered section 107 of Pub. L. 94–258, the Naval Petroleum Reserves Production Act of 1976, by Pub. L. 109–58, title III, § 347(a)(2), August 8, 2005, 119 Stat. 704, and is classified to section 6506a of this title.
