# 42 U.S.C. § 2464a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 99–190, § 101(b) [title VIII, § 8111], Dec. 19, 1985, 99 Stat. 1185, 1222, which related to payloads launched on Titan II launch vehicles and cost effectiveness as against space shuttle launches in fiscal year 1986, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 51, National and Commercial Space Programs, by Pub. L. 111–314.
