# 42 U.S.C. § 2459a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 102–588, title II, § 202, Nov. 4, 1992, 106 Stat. 5112, which related to availability of appropriated amounts for fiscal year 1993, was omitted from the Code following enactment of Title 51, National and Commercial Space Programs, by Pub. L. 111–314.
