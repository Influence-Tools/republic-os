# 42 U.S.C. § 12311 - Establishment of Administration on Children, Youth, and Families

## Text

(a) In general There is established within the Department of Health and Human Services an Administration on Children, Youth, and Families.

(b) Commissioner (1) Establishment (A) In general The Administration on Children, Youth, and Families, as established under subsection (a), shall be headed by a Commissioner on Children, Youth, and Families.

(B) Omitted

(2) Appointment The President, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate, shall appoint the Commissioner.

(Pub. L. 101–501, title IX, § 915, Nov. 3, 1990, 104 Stat. 1263.)
