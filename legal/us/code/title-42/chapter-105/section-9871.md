# 42 U.S.C. § 9871 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

For the purpose of making allotments to States to carry out the activities described in section 9874 of this title, there is authorized to be appropriated $13,000,000 for fiscal year 1995.

(Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 670A, as added Pub. L. 98–558, title I, § 109, Oct. 30, 1984, 98 Stat. 2880; amended Pub. L. 99–425, title III, § 301, Sept. 30, 1986, 100 Stat. 967; Pub. L. 101–501, title III, § 301, Nov. 3, 1990, 104 Stat. 1248; Pub. L. 103–252, title I, § 122, May 18, 1994, 108 Stat. 650.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Amendments1994—Pub. L. 103–252 substituted “is authorized to be appropriated $13,000,000 for fiscal year 1995” for “are authorized to be appropriated $20,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1990 and 1991, and such sums as may be necessary for fiscal years 1992, 1993, and 1994”. 1990—Pub. L. 101–501 substituted “are authorized” for “is authorized”, struck out “1987, 1988, 1989, and” before “1990”, and inserted “and 1991, and such sums as may be necessary for fiscal years 1992, 1993, and 1994” after “1990”. 1986—Pub. L. 99–425 amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section read as follows: “For the purpose of allotments to States to carry out the activities described in section 9874 of this title, there are authorized to be appropriated $20,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1985 and 1986.”
