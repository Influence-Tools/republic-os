# 42 U.S.C. § 71m - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Apr. 9, 1930, ch. 125, § 10(a), 46 Stat. 152, which provided for repeal of limitation upon number of senior surgeons and Assistant Surgeons General at large of Public Health Service on active duty, was executed to section 34 of this title.
