# 42 U.S.C. § 10604 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 10604 was editorially reclassified as section 20110 of Title 34, Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Reports on Amounts Received and Distributed From Fines for Violations of Trade Secrets ProvisionsPub. L. 104–294, title I, § 101(c), Oct. 11, 1996, 110 Stat. 3491, required the Attorney General to report to Congress no later than 2 years and 4 years after Oct. 11, 1996, on fines for offenses under chapter 90 of title 18 deposited in the Crime Victims Fund.
