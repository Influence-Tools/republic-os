# 42 U.S.C. § 18117 - Oversight

## Text

The Inspector General of the Department of Health and Human Services shall have oversight authority with respect to the administration and implementation of this title 11 See References in Text note below. as it relates to such Department.

(Pub. L. 111–148, title I, § 1559, Mar. 23, 2010, 124 Stat. 261.)
