# 42 U.S.C. § 2921 to 2923 - Repealed. Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 683(a), Aug. 13, 1981, 95 Stat. 519

## Notes

Section 2921, Pub. L. 88–452, title V, § 501, Aug. 20, 1964, 78 Stat. 527; Pub. L. 89–794, title V, § 501(a), Nov. 8, 1966, 80 Stat. 1466; Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 107(d), Dec. 23, 1967, 81 Stat. 714; Pub. L. 93–644, § 8(a), Jan. 4, 1975, 88 Stat. 2300, set forth short title for this subchapter as “Headstart-Follow Through Act”.

Section 2922, Pub. L. 88–452, title V, § 502, Aug. 20, 1964, 78 Stat. 527; Pub. L. 89–253, § 25, Oct. 9, 1965, 79 Stat. 977; Pub. L. 89–794, title V, § 501(a), Nov. 8, 1966, 80 Stat. 1466; Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 107(d), Dec. 23, 1967, 81 Stat. 714; Pub. L. 93–644, § 8(a), Jan. 4, 1975, 88 Stat. 2300, set forth Congressional statement of purpose for Project Headstart.

Section 2923, Pub. L. 88–452, title V, § 503, Aug. 20, 1964, 78 Stat. 528; Pub. L. 89–253, § 30(d), Oct. 9, 1965, 79 Stat. 979; Pub. L. 89–794, title V, § 501(a), Nov. 8, 1966, 80 Stat. 1466; Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 107(d), Dec. 23, 1967, 81 Stat. 714; Pub. L. 93–644, § 8(a), Jan. 4, 1975, 88 Stat. 2300, set forth Congressional statement of policy with respect to migrant and Indian children.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see section 9912(a) of this title, prior to the general amendment of chapter 106 (§ 9901 et seq.) of this title by Pub. L. 105–285.
