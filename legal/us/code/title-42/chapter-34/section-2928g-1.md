# 42 U.S.C. § 2928g–1 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–424, § 3(b)(2), Sept. 19, 1972, 86 Stat. 688, required the Secretary of Health, Education, and Welfare to establish policies and procedures to assure that handicapped children received certain enrollment opportunities in Headstart programs and that services were provided to meet their special needs and to report annually to Congress on the status of handicapped children in the Headstart programs. See section 9835(d) of this title.
