# 42 U.S.C. § 18701 - Definitions

## Text

In this division:

(1) Department The term “Department” means the Department of Energy.

(2) Indian Tribe The term “Indian Tribe” has the meaning given the term in section 5304 of title 25.

(3) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Energy.

(Pub. L. 117–58, div. D, § 40001, Nov. 15, 2021, 135 Stat. 923.)
