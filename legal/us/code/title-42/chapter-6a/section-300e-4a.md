# 42 U.S.C. § 300e–4a - Repealed. Pub. L. 99–660, title VIII, § 805(a), Nov. 14, 1986, 100 Stat. 3800

## Notes

Section, act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XIII, § 1305A, as added Nov. 1, 1978, Pub. L. 95–559, § 5(a), 92 Stat. 2133; amended July 10, 1979, Pub. L. 96–32, § 2(e), 93 Stat. 82; Aug. 13, 1981, Pub. L. 97–35, title IX, § 944, 95 Stat. 576, related to loans and loan guarantees for acquisition and construction of ambulatory health care facilities.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal not applicable to any loan or loan guarantee made under this section before Oct. 1, 1985, see section 805(c) of Pub. L. 99–660, set out as an Effective Date of 1986 Amendment note under section 300e–5 of this title. Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1985, see section 815(a) of Pub. L. 99–660, set out as an Effective and Termination Dates of 1986 Amendment note under section 300e–1 of this title.
