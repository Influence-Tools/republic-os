# 42 U.S.C. § 300ee–6 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–607, title IX, § 902, Nov. 4, 1988, 102 Stat. 3171; Pub. L. 100–690, title II, § 2605(a), Nov. 18, 1988, 102 Stat. 4234, which provided for testing of State prisoners, was renumbered section 2648 of the Public Health Service Act by Pub. L. 101–381, title III, § 301(b)(1), Aug. 18, 1990, 104 Stat. 615, and transferred to section 300ff–48 of this title, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 106–345, title III, § 301(a), Oct. 20, 2000, 114 Stat. 1345.
