# 42 U.S.C. § 254c–15 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title III, § 330J, as added Pub. L. 107–251, title II, § 221, Oct. 26, 2002, 116 Stat. 1638; amended Pub. L. 115–334, title XII, § 12608, Dec. 20, 2018, 132 Stat. 5008; Pub. L. 118–84, § 2(1)–(5), Sept. 26, 2024, 138 Stat. 1544, which related to rural emergency medical service training and equipment assistance program, was renumbered section 553 of title V of act July 1, 1944, by Pub. L. 118–84, § 2(6), (7), Sept. 26, 2024, 138 Stat. 1544, 1545, and transferred to section 290ee–10 of this title.
