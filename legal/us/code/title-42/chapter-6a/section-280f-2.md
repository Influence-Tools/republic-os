# 42 U.S.C. § 280f–2 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part $12,500,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030.

(July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title III, § 399J, as added Pub. L. 119–44, title I, § 104(a), Dec. 1, 2025, 139 Stat. 673.)
