# 42 U.S.C. § 300q–1 - Repealed. Pub. L. 96–79, title II, § 203(a)(1), Oct. 4, 1979, 93 Stat. 635

## Notes

Section, act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XVI, § 1621, as added Jan. 4, 1975, Pub. L. 93–641, § 4, 88 Stat. 2265, related to allocation among States of total amount of principal, criteria, availability of unobligated amounts, and reallotments.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective Oct. 1, 1979, see section 204 of Pub. L. 96–79, set out as an Effective Date of 1979 Amendment note under section 300q of this title.
