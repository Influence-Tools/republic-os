# 42 U.S.C. § 300a–29 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 95–626, title III, § 301, Nov. 10, 1978, 92 Stat. 3590, provided that grants or contracts made under this subchapter would be considered to have been made under this chapter for the purposes of sections 300l–2(e) and 300m–3(c)(6) of this title.
