# 42 U.S.C. § 300x–29 - Repealed. Pub. L. 114–255, div. B, title VIII, § 8002(f), Dec. 13, 2016, 130 Stat. 1230

## Notes

Section, act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XIX, § 1929, as added Pub. L. 102–321, title II, § 202, July 10, 1992, 106 Stat. 396, related to submission to Secretary of statewide assessment of needs.

A prior section 1929 of act July 1, 1944, was classified to section 300y–8 of this title prior to repeal by Pub. L. 99–280.
