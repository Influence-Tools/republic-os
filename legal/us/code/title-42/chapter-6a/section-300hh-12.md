## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 107–188, title I, § 121, June 12, 2002, 116 Stat. 611, as amended, which related to Strategic National Stockpile, was renumbered section 319F–2 of the Public Health Service Act by Pub. L. 108–276, § 3(a)(1), July 21, 2004, 118 Stat. 842 and is classified to section 247d–6b of this title.
