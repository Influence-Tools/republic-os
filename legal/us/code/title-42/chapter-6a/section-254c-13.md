# 42 U.S.C. § 254c–13 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

For the purpose of carrying out sections 254c–9 to 254c–13 of this title, there are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 2001 through 2003.

(Pub. L. 106–505, title V, § 525, Nov. 13, 2000, 114 Stat. 2345.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was enacted as part of the Lupus Research and Care Amendments of 2000, and also as part of the Public Health Improvement Act, and not as part of the Public Health Service Act which comprises this chapter.
