# 42 U.S.C. § 285e–11 - Repealed. Pub. L. 109–482, title I, § 103(b)(26), Jan. 15, 2007, 120 Stat. 3688

## Notes

Section, act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title IV, § 445J, formerly § 445I, as added Pub. L. 103–43, title VIII, § 803, June 10, 1993, 107 Stat. 163; renumbered § 445J, Pub. L. 106–505, title VIII, § 801(1), Nov. 13, 2000, 114 Stat. 2349, authorized appropriations to carry out this subpart.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal applicable only with respect to amounts appropriated for fiscal year 2007 or subsequent fiscal years, see section 109 of Pub. L. 109–482, set out as an Effective Date of 2007 Amendment note under section 281 of this title.
