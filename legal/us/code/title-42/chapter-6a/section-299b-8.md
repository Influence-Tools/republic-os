# 42 U.S.C. § 299b–8 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 111–5, div. A, title VIII, § 804, Feb. 17, 2009, 123 Stat. 187, established the Federal Coordinating Council for Comparative Effectiveness Research.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Termination of Federal Coordinating Council for Comparative Effectiveness ResearchPub. L. 111–148, title VI, § 6302, Mar. 23, 2010, 124 Stat. 747, provided that the Federal Coordinating Council for Comparative Effectiveness Research established under this section terminated on Mar. 23, 2010.
