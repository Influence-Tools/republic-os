# 42 U.S.C. § 290bb–1a - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V, § 512, as added Oct. 19, 1984, Pub. L. 98–509, title II, § 206(a), 98 Stat. 2361, and amended, which related to alcohol abuse and alcoholism demonstration projects, was renumbered section 506 of act July 1, 1944, by Pub. L. 102–321, title I, § 106(a), July 10, 1992, 106 Stat. 334, and transferred to section 290aa–5 of this title.
