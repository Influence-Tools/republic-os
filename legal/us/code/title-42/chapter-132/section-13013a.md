# 42 U.S.C. § 13013a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–647, title II, § 218, as added Pub. L. 109–162, title I, § 112(d)(2), Jan. 5, 2006, 119 Stat. 2986, which required the Inspector General of the Department of Justice to submit a report to Congress not later than Dec. 31, 2006, on activities funded by the National Court-Appointed Special Advocate Association, was omitted as obsolete.

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 218 of Pub. L. 101–647 was renumbered section 219 and is classified to section 20324 of Title 34, Crime Control and Law Enforcement.
