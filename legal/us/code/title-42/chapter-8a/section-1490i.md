# 42 U.S.C. § 1490i - Repealed. Pub. L. 98–181, title I [title V, § 506(b)], Nov. 30, 1983, 97 Stat. 1242

## Notes

Section, act July 15, 1949, ch. 338, title V, § 529, as added Nov. 9, 1978, Pub. L. 95–619, title II, § 252(b), 92 Stat. 3236, required the Secretary of Agriculture to promote the use of energy saving techniques through the establishment of minimum property standards for newly constructed residential housing.
