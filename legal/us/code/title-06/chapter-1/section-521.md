# 6 U.S.C. § 521 - Legal status of EOIR

## Text

(a) 11 So in original. No subsec. (b) has been enacted. Existence of EOIR There is in the Department of Justice the Executive Office for Immigration Review, which shall be subject to the direction and regulation of the Attorney General under section 1103(g) of title 8.

(Pub. L. 107–296, title XI, § 1101, Nov. 25, 2002, 116 Stat. 2273.)
