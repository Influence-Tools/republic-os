# 5 U.S.C. § 5115 - Regulations

## Text

The Office of Personnel Management may prescribe regulations necessary for the administration of this chapter, except sections 5109 and 5114.11 See References in Text note below.

(Pub. L. 89–554, Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 457; Pub. L. 95–454, title IX, § 906(a)(2), Oct. 13, 1978, 92 Stat. 1224.)
