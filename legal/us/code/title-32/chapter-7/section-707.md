# 32 U.S.C. § 707 - Use of public buildings for offices by instructors

## Text

Whenever practicable, instructors of the National Guard shall use State armories or other public buildings for offices.

(Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, 70A Stat. 614.)

## Notes

Historical and Revision Notes Revised sectionSource (U.S. Code)Source (Statutes at Large) 70732:74.May 12, 1917, ch. 12 (10th proviso under “National Guard”), 40 Stat. 68.
