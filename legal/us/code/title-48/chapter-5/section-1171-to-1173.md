# 48 U.S.C. § 1171 to 1173 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections 1171 to 1173 were omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence. Section 1171, act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 28, 39 Stat. 555, related to granting of franchises and rights and compensation for property taken or damaged. Section 1172, act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 28, 39 Stat. 555, related to involuntary servitude and penalties therefor. Section 1173, act July 1, 1902, ch. 1369, § 75, 32 Stat. 709, related to corporation engaged in real estate business.
