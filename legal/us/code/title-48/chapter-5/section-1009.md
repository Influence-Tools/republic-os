# 48 U.S.C. § 1009 - Repealed. Oct. 31, 1951, ch. 655, § 56(d), 65 Stat. 729

## Notes

Section, act Mar. 8, 1902, ch. 140, § 9, 32 Stat. 55, related to requirements as to evidence in treason cases in Philippines.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Savings ProvisionAct Oct. 31, 1951, ch. 655, § 56(l), 65 Stat. 730, provided that the repeal of this section should not affect any rights or liabilities existing hereunder on the effective date of that repeal (Oct. 31, 1951).
