# 48 U.S.C. § 1241 to 1243 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections 1241 to 1243 were omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence. Section 1241, acts Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11, § 11, 47 Stat. 769; Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 11; 48 Stat. 463, related to neutralization of the Philippines. Section 1242, acts Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11, § 12, 47 Stat. 769; Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 12, 48 Stat. 463, related to notification to foreign governments of Philippine Independence. Section 1243, acts Jan. 17, 1933, ch. 11, § 13, 47 Stat. 769; Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 13, 48 Stat. 464; Aug. 7, 1939, ch. 502, § 4, 53 Stat. 1231; June 29, 1944, ch. 323, § 1, 58 Stat. 626; Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244, title V, § 511(3), 60 Stat. 158, related to establishment of Filipino Rehabilitation Commission.
