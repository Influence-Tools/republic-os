# 48 U.S.C. § 1632 - Repealed. Pub. L. 90–496, § 14, Aug. 23, 1968, 82 Stat. 842

## Notes

Section, act July 22, 1954, ch. 558, § 19, 68 Stat. 505, authorized Comptroller General of United States to review annually the office and activities of Government Comptroller of Virgin Islands, and report thereon to Governor, Secretary of the Interior, and Congress.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealPub. L. 90–496, § 14, Aug. 23, 1968, 82 Stat. 842, provided that the repeal of this section is effective on the date of enactment of Pub. L. 90–496, which was approved Aug. 23, 1968.
