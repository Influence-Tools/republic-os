# 48 U.S.C. § 1356 - Repealed. June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 35, 62 Stat. 991

## Notes

Section, acts Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 390, § 9, 37 Stat. 565; Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 370, § 3, 42 Stat. 1006; Feb. 16, 1933, ch. 91, § 3, 47 Stat. 817, related to appeals from district courts. See sections 1291, 1292, and 1294 of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding section 1 of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
