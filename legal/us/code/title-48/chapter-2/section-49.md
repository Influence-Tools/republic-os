# 48 U.S.C. § 49 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Jan. 12, 1927, ch. 27, § 1, 44 Stat. 968, which provided for admission to hospitals in the Territory of Alaska was omitted in view of the admission of Alaska into the Union.
