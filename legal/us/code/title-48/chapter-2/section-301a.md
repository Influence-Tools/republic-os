# 48 U.S.C. § 301a - Repealed. Pub. L. 97–468, title VI, § 615(a)(2), Jan. 14, 1983, 96 Stat. 2578

## Notes

Section, act June 24, 1946, ch. 465, 60 Stat. 304, provided that funds available for operation of Alaska Railroad were available for other specified purposes.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal by Pub. L. 97–468 effective on date of transfer of Alaska Railroad to the State [Jan. 5, 1985], pursuant to section 1203 of Title 45, Railroads, see section 615(b) of Pub. L. 97–468.
