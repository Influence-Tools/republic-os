# 48 U.S.C. § 61 to 64 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections 61 to 64, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union. Section 61, acts June 6, 1900, ch. 786, § 2, 31 Stat. 321; May 29, 1928, ch. 901, § 1(109), 45 Stat. 994; July 25, 1947, ch. 332, § 1, 61 Stat. 459, authorized appointment of a Governor for Territory, and detailed his duties. Section 62, acts June 6, 1900, ch. 786, § 10, 31 Stat. 325; Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462, 43 Stat. 1181; Mar. 4, 1931, ch. 516, 46 Stat. 1530; June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 13, 62 Stat. 987; Oct. 15, 1949, ch. 695, § 5(a), 63 Stat. 880, related to appointment of Governor and his compensation. Acts Mar. 3, 1925, Mar. 4, 1931, and Oct. 15, 1949, were repealed by Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a), Sept. 6, 1966, 80 Stat. 646, 648, 655. Section 63, acts June 6, 1900, ch. 786, § 10, 31 Stat. 325; June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 13, 62 Stat. 987, related to expenses of Governor. Section 64, act June 6, 1900, ch. 786, § 2, 31 Stat. 321, directed Governor to make an annual report to President, and empowered the President to confirm or annul acts of Governor.
