# 48 U.S.C. § 131 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act May 7, 1906, ch. 2083, § 1, 34 Stat. 169, which provided for a Delegate in the House of Representatives of the United States and prescribed his qualifications, was omitted in view of the admission of Alaska into the Union.
