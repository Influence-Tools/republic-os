# 48 U.S.C. § 50b to 50d–1 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections 50b to 50d–1, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union. Section 50b, act Mar. 7, 1928, ch. 137, § 1, 45 Stat. 239, authorized Secretary of the Interior to accept donations for school, medical, and reindeer service. Section 50c, act May 14, 1930, ch. 273, § 1, 46 Stat. 321, related to availability of appropriations for education, medical relief, and reindeer. Section 50d, act May 9, 1938, ch. 187, § 1, 52 Stat. 311, empowered Secretary of the Interior to authorize officers to incur obligations for benefit of natives prior to appropriation. Section 50d–1, act June 1, 1944, ch. 220, § 1, 58 Stat. 266, empowered Secretary to authorize officers to incur obligations for benefit of natives in excess of current appropriations.
