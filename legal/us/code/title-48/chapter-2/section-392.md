# 48 U.S.C. § 392 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act May 14, 1898, ch. 299, § 13, 30 Stat. 415, which provided for reciprocity with Canada as to mining rights, was omitted in view of the admission of Alaska into the Union.
