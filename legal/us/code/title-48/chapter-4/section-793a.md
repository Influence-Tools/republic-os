# 48 U.S.C. § 793a - Repealed. June 30, 1954, ch. 428, § 1, 68 Stat. 336

## Notes

Section, act Mar. 2, 1934, ch. 37, § 4, 48 Stat. 361, created a Model Housing Board, and provided for construction and sale of model houses and for creation of a revolving “model housing fund”.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Disposition of Moneys in Revolving FundAct June 30, 1954, ch. 428, § 2, 68 Stat. 336, authorized transfer of any moneys remaining in revolving model housing fund under this section to treasury of Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.
