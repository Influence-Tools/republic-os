# 34 U.S.C. § 50106 - Prohibition of land acquisition

## Text

No funds provided under this chapter shall be used for land acquisition.

(Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 609R, Oct. 12, 1984, 98 Stat. 2106.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 10506 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
