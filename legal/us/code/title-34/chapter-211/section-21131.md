# 34 U.S.C. § 21131 - Repealed. Pub. L. 119–60, div. H, title LXXXII, § 8202(h), Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1844

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 110–401, title II, § 201, Oct. 13, 2008, 122 Stat. 4241, provided for the establishment of additional computer forensic capacity to address the current backlog for computer forensics.

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 17631 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
