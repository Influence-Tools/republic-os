# 34 U.S.C. § 10234 - Prohibition on use of Central Intelligence Agency services

## Text

Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, no use will be made of services, facilities, or personnel of the Central Intelligence Agency.

(Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 815, formerly § 822, as added Pub. L. 96–157, § 2, Dec. 27, 1979, 93 Stat. 1214; renumbered § 815, Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 609B(f), Oct. 12, 1984, 98 Stat. 2093.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 3789k of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 815 of Pub. L. 90–351 was renumbered section 809 and is classified to section 10228 of this title.
