# 34 U.S.C. § 10233 - Prohibition on land acquisition

## Text

No funds under this chapter shall be used for land acquisition.

(Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 814, formerly § 821, as added Pub. L. 96–157, § 2, Dec. 27, 1979, 93 Stat. 1214; renumbered § 814, Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 609B(f), Oct. 12, 1984, 98 Stat. 2093.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 3789j of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 814 of Pub. L. 90–351 was classified to section 3789c of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to repeal by section 609B(e) of Pub. L. 98–473.
