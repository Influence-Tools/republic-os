# 34 U.S.C. § 12212 - Prohibition

## Text

None of the funds authorized under this part shall be used to finance the construction of housing.

(Pub. L. 103–322, title III, § 31133, Sept. 13, 1994, 108 Stat. 1888.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 13853 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
