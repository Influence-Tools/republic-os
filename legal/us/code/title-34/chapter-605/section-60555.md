# 34 U.S.C. § 60555 - Authorization of appropriations for research

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated to the Attorney General to carry out sections 60551, 60552, and 60553 of this title, $5,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, and 2023.

(Pub. L. 110–199, title II, § 245, Apr. 9, 2008, 122 Stat. 692; Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 504(e), Dec. 21, 2018, 132 Stat. 5233.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 17555 of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Amendments2018—Pub. L. 115–391 substituted “and 60553 of this title, $5,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, and 2023” for “60553, and 60554 of this title, $10,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2009 and 2010”.
