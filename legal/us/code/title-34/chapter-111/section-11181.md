L. 101–204, title X, §§ 1001(e)(1), 1002, Dec. 7, 1989, 103 Stat. 1827; renumbered § 299 and amended Pub. L. 102–586, § 2(i)(1)(B), (j), Nov. 4, 1992, 106 Stat. 5006, 5016; Pub. L. 107–273, div.
