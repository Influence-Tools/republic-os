# 34 U.S.C. § 21307 - Allocation of increased formula grant funds

## Text

The Attorney General shall allocate an increase in the amount provided to a State under the covered formula grants under this chapter such that—

(1) 25 percent 11 So in original. Probably should be followed by “of”. the amount of the increase is provided under the program described in section 21301(1)(A) of this title; and

(2) 75 percent 1 the amount of the increase is provided under the program described in section 21301(1)(B) of this title.

(Pub. L. 114–22, title IV, § 408, May 29, 2015, 129 Stat. 258.)
