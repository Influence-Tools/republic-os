# 34 U.S.C. § 21306 - Period of increase

## Text

(a) In general The Attorney General shall provide an increase in the amount provided to a State under the covered formula grants under this chapter for a 2-year period.

(b) Limit The Attorney General may not provide an increase in the amount provided to a State under the covered formula grants under this chapter more than 4 times.

(Pub. L. 114–22, title IV, § 407, May 29, 2015, 129 Stat. 257.)
