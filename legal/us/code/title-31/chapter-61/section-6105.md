# 31 U.S.C. § 6105 - Oversight responsibility of Director

## Text

The Director shall have oversight responsibility for the exercise of all authorities and responsibilities in this chapter not specifically assigned to the Director.

(Added Pub. L. 98–169, § 5, Nov. 29, 1983, 97 Stat. 1115.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 6105, Pub. L. 97–258, Sept. 13, 1982, 96 Stat. 1002, related to authorization of appropriations to carry out this chapter, prior to repeal by section 5 of Pub. L. 98–169. See section 6106 of this title.
