# 31 U.S.C. § 7506 - Effective date

## Text

This chapter shall apply to any non-Federal entity with respect to any of its fiscal years which begin after June 30, 1996.

(Added Pub. L. 98–502, § 2(a), Oct. 19, 1984, 98 Stat. 2334, § 7507; amended Pub. L. 104–156, § 2, July 5, 1996, 110 Stat. 1404; renumbered § 7506, Pub. L. 114–301, § 2(a)(1)(B), Dec. 16, 2016, 130 Stat. 1514.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 7506, added Pub. L. 98–502, § 2(a), Oct. 19, 1984, 98 Stat. 2333; amended Pub. L. 104–156, § 2, July 5, 1996, 110 Stat. 1403, related to the monitoring responsibilities of the Comptroller General, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 114–301, § 2(a)(1)(A), Dec. 16, 2016, 130 Stat. 1514.

Amendments2016—Pub. L. 114–301 renumbered section 7507 of this title as this section. 1996—Pub. L. 104–156 struck out “; report” after “Effective date” in section catchline and amended text generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “(a) This chapter shall apply to any State or local government with respect to any of its fiscal years which begin after December 31, 1984. “(b) The Director, on or before May 1, 1987, and annually thereafter, shall submit to each House of Congress a report on operations under this chapter. Each such report shall specifically identify each Federal agency or State or local government which is failing to comply with this chapter.”
