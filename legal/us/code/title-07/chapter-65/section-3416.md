# 7 U.S.C. § 3416 - Amendments to orders

## Text

The provisions of this chapter applicable to orders shall be applicable to amendments to orders.

(Pub. L. 95–113, title XVII, § 1717, Sept. 29, 1977, 91 Stat. 1040.)

## Notes

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective DateSection effective Oct. 1, 1977, see section 1901 of Pub. L. 95–113, set out as an Effective Date of 1977 Amendment note under section 1307 of this title.
