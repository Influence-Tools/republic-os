# 7 U.S.C. § 2008g - Payment of interest as condition of loan servicing for borrowers

## Text

The Secretary may not reschedule or reamortize a loan for a borrower under this chapter who has not requested consideration under section 1981d(e) of this title unless the borrower pays a portion, as determined by the Secretary, of the interest due on the loan.

(Pub. L. 87–128, title III, § 372, as added Pub. L. 104–127, title VI, § 648(a), Apr. 4, 1996, 110 Stat. 1104.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextThis chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title III of Pub. L. 87–128, Aug. 8, 1961, 75 Stat. 307, known as the Consolidated Farm and Rural Development Act, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of title III to the Code, see Short Title note set out under section 1921 of this title and Tables.
