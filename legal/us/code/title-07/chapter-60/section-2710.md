# 7 U.S.C. § 2710 - Applicability of provisions to amendments to orders

## Text

The provisions of this chapter applicable to orders shall be applicable to amendments to orders.

(Pub. L. 93–428, § 11, Oct. 1, 1974, 88 Stat. 1176.)
