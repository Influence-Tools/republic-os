# 7 U.S.C. § 1100 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 8, 1947, ch. 519, § 1, 61 Stat. 922, provided that this chapter may be cited as the Sugar Act of 1948, and expired on Dec. 31, 1974. A prior section, act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 898, § 1, 50 Stat. 903, provided that this chapter may be cited as the Sugar Act of 1937, and expired on Dec. 31, 1947.
