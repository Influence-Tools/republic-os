# 7 U.S.C. § 9201 - Definitions

## Text

In this chapter:

(1) Animal The term “animal” has the meaning given the term in section 8302 of this title.

(2) Transboundary disease The term “transboundary disease” has the meaning given the term in section 8914(a) of this title.

(3) Veterinary countermeasure The term “veterinary countermeasure” has the meaning given the term in section 8302 of this title.

(Pub. L. 116–260, div. P, § 2, Dec. 27, 2020, 134 Stat. 2159.)
