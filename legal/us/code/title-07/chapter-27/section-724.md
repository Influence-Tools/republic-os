# 7 U.S.C. § 724 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Apr. 21, 1934, ch. 157, § 24, 48 Stat. 607, authorized Secretary of Agriculture to develop new and extended uses for cotton, and made available not to exceed $500,000 out of funds available to Secretary under section 612 of this title.
