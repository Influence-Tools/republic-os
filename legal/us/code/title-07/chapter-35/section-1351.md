# 7 U.S.C. § 1351 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 351, 52 Stat. 60, set forth the legislative findings relating to rice marketing quotas pursuant to this subpart and was omitted in view of the repeal of the remaining sections of the subpart.
