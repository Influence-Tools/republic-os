# 7 U.S.C. § 1337 - Repealed. Pub. L. 87–703, title III, § 317, Sept. 27, 1962, 76 Stat. 622

## Notes

Section, act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 337, 52 Stat. 55, related to adjustment and suspension of quotas.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective only with respect to programs applicable to crops planted for harvest in calendar year 1964 or any subsequent year and marketing years beginning in calendar year 1964, or any subsequent year, see section 323 of Pub. L. 87–703, set out as a note under section 1301 of this title.
