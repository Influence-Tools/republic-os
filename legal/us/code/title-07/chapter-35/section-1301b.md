# 7 U.S.C. § 1301b - Repealed. Pub. L. 85–835, title I, § 108, Aug. 28, 1958, 72 Stat. 993

## Notes

Section, act Aug. 29, 1949, ch. 518, § 3(a), 63 Stat. 676, prescribed standard cotton grade for parity and price support purposes.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealPub. L. 85–835, title I, § 108, Aug. 28, 1958, 72 Stat. 993, provided in part that: “This section [amending section 1423 of this title and repealing this section] shall become effective with the 1961 crop.”
