# 7 U.S.C. § 15 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 369, § 6(c) (part), formerly § 6(b), 42 Stat. 1002, as amended and renumbered, which related to enforcement powers of Commission, was omitted in the general amendment of section 6(c) of act Sept. 21, 1922, by Pub. L. 111–203, title VII, § 753(a), July 21, 2010, 124 Stat. 1750. Section 6(c) is now classified to section 9 of this title.
