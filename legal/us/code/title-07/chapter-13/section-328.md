# 7 U.S.C. § 328 - Power to amend, repeal, etc., reserved

## Text

Congress may at any time amend, suspend, or repeal any or all of the provisions of this subchapter.

(Aug. 30, 1890, ch. 841, § 6, 26 Stat. 419.)
