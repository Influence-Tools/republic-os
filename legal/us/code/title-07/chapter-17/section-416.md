# 7 U.S.C. § 416 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, from act May 11, 1922, ch. 185, 42 Stat. 517, known as the Agriculture Department Appropriation Act, 1923, authorized awarding a contract for up to five years for distribution of plants, and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.
