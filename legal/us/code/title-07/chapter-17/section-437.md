# 7 U.S.C. § 437 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Apr. 21, 1948, ch. 224, § 2, 62 Stat. 197, authorized Secretary of Agriculture to receive the property transferred by former section 436 of this title and directed the Secretary to administer it to best advance the livestock and agricultural interests of the United States, and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Authorization of Appropriations; Abolition of Army Remount ProgramAct Apr. 21, 1948, ch. 224, § 4, 62 Stat. 198, authorized appropriations to carry out former sections 436 to 438 of this title and abolished authority of Department of the Army to conduct a remount breeding program.
