# 7 U.S.C. § 421a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 3, 1926, ch. 769, § 2, 44 Stat. 840, appropriated $25,000 to effectuate the purposes of former section 421 of this title, and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.
