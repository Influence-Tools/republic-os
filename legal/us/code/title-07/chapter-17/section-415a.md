# 7 U.S.C. § 415a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, from act June 16, 1938, ch. 464, title I, 52 Stat. 739, known as the Department of Agriculture Appropriation Act, 1939, related to sale of practical forms of grades of wool and mohair. See section 2247a of this title. Similar provisions were contained in the following prior appropriation acts: June 29, 1937, ch. 404, title I, 50 Stat. 424. June 4, 1936, ch. 489, title I, 49 Stat. 1450. May 17, 1935, ch. 131, title I, 49 Stat. 275. Mar. 26, 1934, ch. 89, 48 Stat. 493. Mar. 3, 1933, ch. 203, 47 Stat. 1458. July 7, 1932, ch. 443, 47 Stat. 636. Feb. 23, 1931, ch. 278, 46 Stat. 1268. May 27, 1930, ch. 341, 46 Stat. 418. Feb. 16, 1929, ch. 227, 45 Stat. 1212. May 16, 1928, ch. 572, 45 Stat. 561. Jan. 18, 1927, ch. 39, 44 Stat. 997.
