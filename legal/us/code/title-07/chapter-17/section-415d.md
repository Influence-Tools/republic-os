# 7 U.S.C. § 415d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act May 17, 1928, ch. 602, § 3, 45 Stat. 594, related to rules and regulations for and deposit of receipts under former sections 415b to 415d of this title, and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.
