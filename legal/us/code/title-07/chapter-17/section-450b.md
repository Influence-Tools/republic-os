# 7 U.S.C. § 450b - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 450b was editorially reclassified as section 2279i of this title.

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 450b, Pub. L. 89–106, § 2, Aug. 4, 1965, 79 Stat. 431, which related to research grants, duration, records, and audit, was transferred to section 450i of this title, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as section 3157 of this title.
