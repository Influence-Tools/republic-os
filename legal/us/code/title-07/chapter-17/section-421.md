# 7 U.S.C. § 421 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 3, 1926, ch. 769, § 1, 44 Stat. 840, directed Secretary of Agriculture to establish a dairying and livestock experiment station in Mandan, North Dakota, and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.
