# 7 U.S.C. § 439a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, July 2, 1948, ch. 818, § 2, 62 Stat. 1234, authorized Secretary of Agriculture to operate plants under former sections 439 to 439e of this title, and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.
