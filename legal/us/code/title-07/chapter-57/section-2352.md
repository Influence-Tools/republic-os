# 7 U.S.C. § 2352 - Form of papers filed

## Text

The Secretary may by regulations prescribe the form of papers to be filed in the Plant Variety Protection Office.

(Pub. L. 91–577, title I, § 22, Dec. 24, 1970, 84 Stat. 1544.)
