# 7 U.S.C. § 551 to 568 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections 551 to 568 of this title, which were comprised of act June 10, 1930, ch. 436, §§ 1–18, 46 Stat. 531, as amended, known as the Perishable Agricultural Commodities Act, 1930, were transferred to sections 499a to 499r of chapter 20A of this title.
