# 7 U.S.C. § 1445b–5 - Repealed. Pub. L. 101–624, title XI, § 1161(a)(2), Nov. 28, 1990, 104 Stat. 3520

## Notes

Section, act Oct. 31, 1949, ch. 792, title I, § 107F, as added Dec. 23, 1985, Pub. L. 99–198, title X, § 1006, 99 Stat. 1448, related to export certificate programs for 1986 through 1990 crops of wheat and feed grains.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective beginning with 1991 crop of an agricultural commodity, with provision for prior crops, see section 1171 of Pub. L. 101–624, set out as an Effective Date of 1990 Amendment note under section 1421 of this title.
