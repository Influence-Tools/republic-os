# 7 U.S.C. § 1433d - Omitted

## Notes

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective and Termination DatesPub. L. 101–239, title I, § 1003(a), Dec. 19, 1989, 103 Stat. 2108, provided that this section is effective only for the 1990 crops.
