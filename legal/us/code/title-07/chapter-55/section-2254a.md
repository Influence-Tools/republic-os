# 7 U.S.C. § 2254a - Availability of funds appropriated for Agricultural Research Service for research related to tobacco or tobacco products

## Text

On and after December 26, 2007, none of the funds appropriated under this heading shall be available to carry out research related to the production, processing, or marketing of tobacco or tobacco products.

(Pub. L. 110–161, div. A, title I, Dec. 26, 2007, 121 Stat. 1850.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextThis heading, referred to in text, refers to the headings “Agricultural Research Service” and “salaries and expenses” of title I of div. A of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2008, Pub. L. 110–161.
