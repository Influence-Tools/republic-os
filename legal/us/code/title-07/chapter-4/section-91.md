# 7 U.S.C. § 91 - Short title

## Text

For convenience of reference, this chapter may be designated and cited as “The Naval Stores Act.”

(Mar. 3, 1923, ch. 217, § 1, 42 Stat. 1435.)

## Notes

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective DateAct Mar. 3, 1923, ch. 217, § 10, 42 Stat. 1437, provided: “That this Act [enacting this chapter] shall become effective at the expiration of ninety days next after the date of its approval [Mar. 23, 1923].”
