# 7 U.S.C. § 504 - “Person” defined

## Text

The word “person” as used in this chapter shall be held to embrace also any partnership, corporation, association, or other legal entity.

(Jan. 14, 1929, ch. 69, § 4, 45 Stat. 1080.)
