# 26 U.S.C. § 5216 - Regulation of operations

## Text

For general provisions relating to operations on bonded premises see part I of this subchapter.

(Added Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201, Sept. 2, 1958, 72 Stat. 1364.)
