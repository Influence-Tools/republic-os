# 26 U.S.C. § 1044 - Repealed. Pub. L. 115–97, title I, § 13313(a), Dec. 22, 2017, 131 Stat. 2133]

## Notes

Section, added Pub. L. 103–66, title XIII, § 13114(a), Aug. 10, 1993, 107 Stat. 430; amended Pub. L. 104–188, title I, § 1703(a), Aug. 20, 1996, 110 Stat. 1875, related to rollover of publicly traded securities gain into specialized small business investment companies.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal applicable to sales after Dec. 31, 2017, see section 13313(c) of Pub. L. 115–97, set out as an Effective Date of 2017 Amendment note under section 1016 of this title.
