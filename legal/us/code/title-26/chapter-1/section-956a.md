# 26 U.S.C. § 956A - Repealed. Pub. L. 104–188, title I, § 1501(a)(2), Aug. 20, 1996, 110 Stat. 1825]

## Notes

Section, added Pub. L. 103–66, title XIII, § 13231(b), Aug. 10, 1993, 107 Stat. 496; amended Pub. L. 104–188, title I, § 1703(i)(2), (3), Aug. 20, 1996, 110 Stat. 1876, related to earnings invested in excess passive assets.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal by Pub. L. 104–188 applicable to taxable years of foreign corporations beginning after Dec. 31, 1996, and to taxable years of United States shareholders within which or with which such taxable years of foreign corporations end, see section 1501(d) of Pub. L. 104–188, set out as an Effective Date of 1996 Amendment note under section 904 of this title.
