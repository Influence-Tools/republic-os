# 26 U.S.C. § 373 - Repealed. Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1901(a)(52), Oct. 4, 1976, 90 Stat. 1773]

## Notes

Section, acts Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736, 68A Stat. 123; June 29, 1956, ch. 463, § 3, 70 Stat. 403, related to loss not recognized in certain railroad reorganizations.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective for taxable years beginning after Dec. 31, 1976, see section 1901(d) of Pub. L. 94–455, set out as an Effective Date of 1976 Amendment note under section 2 of this title.
