# 26 U.S.C. § 6312 - Repealed. Pub. L. 92–5, title I, § 4(a)(2), Mar. 17, 1971, 85 Stat. 5]

## Notes

Section, act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736, 68A Stat. 777, permitted the Secretary to receive Treasury bills, notes and certificates of indebtedness issued by the United States in payment of any internal revenue taxes or stamps.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealPub. L. 92–5, title I, § 4(a), Mar. 17, 1971, 85 Stat. 5, provided that the repeal of this section is effective with respect to obligations issued after Mar. 3, 1971.

RepealsPub. L. 92–5, title I, § 4(a)(2), Mar. 17, 1971, 85 Stat. 5, which repealed this section and provided for the effective date of that repeal, was itself repealed by Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b), Sept. 13, 1982, 96 Stat. 1068, 1081.
