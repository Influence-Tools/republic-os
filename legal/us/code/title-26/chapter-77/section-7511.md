# 26 U.S.C. § 7511 - Repealed. Pub. L. 87–456, title III, § 302(d), May 24, 1962, 76 Stat. 77]

## Notes

Section, act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736, 68A Stat. 900, related to exemption of consular officers and employees of foreign states from payment of internal revenue taxes on imported articles.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective with respect to articles entered, or withdrawn from warehouse, for consumption on or after Aug. 31, 1963, see section 501(a) of Pub. L. 87–456, title V, May 24, 1962, 76 Stat. 78.
