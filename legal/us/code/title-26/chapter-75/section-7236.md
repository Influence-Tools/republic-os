# 26 U.S.C. § 7236 - Repealed. Pub. L. 93–490, § 3(b)(1), Oct. 26, 1974, 88 Stat. 1466]

## Notes

Section, act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736, 68A Stat. 860, set out acts and penalties for violations of laws relating to filled cheese.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal applicable to filled cheese manufactured, imported, or sold after Oct. 26, 1974, see section 3(c) of Pub. L. 93–490, set out as a note under sections 4831 to 4834 of this title.
