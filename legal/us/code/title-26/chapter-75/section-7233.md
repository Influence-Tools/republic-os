# 26 U.S.C. § 7233 - Repealed. Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1952(n)(2)(A), Oct. 4, 1976, 90 Stat. 1846]

## Notes

Section, act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736, 68A Stat. 858, related to failure to pay, or attempt to evade payment of, tax on cotton futures, and other violations.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective on 90th day after Oct. 4, 1976, see section 1952(o) of Pub. L. 94–455, set out as an Effective Date note under section 15b of Title 7, Agriculture.
