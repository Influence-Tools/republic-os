# 26 U.S.C. § 6904 - Prohibition of injunctions

## Text

For prohibition of suits to restrain enforcement of liability of transferee, or fiduciary, see section 7421(b).

(Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736, 68A Stat. 843.)
