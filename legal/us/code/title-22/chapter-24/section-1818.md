## Notes

Section, acts Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937, title I, § 108, 68 Stat. 837; July 8, 1955, ch. 301, § 2(g), 69 Stat. 284, authorized, until June 30, 1956, the transfer of military equipment and supplies to Japan.
