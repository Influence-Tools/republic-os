# 22 U.S.C. § 1613 to 1613d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections, acts Oct. 26, 1951, ch. 575, title III, §§ 301–305, 65 Stat. 647; Sept. 4, 1961, Pub. L. 87–195, pt. IV, § 703(a), 75 Stat. 463; Oct. 19, 1980, Pub. L. 96–470, title I, § 111(a), 94 Stat. 2239, were superseded, effective October 1, 1979, by former section 4619(e) of Title 50, War and National Defense. Section 1613 set forth provisions relating to cooperation in the program by non-recipient countries. Section 1613a related to duties of the Administrator with respect to this chapter. Section 1613b related to applicability of other Federal statutory provisions and to availability of funds. Section 1613c related to charging expenses to local-currency funds. Section 1613d authorized appropriations necessary to carry out the objectives of this chapter.
