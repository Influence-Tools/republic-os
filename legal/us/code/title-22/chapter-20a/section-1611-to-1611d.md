# 22 U.S.C. § 1611 to 1611d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections, act Oct. 26, 1951, ch. 575, title I, §§ 101–105, 65 Stat. 645; H. Res. 163, Mar. 19, 1975, were superseded, effective October 1, 1979, by former section 4619(e) of Title 50, War and National Defense. Section 1611 set forth the Congressional declaration of policy. Section 1611a related to responsibility for administration. Section 1611b related to determinations respecting items to be embargoed. Section 1611c related to resumption of assistance upon determination of compliance by the President. Section 1611d defined “assistance” for purposes of this chapter.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitleAct Oct. 26, 1951, ch. 575, § 1, 65 Stat. 645, which provided that this chapter be cited as the “Mutual Defense Assistance Control Act of 1951”, was superseded, effective October 1, 1979, by former section 4619(e) of Title 50, War and National Defense.
