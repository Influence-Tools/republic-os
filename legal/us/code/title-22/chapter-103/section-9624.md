# 22 U.S.C. § 9624 - Termination

## Text

(a) In general The authorities provided under this subchapter terminate on December 31, 2031.

(b) Termination of Corporation The Corporation shall terminate on the date on which the portfolio of the Corporation is liquidated.

(Pub. L. 115–254, div. F, title II, § 1424, Oct. 5, 2018, 132 Stat. 3499; Pub. L. 119–60, div. H, title LXXXVII, § 8744, Dec. 18, 2025, 139 Stat. 1963.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Amendments2025—Subsec. (a). Pub. L. 119–60 substituted “December 31, 2031” for “7 years after October 5, 2018”.
