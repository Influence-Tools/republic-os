# 22 U.S.C. § 8006 - MTCR adherent status

## Text

Congress finds that India is not an MTCR adherent for the purposes of section 2797b of this title.

(Pub. L. 109–401, title I, § 107, Dec. 18, 2006, 120 Stat. 2738.)
