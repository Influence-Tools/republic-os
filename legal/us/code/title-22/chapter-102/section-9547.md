# 22 U.S.C. § 9547 - Termination

## Text

The provisions of this subchapter shall terminate on the date that is 5 years after August 2, 2017.

(Pub. L. 115–44, title II, § 258, Aug. 2, 2017, 131 Stat. 934.)
