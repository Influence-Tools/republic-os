## Notes

Section, act Jan. 27, 1948, ch. 36, title VII, § 704, as added Pub. L. 96–60, title II, § 204(e), Aug. 15, 1979, 93 Stat. 400; amended Pub. L. 97–241, title I, § 112(b), title III, § 303(b), Aug. 24, 1982, 96 Stat. 278, 291; Pub.
