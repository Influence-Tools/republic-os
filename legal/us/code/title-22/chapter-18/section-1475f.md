# 22 U.S.C. § 1475f - Repealed. Pub. L. 105–277, div. G, subdiv. A, title XIII, § 1336(1), Oct. 21, 1998, 112 Stat. 2681–790

## Notes

Section, act Jan. 27, 1948, ch. 36, title VIII, § 811, as added Pub. L. 101–246, title II, § 209, Feb. 16, 1990, 104 Stat. 53, related to recovery of indebtedness owed by person to United States arising out of activities of United States Information Agency.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective Oct. 1, 1999, see section 1301 of Pub. L. 105–277, set out as an Effective Date note under section 6531 of this title.
