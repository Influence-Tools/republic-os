# 22 U.S.C. § 8181 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this chapter.

(Pub. L. 109–401, title II, § 281, Dec. 18, 2006, 120 Stat. 2753.)
