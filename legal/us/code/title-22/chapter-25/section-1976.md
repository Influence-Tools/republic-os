# 22 U.S.C. § 1976 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such amounts as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

(Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1018, § 6, 68 Stat. 883.)
