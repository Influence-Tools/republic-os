# 22 U.S.C. § 1076a - Repealed. Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1), Oct. 17, 1980, 94 Stat. 2159

## Notes

Section, act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VIII, § 822, as added July 12, 1976, Pub. L. 94–350, title V, § 506, 90 Stat. 838, related to commencement, etc., of payment of annuity.

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465, set out as an Effective Date note under section 3901 of this title.
