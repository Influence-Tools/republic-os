# 22 U.S.C. § 968 - Repealed. Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1), Oct. 17, 1980, 94 Stat. 2159

## Notes

Section, act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V, § 578, as added Sept. 8, 1960, Pub. L. 86–723, § 16, 74 Stat. 834, related to foreign language knowledge as condition for assignment.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465, set out as an Effective Date note under section 3901 of this title.
