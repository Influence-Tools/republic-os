# 22 U.S.C. § 6434 - Applicability of other laws

## Text

Chapter 10 of title 5 shall not apply to the Commission.

(Pub. L. 105–292, title II, § 206, formerly § 204, Oct. 27, 1998, 112 Stat. 2799; renumbered § 206, Pub. L. 106–55, § 1(b)(2), Aug. 17, 1999, 113 Stat. 401; Pub. L. 117–286, § 4(a)(172), Dec. 27, 2022, 136 Stat. 4324.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 206 of Pub. L. 105–292 was renumbered section 209 and is classified to section 6436 of this title.

Amendments2022—Pub. L. 117–286 substituted “Chapter 10 of title 5” for “The Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.)”.
