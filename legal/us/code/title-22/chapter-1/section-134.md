# 22 U.S.C. § 134 - Repealed. Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title XI, § 1132, 60 Stat. 1040

## Notes

Section, act Mar. 2, 1921, ch. 113, 41 Stat. 1215, related to acceptance of gifts of lands, buildings, etc., for use of the Service.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective three months after Aug. 13, 1946, see act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title XI, § 1141, 60 Stat. 1040.
