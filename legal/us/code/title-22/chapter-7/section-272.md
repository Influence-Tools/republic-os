# 22 U.S.C. § 272 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act June 19, 1934, ch. 676, § 2, 48 Stat. 1183, provided that the President, in accepting membership in the International Labor Organization, was to assume no obligation under the covenant of the League of Nations.
