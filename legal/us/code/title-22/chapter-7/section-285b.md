# 22 U.S.C. § 285b - Coordination of policies and operations

## Text

The policies and operations of the representatives of the United States on the Bank shall be coordinated with other United States policies in such manner as the President shall direct.

(Pub. L. 89–369, § 4, Mar. 16, 1966, 80 Stat. 71; Pub. L. 101–240, title V, § 541(d)(2), (f)(3), Dec. 19, 1989, 103 Stat. 2518, 2519.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Amendments1989—Pub. L. 101–240 struck out subsec. (a) designation and struck out subsec. (b) which read as follows: “An annual report with respect to United States participation in the Bank shall be submitted to the Congress by such agency or officer as the President shall designate.”
