# 22 U.S.C. § 286r - United States participation in special drawing account

## Text

The provisions of article XXI(b) of the Articles of Agreement of the Fund shall have full force and effect in the United States and its territories and possessions when the United States becomes a participant in the special drawing account.

(Pub. L. 90–349, § 7, June 19, 1968, 82 Stat. 189; Pub. L. 94–564, § 5(4), Oct. 19, 1976, 90 Stat. 2661.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was not enacted as part of act July 31, 1945, ch. 339, 59 Stat. 512, known as the Bretton Woods Agreement Act, which comprises this subchapter.

Amendments1976—Pub. L. 94–564 substituted “article XXI(b)” for “article XXVII(b)”.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries
