# 22 U.S.C. § 5724 - Consultation with Congress

## Text

In carrying out this subchapter, the President shall consult appropriately with the Congress.

(Pub. L. 102–383, title II, § 204, Oct. 5, 1992, 106 Stat. 1453.)
