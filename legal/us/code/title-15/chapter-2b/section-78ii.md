# 15 U.S.C. § 78ii - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act June 6, 1934, ch. 404, title II, § 210, 48 Stat. 908, transferred the powers, duties and functions of the Federal Trade Commission under subchapter I of chapter 2A of this title to the Securities and Exchange Commission. Pending proceedings before the Federal Trade Commission were continued before the Securities and Exchange Commission.
