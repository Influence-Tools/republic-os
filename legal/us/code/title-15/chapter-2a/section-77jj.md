# 15 U.S.C. § 77jj - Loans to Corporation from Reconstruction Finance Corporation authorized

## Text

The Reconstruction Finance Corporation is authorized to loan out of its funds not to exceed $75,000 for the use of the Corporation.

(May 27, 1933, ch. 38, title II, § 209, 48 Stat. 95.)

## Notes

Executive Documents

Abolition of Reconstruction Finance Corporation Section 6(a) of Reorg. Plan No. 1 of 1957, eff. June 30, 1957, 22 F.R. 4633, 71 Stat. 647, set out as a note under section 601 of this title, abolished the Reconstruction Finance Corporation.
