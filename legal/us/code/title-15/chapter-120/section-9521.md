# 15 U.S.C. § 9521 - Definition

## Text

In this part, the term “MBDA Business Center Program” means the program established under section ___113. 11 So in original. See References in Text note below.

(Pub. L. 117–58, div. K, title I, § 100111, Nov. 15, 2021, 135 Stat. 1451.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextSection ___113, referred to in text, probably means section 100113 of Pub. L. 117–58, which is classified to section 9523 of this title.
