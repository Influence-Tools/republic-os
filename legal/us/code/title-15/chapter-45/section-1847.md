# 15 U.S.C. § 1847 - Maximum obligation

## Text

The maximum obligation of the Board under all outstanding loans guaranteed by it shall not exceed at any time $250,000,000.

(Pub. L. 92–70, § 8, Aug. 9, 1971, 85 Stat. 181.)
