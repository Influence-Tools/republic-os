# 15 U.S.C. § 197f - Repealed. Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title XI, § 1131(54), 60 Stat. 1039

## Notes

Section, act Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 365, § 7, 44 Stat. 1396, related to availability of appropriation during fiscal year ending June 30, 1926, and thereafter for the Department of Commerce for commercial attachés in Europe, South or Central America and elsewhere.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective three months from Aug. 13, 1946, see section 1141 of act Aug. 13, 1946.
