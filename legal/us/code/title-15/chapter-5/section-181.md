# 15 U.S.C. § 181 - Repealed. Pub. L. 87–826, § 3, Oct. 15, 1962, 76 Stat. 953

## Notes

Section, R.S. § 341; acts Aug. 23, 1912, ch. 350, § 1, 37 Stat. 407; Mar. 1, 1919, ch. 86, 40 Stat. 1256, required preparation of an annual statement of merchandise. See section 301 et seq. of Title 13, Census.

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective 180 days after Oct. 15, 1962, see section 4 of Pub. L. 87–826, set out as a note under section 301 of Title 13, Census.
