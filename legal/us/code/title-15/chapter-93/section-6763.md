# 15 U.S.C. § 6763 - Federal funding prohibited

## Text

The Association may not receive, accept, or borrow any amounts from the Federal Government to pay for, or reimburse,11 So in original. The comma probably should not appear. the Association for, the costs of establishing or operating the Association.

(Pub. L. 106–102, title III, § 333, as added Pub. L. 114–1, title II, § 202(a), Jan. 12, 2015, 129 Stat. 27.)
