# 15 U.S.C. § 6734 - Other provisions

## Text

(a) Judicial review The appropriate United States district court shall have exclusive jurisdiction over litigation arising under this section 11 See References in Text note below. involving any redomesticating or redomesticated insurer.

(b) Severability If any provision of this section,1 or the application thereof to any person or circumstances, is held invalid, the remainder of the section,1 and the application of such provision to other persons or circumstances, shall not be affected thereby.

(Pub. L. 106–102, title III, § 314, Nov. 12, 1999, 113 Stat. 1420.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextThis section, referred to in text, probably should be a reference to this subtitle, meaning subtitle B (§§ 311–316) of title III of Pub. L. 106–102, which is classified generally to this subchapter.
