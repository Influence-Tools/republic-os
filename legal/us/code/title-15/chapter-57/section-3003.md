# 15 U.S.C. § 3003 - Acceptance of interstate off-track wager

## Text

No person may accept an interstate off-track wager except as provided in this chapter.

(Pub. L. 95–515, § 4, Oct. 25, 1978, 92 Stat. 1813.)
