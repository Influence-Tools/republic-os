# 15 U.S.C. § 1242 - Introduction, manufacture for introduction, transportation or distribution in interstate commerce; penalty

## Text

Whoever knowingly introduces, or manufactures for introduction, into interstate commerce, or transports or distributes in interstate commerce, any switchblade knife, shall be fined not more than $2,000 or imprisoned not more than five years, or both.

(Pub. L. 85–623, § 2, Aug. 12, 1958, 72 Stat. 562.)

## Notes

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective DateSection effective on the sixtieth day after Aug. 12, 1958, see section 6 of Pub. L. 85–623, set out as a note under section 1241 of this title.
