# 29 U.S.C. § 1304a - Sponsor education and assistance

## Text

(a) Definition In this section, the term “CSEC plan” has the meaning given that term in subsection (f)(1) of section 1060 of this title.

(b) Education The Participant and Plan Sponsor Advocate established under section 1304 of this title shall make itself available to assist CSEC plan sponsors and participants as part of the duties it performs under the general supervision of the Board of Directors under section 1304(b) of this title.

(Pub. L. 113–97, title I, § 105, Apr. 7, 2014, 128 Stat. 1121.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was enacted as part of the Cooperative and Small Employer Charity Pension Flexibility Act, and not as part of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 which comprises this chapter.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries
