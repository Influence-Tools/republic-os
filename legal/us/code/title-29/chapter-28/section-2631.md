# 29 U.S.C. § 2631 - Establishment

## Text

There is established a commission to be known as the Commission on Leave (referred to in this subchapter as the “Commission”).

(Pub. L. 103–3, title III, § 301, Feb. 5, 1993, 107 Stat. 23.)
