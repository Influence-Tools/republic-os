# 29 U.S.C. § 2636 - Termination

## Text

The Commission shall terminate 30 days after the date of the submission of the report of the Commission to Congress.

(Pub. L. 103–3, title III, § 306, Feb. 5, 1993, 107 Stat. 25.)
