# 29 U.S.C. § 2634 - Compensation

## Text

(a) Pay Members of the Commission shall serve without compensation.

(b) Travel expenses Members of the Commission shall be allowed reasonable travel expenses, including a per diem allowance, in accordance with section 5703 of title 5 when performing duties of the Commission.

(Pub. L. 103–3, title III, § 304, Feb. 5, 1993, 107 Stat. 25.)
