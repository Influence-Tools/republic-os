# 29 U.S.C. § 1792 to 1792b - Repealed. Pub. L. 105–220, title I, § 199(b)(2), Aug. 7, 1998, 112 Stat. 1059

## Notes

Section 1792, Pub. L. 97–300, title VII, § 701, as added Pub. L. 102–367, title VI, § 601(a), Sept. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 1099; amended Pub. L. 104–193, title I, § 110(n)(17), Aug. 22, 1996, 110 Stat. 2175, related to establishment and functions of State human resource investment councils.

Section 1792a, Pub. L. 97–300, title VII, § 702, as added Pub. L. 102–367, title VI, § 601(a), Sept. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 1101, related to composition of Councils.

Section 1792b, Pub. L. 97–300, title VII, § 703, as added Pub. L. 102–367, title VI, § 601(a), Sept. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 1102, related to administration of Councils.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries
