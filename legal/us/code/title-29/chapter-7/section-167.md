# 29 U.S.C. § 167 - Short title of subchapter

## Text

This subchapter may be cited as the “National Labor Relations Act”.

(July 5, 1935, ch. 372, § 17, as added June 23, 1947, ch. 120, title I, § 101, 61 Stat. 152.)
