# 46 U.S.C. § 44106 - Conduct of proceedings

## Text

Part A of this subtitle applies to proceedings conducted by the Federal Maritime Commission under this chapter.

(Pub. L. 109–304, § 7, Oct. 6, 2006, 120 Stat. 1555.)
