# 46 U.S.C. § 110 - Foreign vessel

## Text

In this title, the term “foreign vessel” means a vessel of foreign registry or operated under the authority of a foreign country.

(Pub. L. 109–304, § 4, Oct. 6, 2006, 120 Stat. 1487.)
