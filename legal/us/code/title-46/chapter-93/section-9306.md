# 46 U.S.C. § 9306 - State regulation prohibited

## Text

A State or political subdivision of a State may not regulate or impose any requirement on pilotage on the Great Lakes.

(Pub. L. 98–89, Aug. 26, 1983, 97 Stat. 559.)

## Notes

Historical and Revision Notes Revised sectionSource section (U.S. Code) 930646:216g Section 9306 prohibits State or local regulations of pilotage on the Great Lakes and is part of the exception provided by law envisioned under section 8501(a).
