# 46 U.S.C. § 60305 - Vessels in distress

## Text

A vessel is exempt from tonnage taxes and light money when it enters because it is in distress.

(Pub. L. 109–304, § 9(b), Oct. 6, 2006, 120 Stat. 1678.)
