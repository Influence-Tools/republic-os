# 18 U.S.C. § 3736 - Certiorari—(Rule)

## Text

See Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure Petition to Supreme Court, time, Rule 37(b).

(June 25, 1948, ch. 645, 62 Stat. 845.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextRule 37 of the Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure was abrogated Dec. 4, 1967, eff. July 1, 1968. Provisions of such former rule for certiorari are covered by rule 19 et seq. of the Rules of the United States Supreme Court.
