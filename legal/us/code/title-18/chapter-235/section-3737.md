# 18 U.S.C. § 3737 - Record—(Rule)

## Text

See Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure Preparation, form; typewritten record, Rule 39(b). Exceptions abolished, Rule 51. Bill of exceptions unnecessary, Rule 37(a)(1).

(June 25, 1948, ch. 645, 62 Stat. 846.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextRules 37 and 39 of the Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure were abrogated Dec. 4, 1967, eff. July 1, 1968, and are covered by Rule 10, Federal Rules of Appellate Procedure, set out in the Appendix to Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
