# 18 U.S.C. § 3733 - Assignment of errors—(Rule)

## Text

See Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure Assignments of error on appeal abolished, Rule 37(a)(1). Necessity of specific objection in order to assign error in instructions, Rule 30.

(June 25, 1948, ch. 645, 62 Stat. 845.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextRule 37 of the Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure was abrogated Dec. 4, 1947, eff. July 1, 1968, and is covered by Rule 3, Federal Rules of Appellate Procedure, set out in the Appendix to Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
