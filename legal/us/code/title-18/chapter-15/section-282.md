# 18 U.S.C. § 282 - Repealed. Pub. L. 87–849, § 2, Oct. 23, 1962, 76 Stat. 1126]

## Notes

Section, act June 25, 1948, ch. 645, 62 Stat. 697, related to practice in Court of Claims by Members of Congress. Section was supplanted by section 204 of this title.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective 90 days after Oct. 23, 1962, see section 4 of Pub. L. 87–849, set out as an Effective Date note under section 201 of this title.
