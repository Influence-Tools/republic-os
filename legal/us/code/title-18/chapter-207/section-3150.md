# 18 U.S.C. § 3150 - Applicability to a case removed from a State court

## Text

The provisions of this chapter apply to a criminal case removed to a Federal court from a State court.

(Added Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 203(a), Oct. 12, 1984, 98 Stat. 1984.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 3150, added Pub. L. 89–465, § 3(a), June 22, 1966, 80 Stat. 216, related to penalties for failure to appear, prior to repeal in the revision of this chapter by section 203(a) of Pub. L. 98–473.
