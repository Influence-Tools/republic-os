# 8 U.S.C. § 1440a to 1440d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections, act June 30, 1953, ch. 162, §§ 1–4, 67 Stat. 108–110, which authorized naturalization of persons who served in the Armed Forces after June 29, 1950, and not later than July 1, 1955, were omitted as obsolete, since the provisions of section 1 of act June 30, 1953, required the petition for naturalization to be filed not later than December 31, 1955. See sections 1440 and 1440e of this title.
