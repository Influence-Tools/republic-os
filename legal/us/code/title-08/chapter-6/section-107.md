## Notes

Section, acts Aug. 18, 1894, ch. 301, § 1, 28 Stat 391; Aug. 1, 1914, ch. 223, § 1, 38 Stat. 666; June 5, 1920, ch. 235, § 1, 41 Stat. 936, provided for appointment of commissioners of immigration at the several ports.
