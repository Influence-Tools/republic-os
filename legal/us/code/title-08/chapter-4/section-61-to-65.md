# 8 U.S.C. § 61 to 65 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 61, R.S. 2032, related to continuation of laws then in force. Section 62, R.S. 2033, related to enforcement of laws by former Secretary of War. Section 63, acts Mar. 3, 1879, ch. 182, § 2, 20 Stat. 402; Feb. 1, 1888, ch. 4, § 1, 25 Stat. 9; July 1, 1898, ch. 546, 30 Stat. 640, related to claims for pay or bounty. Section 64, act July 1, 1902, ch. 1351, 32 Stat. 556, related to retained bounty fund. Section 65, R.S. § 2037, related to wives and children of colored soldiers.
