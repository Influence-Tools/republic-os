# 30 U.S.C. § 541f - Disbursement of moneys

## Text

All moneys received under the provisions of this chapter shall be paid into the Treasury of the United States and distributed in the same manner as provided in section 191 of this title and sections 437, 438, and 439 11 See References in Text note below. of title 48.

(Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 795, § 7, 69 Stat. 681.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextSections 437, 438, and 439 of title 48, referred to in text, were repealed by Pub. L. 86–252, § 1, Sept. 9, 1959, 73 Stat. 490.
