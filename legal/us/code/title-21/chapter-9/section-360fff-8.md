# 21 U.S.C. § 360fff–8 - Sunset

## Text

This part shall cease to be effective at the end of fiscal year 2022.

(June 25, 1938, ch. 675, § 586H, as added Pub. L. 116–136, div. A, title III, § 3854(b)(4), Mar. 27, 2020, 134 Stat. 456.)
