# 21 U.S.C. § 379c - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act June 25, 1938, ch. 675, § 711, as added Nov. 28, 1990, Pub. L. 101–635, title II, § 201, 104 Stat. 4584, which related to recovery and retention of fees for freedom of information requests, was renumbered section 731 of act June 25, 1938, by Pub. L. 102–571, title I, § 106(6), Oct. 29, 1992, 106 Stat. 4499, and transferred to section 379f of this title.
