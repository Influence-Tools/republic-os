# 13 U.S.C. § 14 - Repealed. Pub. L. 89–473, § 2(a), June 29, 1966, 80 Stat. 221]

## Notes

Section, added Pub. L. 87–489, § 1(a), June 19, 1962, 76 Stat. 104, provided for reimbursement between appropriations. See section 1534 of Title 31, Money and Finance.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

RepealsPub. L. 89–473, June 29, 1966, 80 Stat. 221, which repealed this section and struck out item 14 in the analysis of sections comprising this chapter, was itself repealed by Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b), Sept. 13, 1982, 96 Stat. 1068.
