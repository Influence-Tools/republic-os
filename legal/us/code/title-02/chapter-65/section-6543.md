# 2 U.S.C. § 6543 - Designation of reporters

## Text

The reporters of debates in the office of the Secretary of the Senate are hereby designated the official reporters of debates of the Senate.

(Pub. L. 89–545, Aug. 27, 1966, 80 Stat. 354.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 126–2 of this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
