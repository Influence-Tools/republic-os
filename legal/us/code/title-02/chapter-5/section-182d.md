# 2 U.S.C. § 182d - Audits by Comptroller General

## Text

Each of the revolving funds established under sections 182a to 182d of this title shall be subject to audit by the Comptroller General at the Comptroller General’s discretion.

(Pub. L. 106–481, title I, § 104, Nov. 9, 2000, 114 Stat. 2190.)

## Notes

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective DateSection applicable with respect to fiscal year 2002 and each succeeding fiscal year, see section 105 of Pub. L. 106–481, set out as a note under section 182a of this title.
