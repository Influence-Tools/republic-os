# 2 U.S.C. § 134 - Incidental expenses of law library

## Text

The incidental expenses of the law library shall be paid out of the appropriations for the Library of Congress.

(R.S. § 83.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification R.S. § 83 derived from act July 14, 1832, ch. 221, § 3, 4 Stat. 579.
