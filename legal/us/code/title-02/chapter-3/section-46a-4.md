# 2 U.S.C. § 46a–4 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 91–145, Dec. 12, 1969, 83 Stat. 342, made section 46a–3 of this title applicable to President of Senate, and was omitted from the Code in view of the repeal of section 46a–3.
