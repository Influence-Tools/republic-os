# 2 U.S.C. § 88c - Repealed. Pub. L. 91–510, title IV, § 491(e), Oct. 26, 1970, 84 Stat. 1198

## Notes

Section, acts June 14, 1948, ch. 467, 62 Stat. 426; Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 485, 65 Stat. 390; Oct. 13, 1964, Pub. L. 88–652, § 16(b), 78 Stat. 1084, provided for compensation of pages of Senate and House.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective immediately prior to noon on Jan. 3, 1971, see section 601(1) of Pub. L. 91–510, set out as an Effective Date of 1970 Amendment note under section 4301 of this title.
