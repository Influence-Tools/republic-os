# 2 U.S.C. § 72a–1c - Repealed. Pub. L. 95–26, title I, § 106(f), May 4, 1977, 91 Stat. 84

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 94–59, title I, § 108, July 25, 1975, 89 Stat. 276; Pub. L. 94–440, title I, § 102, Oct. 1, 1976, 90 Stat. 1443, authorized Senators to hire staff assistance in connection with their committee memberships. See section 4332 of this title.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealPub. L. 95–26, title I, § 106(g)(1), May 4, 1977, 91 Stat. 84, which provided that the repeal is effective Mar. 1, 1977, was repealed by Pub. L. 95–94, title I, § 111(e)(1), Aug. 5, 1977, 91 Stat. 663.
