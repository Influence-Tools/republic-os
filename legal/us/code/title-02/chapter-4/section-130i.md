# 2 U.S.C. § 130i - Repealed. Pub. L. 112–74, div. G, title I, § 105(1), Dec. 23, 2011, 125 Stat. 1123

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 107–117, div. B, § 905, Jan. 10, 2002, 115 Stat. 2318, established House of Representatives Office of Emergency Planning, Preparedness, and Operations.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Transfer of House Emergency Planning, Preparedness, and Operations Functions to Sergeant at ArmsPub. L. 112–74, div. G, title I, § 105, Dec. 23, 2011, 125 Stat. 1123, provided that: “Effective February 1, 2010— “(1) section 905 of the Emergency Supplemental Act, 2002 (2 U.S.C. 130i) is repealed; and “(2) the functions and responsibilities of the Office of Emergency Planning, Preparedness and Operations under section 905 of such Act are transferred and assigned to the Sergeant at Arms of the House of Representatives.”
