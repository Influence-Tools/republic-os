# 2 U.S.C. § 69a - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 69a was editorially reclassified as section 6514 of this title.

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 69a, Pub. L. 95–94, title I, § 105, Aug. 5, 1977, 91 Stat. 661, provided for expenditure of $1,000 during any fiscal year to conduct orientation seminars for new Senators and their staffs, prior to repeal, effective July 1, 1979, by Pub. L. 96–38, title I, § 107(b), July 25, 1979, 93 Stat. 113.
