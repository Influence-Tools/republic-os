# 2 U.S.C. § 4913 - Regulations of Page Board

## Text

The Page Board shall have authority to prescribe such regulations as may be necessary to carry out sections 4911 to 4913 of this title.

(Pub. L. 97–377, title I, § 127, Dec. 21, 1982, 96 Stat. 1914.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 88b–4 of this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section. Section is based on section 3 of House Resolution No. 611, Ninety-seventh Congress, Nov. 30, 1982, which was enacted into permanent law by Pub. L. 97–377.
