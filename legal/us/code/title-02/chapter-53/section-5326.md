# 2 U.S.C. § 5326 - “Member of the House” defined

## Text

As used in section 5324 of this title the phrase “Member of the House” shall mean a Representative, Representative-elect, Delegate, Delegate-elect, Resident Commissioner, or Resident Commissioner-elect.

(Aug. 21, 1935, ch. 600, § 3, 49 Stat. 680.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 92d of this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective DateSection effective Jan. 3, 1935, see section 4 of act Aug. 21, 1935, set out as a note under section 5324 of this title.
