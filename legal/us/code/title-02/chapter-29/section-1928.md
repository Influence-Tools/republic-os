# 2 U.S.C. § 1928 - Repealed. Pub. L. 111–145, § 2(a)(4)(B)(i), Mar. 4, 2010, 124 Stat. 50

## Notes

Section, R.S. § 1823; Mar. 3, 1921, ch. 124, § 1, 41 Stat. 1291, related to suspension of members of the force.

Codification Section was classified to section 208 of former Title 40, prior to the enactment of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works, by Pub. L. 107–217, § 1, Aug. 21, 2002, 116 Stat. 1062. R.S. § 1823 derived from acts Mar. 3, 1873, ch. 226, 17 Stat. 488; June 20, 1874, ch. 328, 18 Stat. 86; Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 129, 18 Stat. 345.
