# 2 U.S.C. § 1943 - Repealed. Pub. L. 111–145, § 6(b), Mar. 4, 2010, 124 Stat. 54

## Notes

Section, R.S. § 1825, required members of the Capitol police to pay for their own uniforms.

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was classified to section 211 of former Title 40, prior to the enactment of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works, by Pub. L. 107–217, § 1, Aug. 21, 2002, 116 Stat. 1062. R.S. § 1825 derived from act July 20, 1868, ch. 176, § 1, 15 Stat. 94.
