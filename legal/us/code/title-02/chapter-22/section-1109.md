# 2 U.S.C. § 1109 - Authorization for appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this chapter.

(Pub. L. 100–458, title I, § 120, Oct. 1, 1988, 102 Stat. 2176.)
