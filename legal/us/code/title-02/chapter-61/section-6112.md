# 2 U.S.C. § 6112 - Compensation of Deputy President pro tempore of Senate

## Text

Effective January 5, 1977, the compensation of a Deputy President pro tempore of the Senate shall be at a rate equal to the rate of annual compensation of the President pro tempore and the Majority and Minority Leaders of the Senate.

(Pub. L. 95–26, title I, May 4, 1977, 91 Stat. 79.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 32a of this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section. Section is from the Supplemental Appropriations Act, 1977.
