# 43 U.S.C. § 683 to 687 - Repealed. Dec. 16, 1930, ch. 14, § 1, 46 Stat. 1029

## Notes

Section 683, R.S. § 2364, related to minimum price for sale of public lands.

Section 684, R.S. § 2358, related to authorization of President to cause public lands to be offered for sale.

Section 685, R.S. § 2359, related to advertising of sale of public lands.

Section 686, act Jan. 12, 1877, ch. 18, § 2, 19 Stat. 221, related to publication of all executive proclamations relating to sale of public lands.

Section 687, R.S. § 2360, related to duration of sale of public lands.
