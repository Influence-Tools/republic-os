# 43 U.S.C. § 1200c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 6, 1954, ch. 463, § 6, 68 Stat. 453, which related to ratification of contracts under former sections 1198 to 1200e of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
