# 43 U.S.C. § 1200e - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 6, 1954, ch. 463, § 8, 68 Stat. 453, which authorized appropriations for relocation of the members of the Yankton Sioux Tribe, South Dakota, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
