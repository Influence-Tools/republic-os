# 43 U.S.C. § 1200 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 6, 1954, ch. 463, § 3, 68 Stat. 453, which related to determination of just compensation for property conveyed under former section 1199(a) of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
