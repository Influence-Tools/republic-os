# 43 U.S.C. § 1221 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 24, 1954, ch. 900, § 1, 68 Stat. 789, which related to sale of certain public lands in Wisconsin, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
