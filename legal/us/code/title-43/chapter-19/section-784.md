# 43 U.S.C. § 784 - Repealed. Pub. L. 85–56, title XXII, § 2202(1), June 17, 1957, 71 Stat. 162

## Notes

Section, R.S. § 471; act July 3, 1930, ch. 863, § 2, 46 Stat. 1016, required Administrator of Veterans’ Affairs to perform those duties in execution of bounty-land laws as the President prescribed.
