# 43 U.S.C. § 2409 - Termination of authority

## Text

The authority of the Secretary to carry out this subchapter terminates on September 30, 2016.

(Pub. L. 109–451, title I, § 110, Dec. 22, 2006, 120 Stat. 3356.)
