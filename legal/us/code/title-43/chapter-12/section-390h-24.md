# 43 U.S.C. § 390h–24 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 102–575, title XVI, § 1639, as added Pub. L. 110–229, title V, § 511(a), May 8, 2008, 122 Stat. 840, which related to the Eastern Municipal Water District recycled water system pressurization and expansion project, California, was omitted pursuant to subsec. (e) of section, which provided that the authority of the Secretary of the Interior to carry out any provisions of this section terminated 10 years after May 8, 2008.
