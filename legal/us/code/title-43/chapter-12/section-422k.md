# 43 U.S.C. § 422k - Supplement to Federal reclamation laws; short title

## Text

This subchapter shall be a supplement to the Federal reclamation laws and may be cited as the Small Reclamation Projects Act of 1956.

(Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 972, § 11, 70 Stat. 1047.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextThe Federal reclamation laws, referred to in text, are defined in section 422b of this title.
