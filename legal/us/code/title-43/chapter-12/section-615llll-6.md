# 43 U.S.C. § 615llll–6 - Repealed. Pub. L. 100–516, § 12(a), Oct. 24, 1988, 102 Stat. 2572

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 94–228, title IV, § 407, Mar. 11, 1976, 90 Stat. 209, authorized appropriations for Pollock-Herreid Unit. Section 12(a) of Pub. L. 100–516 provided in part that: “The Pollock-Herreid Unit shall remain an authorized feature of the Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin Program.”
