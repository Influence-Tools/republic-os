# 43 U.S.C. § 616t to 616w - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 616t, Pub. L. 87–706, § 1, Sept. 27, 1962, 76 Stat. 634, authorized construction, operation, and maintenance of Baker project, Oregon. Section 616u, Pub. L. 87–706, § 2, Sept. 27, 1962, 76 Stat. 634, related to extension of period of repayment of construction costs, excess lands and conditions for furnishing water to these lands, and computation of acreage. Section 616v, Pub. L. 87–706, § 3, Sept. 27, 1962, 76 Stat. 634, related to construction, operation, and maintenance of public recreational facilities and development of fish and wildlife in connection with this project and operation of this project for flood control. Section 616w, Pub. L. 87–706, § 4, Sept. 27, 1962, 76 Stat. 635, authorized appropriations for this project.
