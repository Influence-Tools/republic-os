# 43 U.S.C. § 390h–17a - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 102–575, title XVI, § 1636, as added Pub. L. 108–316, § 1(b), Oct. 5, 2004, 118 Stat. 1202, which related to the Williamson County, Texas, water recycling and reuse project, was renumbered section 1637 of Pub. L. 102–575 by Pub. L. 109–70, § 2(a)(1), Sept. 21, 2005, 119 Stat. 2009, and transferred to section 390h–19 of this title.
