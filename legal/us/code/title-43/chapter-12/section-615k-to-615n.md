# 43 U.S.C. § 615k to 615n - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 615k, act Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 981, § 1, 70 Stat. 1059, authorized construction, operation, and maintenance of Little Wood River Project, Idaho. Section 615l, act Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 981, § 2, 70 Stat. 1059, related to construction, operation, and maintenance of public recreational facilities in connection with this project. Section 615m, act Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 981, § 3, 70 Stat. 1059, related to preservation of fish and wildlife in connection with this project and the operation of this project in accordance with water rights. Section 615n, act Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 981, § 4, 70 Stat. 1060, related to authorization of appropriations for this project.
