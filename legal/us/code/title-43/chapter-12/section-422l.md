# 43 U.S.C. § 422l - Application of this subchapter to Hawaii

## Text

This subchapter as heretofore and hereafter amended, shall apply to the State of Hawaii.

(Pub. L. 86–624, § 31, July 12, 1960, 74 Stat. 421.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was enacted as a part of the Hawaii Omnibus Act, and not as a part of the Small Reclamation Projects Act of 1956 which comprises this subchapter.
