# 38 U.S.C. § 7114 - Internship program

## Text

The Secretary shall establish a competitive internship program of the Board for individuals enrolled in the first or second year of law schools accredited by the American Bar Association.

(Added Pub. L. 118–196, § 5(a), Dec. 23, 2024, 138 Stat. 2673.)

## Notes

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Deadline for Establishing Internship ProgramPub. L. 118–196, § 5(c), Dec. 23, 2024, 138 Stat. 2673, provided that: “The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall establish the internship program required by section 7114 of such title [meaning title 38, United States Code], as added by subsection (a), not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act [Dec. 23, 2024].”
