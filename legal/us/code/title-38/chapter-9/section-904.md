# 38 U.S.C. § 904 - Equipment and weapons

## Text

The Secretary shall furnish Department police officers with such weapons and related equipment as the Secretary determines to be necessary and appropriate.

(Added Pub. L. 102–83, § 2(a), Aug. 6, 1991, 105 Stat. 399.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Prior ProvisionsPrior section 904 was renumbered section 2304 of this title. Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in section 218(d) of this title prior to repeal by Pub. L. 102–83, § 2(a).
