# 25 U.S.C. § 141 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, R.S. § 2110, which related to issuance of army rations to Indians, was omitted on recommendation of Secretary of the Interior that this section be repealed because the practice of issuing army rations to Indians is no longer in use.
