# 25 U.S.C. § 112 - Persons present at delivery of goods and money

## Text

The superintendent, agent, or subagent, together with such military officer as the President may direct, shall be present, and certify to the delivery of all goods and money required to be paid or delivered to the Indians.

(R.S. § 2088.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification R.S. § 2088 derived from act June 30, 1834, ch. 162, § 13, 4 Stat. 737.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Indian AgentsThe services of Indian agents have been dispensed with. See note set out under section 64 of this title.
