# 25 U.S.C. § 5208 - Application of provisions to Osage County

## Text

This chapter shall not relate to or affect Osage County, Oklahoma.

(June 26, 1936, ch. 831, § 8, 49 Stat. 1968.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 508 of this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
