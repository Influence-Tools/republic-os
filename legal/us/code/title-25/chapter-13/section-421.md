# 25 U.S.C. § 421 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 421, act May 17, 1900, ch. 479, § 1, 31 Stat. 179, which provided for free homesteads to settlers, commutation rights, and payments to Indians, was transferred to section 179 of Title 43, Public Lands.
