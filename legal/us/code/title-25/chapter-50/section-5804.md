# 25 U.S.C. § 5804 - Regulations

## Text

Not later than 180 days after October 20, 2020, the Secretary shall promulgate regulations to implement the program.

(Pub. L. 116–174, § 5, Oct. 20, 2020, 134 Stat. 847.)
