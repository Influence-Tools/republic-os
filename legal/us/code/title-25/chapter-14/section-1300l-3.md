# 25 U.S.C. § 1300l–3 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–434, title II, § 205, Oct. 31, 1994, 108 Stat. 4534, which related to tribal membership rolls, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
