# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–5 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 6, Dec. 22, 1974, 88 Stat. 1714, which set out considerations and guidelines for preparation of report by mediator and final adjudication by District Court, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
