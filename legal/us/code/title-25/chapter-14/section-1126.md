# 25 U.S.C. § 1126 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–309, § 5, June 2, 1972, 86 Stat. 200, which directed that funds be distributed equally to the individuals enrolled, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
