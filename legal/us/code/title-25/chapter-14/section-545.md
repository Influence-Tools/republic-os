# 25 U.S.C. § 545 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Mar. 29, 1948, ch. 160, § 3, 62 Stat. 93, which related to liability of judgment funds for debts contracted prior to the passage of this section and former section 544 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
