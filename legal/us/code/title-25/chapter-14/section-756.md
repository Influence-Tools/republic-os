# 25 U.S.C. § 756 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 16, 68 Stat. 1103, which related to cancellation of debts of the tribe to the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
