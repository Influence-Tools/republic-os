# 25 U.S.C. § 610a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–534, § 2, Sept. 28, 1968, 82 Stat. 884, which authorized the sale or exchange of lands owned by or in trust for the Swinomish Indian Tribal Community and required that the land values involved in an exchange be equal or be equalized by the payment of money, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
