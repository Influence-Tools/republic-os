# 25 U.S.C. § 788d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–504, § 4, Sept. 21, 1968, 82 Stat. 855, which authorized the prescription of rules and regulations to carry out the provisions of former sections 788a to 788d of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
