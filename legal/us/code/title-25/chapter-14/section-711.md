# 25 U.S.C. § 711 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 95–195, § 2, Nov. 18, 1977, 91 Stat. 1415, which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 95–195, § 1, Nov. 18, 1977, 91 Stat. 1415, provided that Pub. L. 95–195, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Siletz Indian Tribe Restoration Act”.
