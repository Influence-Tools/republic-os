# 25 U.S.C. § 715f - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–42, § 8, June 28, 1989, 103 Stat. 93, which provided for governance of the Tribe by an Interim Council until the adoption of a new tribal constitution and bylaws, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
