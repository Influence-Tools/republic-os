# 25 U.S.C. § 967a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–717, § 1, Nov. 2, 1966, 80 Stat. 1114, which related to per capita distribution out of funds appropriated to pay a certain judgment to tribal members living on Nov. 2, 1966, and use of balance of funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
