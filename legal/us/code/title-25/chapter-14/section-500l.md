# 25 U.S.C. § 500l - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 13, 50 Stat. 902, which directed Secretary, whenever practicable, to appoint natives to administer the industry, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
