# 25 U.S.C. § 903b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–197, § 4, Dec. 22, 1973, 87 Stat. 771, which set out provisions relating to the Menominee Restoration Committee, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
