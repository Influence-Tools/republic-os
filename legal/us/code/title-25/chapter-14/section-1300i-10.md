# 25 U.S.C. § 1300i–10 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–580, § 11, Oct. 31, 1988, 102 Stat. 2935, which set out special considerations related to certain tribal lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
