# 25 U.S.C. § 715h - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 110–75, § 1, Aug. 13, 2007, 121 Stat. 724, which related to conveyances of land and interests after Jan. 1, 2007, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
