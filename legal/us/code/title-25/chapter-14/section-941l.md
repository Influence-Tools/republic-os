# 25 U.S.C. § 941l - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–116, § 14, Oct. 27, 1993, 107 Stat. 1136, which related to rights and responsibilities with respect to the conduct of games of chance, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
