# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–16 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 17, Dec. 22, 1974, 88 Stat. 1720, which related to title, possession, and enjoyment of lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
