# 25 U.S.C. § 660 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–507, § 2, Sept. 21, 1968, 82 Stat. 860, which provided for the 1964 appropriation for certain judgments to be distributed to tribe members in equal shares, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
