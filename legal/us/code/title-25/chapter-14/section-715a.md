# 25 U.S.C. § 715a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Pub. L. 101–42, § 3, June 28, 1989, 103 Stat. 91, which related to restoration of Federal recognition, rights, and privileges to the Coquille Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
