# 25 U.S.C. § 1300d–7 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–555, title III, § 303, Oct. 25, 1972, 86 Stat. 1170, which related to payments to minors and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
