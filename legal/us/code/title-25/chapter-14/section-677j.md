# 25 U.S.C. § 677j - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 11, 68 Stat. 873, which set out permitted uses of tribal funds and imposed restrictions on the use of tribal funds by the mixed-blood group until its adoption of a plan for termination of Federal supervision of the group, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
