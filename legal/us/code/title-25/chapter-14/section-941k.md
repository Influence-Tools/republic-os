# 25 U.S.C. § 941k - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–116, § 13, Oct. 27, 1993, 107 Stat. 1136, which related to acquisition or disposal of real estate outside the reservation by the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
