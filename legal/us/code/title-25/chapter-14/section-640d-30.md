# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–30 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 32, as added Pub. L. 100–666, § 7, Nov. 16, 1988, 102 Stat. 3932; amended Pub. L. 101–121, title I, § 120, Oct. 23, 1989, 103 Stat. 722, which related to the Navajo Rehabilitation Trust Fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application. Another section 32 of Pub. L. 93–531 was enacted by Pub. L. 100–696, title IV, § 407, Nov. 18, 1988, 102 Stat. 4593, and was classified to section 640d–31 of this title prior to omission from the Code.
