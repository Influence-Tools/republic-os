# 25 U.S.C. § 500e - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 6, 50 Stat. 900, which established a revolving fund for purposes of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
