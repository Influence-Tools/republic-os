# 25 U.S.C. § 1130 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–309, § 9, June 2, 1972, 86 Stat. 200, which authorized prescription of rules and regulations to carry out the provisions of former sections 1122 to 1130 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
