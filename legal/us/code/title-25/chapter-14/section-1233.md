# 25 U.S.C. § 1233 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 91–417, § 3, Sept. 25, 1970, 84 Stat. 868, which related to distribution of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
