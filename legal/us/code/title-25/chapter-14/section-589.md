# 25 U.S.C. § 589 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–206, § 9, Dec. 18, 1971, 85 Stat. 739, which exempted distributed funds from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
