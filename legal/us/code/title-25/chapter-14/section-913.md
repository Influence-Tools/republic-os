# 25 U.S.C. § 913 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 86–97, § 3, July 17, 1959, 73 Stat. 222, which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees, minors, and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
