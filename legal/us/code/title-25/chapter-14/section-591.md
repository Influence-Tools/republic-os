# 25 U.S.C. § 591 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act June 8, 1940, ch. 285, § 1, 54 Stat. 254, which related to reservation of Chippewa National Forest lands for the Minnesota Chippewa Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
