# 25 U.S.C. § 531 to 535 - Repealed. Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 12, 68 Stat. 721

## Notes

Sections 531 to 535, act Aug. 28, 1937, ch. 874, §§ 2–6, 50 Stat. 872, 873, related to revolving loan fund.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealAct Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 12, 68 Stat. 721, provided that the repeal is effective on the date of the transfer of title to tribal property to a trustee, corporation, or other legal entity pursuant to former section 564e of this title.
