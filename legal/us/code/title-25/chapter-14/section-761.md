# 25 U.S.C. § 761 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 96–227, § 2, Apr. 3, 1980, 94 Stat. 317; Pub. L. 109–126, § 4, Dec. 7, 2005, 119 Stat. 2547, which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 96–227, § 1, Apr. 3, 1980, 94 Stat. 317, provided that Pub. L. 96–227, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Paiute Indian Tribe of Utah Restoration Act”.
