# 25 U.S.C. § 677m - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 14, 68 Stat. 875, which related to procedure by Secretary of the Interior if distribution to individual mixed-blood members was not completed within seven years from Aug. 27, 1954, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
