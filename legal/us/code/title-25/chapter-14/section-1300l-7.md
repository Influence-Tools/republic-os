# 25 U.S.C. § 1300l–7 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–434, title II, § 209, Oct. 31, 1994, 108 Stat. 4536, which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to promulgate regulations to carry out the provisions of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
