# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–13 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 14, Dec. 22, 1974, 88 Stat. 1718; Pub. L. 100–666, § 4(b), Nov. 16, 1988, 102 Stat. 3930, which related to relocation of households and members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
