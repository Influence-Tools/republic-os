# 25 U.S.C. § 1034 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 88–457, § 4, Aug. 20, 1964, 78 Stat. 555, which provided for per capita distributions to members of the Cherokee Band upon completion of roll, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
