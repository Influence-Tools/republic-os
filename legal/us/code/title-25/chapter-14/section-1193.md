# 25 U.S.C. § 1193 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 91–259, § 3, May 21, 1970, 84 Stat. 254, which provided that judgment funds would remain tribal funds until distributed, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
