# 25 U.S.C. § 714c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 98–481, § 5, Oct. 17, 1984, 98 Stat. 2252, which related to governance of tribe by an Interim Council, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
