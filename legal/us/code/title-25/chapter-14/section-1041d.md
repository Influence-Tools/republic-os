# 25 U.S.C. § 1041d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 106–568, title VII, § 706, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2915, which recognized the constitution and bylaws and governing body as constituted on Dec. 27, 2000, as the governing documents and governing body of the tribe and provided the tribe with the right to reorganize its tribal government, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
