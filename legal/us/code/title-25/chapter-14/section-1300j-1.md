# 25 U.S.C. § 1300j–1 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–323, § 2, Sept. 21, 1994, 108 Stat. 2153, which affirmed Federal recognition of the Pokagon Band, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
