# 25 U.S.C. § 1035 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 88–457, § 5, Aug. 20, 1964, 78 Stat. 555, which related to distribution of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
