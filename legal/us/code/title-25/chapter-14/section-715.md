# 25 U.S.C. § 715 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–42, § 2, June 28, 1989, 103 Stat. 91, which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 101–42, § 1, June 28, 1989, 103 Stat. 91, provided that Pub. L. 101–42, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Coquille Restoration Act”.
