# 25 U.S.C. § 564q - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 18, 68 Stat. 722, which related to termination of Federal trust relationship to the affairs of the tribe and its members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
