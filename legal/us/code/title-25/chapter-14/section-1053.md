# 25 U.S.C. § 1053 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 88–506, § 3, Aug. 30, 1964, 78 Stat. 639, which exempted funds distributed in accordance with this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
