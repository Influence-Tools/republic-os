# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–15 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 16, Dec. 22, 1974, 88 Stat. 1720, which provided for payment of fair rental value for use of lands subsequent to date of partition, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
