# 25 U.S.C. § 573 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 27, 1939, ch. 387, § 3, 53 Stat. 1129, which appropriated from the judgment fund sums for the purchase of lands, the establishment of a loan fund, and the establishment and administration of productive enterprises, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
