# 25 U.S.C. § 941f - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–116, § 8, Oct. 27, 1993, 107 Stat. 1125, which related to transitional and provisional tribal government, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
