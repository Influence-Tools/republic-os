# 25 U.S.C. § 1252 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–253, § 2, Mar. 17, 1972, 86 Stat. 64, which exempted funds distributed to members of the tribes from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
