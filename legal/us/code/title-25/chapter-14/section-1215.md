# 25 U.S.C. § 1215 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–454, title II, § 205, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4793; Pub. L. 104–109, § 10, Feb. 12, 1996, 110 Stat. 765, which related to prohibition against duplicative services, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
