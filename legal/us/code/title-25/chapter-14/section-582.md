# 25 U.S.C. § 582 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–206, § 2, Dec. 18, 1971, 85 Stat. 737, which credited funds to the Shoshone-Bannock Tribes of the Fort Hall Reservation for certain claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
