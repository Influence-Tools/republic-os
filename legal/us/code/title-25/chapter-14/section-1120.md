# 25 U.S.C. § 1120 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–659, § 10, Oct. 14, 1966, 80 Stat. 910, which exempted distributed funds from Federal and State income taxes and provided for payment of certain costs, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
