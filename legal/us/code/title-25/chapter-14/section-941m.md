# 25 U.S.C. § 941m - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–116, § 15, Oct. 27, 1993, 107 Stat. 1136, which related to severability, applicability of other laws, and other general provisions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
