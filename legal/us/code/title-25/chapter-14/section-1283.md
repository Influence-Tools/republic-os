# 25 U.S.C. § 1283 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–438, § 3, Sept. 29, 1972, 86 Stat. 741, which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
