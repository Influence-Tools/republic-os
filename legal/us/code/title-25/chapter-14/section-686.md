# 25 U.S.C. § 686 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1011, § 3, 68 Stat. 879, which provided that payments made under former sections 684 to 686 of this title would not be considered “other income and resources”, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
