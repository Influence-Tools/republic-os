# 25 U.S.C. § 638 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Apr. 19, 1950, ch. 92, § 8, 64 Stat. 46, which related to participation by Tribal Councils in the administration of the program authorized by this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
