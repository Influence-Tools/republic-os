# 25 U.S.C. § 564g - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 8, 68 Stat. 720; Pub. L. 85–132, § 1(h), (i), Aug. 14, 1957, 71 Stat. 348; Pub. L. 85–731, § 11, Aug. 23, 1958, 72 Stat. 819, which directed the transfer of individual property of tribe members held in trust by the United States to such tribe members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
