# 25 U.S.C. § 563 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act May 29, 1953, ch. 86, § 1, 67 Stat. 40, which provided for payment of salaries and expenses for Klamath Tribe Officials out of tribal funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 563, acts June 25, 1938, ch. 710, 52 Stat. 1207; Aug. 7, 1939, ch. 519, 53 Stat. 1244; May 15, 1945, ch. 123, 59 Stat. 167, provided for payment of salaries and expenses of Klamath Tribe officials out of tribal funds but limited the amount of such expenditures to $15,000 per annum, prior to repeal by act May 29, 1953, ch. 86, § 2, 67 Stat. 40.
