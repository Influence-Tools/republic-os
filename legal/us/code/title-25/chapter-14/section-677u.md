# 25 U.S.C. § 677u - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 22, 68 Stat. 877, which related to protection of minors, persons non compos mentis, and other members needing assistance, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
