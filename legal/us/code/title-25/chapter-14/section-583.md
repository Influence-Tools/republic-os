# 25 U.S.C. § 583 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–206, § 3, Dec. 18, 1971, 85 Stat. 737, which credited funds to the Northwestern Bands of Shoshone Indians for certain claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
