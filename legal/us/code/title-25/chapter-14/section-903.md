# 25 U.S.C. § 903 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–197, § 2, Dec. 22, 1973, 87 Stat. 770, which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 93–197, § 1, Dec. 22, 1973, 87 Stat. 770, provided that Pub. L. 93–197, enacting this subchapter and repealing sections 891 to 902 of this title, could be cited as the “Menominee Restoration Act”.
