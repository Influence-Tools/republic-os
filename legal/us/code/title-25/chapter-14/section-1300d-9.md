# 25 U.S.C. § 1300d–9 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–555, title III, § 305, Oct. 25, 1972, 86 Stat. 1170, which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to prescribe rules and regulations to carry out the provisions of this part, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
