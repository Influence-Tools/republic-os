# 25 U.S.C. § 1300b–3 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–467, § 4, Oct. 6, 1972, 86 Stat. 781, which related to payments to minors and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
