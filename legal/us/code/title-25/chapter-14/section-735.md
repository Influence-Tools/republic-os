# 25 U.S.C. § 735 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–89, title II, § 205, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 671, which related to adoption of a new constitution and bylaws, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
