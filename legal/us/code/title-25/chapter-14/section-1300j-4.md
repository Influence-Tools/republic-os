# 25 U.S.C. § 1300j–4 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–323, § 5, Sept. 21, 1994, 108 Stat. 2154, which related to adoption of a constitution and bylaws and election of a governing body for the band, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
