# 25 U.S.C. § 1153 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–114, § 3, Oct. 24, 1967, 81 Stat. 335, which related to distribution of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disabilities, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
