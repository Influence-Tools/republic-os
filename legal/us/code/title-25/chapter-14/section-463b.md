# 25 U.S.C. § 463b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 28, 1937, ch. 527, § 2, 50 Stat. 536, which related to purchase of private lands for the use and benefit of the Papago Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
