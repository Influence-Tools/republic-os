# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–1 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 2, Dec. 22, 1974, 88 Stat. 1712, which related to negotiating teams for the Navajo and Hopi Tribes and negotations between the tribes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
