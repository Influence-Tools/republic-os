# 25 U.S.C. § 677i - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 10, 68 Stat. 873; Pub. L. 87–698, Sept. 25, 1962, 76 Stat. 597, which related to division of assets between full and mixed-blood groups, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
