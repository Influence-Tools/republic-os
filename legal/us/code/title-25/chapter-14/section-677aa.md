# 25 U.S.C. § 677aa - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 28, 68 Stat. 878, which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to proceed in any manner in any action upon non-agreement between mixed-blood and full-blood groups, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
