# 25 U.S.C. § 1300n - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 106–568, title XIV, § 1402, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2939, which set out congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 106–568, title XIV, § 1401, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2939, provided that title XIV of Pub. L. 106–568, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Graton Rancheria Restoration Act”.
