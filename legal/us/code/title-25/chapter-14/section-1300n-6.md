# 25 U.S.C. § 1300n–6 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 106–568, title XIV, § 1408, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2941, which related to ratification of a final constitution for the tribe and election of tribal officials, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
