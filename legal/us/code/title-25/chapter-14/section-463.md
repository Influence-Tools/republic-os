# 25 U.S.C. § 463 - Transferred

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section 463 was editorially reclassified as section 5103 of this title.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Restoration of Vacant and Undisposed-of Ceded Lands in Certain Indian ReservationsPub. L. 85–420, May 19, 1958, 72 Stat. 121, which provided that lands classified as vacant and undisposed-of ceded lands (including townsite lots) on the Indian reservations at Klamath River, California, Coeur d’Alene, Idaho, Crow, Montana, Fort Peck, Montana, and Spokane, Washington, would be restored to tribal ownership, subject to valid existing rights, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
