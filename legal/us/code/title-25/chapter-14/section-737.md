# 25 U.S.C. § 737 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–89, title II, § 207, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 672, which related to gaming activities on the reservation and on lands of the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
