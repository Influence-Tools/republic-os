# 25 U.S.C. § 992 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 87–775, § 2, Oct. 9, 1962, 76 Stat. 776, which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
