# 25 U.S.C. § 676b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 91–420, § 1, Sept. 25, 1970, 84 Stat. 871, which related to uses of unexpended balance of fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
