# 25 U.S.C. § 1300m–6 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–454, title III, § 308, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4795, which related to adoption of a tribal constitution and bylaws and election of tribal officials, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
