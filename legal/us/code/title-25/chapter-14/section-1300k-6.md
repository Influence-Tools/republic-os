# 25 U.S.C. § 1300k–6 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–324, § 8, Sept. 21, 1994, 108 Stat. 2159, which related to adoption of constitutions and election of governing bodies of the bands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
