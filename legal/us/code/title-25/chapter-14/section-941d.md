# 25 U.S.C. § 941d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–116, § 6, Oct. 27, 1993, 107 Stat. 1122, which related to ratification of prior transfers of land or natural resources and extinguishment of aboriginal title, rights, and claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
