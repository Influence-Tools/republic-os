# 25 U.S.C. § 1184 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–508, § 4, Sept. 21, 1968, 82 Stat. 862, which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees and enrollees who are less than twenty-one years of age or under a legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
