# 25 U.S.C. § 658 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act May 24, 1950, ch. 196, § 2, 64 Stat. 190, which provided for a distribution of $150 from the judgment fund to each enrolled Indian, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
