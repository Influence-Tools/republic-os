# 25 U.S.C. § 593 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act June 8, 1940, ch. 285, § 3, 54 Stat. 255, which authorized exchanges of allotted, restricted, and tribal lands for Chippewa National Forest lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
