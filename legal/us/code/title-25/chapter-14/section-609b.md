# 25 U.S.C. § 609b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 91–413, § 1, Sept. 25, 1970, 84 Stat. 865, which related to authorized uses of funds appropriated to pay certain judgments to the Yakima Tribes of the Yakima Reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
