# 25 U.S.C. § 1300c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–468, § 1, Oct. 6, 1972, 86 Stat. 782, which related to distribution of judgment funds and payment of attorney fees and expenses, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
