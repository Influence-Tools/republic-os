# 25 U.S.C. § 1300m–5 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–454, title III, § 307, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4795, which provided for governance of the tribe by an Interim Council until adoption of a new tribal constitution and bylaws, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
