# 25 U.S.C. § 1261 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–254, § 1, Mar. 18, 1972, 86 Stat. 64, which related to distribution of judgment funds and payment of attorney fees and expenses, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
