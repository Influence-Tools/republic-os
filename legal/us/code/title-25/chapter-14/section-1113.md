# 25 U.S.C. § 1113 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–659, § 3, Oct. 14, 1966, 80 Stat. 909, which related to membership roll of Miami Indians of Indiana, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
