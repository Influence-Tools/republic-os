# 25 U.S.C. § 883a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–586, § 2, Oct. 27, 1972, 86 Stat. 1295, which related to payments to members of the Osage Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
