# 25 U.S.C. § 622 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1949, ch. 425, § 2, 63 Stat. 605, which related to exchange of Pueblo and Navajo tribal lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
