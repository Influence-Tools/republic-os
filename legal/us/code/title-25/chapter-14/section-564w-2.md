# 25 U.S.C. § 564w–2 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 29, as added Pub. L. 93–102, Aug. 16, 1973, 87 Stat. 349, which related to Federal acquisition of tribal land, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
