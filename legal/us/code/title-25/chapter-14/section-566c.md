# 25 U.S.C. § 566c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 99–398, § 5, Aug. 27, 1986, 100 Stat. 850, which related to hunting, fishing, trapping, and water rights, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
