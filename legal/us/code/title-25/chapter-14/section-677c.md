# 25 U.S.C. § 677c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 4, 68 Stat. 868, which related to transfer of members from full-blood roll to mixed-blood group, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
