# 25 U.S.C. § 690 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 88–663, Oct. 13, 1964, 78 Stat. 1093, which related to distribution of judgment fund and tax exemption of per capita payments, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
