# 25 U.S.C. § 872 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 85–395, § 2, May 9, 1958, 72 Stat. 105, which authorized per capita distributions to tribal members from funds appropriated for a certain judgment against the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
