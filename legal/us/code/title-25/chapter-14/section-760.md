# 25 U.S.C. § 760 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 23, 68 Stat. 1104, which related to education and training program prior to the issuance of the proclamation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
