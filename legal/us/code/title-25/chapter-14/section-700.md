# 25 U.S.C. § 700 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 733, § 10, 68 Stat. 726, which related to transfer of property to minors, persons non compos mentis, and other members needing assistance, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
