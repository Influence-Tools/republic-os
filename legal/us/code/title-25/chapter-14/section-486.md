# 25 U.S.C. § 486 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 14, 1954, ch. 472, § 3, 68 Stat. 468, which authorized prescription of regulations to carry out sections 484 to 486 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
