# 25 U.S.C. § 590b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–442, § 2, Sept. 29, 1972, 86 Stat. 744, which related to disposition of funds credited to the Shoshone-Bannock Tribes of the Fort Hall Reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
