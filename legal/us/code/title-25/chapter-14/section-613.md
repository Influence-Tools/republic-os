# 25 U.S.C. § 613 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, acts May 19, 1947, ch. 80, § 3, 61 Stat. 102; Aug. 30, 1951, ch. 367, § 2, 65 Stat. 209; July 17, 1953, ch. 223, 67 Stat. 179; Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 638, 69 Stat. 557; July 25, 1956, ch. 723, § 1, 70 Stat. 642; Pub. L. 85–610, § 2, Aug. 8, 1958, 72 Stat. 541, which related to uses of trust funds and amount of per capita payments, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
