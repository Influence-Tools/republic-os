# 25 U.S.C. § 1102 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–656, § 2, Oct. 14, 1966, 80 Stat. 906, which related to distribution of funds to tribe members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
