# 25 U.S.C. § 754 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 14, 68 Stat. 1103, which related to advances or expenditures from tribal funds pending completion of property dispositions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
