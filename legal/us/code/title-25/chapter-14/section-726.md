# 25 U.S.C. § 726 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 23, 1954, ch. 831, § 6, 68 Stat. 769, which related to applicability of Federal and State laws on and after the date of the proclamation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
