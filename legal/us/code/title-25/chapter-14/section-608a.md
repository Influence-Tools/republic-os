# 25 U.S.C. § 608a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 28, 1955, ch. 423, § 2, 69 Stat. 393; Pub. L. 88–540, § 2, Aug. 31, 1964, 78 Stat. 748, which related to sale of tribal trust lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
