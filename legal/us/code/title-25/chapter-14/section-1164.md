# 25 U.S.C. § 1164 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–117, § 4, Oct. 31, 1967, 81 Stat. 338, which related to claims by living enrollees or by the heirs or legatees of deceased enrollees for per capita shares, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
