# 25 U.S.C. § 1300g–2 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–89, title I, § 103, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 667, which provided for restoration of Federal trust relationship between the United States and the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
