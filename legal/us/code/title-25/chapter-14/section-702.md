# 25 U.S.C. § 702 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 733, § 12, 68 Stat. 727, which related to execution of patents, deeds, and other instruments by the Secretary of the Interior, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
