# 25 U.S.C. § 500b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 3, 50 Stat. 900, which required filing of claims to title to reindeer by nonnatives, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
