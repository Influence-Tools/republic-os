# 25 U.S.C. § 997 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 87–775, § 7, Oct. 9, 1962, 76 Stat. 777, which provided for costs incident to making payments to be paid from the judgment fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
