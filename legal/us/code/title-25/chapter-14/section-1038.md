# 25 U.S.C. § 1038 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 88–457, § 8, Aug. 20, 1964, 78 Stat. 556, which authorized prescription of rules and regulations to carry out the provisions of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
