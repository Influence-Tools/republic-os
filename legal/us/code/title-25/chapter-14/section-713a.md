# 25 U.S.C. § 713a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 98–165, § 3, Nov. 22, 1983, 97 Stat. 1064, which related to consideration of Confederated Tribes of Grand Ronde Community as one tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
