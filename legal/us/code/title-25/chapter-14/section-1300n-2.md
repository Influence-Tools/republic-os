# 25 U.S.C. § 1300n–2 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 106–568, title XIV, § 1404, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2939, which provided for restoration of Federal recognition of the tribe and of all rights and privileges of the tribe and its members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
