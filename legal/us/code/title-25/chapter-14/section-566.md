# 25 U.S.C. § 566 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 99–398, § 2, Aug. 27, 1986, 100 Stat. 849; Pub. L. 99–514, § 2, Oct. 22, 1986, 100 Stat. 2095, which related to restoration of Federal recognition, rights, and privileges to the tribe and tribe members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 99–398, § 1, Aug. 27, 1986, 100 Stat. 849, provided that Pub. L. 99–398, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Klamath Indian Tribe Restoration Act”.
