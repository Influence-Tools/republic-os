# 25 U.S.C. § 1300h–7 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–420, § 9, Sept. 8, 1988, 102 Stat. 1579; Pub. L. 101–301, § 7, May 24, 1990, 104 Stat. 210, which related to constitutional amendment, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
