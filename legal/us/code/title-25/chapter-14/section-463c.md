# 25 U.S.C. § 463c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 28, 1937, ch. 527, § 3, 50 Stat. 536, which related to gift of lands by the State of Arizona to the Papago Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
