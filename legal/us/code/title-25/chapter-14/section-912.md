# 25 U.S.C. § 912 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 86–97, § 2, July 17, 1959, 73 Stat. 222, which related to per capita payments to tribal members from funds appropriated for a certain judgment against the United States and exemption of such payments from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
