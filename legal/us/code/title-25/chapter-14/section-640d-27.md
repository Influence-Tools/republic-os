# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–27 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 29, as added Pub. L. 96–305, § 11, July 8, 1980, 94 Stat. 934, which related to attorney fees, costs and expenses for litigation or court action, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
