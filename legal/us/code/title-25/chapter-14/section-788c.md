# 25 U.S.C. § 788c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–504, § 3, Sept. 21, 1968, 82 Stat. 855, which related to distribution of judgment funds to heirs of deceased enrollees, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
