# 25 U.S.C. § 724 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 23, 1954, ch. 831, § 4, 68 Stat. 769, which provided for cancellation of certain indebtedness effective on the date of the proclamation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
