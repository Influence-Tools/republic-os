# 25 U.S.C. § 609c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 95–433, § 1, Oct. 10, 1978, 92 Stat. 1047; Pub. L. 103–435, § 17(b), Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4573, which related to disbursement of minors’ shares of judgment funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
