# 25 U.S.C. § 564w–1 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 28, as added Pub. L. 85–731, § 1, Aug. 23, 1958, 72 Stat. 816; amended Pub. L. 86–247, Sept. 9, 1959, 73 Stat. 477; Pub. L. 105–312, title II, § 205, Oct. 30, 1998, 112 Stat. 2957; Pub. L. 105–321, § 4(e), Oct. 30, 1998, 112 Stat. 3025, which related to Klamath Indian Forest and Klamath Marsh, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
