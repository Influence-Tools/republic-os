# 25 U.S.C. § 712b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 97–391, § 4, Dec. 29, 1982, 96 Stat. 1961; Pub. L. 100–139, § 7(a), Oct. 26, 1987, 101 Stat. 828, which related to organization of tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
