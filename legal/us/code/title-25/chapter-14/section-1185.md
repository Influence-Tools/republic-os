# 25 U.S.C. § 1185 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–508, § 5, Sept. 21, 1968, 82 Stat. 862, which exempted funds distributed under the provisions of this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
