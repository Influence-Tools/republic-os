# 25 U.S.C. § 715d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–42, § 6, June 28, 1989, 103 Stat. 92, which related to criminal and civil jurisdiction within the reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
