# 25 U.S.C. § 983c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–484, § 5, Oct. 31, 1990, 104 Stat. 1168; Pub. L. 104–109, § 12, Feb. 12, 1996, 110 Stat. 765, which related to eligibility of the tribe and its members for Federal services and benefits, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
