# 25 U.S.C. § 640c–2 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–189, § 6, as added Pub. L. 96–374, title XIII, § 1351(c), Oct. 3, 1980, 94 Stat. 1501; amended Pub. L. 100–297, title V, § 5403(b), Apr. 28, 1988, 102 Stat. 416; Pub. L. 110–315, title IX, § 946(e), Aug. 14, 2008, 122 Stat. 3469, which related to effect of subchapter on other laws, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
