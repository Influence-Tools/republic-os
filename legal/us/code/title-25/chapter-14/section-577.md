# 25 U.S.C. § 577 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 27, 1939, ch. 387, § 7, 53 Stat. 1130, which related to liability of judgment funds for debts, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
