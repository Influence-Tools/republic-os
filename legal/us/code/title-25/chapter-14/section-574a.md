# 25 U.S.C. § 574a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–435, § 15, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4573, which related to acquisition of lands for the benefit of individual tribes on the Wind River Reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
