# 25 U.S.C. § 694 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 733, § 4, 68 Stat. 725, which related to personal property rights of each member upon publication of roll, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
