# 25 U.S.C. § 994 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 87–775, § 4, Oct. 9, 1962, 76 Stat. 776, which exempted funds distributed under this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
