# 25 U.S.C. § 1205 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 91–283, § 5, June 19, 1970, 84 Stat. 313, which provided for certain moneys withheld from per capita distribution to be credited to a joint account for expenditure for official salaries and expenses of Fort Peck Tribes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
