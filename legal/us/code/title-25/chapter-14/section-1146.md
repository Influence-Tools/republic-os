# 25 U.S.C. § 1146 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–93, § 6, Sept. 27, 1967, 81 Stat. 230, which exempted funds distributed per capita from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
