# 25 U.S.C. § 1300h - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–420, § 2, Sept. 8, 1988, 102 Stat. 1577, which set out congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 100–420, § 1, Sept. 8, 1988, 102 Stat. 1577, provided that Pub. L. 100–420, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Lac Vieux Desert Band of Lake Superior Chippewa Indians Act”.
