# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–24 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 25, Dec. 22, 1974, 88 Stat. 1722; Pub. L. 96–40, July 30, 1979, 93 Stat. 318; Pub. L. 96–305, § 10, July 8, 1980, 94 Stat. 933; Pub. L. 98–48, July 13, 1983, 97 Stat. 244; Pub. L. 100–666, §§ 2, 4(b), Nov. 16, 1988, 102 Stat. 3929, 3930; Pub. L. 102–180, § 2, Dec. 2, 1991, 105 Stat. 1230; Pub. L. 104–15, § 1, June 21, 1995, 109 Stat. 189; Pub. L. 104–301, § 10, Oct. 11, 1996, 110 Stat. 3652; Pub. L. 108–204, title I, § 102, Mar. 2, 2004, 118 Stat. 543, which authorized appropriations for certain purposes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
