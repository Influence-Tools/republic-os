# 25 U.S.C. § 953 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 86–339, § 3, Sept. 21, 1959, 73 Stat. 602; Pub. L. 105–308, § 4(a), Oct. 30, 1998, 112 Stat. 2934, which related to determination of value of unallotted and allotted lands and equalization of value of allotments and designated certain lands not subject to allotment as tribal reserves, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
