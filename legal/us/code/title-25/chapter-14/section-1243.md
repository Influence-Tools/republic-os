# 25 U.S.C. § 1243 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–59, § 3, July 29, 1971, 85 Stat. 158, which related to applications for enrollment in the band, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
