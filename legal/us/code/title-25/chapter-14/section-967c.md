# 25 U.S.C. § 967c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–717, § 3, Nov. 2, 1966, 80 Stat. 1115, which related to tax exemption of funds distributed under the provisions of former sections 967a to 967d of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
