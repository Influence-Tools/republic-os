# 25 U.S.C. § 715g - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–42, § 9, June 28, 1989, 103 Stat. 93, which related to adoption of a tribal constitution and election of tribal officials, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
