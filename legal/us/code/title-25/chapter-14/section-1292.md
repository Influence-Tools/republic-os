# 25 U.S.C. § 1292 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–456, § 2, Oct. 3, 1972, 86 Stat. 762, which set out requirements for inclusion on membership roll, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
