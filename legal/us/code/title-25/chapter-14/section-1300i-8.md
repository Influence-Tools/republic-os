# 25 U.S.C. § 1300i–8 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–580, § 9, Oct. 31, 1988, 102 Stat. 2932; Pub. L. 101–121, title III, § 315, Oct. 23, 1989, 103 Stat. 744; Pub. L. 101–301, § 9(3), May 24, 1990, 104 Stat. 211, which related to recognition and organization of the Yurok Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
