# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–17 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 18, Dec. 22, 1974, 88 Stat. 1721, which related to actions for accounting, fair value of grazing, and claims for damages to land, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
