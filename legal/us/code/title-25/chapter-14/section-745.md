# 25 U.S.C. § 745 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 5, 68 Stat. 1100, which related to transfer of tribal real property that is under supervision of the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
