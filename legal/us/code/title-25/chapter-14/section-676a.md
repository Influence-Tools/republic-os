# 25 U.S.C. § 676a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–60, Aug. 1, 1967, 81 Stat. 164; Pub. L. 90–332, June 7, 1968, 82 Stat. 171, which related to distribution of judgment fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
