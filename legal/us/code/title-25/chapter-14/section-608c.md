# 25 U.S.C. § 608c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 28, 1955, ch. 423, § 4, 69 Stat. 393, which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to prescribe regulations necessary to carry out the purposes of former sections 608 to 608c of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
