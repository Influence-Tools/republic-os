# 25 U.S.C. § 1300g–1 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–89, title I, § 102, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 666, which redesignated the Tiwa Indians of Ysleta, Texas, as the Ysleta del Sur Pueblo, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
