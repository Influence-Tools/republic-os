# 25 U.S.C. § 755 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 15, 68 Stat. 1103, which related to execution of patents, deeds, and other instruments by the Secretary of the Interior, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
