# 25 U.S.C. § 554 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act June 1, 1938, ch. 310, § 4, 52 Stat. 606, which related to disposition of payment upon the death of an Indian, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
