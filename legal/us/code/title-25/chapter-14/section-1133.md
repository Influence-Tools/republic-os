# 25 U.S.C. § 1133 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–660, § 3, Oct. 14, 1966, 80 Stat. 911, which related to distribution of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
