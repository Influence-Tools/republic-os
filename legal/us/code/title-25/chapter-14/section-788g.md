# 25 U.S.C. § 788g - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–506, § 3, Sept. 21, 1968, 82 Stat. 859, which related to distribution of judgment funds to heirs of deceased enrollees, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
