# 25 U.S.C. § 1154 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–114, § 4, Oct. 24, 1967, 81 Stat. 335, which exempted funds distributed under the provisions of this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
