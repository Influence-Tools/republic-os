# 25 U.S.C. § 610b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–534, § 3, Sept. 28, 1968, 82 Stat. 884, which related to title to lands acquired pursuant to this subchapter, tax exemptions for lands within the boundaries of the Swinomish Indian Reservation, and prohibition of restrictions on lands outside such boundaries, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
