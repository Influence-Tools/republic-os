# 25 U.S.C. § 713e - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 98–165, § 7, Nov. 22, 1983, 97 Stat. 1067, which related to membership rolls and voting rights of members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
