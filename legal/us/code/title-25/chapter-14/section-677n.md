# 25 U.S.C. § 677n - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 15, 68 Stat. 876, which related to disposal by mixed-blood members of their individual interests in tribal assets, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
