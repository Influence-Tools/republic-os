# 25 U.S.C. § 1300i–9 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–580, § 10, Oct. 31, 1988, 102 Stat. 2934, which related to establishment of a plan for economic development for the Yurok Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
