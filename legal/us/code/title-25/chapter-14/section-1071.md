# 25 U.S.C. § 1071 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 88–551, Aug. 31, 1964, 78 Stat. 755, which provided for per capita payments to enrolled tribal members from funds appropriated for a judgment dated Mar. 1, 1960, and exemption of such payments from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
