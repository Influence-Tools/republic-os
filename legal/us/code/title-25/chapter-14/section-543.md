# 25 U.S.C. § 543 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 7, 1939, ch. 552, § 3, 53 Stat. 1253, which related to liability of judgment funds for debts, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
