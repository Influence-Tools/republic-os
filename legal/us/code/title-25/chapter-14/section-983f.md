# 25 U.S.C. § 983f - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–484, § 8, Oct. 31, 1990, 104 Stat. 1169, which related to adoption of a tribal constitution and election of tribal officials, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
