# 25 U.S.C. § 713d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 98–165, § 6, Nov. 22, 1983, 97 Stat. 1066, which related to the tribal constitution and bylaws and election of the tribal governing body, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
