# 25 U.S.C. § 1300e–4 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–557, § 5, Oct. 25, 1972, 86 Stat. 1172, which related to expenditure of funds withheld from distribution under former section 1300e–3 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
