# 25 U.S.C. § 571 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 27, 1939, ch. 387, § 1, 53 Stat. 1128, which directed the Secretary of the Interior to prepare a membership roll of the Shoshone Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
