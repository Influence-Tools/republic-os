# 25 U.S.C. § 783 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 1, 1955, ch. 444, § 4, 69 Stat. 432, which related to payments to minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
