# 25 U.S.C. § 766 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 96–227, § 7, Apr. 3, 1980, 94 Stat. 320; Pub. L. 109–126, § 4, Dec. 7, 2005, 119 Stat. 2547, which related to transfer of real property for reservation and plan for enlargement, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
