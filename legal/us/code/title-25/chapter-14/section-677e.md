# 25 U.S.C. § 677e - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 6, 68 Stat. 868, which granted to mixed-blood members of the tribe the right to organize for their common welfare, adopt a constitution and bylaws, and select representatives, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
