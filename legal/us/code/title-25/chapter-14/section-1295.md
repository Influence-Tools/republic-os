# 25 U.S.C. § 1295 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–456, § 5, Oct. 3, 1972, 86 Stat. 763, which related to payments to heirs or legatees of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
