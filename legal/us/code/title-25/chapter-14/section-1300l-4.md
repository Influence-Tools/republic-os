# 25 U.S.C. § 1300l–4 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–434, title II, § 206, Oct. 31, 1994, 108 Stat. 4535; Pub. L. 104–109, § 8(b), Feb. 12, 1996, 110 Stat. 765, which provided for governance of the tribe by an Interim Council until adoption of a new tribal constitution and bylaws, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
