# 25 U.S.C. § 881a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 91–401, § 2, Sept. 16, 1970, 84 Stat. 838, which related to payments to minor members of the Potawatomi Indians and members under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
