# 25 U.S.C. § 564k - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 12, 68 Stat. 721, which transferred to the tribe for collection all loans made from the reimbursable loan fund established by former section 531 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
