# 25 U.S.C. § 564s - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 20, 68 Stat. 723, which related to set-off of indebtedness payable to the tribe or to the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
