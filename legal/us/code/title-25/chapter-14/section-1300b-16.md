# 25 U.S.C. § 1300b–16 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 97–429, § 7, Jan. 8, 1983, 96 Stat. 2270, which related to provision of Federal Indian services, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
