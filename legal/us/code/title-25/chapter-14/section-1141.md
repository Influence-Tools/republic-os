# 25 U.S.C. § 1141 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–93, § 1, Sept. 27, 1967, 81 Stat. 229, which related to distribution of judgment funds and payment of attorney’s fees and expenses, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
