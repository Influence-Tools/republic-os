# 25 U.S.C. § 1300i–11 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–580, § 14, Oct. 31, 1988, 102 Stat. 2936; Pub. L. 101–301, § 9(4), May 24, 1990, 104 Stat. 211; Pub. L. 102–572, title IX, § 902(b)(1), Oct. 29, 1992, 106 Stat. 4516, which related to claims challenging the partition of the joint reservation pursuant to former section 1300i–1 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
