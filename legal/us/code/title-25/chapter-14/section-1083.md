# 25 U.S.C. § 1083 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–655, § 3, Oct. 14, 1966, 80 Stat. 905, which related to criteria to be employed in preparing Quileute base roll, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
