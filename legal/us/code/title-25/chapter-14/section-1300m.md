# 25 U.S.C. § 1300m - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–454, title III, § 302, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4793, which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 103–454, title III, § 301, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4793, provided that title III of Pub. L. 103–454, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Paskenta Band Restoration Act”.
