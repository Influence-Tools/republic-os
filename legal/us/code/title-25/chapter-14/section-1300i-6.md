# 25 U.S.C. § 1300i–6 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–580, § 7, Oct. 31, 1988, 102 Stat. 2931, which related to division of remaining funds after Settlement Fund payments have been made, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
