# 25 U.S.C. § 1272 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–295, § 2, May 16, 1972, 86 Stat. 139, which related to payment of shares to minors and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
