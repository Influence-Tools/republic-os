# 25 U.S.C. § 677h - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 9, 68 Stat. 869, which related to the sale or other disposition of certain described lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
