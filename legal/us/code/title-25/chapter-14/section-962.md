# 25 U.S.C. § 962 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 87–235, § 2, Sept. 14, 1961, 75 Stat. 508, which directed that roll prepared pursuant to former section 961 of this title would constitute membership as of Sept. 14, 1961, and provided for enrollment of children born after such date, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
