# 25 U.S.C. § 1300i–1 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–580, § 2, Oct. 31, 1988, 102 Stat. 2925, which related to partition of the joint reservation and establishment of the Hoopa Valley Reservation and the Yurok Reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
