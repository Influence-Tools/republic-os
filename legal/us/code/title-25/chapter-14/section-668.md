# 25 U.S.C. § 668 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–312, § 1, June 14, 1972, 86 Stat. 216, which related to the sale of lands held by the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
