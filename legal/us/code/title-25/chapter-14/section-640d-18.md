# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–18 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 19, Dec. 22, 1974, 88 Stat. 1721; Pub. L. 96–305, § 8, July 8, 1980, 94 Stat. 932, which provided for a reduction of livestock within the joint use area, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
