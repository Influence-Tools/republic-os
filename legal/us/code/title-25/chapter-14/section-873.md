# 25 U.S.C. § 873 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 85–395, § 3, May 9, 1958, 72 Stat. 106; Pub. L. 86–540, June 29, 1960, 74 Stat. 252, which related to payments to next of kin or legatees, minors, and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
