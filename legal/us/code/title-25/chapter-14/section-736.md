# 25 U.S.C. § 736 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–89, title II, § 206, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 671, which established a Federal reservation and set out provisions relating to conveyances of title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
