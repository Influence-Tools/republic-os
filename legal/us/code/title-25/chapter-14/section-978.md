# 25 U.S.C. § 978 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 87–629, § 8, Sept. 5, 1962, 76 Stat. 430, which related to taxation of property distributed under this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
