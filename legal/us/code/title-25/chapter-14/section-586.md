# 25 U.S.C. § 586 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–206, § 6, Dec. 18, 1971, 85 Stat. 738, which related to distribution of funds to members of the Northwestern Band of Shoshone Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
