# 25 U.S.C. § 1300n–4 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 106–568, title XIV, § 1406, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2940, which related to tribal membership rolls, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
