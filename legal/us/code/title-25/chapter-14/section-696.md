# 25 U.S.C. § 696 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 733, § 6, 68 Stat. 725, which related to transfer of individual property by the Secretary of the Interior to members of each tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
