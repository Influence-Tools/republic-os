# 25 U.S.C. § 564h - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 9(a), (b), 68 Stat. 720, 721, which related to applicability of Federal and State laws to probate of property of deceased members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
