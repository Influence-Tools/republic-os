# 25 U.S.C. § 752 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 12, 68 Stat. 1103, which provided that nothing in this subchapter would abrogate any water rights of a tribe or its members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
