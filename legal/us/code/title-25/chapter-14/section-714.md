# 25 U.S.C. § 714 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 98–481, § 2, Oct. 17, 1984, 98 Stat. 2250, which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 98–481, § 1, Oct. 17, 1984, 98 Stat. 2250, provided that Pub. L. 98–481, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Coos, Lower Umpqua, and Siuslaw Restoration Act”.
