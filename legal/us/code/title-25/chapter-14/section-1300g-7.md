# 25 U.S.C. § 1300g–7 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–89, title I, § 108, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 669; Pub. L. 112–157, § 1, Aug. 10, 2012, 126 Stat. 1213, which related to tribal membership, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
