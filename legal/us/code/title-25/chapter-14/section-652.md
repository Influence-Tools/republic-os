# 25 U.S.C. § 652 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act May 18, 1928, ch. 624, § 2, 45 Stat. 602; Pub. L. 97–164, title I, § 150, Apr. 2, 1982, 96 Stat. 46; Pub. L. 100–352, § 6(b), June 27, 1988, 102 Stat. 663; Pub. L. 102–572, title IX, § 902(b)(1), Oct. 29, 1992, 106 Stat. 4516, which related to claims against the United States for appropriated lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
