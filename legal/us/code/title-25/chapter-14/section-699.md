# 25 U.S.C. § 699 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 733, § 9, 68 Stat. 726, which related to taxation of property distributed under this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
