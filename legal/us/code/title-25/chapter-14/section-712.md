# 25 U.S.C. § 712 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 97–391, § 2, Dec. 29, 1982, 96 Stat. 1960; Pub. L. 100–139, § 5(b), Oct. 26, 1987, 101 Stat. 827, which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short Title of 1987 AmendmentPub. L. 100–139, § 1, Oct. 26, 1987, 101 Stat. 822, provided that Pub. L. 100–139 [see Tables for classification] could be cited as the “Cow Creek Band of Umpqua Tribe of Indians Distribution of Judgment Funds Act of 1987”.

Short TitlePub. L. 97–391, § 1, Dec. 29, 1982, 96 Stat. 1960, provided that Pub. L. 97–391, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Cow Creek Band of Umpqua Tribe of Indians Recognition Act”.
