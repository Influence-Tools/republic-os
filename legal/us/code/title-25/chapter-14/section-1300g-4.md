# 25 U.S.C. § 1300g–4 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–89, title I, § 105, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 667, which set out provisions relating to tribal reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
