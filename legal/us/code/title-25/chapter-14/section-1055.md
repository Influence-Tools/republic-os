# 25 U.S.C. § 1055 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 88–506, § 5, Aug. 30, 1964, 78 Stat. 639, which authorized prescription of rules and regulations to carry out the provisions of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
