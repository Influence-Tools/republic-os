# 25 U.S.C. § 773 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1085, § 3, 68 Stat. 979, which related to payments on behalf of deceased enrollees, minors, and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
