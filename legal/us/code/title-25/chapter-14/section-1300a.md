# 25 U.S.C. § 1300a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–461, § 2, Oct. 6, 1972, 86 Stat. 769, which related to set aside of percentage of funds for the benefit of the Payson Indian Band, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
