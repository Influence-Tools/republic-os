# 25 U.S.C. § 712a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 97–391, § 3, Dec. 29, 1982, 96 Stat. 1960; Pub. L. 100–139, § 5(b), Oct. 26, 1987, 101 Stat. 827, which related to extension of Federal recognition, rights, and privileges to the Cow Creek Band of the Umpqua Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
