# 25 U.S.C. § 495 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Mar. 3, 1891, ch. 561, § 15, 26 Stat. 1101, which reserved the Annette Islands for the use of the Metlakahtla Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
