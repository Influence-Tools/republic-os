# 25 U.S.C. § 590 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–206, § 10, Dec. 18, 1971, 85 Stat. 739, which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to prescribe rules and regulations, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
