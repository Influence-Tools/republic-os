# 25 U.S.C. § 500c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 4, 50 Stat. 900, which authorized Secretary to accept gifts for purposes of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
