# 25 U.S.C. § 957 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 86–339, § 7, Sept. 21, 1959, 73 Stat. 605, which provided that allotments in accordance with the provisions of this subchapter be deemed complete and full equalization of allotments on the reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
