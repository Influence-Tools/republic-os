# 25 U.S.C. § 983a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–484, § 3, Oct. 31, 1990, 104 Stat. 1167, which related to Federal recognition of the Ponca Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
