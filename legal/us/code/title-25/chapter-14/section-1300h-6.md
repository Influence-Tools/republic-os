# 25 U.S.C. § 1300h–6 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–420, § 8, Sept. 8, 1988, 102 Stat. 1579, which related to distribution of judgment funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
