# 25 U.S.C. § 1300l–5 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–434, title II, § 207, Oct. 31, 1994, 108 Stat. 4535, which related to adoption of a tribal constitution and bylaws and election of tribal officials, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
