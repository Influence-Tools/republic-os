# 25 U.S.C. § 1300j–6 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–323, § 7, Sept. 21, 1994, 108 Stat. 2154, which listed counties in Michigan comprising the band’s service area, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
