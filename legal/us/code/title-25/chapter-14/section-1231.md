# 25 U.S.C. § 1231 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 91–417, § 1, Sept. 25, 1970, 84 Stat. 868, which provided for judgment funds to be distributed in equal shares to enrolled members of the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
