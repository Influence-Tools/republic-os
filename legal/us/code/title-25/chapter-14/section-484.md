# 25 U.S.C. § 484 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 14, 1954, ch. 472, § 1, 68 Stat. 467, which related to conversion of exchange assignments of tribal lands on certain Sioux reservations into trust titles and continued trust and tax exemption thereon, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
