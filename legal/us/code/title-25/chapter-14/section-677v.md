# 25 U.S.C. § 677v - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 23, 68 Stat. 877, which related to termination of Federal trust relationship to mixed-blood member of the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
