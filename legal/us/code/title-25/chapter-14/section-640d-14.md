# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–14 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 15, Dec. 22, 1974, 88 Stat. 1719; Pub. L. 96–305, § 7, July 8, 1980, 94 Stat. 932; Pub. L. 100–666, §§ 4(b), 10, Nov. 16, 1988, 102 Stat. 3930, 3934, which related to relocation housing, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
