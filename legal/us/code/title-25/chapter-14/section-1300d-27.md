# 25 U.S.C. § 1300d–27 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 105–387, § 8, Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3475, which related to challenges to the constitutionality or validity of distributions under this part, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
