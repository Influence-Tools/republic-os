# 25 U.S.C. § 1041 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 106–568, title VII, § 702, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2913, which set out Congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 106–568, title VII, § 701, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2913, provided that title VII of Pub. L. 106–568, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Shawnee Tribe Status Act of 2000”.
