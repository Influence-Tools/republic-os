# 25 U.S.C. § 787 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–76, § 2, Aug. 29, 1967, 81 Stat. 177, which related to advances and expenditures from funds deposited pursuant to former sections 786 to 788 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
