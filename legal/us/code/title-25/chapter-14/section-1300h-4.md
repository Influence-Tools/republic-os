# 25 U.S.C. § 1300h–4 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–420, § 6, Sept. 8, 1988, 102 Stat. 1578, which related to organization of the tribe, adoption of a tribal constitution, and election of a tribal governing body, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
