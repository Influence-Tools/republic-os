# 25 U.S.C. § 635 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Apr. 19, 1950, ch. 92, § 5, 64 Stat. 46; Pub. L. 86–505, § 1, June 11, 1960, 74 Stat. 199, which related to disposition of lands owned by the Navajo and Hopi Tribes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
