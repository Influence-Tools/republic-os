# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–20 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 21, Dec. 22, 1974, 88 Stat. 1722, which related to the use and right of access to religious shrines on the reservation of the other tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
