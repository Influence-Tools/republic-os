# 25 U.S.C. § 1134 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–660, § 4, Oct. 14, 1966, 80 Stat. 911, which exempted funds distributed under the provisions of this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
