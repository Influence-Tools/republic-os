# 25 U.S.C. § 711a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 95–195, § 3, Nov. 18, 1977, 91 Stat. 1415, which related to Federal recognition of the Siletz Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
