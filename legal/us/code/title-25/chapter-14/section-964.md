# 25 U.S.C. § 964 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 87–235, § 4, Sept. 14, 1961, 75 Stat. 508, which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
