# 25 U.S.C. § 465b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Feb. 24, 1942, ch. 113, § 2, 56 Stat. 121, which defined “Klamath Tribe of Indians”, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
