# 25 U.S.C. § 1182 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–508, § 2, Sept. 21, 1968, 82 Stat. 861, which provided for judgment funds to be divided equally between the Cherokee Delawares and the Delaware Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
