# 25 U.S.C. § 788b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–504, § 2, Sept. 21, 1968, 82 Stat. 855, which related to distribution of certain judgment funds to persons whose names appear on the roll, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
