# 25 U.S.C. § 640d–29 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 93–531, § 31, as added Pub. L. 100–666, § 5, Nov. 16, 1988, 102 Stat. 3931, which related to restrictions on lobbying, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
