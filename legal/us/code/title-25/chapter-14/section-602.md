# 25 U.S.C. § 602 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 9, 1946, ch. 933, § 2, 60 Stat. 969, which related to applications for tribal membership by persons excluded from enrollment, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
