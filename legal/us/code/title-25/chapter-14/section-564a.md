# 25 U.S.C. § 564a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 2, 68 Stat. 718; Pub. L. 85–132, § 1(f), Aug. 14, 1957, 71 Stat. 348, which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
