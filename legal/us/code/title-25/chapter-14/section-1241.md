# 25 U.S.C. § 1241 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–59, § 1, July 29, 1971, 85 Stat. 158, which related to distribution of judgment funds and payment of attorney fees and expenses, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
