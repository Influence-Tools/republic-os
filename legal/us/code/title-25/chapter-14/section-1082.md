# 25 U.S.C. § 1082 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 89–655, § 2, Oct. 14, 1966, 80 Stat. 905, which directed the preparation of membership rolls for the Quileute and Hoh Tribes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
