# 25 U.S.C. § 1300l–1 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–434, title II, § 203, Oct. 31, 1994, 108 Stat. 4533; Pub. L. 104–109, § 8(a), Feb. 12, 1996, 110 Stat. 764, which related to establishment of a plan for economic development for the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
