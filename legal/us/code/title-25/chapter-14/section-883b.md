# 25 U.S.C. § 883b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 92–586, § 3, Oct. 27, 1972, 86 Stat. 1296, which related to filing of claims by members of the Osage Tribe and reversion of unclaimed shares, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
