# 25 U.S.C. § 610e - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 90–534, § 7, Sept. 28, 1968, 82 Stat. 884, which authorized the assignment of income by the Swinomish Indian Tribal Community, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
