# 25 U.S.C. § 1222 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 91–364, § 2, July 31, 1970, 84 Stat. 688, which provided for a portion of certain judgment funds obtained by the tribe to be used for improvement and maintenance of the Peoria Indian Cemetery and for the balance of such funds to be distributed in equal shares to tribe members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
