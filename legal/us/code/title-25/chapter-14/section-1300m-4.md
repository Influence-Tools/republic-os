# 25 U.S.C. § 1300m–4 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–454, title III, § 306, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4794, which related to tribal membership rolls, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
