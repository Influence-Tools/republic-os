# 25 U.S.C. § 991 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 87–775, § 1, Oct. 9, 1962, 76 Stat. 776, which provided for per capita payments to tribal members from funds appropriated for a certain judgment against the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
