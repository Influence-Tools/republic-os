# 25 U.S.C. § 941h - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–116, § 10, Oct. 27, 1993, 107 Stat. 1126, which set out administrative provisions relating to jurisdiction, taxation, and other matters, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
