# 25 U.S.C. § 1715 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 95–395, title II, § 201, as added Pub. L. 96–601, § 5(a), Dec. 24, 1980, 94 Stat. 3498, which provided for tax exemption for settlement lands, with certain exceptions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
