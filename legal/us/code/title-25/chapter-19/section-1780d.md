# 25 U.S.C. § 1780d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 109–286, § 6, Sept. 27, 2006, 120 Stat. 1221, which related to establishment and management of the Pueblo de San Ildefonso Land Claims Settlement Fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
