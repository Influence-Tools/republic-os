# 25 U.S.C. § 1722 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 96–420, § 3, Oct. 10, 1980, 94 Stat. 1786, which set out definitions for this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
