# 25 U.S.C. § 1773e - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–41, § 7, June 21, 1989, 103 Stat. 87, which appropriated $100,000 for navigation equipment at Commencement Bay as part of the fisheries aspect of the Settlement Agreement, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
