# 25 U.S.C. § 1703 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 95–395, § 4, Sept. 30, 1978, 92 Stat. 814, which established the Rhode Island Indian Claims Settlement Fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
