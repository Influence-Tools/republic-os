# 25 U.S.C. § 1773f - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–41, § 8, June 21, 1989, 103 Stat. 87, which related to funds for economic development and land acquisition, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
