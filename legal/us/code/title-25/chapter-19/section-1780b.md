# 25 U.S.C. § 1780b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 109–286, § 4, Sept. 27, 2006, 120 Stat. 1219, which provided for judgment and dismissal of certain litigation pending on Sept. 27, 2006, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
