# 25 U.S.C. § 1771e - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–95, § 7, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 707, which related to jurisdiction over settlement lands and restraint on alienation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
