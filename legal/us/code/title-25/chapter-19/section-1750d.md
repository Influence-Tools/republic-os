# 25 U.S.C. § 1750d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 105–83, title VII, § 706, Nov. 14, 1997, 111 Stat. 1626, which identified the Miccosukee Indian Reservation lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
