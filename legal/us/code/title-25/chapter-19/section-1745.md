# 25 U.S.C. § 1745 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 97–399, § 6, Dec. 31, 1982, 96 Stat. 2014, which provided for special provisions for the Miccosukee Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
