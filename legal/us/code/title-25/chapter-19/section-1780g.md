# 25 U.S.C. § 1780g - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 109–286, § 9, Sept. 27, 2006, 120 Stat. 1224, which related to trust status of Settlement Area Land and National Forest boundaries, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
