# 25 U.S.C. § 1778g - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 106–568, title VI, § 609, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2912, which set forth provisions relating to effect of this subchapter and the Settlement Agreement on eligibility for benefits and other services, preservation of existing rights, and amendment of the Settlement Agreement, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
