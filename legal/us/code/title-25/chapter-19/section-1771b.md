# 25 U.S.C. § 1771b - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–95, § 4, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 705, which related to approval of prior transfers and extinguishment of aboriginal title and claims of Gay Head Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
