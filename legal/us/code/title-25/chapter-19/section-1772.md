# 25 U.S.C. § 1772 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–228, § 2, Dec. 31, 1987, 101 Stat. 1556, which set out congressional findings and policy, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 100–228, § 1, Dec. 31, 1987, 101 Stat. 1556, provided that Pub. L. 100–228, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Seminole Indian Land Claims Settlement Act of 1987”.
