# 25 U.S.C. § 1727 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 96–420, § 8, Oct. 10, 1980, 94 Stat. 1795, which provided for implementation of the Indian Child Welfare Act in jurisdiction over Indian child custody proceedings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
