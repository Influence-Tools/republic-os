# 25 U.S.C. § 1775g - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–377, § 9, Oct. 19, 1994, 108 Stat. 3506, which related to the effect of revocation of the State Agreement or the gaming compact, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
