# 25 U.S.C. § 1780k - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 109–286, § 13, Sept. 27, 2006, 120 Stat. 1228, which provided for boundaries between the Pueblo of Santa Clara and the Pueblo de San Ildefonso, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
