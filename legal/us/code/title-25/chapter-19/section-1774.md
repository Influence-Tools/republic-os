# 25 U.S.C. § 1774 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–503, § 2, Nov. 3, 1990, 104 Stat. 1292, which set out congressional findings and purposes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 101–503, § 1, Nov. 3, 1990, 104 Stat. 1292, provided that Pub. L. 101–503, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Seneca Nation Settlement Act of 1990”.
