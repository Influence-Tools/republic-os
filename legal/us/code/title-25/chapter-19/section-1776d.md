# 25 U.S.C. § 1776d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–444, § 6, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4638; amended Pub. L. 103–435, § 23, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4575, which provided for the establishment and administration of the Crow Tribal Trust Fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
