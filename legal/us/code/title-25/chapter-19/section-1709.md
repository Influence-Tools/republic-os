# 25 U.S.C. § 1709 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 95–395, § 10, Sept. 30, 1978, 92 Stat. 817, which provided for preservation of certain other Federal benefits, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
