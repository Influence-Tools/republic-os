# 25 U.S.C. § 1771c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–95, § 5, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 705, which provided for conditions precedent to Federal purchase of settlement lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
