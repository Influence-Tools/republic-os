# 25 U.S.C. § 1773a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–41, § 3, June 21, 1989, 103 Stat. 84, which provided for resolution of Puyallup tribal land claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
