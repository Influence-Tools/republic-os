# 25 U.S.C. § 1755 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 98–134, § 6, Oct. 18, 1983, 97 Stat. 855, which related to State jurisdiction over the reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
