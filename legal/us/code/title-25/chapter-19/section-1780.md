# 25 U.S.C. § 1780 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 109–286, § 2, Sept. 27, 2006, 120 Stat. 1218, which set out definitions of terms and purposes of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 109–286, § 1, Sept. 27, 2006, 120 Stat. 1218, provided that Pub. L. 109–286, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Pueblo de San Ildefonso Claims Settlement Act of 2005”.
