# 25 U.S.C. § 1773c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–41, § 5, June 21, 1989, 103 Stat. 86, which related to standards to be applied by the Secretary of the Interior in accepting additional lands in trust, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
