# 25 U.S.C. § 1771d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–95, § 6, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 706, which related to the purchase and transfer of settlement lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
