# 25 U.S.C. § 1754 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 98–134, § 5, Oct. 18, 1983, 97 Stat. 853; Pub. L. 99–514, § 2, Oct. 22, 1986, 100 Stat. 2095, which related to establishment and administration of the Mashantucket Pequot Settlement Fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
