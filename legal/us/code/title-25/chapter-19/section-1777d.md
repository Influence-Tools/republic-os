# 25 U.S.C. § 1777d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 106–425, § 6, Nov. 1, 2000, 114 Stat. 1895, which related to affirmation of accurate boundaries of the Santo Domingo Pueblo Grant, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
