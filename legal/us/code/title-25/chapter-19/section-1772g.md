# 25 U.S.C. § 1772g - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–228, § 9, Dec. 31, 1987, 101 Stat. 1561, which described results of revocation of the Settlement Agreement or any part thereof, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
