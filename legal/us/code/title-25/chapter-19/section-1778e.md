# 25 U.S.C. § 1778e - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 106–568, title VI, § 607, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2911, which related to conveyances to certain water districts of permanent flowage easements as to Indian trust lands and Federal lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
