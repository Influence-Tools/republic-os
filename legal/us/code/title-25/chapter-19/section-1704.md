# 25 U.S.C. § 1704 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 95–395, § 5, Sept. 30, 1978, 92 Stat. 814, which related to option agreements to purchase private settlement lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
