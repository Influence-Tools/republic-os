# 25 U.S.C. § 1775d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–377, § 6, Oct. 19, 1994, 108 Stat. 3505, which related to consent of the United States to State assumption of criminal jurisdiction, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
