# 25 U.S.C. § 1780a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 109–286, § 3, Sept. 27, 2006, 120 Stat. 1219, which related to ratification and correction of the Settlement and Los Alamos Agreements, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
