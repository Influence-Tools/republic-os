# 25 U.S.C. § 1779 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 107–331, title VI, § 602, Dec. 13, 2002, 116 Stat. 2845, which set out congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 107–331, title VI, § 601, Dec. 13, 2002, 116 Stat. 2845, provided that title VI of Pub. L. 107–331, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Cherokee, Choctaw, and Chickasaw Nations Claims Settlement Act”.
