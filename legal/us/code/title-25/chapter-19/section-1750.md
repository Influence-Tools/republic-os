# 25 U.S.C. § 1750 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 105–83, title VII, § 702, Nov. 14, 1997, 111 Stat. 1624, which set out congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 105–83, title VII, § 701, Nov. 14, 1997, 111 Stat. 1624, provided that title VII of Pub. L. 105–83, enacting this part, could be cited as the “Miccosukee Settlement Act of 1997”.
