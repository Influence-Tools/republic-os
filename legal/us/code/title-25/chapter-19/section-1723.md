# 25 U.S.C. § 1723 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 96–420, § 4, Oct. 10, 1980, 94 Stat. 1787, which related to approval of prior transfers and extinguishment of Indian title and claims of Indians within the State of Maine, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
