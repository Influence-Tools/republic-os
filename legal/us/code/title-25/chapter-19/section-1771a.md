# 25 U.S.C. § 1771a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 100–95, § 3, Aug. 18, 1987, 101 Stat. 704, which related to the Gay Head Indian claims settlement fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
