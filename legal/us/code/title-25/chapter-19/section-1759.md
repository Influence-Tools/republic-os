# 25 U.S.C. § 1759 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 98–134, § 10, Oct. 18, 1983, 97 Stat. 856, which provided for a general discharge and release of the State of Connecticut, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
