# 25 U.S.C. § 1775c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–377, § 5, Oct. 19, 1994, 108 Stat. 3504, which provided for conveyance of lands described in exhibits A and B of the State Agreement, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
