# 25 U.S.C. § 1721 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 96–420, § 2, Oct. 10, 1980, 94 Stat. 1785, which set out Congressional findings and declaration of policy, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 96–420, § 1, Oct. 10, 1980, 94 Stat. 1785, provided that Pub. L. 96–420, enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Maine Indian Claims Settlement Act of 1980”.
