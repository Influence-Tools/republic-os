# 25 U.S.C. § 1753 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 98–134, § 4, Oct. 18, 1983, 97 Stat. 852, which related to extinguishment of aboriginal titles and Indian claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
