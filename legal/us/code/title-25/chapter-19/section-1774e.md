# 25 U.S.C. § 1774e - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–503, § 7, Nov. 3, 1990, 104 Stat. 1296, which related to conditions precedent to payment of United States and State funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
