# 25 U.S.C. § 1779c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 107–331, title VI, § 605, Dec. 13, 2002, 116 Stat. 2848, which related to settlement of claims, appropriations, and allocation of funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
