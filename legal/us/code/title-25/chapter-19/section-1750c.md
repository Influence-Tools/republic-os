# 25 U.S.C. § 1750c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 105–83, title VII, § 705, Nov. 14, 1997, 111 Stat. 1625, which related to authority of the Secretary of the Interior as Trustee for the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
