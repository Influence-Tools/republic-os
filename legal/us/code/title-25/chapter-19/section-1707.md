# 25 U.S.C. § 1707 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 95–395, § 8, Sept. 30, 1978, 92 Stat. 816, which related to purchase and transfer of private settlement lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
