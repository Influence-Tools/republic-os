# 25 U.S.C. § 1725 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 96–420, § 6, Oct. 10, 1980, 94 Stat. 1793; Pub. L. 97–428, § 3, Jan. 8, 1983, 96 Stat. 2268, which provided for applicability of State law, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
