# 25 U.S.C. § 1741 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 97–399, § 2, Dec. 31, 1982, 96 Stat. 2012, which set out congressional findings and declaration of policy, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 97–399, § 1, Dec. 31, 1982, 96 Stat. 2012, provided that Pub. L. 97–399, enacting this part A (§ 1741 et seq.) of this subchapter, could be cited as the “Florida Indian Land Claims Settlement Act of 1982”.
