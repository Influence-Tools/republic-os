# 25 U.S.C. § 1780h - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 109–286, § 10, Sept. 27, 2006, 120 Stat. 1225, which related to management of lands prior to conveyance, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
