# 25 U.S.C. § 1776e - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–444, § 7, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4640, which related to effect of Federal recognition of the tribe on eligibility of a tribe or a member of a tribe for other Federal services, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
