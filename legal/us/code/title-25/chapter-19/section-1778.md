# 25 U.S.C. § 1778 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 106–568, title VI, § 602, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2906, which set out congressional findings and purpose, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 106–568, title VI, § 601, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2906, provided that title VI of Pub. L. 106–568, enacting this subchapter and provisions formerly set out as a note under this section, could be cited as the “Torres-Martinez Desert Cahuilla Indians Claims Settlement Act”.
