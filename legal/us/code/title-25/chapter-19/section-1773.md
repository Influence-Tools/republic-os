# 25 U.S.C. § 1773 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–41, § 2, June 21, 1989, 103 Stat. 83, which set out congressional findings and purpose, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 101–41, § 1, June 21, 1989, 103 Stat. 83, provided that Pub. L. 101–41, enacting this subchapter and provisions formerly set out as a note under section 1773a of this title, could be cited as the “Puyallup Tribe of Indians Settlement Act of 1989”.
