# 25 U.S.C. § 1776f - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–444, § 8, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4640, which related to exchanges of land or minerals, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
