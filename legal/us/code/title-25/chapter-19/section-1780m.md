# 25 U.S.C. § 1780m - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 109–286, § 15, Sept. 27, 2006, 120 Stat. 1229, which provided for rule of construction and judicial review of the Settlement and Los Alamos Agreements, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
