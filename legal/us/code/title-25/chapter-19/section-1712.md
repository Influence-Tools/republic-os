# 25 U.S.C. § 1712 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 95–395, § 13, Sept. 30, 1978, 92 Stat. 817, which related to approval of prior transfers and extinguishment of claims and aboriginal title outside the town of Charlestown, Rhode Island, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
