# 25 U.S.C. § 1776j - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–444, § 12, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4642, which provided that terms and conditions of this subchapter and the Settlement Agreement would constitute full and complete satisfaction of certain claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
