# 25 U.S.C. § 1774d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 101–503, § 6, Nov. 3, 1990, 104 Stat. 1295, which related to sources of settlement funds and distribution and use of amounts, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
