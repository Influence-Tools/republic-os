# 25 U.S.C. § 1779d - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 107–331, title VI, § 606, Dec. 13, 2002, 116 Stat. 2851, which related to establishment and management of a separate tribal trust fund for each of the Indian Nations, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
