# 25 U.S.C. § 1730 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 96–420, § 11, Oct. 10, 1980, 94 Stat. 1796, which related to transfer of tribal trust funds held by the State of Maine, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
