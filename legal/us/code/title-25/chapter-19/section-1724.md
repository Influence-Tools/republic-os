# 25 U.S.C. § 1724 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 96–420, § 5, Oct. 10, 1980, 94 Stat. 1788, which related to the Maine Indian Claims Settlement and Land Acquisition Funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
