# 25 U.S.C. § 1778c - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 106–568, title VI, § 605, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2908, which related to establishment and administration of tribal and allottees settlement trust fund accounts, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
