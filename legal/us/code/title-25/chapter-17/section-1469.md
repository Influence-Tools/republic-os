# 25 U.S.C. § 1469 - Rules and regulations

## Text

The Secretary shall promulgate rules and regulations to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 93–262, title I, § 109, Apr. 12, 1974, 88 Stat. 79.)
