# 25 U.S.C. § 2808 - Source of funds

## Text

Any expenses incurred by the Secretary under this chapter shall be paid from funds appropriated under section 13 of this title.

(Pub. L. 101–379, § 9, Aug. 18, 1990, 104 Stat. 477.)
