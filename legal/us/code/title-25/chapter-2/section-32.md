# 25 U.S.C. § 32 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act July 1, 1898, ch. 545, § 1, 30 Stat. 595, required Indian agents to account for funds received and to be responsible for such funds under their official bonds. The services of Indian agents have been dispensed with since 1908. See section 64 of this title and note set out thereunder.
