# 25 U.S.C. § 40 - Limits of superintendencies, agencies, and sub­agencies

## Text

The limits of each superintendency, agency, and subagency shall be established by the Secretary of the Interior, either by tribes or geographical boundaries.

(R.S. § 2066.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification R.S. § 2066 derived from acts June 30, 1834, ch. 162, § 7, 4 Stat. 736; Mar. 3, 1847, ch. 66, § 1, 9 Stat. 203.
