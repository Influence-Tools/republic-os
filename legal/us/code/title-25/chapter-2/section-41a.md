# 25 U.S.C. § 41a - Indian inspectors

## Text

Indian inspectors shall on and after March 4, 1909 be termed inspectors, and shall be included in the classified service.

(Mar. 4, 1909, ch. 297, § 1, 35 Stat. 888.)
