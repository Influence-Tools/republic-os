# 25 U.S.C. § 57 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462, 43 Stat. 1147, which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to allow employees in the Indian Service heat and light for quarters without charge, was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts.
