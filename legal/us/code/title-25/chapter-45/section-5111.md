# 25 U.S.C. § 5111 - Allotments or holdings outside of reservations

## Text

Nothing contained in this Act shall be construed to relate to Indian holdings of allotments or homesteads upon the public domain outside of the geographic boundaries of any Indian reservation now existing or established hereafter.

(June 18, 1934, ch. 576, § 8, 48 Stat. 986.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextThis Act, referred to in text, is act June 18, 1934, ch. 576, 48 Stat. 984, popularly known as the Indian Reorganization Act, which is classified generally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under section 5101 of this title and Tables.

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 468 of this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
