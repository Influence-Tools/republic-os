# 24 U.S.C. § 36 - Repealed. June 7, 1956, ch. 374, § 306(2), 70 Stat. 254

## Notes

Section, act May 10, 1943, ch. 95, § 6, 57 Stat. 41, made sections 32 to 36 of this title applicable to dependents of personnel of the Coast Guard.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective six months after June 7, 1956, see section 307 of act June 7, 1956, ch. 374, 70 Stat. 254.
