# 24 U.S.C. § 78 - Repealed. Pub. L. 85–56, title XXII, § 2202(58), June 17, 1957, 71 Stat. 164

## Notes

Section, act July 19, 1897, ch. 1, 30 Stat. 121, authorized Board of Managers of National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers to condemn land for domiciliary purposes.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal effective Jan. 1, 1958, see section 2301 of Pub. L. 85–56, title XXIII, June 17, 1957, 71 Stat. 172.
