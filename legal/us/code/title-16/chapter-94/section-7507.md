# 16 U.S.C. § 7507 - Funding

## Text

There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary for fiscal years 2017, 2018, and 2019 for this chapter.

(Pub. L. 114–113, div. O, title IX, § 908, Dec. 18, 2015, 129 Stat. 3034.)
