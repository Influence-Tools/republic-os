# 16 U.S.C. § 558f - Promotion of volunteer opportunities

## Text

The Secretaries shall promote volunteer opportunities in areas administered by the Secretaries.

(Pub. L. 92–300, § 7, as added Pub. L. 118–234, title III, § 341, Jan. 4, 2025, 138 Stat. 2914.)
