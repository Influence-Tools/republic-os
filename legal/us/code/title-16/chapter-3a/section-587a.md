# 16 U.S.C. § 587a - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act June 19, 1934, ch. 648, title II, § 1, 48 Stat. 1056, made the provisions of the Federal Employees Compensation Act applicable to enrollees under this subchapter and became obsolete with the expiration of the other sections of this subchapter.
