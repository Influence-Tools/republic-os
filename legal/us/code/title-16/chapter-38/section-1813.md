# 16 U.S.C. § 1813 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 94–265, title I, § 103, Apr. 13, 1976, 90 Stat. 336, which related to exclusion of highly migratory species of fish from exclusive fishery management authority, was omitted in the general revision of this subchapter by section 101(b) of Pub. L. 99–659. See section 1812 of this title.
