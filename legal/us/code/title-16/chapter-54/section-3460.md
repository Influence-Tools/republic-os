# 16 U.S.C. § 3460 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

(a) In general There are authorized to be 11 So in original. Probably should be followed by “appropriated”. such sums as are necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(b) Loans The Secretary shall not use more than $15,000,000 of any funds made available for a fiscal year to make loans under this subchapter.

(c) Availability Funds appropriated to carry out this subchapter shall remain available until expended.

(Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1537, as added Pub. L. 107–171, title II, § 2504, May 13, 2002, 116 Stat. 274.)
