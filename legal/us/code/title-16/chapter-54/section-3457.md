# 16 U.S.C. § 3457 - Repealed. Pub. L. 110–234, title II, § 2805(d), May 22, 2008, 122 Stat. 1089, and Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a), title II, § 2805(d), June 18, 2008, 122 Stat. 1664, 1817

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1534, as added Pub. L. 107–171, title II, § 2504, May 13, 2002, 116 Stat. 273, related to evaluation of program established under this subchapter and submission of report not later than June 30, 2005.

Editorial Notes

Codification Pub. L. 110–234 and Pub. L. 110–246 repealed this section. Pub. L. 110–234 was repealed by section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246.

Prior ProvisionsA prior section 3457, Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1534, Dec. 22, 1981, 95 Stat. 1340, related to the Resource Conservation and Development Policy Board, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by Pub. L. 107–171.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealRepeal of this section and repeal of Pub. L. 110–234 by Pub. L. 110–246 effective May 22, 2008, the date of enactment of Pub. L. 110–234, see section 4 of Pub. L. 110–246, set out as an Effective Date note under section 8701 of Title 7, Agriculture.
