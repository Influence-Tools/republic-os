# 16 U.S.C. § 831n–2 - Bonds; limitation of issuance under sections 831n and 831n–1

## Text

No bonds shall be issued by the Corporation after the date of enactment of this section under section 831n or 831n–1 of this title.

(May 18, 1933, ch. 32, § 15b, as added July 26, 1939, ch. 366, 53 Stat. 1083.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextThe date of enactment of this section, referred to in text, probably means July 26, 1939.
