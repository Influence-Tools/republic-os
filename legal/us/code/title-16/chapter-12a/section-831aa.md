# 16 U.S.C. § 831aa - Laws repealed

## Text

All acts or parts of acts in conflict with this chapter are repealed, so far as they affect the operations contemplated by this chapter.

(May 18, 1933, ch. 32, § 28, 48 Stat. 71.)
