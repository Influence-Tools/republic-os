# 16 U.S.C. § 2501 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 95–625, title X, § 1002, Nov. 10, 1978, 92 Stat. 3538, which related to congressional findings, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs, by Pub. L. 113–287.
