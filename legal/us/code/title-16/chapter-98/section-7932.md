# 16 U.S.C. § 7932 - No priority

## Text

Nothing in this title 11 See References in Text note below. or the amendments made by this title 1 provides a preference to hunting, fishing, or recreational shooting over any other use of Federal land or water.

(Pub. L. 116–9, title IV, § 4402, Mar. 12, 2019, 133 Stat. 766.)
