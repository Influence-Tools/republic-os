# 16 U.S.C. § 3708 - Reservation of right to amend or repeal chapter

## Text

The Congress expressly reserves the right to repeal or amend this chapter at any time.

(Pub. L. 98–244, § 9, Mar. 26, 1984, 98 Stat. 110.)
