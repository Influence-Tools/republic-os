# 16 U.S.C. § 7203 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

(Pub. L. 111–11, title II, § 2003, Mar. 30, 2009, 123 Stat. 1096.)
