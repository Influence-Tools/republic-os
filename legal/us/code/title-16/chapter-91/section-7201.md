# 16 U.S.C. § 7201 - Definitions

## Text

In this chapter:

(1) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

(2) System The term “system” means the National Landscape Conservation System established by section 7202(a) of this title.

(Pub. L. 111–11, title II, § 2001, Mar. 30, 2009, 123 Stat. 1094.)
