# 16 U.S.C. § 3169 - Valid existing right of access

## Text

Nothing in this subchapter shall be construed to adversely affect any valid existing right of access.

(Pub. L. 96–487, title XI, § 1109, Dec. 2, 1980, 94 Stat. 2464.)
