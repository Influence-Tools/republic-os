# 16 U.S.C. § 4801 to 4805 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Sections 4801 to 4805 were omitted as expired pursuant to section 4807 of this title. Section 4801, Pub. L. 102–335, § 2, Aug. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 859, related to findings, purposes, and definitions. Section 4802, Pub. L. 102–335, § 3, Aug. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 860, related to Pacific yew conservation and management. Section 4803, Pub. L. 102–335, § 4, Aug. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 861, related to research regarding ecology of Pacific yew, development of alternative methods of procuring taxol, and propagation of species. Section 4804, Pub. L. 102–335, § 5, Aug. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 861, related to collection and sale of Pacific yew resources. Section 4805, Pub. L. 102–335, § 6, Aug. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 862, related to construction of chapter with other laws.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Short TitlePub. L. 102–335, § 1(a), Aug. 7, 1992, 106 Stat. 859, provided that Pub. L. 102–335 (this chapter) could be cited as the “Pacific Yew Act”.
