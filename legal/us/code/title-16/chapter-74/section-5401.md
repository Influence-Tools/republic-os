# 16 U.S.C. § 5401 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 103–451, § 2, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4769, which related to congressional findings, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs, by Pub. L. 113–287.
