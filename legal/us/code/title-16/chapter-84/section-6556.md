# 16 U.S.C. § 6556 - Termination of effectiveness

## Text

The authority provided by this subchapter terminates effective October 1, 2023.

(Pub. L. 108–148, title IV, § 406, Dec. 3, 2003, 117 Stat. 1911; Pub. L. 115–334, title VIII, § 8406(a), Dec. 20, 2018, 132 Stat. 4845.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Amendments2018—Pub. L. 115–334 amended section generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this subchapter for each of fiscal years 2004 through 2008.”
