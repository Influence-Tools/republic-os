# 16 U.S.C. § 6541 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, Pub. L. 108–148, title III, § 301, Dec. 3, 2003, 117 Stat. 1902, which provided congressional findings and purposes of title III of Pub. L. 108–148 (enacting this subchapter and section 2103b of this title), was omitted in view of the repeal of sections 2103b and 6542 of this title.
