# 16 U.S.C. § 423l–1 - Short title; definitions

## Text

(a) Short title Sections 423l–1 to 423l–6 of this title may be cited as the “Richmond National Battlefield Park Act of 2000”.

(b) Definitions In sections 423l–1 to 423l–6 of this title:

(1) Battlefield park The term “battlefield park” means the Richmond National Battlefield Park.

(2) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

(Pub. L. 106–511, title V, § 501, Nov. 13, 2000, 114 Stat. 2373.)
