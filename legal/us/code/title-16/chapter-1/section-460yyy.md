# 16 U.S.C. § 460yyy - Definitions

## Text

In this subchapter:

(1) Conservation Area The term “Conservation Area” means the Fort Stanton-Snowy River Cave National Conservation Area established by section 460yyy–1(a) of this title.

(2) Management plan The term “management plan” means the management plan developed for the Conservation Area under section 460yyy–2(c) of this title.

(3) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior, acting through the Director of the Bureau of Land Management.

(Pub. L. 111–11, title II, § 2201, Mar. 30, 2009, 123 Stat. 1099.)
