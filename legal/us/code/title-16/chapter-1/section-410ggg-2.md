# 16 U.S.C. § 410ggg–2 - World War II home front study

## Text

The Secretary shall conduct a theme study of the World War II home front to determine whether other sites in the United States meet the criteria for potential inclusion in the National Park System in accordance with section 100507 of title 54.

(Pub. L. 106–352, § 4, Oct. 24, 2000, 114 Stat. 1372.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification In text, “section 100507 of title 54” substituted for “section 8 of Public Law 91–383 (16 U.S.C. 1a–5)” on authority of Pub. L. 113–287, § 6(e), Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3272, which Act enacted Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs.
