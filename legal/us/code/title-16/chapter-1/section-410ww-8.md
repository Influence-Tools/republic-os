# 16 U.S.C. § 410ww–8 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this part.

(Pub. L. 102–419, title I, § 109, Oct. 16, 1992, 106 Stat. 2143; Pub. L. 106–356, § 2(b), Oct. 24, 2000, 114 Stat. 1391.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Amendments2000—Pub. L. 106–356 struck out “: Provided, That the amount to be appropriated for the operation, development or restoration of non-federally owned properties within the boundaries of the park shall not exceed $200,000” after “this part”.
