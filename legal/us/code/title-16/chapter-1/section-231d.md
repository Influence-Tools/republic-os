# 16 U.S.C. § 231d - Repeal of inconsistent laws

## Text

All Acts or parts of Acts inconsistent with this subchapter are repealed to the extent of such inconsistency.

(Aug. 10, 1939, ch. 640, § 5, 53 Stat. 1342.)
