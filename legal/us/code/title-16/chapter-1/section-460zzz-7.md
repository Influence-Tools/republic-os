# 16 U.S.C. § 460zzz–7 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 111–11, title II, § 2408, Mar. 30, 2009, 123 Stat. 1108.)
