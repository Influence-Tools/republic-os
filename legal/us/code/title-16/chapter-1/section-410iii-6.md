# 16 U.S.C. § 410iii–6 - Management of Park

## Text

(a) Management plan The Secretary, in consultation with the Commission, shall prepare a management plan for the Park. In particular, the management plan shall contain provisions to address the needs of owners of non-Federal land, including independent nonprofit organizations within the boundaries of the Park.

(b) Submission of plan to Congress Not later than 3 years after December 19, 2002, the Secretary shall submit the management plan for the Park to the Committee on Resources of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Energy and Natural Resources of the Senate.

(Pub. L. 107–373, § 8, Dec. 19, 2002, 116 Stat. 3106.)

## Notes

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries
