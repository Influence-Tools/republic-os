# 16 U.S.C. § 410tt–5 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 102–247, title I, § 107, Feb. 24, 1992, 106 Stat. 37.)
