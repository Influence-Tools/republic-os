# 16 U.S.C. § 410ccc–26 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 103–449, title IV, § 406, Nov. 2, 1994, 108 Stat. 4765.)
