# 16 U.S.C. § 410kkk–3 - References

## Text

Any reference in any law (other than this subchapter), regulation, document, record, map or other paper of the United States to “Fort Clatsop National Memorial” shall be considered a reference to the “Lewis and Clark National Historical Park”.

(Pub. L. 108–387, title I, § 105(b), Oct. 30, 2004, 118 Stat. 2236.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

References in TextThis subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of Pub. L. 108–387, Oct. 30, 2004, 118 Stat. 2234, which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Short Title note set out under section 410kkk of this title and Tables.
