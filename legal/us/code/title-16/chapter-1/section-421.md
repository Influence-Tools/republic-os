# 16 U.S.C. § 421 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 355, § 1, 37 Stat. 442; July 26, 1947, ch. 343, title II, § 205(a), 61 Stat. 501, related to vacancies occurring in commissions in charge of parks. Section was restated as section 103303 of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs, by Pub. L. 113–287, § 3, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3166.
