# 16 U.S.C. § 460rrr–7 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 109–337, § 9, Oct. 12, 2006, 120 Stat. 1782.)
