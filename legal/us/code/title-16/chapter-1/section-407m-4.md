# 16 U.S.C. § 407m–4 - Erection of replica of Graff House; maintenance

## Text

The Secretary is further authorized to erect on the site aforesaid, with donated funds, a replica of the Graff House and to furnish and maintain the same.

(Pub. L. 88–477, § 2, Aug. 21, 1964, 78 Stat. 587.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was not enacted as a part of act June 28, 1948, ch. 687, 62 Stat. 1061, as amended, which comprises this subchapter.
