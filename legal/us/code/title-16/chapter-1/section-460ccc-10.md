# 16 U.S.C. § 460ccc–10 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 101–621, § 12, Nov. 16, 1990, 104 Stat. 3346.)
