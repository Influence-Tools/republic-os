# 16 U.S.C. § 460zz–6 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this part.

(Pub. L. 100–696, title VII, § 707, Nov. 18, 1988, 102 Stat. 4607.)
