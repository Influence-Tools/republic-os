# 16 U.S.C. § 410eee–1 - Definitions

## Text

As used in this subchapter:

(1) Historical park The term “historical park” means the Adams National Historical Park established in section 410eee–2 of this title.

(2) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

(Pub. L. 105–342, § 3, Nov. 2, 1998, 112 Stat. 3201.)
