# 16 U.S.C. § 460l–5a - Repealed. Pub. L. 100–203, title V, § 5201(d)(1), Dec. 22, 1987, 101 Stat. 1330–266

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 96–514, title I, § 100, Dec. 12, 1980, 94 Stat. 2960, provided for revenues received from recreation fee collections by Federal agencies to be paid into the Land and Water Conservation Fund and to be available for appropriation for any and all authorized purposes.
