# 16 U.S.C. § 410iii–12 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 107–373, § 14, Dec. 19, 2002, 116 Stat. 3110.)
