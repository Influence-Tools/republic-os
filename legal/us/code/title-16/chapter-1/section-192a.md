# 16 U.S.C. § 192a - Boundaries revised; excluded lands transferred

## Text

thence southerly to the southwest corner of the northeast quarter of the northeast quarter of said section; thence westerly to the southeast corner of the northwest quarter of the northwest quarter of said section;
