# 16 U.S.C. § 410rr–6 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 101–313, title II, § 207, June 27, 1990, 104 Stat. 279.)
