# 16 U.S.C. § 412 - Omitted

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section, act May 15, 1896, ch. 182, § 2, 29 Stat. 121; July 26, 1947, ch. 343, title II, § 205(a), 61 Stat. 501, related to camps for military instruction; regulations for militia. Section was restated as section 103302 of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs, by Pub. L. 113–287, § 3, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3165.
