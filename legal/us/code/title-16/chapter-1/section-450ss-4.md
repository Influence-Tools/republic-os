# 16 U.S.C. § 450ss–4 - Repealed. Pub. L. 108–199, div. F, title V, § 544(e)(1), Jan. 23, 2004, 118 Stat. 348

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 105–58, § 6, Oct. 9, 1997, 111 Stat. 1264, provided for duties and authorities of Oklahoma City National Memorial Trust.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Effective Date of RepealPub. L. 108–199, div. F, title V, § 544(e)(2), Jan. 23, 2004, 118 Stat. 348, provided that: “The repeal under this subsection [repealing this section] shall take effect upon the transfer of the Memorial property, rights, authorities, and duties pursuant to the amendments made by subsection (d) [amending section 450ss–3 of this title].”
