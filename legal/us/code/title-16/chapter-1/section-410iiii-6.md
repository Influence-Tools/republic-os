# 16 U.S.C. § 410iiii–6 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated $1,250,000 to carry out the purposes of this subchapter including land acquisition and initial development.

(Pub. L. 102–525, title I, § 107, formerly § 106, Oct. 26, 1992, 106 Stat. 3439; renumbered § 107, Pub. L. 117–123, § 3(h)(1), May 12, 2022, 136 Stat. 1199.)
