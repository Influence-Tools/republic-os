# 16 U.S.C. § 426p - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There is hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of sections 426n to 426p of this title.

(Pub. L. 100–205, § 4, formerly § 3, Dec. 23, 1987, 101 Stat. 1433; renumbered § 4, Pub. L. 102–225, § 1(4), Dec. 11, 1991, 105 Stat. 1683.)
