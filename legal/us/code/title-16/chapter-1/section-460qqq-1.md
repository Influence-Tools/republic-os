# 16 U.S.C. § 460qqq–1 - Definitions

## Text

In this subchapter:

(1) Conservation Area The term “Conservation Area” means the Sloan Canyon National Conservation Area established by section 460qqq–2(a) of this title.

(2) Federal parcel The term “Federal parcel” means the parcel of Federal land consisting of approximately 500 acres that is identified as Tract A on the map entitled “Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act” and dated October 1, 2002.

(3) Management plan The term “management plan” means the management plan for the Conservation Area developed under section 460qqq–3(b) of this title.

(4) Map The term “map” means the map entitled “Proposed Sloan Canyon Expansion” and dated May 20, 2024.

(Pub. L. 107–282, title VI, § 603, Nov. 6, 2002, 116 Stat. 2009; Pub. L. 119–91, § 3(a)(1), May 19, 2026, 140 Stat. 822.)
