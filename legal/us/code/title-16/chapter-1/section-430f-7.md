# 16 U.S.C. § 430f–7 - Definitions

## Text

In sections 430f–6 to 430f–12 of this title:

(1) Map The term “Map” means the map entitled “Park Boundary-Corinth Unit”, numbered 304A/80009, and dated April 2007.

(2) Park The term “Park” means the Shiloh National Military Park.

(3) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

(4) Unit The term “Unit” means the Corinth Unit of Shiloh National Military Park established under section 430f–8 of this title.

(Pub. L. 106–271, § 3, Sept. 22, 2000, 114 Stat. 793; Pub. L. 110–161, div. F, title I, § 127(1), Dec. 26, 2007, 121 Stat. 2122.)
