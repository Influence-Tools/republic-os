# 16 U.S.C. § 450ff–6 - Change in name of Fort Vancouver National Monument

## Text

Fort Vancouver National Monument is redesignated Fort Vancouver National Historic Site.

(Pub. L. 87–78, § 4, June 30, 1961, 75 Stat. 197.)
