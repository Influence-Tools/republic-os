# 16 U.S.C. § 460iii–6 - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 103–64, § 7, Aug. 4, 1993, 107 Stat. 310.)
