# 16 U.S.C. § 460rrr–8 - Termination of Commission

## Text

The Commission shall terminate on the date that is 10 years after October 12, 2006.

(Pub. L. 109–337, § 10, Oct. 12, 2006, 120 Stat. 1782.)
