# 16 U.S.C. § 667g–2 - Authorization of appropriations for reimbursement of Commodity Credit Corporation

## Text

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to reimburse the Commodity Credit Corporation for its investment in grain transferred pursuant to sections 667g to 667g–2 of this title.

(Pub. L. 87–152, § 3, Aug. 17, 1961, 75 Stat. 389.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Codification Section was formerly classified to section 449 of Title 7, Agriculture, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
