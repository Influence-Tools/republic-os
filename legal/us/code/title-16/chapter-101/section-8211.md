# 16 U.S.C. § 8211 - Nonapplicability of Federal Advisory Committee Act

## Text

Chapter 10 of title 5 (commonly known as the “Federal Advisory Committee Act”), shall not apply to—

(1) the Board; or

(2) any Partnership.

(Pub. L. 116–188, title II, § 211, Oct. 30, 2020, 134 Stat. 937; Pub. L. 118–198, title II, § 207, Dec. 23, 2024, 138 Stat. 2683.)

## Notes

Editorial Notes

Amendments2024—Pub. L. 118–198 substituted “Chapter 10 of title 5 (commonly known as the ‘Federal Advisory Committee Act’),” for “The Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.)” in introductory provisions.
