# 16 U.S.C. § 1423h - Authorization of appropriations

## Text

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out the functions and responsibilities of the Secretary under this subchapter and the Agreement $1,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2006 through 2010.

(b) Commission There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out functions and responsibilities of the United States Section 11 So in original. Probably should not be capitalized. $150,000 for each of fiscal years 2006 through 2010.

(c) Alaskan cooperative management program There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out this subchapter and the Agreement in Alaska $150,000 for each of fiscal years 2006 through 2010.

(Pub. L. 92–522, title V, § 509, as added Pub. L. 109–479, title IX, § 902(a), Jan. 12, 2007, 120 Stat. 3665.)
