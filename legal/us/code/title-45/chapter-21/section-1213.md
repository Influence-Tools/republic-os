# 45 U.S.C. § 1213 - Conflict with other laws

## Text

The provisions of this chapter shall govern if there is any conflict between this chapter and any other law.

(Pub. L. 97–468, title VI, § 614, Jan. 14, 1983, 96 Stat. 2577.)
