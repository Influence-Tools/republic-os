# 45 U.S.C. § 916 - Repealed. Pub. L. 104–88, title III, § 328, Dec. 29, 1995, 109 Stat. 952

## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 96–101, § 18, Nov. 4, 1979, 93 Stat. 746; Pub. L. 96–254, title I, § 116, May 30, 1980, 94 Stat. 405, provided that this chapter be in lieu of directed service on any line of the Milwaukee Railroad with certain exceptions.
