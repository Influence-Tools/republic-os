## Notes

Section, Pub. L. 93–236, title VI, § 603, Jan. 2, 1974, 87 Stat. 1023, related to freight rates for recyclables.

Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries

Investigation of Discriminatory Freight Rates for Transportation of Recyclable or Recycled MaterialsPub.
