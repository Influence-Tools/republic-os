# Wyoming HB 43 (2017) — Board of insurance examiners-repeal.

AN ACT relating to insurance producers and adjusters; requiring the commissioner of insurance to conduct a review of licensing examination procedures; establishing a review panel to assist in the commissioner's review of licensing exam procedures; authorizing reimbursement of expenses; repealing the state board of insurance agent examiners; making conforming amendments; and providing for an effective date.
