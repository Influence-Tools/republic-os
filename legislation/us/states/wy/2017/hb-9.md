# Wyoming HB 9 (2017) — Student ownership and privacy rights.

AN ACT relating to public education; prohibiting the conveyance or transfer of ownership rights of writings and other communications created by a student at the University of Wyoming or any community college in the state as specified; providing for an expectation of privacy in student writings and other communications as specified; specifying applicability; and providing for an effective date.
