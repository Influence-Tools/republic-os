# Wyoming SF 92 (2017) — Local governments-plan review authority.

AN ACT relating to fire protection; authorizing local governmental entities to assume sole plan review authority; permitting assistance by the state fire marshal; making conforming amendments; clarifying cross references related to enforcement, inspections, plan review authority and appeals; and providing for an effective date.
