# Wyoming SF 44 (2017) — Speech pathology and audiology.

AN ACT relating to speech pathology and audiology; modifying requirements for licensure; modifying provisions for suspension, revocation and renewal of licenses; expanding list of persons eligible to serve on the board of speech pathology and audiology; providing for a vice chairman of the board; specifying functions of the board; modifying fees; modifying applicability of licensing provisions; providing for and revising definitions; making conforming amendments; removing obsolete language; and providing for an effective date.
