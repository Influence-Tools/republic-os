# Wyoming HB 4 (2017) — NRC agreement state amendments.

AN ACT relating to the nuclear regulatory agreement and nuclear regulatory functions of the state developed under the agreement; requiring the adoption of a fee structure; permitting the assessment of fees as specified; clarifying scope of agreement and materials subject to state regulation; amending definitions; excluding certain laboratories from state regulation; clarifying reciprocity of out of state licenses; amending requirements related to financial assurance arrangements; authorizing the director to impound materials as specified; making conforming amendments; and providing for an effective date.
