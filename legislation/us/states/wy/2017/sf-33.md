# Wyoming SF 33 (2017) — Computer extortion.

AN ACT relating to offenses against computer property; creating the criminal offense of computer extortion; specifying elements of the offense; providing penalties; expanding the list of computer crimes to be investigated by the division of criminal investigation; and providing for an effective date.
