# Wyoming SF 27 (2017) — State archives-amendments.

AN ACT relating to archives; amending provisions related to the reproduction, storage, transfer and disposition of public records; authorizing state archives and the records committee to establish standards and best practices as specified; amending the definition of a public record to provide for electronic records; amending and repealing obsolete language; and providing for an effective date.
