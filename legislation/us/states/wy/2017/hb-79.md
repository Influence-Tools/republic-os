# Wyoming HB 79 (2017) — State funds equity investments pool.

AN ACT relating to public funds; creating the pool A investment account; specifying the maximum percentage of the pool which may be invested in equities including stocks of corporations; specifying state funds which are to be included in the pool; providing conforming amendments for the investment of permanent funds of the state; and providing for an effective date.
