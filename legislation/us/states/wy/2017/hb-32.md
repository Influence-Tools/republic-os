# Wyoming HB 32 (2017) — Ski Safety Act.

AN ACT relating to skiing; imposing duties on skiers and ski area operators; providing for skier safety and liability as specified; establishing assumption of risk for skiers; removing skiing in ski areas from the listing of activities which constitute a sport or recreational opportunity under the Recreation Safety Act; and providing for an effective date.
