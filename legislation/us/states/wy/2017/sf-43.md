# Wyoming SF 43 (2017) — Alcoholic beverages revisions.

AN ACT relating to alcoholic beverages; repealing requirement license and permit fees be paid in cash or by certified check; repealing restrictions on license and permit holding by a mayor, city or town council member, county commissioner and law enforcement officer; modifying requirements for special malt beverage permits for public auditoriums, civic centers or events centers; modifying provisions related to hours of operations; modifying minimum purchase requirements for permit and license holders; modifying and clarifying application of population formula for licenses; repealing certain preference right; repealing limitation on issuance of restaurant licenses; repealing provisions related to prohibited acts in a licensed building or premises; repealing obsolete provisions; conforming provisions; and providing for an effective date.
