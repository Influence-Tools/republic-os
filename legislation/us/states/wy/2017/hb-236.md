# Wyoming HB 236 (2017) — School finance-omnibus education funding.

AN ACT relating to school finance; an omnibus bill addressing education funding; revising the existing education funding formula; providing additional revenue for education by transferring existing revenues; providing for moratoria on new alternative schools and purchases and leases of school buses and other restrictions on education expenditures; specifying various dates for determination of funds to be distributed and for other implementation purposes; establishing a joint select committee on education funding; requiring reports; providing appropriations; modifying previous appropriations; repealing certain distributions related to education funding; providing conformance with other laws; providing sunset dates; and providing for effective dates.
