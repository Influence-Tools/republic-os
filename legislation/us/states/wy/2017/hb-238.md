# Wyoming HB 238 (2017) — Nude image of a minor disseminated or possessed by a minor.

AN ACT relating to crimes and offenses; creating offenses for the dissemination or possession of a nude image of a minor by a minor; specifying elements of the offenses; providing definitions; providing penalties; and providing for an effective date.
