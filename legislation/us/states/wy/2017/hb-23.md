# Wyoming HB 23 (2017) — Secretary of state's office-amendments.

AN ACT relating to the secretary of state; modifying time period, publication and notice requirements relating to business entities; amending requirements for the secretary of state to publish certain documents; removing requirement for the secretary of state to distribute copies of the election code to county clerks; accordingly repealing the requirement that county clerks distribute copies of election code to election judges; providing an appropriation; and providing for an effective date.
