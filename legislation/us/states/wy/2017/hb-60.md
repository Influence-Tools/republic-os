# Wyoming HB 60 (2017) — State investment policy.

AN ACT relating to public funds; amending restrictions on the investment of permanent funds; providing for a strategy to evaluate the risks of investments; requiring the state loan and investment board to establish metrics for managing and mitigating the risks of investments; requiring evaluations of the risks of investments as specified; requiring reports; and providing for an effective date.
