# Wyoming HB 2 (2017) — Storage tanks.

AN ACT relating to storage tanks; providing licensure requirements for tank operators, installers and testers; providing closure requirements for temporarily out of use tanks; modifying provisions related to financial assurance coverage; clarifying provisions related to tank fees; providing replacement requirements for specified piping; requiring interstitial monitoring for the lifetime of specified tanks and piping; requiring automatic fuel shutoffs as specified; amending definitions; conforming provisions; providing rulemaking authority; and providing for an effective date.
