# North Dakota SB 2050 (68) — AN ACT to create and enact a new section to chapter 54-24.2 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to the ability of the state librarian to recalculate grant payments to public libraries; to amend and reenact sections 40-38-09, 54-24-09, 54-24.2-03, and 54-24.4-05 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to the public annual report, distribution of state publications to libraries, the computation of mill levies, and the duties of the library coordinating council; to repeal sections 40-38-10 and 54-24.2-02.2 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to a political subdivision's ability to establish a library without an election and local maintenance efforts of public libraries; and to provide an effective date.

Relating to the ability of the state librarian to recalculate grant payments to public libraries; to amend and reenact sections 40‑38‑09, 54‑24‑09, 54‑24.2‑03, and 54‑24.4‑05 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to the public annual report, distribution of state publications to libraries, the computation of mill levies, and the duties of the library coordinating council; to repeal sections 40‑38‑10 and 54‑24.2‑02.2 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to a political subdivision's ability to establish a library without an election and local maintenance efforts of public libraries; and to provide an effective date.
