Relating to voluntary liquidation of credit unions; to amend and reenact section 6‑01‑04.1 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to the removal of officers, directors, and employees of financial corporations or institutions; and to repeal chapter 6‑06.1 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to voluntary liquidation of credit unions.
