# North Dakota SB 2090 (68) — AN ACT to create and enact chapter 13-12 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to residential mortgage lenders; to amend and reenact sections 13-04.1-01, 13-04.1-02.1, 13-04.1-03, 13-04.1-08, 13-04.1-08.1, 13-04.1-09, 13-04.1-09.3, 13-04.1-10, 13-04.1-13, 13-04.1-14, and 13-04.1-17 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to money brokers; to repeal section 13-04.1-16 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to call reports; to provide a penalty; and to provide for application.

Relating to residential mortgage lenders; to amend and reenact sections 13‑04.1‑01, 13‑04.1‑02.1, 13‑04.1‑03, 13‑04.1‑08, 13‑04.1‑08.1, 13‑04.1‑09, 13‑04.1‑09.3, 13‑04.1‑10, 13‑04.1‑13, 13‑04.1‑14, and 13‑04.1‑17 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to money brokers; to repeal section 13‑04.1‑16 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to call reports; to provide a penalty; and to provide for application.
