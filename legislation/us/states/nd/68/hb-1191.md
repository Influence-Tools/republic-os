# North Dakota HB 1191 (68) — AN ACT to amend and reenact subsection 2 of section 12-60-24 and sections 49-07-01.1, 51-05.1-01, 51-05.1-01.2, 51-05.1-04.1, and 51-05.1-05 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to criminal history record checks of licenses, the registration of auctioneers, and cross-references; to repeal sections 51-05.1-01.1, 51-05.1-02, 51-05.1-02.1, and 51-05.1-03 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to auctioneers' and clerks' licenses; and to provide an effective date.

Relating to criminal history record checks of licenses, the registration of auctioneers, and cross-references; to repeal sections 51‑05.1‑01.1, 51‑05.1‑02, 51‑05.1‑02.1, and 51‑05.1‑03 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to auctioneers' and clerks' licenses; and to provide an effective date.
