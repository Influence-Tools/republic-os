# North Dakota SB 2051 (68) — AN ACT to create and enact a new section to chapters 25-03.2 and 50-06 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to fingerprint criminal history record investigations for psychiatric residential facilities for children and for shelter care programs; and to amend and reenact subdivision f of subsection 2 of section 12-60-24 and sections 25-03.2-03, 25-03.2-04, 50-06-01.9, 50-11-06.8, 50-12-03, and 50-25.1-11.1 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to fingerprint criminal history record checks for certified family foster home for children providers and psychiatric residential treatment facility for children, identifying who the department of health and human services may require criminal history record checks from, and criminal history record checks for volunteers and students for field placement at child-placing agencies and children's advocacy centers.

Relating to fingerprint criminal history record investigations for psychiatric residential facilities for children and for shelter care programs; and to amend and reenact subdivision f of subsection 2 of section 12‑60‑24 and sections 25‑03.2‑03, 25‑03.2‑04, 50‑06‑01.9, 50‑11‑06.8, 50‑12‑03, and 50‑25.1‑11.1 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to fingerprint criminal history record checks for certified family foster home for children providers and psychiatric residential treatment facility for children, identifying who the department of health and human services may require criminal history record checks from, and criminal history record checks for volunteers and students for field placement at child-placing agencies and children's advocacy centers.
