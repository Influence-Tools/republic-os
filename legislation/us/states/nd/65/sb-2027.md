# North Dakota SB 2027 (65) — AN ACT to create and enact chapters 4.1‑23, 4.1‑33, 4.1‑34, 4.1‑35, 4.1‑36, 4.1‑37, 4.1‑38, 4.1‑39, and 4.1‑40 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to revisions of agriculture laws regarding plant pests, pesticides, chemigation regulation, pesticide container disposal, anhydrous ammonia facilities, anhydrous ammonia risk management, crop protection products, and the fertilizer and soil conditioner law; to amend and reenact subsection 19 of section 19‑02.1‑01 and sections 23‑01‑01.1 and 23‑01‑25 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to the definition of a pesticide chemical and the state department of health; to repeal chapters 19‑18, 19‑20.1, 19‑20.2, and 19‑20.3 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to pesticides, fertilizers and soil conditioners, anhydrous ammonia facilities, and anhydrous ammonia risk management; and to provide a penalty.
