# North Dakota SB 2037 (66) — AN ACT to create and enact chapters 38‑23 and 38‑24 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to the disposal and storage of high‑level radioactive waste and subsurface storage and retrieval of nonhydrocarbons; to amend and reenact sections 12.1‑06.1‑01 and 38‑19‑09 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to the definition of illegal transportation or disposal of radioactive waste material or hazardous waste and disposition of unusable products; to repeal chapter 23‑20.2 of the North Dakota Century Code, relating to the disposal of nuclear waste material; to provide a penalty; and to provide a continuing appropriation.
