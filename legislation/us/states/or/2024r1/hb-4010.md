# Oregon HB 4010 (2024R1) — Relating to health care; prescribing an effective date.

Digest: The Act makes changes to laws about prescription drugs and some health care providers. The Act says the Oregon State Hospital does not have to do certain things about staffing. The Act changes the term "physician assistant" to "physician associate." (Flesch Readability Score: 62.1).
