# Oregon SB 1568 (2024R1) — Relating to a prevailing rate of wage for electrical workers; and prescribing an effective date.

Digest: Makes pay for electricians equal to the pay in a union contract in parts of the state where there is a union contract and the union is the only one that speaks for electricians. Takes effect 91 days after session ends (Flesch Readability Score: 60.6).
