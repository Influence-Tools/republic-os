# Oregon SB 1509 (2024R1) — Relating to Columbia River fisheries; declaring an emergency.

<b>Digest: The Act stops the lottery for Columbia River gillnet fishing permits. The Act directs an agency to start a work group to make suggestions on creating a program for buying the permits from willing sellers. The Act directs the agency to report on the suggestions of the work group on a due date. The Act goes into effect when the Governor signs it. (Flesch Readability Score: 61.9).</b>
