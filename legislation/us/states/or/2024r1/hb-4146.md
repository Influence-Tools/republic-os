# Oregon HB 4146 (2024R1) — Relating to victims; declaring an emergency.

<b>Digest: The Act allows petitions for certain abuse court orders to be filed in the county in which the abuse occurred. The Act also changes an element of a crime related to violating privacy. The Act goes into effect when the Governor signs it. (Flesch Readability Score: 60.4).</b>
