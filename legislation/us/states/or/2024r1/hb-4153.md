# Oregon HB 4153 (2024R1) — Relating to artificial intelligence; declaring an emergency.

Digest: Creates a task force to look for and find words and meanings related to artificial intelligence that may be used in laws. Makes task force report its findings on or before December 1, 2024. (Flesch Readability Score: 60.1).
