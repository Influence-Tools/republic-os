# Oregon SB 1585 (2024R1) — Relating to Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program benefits; prescribing an effective date.

Digest: The Act requires DHS to set up a task force and come up with a plan for a program that allows people who receive SNAP aid to be able to use the aid to buy hot foods and hot foods that are ready to eat. The Act says who must be on the task force. The Act requires DHS to take steps to carry out the program. (Flesch Readability Score: 87).
