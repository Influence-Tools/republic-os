# Oregon SB 1523 (2024R1) — Relating to records of live birth; and declaring an emergency.

Digest: The Act allows an adult who applies to a state agency to access a sealed original birth record to ask the state agency to add or change the name of a biological parent. Takes effect when the Governor signs it. (Flesch Readability Score: 60.1).
