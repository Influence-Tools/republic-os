# Oregon SB 1502 (2024R1) — Relating to public meetings of educational institution boards.

<b>Digest: Requires most public schools and college boards to video record their meetings and post the meeting recordings on their websites and social media sites. This does not apply if a meeting is private or if a school district is very small. (Flesch Readability Score: 61.7).</b>
