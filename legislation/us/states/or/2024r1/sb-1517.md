# Oregon SB 1517 (2024R1) — Relating to the urban flood safety and water quality district; prescribing an effective date.

Digest: The Act would let the urban flood safety and water quality district impose an annual charge on lands that are benefited by district works. The Act would let the district impose a fee on cities and counties for district costs not covered by the annual charge. The Act would change how the district winds up other water-related districts. (Flesch Readability Score: 63.2).
