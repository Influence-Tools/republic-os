# Oregon HB 2698 (2025R1) — Relating to homeownership goal; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes a statewide goal for homeownership. Requires the Housing and Community Services Department to track goal progress [<i>as part of the Oregon Housing Needs Analysis</i>]. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
