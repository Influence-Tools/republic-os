# Oregon HB 2800 (2025R1) — Relating to professional employer organizations under workers' compensation law; and prescribing an effective date.

</b> </i>] Changes the term "worker leasing company" to "professional employer organization" in statute to reflect the use of [<i>PEOs by businesses to coemploy workers</i>] <b>agreements under which professional employer organizations and client employers allocate employer responsibilities for some or all of the client employer's workers</b>. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
