# Oregon HB 2935 (2025R1) — Relating to pretrial release.

Directs the Oregon Criminal Justice Commission to study pretrial release and include certain data in the study. Requires the commission to provide the results of the study to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to the judiciary no later than May 1, 2027. Sunsets January 2, 2028.
