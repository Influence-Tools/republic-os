# Oregon HB 3474 (2025R1) — Relating to the Secretary of State conducting a study of changes to the U.S. Postal Service.

Requires the Secretary of State to study changes to the United States Postal Service. Requires the secretary to analyze how any changes may impact Oregon's vote-by-mail system. Requires the secretary to submit a report to the Legislative Assembly. Sunsets on January 2, 2027.
