# Oregon HB 2563 (2025R1) — Relating to explanations required in connection with premium increases upon renewals of certain insurance policies; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires an insurer to provide to an insured a clear and reasonable explanation of the reasons for any increase in the premium amount for a qualified policy upon renewal of the qualified policy. Specifies standards for a clear and reasonable explanation. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
