# Oregon HB 2134 (2025R1) — Relating to termination of fixed term residential tenancies.

Allows tenant to terminate a tenancy during the fixed term without penalty upon 30 days' notice if the landlord delivers certain notices terminating the tenancy in 90 days or more. [<i>Declares an emergency, effective on passage.</i>].
