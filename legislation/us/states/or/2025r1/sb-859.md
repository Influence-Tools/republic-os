# Oregon SB 859 (2025R1) — Relating to accounting adjustments concerning the paid leave program; and prescribing an effective date.

Authorizes the Director of the Employment Department to compromise or adjust certain debts and overpayments relating to the paid family and medical leave program. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
