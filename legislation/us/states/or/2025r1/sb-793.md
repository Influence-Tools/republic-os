# Oregon SB 793 (2025R1) — Relating to easements on state land; and declaring an emergency.

</b> </i>] Directs the Director of the Department of State Lands to establish application fees and compensation rates for easements on state land<b> located within the territorial sea</b>. Directs the Department of State Lands to submit a progress report to committees of the Legislative Assembly by February 15, [<i>2027</i>]<b> 2026</b>. [<i>Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.</i>] <b>Declares an emergency, effective on passage.</b>
