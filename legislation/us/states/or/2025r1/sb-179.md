# Oregon SB 179 (2025R1) — Relating to landowner immunity.

Makes permanent the temporary changes made to the landowner immunity laws by chapter 64, Oregon Laws 2024.<b> Extends local government immunity to explicitly include claims arising via the use of nonhorse equines.</b>
