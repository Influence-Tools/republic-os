# Oregon HB 2465 (2025R1) — Relating to the authority of supervising officers; and declaring an emergency.

</b> </i>] Provides that the unlawful departure of a person from the custody of a parole and probation officer constitutes the crime of escape. Authorizes parole and probation officers to view digital copies of driver license photographs retained by the Department of Transportation. <b>Declares an emergency, effective on passage.</b>
