# Oregon SB 360 (2025R1) — Relating to machine-powered pesticide application devices; and prescribing an effective date.

Creates a pesticide licensing exemption for certain employees using a battery-powered device to apply pesticides. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
