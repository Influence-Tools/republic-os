# Oregon SB 5517 (2025R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the Oregon Department of Emergency Management; and declaring an emergency.

Appropriates moneys from the General Fund to the Oregon Department of Emergency Management for certain biennial expenses. Limits certain biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by the department. Limits certain biennial expenditures by the department from federal funds. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
