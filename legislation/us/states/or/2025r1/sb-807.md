# Oregon SB 807 (2025R1) — Relating to the Oregon National Guard; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes the Adjutant General to pay a reenlistment or retention bonus to a member of the Oregon National Guard. Requires a member to repay the bonus if the member fails to complete the period of service or conditions agreed to in exchange for the bonus. Provides for exceptions. <b>Declares an emergency, effective on passage.</b>
