# Oregon HB 3038 (2025R1) — Relating to education; and declaring an emergency.

Adjusts purposes and eligible recipients of grants related to STEM education. Abolishes the Connecting Education to Careers Account. Clarifies the method for funding approved recovery schools that are also public charter schools. Provides that children who have a 504 Plan or who are deaf, hard of hearing or deaf-blind may attend the Oregon School for the Deaf<b> and that a parent or guardian must consent to a placement in the Oregon School for the Deaf</b>. Prescribes requirements related to comprehensive safety programs in schools. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
