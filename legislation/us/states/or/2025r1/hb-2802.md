# Oregon HB 2802 (2025R1) — Relating to lump sum payments of permanent partial disability awards.

[<i>Requires</i>] <b>Establishes the circumstances in which</b> an insurer <b>is required</b> to make a lump sum payment of a permanent partial disability award [<i>if the worker waives reconsideration of the notice of closure or the award has become final by operation of law</i>].
