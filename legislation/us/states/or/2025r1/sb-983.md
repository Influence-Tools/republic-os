# Oregon SB 983 (2025R1) — Relating to the adoption of local budgets; and declaring an emergency.

Permits local public officials to participate in discussion and debate and to vote on the adoption of a local budget that includes compensation for the public official or a relative of the public official after announcing an actual conflict of interest.<b> Declares an emergency, effective on passage.</b>
