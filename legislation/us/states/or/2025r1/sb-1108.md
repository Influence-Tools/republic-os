# Oregon SB 1108 (2025R1) — Relating to paid time off for employees to donate blood.

</b> </i>] [<i>Requires employers to allow employees to take up to four hours of blood donation leave per calendar year without the loss of time, pay or benefits.</i>] <b>Makes blood donation that is made in connection with a voluntary program that is approved or accredited by the American Association of Blood Banks or the American Red Cross a permissible use of sick time.</b>
