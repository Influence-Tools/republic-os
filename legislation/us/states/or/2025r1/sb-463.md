# Oregon SB 463 (2025R1) — Relating to reporting on the soundness of the state's Insurance Fund; and declaring an emergency.

Requires the Oregon Department of Administrative Services to submit by January [<i>15</i>]<b> 31</b> of each even-numbered year a report to the Legislative Assembly concerning the soundness of the state's Insurance Fund. Specifies the contents of the report and directs agencies of state government and participants in the fund to cooperate with and assist the department in carrying out the department's duties under the Act. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
