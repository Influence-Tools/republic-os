# Oregon SB 864 (2025R1) — Relating to bonding requirements for landscape contracting businesses.

</b> </i>] Sets [<i>the</i>]<b> a</b> bonding requirement<b> of $20,000</b> for applicants for a landscape contracting business license or <b>for</b> licensed landscape contracting businesses [<i>at $20,000 if the applicant or licensed landscape contracting business charges $100,000 or less for landscape jobs that the applicant or licensed landscape contracting business undertakes regularly and at $50,000 if the applicant or licensed landscape contracting business charges more than $100,000 for any landscape job</i>].
