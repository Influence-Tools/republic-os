# Oregon HB 2370 (2025R1) — Relating to fees imposed by the Public Utility Commission; and declaring an emergency.

Increases the annual fee amount that the Public Utility Commission may impose on public utilities for the purpose of defraying costs of the commission. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
