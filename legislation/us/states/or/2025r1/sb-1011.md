# Oregon SB 1011 (2025R1) — Relating to Public Law 280; and declaring an emergency.

Allows a federally recognized Indian tribe located in this state to submit a request to the Governor for retrocession of Public Law 280 jurisdiction. Prescribes the Governor's duties. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
