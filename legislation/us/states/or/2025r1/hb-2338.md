# Oregon HB 2338 (2025R1) — Relating to tax preparation professionals.

</b> </i>] Creates a new tax professional certification known as a [<i>certified</i>]<b> registered</b> tax aide. Authorizes [<i>certified</i>]<b> registered</b> tax aides to assist certain tax professionals in preparing tax returns. Prescribes requirements for continuing education, training and fees. Directs the State Board of Tax Practitioners to oversee certification of [<i>certified</i>]<b> registered</b> tax aides.
