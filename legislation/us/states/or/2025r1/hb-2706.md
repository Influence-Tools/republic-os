# Oregon HB 2706 (2025R1) — Relating to businesses regulated by the Department of Transportation.

</b> </i>] [<i>Directs the Department of Transportation to provide two days' notice to vehicle dealers and dismantlers before inspecting records unless the department is responding to a complaint.</i>] <b>Extends a dismantler certificate validity period by one year. Increases the fee for a certificate.</b>
