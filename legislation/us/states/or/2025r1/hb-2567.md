# Oregon HB 2567 (2025R1) — Relating to heat pump programs.

Requires an eligible entity that receives a grant under the Heat Pump Deployment Program to agree to use best practices to maintain the security and confidentiality of personal information of individuals who apply for or receive financial assistance under the program from the eligible entity. <b>Allows the State Department of Energy to award additional grant moneys to an eligible entity utilizing an existing performance agreement if additional funds become available.</b> Allows the State Department of Energy to provide an additional incentive amount under the rental home heat pump program for contractors who install heat pumps in rural or frontier communities. Extends the sunset of the rental home heat pump program from January 2, 2026, to January 2, 2032. Requires the department to provide a report each even-numbered year to the Legislative Assembly on the grants and rebates under the program.
