# Oregon SB 832 (2025R1) — Relating to civil penalties for laws implemented by the State Department of Agriculture.

Makes certain changes related to civil penalties for violations related to disease control. Authorizes the imposition of civil penalties for violations related to swine. Authorizes the imposition of civil penalties for violations related to commercial animal feeds.
