# Oregon HB 2551 (2025R1) — Relating to security on campus.

Directs the governing board of a public university to [<i>request and review</i>] <b>ensure that</b> background information about special campus security officers <b>is requested from the Department of Public Safety Standards and Training and reviewed</b> before commissioning the officer. Allows the department [<i>of Public Safety Standards and Training</i>] to share information about applicants with a public university.
