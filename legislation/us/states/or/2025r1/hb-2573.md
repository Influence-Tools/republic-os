# Oregon HB 2573 (2025R1) — Relating to emergency medical services for residents in long term care settings; and prescribing an effective date.

Modifies the requirements for the Senior Emergency Medical Services Innovation Program. Extends sunsets for the program and the prohibition on local government regulation of long term care facilities and residential care facilities. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
