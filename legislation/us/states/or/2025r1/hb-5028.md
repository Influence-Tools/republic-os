# Oregon HB 5028 (2025R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the State Board of Pharmacy; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by the State Board of Pharmacy. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
