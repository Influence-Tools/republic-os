# Oregon HB 3410 (2025R1) — Relating to health care; and declaring an emergency.

Amends portions of an Act that restricts management services organizations from owning a majority interest in professional medical entities and that prohibits noncompetition agreements, nondisparagement agreements and nondisclosure agreements between medical licensees and other persons. Makes a prohibition on the use of noncompetition agreements retroactive. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
