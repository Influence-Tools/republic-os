# Oregon SB 735 (2025R1) — Relating to the application of abbreviated school day program requirements to students who are enrolled in a public charter school; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that abbreviated school day program requirements do not apply to students in public charter schools if certain conditions are met. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
