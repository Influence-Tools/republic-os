# Oregon SB 110 (2025R1) — Relating to revenue.

</b> </i>] [<i>Requires the Legislative Revenue Officer to study the personal income tax system. Directs the Legislative Revenue Officer to submit findings to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to revenue not later than December 1, 2026.</i>] <b>Increases the amount of incremental baseball tax revenues that the Oregon Department of Administrative Services may grant for the purpose of constructing a major league baseball stadium. Increases the minimum cost of the stadium. Increases the salary threshold for purposes of calculating incremental baseball tax revenues.</b>
