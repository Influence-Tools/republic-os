# Oregon HB 5036 (2025R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the Real Estate Agency; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by the Real Estate Agency. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
