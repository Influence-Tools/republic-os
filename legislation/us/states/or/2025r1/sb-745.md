# Oregon SB 745 (2025R1) — Relating to the frequency of meetings regarding abbreviated school day programs for students who are terminally ill; and declaring an emergency.

Allows the parent or foster parent of a terminally ill student to consent to meeting once every year to review the student's abbreviated school day program. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
