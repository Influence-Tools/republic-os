# Oregon HB 5022 (2025R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the Oregon Medical Board; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by the Oregon Medical Board. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
