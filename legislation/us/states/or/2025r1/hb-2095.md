# Oregon HB 2095 (2025R1) — Relating to revenue; and prescribing an effective date.

</b> </i>] Modifies the tax credit for research conducted by a semiconductor company. Omits an obsolete provision concerning the election of alternative incremental credit under related federal law.<b> Aligns dates in tax credit provisions with underlying sunset dates for credits.</b> Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
