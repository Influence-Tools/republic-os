# Oregon HB 3936 (2025R1) — Relating to the security of state assets.

</b> </i>] Prohibits any hardware, software or service that uses artificial intelligence from being installed or downloaded onto or used or accessed by state information technology assets if the artificial intelligence is developed or owned by a [<i>corporate entity that is incorporated or registered under the laws of a foreign country</i>]<b> covered vendor</b>. Provides for exceptions.
