# Oregon HB 2332 (2025R1) — Relating to air curtain incinerators; and prescribing an effective date.

Prohibits the Department of Environmental Quality from requiring a Title V operating permit for certain air curtain incinerators. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
