# Oregon HB 3605 (2025R1) — Relating to unlawful practices in home solicitation sales; and prescribing an effective date.

Punishes a violation of the laws that regulate home solicitation sales as an unlawful practice under the Unlawful Trade Practices Act. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
