# Oregon HB 2586 (2025R1) — Relating to nonresident tuition exemption for asylum seekers; and declaring an emergency.

Permits an asylum seeker who is a student at a public university in this state <b>or Oregon Health and Science University</b> to receive an exemption from nonresident tuition and fees. <b>Declares an emergency, effective on passage.</b>
