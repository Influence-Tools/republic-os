# Oregon HB 3449 (2025R1) — Relating to a pilot program to provide support to foster child students; and declaring an emergency.

Allows moneys available for a pilot program to provide support to foster child students to be used in other schools of the grantee's school district. [<i>Requires collaboration between the Department of Education and the Department of Human Services for the administration of the pilot program.</i>] Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
