# Oregon SB 684 (2025R1) — Relating to housing; and prescribing an effective date.

</b> </i>] [<i>Directs the Housing and Community Services Department to establish a program to issue low-interest short-term loans for the construction of mixed income housing.</i>] Amends the definition of "residential housing" to include certain mixed income housing. Requires the<b> Housing and Community Services</b> Department to adopt rules for long-term financing of residential housing on or before January 1, 2027. Requires the department to report on <b>recommendations for mixed income housing by November 15, 2025, and report on </b>updates to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to housing by September 15, 2026. [<i>Establishes the Mixed Income Public Development Loan Fund to be continually appropriated to the department for those purposes.</i>] Reduces limitations on the mixed income housing that housing authorities may finance, develop, own, operate or manage. <b>Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.</b>
