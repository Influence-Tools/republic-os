# Oregon HB 2453 (2025R1) — Relating to education; and declaring an emergency.

</b> </i>] [<i>Requires the Department of Education to study the adequacy of public education in this state. Directs the department to submit findings to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to education not later than September 15, 2026.</i>] <b>Removes the requirement that an educational equity advisory committee must advise a school district board. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.</b>
