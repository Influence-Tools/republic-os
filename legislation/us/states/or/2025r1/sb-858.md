# Oregon SB 858 (2025R1) — Relating to the administration of the family and medical leave insurance program; and prescribing an effective date.

[<i>Requires the Director of the Employment Department to conduct periodic assessments to determine the solvency of the Paid Family and Medical Leave Insurance Fund. Specifies the actions that the director may take to maintain or restore solvency when the director determines there is a risk of insolvency.</i>] Makes technical changes to statutory provisions concerning the administration of the family and medical leave insurance program. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
