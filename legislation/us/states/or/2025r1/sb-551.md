# Oregon SB 551 (2025R1) — Relating to plastic waste.

</b> </i>] Prohibits restaurants and retailers from providing single-use checkout bags to consumers. [<i>Prohibits food and beverage providers and convenience stores from providing single-use plastic utensils or single-use plastic condiment packaging to consumers unless requested.</i>] [<i>Prohibits lodging establishments from providing small plastic personal health or beauty product containers unless requested.</i>].
