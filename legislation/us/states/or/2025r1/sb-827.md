# Oregon SB 827 (2025R1) — Relating to the Oregon Solar and Storage Rebate Program.

Expands the Oregon Solar and Storage Rebate Program to offer rebates for energy storage systems that will be paired at the time of installation with previously purchased and installed solar electric systems.
