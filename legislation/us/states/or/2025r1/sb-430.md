# Oregon SB 430 (2025R1) — Relating to disclosures required in connection with online transactions.

Prohibits a person that offers or sells goods or services online [<i>to a resident of this state</i>] from advertising, displaying or offering a price for the goods or services that does not include all fees or charges that a purchaser must pay to complete a transaction for the goods or services, other than taxes or fees that a governmental body imposes on the transaction<b>,</b> [<i>or</i>] reasonable charges that the person actually incurs to ship the goods or provide the services<b> or a service fee that is calculated according to distance or a purchaser's selections</b>. Specifies exemptions. Punishes a violation of the Act as a unlawful practice under the Unlawful Trade Practices Act. [<i>Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.</i>].
