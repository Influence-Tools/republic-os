# Oregon HB 2306 (2025R1) — Relating to courts.

</b> </i>] Allows [<i>the Marion County</i>] <b>a county court or</b> board of county commissioners <b>in any county</b> to establish a justice of the peace district that includes the county seat. Increases the personal payment that may be charged for marriage solemnization and directs the State Court Administrator to adjust the payment amount to reflect changes in the Consumer Price Index.
