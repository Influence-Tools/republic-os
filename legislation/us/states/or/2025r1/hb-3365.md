# Oregon HB 3365 (2025R1) — Relating to climate change instruction in public schools.

</b> </i>] Directs the State Board of Education to ensure that [<i>academic content standards for core subjects include sufficient instruction on the causes and effects of climate change and strategies for responding to such causes and effects</i>] <b>any revisions to the academic content standards for certain core subjects include standards that address the causes and effects of climate change and strategies for mitigating, adapting to and strengthening community resilience to those causes and effects</b>. Applies to academic content standards that are reviewed and revised on or after the effective date of the Act.
