# Oregon HB 3328 (2025R1) — Relating to expending county road fund moneys for patrolling.

Adds Grant County to the list of counties that may use road fund moneys from federal forest reserves payments for patrolling on the county's roads. [<i>Declares an emergency, effective on passage.</i>].
