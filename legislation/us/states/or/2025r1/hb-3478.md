# Oregon HB 3478 (2025R1) — Relating to dispensing nonretail fuel; and prescribing an effective date.

Eliminates gallonage requirements for eligibility for nonretail cardlock dispensing of motor vehicle fuel. Increases cardlock facility licensing period from one year to three years. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
