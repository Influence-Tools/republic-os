# Oregon SB 826 (2025R1) — Relating to the Oregon Statewide Communication Interoperability Plan; and declaring an emergency.

Transfers duties, functions and powers related to the Oregon Statewide Communication Interoperability Plan from the State Chief Information Officer to the Oregon Department of Emergency Management. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
