# Oregon HB 2942 (2025R1) — Relating to reimbursement of pharmacy services; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires the Oregon Health Authority and coordinated care organizations to reimburse pharmacies and pharmacists in the same manner as other health care providers for certain services related to HIV treatment. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
