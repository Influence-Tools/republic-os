# Oregon HB 5023 (2025R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the Oregon State Board of Nursing; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by the Oregon State Board of Nursing. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
