# Oregon HB 5040 (2025R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board; and declaring an emergency.

Limits expenditures of lottery funds from the Watershed Conservation Grant Fund for local grant expenditures by the Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board for a six-year period beginning July 1, 2025. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
