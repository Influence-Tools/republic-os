# Oregon SB 558 (2025R1) — Relating to cannabis market growth; and prescribing an effective date.

Allows a marijuana licensee to provide samples of marijuana items to other marijuana licensees at temporary events registered with the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission. Allows the commission to establish by rule a temporary events registration system. Allows a marijuana wholesaler to sell marijuana items to a marijuana retailer at a temporary event. Allows a marijuana producer to provide samples of seeds and immature marijuana plants to permitted workers. Allows a marijuana [<i>producer, marijuana processor and marijuana wholesaler</i>] <b>licensee</b> to provide samples of marijuana items in specified amounts to permitted workers. <b>Allows a marijuana producer to transfer usable marijuana to or receive usable marijuana from another marijuana producer.</b> Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
