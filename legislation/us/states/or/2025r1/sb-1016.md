# Oregon SB 1016 (2025R1) — Relating to transportation of the personal belongings of foster children.

</b> </i>] <b>Directs the Department of Human Services to maintain an inventory of appropriate carriers for the transportation of a foster child's personal belongings.</b> Prohibits the department [<i>of Human Services</i>] from using trash bags to transport the personal belongings of a foster child.
