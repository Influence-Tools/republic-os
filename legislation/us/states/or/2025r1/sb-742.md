# Oregon SB 742 (2025R1) — Relating to electronic meetings to discuss an abbreviated school day program placement; and declaring an emergency.

Allows a student's parent or foster parent to consent to attending an initial meeting regarding an abbreviated school day program placement by telephone or other electronic means. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
