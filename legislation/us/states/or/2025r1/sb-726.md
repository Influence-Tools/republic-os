# Oregon SB 726 (2025R1) — Relating to landfill emissions monitoring; and prescribing an effective date.

</b> </i>] Requires the owner or operator of a municipal solid waste landfill <b>located in Benton County</b> to conduct surface emissions monitoring and report data as specified in the Act. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
