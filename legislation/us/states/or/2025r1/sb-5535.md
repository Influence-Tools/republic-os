# Oregon SB 5535 (2025R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the Public Records Advocate; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by the Public Records Advocate. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
