# Oregon HB 2680 (2025R1) — Relating to certifications required for work with fenestration systems in public buildings; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires a contractor, as a condition of receiving public funds to install, repair or remodel a fenestration product or system in a public building, to have a national certification as a glazing contractor and at least one employee that has a national certification as an architectural glass and metal technician. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
