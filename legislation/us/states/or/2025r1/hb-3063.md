# Oregon HB 3063 (2025R1) — Relating to students in hospital educational programs.

Provides that a student who is in a hospital educational program shall continue to remain enrolled in the school district in which the student had been a resident prior to being in the hospital educational program only for the purpose of maintaining access to classroom curricula and associated technology.
