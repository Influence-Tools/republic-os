# Oregon SB 1051 (2025R1) — Relating to the State Forester; and declaring an emergency.

Transfers the authority to appoint a State Forester from the State Board of Forestry to the Governor, subject to Senate confirmation. <b>Declares an emergency, effective on passage.</b>
