# Oregon SB 221 (2025R1) — Relating to a fish enhancement program; and declaring an emergency.

Directs the State Department of Fish and Wildlife to assist in the establishment of fall Chinook fish incubation nursery programs, and to report on the programs to the Legislative Assembly. <b>Sunsets January 2, 2040.</b> Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
