# Oregon HB 2558 (2025R1) — Relating to activities regulated by the State Marine Board; and prescribing an effective date.

</b> </i>] Modifies the definition of "charter guide" for purposes of outfitter and guide laws. <b>Requires a person to register with the State Marine Board before acting as a crew member. Defines "crew member" for the purposes of outfitter and guide laws.</b> Increases the registration fee for all outfitters and guides. Becomes operative November 1, 2025. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
