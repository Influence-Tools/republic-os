# Oregon SB 5509 (2025R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the Construction Contractors Board; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by the Construction Contractors Board. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
