# Oregon SB 789 (2025R1) — Relating to the Oregon Board of Psychology; and prescribing an effective date.

Authorizes the Oregon Board of Psychology to assess costs associated with disciplinary action to the person against whom the disciplinary action is taken. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
