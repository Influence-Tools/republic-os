# Oregon HB 2565 (2025R1) — Relating to contracts with federally funded research and development centers; and prescribing an effective date.

Exempts contracts with <b>certain</b> federally funded research and development centers from the provisions of the Public Contracting Code. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
