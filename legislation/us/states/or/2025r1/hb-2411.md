# Oregon HB 2411 (2025R1) — Relating to industrial development.

Authorizes the Oregon Business Development Department to provide financial assistance to projects related to industrial land. Establishes the Industrial Site Loan Fund in the State Treasury. [<i>Allocates lottery funds for deposit in the fund for the purpose of funding financial assistance to industrial land projects.</i>].
