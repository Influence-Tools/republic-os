# Oregon HB 2010 (2025R1) — Relating to funding to improve access to health care; and prescribing an effective date.

Extends the assessment on earnings from health plan premiums, the assessment on payments by the Oregon Health Authority to managed care organizations, the assessment on hospitals and the Oregon Reinsurance Program.<b> Allows the Director of the Oregon Health Authority to adopt different rates of assessment for the net inpatient revenue and net outpatient revenue of hospitals. Extends the temporary increase in net reimbursement guaranteed to the Oregon Health and Science University for the costs of services paid for with Medicaid funds.</b> Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
