# Oregon HB 2975 (2025R1) — Relating to crimes constituting domestic violence; and prescribing an effective date.

</b> </i>] Provides that <b>a pleading,</b> an admission or a finding that<b> criminal conduct constitutes</b> a crime [<i>constitutes</i>]<b> involving</b> domestic violence is not an element of the crime for merger purposes. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
