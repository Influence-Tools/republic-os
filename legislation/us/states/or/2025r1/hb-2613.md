# Oregon HB 2613 (2025R1) — Relating to minor victims.

</b> </i>] [<i>Requires the Department of Human Services to study how to improve services provided to victims of child abuse in this state. Directs the department to submit findings to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to human services not later than September 15, 2026.</i>] [<i>Sunsets on January 2, 2027.</i>] <b>Prescribes the factors that the court must consider when determining whether a child is in immediate danger for the purpose of granting a request for a temporary order providing for the custody of, or parenting time with, the child.</b>
