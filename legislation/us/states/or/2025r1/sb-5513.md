# Oregon SB 5513 (2025R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the Department of Justice; and declaring an emergency.

Appropriates moneys from the General Fund to the Department of Justice for biennial expenses of district attorneys. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
