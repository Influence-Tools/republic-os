# Oregon HB 2508 (2025R1) — Relating to student data; and declaring an emergency.

Directs the Department of Education to develop and implement a standardized method to be used by school districts, education service districts and the department to electronically create, collect, use, maintain, disclose, transfer and access student data. Prescribes requirements of the method. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
