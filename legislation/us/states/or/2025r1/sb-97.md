# Oregon SB 97 (2025R1) — Relating to funding for services in domestic relations cases; and declaring an emergency.

Directs county treasurers and presiding judges of judicial districts to prepare and submit certain financial reports regarding conciliation and mediation accounts. Authorizes a county governing body to increase fees for issuances of marriage licenses and registrations of Declarations of Domestic Partnership. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
