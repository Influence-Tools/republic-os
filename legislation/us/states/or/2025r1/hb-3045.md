# Oregon HB 3045 (2025R1) — Relating to the State Board of Pharmacy; and prescribing an effective date.

Authorizes the State Board of Pharmacy to require a person under investigation by the board to undergo a mental, physical, chemical dependency or competency evaluation. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
