# Oregon HB 2140 (2025R1) — Relating to funding of the State School Fund; and declaring an emergency.

Modifies the method by which the Oregon Department of Administrative Services and the Legislative Fiscal Officer project the costs of programs that are funded by the State School Fund. Directs the Department of Education to modify the manner in which certain grants from the State School Fund are distributed to school districts. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
