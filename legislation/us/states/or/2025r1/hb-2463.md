# Oregon HB 2463 (2025R1) — Relating to small claims.

</b> </i>] <b>Increases the time for a defendant to respond to a claim in small claims court.</b> Clarifies that the total amount or value of a claim required to request a jury trial in small claims court includes identified prejudgment or preaward interest, fees and costs.
