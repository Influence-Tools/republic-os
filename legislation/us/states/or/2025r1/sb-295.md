# Oregon SB 295 (2025R1) — Relating to pharmacy.

</b> </i>] [<i>Authorizes a pharmacist to assess, diagnose and treat certain health conditions. Includes the assessment, diagnosis and treatment of certain conditions in the practice of pharmacy. Authorizes a pharmacist to prescribe and dispense certain drugs and devices. Directs the Public Health and Pharmacy Formulary Advisory Committee to develop a list of health conditions that a pharmacist may assess, diagnose and treat.</i>] [<i>Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.</i>] <b>Makes permanent a pharmacist's ability to test for and treat COVID-19.</b>
