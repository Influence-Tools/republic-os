# Oregon HB 2341 (2025R1) — Relating to the Department of Veterans' Affairs.

Adds a uniformed service member's or veteran's e-mail address to the information that the Director of Human Services, Early Learning System Director, Director of Transportation and Director of the Oregon Health Authority are required to provide to the Director of Veterans' Affairs.
