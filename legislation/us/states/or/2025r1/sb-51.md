# Oregon SB 51 (2025R1) — Relating to housing; and declaring an emergency.

Requires the Housing and Community Services Department to distribute funds to the Housing Development Center, Inc. to support affordable housing property management. Sunsets June 30, 2027. [<i>Requires the department to implement a program advancing culturally responsive property management services.</i>] Requires the department to establish a program to coordinate responses to, and to report on, preservation of affordable housing, including manufactured dwelling parks and marinas. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
