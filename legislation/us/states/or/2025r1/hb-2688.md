# Oregon HB 2688 (2025R1) — Relating to the application of a prevailing rate of wage to off-site custom work for use in public works; and prescribing an effective date.

Applies the prevailing rate of wage to certain off-site bespoke work fabricated, preconstructed, assembled or constructed in accordance with specifications for a particular public works. Specifies examples of such bespoke work. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
