# Oregon SB 1173 (2025R1) — Relating to product liability civil actions; and prescribing an effective date.

</b> </i>] Provides that [<i>a health care facility is</i>] <b>certain entities providing a product as part of health care services are</b> not a manufacturer, distributor, seller or lessor of [<i>a</i>] <b>the</b> product for purposes of a product liability civil action if the [<i>health care facility</i>] <b>entity</b> was not involved in the design or manufacture of the product. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
