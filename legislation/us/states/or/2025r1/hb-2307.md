# Oregon HB 2307 (2025R1) — Relating to fluorescent lamps purchased by school districts; and declaring an emergency.

Exempts, until January 2, 2030, lamps purchased by a school district from the prohibition on the sale of certain fluorescent lamps. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
