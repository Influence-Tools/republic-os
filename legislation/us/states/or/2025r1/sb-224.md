# Oregon SB 224 (2025R1) — Relating to addresses of individuals associated with candidate campaign committees.

</b> </i>] Prohibits the Secretary of State from publishing the residence address of certain individuals who are affiliated with a [<i>candidate's principal campaign</i>] <b>political</b> committee on the electronic filing system maintained by the secretary.
