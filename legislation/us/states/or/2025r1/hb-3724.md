# Oregon HB 3724 (2025R1) — Relating to cannabis; and prescribing an effective date.

Prohibits advertising, packaging and labeling of marijuana items in a manner likely to cause minors to unlawfully possess or consume marijuana items. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
