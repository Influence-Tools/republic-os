# Oregon HB 2375 (2025R1) — Relating to wind energy facilities.

</b> </i>] Requires a person who is developing or repowering a wind energy facility to apply to the Federal Aviation Administration <b>and, if applicable, the Federal Communications Commission, for approval</b> for <b>the</b> installation <b>and use</b> of [<i>a</i>] light-mitigating technology [<i>system</i>] <b>systems</b> and, if approved, install the [<i>system</i>] <b>systems</b> within 24 months<b> or as soon as reasonably practicable thereafter</b>. <b>Prohibits a county or city from allowing or permitting a wind energy facility if the person seeking to develop or repower the wind energy facility does not apply for approval for the installation and use of light-mitigating technology systems.</b>
