# Oregon HB 2271 (2025R1) — Relating to employer taxes.

</b> </i>] Provides a credit against an employer's unemployment insurance taxes for calendar <b>year 2025</b> [<i>years 2025, 2026 and 2027</i>] if the employer's tax rate for calendar year 2025 is at least [<i>three</i>] <b>2.5</b> percentage points less than the employer's tax rate for calendar year 2024.
