# Oregon HB 2725 (2025R1) — Relating to the inclusion of ports in strategic investment program agreements; and prescribing an effective date.

Limits the kind of port districts that are required to be involved in strategic investment program agreements with respect to eligible projects that are located in the port district's territory. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
