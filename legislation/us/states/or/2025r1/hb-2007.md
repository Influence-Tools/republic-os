# Oregon HB 2007 (2025R1) — Relating to summer learning; and declaring an emergency.

Modifies requirements for the summer learning program. Directs the Department of Education to provide reports based on information provided by grant recipients. <b>Establishes the Summer Learning Grant Program Fund.</b> Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
