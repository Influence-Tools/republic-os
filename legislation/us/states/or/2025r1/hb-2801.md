# Oregon HB 2801 (2025R1) — Relating to the time frame for allowing changes to determined claims in the Upper Klamath Basin.

Allows the Water Resources Department to approve leases or temporary transfers of certain water rights in the Upper Klamath Basin until a court issues a final water rights decree. .
