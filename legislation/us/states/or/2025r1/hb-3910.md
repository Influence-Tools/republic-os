# Oregon HB 3910 (2025R1) — Relating to domestic water supply district authority; and declaring an emergency.

</b> </i>] Expands the justifications for a domestic water supply district to exercise the powers of a sanitary district. <b>Requires a public hearing on the exercise of the powers and written consent from any sanitary district or other provider of sanitary services within the district.</b> Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
