# Oregon HB 2728 (2025R1) — Relating to member data under the Public Employees Retirement System; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs the Public Employees Retirement System to include information about the use of overtime hours in calculating the final average salary of a member of the system who is retiring. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
