# Oregon HB 5018 (2025R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the State Board of Licensed Social Workers; and declaring an emergency.

[<i>Appropriates moneys from the General Fund to the State Board of Licensed Social Workers.</i>] Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by the [<i>board</i>]<b> State Board of Licensed Social Workers</b>. Declares an emergency, effective July 1, 2025.
