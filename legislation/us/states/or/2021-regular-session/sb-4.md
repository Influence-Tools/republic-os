# Oregon SB 4 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to youth with specialized needs; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Directs Oregon Health Authority to study and make recommendations for improving coordination of youth mental health services among state agencies. Requires authority to submit report on findings to interim committees of Legislative Assembly no later than September 15, 2022.</i>]
