# Oregon HB 2560 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to expanding remote participation of public in self-government through use of technology.

Requires governing body of public body, to extent reasonably possible, to make all meetings accessible remotely through technological means and provide opportunity for members of general public to remotely submit oral and written testimony <i>during meetings to extent in-person oral and written testimony is allowed</i>]. Imposes same requirements on hearings under ORS chapters 197 and 215.
