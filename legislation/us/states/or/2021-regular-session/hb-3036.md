# Oregon HB 3036 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to physician assistants; and declaring an emergency.

Allows physician assistant to register with Oregon Medical Board for authority to dispense prescription drugs. Specifies requirements for dispensation by physician assistant. Allows board to license out-of-state physician assistant to practice medicine in Oregon.
