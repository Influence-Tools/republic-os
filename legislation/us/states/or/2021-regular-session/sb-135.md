# Oregon SB 135 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to child abuse investigation records.

Authorizes disclosure of child abuse investigation reports and records to Oregon Youth Authority. Permits Oregon Youth Authority and Department of Corrections to disclose records to certain persons for purposes of second look hearing.
