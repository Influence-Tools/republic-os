# Oregon SB 2 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to proton beam therapy.

Prohibits insurers from imposing prior authorization or other utilization review requirements on coverage of proton beam therapy<b> for prostate cancer</b> that <i>do not apply to</i>]<b> are more restrictive than prior authorization or utilization review requirements applied to</b> coverage of radiation therapy.
