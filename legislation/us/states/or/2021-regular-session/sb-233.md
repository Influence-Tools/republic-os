# Oregon SB 233 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the transfer of academic credit; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Higher Education Coordinating Commission to establish common course numbering system for <i>all</i>] introductory and lower level courses <b>with similar learning outcomes</b> offered at<b> accelerated college credit programs,</b> public post-secondary institutions of education<b> and participating nonpublic post-secondary institutions of education</b>. Requires all public post-secondary institutions of education to adopt system and accept transfers of academic credit <b>subject to common course numbering system</b> earned at other <i>public post-secondary</i>] institutions of education.
