# Oregon HB 2049 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the Innovative Grant Program.

Establishes Innovative Grant Fund. Directs Oregon Criminal Justice Commission to establish and administer Innovative Grant Program to award grants to programs that enhance public safety and meet other requirements.<b> Appropriates moneys to commission for Innovative Grant Program.</b>
