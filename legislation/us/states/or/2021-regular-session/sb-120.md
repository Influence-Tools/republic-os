# Oregon SB 120 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to calculation of interest on refunds paid by Department of Revenue; and prescribing an effective date.

Aligns direction to Department of Revenue to pay interest on certain tax refunds with other tax refund provisions and with current systems and processes. Provides for uniform 45-day grace period. Applies to tax refunds owing as of January 1, 2018.
