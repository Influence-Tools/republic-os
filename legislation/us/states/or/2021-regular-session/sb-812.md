# Oregon SB 812 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to child support.

<i>Directs Department of Justice to study and make recommendations on provisions of state law related to child support. Requires department to submit report on findings to Legislative Assembly by January 1, 2023.</i>]<b> Modifies eligibility requirements for exception to income withholding for child support obligations.</b>
