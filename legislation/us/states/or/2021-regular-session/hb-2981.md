# Oregon HB 2981 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to palliative care.

Requires Oregon Health Authority to establish and administer program to provide<b>, through coordinated care organizations,</b> palliative care services<b>,</b> <i>and support provision of home- and community-based end of life care. Specifies eligibility, services and provider qualifications</i>]<b> as determined by interdisciplinary team, to patients in their choice of residences</b>.
