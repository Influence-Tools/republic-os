# Oregon HB 3183 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the education of children with hearing conditions.

<i>Requires school district to provide information about certain services and placements available for children who are deaf or deafblind or who are hard of hearing and to ensure that representative of Oregon School for the Deaf is present for certain meetings related to individualized education programs or 504 Plans.</i>]
