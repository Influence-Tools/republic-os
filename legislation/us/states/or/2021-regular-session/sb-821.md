# Oregon SB 821 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to support orders.

Creates process to rebut presumption of inability to pay support order after support order is suspended and procedure upon reinstatement following rebuttal of presumption. Declares that reinstatement of previously suspended support order is considered substantial change of circumstances for purposes of child support modification proceedings. Delays support order modification proceedings until reinstatement of suspended support order.
