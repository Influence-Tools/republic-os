# Oregon SB 750 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the Oregon Liquor Control Commission.

Authorizes Oregon Liquor Control Commission to grant temporary letter of authority to eligible applicant for any license issued by commission. Specifies that temporary letter of authority may be valid for up to 180 days.
