# Oregon SB 200 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to district attorney policies.

Requires district attorney of each county to adopt written policies concerning guilty except for insanity <i>cases</i>]<b> dispositions</b>. Requires policies to be adopted and made available to public no later than December 1, 2022.
