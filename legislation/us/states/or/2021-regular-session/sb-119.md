# Oregon SB 119 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to charitable contributions of art objects; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires taxpayer-creator to obtain appraisal report to substantiate fair market value of art object for purpose of subtraction allowed for donation of art object. Eliminates requirement that taxpayer-creator submit appraisal report with tax return.
