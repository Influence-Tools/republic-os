# Oregon HB 3373 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to taxpayers; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes office of Taxpayer Advocate in Department of Revenue. Establishes functions, powers and duties of office. Authorizes Taxpayer Advocate to issue order directing action by department if taxpayer experiences significant hardship due to administration of tax laws by department. Requires Taxpayer Advocate to report biennially on operation of office to committee of Legislative Assembly related to revenue. Becomes operative January 1, 2022.
