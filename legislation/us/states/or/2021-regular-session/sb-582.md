# Oregon SB 582 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to modernizing Oregon's recycling system.

Establishes producer responsibility program for packaging, printing and writing paper and food serviceware. Requires producers of covered products to join producer responsibility organization that implements producer responsibility program plan approved by Department of Environmental Quality.
