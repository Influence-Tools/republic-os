# Oregon HB 2517 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to managing the utilization of health care.

Requires <i>coordinated care organizations to report specified information to</i>] Oregon Health Authority<b> to compile and annually post to website report on information reported to authority by coordinated care organizations</b> regarding requests for prior authorization.
