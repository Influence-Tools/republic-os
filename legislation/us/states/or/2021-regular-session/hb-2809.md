# Oregon HB 2809 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to temporary siting of recreational vehicles on properties with dwellings destroyed by natural disasters.

Allows temporary siting for up to 24 months of recreational vehicles on properties with dwellings that natural disasters have made uninhabitable.
