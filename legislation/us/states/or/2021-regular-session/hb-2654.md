# Oregon HB 2654 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to use of easements for provision of broadband.

Authorizes electric <i>utility</i>]<b> cooperative</b> to use or allow for use of electric easement in provision of broadband services<b>, subject to certain conditions and exceptions</b>. Provides process for property owner to bring action for damages if use in provision of broadband services results in expanded use of easement.
