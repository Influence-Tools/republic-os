# Oregon HB 2958 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to prescription drugs; and prescribing an effective date.

Allows pharmacist to prescribe, dispense and administer preexposure prophylactic antiretroviral therapies and post-exposure prophylactic antiretroviral therapies. Directs State Board of Pharmacy to adopt rules.
