# Oregon SB 744 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to high school diploma requirements; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Education to review state requirements for high school diploma and to make recommendations related to requirements. Requires department to provide report to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to education and to State Board of Education.
