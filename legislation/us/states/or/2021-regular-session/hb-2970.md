# Oregon HB 2970 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to health care; and declaring an emergency.

Defines "device" for purposes of practice of advanced nonablative esthetics. Prohibits use of device not registered with United States Food and Drug Administration. Defines "mechanical or electrical apparatus, appliance or device" for purposes of esthetics.
