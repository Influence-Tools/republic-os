# Oregon SB 580 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to mandatory subjects of collective bargaining.

Amends definition of "employment relations" to include class size and caseload limits as mandatory collective bargaining subjects <i>for school districts</i>] <b>in schools that qualify for certain federal assistance</b>.
