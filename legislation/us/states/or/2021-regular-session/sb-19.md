# Oregon SB 19 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to Department of Corrections institutions; and declaring an emergency.

Requires state to offer to transfer title and assets of Warner Creek Correctional Facility to Lake County if closed. Authorizes county to rezone institution land by ordinance.
