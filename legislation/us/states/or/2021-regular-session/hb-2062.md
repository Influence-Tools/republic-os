# Oregon HB 2062 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to energy efficiency standards; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes energy efficiency standards for certain appliances sold or offered for sale in this state. Removes requirement that certain appliances sold or offered for sale in this state meet state energy efficiency standards.
