# Oregon HB 2323 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to communicating false information about an election.

Prohibits knowingly communicating materially false statement<b>, including by electronic or telephonic means,</b> with intent to mislead electors about date of election, deadline for delivering ballot, voter registration deadline, method of registering to vote, locations at which elector may deposit ballot, qualifications of electors or voter registration status within 30 days of primary election or special election or within 60 days of general election.
