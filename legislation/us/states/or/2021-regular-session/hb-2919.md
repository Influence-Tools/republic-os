# Oregon HB 2919 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to disclosure of costs to enroll in classes at public institutions of higher education.

Requires each public university and community college to prominently display <i>total</i>]<b>, or establish link to website that displays, estimated</b> costs of all required course materials and<b> directly related course</b> fees for no less than 75 percent of total <b>for-credit</b> courses offered by public university or community college.<b> Establishes how cost information must be displayed, secured and aggregated.</b>
