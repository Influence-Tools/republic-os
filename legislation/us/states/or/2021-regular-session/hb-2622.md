# Oregon HB 2622 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to surgical smoke.

Requires hospitals and ambulatory surgical centers to<b> adopt policies that require</b> use<b> of</b> smoke evacuation system during surgical procedures likely to generate surgical smoke.
