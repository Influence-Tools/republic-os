# Oregon SB 766 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to sexual abuse restraining orders; and declaring an emergency.

Modifies date of expiration of sexual abuse restraining order for orders entered when petitioner was under 18 years of age. Specifies procedures when service on respondent of sexual abuse restraining order is completed by alternative service.
