# Oregon HB 3375 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to floating offshore wind energy; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes goal of planning for development of <b>up to</b> three gigawatts of <i>commercial scale</i>] floating offshore wind energy projects within federal waters off Oregon Coast by 2030.<b> Establishes policy position of State of Oregon related to offshore energy research and development in federal waters off Oregon Coast.</b>
