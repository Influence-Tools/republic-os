# Oregon HB 2682 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to safety standards for construction vehicles; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Directs Construction Contractors Board to adopt rules that require persons that lease construction vehicles in this state to maintain construction vehicles in condition that allows for safe and reliable operation of construction vehicles, to keep certain logs, records and documents and to provide or offer to provide training in safe and reliable operation of construction vehicles.</i>]
