# Oregon HB 2344 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to labeling requirements for wipes; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires persons that have responsibility for or control over packaging and labeling <i>disposable cleaning cloths or similar implements</i>]<b> premoistened nonwoven disposable wipes</b> with specified characteristics to comply with certain labeling requirements for product packaging.
