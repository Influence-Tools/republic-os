# Oregon HB 3335 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to housing; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Housing and Community Services Department to grant moneys to nonprofit organization for ADU community pilot programs that develop accessory dwelling units for income-eligible homeowners and that are available for lease by income-eligible tenants. Sunsets January 2, 2024.
