# Oregon SB 551 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to employees at public institutions of higher education; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that part-time faculty member at public institution of higher education <b>qualifies for employee-only health care benefits if part-time faculty member works at level equal to at least half of full-time equivalent employee during at least three of four previous academic terms.</b> <i>who qualifies for health care benefits will</i>]<b> Requires part-time faculty member to </b>pay 10 percent of insurance premiums for employee coverage.
