# Oregon SB 131 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to investigations of deaths.

Adds supervisors of youth correction facilities and juvenile community supervision officers to list of professionals required to make notification of death and cooperate in investigation of death by providing records and other materials. Requires cooperation with both medical examiner and medical-legal death investigator.
