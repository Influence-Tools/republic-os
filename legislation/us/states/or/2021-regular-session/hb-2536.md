# Oregon HB 2536 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to school meals provided at no charge to students; and declaring an emergency.

Requires school or school district to offer lunches and breakfasts at no charge to student if school or school district meets requirements of special provisions of federal meal program or if student is from household with income that does not exceed 300 percent of federal poverty guidelines. Clarifies reimbursement rate to schools and school districts that offer breakfast or lunch at no charge to student.
