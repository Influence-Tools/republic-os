# Oregon HB 2605 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to tsunami risk.

Imposes tsunami design standards for certain buildings and other structures. <i>Allows local government to impose vertical evacuation standards for new or existing buildings and other structures as part of local tsunami evacuation plan.</i>]
