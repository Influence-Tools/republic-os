# Oregon HB 2105 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to alternatives to protective proceedings.

<i>Prescribes standards for supported decision-making agreements. Provides scope of valid agreement. Limits liability of supporter acting under authority granted in written supported decision-making agreement. Limits liability of individuals relying on supporter's authority under written supported decision-making agreement.</i>]
