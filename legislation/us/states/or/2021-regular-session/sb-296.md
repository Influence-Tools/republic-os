# Oregon SB 296 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to judicial authority during statewide emergencies; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Chief Justice of Supreme Court, during period of statewide emergency and upon finding of good cause, to extend or suspend time period or time requirement in rule or statute in specified court proceedings. Authorizes Chief Justice to direct or permit electronic court appearances during period of statewide emergency.
