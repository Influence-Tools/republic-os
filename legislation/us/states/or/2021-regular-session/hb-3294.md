# Oregon HB 3294 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to feminine hygiene products in public schools; and declaring an emergency.

Requires every public education provider to provide both tampons and sanitary pads at no cost to students. Phases in requirement. Provides funding for costs incurred by public education provider.
