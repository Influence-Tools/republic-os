# Oregon HB 2331 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to over-the-air broadcasting; and prescribing an effective date.

Excludes <i>over-the-air broadcasting</i>] from meaning of "communication<b>,</b>" for purposes of central assessment for property taxation<b>, services of television and radio stations licensed by Federal Communications Commission that use primarily earth-based transmitters to broadcast programming via radio waves to television or radio receivers that use indoor or outdoor antennas for reception</b>.
