# Oregon HB 2139 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the Rural Veterans Healthcare Transportation Grant Program.

Establishes Rural Veterans Healthcare Transportation Grant Program within Department of Veterans' Affairs. Directs department to provide grants to entities to provide transportation to veterans in rural areas to access services for physical, mental or behavioral health care.
