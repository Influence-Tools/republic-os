# Oregon SB 59 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the Columbia River Fisheries Enhancement Fund; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Eliminates</i>]<b> Extends</b> sunset on Columbia River Fisheries Enhancement Fund. <i>Eliminates</i>]<b> Extends</b> sunset on State Fish and Wildlife Commission authority to charge for endorsements to certain Columbia Basin fishing licenses and deposit endorsement moneys to fund. <i>Makes</i>]<b> Extends sunset on</b> youth license and pioneer license exemptions from endorsement charges <i>permanent</i>].<b> Provides that commission may not require or collect endorsement charges under certain circumstances.</b>
