# Oregon HB 2415 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to electronic access to building codes information; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires Department of Consumer and Business Services to <i>conduct study to evaluate feasibility of and develop standards and specifications for system that allows for performing electronic field inspections and construction progress inspections and to verify compliance with building code requirements on site</i>]<b> adopt rules to establish minimum standards and statewide consistency in electronic processes for reviewing plans, issuing permits and performing field inspections</b>.
