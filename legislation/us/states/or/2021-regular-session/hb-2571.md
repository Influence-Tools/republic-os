# Oregon HB 2571 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to liability for prescribed fires; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs Department of Consumer and Business Services, in consultation with State Forestry Department, <b>Oregon Forest and Industries Council, Oregon Small Woodlands Association,</b> Oregon State University<b>,</b> <i>and</i>] Oregon Prescribed Fire Council<b> and representative of insurance industry</b>, to study liability for prescribed fires. Requires Department of Consumer and Business Services to report to appropriate committee or interim committee of Legislative Assembly on results of study no later than July 1, 2022.
