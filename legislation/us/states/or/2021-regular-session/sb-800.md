# Oregon SB 800 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to health care; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes Oregon Essential Workforce Health Care Program in Oregon Health Authority to provide <i>health care to employees of</i>]<b> supplemental payments, as approved by Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services, to </b>long term care facilities, residential <i>care</i>] facilities and in-home care agencies that<b> elect to</b> participate <i>in medical assistance program. Directs authority to contract with entity to administer program</i>] <b>and meet specified requirements, to be used to provide health care benefits to employees of facilities</b>.
