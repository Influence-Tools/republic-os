# Oregon HB 2102 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to programs administered by the Department of Human Services; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Department of Human Services to report to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to human services <i>any changes needed to statutes governing programs administered by department due to changes to budget</i>]<b>, by December 31, 2022, comprehensive proposal for department staffing that meets specified criteria</b>.
