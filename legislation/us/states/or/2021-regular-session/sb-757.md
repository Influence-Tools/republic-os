# Oregon SB 757 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to children; and declaring an emergency.

Limits availability of conditional enrollment in Central Background Registry to individuals who have successfully completed criminal background check in Oregon and, if applicable, in state of residence. Modifies definition of "exempt prohibited individual" to include individuals whose certification or registration has been revoked or whose enrollment in Central Background Registry has been removed only if revocation or removal was for cause.
