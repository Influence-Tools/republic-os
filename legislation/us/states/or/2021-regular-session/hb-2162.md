# Oregon HB 2162 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the Department of Public Safety Standards and Training; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs Department of Public Safety Standards and Training to designate one or more accrediting bodies for law enforcement agencies with 35 or more sworn police officers. Phases in requirement for law enforcement agencies to become accredited based on agency size.
