# Oregon HB 2032 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to authorizing regional implementation of traditionally maintained channels program; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes that Department of State Lands or State Department of Agriculture rules implementing traditionally maintained channels program may provide for implementation on region-by-region basis. Requires implementation throughout state within five-year period.
