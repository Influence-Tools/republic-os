# Oregon SB 90 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to public disclosure; and prescribing an effective date.

Exempts from public disclosure addresses of individuals with intellectual or developmental disabilities residing in adult foster homes, residential training facilities or residential training homes<b>, unless public interest requires disclosure in particular instance</b>.
