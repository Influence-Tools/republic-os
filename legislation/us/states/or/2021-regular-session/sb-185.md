# Oregon SB 185 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the governance of nonprofit corporations.

<b>Provides that, and</b> specifies<b> procedures and</b> circumstances under which<b>,</b> members of nonprofit corporation and board of directors of nonprofit corporation may discuss issues or take action by<b> electronic mail or other</b> electronic means without meeting.<b> Provides that affirmative vote of majority of board of directors to approve action under specified circumstances is act of board of directors and is effective on date specified in electronic mail announcement.</b>
