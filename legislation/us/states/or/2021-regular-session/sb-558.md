# Oregon SB 558 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to insurance coverage of prescription drugs.

Prohibits insurance policy or contract that provides coverage for prescription drug to require prescription for drug<b> prescribed for medical assistance recipient</b> to be filled or refilled at mail order pharmacy or to <i>prohibit</i>]<b> require</b> prescription <i>from being</i>]<b> to be</b> filled or refilled at <i>local</i>] pharmacy<b> that is not local and enrolled in medical assistance program</b>.<b> Creates exception regarding specialty drugs.</b>
