# Oregon SB 317 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to alcoholic beverages; and declaring an emergency.

Allows holder of full on-premises sales license to make retail sales of mixed drinks <b>and single servings of wine</b> in sealed containers for off-premises consumption. Defines "mixed drink" and "sealed container."
