# Oregon HB 2927 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to emergency management; and declaring an emergency.

Renames Office of Emergency Management as Oregon Department of Emergency Management. Establishes department as independent state agency. Directs department to carry out certain functions related to regional and statewide emergency response and preparedness.
