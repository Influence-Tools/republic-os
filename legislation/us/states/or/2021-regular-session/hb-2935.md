# Oregon HB 2935 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to discrimination.

Limits authority of school district to become member of voluntary organization that administers interscholastic activities unless organization implements <i>policy that prohibits discrimination based on race, color or national origin</i>] <b>equity focused policies that meet certain requirements</b>.
