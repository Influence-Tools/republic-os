# Oregon SB 763 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to licensing pharmaceutical representatives; and prescribing an effective date.

Provides that person may not engage in business as pharmaceutical representative without obtaining license from Director of Department of Consumer and Business Services. Specifies application requirements and procedures, required <i>qualifications of</i>]<b> professional educational requirements for</b> licensee and basis on which director may issue, renew or reinstate license. <i>Prescribes certain duties of and</i>] Prohibits certain actions by licensee. Enables director to punish violations of Act by suspending <i>,</i>]<b> or</b> revoking <i>or refusing to renew or reinstate</i>] license<b> and by imposing civil penalty of not less than $1,000 and not more than $3,000 for violation of Act</b>.
