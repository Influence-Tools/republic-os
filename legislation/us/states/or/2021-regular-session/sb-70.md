# Oregon SB 70 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to disparities in health outcomes for communities impacted by discrimination; and declaring an emergency.

Defines "regional health equity coalition" and "regional health equity coalition model." Requires Oregon Health Authority to work with regional health equity coalitions. <i>Appropriates moneys to fund additional grants for organizations seeking to build regional health equity coalitions and to provide additional staff support.</i>]
