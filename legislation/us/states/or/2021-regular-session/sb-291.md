# Oregon SB 291 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to housing.

Requires landlords <b>who require applicant screening charges</b> to adopt certain written screening criteria made available to applicants before accepting application. Limits criminal <i>behavior</i>] <b>charges and convictions</b> landlord may consider in screening applicant. Requires landlord to conduct individualized assessment of applicant and specify any basis for denial.
