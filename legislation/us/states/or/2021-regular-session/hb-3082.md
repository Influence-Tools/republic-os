# Oregon HB 3082 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the contract price required for an exemption from competitive bidding requirements for public improvement contracts; and prescribing an effective date.

Raises contract price at which public improvement contract solicitations are exempt from competitive bidding requirement from $5,000 to $10,000.
