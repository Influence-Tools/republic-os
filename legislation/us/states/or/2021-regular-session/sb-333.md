# Oregon SB 333 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to renewable hydrogen; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Directs Legislative Revenue Officer to conduct study related to renewable hydrogen</i>]<b> Directs State Department of Energy to conduct study of benefits of, and barriers to, renewable hydrogen production and use in Oregon</b> and report results to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to revenue no later than September 15, 2022.
