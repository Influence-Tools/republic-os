# Oregon SB 778 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the Office of Immigrant and Refugee Advancement; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes Office of Immigrant and Refugee Advancement to <i>implement and oversee</i>] <b>operate</b> statewide immigrant and refugee integration strategy. <i>Directs Governor to appoint director of office.</i>] <b>Provides that director of office is chosen through open hiring process.</b> Appropriates funds to office.
