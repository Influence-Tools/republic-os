# Oregon HB 3379 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to crib bumper pads; and declaring an emergency.

Prohibits manufacturing, retrofitting, selling, leasing, subletting or otherwise placing into stream of commerce any crib bumper pads or crib equipped with crib bumper pads.
