# Oregon SB 727 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to taxation; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes elective entity-level, pass-through business alternative income tax. Imposes tax based on sum of members' shares of distributive proceeds. Transfers proceeds of tax to General Fund. Creates refundable personal income tax credit allowable to members of pass-through entity that elects to pay entity level tax. Allows credit in amount of member's pro rata share of entity-level tax paid.
