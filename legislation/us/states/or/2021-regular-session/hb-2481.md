# Oregon HB 2481 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to law enforcement equipment; and prescribing an effective date.

Prohibits law enforcement agencies from receiving certain military surplus equipment from federal government. Specifies requirements when military surplus equipment is purchased<b>,</b> <i>or</i>] requested<b> or received by law enforcement agency</b>.
