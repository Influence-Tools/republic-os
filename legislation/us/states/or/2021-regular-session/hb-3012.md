# Oregon HB 3012 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to student fees at public institutions of higher education.

Limits ability of public university to refuse new mandatory incidental fees or process for collecting mandatory incidental fees. Limits ability of community college to refuse new mandatory student-initiated fees or process for collecting mandatory student-initiated fees.
