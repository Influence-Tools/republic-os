# Oregon SB 578 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to appointment of legal counsel for certain persons in protective proceedings.

Requires court, under certain circumstances, to appoint legal counsel for respondent or protected person in protective proceeding. Requires payment for appointed counsel from guardianship or conservatorship estate of respondent or protected person or at state expense. Provides for phase-in among counties over three-year period beginning in 2022. <i>Directs certain county circuit courts to make annual reports regarding protective proceeding statistics to Judicial Department.</i>] Directs Judicial Department to <i>present results of reports in report</i>]<b> submit annual report regarding protective proceeding statistics</b> to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to judiciary. <i>Sunsets reporting requirement on January 2, 2025.</i>].
