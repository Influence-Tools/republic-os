# Oregon SB 250 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the office of the county clerk; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that county clerk shall establish regular business hours of at least <b>four hours per day, and</b> 30 hours per <i>work</i>] week<b>, Monday through Friday,</b> for receipt and certification of instruments presented for recording. Allows for reduction in certain circumstances.
