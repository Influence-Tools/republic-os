# Oregon SB 319 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to a memorial to Oregon's Vietnam War veterans; and declaring an emergency.

Dedicates portion of State Capitol State Park to Vietnam War memorial to be funded and constructed by qualified nonprofit corporation. Exempts memorial from certain land use laws.
