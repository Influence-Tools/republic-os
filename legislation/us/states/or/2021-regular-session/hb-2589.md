# Oregon HB 2589 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to receiving credit for competencies demonstrated prior to enrolling in post-secondary institution of education.

Requires public institutions of higher education to <i>bypass</i>]<b> have same</b> developmental educational requirements and placement tests for <i>each</i>] <b>incoming</b> student who <b>completes high school and institution determines is college ready and incoming student who received certificate for passing</b> <i>receives grade on</i>] approved high school equivalency test <b>and received grade indicating student is college ready</b> <i>indicating student is fully qualified to bypass requirements and tests</i>].
