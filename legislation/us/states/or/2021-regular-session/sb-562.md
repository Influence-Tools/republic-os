# Oregon SB 562 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to children; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Declares that person may not be disqualified from service as child welfare service provider based on certain traits. Declares that child, ward or youth may not be prohibited from, disciplined for or retaliated against for publicly or privately speaking about child's, ward's or youth's experience receiving child welfare services.</i>]
