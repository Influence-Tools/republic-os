# Oregon HB 2593 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to funding for search and rescue; and prescribing an effective date.

Permits Office of Emergency Management to enter into agreement with nonprofit organization representing sheriffs under which organization is authorized to administer program to produce and sell outdoor recreation search and rescue cards. Establishes requirements for agreement.
