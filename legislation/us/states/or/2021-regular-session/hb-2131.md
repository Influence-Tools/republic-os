# Oregon HB 2131 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to liability for amounts held in trust; and prescribing an effective date.

Provides for liability of officer, employee or member of entity for unpaid amounts of taxes or fees collected by entity and held in trust for payment to Department of Revenue. Extends uniform liable entity procedures to liabilities under various tax and fee programs.
