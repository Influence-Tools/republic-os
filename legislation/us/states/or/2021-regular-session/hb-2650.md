# Oregon HB 2650 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to police dogs; and prescribing an effective date.

Authorizes emergency medical services providers to <i>transport or treat</i>]<b> provide emergency transportation for treatment to</b> police dogs injured in line of duty, provided that such transportation <i>or treatment</i>] does not interfere with emergency transportation <i>or treatment</i>] of humans.
