# Oregon SB 218 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to conditional discharge.

Expands types of charges eligible for conditional discharge to include any misdemeanor or Class C felony<b>, other than driving while under the influence of intoxicants,</b> if defendant has been<b> formally</b> accepted into specialty court.
