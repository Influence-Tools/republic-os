# Oregon HB 3037 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to youth suicide response; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs medical examiner <b>or medical-legal death investigator</b> to report <i>suspected suicides involving</i>]<b> deaths of</b> decedents 24 years of age or younger to local mental health authority<b> if district medical examiner, assistant district medical examiner, pathologist or designee of district medical examiner reasonably believes manner of death was suicide</b>.
