# Oregon SB 496 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to eligibility for unemployment insurance benefits; and declaring an emergency.

Removes prohibition on payment of unemployment insurance benefits to nonprofessional employees of educational institution providing school food services<b>,</b> <i>or services as employee of federal Head Start program</i>] <b>early intervention services or prekindergarten services</b> for weeks of unemployment commencing during period between two successive academic years or terms.
