# Oregon HB 3355 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to law enforcement officer identification; and declaring an emergency.

Specifies identification required on uniforms of law enforcement officers working in crowd management in city with population over <i>150,000</i>]<b> 60,000</b>. Exempts members of Oregon State Police.
