# Oregon SB 61 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to advice offered by Oregon Government Ethics Commission.

Authorizes Oregon Government Ethics Commission to provide written commission advisory opinions, staff advisory opinions and oral or written staff advice on application of executive session provisions of Oregon public meetings law. Grants specified safe harbor provisions to persons who rely in good faith on commission opinions or advice.
