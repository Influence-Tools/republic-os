# Oregon HB 2069 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to sage grouse mitigation funding.

Establishes Sage Grouse Mitigation Program In-Lieu Fee Fund. Continuously appropriates moneys in fund to State Department of Fish and Wildlife for supporting efforts to restore, protect, enhance or increase habitat for sage grouse, including for related administrative costs, related research or assessment activities or insurance for sage grouse habitat.
