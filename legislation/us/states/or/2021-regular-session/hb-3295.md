# Oregon HB 3295 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to marijuana revenue; and prescribing an effective date.

Modifies <i>city and</i>] county eligibility requirements for transfer of moneys from <i>marijuana revenue</i>] <b>Oregon Marijuana Account</b>. <b>Requires county to convene cannabis advisory panel before county adopts specified ordinance related to marijuana to be eligible for transfers of moneys. Creates exception to convening of county cannabis advisory panel before ordinance adoption for certain counties.
