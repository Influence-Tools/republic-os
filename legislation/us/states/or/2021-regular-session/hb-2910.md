# Oregon HB 2910 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to emergency medical services providers; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Oregon Health Authority, upon receipt of federal approval, to assess fee on emergency medical services providers and use fee<b>, interest, penalties assessed on providers and associated federal financial participation less any costs incurred by authority to administer program</b> to reimburse<b> emergency medical services providers for</b> costs of emergency medical services transports.
