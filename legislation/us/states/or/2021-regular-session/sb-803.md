# Oregon SB 803 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to catalytic converters.

Prohibits scrap metal business from purchasing or receiving catalytic converters, except from commercial seller or owner of vehicle from which catalytic converter was removed. Modifies record keeping requirements for purchase or receipt of metal property.
