# Oregon SB 154 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to solar projects; and prescribing an effective date.

Extends sunset of program under which property constituting solar project may be exempt and instead pay fee in lieu of property taxes. <b>Provides that agreement between county and owner or person in possession or control of solar project must indicate property tax treatment of land on which solar project is located and set rate of fee within range of $5,500 to $7,000 per megawatt of nameplate capacity.</b>
