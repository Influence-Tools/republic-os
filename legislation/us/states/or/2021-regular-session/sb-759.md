# Oregon SB 759 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to collective bargaining rights of legislative employees.

Directs <i>Legislative Administrator</i>] <b>presiding officers of each house of Legislative Assembly</b> to represent legislative department in collective bargaining negotiations with legislative department employee bargaining units. <b>Permits presiding officers to delegate collective bargaining responsibility to chief negotiator.</b>
