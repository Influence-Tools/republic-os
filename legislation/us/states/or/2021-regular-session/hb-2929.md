# Oregon HB 2929 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to police officer misconduct.

Modifies police officer's duty to report misconduct. <b>Requires police officers to report violation of certain minimum standards.</b> Requires law enforcement unit to investigate reports of misconduct<b> and violation of standards</b>.
