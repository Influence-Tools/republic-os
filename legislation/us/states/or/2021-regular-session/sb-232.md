# Oregon SB 232 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to educator diversity; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Directs Department of Education, in collaboration with Teacher Standards and Practices Commission, to conduct study to identify most effective methods to diversify educator workforce and to report results of study to interim committee of Legislative Assembly related to education.</i>]
