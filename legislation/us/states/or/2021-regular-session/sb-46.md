# Oregon SB 46 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to an insurer's treatment of claims under a disability insurance policy; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires insurer that issues or renews disability<b> income</b> insurance policy in this state to establish, maintain and follow certain procedures with respect to claims, determinations of claims that are adverse to claimant, appeals of determinations, communications with claimant and related operations.
