# Oregon HB 3318 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to use of land; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires, if certain conditions are met, Department of Land Conservation and Development to approve City of Bend's expansion of urban growth boundary to include specified lands and allows department to approve other land use regulations for those lands. Sunsets provisions on January 2, 2030.
