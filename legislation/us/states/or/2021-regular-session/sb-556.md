# Oregon SB 556 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to in-person inspections; and declaring an emergency.

Specifies that on-site inspections must be in person for <i>child-caring agencies, proctor foster homes, private residential boarding schools, foster homes, youth offender foster homes,</i>] health care facilities, home health agencies, residential<b> care</b> facilities, adult foster homes, <i>developmental disability child foster homes,</i>] hospice programs and qualified residential treatment programs.
