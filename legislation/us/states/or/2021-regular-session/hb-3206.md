# Oregon HB 3206 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the use of certain Employment Department funds; and declaring an emergency.

Expands permissible uses of amounts in Employment Department Special Fraud Control Fund, including payment of costs associated with federal Lost Wages Assistance program. Provides for methods of collection from debtor of reimbursements to Federal Emergency Management Agency for benefit overpayments required under Lost Wages Assistance program.
