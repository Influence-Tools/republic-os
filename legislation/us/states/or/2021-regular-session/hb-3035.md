# Oregon HB 3035 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to health care for adults in custody; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Directs Department of Corrections and Oregon Health Authority to establish healthcare navigator pilot program within Coffee Creek Correctional Facility. Directs department, in consultation with authority, to develop health assessment for persons entering facility. Establishes standards for provision of health care to persons in custody. Requires that person in custody be offered copy of medical records upon release.</i>]
