# Oregon SB 103 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to animal emergency operations planning.

Transfers duty to prepare written animal emergency operations plan from Office of Emergency Management to State Department of Agriculture. Requires department to cooperate with Department of Human Services in developing plan.
