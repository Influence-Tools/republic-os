# Oregon SB 204 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to criminal justice agencies.

Adds civilian <b>or community</b> oversight <b>boards,</b> agencies and review bodies designated by municipalities or law enforcement agencies to definition of "criminal justice agency." Allows civilian <b>or community</b> oversight <b>boards,</b> agencies and review bodies to access Law Enforcement Data System.
