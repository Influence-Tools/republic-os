# Oregon HB 2026 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to resident preferences in employment; and declaring an emergency.

Requires public employers to give preferences to residents of <i>Eastern Oregon Border Economic Development Region</i>] <b>Malheur County</b> for civil service positions if majority of work of position will be performed within <b>Eastern Oregon Border Economic Development</b> Region. <b>Provides that resident applicant who receives position must maintain residency in Malheur County for at least five consecutive years following date on which employment begins. Provides that failure to maintain residency shall be considered voluntary termination of employment. Provides that resident applicant who elects not to receive preference is not subject to residency requirement. Authorizes Eastern Oregon Border Economic Development Board, in consultation with governing body of Malheur County, to prescribe method for employee to notify employer of county of residence.</b>
