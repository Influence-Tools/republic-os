# Oregon SB 259 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to redistricting; and declaring an emergency.

Modifies statutory deadlines for reapportionment of congressional districts and judicial review.<b> Establishes dates by which special judicial panel must be appointed and by which panel and Oregon Supreme Court must establish briefing schedules.</b>
