# Oregon HB 3041 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to gender identity; and declaring an emergency.

Removes "gender identity" from definition of "sexual orientation" in ORS 174.100 and creates standalone definition of "gender identity" in ORS 174.100. Adds "gender identity" or "gender identities" to all statutes that reference "sexual orientation" or "sexual orientations."
