# Oregon SB 79 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to housing disparities.

Authorizes Housing and Community Services Department to provide grants <i>and loans to individuals for down payment assistance and grants, loans</i>] and technical assistance to organizations increasing homeownership program access to persons of color. Allows department to define "persons of color" by rule. <i>Authorizes expending Home Ownership Assistance Account funds for those purposes.</i>] Makes Oregon Housing Fund and subaccounts available to federally recognized Indian tribes that own land in this state.<b> Amends uses of Home Ownership Assistance Account.</b>
