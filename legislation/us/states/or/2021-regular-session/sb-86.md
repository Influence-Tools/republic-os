# Oregon SB 86 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to licensing of residential facilities.

Authorizes licensing agency to <b>revoke, suspend,</b> impose conditions on<b> or refuse to renew</b> license to operate residential<b> training</b> facility<b> or residential training home under specified conditions</b> and to consider performance and experience of applicant or licensee in other states or jurisdictions. Requires agency to adopt rules for imposition of conditions.
