# Oregon HB 3164 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the application of the crime of interfering with a peace officer or parole and probation officer; and declaring an emergency.

Modifies crime of interfering with a peace officer or parole and probation officer.<b> Removes refusal to obey officer order as manner of committing crime.</b> Provides that person may not be arrested for or charged with interfering with a peace officer or parole and probation officer <i>for conduct that would constitute any</i>]<b> if person is arrested or charged for</b> other criminal offense<b> based on same conduct</b>.
