# Oregon HB 3092 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to ground water levels; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires Water Resources Department to establish <i>grant</i>] program for purpose of sharing cost of remediating reductions in amount of ground water available for domestic personal use in Greater Harney Valley Groundwater Area of Concern due to overallocation of ground water <i>for agricultural use</i>].
