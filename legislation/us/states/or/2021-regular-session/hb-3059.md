# Oregon HB 3059 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to unlawful assemblies.

<i>Repeals</i>]<b> Modifies</b> statute authorizing law enforcement officers to command dispersal of unlawful assemblies and arrest participants who do not disperse.<b> Removes requirement to go among persons unlawfully assembled and arrest persons. Authorizes arrest for unlawful activity constituting offense.</b>
