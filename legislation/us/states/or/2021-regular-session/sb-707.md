# Oregon SB 707 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to notices to children placed in out-of-state residential facilities; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Human Services to provide certain notices to children and wards whom department placed in out-of-state residential facilities between January 1, 2016, and June 30, 2020, and <b>upon request</b> to provide representatives of children and wards with certain documentation and information.<b>
