# Oregon SB 279 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to school attendance of children in foster care; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Human Services, to extent practicable, to avoid scheduling meetings and appointments involving child in foster care during school day.<b> Requires caseworker to ensure child's case file indicates number of school days missed during semester if child misses more than four school days due to visitations scheduled by department.
