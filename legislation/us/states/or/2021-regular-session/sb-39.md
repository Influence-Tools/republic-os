# Oregon SB 39 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the Oregon State Board of Nursing; and prescribing an effective date.

Amends definition of "practice of nursing." Changes deadline for renewal of nursing licenses and nurses' authority to prescribe. Removes references to "noninjectable medication." Clarifies controlled substances that authorized nurse may prescribe.
