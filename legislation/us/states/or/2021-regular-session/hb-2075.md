# Oregon HB 2075 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to radiation; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes vendor license and annual fee for persons engaging in certain conduct regarding radiation devices and equipment, including X-ray machines and tanning devices. Modifies registration fee for certain radiation devices and equipment from per machine basis to per tube basis. Increases registration fee for tanning devices.
