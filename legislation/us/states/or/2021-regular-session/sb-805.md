# Oregon SB 805 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to vertical homeownership structures; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Specifies length of period within which person or public body may bring action for construction defect in vertical homeownership structures as six years. Defines "vertical homeownership structure.</i>]
