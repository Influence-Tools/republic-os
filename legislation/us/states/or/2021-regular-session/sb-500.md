# Oregon SB 500 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to the Public Records Advisory Council; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes Public Records Advocate as independent office<b> within executive department</b>. Requires Public Records Advocate to select and appoint Deputy Public Records Advocate and authorizes advocate to delegate authority, power or duty to act to deputy.
