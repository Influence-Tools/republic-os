# Oregon SB 493 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to determinations of the prevailing rate of wage in a locality; and prescribing an effective date.

Provides that prevailing rate of wage for trade or occupation in locality is rate of wage set forth in collective bargaining agreement for trade or occupation in locality or, if more than one collective bargaining agreement covers trade or occupation in locality, <i>average of</i>]<b> highest</b> rate of wage <i>set forth in</i>]<b> among</b> collective bargaining agreements for trade or occupation in locality.
