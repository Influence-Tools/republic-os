# Oregon HB 2363 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to temporary sales licenses; and declaring an emergency.

Allows certain holders of temporary event licenses to sell <b>specified</b> alcoholic beverages for on and off-premises consumption at more than one location on licensed premises <b>or have up to three premises licensed under single temporary sales license and operate for up to 30 days</b>. <b>Applies to temporary sales licenses issued on or after January 1, 2021.</b>
