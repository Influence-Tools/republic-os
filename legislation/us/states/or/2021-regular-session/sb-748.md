# Oregon SB 748 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to benefit plans.

Requires Public Employees' Benefit Board, Oregon Educators Benefit Board and private insurers to provide insurance coverage to adult disabled children <i>and adult disabled siblings</i>] of insureds under certain circumstances.
