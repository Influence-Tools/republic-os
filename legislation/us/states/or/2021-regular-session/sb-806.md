# Oregon SB 806 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to alcoholic beverages; and declaring an emergency.

Includes "fortified cider" in definition of "cider." Allows holder of winery license to purchase from Oregon Liquor Control Commission distilled liquors for fortifying cider. <i>Allows holder of direct shipper permit to ship up to five cases of wine per month to Oregon resident.</i>] Allows county to become licensee of commission. Deletes requirement that commission charge application fee for new licenses. Provides that commission may allow applicant to defer or waive payment of annual license fee if Governor declares state of emergency.
