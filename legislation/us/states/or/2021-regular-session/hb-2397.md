# Oregon HB 2397 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to emergency medical services for residents in long term care settings.

Establishes Senior Emergency Medical Services Innovation Program in Department of Human Services to fund and monitor certain local public sector pilot projects related to emergency medical services for seniors. Sunsets January 2, 2027.
