# Oregon HB 2163 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to rental assistance; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes long-term rental assistance program in Housing and Community Services Department<b> operating in three to four regions</b>. Requires department to report on program to Oregon Housing Stability Council by <i>December 31 of even years</i>] <b>September 15, 2022, and September 15, 2023</b>. Establishes <i>Rental</i>] <b>Long-Term Rent </b>Assistance Fund to fund program.
