# Oregon HB 2681 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to inactive voters; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Requires each county clerk to file annual report with Secretary of State identifying each voter who clerk moved to inactive status during previous calendar year and specifying reason for moving voter to inactive status.</i>]
