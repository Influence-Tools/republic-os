# Oregon HB 2362 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to health care providers.

Requires approval from Department of Consumer and Business Services or Oregon Health Authority before any mergers, acquisitions, contracts or affiliations of health care entities and other entities if entities <i>had $25 million or more</i>]<b> meet or exceed specified thresholds</b> in average net patient revenue or in gross amount of premiums in preceding three fiscal years <i>or net patient revenue of $10 million or more</i>]. Specifies exceptions and procedures.
