# Oregon SB 420 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to community benefit contracts; and prescribing an effective date.

Permits<b> contracting agency or</b> local contract review board <i>or local contracting agency</i>] to enact or adopt, as appropriate, ordinance, resolution, rule, regulation or other legislative or administrative measure to designate certain public improvement contracts as community benefit contracts. Specifies elements that community benefit contract must incorporate. Specifies duties of<b> contracting agency or</b> local contract review board <i>or local contracting agency</i>] before advertising, soliciting or awarding community benefit contract.
