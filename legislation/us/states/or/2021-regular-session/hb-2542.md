# Oregon HB 2542 (2021 Regular Session) — Relating to disclosure of mandatory fees at public institutions of higher education.

Requires each public university and community college to prominently display mandatory fees charged. Requires each public university and community college to provide annual report to Higher Education Coordinating Commission detailing compliance with requirement.
