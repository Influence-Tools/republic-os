# Oregon HB 2254 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to exemption from labeling for exported horticultural commodities.

Exempts horticultural commodities and commodity packs from labeling requirements if commodity is being exported from United States and shipping container used to transport commodity or commodity pack is labeled as being for export only and as otherwise required by State Department of Agriculture.
