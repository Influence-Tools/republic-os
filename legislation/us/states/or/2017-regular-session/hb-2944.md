# Oregon HB 2944 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the Housing Choice Landlord Guarantee Program; and declaring an emergency.

Limits landlord assistance under Housing Choice Landlord Guarantee Program to damages awarded in judgment following hearing in which landlord proves amount of damages.
