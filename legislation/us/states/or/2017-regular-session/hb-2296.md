# Oregon HB 2296 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to processes affecting the physical condition of wells.

Increases required bond amounts for persons drilling wells. Increases application fee for property owner seeking permit to construct, alter, abandon or convert well. Eliminates use of well drilling machine as condition for requiring property owner to obtain permit.
