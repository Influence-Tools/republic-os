# Oregon HB 3447 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the use of real property for child care.

Prohibits provision in instrument conveying real property that restricts use of real property as certified or registered family child care home or as premises of exempt family child care provider. Prohibits enforcement of condominium or homeowners association prohibition or restriction of use of unit as certified or registered family child care home or as premises of exempt family child care provider.
