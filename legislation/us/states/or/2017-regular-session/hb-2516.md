# Oregon HB 2516 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to abandoned cemeteries; and declaring an emergency.

Allows State Parks and Recreation Department to issue permits to entities to restore, maintain and preserve abandoned cemeteries. Allows department to adopt rules relating to issuance of permits.
