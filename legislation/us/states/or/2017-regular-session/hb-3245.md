# Oregon HB 3245 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to applications for amendments to a city comprehensive plan.

Permits city to authorize planning commission or hearings officer to conduct hearings and make <b>final </b>decisions on applications for amendments to city comprehensive plan map.<b> Permits person who appeared or participated in proceedings orally or in writing to appeal or petition for review of final decision to city governing body.</b>
