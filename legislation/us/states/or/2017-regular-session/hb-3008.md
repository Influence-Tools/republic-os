# Oregon HB 3008 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to false employment records.

Prohibits employer from requiring employee to create, file or sign documents containing information <b>that employer knows is false</b> related to hours worked or compensation received by employee. Establishes private cause of action. Authorizes court to award statutory damages of $1,000 or more per violation, injunctive relief, attorney fees and costs. Authorizes Commissioner of Bureau of Labor and Industries to assess civil penalty up to $1,000 per violation.
