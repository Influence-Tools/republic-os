# Oregon SB 116 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to military special access programs.

Requires State Forester to make any recreational opportunities in state forests free<b>, upon showing of certain identification and documentation,</b> for disabled veterans and <b>for</b> persons<b> who are</b> on leave from military active duty on certain holidays.
