# Oregon HB 3262 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to psychotropic medication; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Department of Human Services, in collaboration with other agencies, to adopt rules related to prescription of psychotropic medications to elderly persons and persons with disabilities. <b>Directs department to convene advisory committee to study implementation of rules adopted under Act and report to interim committee of Legislative Assembly related to human services by December 31, 2017.</b>
