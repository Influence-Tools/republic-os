# Oregon HB 3216 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to students in poverty families; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Chief Education Office to conduct study for purpose of identifying practices that assist students in poverty families. Directs office to submit report to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to education no later than September 15, 2018.
