# Oregon HB 2250 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to inmate release plan submission deadlines; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Corrections to adopt rules establishing deadlines by which department must submit proposed and revised inmate release plans to State Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision.
