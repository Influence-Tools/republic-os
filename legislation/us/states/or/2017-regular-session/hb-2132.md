# Oregon HB 2132 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to local government programs to finance improvements to real property; and prescribing an effective date.

Expands purposes for which improvements may be made under local government financing program to include energy storage, smart electric vehicle charging stations and water efficiency. Clarifies provisions relating to liens on benefited qualifying real property.
