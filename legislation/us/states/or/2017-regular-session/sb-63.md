# Oregon SB 63 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to correspondence of agencies exercising jurisdiction over persons.

Removes requirement that Psychiatric Security Review Board or Oregon Health Authority notify court or juvenile department of hearing concerning commitment of person found guilty except for insanity or youth found responsible except for insanity.
