# Oregon SB 1055 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to family law proceedings involving deployed parents in military service.

Permits court to enter temporary order allowing or requiring reasonable visitation between child of deployed parent and stepparent, grandparent or other family member related to child<b> with whom child has ongoing relationship</b>.
