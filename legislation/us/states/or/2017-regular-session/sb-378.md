# Oregon SB 378 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to charitable checkoffs; and prescribing an effective date.

Adds Oregon Volunteer Firefighters Association to list of entities eligible for individual income tax return checkoff contribution.<b> Provides conditions for use of contributions.</b>
