# Oregon SB 57 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the Oregon Public Guardian and Conservator.

Prohibits court from appointing deputy public guardian and conservator as fiduciary and requires court to appoint Oregon Public Guardian and Conservator as fiduciary.
