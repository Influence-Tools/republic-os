# Oregon HB 2114 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to prescription drugs; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs Oregon Medical Board, Oregon State Board of Nursing<b>, Oregon Board of Naturopathic Medicine</b> and Oregon Board of Dentistry to provide licensees of boards with opioid and opiate prescribing guidelines and recommendations established by association of licensee practitioners.
