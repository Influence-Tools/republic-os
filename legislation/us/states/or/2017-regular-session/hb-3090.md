# Oregon HB 3090 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to hospital discharge procedures; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires hospital to adopt policy for release of patients from emergency department following treatment for behavioral health crisis.<b> Requires hospitals to report and Oregon Health Authority to compile information about policies adopted. Requires authority to report to Legislative Assembly no later than January 1, 2018.
