# Oregon HB 2356 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to debt collection practices; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes requirements under which debt buyer or debt collector that acts on debt buyer's behalf may bring legal action to collect debt. Specifies notice that debt buyer must give to debtor and documents that debt buyer must give to debtor at debtor's request. Makes violation of requirements unlawful collection practice.
