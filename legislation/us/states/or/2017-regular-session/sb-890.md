# Oregon SB 890 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to privileged city information disclosed to elected city auditor; and declaring an emergency.

<b>Defines "legally protected material" to mean information and records of city that are protected by attorney-client privilege held by city and attorney work product prepared in course of providing legal services to city.</b> Provides that disclosure of legally protected material to elected city auditor does not waive privilege with respect to any other person, to extent legally protected material is disclosed for purpose of audit or investigation.
