# Oregon SB 182 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to professional development for educators in early learning through grade 12; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes Educator Advancement Council to provide resources related to educator professional learning and other educator supports. Prescribes duties of council, including establishment of educator networks. Requires council to submit report on progress of council to Legislative Assembly. Phases in transfer of duties of Network of Quality Teaching and Learning to council. Abolishes network on June 30, 2019.
