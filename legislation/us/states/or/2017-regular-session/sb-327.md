# Oregon SB 327 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to recreational immunity from claims of persons entering land for certain purposes; and declaring an emergency.

Provides recreational immunity to holder of any legal or equitable title of land. Extends recreational immunity to employees and agents of owner of land when acting within scope of duties and those with private property interests in land.
