# Oregon SB 497 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to orders setting aside arrest records.

Provides that person may file motion to set aside record of criminal citation or criminal charge in addition to record of arrest. Provides that in certain circumstances, prior criminal citation or criminal charge within specified time period disqualifies person from filing motion to set aside record of arrest, criminal citation, criminal charge or conviction.
