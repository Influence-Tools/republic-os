# Oregon SB 488 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to towing.

Requires law enforcement agency that recovers vehicle reported as stolen to share owner's contact information with tower that assumes control of vehicle. Requires tower to attempt to provide certain notice to owner of stolen vehicle as soon as is practicable.
