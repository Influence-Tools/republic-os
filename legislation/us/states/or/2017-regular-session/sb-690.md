# Oregon SB 690 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to Certificates of Good Standing.

Establishes procedures for petitioning for and issuing Certificate of Good Standing.<b> Limits eligibility to persons convicted of nonperson felony or Class A misdemeanor other than person Class A misdemeanor. Requires written verification of successful completion of supervision for certain applicants.</b>
