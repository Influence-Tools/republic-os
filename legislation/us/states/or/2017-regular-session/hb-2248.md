# Oregon HB 2248 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to disclosure of certain juvenile records to Department of Corrections; and declaring an emergency.

Extends sunset date on temporary law authorizing Oregon Youth Authority and county juvenile departments to disclose and provide copies of reports and other materials relating to child, ward, youth or youth offender's history and prognosis to Department of Corrections for purpose of exercising custody or supervising person committed to department's legal and physical custody.
