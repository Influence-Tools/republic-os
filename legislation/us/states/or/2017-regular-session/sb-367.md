# Oregon SB 367 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to disclosure of protected health information regarding inmate of Department of Corrections facility.

Permits health care provider to disclose protected health information of inmate of correctional facility to physician of employee of correctional facility<b> or Oregon Corrections Enterprises</b> who is exposed to bodily fluids of inmate if exposure is substantial and inmate has tested positive for HIV, hepatitis B or C or other communicable disease.
