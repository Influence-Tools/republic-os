# Oregon HB 2624 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to foreign financial institutions.

Exempts out-of-state banks, extranational institutions and foreign associations from requirement to file notice before dealing in notes secured by mortgages or trust deeds, if authorized to conduct banking business in this state by certificate of Department of Consumer and Business Services.
