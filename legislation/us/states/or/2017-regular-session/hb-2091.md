# Oregon HB 2091 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to telecommunications carriers; and prescribing an effective date.

Authorizes Public Utility Commission to use universal service fund moneys to encourage broadband service availability and to provide support to telecommunications carriers that provide both basic telephone service and broadband service.<b> Limits amount of surcharge imposed for purpose of depositing moneys in fund.</b>
