# Oregon HB 3014 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to respiratory therapists; and declaring an emergency.

Requires applicants for initial license to practice respiratory therapy to hold credential as Registered Respiratory Therapist. Applies to applications received by Health Licensing Office on or after operative date of Act.
