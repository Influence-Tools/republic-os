# Oregon HB 2482 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to self-service fuel dispensaries in low-population counties.

Limits self-service fuel dispensing to low-population counties of eastern Oregon and specified counties. Removes hours of operation restriction on self-service at filling stations, service stations, garages and other dispensaries in low-population counties of eastern Oregon.
