# Oregon HB 5030 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the financial administration of the State Board of Pharmacy; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by State Board of Pharmacy.
