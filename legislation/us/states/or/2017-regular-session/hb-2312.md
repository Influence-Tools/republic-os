# Oregon HB 2312 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to implementation of the federal Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act; and declaring an emergency.

Updates certain terms and references used throughout state law to conform with changes to federal law associated with enactment of federal Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act. Changes name of State Workforce Investment Board to State Workforce Development Board.
