# Oregon SB 743 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to dextromethorphan.

Creates violation prohibiting business that makes retail sales of product containing dextromethorphan, or employee of business, from selling or delivering product to individual 17 years of age or younger unless individual has valid prescription.
