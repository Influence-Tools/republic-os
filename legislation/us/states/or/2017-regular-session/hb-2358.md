# Oregon HB 2358 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to gaming.

Increases charitable gaming maximum prize value for bingo or lotto games. Increases percentage of annual handle amount that may be spent on charitable gaming operating expenses. Increases amount of time that person may spend administering or operating charitable gaming. Increases amount of time that gaming licensee may operate charitable gaming.
