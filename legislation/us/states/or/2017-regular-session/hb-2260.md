# Oregon HB 2260 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to child care information maintained by the Office of Child Care; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Office of Child Care to maintain information about child care facilities and providers through electronic records systems. Authorizes office to share information with other public entities when sharing information would support health or safety of children in child care.
