# Oregon HB 3340 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to apprenticeships; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Department of Education to develop and provide to public high schools written materials regarding apprenticeship opportunities. Requires public high schools to disseminate materials to students and to parents or guardians of students.
