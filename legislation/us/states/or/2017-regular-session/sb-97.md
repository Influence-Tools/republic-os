# Oregon SB 97 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to modernizing insurance corporate governance; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that Director of Department of Consumer and Business Services must act as, or acknowledge another regulatory official as, group-wide supervisor for internationally active insurance group. Specifies criteria for and conditions under which director must assume authority to act as group-wide supervisor or acknowledge other regulatory official's authority as group-wide supervisor. Specifies actions director may take as group-wide supervisor.
