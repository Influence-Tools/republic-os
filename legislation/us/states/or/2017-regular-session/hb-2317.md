# Oregon HB 2317 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to health professional regulatory boards; and declaring an emergency.

Increases term of member of State Mortuary and Cemetery Board from three years to four years. Specifies that member may be removed from board if, during term, member no longer meets qualifications for office.
