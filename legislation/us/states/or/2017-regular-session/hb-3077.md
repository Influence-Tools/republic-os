# Oregon HB 3077 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to crime victims.

Prohibits defense attorney, or district attorney if defendant is not represented by attorney, from disclosing to defendant victim's electronic mail address and social media account identifying information unless court authorizes or orders disclosure.
