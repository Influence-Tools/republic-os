# Oregon HB 2616 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to right to counsel in juvenile delinquency proceedings.

Sets forth circumstances under which court shall appoint counsel for youth in juvenile delinquency proceedings. Prohibits court from accepting waiver of counsel by youth, with exceptions.
