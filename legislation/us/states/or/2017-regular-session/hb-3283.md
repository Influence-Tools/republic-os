# Oregon HB 3283 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to animals; and declaring an emergency.

Amends findings by Legislative Assembly regarding provision of care and treatment for impounded or seized animals. Revises provisions regarding defendant's payment of costs to provide animal with minimum care.
