# Oregon HB 2737 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to construction standards for small homes; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires Director of Department of Consumer and Business Services to adopt construction standards for homes that have floor area of not more than 600 square feet. Identifies certain matters for inclusion in standards. <b>Requires that initial construction standards for small homes become effective by January 1, 2018.</b> Requires director to report to committee of Legislative Assembly no later than March 1, 2019, regarding implementation and use of construction standards for small homes. Allows inclusion of director's recommendations regarding small home construction standards in report.
