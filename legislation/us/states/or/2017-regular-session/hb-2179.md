# Oregon HB 2179 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to onsite treatment of certain wastes prior to application on exclusive farm use land; and declaring an emergency.

Permits onsite treatment of septage prior to application <b>of biosolids</b> on exclusive farm use land using treatment facilities that are portable, temporary and transportable by truck trailer<b> during authorized period of time</b>.
