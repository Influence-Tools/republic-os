# Oregon HB 2360 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to sex offender reporting; and declaring an emergency.

Modifies reporting obligation for sex offenders by requiring report within 10 days following change of residence. Removes requirement that sex offender have acquired new residence before obligation to report is triggered.
