# Oregon SB 84 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to a surcharge collected for the purpose of funding telecommunications programs.

Makes surcharge collected for purposes related to protection for residential services and assistance for persons with disabilities apply to paying retail subscribers who have interconnected voice over internet protocol service.
