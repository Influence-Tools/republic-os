# Oregon HB 3063 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to housing for individuals with mental illness; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Housing and Community Services Department, in collaboration with Oregon Health Authority, to disburse moneys in Housing for Mental Health Fund for development of community-based housing, crisis intervention services, rental subsidies and other housing-related services for individuals with mental illness <b>and individuals with substance use disorders</b>. Requires department to convene advisory group.
