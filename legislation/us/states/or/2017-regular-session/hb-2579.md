# Oregon HB 2579 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to supervision of young offenders.

Authorizes Oregon Youth Authority to provide reentry support and services, for specified period of time, to person who completes Department of Corrections incarceration sentence <b>while</b> in physical custody of authority.
