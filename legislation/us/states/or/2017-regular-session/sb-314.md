# Oregon SB 314 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to Relief Nursery programs; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Early Learning Division to support Relief Nursery programs statewide. Requires Relief Nursery programs to participate in statewide independent evaluation. Describes how funding to support Relief Nursery programs may be used.
