# Oregon SB 476 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to disabled veterans as preferred workers eligible for reemployment assistance; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires Advisory Committee to Director of Veterans' Affairs to review, consider and report to interim legislative committees relating to veterans whether and to what extent disabled veteran should qualify as preferred worker.
