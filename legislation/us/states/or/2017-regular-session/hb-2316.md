# Oregon HB 2316 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to review of comprehensive plans that require application of statewide planning goal concerning buildable lands for residential use for certain cities.

Requires city with population of less than 25,000 to determine estimated housing need for 20-year period, inventory buildable land and adopt measures as part of periodic or legislative review of comprehensive plan.
