# Oregon SB 819 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to Critical Incident Response Teams; and declaring an emergency.

Expands parameters of duties and responsibilities of Critical Incident Response Team assigned by Department of Human Services upon becoming aware of fatality of child that was result of abuse or neglect.
