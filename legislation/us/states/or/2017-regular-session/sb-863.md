# Oregon SB 863 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to cannabis; and declaring an emergency.

Prohibits marijuana retailers from recording, retaining and transferring type of information that is contained on passport, driver license, military identification card or other identification card that bears picture of person. Creates exemptions.
