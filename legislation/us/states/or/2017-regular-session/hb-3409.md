# Oregon HB 3409 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to exceptions to compulsory school attendance.

Exempts from compulsory school attendance requirements children who are present in United States on nonimmigrant visa and who are attending <b>private,</b> accredited English language learner program in preparation for attending private high school or college.
