# Oregon SB 323 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to arson caused by the manufacture of controlled substances.

Creates crime of arson incident to the manufacture of a controlled substance in the second degree if person manufactures controlled substance and causes fire or explosion that damages building or damages property in amount exceeding $750. Punishes by maximum of five years' imprisonment, $125,000 fine, or both.
