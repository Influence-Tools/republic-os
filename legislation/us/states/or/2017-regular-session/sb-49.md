# Oregon SB 49 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to juvenile fitness to proceed proceedings.

Prohibits removal of youth from current placement for purpose of receiving fitness to proceed evaluation<b> unless youth is placed in detention facility or youth correction facility</b>.
