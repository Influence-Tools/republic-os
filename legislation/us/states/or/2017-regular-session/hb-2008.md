# Oregon HB 2008 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to manufactured dwellings; and declaring an emergency.

Updates amount landlord must pay tenant of manufactured dwelling park upon closure of park to convert to other use. Requires Office of Manufactured Dwelling Park Community Relations to establish by rule process to annually recalculate amount landlord must pay tenant upon closure of park to convert to other use.
