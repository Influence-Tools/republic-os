# Oregon HB 2253 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the supervision of Department of Corrections inmates performing certain work; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that Department of Corrections inmate may perform electrical or plumbing work if supervised by licensed electrician or plumber who is employee of Oregon Corrections Enterprises.
