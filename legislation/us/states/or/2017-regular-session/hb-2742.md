# Oregon HB 2742 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the Wildfire Damage Housing Relief Account; and declaring an emergency.

Increases household income limitation for receipt of financial assistance from Wildfire Damage Housing Relief Account. Increases <b>maximum</b> amount of each grant issued from account. Allows reduction or refusal of grant if household receives other compensation for loss.
