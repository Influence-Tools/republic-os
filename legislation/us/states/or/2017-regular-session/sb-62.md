# Oregon SB 62 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to intrastate mutual assistance.

Amends intrastate mutual assistance compact to require state government participation and allow tribal government participation. Directs Office of Emergency Management to adopt procedures for requesting and providing intrastate assistance.
