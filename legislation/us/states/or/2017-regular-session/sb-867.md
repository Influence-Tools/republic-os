# Oregon SB 867 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to maritime sector workforce development; and prescribing an effective date.

Creates Task Force on Maritime Sector Workforce Development. Directs task force to study and develop description of maritime sector and maritime sector workforce in Oregon and develop recommendations for State Workforce Investment Board to incorporate consideration of maritime sector into statewide workforce investment system plan.
