# Oregon HB 2763 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to teachers.

Authorizes Teacher Standards and Practices Commission to disburse moneys from National Board Certification Fund for reimbursement of certain costs related to national board certification of teachers. Expands sources of moneys for fund.
