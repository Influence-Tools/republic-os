# Oregon SB 162 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to tax credits; and prescribing an effective date.

Modifies definition of "business tax credit," as allowed to shareholders of S corporation, to remove Department of Revenue rulemaking authority. <b>Updates list of eligible tax credits.</b> Modifies tax credit allowed for employment-related dependent care expenses to conform to operation of related federal tax credit. Prescribes eligibility requirements for earned income for allowance of credit.<b> Allows nonmarried taxpayer to claim credit. Prohibits payment of interest for refundable portion of credit.</b>
