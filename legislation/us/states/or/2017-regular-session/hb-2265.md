# Oregon HB 2265 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the 150-day period after which parties involved in negotiations over the terms of a collective bargaining agreement may notify the Employment Relations Board of the need for a mediator.

Allows parties to agree in writing to starting date for 150-day period after which parties involved in negotiations over terms of collective bargaining agreement may notify Employment Relations Board of need for mediator. Removes requirement that parties receive initial proposal before 150-day period begins.
