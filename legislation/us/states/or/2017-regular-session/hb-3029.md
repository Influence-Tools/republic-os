# Oregon HB 3029 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to compulsory school attendance; and declaring an emergency.

Allows parent or legal guardian of child whose sixth birthday occurred on or before September 1 immediately preceding beginning of current school year to delay enrolling child in public full-time school for one year for purpose of better meeting child's needs for cognitive, social or physical development.
