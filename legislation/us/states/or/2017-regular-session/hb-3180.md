# Oregon HB 3180 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to disclosure of information for purposes of administering transient lodging taxes; and prescribing an effective date.

Authorizes Department of Revenue and units of local government to disclose to each other information related to administration of transient lodging tax laws. Provides process for ensuring confidentiality of state transient lodging tax information.
