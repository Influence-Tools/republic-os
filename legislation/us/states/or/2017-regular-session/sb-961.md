# Oregon SB 961 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to driving while under the influence of intoxicants diversion agreements.

Allows defendant to file petition for driving while under influence of intoxicants diversion agreement 14 days after prosecuting attorney sends evidence under certain circumstances.
