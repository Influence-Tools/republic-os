# Oregon HB 2186 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to workers' compensation coverage for self-insured public bodies.

Provides that Director of Department of Consumer and Business Services may certify self-insurance program created by intergovernmental agreement as self-insured employer for purposes of workers' compensation insurance.
