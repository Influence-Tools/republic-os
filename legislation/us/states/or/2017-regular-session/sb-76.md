# Oregon SB 76 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to unarmed combat sports; and prescribing an effective date.

Defines "unarmed combat sports." Authorizes Oregon State Athletic Commission to regulate unarmed combat sports. Permits Superintendent of State Police to approve amateur athletic organizations. Requires promoters of unarmed combat sports events to reimburse medical personnel and certain officials for specified expenses incurred during events.
