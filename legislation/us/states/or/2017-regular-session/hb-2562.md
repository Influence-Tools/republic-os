# Oregon HB 2562 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to notices required from lenders with contracts for reverse mortgages.

Requires lender that has contract with person for reverse mortgage to send person<b> or escrow agent, title insurance company or other agent that pays property taxes on person's behalf</b> notice that states that title to property that is subject to reverse mortgage remains with person and person is responsible for paying property taxes and related taxes on property. Provides that lender must send notice each year at least 60 days before property taxes are due on property.
