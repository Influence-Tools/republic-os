# Oregon SB 1032 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the Oregon Promise program; and declaring an emergency.

Amends Oregon Promise program to remove prohibition on awarding more than $10 million in grants per fiscal year. Makes requirement that person participating in program pay $50 per term in tuition payable by reducing amount of grant awarded, instead of requiring separate payment by person.
