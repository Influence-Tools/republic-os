# Oregon HB 2903 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to child-caring agencies; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Department of Human Services to immediately place conditions on license, certificate or other authorization of child-caring agency prior to hearing if department finds there is serious danger to public health or safety.
