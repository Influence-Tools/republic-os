# Oregon HB 5024 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the financial administration of the Oregon State Board of Nursing; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by Oregon State Board of Nursing.
