# Oregon HB 2359 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to copies of notices of a denial of eligibility for a foreclosure avoidance measure.

Removes requirement for beneficiary in trust deed to send, and Attorney General to receive, copy of notice that beneficiary has denied grantor's eligibility for foreclosure avoidance measure.
