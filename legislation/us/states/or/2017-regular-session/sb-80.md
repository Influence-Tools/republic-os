# Oregon SB 80 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to assistance to veterans who are members of federally recognized Indian tribes.

Directs Director of Veterans' Affairs to provide aid and assistance to federally recognized Indian tribes in Oregon with regard to programs of service for Native American veterans and accreditation of federal tribal veterans representatives.<b> Requires consultation with appropriate tribal officials and members.</b>
