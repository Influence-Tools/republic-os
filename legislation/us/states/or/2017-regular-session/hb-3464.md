# Oregon HB 3464 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the involvement of public bodies in immigration enforcement; and declaring an emergency.

Prohibits public body from disclosing specified information concerning person unless required by state or federal law. Prohibits public body from inquiring concerning person's citizenship or immigration status except as required by state or federal law or when determining benefit eligibility.
