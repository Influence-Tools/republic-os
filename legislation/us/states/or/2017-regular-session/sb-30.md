# Oregon SB 30 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to unitary business determinations of corporate affiliates; and prescribing an effective date.

In making determination of whether corporate affiliates are members of unitary group, allows reference to any corporation that is owned or controlled directly or indirectly by same interests.
