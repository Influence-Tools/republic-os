# Oregon SB 767 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to sex offenders.

Extends, from December 1, 2018, to December 1, 2022, deadline by which State Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision must classify existing registrant obligated to report as sex offender prior to January 1, 2014. Provides that only existing registrants classified as level three, highest risk, or level two, moderate risk, may petition classifying agency for review. <b>Removes Oregon Health Authority as classifying agency.</b>
