# Oregon HB 2314 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to higher education; and declaring an emergency.

Permits Higher Education Coordinating Commission to establish by rule minimum <b>hourly training requirement or student proficiency-based training requirement</b> standards required for graduating from hair design, barbering, esthetics or nail technology schools.
