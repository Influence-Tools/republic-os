# Oregon HB 3427 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to dams with high hazard ratings.

Requires owner or operator of dam with high hazard rating to develop emergency action plan for dam. Requires determining frequency for conducting of emergency response exercises at dams with high hazard rating. Requires Water Resources Department to conduct periodic inspection of dams with high hazard rating.<b> Creates exception.</b>
