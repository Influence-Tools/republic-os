# Oregon SB 980 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to service in connection with recalled motor vehicles.

Requires manufacturer to compensate manufacturer's franchisee for all labor and parts manufacturer requires franchisees to use to perform repairs on vehicles that are subject to recall.
