# Oregon SB 231 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to mental health issues in higher education; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes Task Force on Student Mental Health Support. Directs task force to investigate mental health issues and substance abuse disorders among students, with emphasis on college recruitment, retention and completion<b> and how issues and disorders differ across different communities in this state</b>.
