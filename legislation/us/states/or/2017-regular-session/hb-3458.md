# Oregon HB 3458 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to work time limits; and declaring an emergency.

Directs employer in specified industries to pay greater of daily or weekly overtime when employee is eligible for both in same workweek. Defines "workweek" for purposes of calculating weekly hours worked. Declares that employer satisfies certain overtime laws by paying greater of applicable daily or weekly overtime.
