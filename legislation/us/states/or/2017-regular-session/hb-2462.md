# Oregon HB 2462 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to exemptions from maximum vehicle weight limitations.

Increases amount by which vehicle equipped with fully functional idle reduction system designed to reduce fuel use and emissions from engine idling may exceed maximum weight limitations.
