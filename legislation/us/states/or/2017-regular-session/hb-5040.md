# Oregon HB 5040 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the financial administration of the Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board to allocate moneys from Watershed Conservation Operating Fund to various state agencies to implement Article XV, section 4b, of Oregon Constitution.
