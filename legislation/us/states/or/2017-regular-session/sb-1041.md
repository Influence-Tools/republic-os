# Oregon SB 1041 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to treatment funded by public moneys; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Oregon Criminal Justice Commission to study expenditure of public moneys on alcohol and drug treatment and report results to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to judiciary on or before September 15, 2019.
