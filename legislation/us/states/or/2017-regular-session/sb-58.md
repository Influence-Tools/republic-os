# Oregon SB 58 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the Long Term Care Ombudsman; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Long Term Care Ombudsman to appoint Residential Facilities Ombudsman within office of Long Term Care Ombudsman. Specifies duties of Residential Facilities Ombudsman. Renames Residential Facilities Advisory Committee as Residential Ombudsman and Public Guardianship Advisory Board.
