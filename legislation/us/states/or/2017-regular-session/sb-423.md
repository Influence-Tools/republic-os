# Oregon SB 423 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to physician assistants.

Allows physician assistants to dispense controlled substances in Schedules III and IV under federal Controlled Substances Act.<b> Requires physician assistants to report dispensing of controlled substances to Oregon Health Authority.</b>
