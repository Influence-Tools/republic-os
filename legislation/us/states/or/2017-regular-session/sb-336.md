# Oregon SB 336 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to responsible managing individuals.

Requires inclusion of certain information in notification that contractor or business sends to Construction Contractors Board upon person ceasing to be responsible managing individual for contractor or business. Allows contractor or business to operate with temporary responsible managing individual for limited period.
