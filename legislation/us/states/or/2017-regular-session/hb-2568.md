# Oregon HB 2568 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to utilities; and declaring an emergency.

Specifies, for purposes of exempting certain vehicles from overall allowable length of vehicles under Vehicle Code, that unplanned disruption in services provided by public utility, telecommunications utility, people's utility district<b>, municipality</b> or electric cooperative is emergency if disruption occurs outside normal business hours during which public utility, telecommunications utility, people's utility district<b>, municipality</b> or electric cooperative may acquire variance permit.
