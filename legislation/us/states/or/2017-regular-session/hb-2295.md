# Oregon HB 2295 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to fees charged for Water Resources Department services; and declaring an emergency.

Increases fees, maximum fees and fee return thresholds for Water Resources Department services. Makes fee, maximum fee and fee return threshold increases operative July 1, 2017. Eliminates fee, maximum fee and fee return threshold reductions scheduled for July 1, 2017.
