# Oregon HB 2510 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to electric vehicle charging stations; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes commercial tenant to install on premises and use electric vehicle charging station. Declares charging station to be personal property of tenant unless different result is negotiated between parties.
