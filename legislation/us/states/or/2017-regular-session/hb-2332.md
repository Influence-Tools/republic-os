# Oregon HB 2332 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the ratio of state agency public employees to supervisory employees.

Requires certain state agencies to determine maximum ratio of supervisory employees to nonsupervisory employees and report maximum supervisory ratio to Joint Committee on Ways and Means during biennial budget process.
