# Oregon SB 374 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the Real ID Act of 2005; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Department of Transportation to issue driver licenses, driver permits and identification cards that meet requirements of federal Real ID Act of 2005. Real ID compliant documents shall be known as Real IDs.
