# Oregon SB 561 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to dentistry; and declaring an emergency.

Exempts from prohibition on practice of dentistry and dental hygiene without valid license students of dentistry and dental hygiene engaged in certain clinical studies performed under indirect supervision of dental faculty member.
