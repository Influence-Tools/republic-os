# Oregon HB 2845 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to statewide social studies standards; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Education to convene advisory group to develop statewide ethnic studies standards for adoption into existing statewide social studies standards for public kindergarten through grade 12. Directs department to adopt ethnic studies standards into existing statewide social studies standards for public kindergarten through grade 12.
