# Oregon SB 931 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to alternate jurors.

<b>Provides that court has discretion over selection of alternate jurors and when alternate jurors are informed of alternate status.</b> Authorizes court<b>, with prior agreement of parties,</b> to replace discharged juror with alternate juror in either guilt or sentencing phase of criminal case after jury begins deliberations.
