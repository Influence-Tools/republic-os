# Oregon SB 769 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to disposing of material that displays Social Security numbers.

Provides that person may not dispose of<b>, or transfer to another person for disposal,</b> material or media that display Social Security number unless person, before disposing of material or media, makes Social Security number unreadable or unrecoverable or ensures that person that ultimately disposes of media or material makes Social Security number<b> unreadable or unrecoverable</b>.
