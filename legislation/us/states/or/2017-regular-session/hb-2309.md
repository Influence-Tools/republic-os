# Oregon HB 2309 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to forensic evaluations of criminal defendants.

Provides that progress reports of defendants lacking fitness to proceed who have been committed to state mental hospital or facility for treatment<b> may consist of updates to original examination reports or evaluations</b>.
