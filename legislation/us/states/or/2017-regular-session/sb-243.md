# Oregon SB 243 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to abuse reporting regarding children in care; and declaring an emergency.

Expands definition of "child in care" to include children receiving care and services from certified foster homes and developmental disabilities residential facilities.<b> Excludes child from definition of "child in care" when care is provided in child's home by child's parent.
