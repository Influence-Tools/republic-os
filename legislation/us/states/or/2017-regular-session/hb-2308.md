# Oregon HB 2308 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the maximum period of commitment.

Provides that in calculation of maximum period of commitment for defendant lacking fitness to proceed, defendant must receive credit for each day defendant is held in jail unless defendant is charged with certain offenses requiring mandatory minimum sentences.
