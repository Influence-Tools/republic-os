# Oregon SB 899 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to receivership.

Creates uniform procedures for receiverships initiated in courts of this state, with certain exceptions. Provides that Act controls over conflicting provisions of ORCP 80 with respect to receiverships governed by Act.
