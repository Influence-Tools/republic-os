# Oregon HB 2315 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to tax credit for affordable housing lenders; and prescribing an effective date.

Provides that corporate excise tax credit allowed to lenders that finance affordable housing is not barred by receipt of housing vouchers by tenants.
