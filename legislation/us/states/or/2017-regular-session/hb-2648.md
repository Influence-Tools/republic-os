# Oregon HB 2648 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to grants to decrease rates of school absenteeism; and declaring an emergency.

Adds school social worker to list of professions included in school-based system for which school district or education service district may receive moneys for decreasing rates of school absenteeism.
