# Oregon HB 2998 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to transfer of credits to public post-secondary institutions of education; and declaring an emergency.

Requires community colleges and public universities to establish foundational curriculum or foundational curricula for first year of coursework at public post-secondary institutions of education and establishes requirements for foundational curricula.
