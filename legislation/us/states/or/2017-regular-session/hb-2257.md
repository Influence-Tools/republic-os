# Oregon HB 2257 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to fees for instructional materials; and declaring an emergency.

Allows Department of Education to waive, under limited circumstances, fee for publisher or supplier who submits textbook title or item of instructional material for review by State Board of Education.
