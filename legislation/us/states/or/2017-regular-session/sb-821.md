# Oregon SB 821 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the Emergency Housing Account.

Directs Oregon Housing Stability Council, with advice of Community Action Partnership of Oregon, to develop policy for awarding grants to organizations that shall use funds in Emergency Housing Account to align with federal strategies and resources available to prevent and end homelessness.
