# Oregon HB 2930 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to required notifications to military regarding certain child abuse reports.

Authorizes Department of Human Services or law enforcement agency to collect information concerning military status of parent or guardian of child who is subject of report of child abuse and share information with appropriate military authorities.
