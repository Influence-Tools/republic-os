# Oregon HB 5009 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the financial administration of the State Department of Energy; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by State Department of Energy.
