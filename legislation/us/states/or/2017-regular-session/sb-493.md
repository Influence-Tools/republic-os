# Oregon SB 493 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to assault.

Creates new manner of committing crime of assault in the fourth degree when person with criminal negligence causes serious physical injury to vulnerable user of public way by means of motor vehicle. Punishes by maximum of one year's imprisonment, $6,250 fine, or both.
