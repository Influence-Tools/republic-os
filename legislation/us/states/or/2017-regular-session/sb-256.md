# Oregon SB 256 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to Willamette Falls navigation infrastructure; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes Willamette Falls Locks Commission as advisory body for issues relating to repair, reopening, operation, maintenance and future transfer of ownership of navigational canal and locks.
