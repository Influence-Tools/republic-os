# Oregon HB 2400 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the administration of local transient lodging taxes; and prescribing an effective date.

Authorizes any state agency or department to enter into agreements with any political subdivision for collection, enforcement, administration and distribution of local transient lodging taxes of such political subdivision.
