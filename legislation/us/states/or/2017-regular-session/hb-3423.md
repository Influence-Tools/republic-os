# Oregon HB 3423 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to date to enroll in community college to qualify for the Oregon Promise.

Exempts person serving in Oregon National Guard from requirement to enroll in community college courses within six months of attaining previous highest level of education in order to qualify for Oregon Promise grant<b>, provided that person enrolls in community college courses within six months of completing initial active duty training</b>.
