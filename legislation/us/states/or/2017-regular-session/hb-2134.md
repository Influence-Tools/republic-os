# Oregon HB 2134 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to low-income electric bill payment assistance; and prescribing an effective date.

Changes process by which Public Utility Commission collects moneys from electric companies for purposes related to low-income electric bill payment assistance. Raises amount that may be collected from $15 million to $20 million.
