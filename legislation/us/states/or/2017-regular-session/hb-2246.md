# Oregon HB 2246 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the High School Graduation and College and Career Readiness Act; and declaring an emergency.

Expands entities that are eligible to receive apportionments under Ballot Measure 98 (2016) to include Oregon School for the Deaf, Youth Corrections Education Program and Juvenile Detention Education Program. Prescribes method for calculating apportionments for school and programs.
