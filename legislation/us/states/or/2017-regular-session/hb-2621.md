# Oregon HB 2621 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to crime victim compensation.

Specifies what constitutes notification to law enforcement officials for purpose of determining eligibility of crime victim for <b>Department of Justice compensation award. Specifies circumstances that are prima facie evidence of good cause for victim's failure to notify law enforcement in timely manner or failure to cooperate with law enforcement.
