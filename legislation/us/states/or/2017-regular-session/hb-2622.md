# Oregon HB 2622 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to abuse prevention; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes financial institution to refuse certain financial transactions when financial institution reasonably believes financial exploitation of vulnerable person may have occurred, may have been attempted or is being attempted.
