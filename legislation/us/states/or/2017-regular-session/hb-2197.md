# Oregon HB 2197 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to cannabis; and prescribing an effective date.

Allows Department of Revenue to enter into agreement with qualified governing body of federally recognized Indian tribe for purpose of making rebate payments for estimate of tax on marijuana items. Specifies requirement by which governing body is considered qualified.
