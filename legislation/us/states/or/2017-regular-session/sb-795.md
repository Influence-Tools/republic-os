# Oregon SB 795 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to crime victims.

Requires medical assessment provider or law enforcement officer to contact victim advocate and make reasonable efforts to ensure that victim advocate is present and available at medical facility.<b> Specifies what victim advocate is obligated to do and prohibited from doing.</b>
