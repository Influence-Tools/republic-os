# Oregon HB 3398 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to state financial administration; and declaring an emergency.

Extends sunset on Oregon Medical Insurance Pool Account to January 2, 2018, and continuously appropriates moneys in account to Department of Consumer and Business Services. Requires balance of moneys in account to be refunded to insurers on November 1, 2017.
