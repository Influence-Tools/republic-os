# Oregon HB 2279 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to registration of appraisers; and prescribing an effective date.

Transfers to Department of Revenue sole authority for examining and registering property appraisers and setting education and experience requirements. Consolidates Property Appraiser I, II and III classifications into single Property Appraiser classification. Eliminates general knowledge examination requirement.
