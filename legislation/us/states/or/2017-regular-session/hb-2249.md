# Oregon HB 2249 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to Department of Corrections provision of supplemental funding to counties for reentry support for certain offenders released before attaining 25 years of age.

Authorizes Department of Corrections to enter into agreements or arrangements with counties to provide supplemental funding for reentry support and services for offenders released before attaining 25 years of age.
