# Oregon HB 2533 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to bee colonies.

Eliminates State Department of Agriculture bee colony registration fee for colonies having primary purpose of producing new queens or workers to start new colony or add to existing colony. Limits local government charge for registering hive in residential area.
