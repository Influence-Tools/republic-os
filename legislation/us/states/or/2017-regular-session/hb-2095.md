# Oregon HB 2095 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to amendment to an urban growth boundary by a metropolitan service district based on a one-time revision of the most recent demonstration of sufficient buildable lands.

Allows metropolitan service district to revise demonstration of sufficient buildable lands on single occasion under certain circumstances and to expand urban growth boundary if metropolitan service district concludes expansion is warranted.
