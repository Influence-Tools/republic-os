# Oregon SB 79 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to foreclosures of residential trust deeds by the Department of Veterans' Affairs; and declaring an emergency.

Permits Department of Veterans' Affairs, in seeking judicial or nonjudicial foreclosure on residential trust deed, to file affidavit that states that department is exempt from requirement to request or participate in resolution conference.
