# Oregon HB 2457 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to resolving student complaints against schools; and declaring an emergency.

Alters process for resolving student complaints against certain schools. Permits Higher Education Coordinating Commission to refer student complaints to other entities for resolution. Exempts student complaints from disclosure under public records law.
