# Oregon HB 2150 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to electronic administration of alcoholic beverage tax provisions; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs Oregon Liquor Control Commission to allow manufacturers or importing distributors of wine, cider or malt beverages to file required statements and pay privilege taxes by electronic means.
