# Oregon SB 398 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to increased employee awareness of the earned income tax credit; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires Commissioner of Bureau of Labor and Industries to adopt rules to require employers to provide written notice to employees about state and federal earned income tax credits. Requires Bureau of Labor and Industries to include information about earned income tax credits in posters regarding state minimum wage.
