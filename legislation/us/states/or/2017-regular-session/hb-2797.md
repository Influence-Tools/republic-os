# Oregon HB 2797 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to fines; and declaring an emergency.

Increases presumptive fine for violations, including traffic violations occurring in certain locations. Increases minimum fine for violations subject to presumptive fine. Increases amount of fine imposed in criminal action payable to state.
