# Oregon SB 319 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to cannabis; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes local governments to allow medical marijuana dispensaries and marijuana retailers licensed by Oregon Liquor Control Commission to be located within certain distance of schools.
