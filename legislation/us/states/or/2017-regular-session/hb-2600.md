# Oregon HB 2600 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to court appointed special advocates; and declaring an emergency.

Transfers authority over court appointed special advocates and CASA Volunteer Programs from Oregon Volunteers Commission for Voluntary Action and Service to Oregon Department of Administrative Services.
