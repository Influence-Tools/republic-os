# Oregon HB 2284 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to taxpayers making conflicting claims under personal income tax laws; and prescribing an effective date.

Expands provisions for resolution of conflicting claims to taxpayer dependents to include conflicting returns or reports addressing other items allowed under personal income tax laws. Modifies process requirements used by Department of Revenue for collateral audit.
