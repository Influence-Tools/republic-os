# Oregon HB 2343 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to comprehensive energy reporting.

Replaces requirements for State Department of Energy to complete biennial comprehensive energy plan and biennial energy forecast with requirement for department to complete biennial comprehensive energy report. Establishes elements of report.
