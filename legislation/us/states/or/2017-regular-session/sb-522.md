# Oregon SB 522 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to recovery against life insurance proceeds required in certain family law judgments.

Authorizes person specified in family law judgment as court-ordered beneficiary to recover against third-party beneficiary named by obligor in separate civil action. Provides that entry of family law judgment serves as constructive notice of court-ordered beneficiary's entitlement to life insurance proceeds<b> to any named third-party beneficiary</b>.
