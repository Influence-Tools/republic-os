# Oregon HB 2947 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to state financial administration.

Directs Oregon Department of Administrative Services to submit annual report to Legislative Assembly on amounts of<b> liquidated and delinquent</b> debt written off, abated or canceled by specified state agencies in previous fiscal year.
