# Oregon HB 2281 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to disqualification of forestland; and prescribing an effective date.

Provides that disqualification of land designated as forestland shall be deemed to occur as of January 1 assessment date of assessment year in which discovery occurs, regardless of when discovery actually occurs.
