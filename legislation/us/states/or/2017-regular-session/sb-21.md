# Oregon SB 21 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to accounting; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Oregon Board of Accountancy to disclose confidential information to certain public entities. Requires public entities that receive information to take reasonable steps to maintain confidentiality of information.
