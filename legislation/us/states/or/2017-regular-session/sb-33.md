# Oregon SB 33 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to tax interest rate computations; and prescribing an effective date.

In computation by Department of Revenue of interest due on tax deficiency owed to department or refunds of tax owed by department, replaces rate based on month or partial month with annual percentage rate computed daily.<b> Standardizes department procedures by delaying running of interest on overpayments of certain taxes.</b>
