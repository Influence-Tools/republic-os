# Oregon HB 2432 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to art therapy; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Health Licensing Office to issue license to engage in practice of art therapy to qualified applicant. Requires person licensed to practice art therapy to comply with rules adopted by office. <b>Allows office to impose discipline on person practicing art therapy for certain violations. Directs office to provide administrative and regulatory oversight to art therapy program.</b> Directs office to adopt rules relating to art therapy.
