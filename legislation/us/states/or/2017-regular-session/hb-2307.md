# Oregon HB 2307 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to evaluations on the issue of insanity.

Provides that certified evaluator who conducts court-ordered evaluation of criminal defendant concerning issue of insanity is not obligated to evaluate defendant's fitness to proceed unless issue of defendant's fitness to proceed arises during evaluation.
