# Oregon HB 2066 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to tax expenditures; and prescribing an effective date.

Extends sunset for tax credits for reservation enterprise zones, affordable housing lenders, rural medical providers and fish screening devices. Increases total amount of qualified loans eligible for affordable housing lender credit for fiscal year. Limits eligibility for rural medical provider credit based on adjusted gross income, with exceptions for certain physician specialties, and limits lifetime use of credit. Sunsets biomass tax credit. Applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2018.
