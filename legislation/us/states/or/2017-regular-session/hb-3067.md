# Oregon HB 3067 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to family supports; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Marion and Polk Counties to establish pilot CourtCare programs to provide quality child care to individuals and families who are participating in court proceedings at local courthouse or transacting business at local governmental office. Requires pilot CourtCare program to also serve as clearinghouse for information and resource referrals relating to family support services in local community.
