# Oregon HB 3288 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to noninstructional professional positions at public institutions of higher education.

Requires each public university to submit <b>to Higher Education Coordinating Commission</b> report identifying causes of increases in administrative positions and other cost drivers at university. Requires commission to compile reports and submit compiled report </b>to Joint Committee on Ways and Means as part of commission's budget presentation during each regular session of Legislative Assembly held during odd-numbered year.
