# Oregon SB 225 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to liability for campaign finance violations; and prescribing an effective date.

Provides that treasurer of political committee or treasurer of petition committee is personally responsible for performance of specified duties.<b> Permits treasurer to designate elector to be liable for civil penalties imposed for failure to file required statements for committee or for failure to include information required in statements filed for committee.</b>
