# Oregon HB 2355 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to public safety; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Oregon Criminal Justice Commission to develop method for recording data concerning officer-initiated pedestrian and traffic stops. Directs Department of Public Safety Standards and Training to develop training and procedures for recording data.
