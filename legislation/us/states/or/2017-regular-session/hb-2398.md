# Oregon HB 2398 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to medical assistance.

Prohibits health care provider from billing medical assistance <b>applicant or</b> recipient except as provided by Oregon Health Authority by rule. Requires health care provider to wait for payment for 90 days before assigning claim for collection.
