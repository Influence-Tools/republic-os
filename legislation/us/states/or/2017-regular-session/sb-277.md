# Oregon SB 277 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to rental space for certain types of dwellings in facilities; and declaring an emergency.

Increases notice period for termination of rental agreement and removal of manufactured dwelling or floating home. <b>Permits landlord to terminate rental agreement with 30 days' written notice if disrepair or deterioration of manufactured dwelling or floating home creates risk of imminent and serious harm to dwellings or persons within facility.</b> Requires landlord to notify tenant of specific disrepair or deterioration causing termination and describe repairs necessary to avoid termination.
