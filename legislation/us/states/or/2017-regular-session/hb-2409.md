# Oregon HB 2409 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to traffic violations; and prescribing an effective date.

Permits city to issue citation for speeding using red light camera in conjunction with other technology that is capable of measuring speed. Applies to speeding violations 11 miles per hour or greater.
