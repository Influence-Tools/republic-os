# Oregon HB 3106 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to the preschool program administered by the Early Learning Division.

Directs Early Learning Hubs to convene annual meetings between representatives of preschool programs administered by Early Learning Division for purpose of coordinating enrollment of eligible children in programs.
