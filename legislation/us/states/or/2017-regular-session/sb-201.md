# Oregon SB 201 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to salary of employees of universities with a governing board under the Oregon Public Service Retirement Plan.

Provides that remuneration paid to certain employee of university with governing board who resides and performs service in United States is deemed includable in employee's taxable income during continuous period of employment with university.
