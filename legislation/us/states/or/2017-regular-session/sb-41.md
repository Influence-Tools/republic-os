# Oregon SB 41 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to workforce information.

Authorizes Employment Department to disclose information in department records to public universities and Oregon Health and Science University to extent necessary to carry out planning, performance measurement, program analysis, socioeconomic analysis or policy analysis functions. Authorizes department to disclose to any person establishment level information secured from public universities and Oregon Health and Science University.
