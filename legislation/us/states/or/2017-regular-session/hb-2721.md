# Oregon HB 2721 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to road worker safety; and declaring an emergency.

Increases penalties for assault committed against flagger or highway worker who is performing official duties. Punishes by maximum of five years' imprisonment, $125,000 fine, or both.
