# Oregon SB 251 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to tax withholding of lottery prize payment; and prescribing an effective date.

Decreases amount of lottery prize payment above which Oregon State Lottery Commission must withhold personal income tax for payment to Department of Revenue. Becomes operative January 1, 2018.
