# Oregon HB 2264 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to fees paid to State Conciliation Service by qualified arbitrators.

Increases application fee for individual who applies to be included on State Conciliation Service list of qualified arbitrators for labor controversy. Increases annual fee for individual to remain on State Conciliation Service list of qualified arbitrators for labor controversy.
