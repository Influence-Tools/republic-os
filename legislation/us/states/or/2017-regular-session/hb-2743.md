# Oregon HB 2743 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to economic development of land adjacent to an airport in a rural area.

Directs Land Conservation and Development Commission to establish and implement pilot program to implement master plan for economic development on land adjacent to airport with approved airport master plan in rural area. Establishes criteria for pilot program.
