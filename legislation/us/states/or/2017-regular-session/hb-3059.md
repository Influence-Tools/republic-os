# Oregon HB 3059 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to special declarant rights.

Precludes right of recovery by transferor of special declarant rights to interest in condominium<b> or planned community</b>. Permits transferor to recover transferred special declarant right <b>in condominium or planned community</b> only by subsequent qualifying instrument.
