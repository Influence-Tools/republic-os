# Oregon SB 274 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to immunizations.

Requires each post-secondary educational institution that provides housing for students to provide to each student enrolling or registering at institution for first time information on vaccine-preventable diseases known to occur in individuals between 16 and 21 years of age.
