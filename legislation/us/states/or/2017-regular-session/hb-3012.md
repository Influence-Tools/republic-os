# Oregon HB 3012 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to siting of residential structures on land zoned for certain uses.

Permits <b>county to allow</b> owner of lot or parcel of at least two acres zoned for rural residential uses to construct new single-family dwelling on lot or parcel if owner converts existing historic home to accessory dwelling unit.
