# Oregon HB 3405 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to payment of dependent care tax credits in installments; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires Department of Human Services to seek waiver of federal requirements in Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program in order to allow recipients of Supplemental Nutrition Assistance to receive advance installment payments of tax credit for dependent care expenses.
