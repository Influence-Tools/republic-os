# Oregon HB 3061 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to anonymous text message Internet websites.

Establishes Task Force on Anonymous Text Message Services. Directs task force to study law changes necessary to regulate and prohibit use of Internet websites that provide anonymous text message services. Sunsets task force on December 31, 2019.
