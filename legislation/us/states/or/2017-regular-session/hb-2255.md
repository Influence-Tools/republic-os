# Oregon HB 2255 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to dairy industry products.

Authorizes State Department of Agriculture to adopt recommended or required practices for ensuring safety of milk, fluid milk or dairy products. Removes prohibition on suspending use of fluid milk grade designation due to first violation of laws regulating dairy industry.
