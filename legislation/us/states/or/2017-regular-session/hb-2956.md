# Oregon HB 2956 (2017 Regular Session) — Relating to evaluations of Early Learning Hubs; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Early Learning Council to conduct evaluation of Early Learning Hubs and submit report on evaluation each odd-numbered year. Requires additional report in 2018.
