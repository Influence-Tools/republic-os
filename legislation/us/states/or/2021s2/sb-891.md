# Oregon SB 891 (2021S2) — Relating to residential tenancies; declaring an emergency.

Extends suspension of termination of residential tenancies for nonpayment of rent for tenants who have applied for emergency rental assistance and provided documentation of application to their landlord on or before June 30, 2022, throughout period that application is pending. Amends notice provided with eviction summons delivered by clerk. Adds additional requirements for certain rental assistance providers.
