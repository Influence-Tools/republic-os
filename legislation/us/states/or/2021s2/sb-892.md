# Oregon SB 892 (2021S2) — Relating to financial assistance for agricultural producers; declaring an emergency.

Directs State Department of Agriculture to establish forgivable loan program to provide financial assistance to farming and ranching producers in Oregon with lost gross farm income in calendar year 2021 due to qualifying natural disaster.
