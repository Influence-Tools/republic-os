# Oregon HB 4208 (2020S1) — Relating to the use of tools by law enforcement agencies; and declaring an emergency.

Prohibits law enforcement agencies from using tear gas <i>or from using long range acoustic devices or sound cannons.</i>]<b> for purposes of crowd control except in circumstances constituting riot.
