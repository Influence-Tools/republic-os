# Oregon HB 4203 (2020S1) — Relating to use of force by peace officers; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that peace officer may not use force that <i>limits ability of person to breathe, including but not limited to choke holds</i>]<b> impedes normal breathing or circulation of blood of another person by applying pressure on throat or neck, unless peace officer may use deadly physical force</b>.
