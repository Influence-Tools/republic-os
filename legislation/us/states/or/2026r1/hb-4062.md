# Oregon HB 4062 (2026R1) — Relating to evaluating Business Oregon programs; and declaring an emergency.

Requires the Director of the Oregon Business Development Department to evaluate the programs administered by the department and provide recommendations for streamlining them. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
