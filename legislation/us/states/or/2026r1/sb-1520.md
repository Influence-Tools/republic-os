# Oregon SB 1520 (2026R1) — Relating to the administration of moneys within the Paid Family and Medical Leave Insurance Fund; and prescribing an effective date.

Authorizes the Director of the Employment Department to adopt rules establishing an accounting system for handling moneys in the Paid Family and Medical Leave Insurance Fund. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
