# Oregon HB 4130 (2026R1) — Relating to farm use.

</b> </i>] <b>Allows property tax special assessment for land under processing facilities.</b> Clarifies<b>, for purposes of the special assessment,</b> the meaning of "preparing" products or by-products raised for human or animal use on a farm unit for purposes of the definition of "farm use."
