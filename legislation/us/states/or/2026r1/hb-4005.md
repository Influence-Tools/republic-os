# Oregon HB 4005 (2026R1) — Relating to Water Professionals Appreciation Week; and prescribing an effective date.

Designates the first Saturday of October of each year through the end of the Sunday of the following weekend as Water Professionals Appreciation Week. <b>Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.</b>
