# Oregon HB 4060 (2026R1) — Relating to fluorescent lamps; and declaring an emergency.

</b> </i>] Exempts sales to purchasers with facilities totaling more than one million square feet from a prohibition on sales of certain fluorescent lamps until January 1, 2030. <b>Exempts residential care facilities and long term care facilities from the requirement to submit for review plans to replace light fixtures designed to use prohibited fluorescent lamps.</b> Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
