# Oregon HB 4047 (2026R1) — Relating to rural emergency hospitals; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires the Oregon Health Authority to adopt rules and procedures to allow a hospital to apply for a license as a rural emergency hospital. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
