# Oregon HB 4025 (2026R1) — Relating to the residential rates of public utilities; and prescribing an effective date.

Allows an increase in the residential rates of a public utility to take effect from November 1 to March 31 for public utilities other than public utilities that provide electricity or natural gas services. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
