# Oregon HB 4123 (2026R1) — Relating to landlord disclosure of confidential information; and prescribing an effective date.

Limits the circumstances under which a landlord may disclose confidential information. Authorizes statutory damages for individuals affected by knowing violations. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
