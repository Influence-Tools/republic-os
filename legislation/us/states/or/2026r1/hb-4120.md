# Oregon HB 4120 (2026R1) — Relating to smoking policies in residential tenancies.

Allows residential landlords of multifamily housing to convert premises to nonsmoking for existing tenants upon [<i>90</i>]<b> 180</b> days' written notice. Applies to new and existing rental agreements.
