# Oregon SB 1513 (2026R1) — Relating to real estate professionals; and declaring an emergency.

</b> </i>] [<i>Provides that real estate teams are not required to change terms used in the team name until July 1, 2027.</i>] <b>Delays until July 1, 2027, the requirement that a real estate team change the terms used in the team name.</b> Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
