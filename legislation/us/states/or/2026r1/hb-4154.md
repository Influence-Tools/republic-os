# Oregon HB 4154 (2026R1) — Relating to student attendance data; and declaring an emergency.

Requires the compilation of data about students who are regular attenders or who are chronically absent. [<i>Takes effect July 1, 2026.</i>] <b>Declares an emergency, effective on passage.</b>
