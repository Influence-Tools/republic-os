# Oregon HB 4052 (2026R1) — Relating to a tax credit for de novo banks; and prescribing an effective date.

Creates a corporate excise tax credit for each of the first three years that a bank does business in this state. Applies only to Oregon-chartered banks that commence business in tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2027, and before January 1, 2033. Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
