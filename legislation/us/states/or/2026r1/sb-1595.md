# Oregon SB 1595 (2026R1) — Relating to professional workforce; and declaring an emergency.

Requires a professional licensing board to publish specified guidance regarding licensure and ensure certain staff receive culturally responsive training not later than January 1, 2028. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
