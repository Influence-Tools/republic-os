# Oregon HB 4402 (2020S3) — Relating to claims arising during the COVID-19 emergency period; declaring an emergency.

Limits liability of school districts, union high schools, education service districts, public charter schools<b>, private schools providing instruction to any grade from kindergarten through grade 12 </b>and community colleges for certain claims arising during COVID-19 emergency period.
