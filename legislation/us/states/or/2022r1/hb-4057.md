# Oregon HB 4057 (2022R1) — Relating to minimum energy efficiency standards; and prescribing an effective date.

Adds "spray sprinkler bodies" to minimum energy efficiency standards. Adds "spray sprinkler bodies" to prohibition against sale or installation of products that do not meet minimum energy efficiency standards.
