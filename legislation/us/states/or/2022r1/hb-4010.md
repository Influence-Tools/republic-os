# Oregon HB 4010 (2022R1) — Relating to kratom; and prescribing an effective date.

Prohibits sale of kratom products by processor unless processor is registered with Department of Revenue. Directs department to adopt rules to carry out regulations. Authorizes imposition of civil penalty for certain violations.
