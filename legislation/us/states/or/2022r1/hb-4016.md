# Oregon HB 4016 (2022R1) — Relating to cannabis; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission to, based on supply of and demand for marijuana, refuse to issue initial marijuana production<b>, processing, wholesale and retail</b> licenses for amount of time commission determines necessary. Requires commission to study effects of refusal to issue production licenses and submit report annually to <b>committee or</b> interim committee of Legislative Assembly related to economic development. <i>Authorizes State Department of Agriculture to, based on supply of and demand for industrial hemp, refuse to issue industrial hemp grower licenses for amount of time department determines necessary. Requires department to study effects of refusal to issue grower licenses and submit report annually to interim committee of Legislative Assembly related to agriculture.</i>] Sunsets authority March 31, 2024.
