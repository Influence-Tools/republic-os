# Oregon HB 4056 (2022R1) — Relating to distribution of marijuana tax revenues; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires annual adjustment for inflation of amount to be retained, for distribution to cities, counties and other entities from Oregon Marijuana Account, prior to transfer of remainder of account balance to Drug Treatment and Recovery Services Fund.
