# Oregon SB 1547 (2022R1) — Relating to children; and declaring an emergency.

Requires operators, employees and certain volunteers of preschool recorded programs and school-age recorded programs to be enrolled in Central Background Registry. Authorizes Office of Child Care to conduct certain investigations of preschool recorded programs and school-age recorded programs. Creates temporary exception to requirement that employees and certain volunteers of preschool recorded programs and school-age recorded programs must be enrolled in Central Background Registry. Becomes operative on January 1, 2023. <b>Appropriates moneys from General Fund to Department of Education for carrying out provisions of Act regarding recorded programs.</b>
