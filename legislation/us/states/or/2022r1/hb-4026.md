# Oregon HB 4026 (2022R1) — Relating to state moneys for school districts impacted by wildfires; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Education to award grants to wildfire-impacted school districts in amounts to cover funding decreases resulting from decreases in district average daily membership, as compared to 2019-2020 school year, that are caused by wildfires. Sunsets on July 1, 2025.
