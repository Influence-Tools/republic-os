# Oregon HB 4051 (2022R1) — Relating to housing; and prescribing an effective date.

Extends sunset of Task Force on Homelessness and Racial Disparities until January 2, 2026. Requires task force to provide interim report by September 15, 2022, and final report by March 31, 2023, on specific pathways for policy changes.<b> Increases appropriation for task force.</b>
