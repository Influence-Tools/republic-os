# Oregon SB 1509 (2022R1) — Relating to Nisei veterans; and prescribing an effective date.

Provides that<b> portion of</b> Oregon Route 35<b>, beginning where highway intersects with U.S. Highway 26 and ending where highway intersects with U.S. Highway 30,</b> shall also be known as Oregon Nisei Veterans World War II <i>Historic</i>]<b> Memorial</b> Highway.
