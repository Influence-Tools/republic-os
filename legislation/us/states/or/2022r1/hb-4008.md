# Oregon HB 4008 (2022R1) — Relating to public safety; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Commission on Statewide Law Enforcement Standards of Conduct and Discipline to hire executive director and support staff, and to enter into interagency or intergovernmental agreement for provision of support staff.
