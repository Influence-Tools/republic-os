# Oregon HB 4074 (2022R1) — Relating to cannabis; and declaring an emergency.

Requires employee or worker of marijuana licensee to report human trafficking on licensed premises to Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission. <i>Directs commission to establish human trafficking coordinator position.</i>] Directs State Department of Agriculture to require any rebuttal testing of cannabis to be performed by department staff member in department-operated laboratory.
