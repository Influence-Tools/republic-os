# Oregon SB 1585 (2022R1) — Relating to workers' compensation death benefits; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Consumer and Business Services<b>, Employment Department</b> and Oregon Health Authority to enter into intergovernmental agreement for <i>disclosure of data related to outbreak of COVID-19 in workplaces</i>] <b>sharing of information</b> to enable Department <b>of Consumer and Business Services</b> to inform beneficiaries of <b>their</b> rights to workers' compensation death benefits.<b> Restricts Oregon Health Authority to sharing information regarding deaths from COVID-19 associated with workplace outbreaks published by authority.</b>
