# Oregon HB 4021 (2022R1) — Relating to the homestead property tax deferral program; and prescribing an effective date.

Extends period in which taxpayers, upon transfer of ownership of homestead or moving manufactured structure or floating home out of state, may discontinue participation in homestead property tax deferral program in assessment year.
