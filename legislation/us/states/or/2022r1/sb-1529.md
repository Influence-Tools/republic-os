# Oregon SB 1529 (2022R1) — Relating to health care; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Allows volunteer qualified health care providers to administer health care services in this state during health care emergency.</i>] Allows Public Health Director to direct and deploy volunteer <b>emergency</b> health care providers <i>during health care emergency</i>] <b>under specified circumstances</b>. Requires Oregon Health Authority to provide workers' compensation coverage for volunteer <b>emergency</b> health care providers. <i>Defines "health care emergency.</i>]
