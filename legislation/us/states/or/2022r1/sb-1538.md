# Oregon SB 1538 (2022R1) — Relating to dental care for Pacific Islanders legally residing in Oregon under the Compact of Free Association.

Establishes COFA Dental Program in Oregon Health Authority to provide dental care to low-income citizens of Pacific Islands in Compact of Free Association who reside in Oregon. Specifies eligibility requirements for program and duties of authority in administering program.
