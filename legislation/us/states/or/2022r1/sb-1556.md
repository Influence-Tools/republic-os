# Oregon SB 1556 (2022R1) — Relating to caregivers; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Department of Human Services to establish certification process for direct care providers of home or community-based services and implement online registry of direct care providers of home or community-based services. Specifies requirements for certification and information to be displayed on online registry.
