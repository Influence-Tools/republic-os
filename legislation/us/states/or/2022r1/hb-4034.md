# Oregon HB 4034 (2022R1) — Relating to health care; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Deletes requirement that coordinated care organization collect specified data from members and submit data to Oregon Health Authority. Allows authority to make certain releases of data.</i>] Allows pharmacy intern to transfer drug containing pseudoephedrine or ephedrine to person 18 years of age or older without prescription.
