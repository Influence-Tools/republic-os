# Oregon SB 1513 (2022R1) — Relating to mandatory overtime shifts.

Prohibits employer from taking adverse employment action against employee employed in certain manufacturing establishments who refuses to work mandatory overtime shift unless employer has provided employee with at least <i>two weeks'</i>] <b>five days'</b> advance notice of overtime shift<b>, including date and time of overtime shift</b>.
