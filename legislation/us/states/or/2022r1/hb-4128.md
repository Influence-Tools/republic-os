# Oregon HB 4128 (2022R1) — Relating to zoonotic disease; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Legislative Policy and Research Office to prepare report concerning Oregon's current framework for preventing, monitoring and responding to zoonotic disease. Directs office to present report to committee or interim committee of Legislative Assembly related to public health not later than December 31, 2022.
