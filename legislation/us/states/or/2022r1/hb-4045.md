# Oregon HB 4045 (2022R1) — Relating to public health approaches to community violence prevention; and declaring an emergency.

<b>Directs Oregon Department of Administrative Services to distribute moneys to nonprofit organization to provide grants to organizations for community violence prevention and intervention measures. Requires nonprofit organization to report annually to department. Appropriates moneys from General Fund to department for distribution to nonprofit organization.</b>
