# Oregon SB 1502 (2022R1) — Relating to tax credits for small forestland owners; and prescribing an effective date.

Creates income or corporate excise tax credit allowed to small forestland owner that elects to observe riparian timber harvest restriction applicable to large forestland owner, in lieu of using harvest option available to small forestland owner.<b> Includes no-harvest dry channel portion of small nonfish perennial stream in forest conservation area eligible for tax credit. Prohibits transfer of credit to successor forestland owner.</b> Provides for allowance of credit and retention of harvest restrictions for 50 years.
