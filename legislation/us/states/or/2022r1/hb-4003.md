# Oregon HB 4003 (2022R1) — Relating to nursing; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Oregon State Board of Nursing to issue nurse internship license to qualified applicant. Allows nurse intern to practice nursing subject to certain limitations and under supervision of registered nurse.
