# Oregon SB 1574 (2022R1) — Relating to sexual assault forensic evidence kits.

Includes<b> specified documentation, when authorized by victim,</b> in definition of "sexual assault forensic evidence kit" <i>chart prepared by sexual assault nurse examiner</i>] for purposes of statutes regulating collection, testing and retention of kits.
