# Oregon SB 1520 (2022R1) — Relating to Oregon's program for redemption of beverage container refund values; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires certain distributors that do not participate in distributor cooperative to provide services for processing and paying refund value for beverage containers. Establishes fees for distributors.
