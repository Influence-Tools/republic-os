# Oregon HB 4059 (2022R1) — Relating to large-scale project labor standards; and declaring an emergency.

Redefines "large-scale project" as "covered project" for purposes of contractor labor standards. Clarifies application of labor standards based on project's capacity rating. Clarifies good faith exception to required total work hours performed by apprentices.
