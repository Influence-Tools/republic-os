# Oregon SB 1701 (2020S2) — Relating to unemployment insurance benefits; declaring an emergency.

Provides that unemployed individual receiving unemployment insurance benefits <b>for weeks ending before January 1, 2022,</b> who has earnings from less than full-time employment may earn greater of $300 or one-third of individual's weekly benefit amount before individual's weekly benefit amount is reduced.
