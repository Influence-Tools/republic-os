# Oregon SB 1016 (2023R1) — Relating to regional family support networks; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs Department of Human Services to establish and maintain regional family support networks for families of individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities. Permits department to contract with community organizations for provision of regional family support network services.
