# Oregon SB 780 (2023R1) — Relating to unlawful fender height modifications.

Creates offense of unlawful fender height modifications.<b> Provides that offense does not apply if motor vehicle is carrying heavy load that causes rear fender to be four or more inches lower than front fender.</b> Punishes by maximum fine of $500, unless commission of offense contributes to accident. Punishes by maximum fine of $2,000 if commission of offense contributes to accident.
