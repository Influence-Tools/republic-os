# Oregon HB 2294 (2023R1) — Relating to pilot projects for firefighter apprenticeship training.

Establishes program within Apprenticeship and Training Division of Bureau of Labor and Industries to make grants to local service districts and local joint committees that develop and administer pilot projects for firefighter apprenticeship training.
