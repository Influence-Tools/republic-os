# Oregon SB 1043 (2023R1) — Relating to opioid overdose reversal medication.

Requires hospitals and other specified facilities that provide substance use disorder treatment to provide to specified patients upon discharge or release two doses of opioid overdose reversal medication and necessary medical supplies to administer medication. Creates exception.
