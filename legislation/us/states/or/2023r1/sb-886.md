# Oregon SB 886 (2023R1) — Relating to the release of animal data.

Removes requirement that State Department of Fish and Wildlife obtain confidentiality agreement when disclosing certain animal data to federal government, tribal government, public body, public utility or college or university. Provides that department refusal to disclose certain animal data is not subject to review of order in contested case.
