# Oregon HB 2110 (2023R1) — Relating to municipal audits.

Removes requirement for certain state agencies to withhold moneys from municipal corporations that fail to file audit reports with Secretary of State. Removes certain provisions relating to addressing deficiencies identified in audit reports.
