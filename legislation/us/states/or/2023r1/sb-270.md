# Oregon SB 270 (2023R1) — Relating to educational opportunities for adults in custody.

Authorizes <b>Department of Corrections to enter into agreements to offer academic programs to adults</b> <i>adult</i>] in custody <i>to enroll in</i>] <b>with</b> academic program at any community college in state, any distance learning academic program or any other higher education academic program <i>that adult in custody applies for and is accepted into</i>], provided that enrollment in academic program is consistent with administrative rules and regulations adopted by Department of Corrections<b> and, as applicable, federal regulations relating to federal Pell Grant</b>.
