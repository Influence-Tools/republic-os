# Oregon SB 161 (2023R1) — Relating to the Elliott State Forest; and declaring an emergency.

Moves deadline for occurrence of events related to establishment of Elliott State Research Forest to <i>November 1</i>]<b> December 31</b>, 2023. Makes certain changes to requirement for event concerning habitat conservation plan for Elliott State Forest.
