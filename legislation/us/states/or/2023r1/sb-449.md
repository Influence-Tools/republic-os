# Oregon SB 449 (2023R1) — Relating to amount paid to attend institutions of higher education by persons formerly in adoptive care; and declaring an emergency.

Permits adopted former foster children to have amount of tuition and all fees waived if attending defined institutions of higher education for purposes of pursuing initial undergraduate degree. Defines "adopted former foster child."
