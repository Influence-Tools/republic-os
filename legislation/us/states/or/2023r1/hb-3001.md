# Oregon HB 3001 (2023R1) — Relating to Gold Star Family members; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Transportation to waive fee for Fallen Hero roadside memorial highway sign if applicant is Gold Star Family member and funds are appropriated or otherwise made available to department for purpose of paying fees.
