# Oregon SB 210 (2023R1) — Relating to mandatory abuse reporting.

<i>Requires Department of Human Services to study exceptions to mandatory abuse reporting requirements of department employees who provide assistance to domestic violence victims. Directs department to submit findings to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to human services not later than September 15, 2024.</i>]
