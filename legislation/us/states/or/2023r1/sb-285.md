# Oregon SB 285 (2023R1) — Relating to school district facilities grants.

Increases amounts of grants for school <i>district</i>] facilities under certain grant programs. <b>Authorizes education service districts to receive certain grants.</b> Provides that grant amounts are biennially adjusted for inflation. Modifies procedures for calculating and awarding grants.
