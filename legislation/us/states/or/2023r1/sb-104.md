# Oregon SB 104 (2023R1) — Relating to agency with choice services; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Department of Human Services to contract with one or more agency with choice services providers and with one provider by July 1, 2024. Modifies definition of "agency with choice services." Specifies requirements for agency with choice services providers that contract with department. Deletes requirement that department adopt rules regarding qualifications and reimbursement rates for agency with choice services providers.
