# Oregon HB 3610 (2023R1) — Relating to alcohol; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes Task Force on Alcohol Pricing and Addiction Services. Directs task force to study issues related to alcohol addiction in this state. Directs task force to submit findings to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to health not later than September 15, 2024.
