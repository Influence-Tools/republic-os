# Oregon HB 3563 (2023R1) — Relating to youth workforce development programs; and prescribing an effective date.

Creates Oregon Youth Works Advisory Board to advise on and oversee implementation and alignment of youth workforce development programs for which Higher Education Coordinating Commission is responsible.
