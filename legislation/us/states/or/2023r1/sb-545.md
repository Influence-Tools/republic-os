# Oregon SB 545 (2023R1) — Relating to reuse of containers; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Directs State Department of Agriculture and Oregon Health Authority to adopt rules allowing consumers to use own containers for refilling with food at food establishment. Directs department and authority to adopt rules allowing food establishments to reuse take-home food containers returned to restaurant by consumer.</i>]
