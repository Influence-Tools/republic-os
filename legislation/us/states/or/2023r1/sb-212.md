# Oregon SB 212 (2023R1) — Relating to peer support confidentiality.

Makes confidential and exempts from discovery and public records certain communications and information conveyed during, to facilitate or to follow up on peer support check-in sessions with <i>Oregon Youth Authority</i>] employees<b> of Oregon Youth Authority or county juvenile department</b>. Creates exceptions.
