# Oregon HB 3561 (2023R1) — Relating to scholarships for early childhood professionals; and declaring an emergency.

Specifies that term "early childhood care and education" includes early childhood behavioral and mental health services for purposes of scholarships and grants awarded to ensure state has adequate supply of early childhood care and education professionals.
