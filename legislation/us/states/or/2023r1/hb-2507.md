# Oregon HB 2507 (2023R1) — Relating to property tax exemption; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Allows exception to requirement for property tax exemption that property of industry apprenticeship or training trust be actually and exclusively occupied and used for purposes of industry apprenticeship or training trust for occasional use by corporation exempt from federal income tax for purposes for which corporation is granted federal exemption.</i>]
