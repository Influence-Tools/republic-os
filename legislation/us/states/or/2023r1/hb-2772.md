# Oregon HB 2772 (2023R1) — Relating to domestic terrorism.

<b>Defines terms.</b> Creates crime of domestic terrorism<b> in the first degree</b>. Punishes by maximum penalty of 10 years' imprisonment, $250,000 fine, or both.<b> Creates crime of domestic terrorism in the second degree. Punishes by maximum penalty of five years' imprisonment, $125,000 fine, or both. Directs Oregon Criminal Justice Commission to classify crimes as specified crime categories of sentencing guidelines grid.</b>
