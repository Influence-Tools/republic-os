# Oregon HB 2925 (2023R1) — Relating to health equity; and declaring an emergency.

Extends deadlines for affinity group task forces to complete their work with respect to allocations of resources to address communities' needs and health inequities faced by communities and to report recommendations to Legislative Assembly.
