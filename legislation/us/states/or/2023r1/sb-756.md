# Oregon SB 756 (2023R1) — Relating to school district employees who work with students with specialized needs; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires school district employees assigned to work with students with specialized needs to have access to specified records related to students<b>,</b> <i>and</i>] to be consulted when education plan for student is reviewed or revised<b> and to be provided with adequate training</b>.
