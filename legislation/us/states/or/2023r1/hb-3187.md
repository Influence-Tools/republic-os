# Oregon HB 3187 (2023R1) — Relating to water right examiners.

Makes individuals who possess relevant technical experience and are employed seven or more years by certain types of districts, and certain types of registered professionals, eligible to apply for certification as water right examiners.<b> Sunsets eligibility for certain individuals to apply for certification on July 1, 2027.</b>
