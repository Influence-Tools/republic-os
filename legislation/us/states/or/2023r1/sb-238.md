# Oregon SB 238 (2023R1) — Relating to school comprehensive alcohol and drug abuse policies.

Directs Oregon Health Authority, State Board of Education and Alcohol and Drug Policy Commission to collaborate on developing curricula supplements related to dangers of certain drugs and to laws that provide immunity or other protections related to drug or alcohol use.
