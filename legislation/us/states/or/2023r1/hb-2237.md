# Oregon HB 2237 (2023R1) — Relating to the board of property tax appeals; and prescribing an effective date.

Changes terms of members of board of property tax appeals from one year to <i>four</i>] <b>two</b> years. Makes nonsubstantive housekeeping changes to standardize use of terminology.
