# Oregon SB 981 (2023R1) — Relating to Department of Revenue debt collections.

Authorizes Oregon Department of Administrative Services to exempt certain accounts that originate in Department of Revenue from general requirement to assign liquidated and delinquent account to private collection agency within one year of most recent payment on account.
