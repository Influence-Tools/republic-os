# Oregon SB 585 (2023R1) — Relating to precinct committeeperson eligibility.

Allows otherwise qualified person who becomes United States citizen less than <i>180</i>]<b> 251</b> days before date of primary election to file for and be elected to office of precinct committeeperson, if person registers as member of major political party by filing deadline.
