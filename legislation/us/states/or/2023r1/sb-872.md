# Oregon SB 872 (2023R1) — Relating to the prevention of wildfire in federal forests.

Instructs State Forestry Department to endeavor to partner with federal agencies to undertake certain activities in federal forests related to fire prevention and request that federal agencies fund<b> portions of</b> activities.
