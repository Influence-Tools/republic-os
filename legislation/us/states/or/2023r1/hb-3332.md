# Oregon HB 3332 (2023R1) — Relating to Buy America requirements for public construction projects; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires <i>state contracting agency that procures</i>]<b> Oregon Department of Administrative Services and Department of Transportation, in procuring</b> public improvement contract or contract for public works with estimated contract price of <i>$150,000</i>]<b> $250,000</b> or more<b>,</b> to include in solicitation documents conspicuous notice that resulting public improvement contract or contract for public works will require contractors to use <i>iron,</i>] steel,<b> iron,</b> coatings for<b> steel and</b> iron <i>and steel</i>] and manufactured products that are <i>made</i>] <b>produced</b> in United States.
