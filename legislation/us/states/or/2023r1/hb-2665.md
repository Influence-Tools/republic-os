# Oregon HB 2665 (2023R1) — Relating to temporary staffing agencies; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires Health Licensing Office to establish process to receive and investigate complaints regarding temporary staffing agency. Requires temporary staffing agency to ensure personnel meets certain requirements.
