# Oregon SB 192 (2023R1) — Relating to prescription drugs; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires pharmacy benefit managers to annually report to Department of Consumer and Business Services information about certain rebates, fees, price protection payments and other payments received from prescription drug manufacturers. Requires department to publish aggregated information received from pharmacy benefit managers on department's website.
