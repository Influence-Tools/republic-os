# Oregon HB 3309 (2023R1) — Relating to housing accessibility; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs Housing and Community Services Department to provide report to appropriate interim committee of Legislative Assembly on department's financial support of dwelling units with certain accessibility features. Requires Oregon Housing Stability Council to undertake certain efforts to increase quantity and quality of accessible dwelling units.
