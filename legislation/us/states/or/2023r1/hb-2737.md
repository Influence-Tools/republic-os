# Oregon HB 2737 (2023R1) — Relating to transfers of state property.

Allows agencies to sell, lease or donate <b>agency's</b> <i>state</i>] real property <i>of agency</i>] <b>or interests in real property</b> to eligible Indian tribes notwithstanding ordinary transfer procedures.
