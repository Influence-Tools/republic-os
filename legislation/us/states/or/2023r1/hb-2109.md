# Oregon HB 2109 (2023R1) — Relating to corporations; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Requires Secretary of State to study and evaluate importance of corporations in Secretary of State's business registry functions and revenue. Directs Secretary of State to submit findings, which may include recommendations for legislation, to interim committee of Legislative Assembly related to business not later than September 15, 2024.</i>]
