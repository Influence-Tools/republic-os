# Oregon SB 498 (2023R1) — Relating to estate tax treatment of natural resource property; and prescribing an effective date.

Allows exclusion from taxable estate for value of interest in farm, forestry or fishing business. Applies to estates of decedents dying on or after July 1, 2023.
