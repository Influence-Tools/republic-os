# Oregon HB 5032 (2023R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the Public Records Advocate; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by Public Records Advocate.
