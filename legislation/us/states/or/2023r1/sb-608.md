# Oregon SB 608 (2023R1) — Relating to prescription drugs; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Prohibits insurers offering policies or certificates of health insurance and pharmacy benefit managers from requiring claim for reimbursement of prescription drug to include modifier or other indicator that drug is 340B drug.</i>]
