# Oregon HB 2286 (2023R1) — Relating to health care services provided to Native Americans.

Requires Oregon Health Authority to maintain process to identify claims eligible for enhanced federal match for services provided <i>to American Indians or Alaska Natives</i>] by nontribal providers<b> that have written care coordination agreement with Indian health care provider and</b>, <i>secure</i>] <b>if</b> enhanced federal match <b>is available</b> from Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services<b>,</b> <i>and</i>] return to <i>tribe</i>]<b> Indian health care provider</b> portion of enhanced federal match.
