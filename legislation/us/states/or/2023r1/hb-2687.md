# Oregon HB 2687 (2023R1) — Relating to public applicators; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes State Department of Agriculture to issue public applicator licenses and public trainee certificates to employees of federally recognized Indian tribes for purposes of pesticide application on <i>certain lands adjacent to tribal lands</i>]<b> lands other than Indian country</b>.
