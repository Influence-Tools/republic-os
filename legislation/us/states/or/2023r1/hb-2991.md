# Oregon HB 2991 (2023R1) — Relating to enhancing access to jobs in the early childhood workforce; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Early Learning and Care to commission independent study regarding barriers to attaining high quality early childhood workforce. Requires department to adopt rules that address barriers that impede entry into early childhood workforce by early childhood professionals, no later than December 31, <i>2024</i>] <b>2025</b>.
