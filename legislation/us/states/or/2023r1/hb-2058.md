# Oregon HB 2058 (2023R1) — Relating to financial assistance for agricultural overtime compensation payments; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Oregon Business Development Department to develop and administer <i>loan</i>] <b>repayable award</b> program to provide financial assistance to eligible employers to mitigate costs associated with <i>compliance with</i>] agricultural overtime compensation requirements under section 2, chapter 115, Oregon Laws 2022 (Enrolled House Bill 4002).
