# Oregon HB 2146 (2023R1) — Relating to Gold Star Families memorial highway.

Provides that portion of U.S. Highway 30, beginning where highway intersects with U.S. Highway 101 and ending at Idaho state line, shall also be known as Oregon Gold Star Families Memorial Highway.
