# Oregon SB 661 (2023R1) — Relating to lobbyists; and prescribing an effective date.

Prohibits lobbyist from serving as chairperson of interim <i>committee, legislative work group or legislative task force.</i>]<b> committees or certain legislative work groups or legislative task forces. Provides exceptions.</b>
