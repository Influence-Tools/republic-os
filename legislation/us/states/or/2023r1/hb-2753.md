# Oregon HB 2753 (2023R1) — Relating to the compensation of directors of district school boards; and declaring an emergency.

Allows district school board to choose to provide directors with monthly stipend<b> and individual directors to decline to receive stipend</b>. Requires board to provide reimbursements for actual and necessary expenses if board does not provide stipend.<b> Allows board to choose to provide reimbursements for actual and necessary expenses if board does provide stipend.</b>
