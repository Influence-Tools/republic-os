# Oregon SB 718 (2023R1) — Relating to drought effects on the forfeiture of water rights.

Provides that year in which Governor declares that drought exists, or is likely to exist, within county does not count toward period of time for forfeiture of water right for nonuse for <i>owner</i>]<b> holder</b> of water right within county.
