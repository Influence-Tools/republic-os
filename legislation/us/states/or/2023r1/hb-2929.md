# Oregon HB 2929 (2023R1) — Relating to the enforcement of water laws.

Authorizes Water Resources Department to seek injunctive relief if person has engaged, or is about to engage, in activity that is or will be violation of certain water laws.<b> Sunsets injunctive authority on January 2, 2030.
