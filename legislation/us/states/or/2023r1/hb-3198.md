# Oregon HB 3198 (2023R1) — Relating to early literacy; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes Early Literacy Success Initiative. Prescribes purposes and requirements under initiative, including establishment of Early Literacy Success School Grants, Birth Through Five Literacy Plan and Early Literacy Success Community Grants.
