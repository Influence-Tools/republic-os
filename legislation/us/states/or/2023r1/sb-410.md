# Oregon SB 410 (2023R1) — Relating to pharmacy technician licensure; and prescribing an effective date.

Allows State Board of Pharmacy to adopt rules to issue temporary license to perform duties of pharmacy technician. Specifies that temporary license may be renewed once.
