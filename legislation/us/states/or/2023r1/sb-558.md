# Oregon SB 558 (2023R1) — Relating to over-the-counter hearing aids; and declaring an emergency.

Exempts from regulation by Advisory Council on Hearing Aids and Health Licensing Office over-the-counter hearing aid. Defines "over-the-counter hearing aid."
