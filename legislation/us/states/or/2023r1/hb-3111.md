# Oregon HB 3111 (2023R1) — Relating to public records; and declaring an emergency.

Exempts from public disclosure personal information of employees and retirees maintained by retirement system operated by local government. Clarifies that personal information of employees and volunteers of public body is exempt from disclosure regardless of record in which information is maintained.
