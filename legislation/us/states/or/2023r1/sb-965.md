# Oregon SB 965 (2023R1) — Relating to health care providers.

Allows Oregon Health Authority to conduct off-site investigation after receiving certain complaints against hospitals. <i>Allows initial licensure inspections to be conducted off-site for specified providers of home- and community-based care and hospice care.</i>]
