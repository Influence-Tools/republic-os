# Oregon HB 2806 (2023R1) — Relating to public meetings; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes governing body of public body to meet in executive session to consider matters relating to safety of governing body, public body staff and public body volunteers and to security of public body facilities and meeting spaces, and relating to cyber security infrastructure and responses to cyber security threats.
