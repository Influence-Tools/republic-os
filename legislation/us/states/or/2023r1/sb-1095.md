# Oregon SB 1095 (2023R1) — Relating to congressional districts; and declaring an emergency.

Provides transitional guidance and adjusts membership of<b> certain</b> boards and commissions whose governing body membership is based in whole or part on congressional districts, to account for increase in congressional districts.
