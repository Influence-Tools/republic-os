# Oregon HB 2576 (2023R1) — Relating to judicial review of local income tax questions; and prescribing an effective date.

Confers exclusive jurisdiction on Oregon Tax Court for judicial review of questions <i>concerning income taxes imposed by local governments</i>]<b> arising under local government tax laws that impose taxes on or measured by net income</b>.
