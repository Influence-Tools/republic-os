# Oregon HB 3148 (2023R1) — Relating to missing persons.

Authorizes peace officer to <b>issue investigative subpoena or</b> request order from circuit court for information regarding missing individual<b> if specified standards are met</b>. Removes authority for peace officer to request investigative subpoena from circuit court.
