# Oregon HB 3300 (2023R1) — Relating to the Oregon Board of Licensed Professional Counselors and Therapists; and prescribing an effective date.

Authorizes Oregon Board of Licensed Professional Counselors and Therapists to issue limited permit to qualified applicant. Deletes requirement that license to practice professional counseling or marriage and family therapy be renewed annually.
