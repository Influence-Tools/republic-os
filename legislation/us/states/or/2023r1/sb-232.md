# Oregon SB 232 (2023R1) — Relating to the practice of medicine via remote means; and prescribing an effective date.

Allows out-of-state physician or physician assistant to provide specified care to patients located in Oregon. Clarifies that practice of medicine using telemedicine occurs where patient is physically located.
