# Oregon HB 2108 (2023R1) — Relating to documents filed with the office of the Secretary of State; and prescribing an effective date.

Removes requirement for Secretary of State to issue written notification and wait 20 days before withdrawing certificate of filing or document submitted for filing. Requires secretary instead to include, in record of filing, statement of reasons for withdrawal and nature of any other action secretary took.
