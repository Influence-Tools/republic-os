# Oregon SB 806 (2023R1) — Relating to life insurance policies covering child support obligations; and declaring an emergency.

Permits obligor who is required to maintain life insurance policy to cover child support obligations to move court to modify portion of judgment requiring maintenance of life insurance policy under specified circumstances.
