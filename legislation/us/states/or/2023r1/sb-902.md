# Oregon SB 902 (2023R1) — Relating to temporary assignment to youth correction facility; and declaring an emergency.

Permits person who is 20 years of age or older at time of resentencing for crime committed when person was under 18 years of age to continue temporary assignment to youth correction facility if person was temporarily assigned to youth correction facility following original sentence.
