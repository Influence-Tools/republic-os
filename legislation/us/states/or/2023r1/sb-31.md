# Oregon SB 31 (2023R1) — Relating to the family and medical leave insurance program; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Requires Employment Department to study issues relating to family and medical leave insurance benefits. Directs department to submit findings to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to business and labor not later than December 1, 2023.</i>]
