# Oregon HB 2812 (2023R1) — Relating to income tax deductions for personal casualty loss; and prescribing an effective date.

Creates Oregon tax subtraction for amounts of personal casualty loss that are barred from deduction on federal tax return because loss is not attributable to federally declared disaster.
