# Oregon HB 2316 (2023R1) — Relating to driving while under the influence of intoxicants.

Expands offense of driving while under influence of intoxicants to include any substance that, when taken into human body, can impair the ability of person to operate vehicle safely. Defines "intoxicant" for purposes of Oregon Vehicle Code. <b>Provides for affirmative defense.
