# Oregon SB 969 (2023R1) — Relating to nursing assistants; and prescribing an effective date.

Removes requirement that Oregon State Board of Nursing certify as nursing assistant any qualified applicant. Allows board to deny certificate and place nursing assistant on probation for specified reasons.
