# Oregon SB 283 (2023R1) — Relating to the education workforce; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Education <b>to develop and implement plan</b> to establish and maintain statewide data system on education workforce in state<b> and to convene steering committee to assist in development of business case for statewide data system</b>. <i>Prescribes requirements and uses of system. Requires department to convene steering committee related to data collection requirements.</i>]<b> Directs Teacher Standards and Practices Commission to develop and implement plan to establish and maintain statewide portal for jobs in education.</b>
