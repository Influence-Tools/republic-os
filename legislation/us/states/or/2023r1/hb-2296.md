# Oregon HB 2296 (2023R1) — Relating to reemployment of retired members of the Public Employees Retirement System.

<i>Makes permanent</i>] <b>Extends sunset from 2024 to 2034 on</b> provisions allowing retired member of Public Employees Retirement System to be reemployed by participating public employer for unlimited number of hours without reduction in pension benefits.
