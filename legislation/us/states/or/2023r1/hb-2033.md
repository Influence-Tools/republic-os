# Oregon HB 2033 (2023R1) — Relating to instruments conveying title to real property; and prescribing an effective date.

Clarifies method of collecting unpaid charges against real property if instrument conveying fee title to such real property to exempt entity is recorded without certificate issued by county assessor attesting that all charges have been paid.
