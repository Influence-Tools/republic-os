# Oregon HB 2804 (2023R1) — Relating to the Department of Human Services' workforce; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires Department of Human Services to conduct annual assessment of workforce capacity and needs in each <i>division</i>]<b> program and unit</b> of department and report results to interim committees of Legislative Assembly no later than <i>February 1</i>]<b> September 30 of</b> each<b> even-numbered</b> year.
