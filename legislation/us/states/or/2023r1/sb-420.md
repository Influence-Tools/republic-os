# Oregon SB 420 (2023R1) — Relating to brain injuries; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Human Services to provide specified services to individuals with brain injuries and to convene Brain Injury Advisory Committee. Requires department to report to Legislative Assembly during odd-numbered year regular sessions on services provided and number of individuals served.
