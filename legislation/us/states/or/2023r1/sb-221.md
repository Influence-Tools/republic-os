# Oregon SB 221 (2023R1) — Relating to the State Department of Geology and Mineral Industries electronic permitting system.

Establishes Electronic Permitting System Subaccount. Requires State Department of Geology and Mineral Industries to collect surcharge on use of electronic permitting system for deposit into subaccount.
