# Oregon SB 819 (2023R1) — Relating to abbreviated school day programs; and declaring an emergency.

Modifies abbreviated school day program requirements. Requires that parent or foster parent must give informed and written consent for student with disability to be placed on program. Prescribes duties of school district superintendent and Department of Education in relation to abbreviated school day programs.
