# Oregon SB 189 (2023R1) — Relating to health care; and prescribing an effective date.

Allows health care facility to <i>transfer</i>]<b> release</b> human pathological waste, which includes biopsy materials, human tissues and anatomical parts that emanate from surgeries, autopsies and obstetrical and laboratory procedures on humans, to donor of pathological waste or authorized representative of donor if waste is intended for cremation, interment or other final disposition<b>, in accordance with rules adopted by Oregon Health Authority</b>.
