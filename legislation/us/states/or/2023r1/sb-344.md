# Oregon SB 344 (2023R1) — Relating to criminal justice.

<i>Directs Oregon Criminal Justice Commission to study use of technology to improve accuracy when measuring recidivism rates of persons convicted of felonies, and to provide results of study to interim committees of Legislative Assembly no later than December 31, 2024.</i>]
