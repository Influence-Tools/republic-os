# Oregon HB 2757 (2023R1) — Relating to crisis services; prescribing an effective date; and providing for revenue raising that requires approval by a three-fifths majority.

Expands and provides funding for coordinated crisis services system including 9-8-8 suicide prevention and behavioral health crisis hotline. <b>Establishes 9-8-8 Trust Fund.</b> Imposes <b>9-8-8</b> tax on consumers and retail subscribers who have telecommunications service or interconnected Voice over Internet Protocol service and who have prepaid telecommunications services, to pay for crisis services system.<b> Exempts from taxation telecommunications services provided, based on need, under plan of assistance.</b>
