# Oregon HB 3560 (2023R1) — Relating to immunity for Higher Education Coordinating Commission; and declaring an emergency.

Prohibits person from suing Higher Education Coordinating Commission for commission's performance, or failure to perform, any duty, function or power relating to psilocybin products following effective date of Ballot Measure 109 (2020).
