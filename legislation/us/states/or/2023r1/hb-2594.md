# Oregon HB 2594 (2023R1) — Relating to throwing lighted material onto certain property.

Modifies penalties for throwing lighted material onto certain property. Punishes by <i>maximum of $1,000 fine,</i>] maximum of $2,000 fine<b>, or</b> <i>if commission of offense creates risk of wildfire and</i>] maximum of six months' imprisonment, $2,500 fine, or both, if <i>commission of offense creates extreme risk of wildfire</i>]<b> conduct results in ignition of fire or occurs within forest protection district during fire season</b>.
