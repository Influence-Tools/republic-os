# Oregon SB 745 (2023R1) — Relating to sex trafficking; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs <b>county</b> juvenile department to ensure that <b>specified</b> youth <b>or adjudicated youth</b> <i>taken into custody</i>] receives sex trafficking screening. Requires that youth <b>or adjudicated youth</b> suspected of being victim of sex trafficking be <i>provided with</i>]<b> referred to</b> appropriate resources, including special advocate. <b>Directs Department of Justice to develop and provide training on standardized screening tool.</b>
