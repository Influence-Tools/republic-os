# Oregon HB 5006 (2023R1) — Relating to state financial administration; and declaring an emergency.

Limits for six-year period beginning July 1, 2023, payment of expenses from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by various state agencies for capital construction.
