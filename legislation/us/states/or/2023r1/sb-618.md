# Oregon SB 618 (2023R1) — Relating to body armor.

Directs court to consider, when determining aggravation at sentencing, whether defendant was wearing body armor <i>at time</i>]<b> in course of and in furtherance</b> of crime<b> or to facilitate immediate flight therefrom</b>.
