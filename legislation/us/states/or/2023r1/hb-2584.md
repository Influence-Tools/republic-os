# Oregon HB 2584 (2023R1) — Relating to physician assistants; and prescribing an effective date.

Specifies physician assistant scope of practice and duties of care when practicing medicine. Removes requirement that physician assistant collaboration agreement with employer include physician assistant performance assessment. <b>Clarifies that physician assistant is authorized to write prescriptions.</b>
