# Oregon SB 1040 (2023R1) — Relating to child care; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Early Learning and Care to establish and administer by rule pilot program for child care micro centers. Requires department to report to committee or interim committee of Legislative Assembly related to early learning and care no later than September 15, 2025.
