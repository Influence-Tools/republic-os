# Oregon SB 556 (2023R1) — Relating to management of resources for benefit of persons in custody of Department of Human Services; and declaring an emergency.

Prohibits Department of Human Services from using specified moneys received on behalf of child in care for maintenance costs. Directs department to maintain separate accounts for each child on whose behalf moneys are received. Specifies types of expenses that may be paid from account. Authorizes department, upon request, to instead pay moneys directly into specified accounts for child's benefit. Creates exceptions.
