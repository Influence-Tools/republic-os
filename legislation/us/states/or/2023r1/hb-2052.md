# Oregon HB 2052 (2023R1) — Relating to registration of business entities that qualify as data brokers; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that data broker may not collect, sell or license brokered personal data within this state unless data broker first registers with Department of Consumer and Business Services. Specifies form, method and contents of application. Specifies exemptions. Provides civil penalty in amount not to exceed $500 for each violation of Act or, for continuing violation, for each day in which violation continues. Caps amount of civil penalty at $10,000 in calendar year.
