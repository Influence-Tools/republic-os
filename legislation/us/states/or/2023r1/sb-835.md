# Oregon SB 835 (2023R1) — Relating to subsurface sewage disposal.

Directs Environmental Quality Commission to adopt rules <i>authorizing single-family dwelling and accessory dwelling unit constructed on same lot or parcel to be permanently connected</i>]<b> setting forth conditions for approving proposal to permanently connect accessory dwelling unit located on same lot or parcel as single-family dwelling</b> to same subsurface sewage disposal system or alternative sewage disposal system.
