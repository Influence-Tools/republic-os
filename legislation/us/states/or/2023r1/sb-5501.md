# Oregon SB 5501 (2023R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the Oregon Board of Accountancy; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by Oregon Board of Accountancy.
