# Oregon HB 3179 (2023R1) — Relating to the permitting of renewable energy facilities.

Modifies definition of "renewable energy facility" for purposes of county permitting process for solar photovoltaic power generation facility. <b>Requires county to require applicant for permit to establish solar photovoltaic power generation facility using certain number of acres to provide decommissioning plan that includes bonding or other security.</b>
