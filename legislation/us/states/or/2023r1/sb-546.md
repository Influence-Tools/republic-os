# Oregon SB 546 (2023R1) — Relating to chemicals used in cosmetic products.

Requires Oregon Health Authority to adopt and maintain list of designated high priority chemicals of concern used in cosmetic products and to periodically review and revise list. Requires authority to post certain information on authority's website.
