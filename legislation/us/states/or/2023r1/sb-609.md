# Oregon SB 609 (2023R1) — Relating to students' eligibility for Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program benefits.

Directs Department of Human Services to consider hours worked as graduate assistant as hours worked in employment for purpose of meeting work requirements in Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program.
