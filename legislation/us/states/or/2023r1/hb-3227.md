# Oregon HB 3227 (2023R1) — Relating to the Custodians' Civil Service Law; and declaring an emergency.

Makes changes to Custodians' Civil Service Law to <b>increase population threshold for subject school districts,</b> remove barriers to filling employment vacancies and remove certain qualifications for members of civil service board.
