# Oregon SB 339 (2023R1) — Relating to crime.

Authorizes court to require as condition of probation <i>for person convicted of harassment</i>] completion of sex offender treatment program and submission to polygraph examinations, if recommended by probation officer and if <b>probationer is on supervision for any sex crime or</b> harassment <i>involved</i>]<b> involving</b> touching of sexual or intimate parts of another person<b>, or was previously convicted of any sex crime</b>.
