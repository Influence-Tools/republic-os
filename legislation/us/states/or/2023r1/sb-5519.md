# Oregon SB 5519 (2023R1) — Relating to the financial administration of the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission.
