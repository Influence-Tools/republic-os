# Oregon SB 692 (2023R1) — Relating to investigations of workplace discrimination at state agencies.

Directs <i>Department of Human Services to develop or acquire and maintain information system for tracking</i>] <b>state agencies to use information system maintained by Oregon Department of Administrative Services to track internal</b> investigations <i>related to</i>] <b>conducted by state agency regarding</b> allegations of workplace discrimination and harassment <b>concerning employees of state agency</b>. <b>Provides minimum requirements for information system.</b>
