# Oregon SB 406 (2023R1) — Relating to housing in Tillamook County.

Requires certain cities and communities in Tillamook County to allow middle housing on residentially zoned lands, to adopt housing production strategies and to accommodate estimated housing needs upon lands inventoried as buildable lands.
