# Oregon SB 785 (2023R1) — Relating to out-of-service parking meters; and declaring an emergency.

Allows parking in spaces regulated by out-of-service <i>city</i>] parking meters <b>operated by public bodies</b> unless notice prohibiting parking is posted.
