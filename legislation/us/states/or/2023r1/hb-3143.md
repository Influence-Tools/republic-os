# Oregon HB 3143 (2023R1) — Relating to the securitization of public utility expenditures other than expenditures for generation asset retirement; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes public utility, upon approval by Public Utility Commission, to issue bonds and securitize debt for costs and expenses incurred or to be incurred by public utility associated with events subject to federal or state declaration of emergency.
