# Oregon HB 2235 (2023R1) — Relating to behavioral health; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Oregon Health Authority to<b> convene work group to</b> study <i>access to behavioral health treatment in rural and medically underserved areas of this state. Directs authority to submit findings to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to health not later than September 15, 2024.</i>]<b> major barriers to workforce recruitment and retention in publicly financed behavioral health system in this state and to develop recommendations on specified topics. Specifies membership.
