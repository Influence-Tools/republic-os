# Oregon HB 2504 (2023R1) — Relating to barriers that impede entry into the early childhood workforce by international early childhood professionals.

<i>Directs Early Learning Council, in consultation with Office of Child Care and Oregon Center for Career Development in Childhood Care and Education, to adopt rules to address barriers impeding entry into early childhood workforce by international early childhood professionals. Requires rules to simplify and eliminate certain administrative inefficiencies that exist within early childhood workforce registry.</i>]
