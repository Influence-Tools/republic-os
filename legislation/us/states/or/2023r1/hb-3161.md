# Oregon HB 3161 (2023R1) — Relating to electric company's plan to meet requirements of renewable portfolio standard.

Removes requirement that electric company file separate implementation plan for meeting requirements of renewable portfolio standard. Directs electric company to describe electric company's plan for meeting requirements of renewable portfolio standard as part of electric company's integrated resource plan.
