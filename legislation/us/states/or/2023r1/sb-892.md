# Oregon SB 892 (2023R1) — Relating to housing; and declaring an emergency.

Amends statutes relating to housing laws and to authority of Housing and Community Services Department and Oregon Housing Stability Council, including changes to department's procurement authority and ability to provide down payment assistance.
