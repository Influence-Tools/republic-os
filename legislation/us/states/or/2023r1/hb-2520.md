# Oregon HB 2520 (2023R1) — Relating to unmanned aircraft systems.

Prohibits person from recklessly, knowingly or intentionally using unmanned aircraft system to interfere with wildfire suppression, law enforcement or emergency response effort. Imposes penalties for violation.
