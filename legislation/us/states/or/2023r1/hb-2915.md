# Oregon HB 2915 (2023R1) — Relating to sales of certain animals by retail pet stores; and prescribing an effective date.

Prohibits retail pet store from offering to sell or selling dogs or cats. Specifies exceptions.<b> Removes exceptions on September 1, 2028.</b> Provides that violation is subject to civil penalty not to exceed $500.
