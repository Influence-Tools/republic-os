# Oregon SB 217 (2023R1) — Relating to cosmetology; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires that cosmetology curriculum taught by school meet standards established by Board of Cosmetology and be approved by Health Licensing Office. <b>Directs board to convene curriculum requirements advisory committee to make curriculum requirements recommendations.</b> Exempts persons performing specified spray tanning services from requirements related to cosmetology.
