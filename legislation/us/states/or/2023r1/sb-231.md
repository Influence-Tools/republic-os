# Oregon SB 231 (2023R1) — Relating to abuse reporting.

Directs Department of Human Services to establish and maintain centralized child abuse reporting system. Modifies mandatory child abuse reporting process. Permits certain child abuse reports to be made electronically. Removes references to designees of department receiving child abuse reports.
