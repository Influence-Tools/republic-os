# Oregon HB 2147 (2023R1) — Relating to veterans.

Directs governing body of each county to designate person to ensure interment of unclaimed <i>human</i>] <b>cremated</b> remains of veteran or survivor of veteran. Limits civil liability <b>for interment of eligible decedent</b> except for gross negligence.
