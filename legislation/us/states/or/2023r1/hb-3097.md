# Oregon HB 3097 (2023R1) — Relating to certificates to use water for hydroelectric purposes within an artificial delivery system.

Provides that municipal corporation or people's utility district may apply for certificate to use water for hydroelectric purposes within <b>piped conduit in</b> artificial delivery system <b>that is delivering water for municipal uses</b> even if municipal corporation or people's utility district is not holder of underlying <b>municipal</b> water right, with permission of holder of underlying <b>municipal</b> water right.
