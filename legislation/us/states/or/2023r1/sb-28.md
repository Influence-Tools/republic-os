# Oregon SB 28 (2023R1) — Relating to elections; and declaring an emergency.

Prescribes method for creating ballot title and explanatory statement for<b> any amendment to Oregon Constitution or statutory Act that passes both houses of Legislative Assembly during 2023 session and is referred to people by Legislative Assembly</b> <i>Senate Joint Resolution 34, if resolution is referred to people</i>].
