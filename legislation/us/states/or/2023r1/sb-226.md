# Oregon SB 226 (2023R1) — Relating to nursing; and prescribing an effective date.

<b>Allows nurse employed by or contracted with certain facilities to execute medical order from out-of-state physician in specified circumstances.</b> Removes requirement that Oregon State Board of Nursing notify State Board of Pharmacy upon authorizing nurse practitioner or clinical nurse specialist to dispense prescription drugs.
