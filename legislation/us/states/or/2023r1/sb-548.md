# Oregon SB 548 (2023R1) — Relating to transportation of foster child's personal effects.

Directs Department of Human Services to maintain inventory of luggage carriers for use when transporting personal effects of foster children. <i>Directs department to maintain record of each instance when trash bags are used to transport foster child's personal effects and explanation of use of trash bag.</i>] Directs department to submit annual reports regarding transportation of personal effects of foster children to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to human services.
