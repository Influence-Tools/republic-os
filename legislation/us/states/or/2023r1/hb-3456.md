# Oregon HB 3456 (2023R1) — Relating to sexual misconduct at institutions of higher education; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes Sexual Misconduct Survey Council. Requires council to biennially distribute base sexual misconduct climate survey and recommendations regarding use of study to each institution of higher education.
