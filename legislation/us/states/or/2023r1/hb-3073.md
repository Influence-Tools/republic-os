# Oregon HB 3073 (2023R1) — Relating to public disclosure of voter records; and prescribing an effective date.

<b>Requires Secretary of State to establish provision to be included in each nominating petition or declaration of candidacy that allows candidate to check box indicating that candidate does not want candidate's residence address to be publicly disclosed except in response to written public records request.
