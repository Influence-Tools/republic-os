# Oregon SB 1048 (2023R1) — Relating to a Department of Transportation small business assistance program; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs Department of Transportation to establish small business development program to assist qualified small businesses in competing for department's public improvement contracts. Specifies requirements for program and how department will determine that business is qualified small business.
