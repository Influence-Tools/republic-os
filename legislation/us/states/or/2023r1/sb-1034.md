# Oregon SB 1034 (2023R1) — Relating to the payment of costs for educational services provided by programs operated by the military; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Education to make biennial transfer to Oregon Military Department from State School Fund for purpose of paying costs of educational services provided through programs operated by military department for at-risk youth.
