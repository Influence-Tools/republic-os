# Oregon SB 229 (2023R1) — Relating to adverse event reporting program.

Updates terminology concerning reporting of serious adverse events. Modifies Oregon Patient Safety Reporting Program. Modifies qualification of members on Oregon Patient Safety Commission who are group purchasers of health care or consumers of health care, and modifies duties of commission.
