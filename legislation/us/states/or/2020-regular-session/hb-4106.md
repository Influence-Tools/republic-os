# Oregon HB 4106 (2020 Regular Session) — Relating to the withdrawal of territory added to a district by a vote of the electors; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that city in Clackamas County annexed into county parks and recreation service district between January 1, 2005, and December 31, 2006, by vote of city electors is deemed withdrawn from and no longer part of county service district as of June 30, 2020.
