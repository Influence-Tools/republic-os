# Oregon HB 2101 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to audits of partnerships; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes partnership and partner audit procedures for Department of Revenue in conformity with federal centralized partnership audit regime. Allows department to issue assessment and collect taxes at partnership level based on adjustments arising from federal partnership-level audit or administrative adjustment request.
