# Oregon HB 2595 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to lobbying by former members of the Legislative Assembly.

Alters period during which former member of Legislative Assembly is prohibited from being paid lobbyist from sine die of next regular session that begins after person ceases to be member to one year after person ceases to be member.
