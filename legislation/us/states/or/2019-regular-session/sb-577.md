# Oregon SB 577 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to bias crimes; and declaring an emergency.

Renames crime of intimidation as bias crime. Adds gender identity to list of characteristics of victim, perception of which constitutes defendant's motive to commit crime. Removes requirement that bias crime in the first degree be committed by two or more persons.
