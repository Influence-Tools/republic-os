# Oregon HB 2010 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to funding to improve access to health care.

Extends Oregon Reinsurance Program and authorizes Department of Consumer and Business Services to request extension of waiver for state innovation. Authorizes department to change attachment point or coinsurance rate for program under specified conditions.
