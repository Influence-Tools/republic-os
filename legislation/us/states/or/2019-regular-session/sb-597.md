# Oregon SB 597 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to indictments.

Authorizes use of pseudonym, initials or other signifier instead of name of <i>witness or</i>] victim<b>, or witness if witness is also victim,</b> in indictment when specified requirements are met. <i>Limits disclosure</i>]<b> Provides for confidentiality</b> of document containing name of witness or victim<b> until entry of final judgment on case unless court orders otherwise based on finding of good cause</b>.
