# Oregon SB 385 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to alternative dispute resolution of parental rights disputes.

<i>Directs</i>]<b> Allows</b> presiding judge of each judicial district to establish alternative dispute resolution conference procedure for custody and parenting time modifications and enforcement proceedings.
