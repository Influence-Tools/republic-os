# Oregon SB 459 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to reserve amounts in tax credit auctions; and prescribing an effective date.

Decreases reserve amounts in auctions for tax credits for certified Opportunity Grant contributions and certified film production development contributions.<b> Sets deadline for auctions.</b> Applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1, <i>2020</i>]<b> 2019</b>, and before January 1, 2024.
