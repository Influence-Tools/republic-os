# Oregon HB 2449 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to tax for emergency communications; and providing for revenue raising that requires approval by a three-fifths majority.

Increases rate of tax for emergency communications. Decreases percentage of moneys in Emergency Communications Account that may be expended for administrative costs.<b> Phases in increase and decrease over one-year period.</b>
