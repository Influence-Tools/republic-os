# Oregon SB 5536 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the financial administration of the Real Estate Agency; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by Real Estate Agency.
