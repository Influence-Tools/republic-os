# Oregon HB 2835 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to recreational use of waterways; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires state agencies to post notice on agency website before restricting or closing site on state public lands where public may access lands for recreational use of floatable natural waterway. Requires state agencies to report on certain activities related to public access sites.
