# Oregon HB 2519 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to hazing at institutions of higher education.

Requires <b>each</b> community <b>college</b> <i>colleges</i>] and <b>each college and university</b> <i>universities</i>] operating in this state that <b>offers baccalaureate degree programs and receives</b> <i>receive</i>] state financial aid to adopt written policy on hazing, provide on-campus policy training on hazing and annually report to <i>Higher Education Coordinating Commission</i>]<b> Legislative Assembly</b> number of hazing incidents reported and investigated by community college<b>, college</b> or university during previous academic year.
