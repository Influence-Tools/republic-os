# Oregon HB 2222 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the Oregon Forestland-Urban Interface Fire Protection Act; and declaring an emergency.

Requires State Forestry Department to annually report to Legislative Assembly or interim committee regarding <i>development, implementation and administration of</i>]<b> implementation and enforcement of property notifications and certifications required under</b> Oregon Forestland-Urban Interface Fire Protection Act.
