# Oregon SB 494 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to employment of individuals with disabilities; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires employers who are authorized to pay subminimum wages to individuals with disabilities to pay certain wage rates until June 30, 2023. Requires employers to pay individuals with disabilities at minimum wage rate by July 1, 2023.
