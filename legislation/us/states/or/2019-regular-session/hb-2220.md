# Oregon HB 2220 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to vaccines administered by dentists; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes <i>trained and certified</i>] dentists to prescribe and administer vaccines. Directs Oregon Board of Dentistry to approve training course on prescription and administration of vaccines. Directs board to adopt rules related to prescription and administration of vaccines by dentists.
