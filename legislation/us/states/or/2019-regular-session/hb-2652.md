# Oregon HB 2652 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the use of helmets on boats.

Requires outfitters and guides to rent, sell or otherwise make available helmets to all passengers <b>on any section of waters rated as class III or higher on commonly accepted scale of river difficulty</b>.
