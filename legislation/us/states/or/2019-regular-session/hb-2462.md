# Oregon HB 2462 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to persons with military service involved in the criminal justice system.

Directs court to, at time of arraignment, notify defendant that status as servicemember may make defendant eligible for treatment programs, <b>diversion,</b> specialty courts or sentencing mitigation. Prohibits use of defendant's status as servicemember from being used as aggravating factor for purposes of sentencing.
