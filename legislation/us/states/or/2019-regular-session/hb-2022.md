# Oregon HB 2022 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the Oregon Virtual School District.

Changes name of Oregon Virtual School District to Oregon Digital Learning <i>Academy</i>]. Expands purposes of Oregon Digital Learning <i>Academy</i>] to require provision of professional development related to online learning.
