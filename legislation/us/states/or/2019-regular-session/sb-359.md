# Oregon SB 359 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to procedures for correcting defective corporate actions.

Provides structure and procedures by which business corporation and nonprofit corporation may ratify and approve defective corporate actions. Permits certain persons to challenge ratification and approval of defective corporate actions.
