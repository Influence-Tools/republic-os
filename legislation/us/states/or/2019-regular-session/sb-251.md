# Oregon SB 251 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to insurance regulation by the Department of Consumer and Business Services; and declaring an emergency.

Updates various statutes in Insurance Code to reflect changes in National Association of Insurance Commissioners model provisions related to reducing reserve requirements for domestic ceding insurers that cede insurance to reinsurers and to regulating insurance adjusters and insurance consultants.
