# Oregon HB 3061 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the transfer of a political subdivision's real property; and declaring an emergency.

Allows political subdivision to convey real property to nonprofit, municipal or private corporation for purpose of providing broadband service. Requires instrument conveying real property to authorize reversion of property to political subdivision if property is not used to provide broadband service.
