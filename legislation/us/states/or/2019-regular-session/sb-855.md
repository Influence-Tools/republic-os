# Oregon SB 855 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to professional practice authorizations; and declaring an emergency.

Directs professional licensing boards to <i>develop pathways to licensure, certification or other authorization to practice occupation or profession for specified persons.</i>] <b>study manner in which persons who are immigrants or refugees become authorized to practice occupation or profession. Directs boards to reduce barriers to authorization to practice for immigrants or refugees.</b> Requires boards to report to Legislative Assembly not later than November 30, 2019.
