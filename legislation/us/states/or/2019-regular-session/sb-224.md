# Oregon SB 224 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to elections; and prescribing an effective date.

Removes requirement that county clerk move elector to inactive status if elector has neither voted nor updated registration for five or more years. Permits military and overseas voters to request ballot by using electronic mail or facsimile machine.
