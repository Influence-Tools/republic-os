# Oregon HB 2328 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to unauthorized use of a vehicle.

Modifies culpable mental state for crime of unauthorized use of a vehicle when person takes, operates, exercises control over or otherwise uses vehicle, boat or aircraft without consent of owner.
