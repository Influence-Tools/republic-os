# Oregon HB 3030 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to professional authorizations; and prescribing an effective date.

Allows professional licensing board to issue <b>nonrenewable</b> temporary authorization to spouse of member of Armed Forces of United States stationed in Oregon and who holds <b>eligible</b> out-of-state authorization to provide occupational or professional service. <b>Specifies expiration date of temporary authorization.</b>
