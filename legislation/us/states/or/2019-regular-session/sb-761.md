# Oregon SB 761 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to electronic signature sheets.

Requires that only elector who has personally printed copy<b>,</b> <b>or requested that separate person print copy,</b> of electronic signature sheet for petition or prospective petition may sign signature sheet.<b> Requires elector to certify that elector received electronic signature sheet in compliance with requirement.
