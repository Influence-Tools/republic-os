# Oregon HB 2576 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to motor vehicles.

<i>Provides that when person's vehicle is totaled or substantially altered person may keep registration plates with vehicle if vehicle issued branded title or person may transfer plates to another vehicle if vehicle's title is canceled or retired.</i>]
