# Oregon SB 373 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to records of individuals placed under the jurisdiction of the Psychiatric Security Review Board; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Exempts from public records disclosure medical records of individuals under jurisdiction of Psychiatric Security Review Board that are maintained by board.</i>]<b> Expands public records law references that govern confidentiality of records maintained by Psychiatric Security Review Board.</b>
