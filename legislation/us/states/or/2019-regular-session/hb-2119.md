# Oregon HB 2119 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to tax administration; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires Department of Revenue to disseminate information on withholding of personal income tax by employer on behalf of employees, in lieu of preparation of withholding table. Allows department to determine amount, form and manner of withholding of tax.<b> Disconnects from federal personal income tax withholding provisions. Aligns terminology with income tax statutes and current practice.</b>
