# Oregon HB 2263 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to accelerated college credit programs; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of Education, in collaboration with Higher Education Coordinating Commission, to administer Accelerated College Credit Planning Partnership Grant Program to encourage partnerships between school districts and post-secondary institutions of education to offer accelerated college credit programs.
