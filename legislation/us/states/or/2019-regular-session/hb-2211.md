# Oregon HB 2211 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to persons with disabilities.

Includes <i>disabled</i>] veteran <b>who has 70 percent total disability rating and received discharge or release under other than dishonorable conditions</b> as person who may apply to Oregon Business Development Department for loan approval.
