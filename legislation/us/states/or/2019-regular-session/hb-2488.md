# Oregon HB 2488 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to cryptocurrency.

Prohibits state government from accepting payments using cryptocurrency. <i>Prohibits candidates for public office from accepting campaign contributions using cryptocurrency.</i>]<b> Prohibits contributions to political candidate, political committee or petition committee using cryptocurrency.</b>
