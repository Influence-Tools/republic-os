# Oregon HB 3116 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to proceeds of county sales; and declaring an emergency.

Requires counties with population of 650,000 or more to use proceeds from sale of foreclosed property to reimburse municipal liens against property for utilities improvements or seismic rehabilitation.
