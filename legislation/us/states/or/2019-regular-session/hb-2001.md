# Oregon HB 2001 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to housing; and declaring an emergency.

Requires cities with population greater than 10,000 <b>or within Metro</b> to allow duplexes in lands zoned for single-family dwellings within urban growth boundary. Requires Metro counties and cities and cities with population greater than 25,000 to allow middle housing in lands zoned for residential uses within urban growth boundary. Requires Land Conservation and Development Commission to draft model ordinances. Requires cities and counties to amend their comprehensive plan and land use regulations to conform with requirements or to directly apply model ordinance developed by commission.
