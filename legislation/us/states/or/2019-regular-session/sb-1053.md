# Oregon SB 1053 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the TriMet Crash Advisory Committee.

Establishes TriMet Crash Advisory Committee. Provides that committee shall review certain crashes involving TriMet vehicles that result in injury or fatality and make reports to TriMet's board of directors as needed.
