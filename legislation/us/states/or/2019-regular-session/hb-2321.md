# Oregon HB 2321 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to public records of county juvenile department employees; and prescribing an effective date.

Exempts from required disclosure pursuant to public records request, home address, home phone number and other specified personal information of county juvenile department employees who primarily perform duties related to youth offenders confined in detention facilities. Requires disclosure of county juvenile department employee information if public interest requires disclosure in particular instance.
