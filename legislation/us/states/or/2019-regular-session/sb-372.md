# Oregon SB 372 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to notification of parties with interest in towed vehicles; and declaring an emergency.

Reduces period in which towers must provide notice to owners and lienholders of vehicle after towing vehicle. Requires electronic notification of lienholders if Department of Transportation has established electronic portal.
