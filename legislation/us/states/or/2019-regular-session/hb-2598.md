# Oregon HB 2598 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to noncharitable trusts with business purposes.

Permits creation of <b>certain</b> noncharitable business purpose <i>trust</i>]<b> trusts</b>. Provides exemption from statutory rule against perpetuities for <b>certain</b> noncharitable business purpose <i>trust</i>]<b> trusts</b> if terms of trust <i>expressly</i>] <b>clearly</b> elect exemption.
