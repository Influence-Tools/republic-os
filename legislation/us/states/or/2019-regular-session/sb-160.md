# Oregon SB 160 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to college credit for courses taken in high school.

Requires public institutions of higher education to provide credit to each student who receives grade of four or higher on International Baccalaureate (IB) exam. Provides method for establishing exceptions to award of credit.
