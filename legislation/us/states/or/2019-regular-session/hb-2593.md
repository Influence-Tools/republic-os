# Oregon HB 2593 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to expression of milk in the workplace; and prescribing an effective date.

Conforms Oregon law related to expression of milk in workplace to federal law. <i>Eliminates</i>] <b>Limits to employers with 10 or fewer employees </b>exemption from providing rest periods for expression of milk in workplace if granting rest period imposes undue hardship.
