# Oregon HB 2577 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to annexations; and declaring an emergency.

Requires minimum three-year delay of effective date for annexation of property that is zoned to allow residential use as permitted use in zone and is in residential use when annexation is initiated. <b>Authorizes record owner of such real property located in territory to be annexed to waive delay of effective date.</b>
