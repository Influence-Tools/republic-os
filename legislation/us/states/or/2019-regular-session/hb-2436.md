# Oregon HB 2436 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to removal-fill laws; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of State Lands to collaborate with certain entities to develop proposal, including recommended legislation for introduction in 2020 regular session, for department to assume partial authority to administer federal wetland dredge and fill permits. Sunsets January 2, 2021.
