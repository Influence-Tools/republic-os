# Oregon SB 1041 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the regulation of coordinated care organizations.

Grants to Oregon Health Authority powers to regulate financial condition of coordinated care organizations that align with powers of Department of Consumer and Business Services to regulate domestic insurers. <b>Requires authority to convene advisory group consisting of coordinated care organization representatives to recommend standards for reconciling differences in reporting needed by authority and reporting required under National Association of Insurance Commissioners' standards.
