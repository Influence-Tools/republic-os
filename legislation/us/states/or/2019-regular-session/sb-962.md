# Oregon SB 962 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to victims of certain crimes.

Directs certifying agencies to certify to United States Citizenship and Immigration Services, within specified time, victim helpfulness in detecting, investigating or prosecuting qualifying criminal activity. Specifies certification procedures.
