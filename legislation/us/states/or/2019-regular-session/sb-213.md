# Oregon SB 213 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to connection to federal tax law; and prescribing an effective date.

Updates connection date to federal Internal Revenue Code and other provisions of federal tax law. <i>Removes tax year of taxpayer as connection date for certain changes in Internal Revenue Code that are related to definition of taxable income.</i>]
