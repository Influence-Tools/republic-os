# Oregon HB 3224 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to district attorney policies.

Directs district attorney of each county to, no later than December 1, 2020, develop and adopt policies relating to discovery, charging decisions<b>,</b> <i>and case disposition</i>]<b> sentencing and program recommendations and other specified subjects,</b> and to make policies available to public on website. Requires review and readoption of policies every five years.
