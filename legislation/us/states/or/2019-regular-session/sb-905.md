# Oregon SB 905 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to school district residency for children in foster care; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes resident school district for child whose parent or guardian voluntarily placed child outside child's home with public or private agency and who is living in licensed, certified or approved substitute care program.
