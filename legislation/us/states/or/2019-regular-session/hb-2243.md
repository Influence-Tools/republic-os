# Oregon HB 2243 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to public libraries.

Defines "public library" as public agency providing <b>to all residents of local government unit</b> free and equal access to library and information services <i>for all residents of local government unit</i>] <b>that are suitable for persons of all ages</b>.
