# Oregon SB 643 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to fuel storage facility compatibility grant program; and declaring an emergency.

Eliminates cap on total amount authorized for grant program for installation of generator connectors on fuel sources at cardlock fuel facilities.<b> Increases maximum amount authorized for each grant.</b>
