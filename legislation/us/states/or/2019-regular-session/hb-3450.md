# Oregon HB 3450 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to mixed-use housing within employment lands.

Allows city with population greater than 75,000 not within metropolitan service district to adopt <b>changes to its comprehensive plan and</b> land use regulations authorizing mixed-use housing within <b>up to 40 acres of</b> lands zoned for employment uses. <b>Requires submission of adopted changes to Land Conservation and Development Commission for review.</b>
