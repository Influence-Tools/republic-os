# Oregon SB 1012 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to land use requirements for marijuana production; and declaring an emergency.

Clarifies that, to qualify for exemption from land use compatibility statement, applicant for marijuana production license is not required to demonstrate continuous registration of marijuana grow site prior to date of application for licensure.
