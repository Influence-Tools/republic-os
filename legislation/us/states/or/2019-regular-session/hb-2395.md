# Oregon HB 2395 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to security measures required for devices that connect to the Internet.

Requires <i>person that manufactures, sells or offers to sell connected device</i>]<b> manufacturer</b> to equip connected device with reasonable security features that protect<b> connected device and</b> information that connected device <i>collects, contains, stores or transmits</i>]<b> stores</b> from access, destruction, modification, use or disclosure that consumer does not authorize.<b> Specifies what may constitute reasonable security features. Specifies exemptions and exclusions from requirement.</b>
