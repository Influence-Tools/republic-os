# Oregon SB 287 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to farm breweries.

Establishes standards for establishment of farm breweries <i>that produce less than 150,000 gallons of malt beverages</i>] on lands zoned for exclusive farm use or mixed farm and forest use<b> for breweries that annually produce under 150,000 barrels of malt beverages total with under 15,000 barrels produced on farm brewery site</b>.
