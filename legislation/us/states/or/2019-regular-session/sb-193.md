# Oregon SB 193 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to apportionment of income of interstate broadcasters; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Provides that interstate broadcaster's market for sales, used in determination of receipts factor in apportionment formula for corporate excise tax, is in state if customer's corporate domicile is in state or if individual customer is resident of state.</i>]
