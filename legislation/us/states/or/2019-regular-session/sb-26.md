# Oregon SB 26 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to employees at facilities under the jurisdiction of the Oregon Health Authority; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Director of the Oregon Health Authority or director's designee to discharge employee at facility under jurisdiction of authority if it has been substantiated that employee physically or sexually abused patient or client.
