# Oregon SB 1039 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to individuals with developmental disabilities.

Authorizes appointment of health care advocate to make health care decisions for individual with developmental disability receiving services through individualized written service plan if individual does not have guardian or health care representative. Specifies requirements for appointment of health care advocate, restrictions on health care advocate and rights of individual for whom health care advocate has been appointed.
