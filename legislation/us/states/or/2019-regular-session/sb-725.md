# Oregon SB 725 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to background checks of individuals who provide care; and prescribing an effective date.

Specifies charges<b>,</b> <i>or</i>] convictions<b>, arrests, deferred sentences, conditional discharges, referrals for diversion programs and pending indictments</b> that may not be considered in fitness determinations. Specifies types of licenses, certifications or employment for which conviction of certain crimes or charges, <b>arrests, conditional discharges, referrals for diversion programs,</b> deferred sentences or pending indictments regarding certain crimes <i>are</i>] <b>may be</b> disqualifying.
