# Oregon HB 2051 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the disclosure of tip line information; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Prohibits</i>] <b>Conditionally exempts from</b> disclosure, pursuant to public records request, <i>of</i>] contents of reports made through Department of State Police statewide tip line<b> unless public interest requires disclosure in particular instance. Provides that personally identifiable information is not subject to public interest balancing</b>. Authorizes disclosure to tip line staff, service providers, law enforcement and specified education persons for purpose of follow-up contact to provide or obtain further information. Provides that further information may be disclosed only to persons authorized to receive tip line information.
