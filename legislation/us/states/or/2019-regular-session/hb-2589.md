# Oregon HB 2589 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to discrimination on the basis of disability; and declaring an emergency.

Clarifies that sexual orientation <i>and transgender status are</i>] <b>is</b> not considered physical or mental <i>impairments</i>]<b> impairment and that individual does not have disability solely by reason of individual's sexual orientation</b>.
