# Oregon SB 1011 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to spousal support.

Provides that obligee spouse's conviction for attempted murder or conspiracy to commit murder of obligor spouse is change of circumstances for purposes of spousal support modification. Directs court, upon <b>petition for dissolution of marriage or</b> motion of obligor spouse, to <i>deny</i>]<b> modify</b> or terminate spousal support to obligee spouse upon obligee spouse's conviction for attempted murder or conspiracy to commit murder of obligor spouse.
