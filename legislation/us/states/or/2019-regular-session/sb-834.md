# Oregon SB 834 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to licensees of the Oregon Board of Dentistry.

Provides that expression of regret or apology made by or on behalf of person licensed by Oregon Board of Dentistry does not constitute admission of liability in civil action and that person who makes expression may not be examined with respect to expression.
