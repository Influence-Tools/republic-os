# Oregon SB 388 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to pardons; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Governor or designee to notify <b>district attorney and</b> presiding judge of circuit court of county of conviction within 10 days of granting pardon. Directs Governor or designee to review pardons granted within five years prior to effective date of Act and notify <b>district attorney and</b> presiding judge of circuit court of county of conviction. Directs presiding judge to, upon receipt of notification, issue order sealing record of conviction and other official records of case. Exempts from sealing records related to pardon.<b> Directs district attorney to notify victim concerning pardon and sealing of records.</b>
