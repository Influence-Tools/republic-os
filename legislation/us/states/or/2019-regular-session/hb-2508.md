# Oregon HB 2508 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to refugees in Oregon; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Department of Human Services to award grants to refugee resettlement agencies to provide specified services to refugees. Appropriates moneys from General Fund to department to award grants.
