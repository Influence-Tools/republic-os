# Oregon HB 2504 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to tax supervising and conservation commissions; and prescribing an effective date.

Increases maximum expenditure appropriation for tax supervising and conservation commissions and increases annual percentage increase in maximum expenditure appropriation. Authorizes commissions to charge for services provided by commissions to nonmember municipal corporations and to apply for and receive grants. Provides that funds earned from charges and grants shall supplement, not supplant, commission's expenditure appropriation.
