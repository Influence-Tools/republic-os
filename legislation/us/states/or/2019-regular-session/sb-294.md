# Oregon SB 294 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to pioneer cemeteries.

Authorizes annexation of land to pioneer cemetery maintenance district upon petition by owner and approval of governing body of district. Requires board of affected counties to file boundary change with county assessor and Department of Revenue.
