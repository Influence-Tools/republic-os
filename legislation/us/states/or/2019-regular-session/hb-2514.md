# Oregon HB 2514 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to privacy.

<i>Provides that person retains privacy and possessory interest in contents of residential garbage receptacle until collection for disposal by sanitation company. Prohibits law enforcement agency from searching or seizing contents without warrant or consent.</i>]
