# Oregon HB 2127 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to tax expenditures.

<i>Extends sunset for exemption from income or corporate excise taxes of capital gains from sale of manufactured dwelling park to certain entities. Creates</i>] <b>Eliminates</b> sunset for provisions granting exemptions from filing and payment of taxes and from establishment of domicile in state to companies and workers in state solely to perform disaster and emergency related work.
