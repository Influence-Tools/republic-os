# Oregon HB 2881 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the per-mile road usage charge program; and prescribing an effective date.

Replaces vehicle weight rating with designation as passenger vehicle as qualification for per-mile road usage charge program. Removes cap on number of vehicles that may participate in program. Increases from 17 to 20 miles per gallon minimum rating required for program. Exempts vehicles with rating of 40 miles per gallon or more from additional registration fees. Requires payment of additional registration fees for exempt vehicle if owner ends voluntary participation in program.
