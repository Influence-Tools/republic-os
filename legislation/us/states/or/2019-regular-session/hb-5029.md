# Oregon HB 5029 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to state financial administration; and declaring an emergency.

Maintains and reserves lottery revenues available to benefit public purposes during biennium beginning July 1, 2019, in Administrative Services Economic Development Fund. Allocates moneys from fund for biennium beginning July 1, 2019.
