# Oregon SB 669 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to assistance with activities of daily living provided to individuals in their own homes.

<i>Modifies requirements for licensing of in-home care agencies. Prohibits retaliation against in-home caregiver for making complaint or reporting violation to Oregon Health Authority. Creates private right of action for retaliation against in-home caregiver who makes complaint or reports violation.</i>]
