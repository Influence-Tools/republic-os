# Oregon HB 2847 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to income tax credits for rural health care practitioners; and prescribing an effective date.

Expands list of hospitals whose medical staff may qualify for specified income tax credit. Applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2020.
