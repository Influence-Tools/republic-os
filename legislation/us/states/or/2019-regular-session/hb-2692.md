# Oregon HB 2692 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the Practitioner-Managed Prescription Drug Plan; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Pharmacy and Therapeutics Committee to publish on website of Oregon Health Authority within seven days recommendations concerning Practitioner-Managed Prescription Drug Plan. Requires Director of the Oregon Health Authority to make decision to approve, disapprove or modify committee's recommendation as soon as practicable and publish decision on website.
