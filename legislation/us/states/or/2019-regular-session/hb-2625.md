# Oregon HB 2625 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to missing Native American women; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Department of State Police to study how to increase <b>and improve</b> criminal justice resources relating to missing<b> and murdered</b> Native American women in Oregon and report to appropriate committee or interim committee of Legislative Assembly no later than September 15, 2020.
