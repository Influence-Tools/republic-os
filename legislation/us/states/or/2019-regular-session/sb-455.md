# Oregon SB 455 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to higher education contracting.

Requires <i>public universities, community colleges and community college districts</i>]<b> certain institutions of higher education</b> to require contractors to employ apprentices and to establish and execute plan for outreach, recruitment and retention of women and minority individuals for certain work relating to improvements to real property.
