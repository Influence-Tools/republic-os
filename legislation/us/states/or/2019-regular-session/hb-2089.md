# Oregon HB 2089 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to requiring consumers to repay certain outstanding consumer loans before lenders make new consumer loans; and prescribing an effective date.

Prohibits title loan lender and payday loan lender from making loan to consumer until seven days after consumer has fully repaid outstanding title loan or payday loan.
