# Oregon SB 39 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to exemptions from registration in occupations regulated by the State Board of Examiners for Engineering and Land Surveying.

Replaces exemption from laws regulating practice of engineering, land surveying and photogrammetric mapping with exemption from registration requirement. Modifies exemption of employee or subordinate of registered professional engineer from statutes regulating practice of engineering. Revises terminology regarding exempted entities. Rearranges exemption provisions.
