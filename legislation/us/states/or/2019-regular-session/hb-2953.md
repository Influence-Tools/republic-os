# Oregon HB 2953 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the impacts of Good Neighbor Authority Agreement projects.

Requires State Forestry Department to report <b>biennially </b>to committee of Legislative Assembly regarding <i>environmental and employment impacts of</i>]<b> outcomes from all projects pursued in state under</b> Good Neighbor Authority Agreement <i>projects funded in whole or in part by moneys made available to department for project purposes</i>].
