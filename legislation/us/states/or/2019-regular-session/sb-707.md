# Oregon SB 707 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to suicide prevention; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes Youth Suicide Intervention and Prevention Advisory Committee. Directs advisory committee to <i>report to Director of Oregon Health Authority each biennium with assessment on implementation of</i>]<b> consult with Youth Suicide Intervention and Prevention Coordinator on updates to</b> Youth Suicide Intervention and Prevention Plan <i>and recommendations for administrative and legislative improvements</i>].
