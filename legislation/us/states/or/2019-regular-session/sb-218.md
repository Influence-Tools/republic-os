# Oregon SB 218 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to cannabis; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Oregon Liquor Control Commission to refuse to issue initial marijuana production licenses based on supply of and demand for marijuana.<b> Directs commission to process certain applications for production licenses that were received on or before June 15, 2018.
