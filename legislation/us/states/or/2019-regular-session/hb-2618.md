# Oregon HB 2618 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to solar incentives; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires State Department of Energy to adopt by rule program for providing rebates for purchase, construction or installation of residential and <b>certain</b> nonresidential solar electric systems and paired solar and storage systems. Sets forth rebate limits under program.
