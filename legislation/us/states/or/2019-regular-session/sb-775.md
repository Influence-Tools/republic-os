# Oregon SB 775 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to criminal records checks.

Requires agencies of state government to <i>use Department of State Police for purposes of requesting</i>] <b>conduct</b> criminal records checks relating to employment by agency<b> through Department of State Police</b>.
