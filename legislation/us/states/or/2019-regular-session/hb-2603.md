# Oregon HB 2603 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to rail transportation.

Directs Department of Transportation to study train delays<b> experienced by trains operated by Amtrak</b> and report on findings to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to transportation on or before September 15, 2020.
