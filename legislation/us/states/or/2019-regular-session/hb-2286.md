# Oregon HB 2286 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to investment of moneys used to fund scholarship program for children of public safety officers.

Places civil forfeiture proceeds used to fund scholarship for children of deceased or disabled public safety officers in account established or designated by State Treasurer in Oregon 529 Savings Network, rather than in Oregon 529 College Savings Plan Subaccount.
