# Oregon HB 3137 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to transient lodging tax collection.

Provides that transient lodging tax becomes due when occupancy of transient lodging with respect to which tax is imposed ends. Provides that transient lodging tax to be remitted with tax return is amount of tax due with respect to all occupancy of transient lodging that ended during reporting period to which return relates.
