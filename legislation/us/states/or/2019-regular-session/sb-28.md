# Oregon SB 28 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to Oregon Health Authority fee amounts; and declaring an emergency.

Increases fees related to tourist facilities, public spas, pools and bathhouses, bed and breakfasts, restaurants and vending machines and for plan review for restaurant construction or remodeling.
