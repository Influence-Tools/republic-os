# Oregon SB 740 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to cancer treatment.

Requires health benefit plans that cover radiation therapy for cancer to cover proton beam therapy on basis no less favorable than other covered benefits.<b> Permits prior authorization or other utilization review.</b>
