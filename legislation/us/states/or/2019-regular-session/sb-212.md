# Oregon SB 212 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to taxation; and prescribing an effective date.

<i>Disallows, for personal income or corporate excise tax purposes, deduction for losses from wagering transactions. Requires addition, in determination of Oregon taxable income, of amount of losses from wagering transactions if deducted on taxpayer's federal return. Applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2019.</i>]
