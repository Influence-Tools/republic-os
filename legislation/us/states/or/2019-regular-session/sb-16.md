# Oregon SB 16 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to special education services; and declaring an emergency.

Specifies types of licensed health care practitioners authorized to perform assessments or examinations for purposes of determining special education services eligibility. Requires practitioners to report information obtained in assessment or examination to school district.
