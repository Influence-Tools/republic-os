# Oregon HB 2341 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to reasonable accommodation for pregnancy-related conditions.

Makes unlawful employment practice for employer to <i>deny reasonable accommodation to known limitations related to pregnancy, childbirth or related medical condition or to</i>]<b>, because of known limitations related to pregnancy, childbirth or related medical condition of job applicant or employee,</b> take certain actions related to reasonable accommodations to known limitations related to pregnancy, childbirth or related medical condition. <b>Specifies that requirements apply to employers who employ six or more persons.</b>
