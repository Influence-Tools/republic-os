# Oregon SB 60 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to health care professional liability limitation; and declaring an emergency.

Allows acupuncturists<b> and podiatric physicians</b> to claim liability limitation for provision of health care services without compensation. Requires biennial registration by health practitioners claiming liability limitation.
