# Oregon HB 2471 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to prosecutions for violation of city motor vehicle parking ordinances.

Provides that, in prosecutions for violation of city motor vehicle parking ordinances, defendant may not be required to pay fine or bail amount before requesting hearing or submitting explanation to court.
