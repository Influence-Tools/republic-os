# Oregon HB 2059 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to State Department of Agriculture food establishment licenses; and declaring an emergency.

Extends sunset on State Department of Agriculture authority to annually impose limited fee increases for certain licenses related to food production. Establishes maximum fee for license following new sunset date to be last fee for license in effect prior to new sunset date.
