# Oregon SB 23 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to data submitted to the Oregon Health Authority by health care facilities.

Requires <i>hospitals to submit emergency department discharge abstract records to</i>] Oregon Health Authority or <i>to</i>] entity contracting with authority to <i>compile</i>] <b>obtain discharge</b> records <b>from hospitals, ambulatory surgical centers and extended stay centers. Permits authority to charge fee to ambulatory surgical centers to cover cost of abstracting and compiling records. Permits authority to require health maintenance organizations to submit ambulatory surgery, inpatient and emergency department discharge records.</b>
