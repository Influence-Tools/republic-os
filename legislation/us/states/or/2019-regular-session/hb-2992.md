# Oregon HB 2992 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to noncompetition agreements.

Establishes as condition of enforceability of noncompetition agreement that employer provide signed, written copy of terms of agreement to employee within <i>seven</i>] <b>30</b> days after termination of employee's employment.
