# Oregon HB 2102 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to disclosure of taxpayer information for fraud prevention; and prescribing an effective date.

Allows Department of Revenue to disclose taxpayer information to multijurisdictional information sharing organization formed to combat identity theft and fraud and to member tax preparation software vendors.
