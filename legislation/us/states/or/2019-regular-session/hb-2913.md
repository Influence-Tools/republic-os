# Oregon HB 2913 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to possessory liens on motor vehicles; and declaring an emergency.

Removes requirement for person to have surety bond in effect before making, altering, repairing, transporting, storing, providing services for, supplying materials for or performing labor in connection with motor vehicle.
