# Oregon HB 2215 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to personal privacy of persons with mental illness; and declaring an emergency.

Adds to rights of persons with mental illness committed to Oregon Health Authority, right to reasonable privacy and security in resting, sleeping, dressing, bathing, personal hygiene and toileting.
