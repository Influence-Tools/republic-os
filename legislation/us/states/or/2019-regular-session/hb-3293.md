# Oregon HB 3293 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to civil proceedings; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that action based on sexual assault or knowingly allowing, permitting or encouraging sexual assault that occurs when person is 18 years of age or older must be commenced within <i>seven</i>] <b>five</b> years from date person discovers, or in exercise of reasonable care should have discovered, causal connection between sexual assault and injury.
