# Oregon SB 596 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to evidence of prostitution resulting from crime reporting.

Provides that if person reports commission of person felony to emergency communications system or law enforcement agency, evidence of prostitution obtained as result of making report may not be used against person in prosecution for prostitution or attempted prostitution.
