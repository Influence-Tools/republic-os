# Oregon SB 1045 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the taxation of property used in a home share program; and prescribing an effective date.

Authorizes city or county to adopt property tax exemption <i>for portion</i>] <b>in amount not to exceed $300,000 of assessed value</b> of principal place of residence used by homeowner to rent space in residence <b>through public or nonprofit home share program</b> to individuals seeking such space. <i>Provides that exemption shall be granted as 100 percent exemption for five consecutive years unless city or county adopts other terms.</i>] Provides that <i>city or county</i>] <b>adopting jurisdiction</b> must require <i>means testing of home share seekers. Requires</i>] home share rental agreement to offer space at rent affordable to home share seekers with income at or below <i>level determined by city or county</i>] <b>60 percent of area median income, must prohibit exemption for home share agreements among family members and must specify period for which exemption may be granted</b>.
