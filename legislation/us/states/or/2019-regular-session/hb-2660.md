# Oregon HB 2660 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to eligibility for unemployment insurance benefits.

Removes prohibition on payment of unemployment insurance benefits to nonprofessional employees of educational institution providing facilities or janitorial services for weeks of unemployment commencing during period between two successive academic years or terms.
