# Oregon SB 824 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to dental licensure examinations; and declaring an emergency.

Allows Oregon Board of Dentistry to accept results of national standardized examinations under specified circumstances when determining fitness of applicant to practice dentistry <b>or dental hygiene</b>. Requires board to accept results from regional and national testing agencies for laboratory and clinical examinations under specified circumstances. Requires board, for certain applicants for licensure, to accept results of clinical board examinations from regional and national testing agencies.
