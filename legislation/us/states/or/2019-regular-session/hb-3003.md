# Oregon HB 3003 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to security for liability of self-insured employers in workers' compensation cases.

Permits Director of Department of Consumer and Business Services to release surety bond or other security on deposit with director if self-insured employer or self-insured employer group obtains paid-up insurance policy satisfactory to director.
