# Oregon SB 770 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to statewide health care coverage; and declaring an emergency.

Establishes <b>Task Force on</b> Universal Health Care <i>Commission</i>] charged with recommending to Legislative Assembly design of Health Care for All Oregon Plan, administered by Health Care for All Oregon Board to provide publicly funded, equitable, affordable, comprehensive and high quality health care to all Oregon residents. Specifies membership and duties of <i>commission</i>]<b> task force</b>. Requires recommendations to be guided by specified values and principles. Prescribes requirements for plan design and items that <i>commission</i>]<b> task force</b> must consider.
