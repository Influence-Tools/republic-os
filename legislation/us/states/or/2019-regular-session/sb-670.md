# Oregon SB 670 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to information provided to voters.

Prohibits name of <i>Secretary of State,</i>] county clerk or filing officer from appearing in official capacity on voters' pamphlet, return identification envelope, secrecy envelope or other ballot information provided to electors if <i>secretary,</i>] county clerk or filing officer is on ballot. <i>Establishes exception to permit name, office and signature of Secretary of State to appear on cover of voters' pamphlet as part of statement stating that pamphlet may be used to assist electors in voting.</i>]
