# Oregon HB 2033 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to child welfare caseworkers.

<i>Removes</i>]<b> Modifies</b> minimum degree requirements for individuals who conduct child abuse investigations or make determinations regarding protective custody of children. <i>Directs Department of Human Services to establish minimum qualifications for such individuals by rule.</i>]<b> Removes exception to degree requirements for certain Department of Human Services employees.</b>
