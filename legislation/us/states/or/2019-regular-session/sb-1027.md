# Oregon SB 1027 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to health care practitioners; and prescribing an effective date.

Allows certain health care practitioner who receives needlestick injury during provision of medical care to patient who is unconscious or otherwise unable to consent to <i>test patient for human immunodeficiency virus</i>] <b>perform blood draw on patient</b> for purpose of determining <i>necessary course of treatment for</i>] <b>whether</b> health care practitioner <b>needs to begin any post-exposure prophylactic treatment</b>.
