# Oregon HB 2585 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to reports of death.

<i>Directs Chief Medical Examiner to complete and file report of death for person within 45 days after receiving report from Search and Rescue Coordinator, in conjunction with county sheriff, that person is missing in wilderness and presumed dead.</i>]
