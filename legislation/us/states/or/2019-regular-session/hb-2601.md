# Oregon HB 2601 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to guardianships.

Limits guardian's authority to limit protected person's preferred associations with third parties. Permits interested person to move court to review guardian's power to limit protected person's associations<b> and requires court to conduct hearing</b>. <b>Permits court to remove guardian for unreasonably limiting protected person's associations or failing to perform certain duties.</b>
