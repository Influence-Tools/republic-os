# Oregon SB 164 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the Oregon Retirement Savings Board.

Makes employer's failure to comply with requirements of Oregon Retirement Savings Plan unlawful practice. Allows employee to file complaint with Commissioner of Bureau of Labor and Industries alleging unlawful practice. Authorizes Oregon Retirement Savings Board to request that commissioner investigate employer's compliance with requirements of plan.
