# Oregon HB 2202 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to veterans.

Directs Bureau of Labor and Industries to develop and administer program to conduct outreach to <i>residents of Oregon who served in Oregon National Guard or reserve component of armed forces</i>]<b> veterans</b> to inform them about trade careers and connect them with available apprenticeship opportunities.
