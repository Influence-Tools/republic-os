# Oregon HB 2658 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to prescription drug costs.

Requires manufacturer of prescription drugs to report to Department of Consumer and Business Services <i>planned</i>]<b> specified</b> increase in price of certain prescription drugs at least 60 days before date of increase. Provides exceptions.
