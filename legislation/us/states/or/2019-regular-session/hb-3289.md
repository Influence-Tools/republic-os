# Oregon HB 3289 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to correctional facilities; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs <i>Department of Corrections</i>]<b> Oregon Criminal Justice Commission</b> to conduct <i>study</i>]<b> studies</b> on<b> local and regional</b> correctional facility <i>siting process and</i>]<b> data collection and provision of health care. Directs commission to convene advisory council for studies. Directs local and regional correctional facilities to submit data for studies no later than January 1, 2020. Directs commission to </b>present report on findings to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to judiciary on or before September 15, 2020. Sunsets January 2, 2021.
