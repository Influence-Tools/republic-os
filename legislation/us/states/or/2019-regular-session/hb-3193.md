# Oregon HB 3193 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to wages.

Requires Commissioner of Bureau of Labor and Industries to notify Construction Contractors Board of valid wage claim filed against contractor <b>or business</b> <i>who is</i>] subject to licensing by board. Requires commissioner to notify board if order of determination has become final and if contractor fails to pay <i>construction debt</i>] <b>unpaid wages</b> within certain time period after date on which order became final <b>unless motion to stay order is pending or has been granted by commissioner</b>.
