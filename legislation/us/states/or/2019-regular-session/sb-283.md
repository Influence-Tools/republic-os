# Oregon SB 283 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to exposure to radiation in schools in this state; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Oregon Health Authority to review peer-reviewed, independently funded scientific studies of health effects of exposure to microwave radiation, particularly exposure that results from use of wireless network technologies in schools and to report results of review to interim committee of Legislative Assembly related to education not later than January 2, 2021. Specifies requirements for review.
