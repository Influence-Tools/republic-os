# Oregon HB 2910 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the Oregon Promise program.

Requires that for person who completes prior educational requirements to participate in Oregon Promise program while confined in correctional facility, six-month period to enroll in courses to participate in program begins after person is first released from correctional facility.
