# Oregon SB 92 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to annexation of lands zoned for residential use.

Authorizes city to provide for as much as 20-year ramp-up to full taxation rate for property in annexed territory. Adds, for purposes of "island annexation," corporate boundaries of another city as permitted portion of boundary surrounding territory to be annexed.
