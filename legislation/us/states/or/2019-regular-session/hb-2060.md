# Oregon HB 2060 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to food establishments lacking valid authorizations required for operation.

Authorizes State Department of Agriculture to order <i>condemnation or</i>] closure of food establishment if department authorization required for operation of food establishment has not been obtained or has lapsed.
