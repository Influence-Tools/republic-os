# Oregon SB 1051 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to mitigating fuel costs; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes, if House Bill 2020 becomes law, credit available to eligible persons for purpose of mitigating carbon price indirectly paid through purchase of fuel to propel eligible motor vehicles on public highways.
