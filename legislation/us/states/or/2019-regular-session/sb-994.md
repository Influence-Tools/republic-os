# Oregon SB 994 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to juveniles.

Requires person who has taken child into custody to request, prior to releasing child to custody of child's noncustodial parent, Department of Human Services to conduct criminal records check on noncustodial parent and all adults in same home as noncustodial parent.
