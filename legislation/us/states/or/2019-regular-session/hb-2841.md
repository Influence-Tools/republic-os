# Oregon HB 2841 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the release of animal data; and declaring an emergency.

Allows State Department of Fish and Wildlife to refuse<b> or condition</b> disclosure of information relevant to department ability to manage or protect described fish or wildlife species or individual members or populations of species. Creates <i>exceptions</i>] <b>exception to refusal authority</b>. Exempts information from disclosure as public record.
