# Oregon SB 362 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to notice of mental defense.

Requires defendant to file notice of intent to introduce evidence of mental defense at least 45 days before trial. Authorizes court, for good cause, to allow defendant to file notice within 45 days before trial.
