# Oregon HB 2983 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to campaign finance; and declaring an emergency.

Requires covered <i>nonprofit</i>] <b>organizations that make political expenditures in excess of specified amount</b> to file <b>with Secretary of State</b> donor identification list that identifies donors that made donations above <i>specified amount</i>] <b>$10,000 during election cycle </b>to covered <i>nonprofit if covered nonprofit makes aggregate political expenditures above specified amount.</i>] <b>organization. Requires covered organization to update list throughout election cycle.</b>
