# Oregon SB 813 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to investigations of child care facilities; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Office of Child Care to make reasonable attempt to identify any <b>child care</b> facility<b>, or person or place providing child care,</b> about which it receives complaint if certain information is provided. Modifies definition of "serious complaint" to direct Office of Child Care to conduct on-site investigation of premises of child care facility when office receives report of alleged child abuse at facility.
