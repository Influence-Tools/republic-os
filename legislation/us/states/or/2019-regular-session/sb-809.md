# Oregon SB 809 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to determining the fitness of individuals to provide care using public funds.

Requires Department of Human Services and Oregon Health Authority to prescribe by rule criteria <i>for</i>]<b> to be considered in</b> making fitness <i>determinations</i>]<b> determination findings of abuse</b>. Provides subject individuals with right to contested case hearing to challenge evaluation of fitness.
