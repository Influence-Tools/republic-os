# Oregon SB 290 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to civil immunity for volunteers fighting wildfires.

Makes person voluntarily fighting wildfire immune from civil liability for injury resulting from good faith performance of firefighting efforts.<b> Creates exceptions.</b>
