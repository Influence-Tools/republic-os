# Oregon SB 933 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to inquiries issued by public bodies about race or ethnicity.

Provides that form or document issued by public body asking person to identify person's race or ethnicity must allow person to select multiple races or ethnicities <i>and include certain categories of race or ethnicity</i>].
