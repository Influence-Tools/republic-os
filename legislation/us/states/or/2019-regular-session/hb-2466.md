# Oregon HB 2466 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to financial practices in associations.

Requires <b>certain</b> homeowners associations and <b>all</b> condominium unit owners associations to <i>be audited by certified public accountant at least once every three years if annual assessments exceed $500,000</i>]<b> carry fidelity bond coverage, unless owners and board have elected otherwise in the preceeding year</b>.
