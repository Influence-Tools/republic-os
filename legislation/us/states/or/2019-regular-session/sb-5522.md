# Oregon SB 5522 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the financial administration of the Oregon Medical Board; and declaring an emergency.

Limits biennial expenditures from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by Oregon Medical Board.
