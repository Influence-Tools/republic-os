# Oregon SB 370 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to federal inspections of records that employers use to verify the employment eligibility of an employee; and declaring an emergency.

Requires employer to provide notice to employees of upcoming inspection by federal agency of employer's records regarding identity and employment eligibility of employees within <i>72 hours</i>] <b>three business days</b> of employer's receipt of notice of inspection from agency.
