# Oregon HB 2609 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to dental directors; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Requires prescription monitoring system established by Oregon Health Authority to be accessible to dental director appointed by authority.</i>] Directs <b>Oregon Health</b> Authority to disclose certain patient information to dental directors for specified purposes. Defines "dental director."
