# Oregon HB 2769 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to a consultant selection process for a public contract for certain services; and prescribing an effective date.

Permits contracting agency, in conducting procurement for architectural, engineering, photogrammetric mapping, transportation planning and land surveying services, to consider pricing policies, proposals and other pricing information as part of screening and selection of consultants in specified circumstances.<b> Specifies allowable contents of pricing proposals. Specifies protest rights for prospective consultants.</b>
