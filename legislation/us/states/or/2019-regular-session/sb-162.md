# Oregon SB 162 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to establishment of 529 accounts by taxpayers; and prescribing an effective date.

Provides that personal income taxpayer may <b>elect to </b>establish account in Oregon 529 Savings Network through <i>election on tax return form</i>]<b> means provided by Department of Revenue</b>. <i>Directs Department of Revenue to provide means for election on form.</i>] Authorizes Oregon 529 Savings Board to work in coordination with department to provide means for establishment of accounts.
