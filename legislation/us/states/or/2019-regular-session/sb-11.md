# Oregon SB 11 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to rights of redemption.

<b>Requires person who purchases residential real property after foreclosure complaint has been filed and before end of redemption period to provide notice to seller regarding relinquishment of interests in property and rights to surplus funds.
