# Oregon SB 1002 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to sentencing.

Prohibits prosecuting attorney from conditioning defendant's plea offer on waiver of eligibility for transitional leave<b>,</b> <i>or certain</i>] sentence reductions<b> or other programs for which executing or releasing authority may consider defendant</b>.
