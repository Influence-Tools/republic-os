# Oregon SB 128 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to supervision of fluoroscopy; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Board of Medical Imaging to issue permit to supervise fluoroscopy to qualified advanced practice registered nurse. Authorizes Board of Medical Imaging, Oregon State Board of Nursing and Oregon Health Authority to adopt rules.
