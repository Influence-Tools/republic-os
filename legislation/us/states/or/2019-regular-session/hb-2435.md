# Oregon HB 2435 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to guest ranches on tracts of land zoned for exclusive farm use.

Makes permanent 2018 law sunsetting April 15, 2020, that allows guest ranches to be established on lands zoned for exclusive farm use in eastern Oregon. <b>Requires new guest ranches to report on operations to county.</b>
