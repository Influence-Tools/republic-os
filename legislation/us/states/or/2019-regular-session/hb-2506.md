# Oregon HB 2506 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to mass transit district investments; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that funds held in trust by <i>mass transit district</i>]<b> TriMet</b> for purpose of paying post-employment benefits other than pensions are not surplus funds and may be invested in accordance with statutory standard of judgment and care for investment of public investment funds.
