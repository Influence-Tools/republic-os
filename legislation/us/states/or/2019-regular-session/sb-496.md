# Oregon SB 496 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to access to educational facilities; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Requires school districts to ensure that all students of school district have equal access to educational facilities. Prohibits certain requirements or restrictions from being imposed on</i>] <b>Prohibits school district from restricting access by alternative education students, English language learners or special education students<b> to specialized learning areas and certain common areas</b>.
