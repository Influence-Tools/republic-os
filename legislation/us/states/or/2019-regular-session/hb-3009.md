# Oregon HB 3009 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to labor organization representation; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that labor organization's charging certain employees reasonable fees and costs for representation that is unrelated to negotiation of collective bargaining agreement is not unfair labor practice.
