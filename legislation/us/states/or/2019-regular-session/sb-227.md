# Oregon SB 227 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to emergency planning; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes cemetery authority, or person with authority to determine disposition of human remains, to move, temporarily store or reinter human remains dislodged from original cemetery plot by natural disaster or other similar emergency.
