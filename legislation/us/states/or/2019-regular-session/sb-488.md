# Oregon SB 488 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to immunization against influenza for incarcerated persons; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Department of Corrections to offer immunization against influenza virus to each inmate in physical custody of department. Requires department to schedule appointment to receive immunization for each inmate who does not decline immunization in writing.
