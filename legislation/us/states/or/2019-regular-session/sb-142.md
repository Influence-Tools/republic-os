# Oregon SB 142 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to communicable health conditions.

Changes "venereal disease" and "sexually transmitted disease" to "sexually transmitted infection." Changes <b>certain references to</b> "human immunodeficiency virus" and "acquired immune deficiency syndrome" to "blood-borne <i>pathogen</i>] <b>infection</b>."
