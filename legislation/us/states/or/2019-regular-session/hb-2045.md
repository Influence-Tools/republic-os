# Oregon HB 2045 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to sex offenders; and declaring an emergency.

Extends deadline by which State Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision must perform risk assessment and classification on existing sex offender registrants to December 1, 2026. Directs board to biennially report to Legislative Assembly beginning February 1, 2021, until December 1, 2026, on assessment and classification progress.
