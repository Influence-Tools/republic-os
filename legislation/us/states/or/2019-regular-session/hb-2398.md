# Oregon HB 2398 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to charitable gaming.

Specifies that compensation paid to employees of charitable gaming events is based on Oregon minimum wage standards. <b>Removes requirement that Department of Justice adopt rules to authorize concurrent participation by certain employees or members in specified events.</b>
