# Oregon SB 396 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the towing of vehicles from fuel dispensaries.

Establishes alternative procedure for use by owner, operator or employee of fuel dispensary to obtain removal of vehicle preventing or hindering access to fuel dispensing equipment.
