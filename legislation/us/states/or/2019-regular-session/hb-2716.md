# Oregon HB 2716 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to campaign finance.

Requires communications made in support of or opposition to candidate <b>to state name of persons that paid for communication. Requires person that makes communication to include specified donor information in specified circumstances. Allows digital communication to include link to active website that prominently displays required donor information </b> <i>or measure to identify whether candidate, petition committee or political committee authorized communication</i>].
