# Oregon HB 2024 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to early childhood care.

Directs Early Learning Division to establish program to improve access to high quality infant and toddler care for families <i>that are eligible to receive employment-related child care subsidy</i>]<b> whose incomes are at or below 200 percent of federal poverty guidelines</b>.
