# Oregon HB 2574 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to shellfish; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Department of Land Conservation and Development to <i>establish electronic information system to</i>] receive, consolidate and organize public record information concerning shellfish mariculture in Oregon. <b>Requires department to establish electronic system to store and share information concerning shellfish mariculture.</b> Appropriates moneys to department for program for biennium beginning July 1, 2019.
