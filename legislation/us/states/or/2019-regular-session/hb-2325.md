# Oregon HB 2325 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the Secretary of State's duties as a filing officer; and prescribing an effective date.

Permits Secretary of State to specify by rule method, including but not limited to electronic method, by which Secretary of State will send notice related to document for which Secretary of State is filing officer.<b> Requires Secretary of State to permit person that files document to elect to receive notice by method Secretary of State specifies. Requires Secretary of State to send notice by mail if person does not elect to receive notice by method Secretary of State specifies.</b>
