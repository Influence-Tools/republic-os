# Oregon HB 2213 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to academic textbook affordability.

<i>Requires each community college and public university to ensure that course description for every academic course offered in succeeding academic term lists all textbooks enrolled students are required to purchase. Creates limited exceptions.</i>]
