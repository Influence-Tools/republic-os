# Oregon SB 522 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to beverage containers.

Prohibits person from<b>, with intent to defraud,</b> <i>intentionally</i>] returning for refund value during one day <i>25</i>]<b> 50</b> or more individual beverage containers that person knows <i>or has good reason to know</i>] were not sold in this state.
