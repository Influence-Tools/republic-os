# Oregon SB 20 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to services provided to individuals with developmental disabilities.

Consolidates eligibility for services to children and adults with developmental disabilities. Modifies types of developmental disability services that may be offered<b>,</b> <i>and</i>] eligibility for services<b> and entities that may deliver services</b>. Establishes new terminology.
