# Oregon SB 47 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to small watercraft; and prescribing an effective date.

Requires person operating nonmotorized boat to have waterway access permit. Eliminates requirement that person operating nonmotorized boat have aquatic invasive species permit. Directs uses of waterway access permit fees.
