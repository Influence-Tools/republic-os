# Oregon SB 246 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to Alcohol Education Program funding.

Eliminates requirement that amount of service permit application fee exceeding service permit program costs be dedicated to Alcohol Education Program. Eliminates special fee on licenses and service permits for support of Alcohol Education Program.
