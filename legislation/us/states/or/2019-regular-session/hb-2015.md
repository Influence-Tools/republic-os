# Oregon HB 2015 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to documents issued by the Department of Transportation; and declaring an emergency.

Eliminates requirement that person provide proof of legal presence before Department of Transportation issues noncommercial driver license, noncommercial driver permit or identification card.
