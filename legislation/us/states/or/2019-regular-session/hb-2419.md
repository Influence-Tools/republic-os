# Oregon HB 2419 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to the licensing processes of the Department of Consumer and Business Services; and prescribing an effective date.

Permits Department of Consumer and Business Services to issue licenses and conduct criminal records checks through Nationwide Multistate Licensing System. Requires certain applicants for licenses department issues to submit fingerprints for individuals with ownership interests in applicant and for individuals with supervisory authority over applicant's operations.
