# Oregon HB 2353 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to public records; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Attorney General, district attorney or court to award penalty to public records requester, <i>plus reasonable attorney fees,</i>] <b>or order fee waiver or fee reduction,</b> if public body responds to request with undue delay or fails to be responsive to request.
