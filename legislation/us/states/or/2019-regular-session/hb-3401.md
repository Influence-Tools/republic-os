# Oregon HB 3401 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to commodity commissions; and declaring an emergency.

Abolishes Oregon Orchardgrass Seed Producers Commission on later of July 1, 2019, or one day after effective date of Act. Provides for Oregon Tall Fescue Commission to assume winding up of Oregon Orchardgrass Seed Producers Commission affairs. Requires Director of Agriculture to determine types and subtypes of commodities formerly assessed by Oregon Orchardgrass Seed Producers Commission that may be assessed by Oregon Tall Fescue Commission.
