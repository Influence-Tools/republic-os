# Oregon SB 27 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to Oregon Drinking Water Quality Act fees; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Oregon Health Authority to adopt by rule schedule for fees assessed on water suppliers to partially defray costs of authority related to performance of certain duties under Oregon Drinking Water Quality Act.
