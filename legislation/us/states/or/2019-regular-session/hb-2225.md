# Oregon HB 2225 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to forest template dwellings.

Defines "center of the subject tract" for siting certain permissible forest dwellings. Prohibits forest dwellings that would not have been permitted before January 1, 2019. <b>Allows exceptions until November 1, 2023.</b> Establishes applicable dates by county.
