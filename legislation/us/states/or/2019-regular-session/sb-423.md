# Oregon SB 423 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to psychological screening for law enforcement officers; and prescribing an effective date.

Prohibits law enforcement agencies from employing person as law enforcement officer unless person has completed psychological screening to determine person's fitness to serve as law enforcement officer.
