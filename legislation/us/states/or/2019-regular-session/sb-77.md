# Oregon SB 77 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to virtual racing; and declaring an emergency.

Allows Oregon Racing Commission to adopt rules to authorize and license Multi-Jurisdictional Simulcasting and Interactive Wagering Totalizator Hubs to conduct simulcast broadcasting of, and mutuel wagering on, animal races.
