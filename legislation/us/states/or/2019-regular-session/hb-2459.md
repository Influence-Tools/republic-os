# Oregon HB 2459 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to lien information statements for real property that is subject to an encumbrance.

<i>Provides procedure for certain persons that hold interest in real property to request and obtain lien information statement that shows amounts necessary to satisfy encumbrance against real property from other person that holds encumbrance.</i>]
