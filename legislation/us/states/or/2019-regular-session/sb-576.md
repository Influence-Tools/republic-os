# Oregon SB 576 (2019 Regular Session) — Relating to campus security officers; and declaring an emergency.

Imposes certain requirements related to private security providers who contract with <b>or are employed by</b> institutions of higher education to provide private security services on campus and special campus security officers commissioned by governing boards of public universities.
