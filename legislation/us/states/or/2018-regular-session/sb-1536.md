# Oregon SB 1536 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to mass transit districts.

Provides that directors of certain mass transit districts be appointed by Governor instead of elected. Provides that current elected directors continue to serve until expiration of their terms, unless earlier discharged by Governor.
