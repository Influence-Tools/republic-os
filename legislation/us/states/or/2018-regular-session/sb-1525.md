# Oregon SB 1525 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to child-caring agencies that provide adoption placement services; and declaring an emergency.

Exempts child-caring agencies that provide adoption placement services but do not provide care to children and do not receive public funds from requirement to provide financial statement audited or reviewed by certified public accountant.
