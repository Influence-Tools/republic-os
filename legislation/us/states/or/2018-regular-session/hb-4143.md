# Oregon HB 4143 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to drugs; and declaring an emergency.

Requires Director of Department of Consumer and Business Services to study barriers to <i>medication assisted</i>] <b>effective</b> treatment for <b>and recovery from</b> substance use disorders, including addictions to opioids and opiates, and to report and make recommendations to Legislative Assembly not later than June 30, 2018. Sunsets January 2, 2019.
