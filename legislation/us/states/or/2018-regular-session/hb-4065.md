# Oregon HB 4065 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to child care; and declaring an emergency.

Prohibits child care providers from enrolling in Central Background Registry or providing child care for five years after denial for cause, revocation or voluntary surrender of certification or registration and for five years after denial for cause, removal or voluntary surrender of enrollment in Central Background Registry.
