# Oregon HB 4022 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to electricity for motor vehicles; and prescribing an effective date.

Permits state agency to locate devices or facilities for providing electricity to public for electric motor vehicles in locations sufficient to meet demand for devices or facilities. Requires Oregon Department of Administrative Services to establish criteria for determining appropriate number of locations for devices and facilities.
