# Oregon HB 4074 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to escheat of United States savings bonds; and declaring an emergency.

Provides proceedings to escheat to State of Oregon matured United States savings bonds presumed abandoned and in custody of state. Authorizes Director of Department of State Lands to seek payment for escheated bonds from United States Treasury.
