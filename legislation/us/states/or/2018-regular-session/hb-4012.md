# Oregon HB 4012 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to reemployment of retired members of the Public Employees Retirement System; and prescribing an effective date.

Extends sunset on provisions allowing retired member of Public Employees Retirement System to be reemployed by participating public employer as teacher of career and technical education without loss of retirement benefits.
