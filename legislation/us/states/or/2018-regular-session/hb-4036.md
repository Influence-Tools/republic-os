# Oregon HB 4036 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to participation in interscholastic activities; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Requires school districts to allow homeschooled students and students who attend public charter schools to participate in interscholastic activities, regardless of whether activities are governed by voluntary association that administers interscholastic activities.</i>]
