# Oregon SB 1554 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to determination of eligibility for public benefits of higher education savings account owner; and prescribing an effective date.

Specifies that amount in account established for higher education expenses is disregarded for purposes of determining account owner's financial eligibility to receive assistance or benefit authorized by law, <b>other than means-tested state financial aid for higher education,</b> to extent permitted under federal law.
