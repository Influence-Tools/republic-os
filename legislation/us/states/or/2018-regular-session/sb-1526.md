# Oregon SB 1526 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to the parental rights of individuals with disabilities; and declaring an emergency.

Prohibits court from considering parent's disability in determining whether to terminate parental rights <i>absent finding that behaviors or limitations related to parent's disability will endanger health, safety or welfare of child or ward even when accommodations or support services are in place</i>]<b> unless parent's conduct related to disability is of such nature and duration as to render parent incapable of providing proper care to child or ward for extended periods of time</b>.
