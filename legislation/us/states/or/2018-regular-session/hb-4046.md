# Oregon HB 4046 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to salary under the Public Employees Retirement System; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that outside compensation of employee of Oregon Health and Science University, public university or community college is not included in employee's salary for purposes of Public Employees Retirement System.
