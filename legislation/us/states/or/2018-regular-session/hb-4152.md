# Oregon HB 4152 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to wildfires; and declaring an emergency.

Requires <i>Office of Emergency Management</i>]<b> State Fire Marshal</b> to issue grants to sheriffs' offices of Multnomah County and Hood River County for expenses related to wildfire recovery, mitigation and preparedness.
