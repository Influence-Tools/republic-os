# Oregon HB 4155 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to Internet service providers; and declaring an emergency.

<i>Prohibits broadband Internet access service provider from disclosing, selling or permitting access to personal information of customers of provider. Creates exceptions, including exception for giving express consent.</i>]
