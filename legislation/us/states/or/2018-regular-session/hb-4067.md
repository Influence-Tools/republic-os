# Oregon HB 4067 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to developmental delays; and prescribing an effective date.

Expands definition of term "child with a disability" for purposes of special education to include children who have developmental delays and who are <i>under 10 years of age</i>]<b> in third grade or lower</b>.
