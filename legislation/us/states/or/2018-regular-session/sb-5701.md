# Oregon SB 5701 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to state financial administration; and declaring an emergency.

Increases limit for six-year period beginning July 1, 2017, on payment of expenses from fees, moneys or other revenues, including Miscellaneous Receipts, but excluding lottery funds and federal funds, collected or received by <i>Department of Corrections</i>]<b> various state agencies</b> for capital construction.
