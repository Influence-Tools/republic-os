# Oregon HB 4031 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to the use of land; and declaring an emergency.

Permits and regulates establishment of guest ranches on eastern Oregon exclusive farm use lands. Deems certain existing guest ranches conforming uses. Sunsets guest ranch provisions on April 15, 2020.
