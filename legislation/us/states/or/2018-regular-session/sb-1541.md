# Oregon SB 1541 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to toxic air contaminants; and declaring an emergency.

Authorizes Environmental Quality Commission to adopt program and rules to reduce public health risks from emissions of toxic air contaminants from individual stationary industrial and commercial air contamination sources.
