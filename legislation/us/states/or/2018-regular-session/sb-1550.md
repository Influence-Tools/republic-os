# Oregon SB 1550 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to food fish.

Authorizes State Fish and Wildlife Commission to adopt record keeping requirements for person engaged in taking, landing, buying or selling of food fish for commercial purposes or otherwise dealing in food fish for commercial purposes. <b>Creates exception.</b> Allows commission <i>and Director of Agriculture</i>]<b>, State Department of Fish and Wildlife, State Department of Agriculture or designees</b> to examine records.
