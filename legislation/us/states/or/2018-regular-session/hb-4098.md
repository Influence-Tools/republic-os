# Oregon HB 4098 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to veterans; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs Department of Veterans' Affairs to develop written material regarding apprenticeship opportunities and provide material to certain agencies, county veterans' service officers and veterans' organizations for distribution to veterans.
