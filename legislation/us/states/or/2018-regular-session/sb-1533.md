# Oregon SB 1533 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to the siting of facilities that incorporate nonclinical treatment settings.

Allows facility providing <b>equine and equine-affiliated</b> therapy or counseling <i>that incorporates activities utilizing nonclinical setting</i>] to be established in area zoned for exclusive farm use if certain conditions are met.
