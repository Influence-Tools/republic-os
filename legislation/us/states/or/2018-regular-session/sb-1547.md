# Oregon SB 1547 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to concussions sustained by youth athletes; and declaring an emergency.

Expands list of health care professionals who can provide medical release to youth athlete who is suspected of having concussion. Prescribes requirements for health care professionals to be qualified to provide medical release.
