# Oregon HB 4130 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to student transportation; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes grant program under which Department of Education awards grants to school districts for percentage of certain student transportation costs for which school district does not receive any amount in distributions from State School Fund<b> or any amount related to transit activities</b>.
