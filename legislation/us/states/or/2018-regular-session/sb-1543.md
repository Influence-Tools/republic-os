# Oregon SB 1543 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to public safety; and declaring an emergency.

Directs Attorney General to appoint members with certain <b>backgrounds or</b> qualifications to batterers' intervention program standards advisory committee. Specifies requirements for program standards.
