# Oregon SB 1501 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to the State Fair Council; and prescribing an effective date.

Reduces minimum and maximum number of members for State Fair Council. Limits consecutive terms of service on council. Requires Governor to adjust terms of office for council members holding office on effective date of Act to achieve staggered expiration of member terms.
