# Oregon HB 4118 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to projects on federal lands; and declaring an emergency.

Expresses state policy regarding pursuit of projects under Good Neighbor Authority Agreement. Instructs signatory state agencies to work with federal land management agencies to prioritize projects.
