# Oregon HB 4149 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to prohibited provisions in criminal action agreements.

Prohibits prosecuting attorney from conditioning plea offer <i>or release on defendant's waiver of specified rights, eligibilities and legal challenges</i>]<b> on requirement that defendant or defense attorney stipulate to unconstitutionality of existing law</b>. Provides that prohibited provision in plea agreement <i>or release agreement</i>] is void and unenforceable.
