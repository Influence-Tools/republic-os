# Oregon SB 1566 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to employer contributions to the Public Employees Retirement System; and prescribing an effective date.

Establishes Employer Incentive Fund. Appropriates moneys in fund to Public Employees Retirement Board for purpose of matching portions of certain lump sum payments of employer contributions to Public Employees Retirement System.
