# Oregon HB 4044 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to a study about educators; and prescribing an effective date.

Directs Chief Education Office to conduct study on recruitment, retention, mentoring and professional development of educators who serve students from student groups that may be at risk for experiencing achievement gap.
