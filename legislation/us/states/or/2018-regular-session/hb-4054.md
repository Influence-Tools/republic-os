# Oregon HB 4054 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to removal of personal property.

Authorizes Department of Transportation to enter into intergovernmental agreement with city having population of 500,000 or more for purpose of removing certain personal property from locations <i>within city's urban growth boundary</i>]<b> owned by department</b>.
