# Oregon HB 4163 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to state financial administration; and declaring an emergency.

Provides that Higher Education Coordinating Commission, instead of Oregon Department of Administrative Services, receives appropriations to pay bond-related costs of certain Article XI-G bonds.
