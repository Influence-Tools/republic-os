# Oregon HB 4088 (2018 Regular Session) — Relating to regulating operations of performing rights societies.

Prohibits certain activities by, and specifies additional duties for, performing rights society in notifying business proprietors concerning proprietors' duties with respect to performing copyrighted musical works and in negotiating contracts for paying royalties for performing copyrighted musical works.
