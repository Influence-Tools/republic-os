# Idaho S 1177 (2017) — APPROPRIATIONS – DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE – Appropriates $38,829,600 to the Department of Commerce for fiscal year 2018; limits the number of authorized full-time equivalent positions to 43; transfers $2,000,000 from the General Fund to the Idaho Opportunity Fund; transfers $31,800 from the Indirect Cost Recovery Fund to the General Fund; and transfers $8,200 from the Indirect Cost Recovery Fund to the Tourism and Promotion Fund.
