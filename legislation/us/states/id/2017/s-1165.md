# Idaho S 1165 (2017) — APPROPRIATIONS – HEALTH AND WELFARE – PUBLIC HEALTH SERVICES – Appropriates $114,110,000 to the Department of Health and Welfare for the Public Health Services Division for fiscal year 2018; limits the number of authorized full-time equivalent positions to 234.69; provides for transfers to the Cooperative Welfare Fund; limits the transfer of funds budgeted for trustee and benefit payments; provides for program integrity; provides for specific use of General Fund moneys; requires an annual report on accreditation status; and appropriates an additional $1,070,300 to the Department of Health and Welfare for the Physical Health Services Program for fiscal year 2017.
