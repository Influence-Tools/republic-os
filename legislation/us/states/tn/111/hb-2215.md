# Tennessee HB 2215 (111) — Private Protective Services - As enacted, changes the fingerprint requirement for contract security company licensee applicants and security guard/officer applicants from three sets of classifiable fingerprints to one set of classifiable electronic fingerprints; reduces from three to one the number of photographs a security guard/officer applicant must submit; and makes certain other changes to the Private Protective Services Licensing and Regulatory Act. - Amends TCA Section 40-35-114 and Title 62, Chapter 35.
