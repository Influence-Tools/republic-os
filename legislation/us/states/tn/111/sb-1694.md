# Tennessee SB 1694 (111) — Sunset Laws - As enacted, extends the state capitol commission to June 30, 2026; adds two citizen members to the commission, with one to be appointed by the speaker of the senate and one to be appointed by the speaker of the house. - Amends TCA Title 4, Chapter 29 and Title 4, Chapter 8.
