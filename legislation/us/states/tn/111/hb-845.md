# Tennessee HB 845 (111) — Water Pollution - As enacted, specifies that soil is not a discarded material constituting waste for purposes of the Tennessee Solid Waste Disposal Act and the Tennessee Hazardous Waste Management Act as long as the soil is intended for use or reuse as soil. - Amends TCA Title 68 and Title 69.
