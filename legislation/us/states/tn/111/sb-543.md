# Tennessee SB 543 (111) — Personal Property - As enacted, revises provisions governing notice for lien enforcement under the Tennessee Self-Service Storage Facility Act; authorizes an online sale to enforce a lien on personal property located at a self-service storage facility, authorizes self-storage facility owners to contact county clerks to determine the owner and any lienholder for a motor vehicle left in a self-storage unit. - Amends TCA Title 66, Chapter 31.
