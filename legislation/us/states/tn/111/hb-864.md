# Tennessee HB 864 (111) — Remedies and Special Proceedings - As enacted, defines "guardian" or "legal guardian" as "a person having the legal authority to provide for the care, supervision, and control of a minor child as established by law or court order" for purposes of criminal injuries compensation. - Amends TCA Title 29, Chapter 13.
