# Tennessee HB 1521 (111) — McLemoresville - Subject to local approval, deletes antiquated provisions related to poll taxes; codifies current practice of city regarding election timing and terms; adds grounds for removal from office for board of mayor and aldermen; authorizes the hiring of a city recorder; removes cap on fines that may be imposed for a violation of ordinances, on property tax, and business tax. - Amends Chapter 507 of the Private Acts of 1949; as amended.
