# Tennessee HB 2868 (113) — Taxes, Hotel Motel - As enacted, prohibits Memphis and Nashville, with regard to hotel motel taxes, having a preexisting privilege tax or authority, from changing the designated use; authorizes those municipalities to otherwise change the allocations of the revenue, except in accordance with and subject to any other restrictions of state law relative to accommodations for transients and hotel occupancy. - Amends TCA Title 67, Chapter 4, Part 14.
