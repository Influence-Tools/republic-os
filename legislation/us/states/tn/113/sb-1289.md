# Tennessee SB 1289 (113) — Business and Commerce - As enacted, generally prohibits a municipality or county from adopting or enforcing an ordinance, order, or regulation that requires a permit or fee for the installation or use of a battery-charged security fence that is in addition to an alarm system permit issued by the municipality or county; imposes installation or operational requirements for a battery-charged security fence that are inconsistent with current requirements in law for a battery-charged security fence in this bill; or prohibits the installation or use of a battery-charged security fence. - Amends TCA Title 62, Chapter 32, Part 3.
