# Tennessee SB 2073 (113) — General Services, Dept. of - As enacted, removes the annual reporting requirements associated with acquiring, owning, and leasing motor vehicles purchased by the commissioner for use as state vehicles; removes the requirement that information collected by the commissioner regarding energy-efficient and alternative fuel motor vehicles be shared on the department's website. - Amends TCA Title 4, Chapter 3, Part 11.
