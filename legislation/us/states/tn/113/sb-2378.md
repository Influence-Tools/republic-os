# Tennessee SB 2378 (113) — Comptroller, State - As enacted, requires the comptroller to conduct a study of all insurers, including insurance pools, that provide policies of workers' compensation coverage to local governmental entities, including a review of each insurer's solvency, a comparative rate study, and an analysis of the process to bid out, procure, or otherwise obtain such a policy; requires the comptroller to report the findings to the speakers no later than January 1, 2025. - Amends TCA Title 12; Title 50 and Title 56.
