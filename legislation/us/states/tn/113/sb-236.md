# Tennessee SB 236 (113) — Game and Fish Laws - As enacted, deletes requirement that the Tennessee Fish and Wildlife Commission file proclamations establishing hunting and fishing seasons with the county clerks for the counties affected and instead requires such proclamations be made available on the Tennessee wildlife resources agency website. - Amends TCA Section 70-4-107.
