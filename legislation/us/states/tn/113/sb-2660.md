# Tennessee SB 2660 (113) — Children's Services, Dept. of - As enacted, require the department to file a petition of termination of adoptive parent's parental rights with a juvenile court, within 90 days of the juvenile courts finding that there has been substantial noncompliance by a parent or guardian with the statement of responsibilities in a permanency plan. - Amends TCA Title 36 and Title 37.
