# Tennessee SB 2652 (113) — Teachers, Principals and School Personnel - As enacted, requires local boards of education to include school safety training in the training requirements for substitute teachers; clarifies that LEAs are prohibited from hiring a substitute teacher whose license in another state is revoked; makes other changes concerning LEA policies for substitute teachers. - Amends TCA Title 49.
