# Tennessee SB 2427 (113) — Alcoholic Beverages - As enacted, permits a retail licensee, beginning July 1, 2014, to sell at retail items related to or incidental to the use, consumption, dispensing, or storage of alcoholic beverages, including any hemp or hemp-derived cannabinoid product for which all required licenses, permits, or certificates have been obtained authorizing the sale of such product within this state. - Amends TCA Title 57, Chapter 3 and Title 57, Chapter 5.
