# Tennessee HB 831 (113) — Tennessee Bureau of Investigation - As enacted, requires the bureau to develop a request for certification form to be completed by the court and submitted to the bureau prior to entering an order of expunction, with a few exceptions; requires the bureau to determine and certify whether a submitted offense is eligible for expunction; prohibits entry of an order of expunction on or after January 1, 2024, unless a certificate of eligibility from the bureau is attached to the order of expunction. - Amends TCA Title 38, Chapter 6 and Title 40, Chapter 32.
