# Tennessee HB 132 (112) — Lottery, Charitable - As enacted, authorizes an organization that is authorized to hold an annual event from the period January 1, 2021, through June 30, 2021, to hold the event no later than 60 calendar days after the event date listed in the annual event application, or June 30, 2021, whichever date is earlier. - Amends TCA Title 3, Chapter 17.
