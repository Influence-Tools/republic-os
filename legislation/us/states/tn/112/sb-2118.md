# Tennessee SB 2118 (112) — Game and Fish Laws - As enacted, transfers assets held in the wildlife management endowment fund to a newly created lifetime sportsman endowment fund, which will be an irrevocable trust for the exclusive benefit of lifetime sportsman license holders; revises other related provisions. - Amends TCA Title 9 and Title 70.
