# Tennessee SB 1088 (112) — Administrative Procedure (UAPA) - As enacted, changes the attorney general and reporter's review of an emergency rule, from not disapproving solely on the basis of failure to meet statutory criteria to requiring that the attorney general not approve an emergency rule that does not meet statutory criteria for adoption. - Amends TCA Title 4, Chapter 5.
