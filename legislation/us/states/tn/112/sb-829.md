# Tennessee SB 829 (112) — Courts, Administrative Office of the - As enacted, requires the AOC to provide each court clerk in a county that has not installed TnCIS with a list of the data that is required under Rules of the Supreme Court of Tennessee or state law; requires the AOC and court to coordinate efforts to ensure that the court clerks submit the required data by January 1, 2022, and in an ongoing manner thereafter, pursuant to procedures established by the AOC. - Amends TCA Section 8-4-115; Title 16 and Title 18, Chapter 1.
