# Tennessee SB 1326 (112) — Taxes, Sales - As enacted, authorizes dealers whose sales and use tax liability for 12 consecutive months has averaged $1,000 or less per month to file returns and payment either monthly or quarterly; requires that the $1,000 be adjusted for inflation and rounded to the nearest $10 every five years, beginning January 1, 2026. - Amends TCA Title 67, Chapter 6, Part 5.
