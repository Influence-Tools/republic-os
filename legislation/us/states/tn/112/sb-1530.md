# Tennessee SB 1530 (112) — Child Abuse - As enacted, adds to the definition of "severe child abuse" the act of knowingly allowing a child to be within a structure where a Schedule I controlled substance, cocaine, methamphetamine, or fentanyl is present and accessible to the child. - Amends TCA Title 37.
