# Tennessee HB 685 (112) — Motor Vehicles - As enacted, requires, at the time of hire, if an entity employs drivers or contracts with drivers who use the drivers' personal vehicles for the delivery of the entity's goods or services and the entity does not provide automobile insurance coverage to those drivers, the entity to inform the driver that the drivers' automobile insurance may not cover commercial uses. - Amends TCA Title 55, Chapter 12 and Title 56.
