# Tennessee HB 2868 (112) — Immigration - As enacted, adds the department of finance and administration to the list of recipients under the requirement that the Tennessee office for refugees at least quarterly transmit certain information to the chairs of the state government committee of the house and state and local government committee of the senate, and to the chair of the budget committee of either the city council or the county legislative body regulating a host community. - Amends TCA Title 4; Title 8; Title 9; Title 37; Title 68 and Title 71.
