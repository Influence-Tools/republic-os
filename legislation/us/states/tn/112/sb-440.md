# Tennessee SB 440 (112) — Law Enforcement - As enacted, establishes a reward for information leading to the arrest of any person responsible for the shooting of a law enforcement officer in the line of duty; sets the reward at $10,000 if the officer is injured in the shooting or $20,000 if the officer is killed in the shooting. - Amends TCA Title 38.
