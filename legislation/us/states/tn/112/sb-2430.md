# Tennessee SB 2430 (112) — Taxes, Sales - As enacted, extends until 2025 the reporting requirement to the department of revenue by certain wholesalers of food, candy, and nonalcoholic beverages regarding net sales of such products to retailers; requires the comptroller to conduct a review of the retail accountability program regarding the reporting required for sales of food, candy, and nonalcoholic beverages, to determine the effectiveness and efficiency of the program. - Amends TCA Section 67-6-410.
