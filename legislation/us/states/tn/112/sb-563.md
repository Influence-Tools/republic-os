# Tennessee SB 563 (112) — Taxes, Personal Property - As enacted, authorizes, from May 25, 2021, to July 1, 2024, the county trustee to proceed against delinquent taxpayers by retaining an agent to collect delinquent personal property taxes, interest, costs, and attorneys' fees. - Amends TCA Title 67, Chapter 5.
