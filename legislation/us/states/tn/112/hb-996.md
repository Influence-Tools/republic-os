# Tennessee HB 996 (112) — Human Services, Dept. of - As enacted, authorizes the department to utilize an enrollment-based child care subsidy payments program that complies with all applicable federal funding requirements and legal authority and that seeks to provide adequate, stable payments to providers of child care services by establishing effective payment practices based upon accurate time and attendance systems. - Amends TCA Title 4; Title 5; Title 6; Title 7; Title 68 and Title 71.
