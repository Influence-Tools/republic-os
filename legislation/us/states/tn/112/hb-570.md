# Tennessee HB 570 (112) — Administrative Procedure (UAPA) - As enacted, increases from 75 to 90 days that amount of time for which the house or senate government operations committee may stay a rule; authorizes additional 90-day stays by committees acting jointly in certain circumstances. - Amends TCA Section 4-5-215.
