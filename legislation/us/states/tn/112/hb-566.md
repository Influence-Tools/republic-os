# Tennessee HB 566 (112) — Administrative Procedure (UAPA) - As enacted, requires a department with rules promulgated and published on the secretary of state's website or with an administratively attached agency with rules so promulgated and published to submit, by December 1, 2023, and every eight years thereafter, a report of the department's chapters in effect as of July 1 of the year that the report is due to the chairs of the house and senate government operations committees. - Amends TCA Title 4.
