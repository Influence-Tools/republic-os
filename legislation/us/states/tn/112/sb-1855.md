# Tennessee SB 1855 (112) — Insurance Companies, Agents, Brokers, Policies - As enacted, exempts from the general requirement that a person must be licensed in order to sell, solicit, or negotiate insurance in this state a medicare product marketing representative, unless licensing as an insurance producer is required under applicable centers for medicare and medicaid services (CMS) regulations. - Amends TCA Title 56, Chapter 6, Part 1.
