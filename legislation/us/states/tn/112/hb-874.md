# Tennessee HB 874 (112) — Foster Care - As enacted, requires the caseworker for a child who has been placed in foster care to document any objection to the child's placement with a relative that is made by another relative or other interested party; requires the department of children's services to include the number of such objections in its annual report on foster care. - Amends TCA Title 36 and Title 37.
