# Tennessee SB 622 (112) — Probation and Parole - As enacted, clarifies that when a person is serving two or more probationary sentences and the person's probation is revoked on one sentence, then the person must receive credit for time served as a result of that revocation against any other concurrent probationary sentence that is subsequently revoked in any jurisdiction in this state. - Amends TCA Title 39; Title 40 and Title 41.
