# Tennessee HB 235 (112) — Purchasing and Procurement - As enacted, increases from $10,000 or more to $25,000 or more, the amount for which a local board of education or the governing body of a public charter school located in a county having a population of 40,000 or more must make purchases or expenditures by competitive bids. - Amends TCA Section 12-3-1212; Section 49-13-111 and Section 49-2-203.
