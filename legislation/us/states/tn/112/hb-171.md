# Tennessee HB 171 (112) — Pensions and Retirement Benefits - As enacted, authorizes a correctional officer of a local government who is a member of the state retirement system to retire under the state retirement system upon completion of 25 years of creditable service; authorizes local governments participating in TCRS to adopt a mandatory age of retirement for correctional officers. - Amends TCA Title 8, Chapter 36.
