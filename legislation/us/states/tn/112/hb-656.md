# Tennessee HB 656 (112) — Municipal Government - As enacted, reduces from 24 hours to 16 hours the required amount of continuing professional education for certified municipal finance officers (CMFO); authorizes a municipality to contract with a certified public accountant to act as a CMFO; removes outdated compliance timelines. - Amends TCA Title 6, Chapter 56, Part 4.
