# Tennessee SB 874 (112) — Taxes, Exemption and Credits - As enacted, exempts from the sales and use tax online access to continuing education courses that meet regulatory requirements for licensed individuals and that are offered by organizations that have received a determination of exemption from the IRS as a charitable organization or business association. - Amends TCA Title 67.
