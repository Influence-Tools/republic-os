# Tennessee SB 745 (112) — Banks and Financial Institutions - As enacted, authorizes a state bank to request from the commissioner a waiver or modification of certain terms and conditions applicable to a national bank; authorizes a state bank to request from the commissioner authority to exercise certain incidental powers or engage in certain incidental activities; requires commissioner to publish a summary of applications made for waiver, modification, or authorization. - Amends TCA Section 45-2-601.
