# Tennessee SB 1075 (112) — Environment and Conservation, Department of - As enacted, requires the commissioner to promulgate rules that take effect by July 1, 2021, to create a commercial use authorization to allow persons wanting to engage in commercial activities at state parks or state natural areas to obtain a permit to do so; specifies that the rules must include fees structured to promote park self-sufficiency and economic development. - Amends TCA Title 4 and Title 11.
