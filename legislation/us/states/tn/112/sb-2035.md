# Tennessee SB 2035 (112) — Tobacco, Tobacco Products - As enacted, defines "smokeless nicotine product"; adds smokeless nicotine products to type of products that are age-restricted to persons aged 21 years and older under the Prevention of Youth Access to Tobacco, Smoking Hemp, and Vapor Products Act; specifies that nicotine replacement therapy products as defined and approved by the federal food and drug administration are not smokeless nicotine products. - Amends TCA Title 39, Chapter 17, Part 15.
