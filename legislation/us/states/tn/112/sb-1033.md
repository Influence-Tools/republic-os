# Tennessee SB 1033 (112) — Landlord and Tenant - As enacted, authorizes a tenant to terminate a residential rental or lease agreement entered into or renewed on or after July 1, 2021, upon the tenant providing the landlord with written notice stating that the tenant or household member is a domestic abuse victim, sexual assault victim, or stalking victim, regardless of whether the victim is an adult or a child; establishes requirements for the tenant and landlord in such situations. - Amends TCA Title 13; Title 45; Title 47; Title 48; Title 56 and Title 66.
