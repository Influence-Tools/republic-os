# Tennessee HB 1441 (110) — Tipton County - Subject to local approval, repeals requirement that owners of real property obtain a building permit; repeals authorization to hunt red foxes; repeals provisions relative to litter and refuse control; increases from four to five the membership of the Purchasing Committee; revises certain notice requirements; revises provisions relative to jurisdiction of Purchasing Committee; deletes provision authorizing attorneys to appoint a substitute judge under certain circumstances; deletes provision authorizing the governor to fill a judicial vacancy; requires certain tax proceeds to be used for community development. - Amends Chapter 518 of the Private Acts of 1941; as amended.
