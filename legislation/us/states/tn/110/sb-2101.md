# Tennessee SB 2101 (110) — State Government - As enacted, deletes various reports required from the Tennessee higher education commission (THEC) and the department of transportation; changes the due date for THEC's annual report on the lottery scholarship program from March 15 to October 1. - Amends TCA Title 4, Chapter 3, Part 23; Title 49, Chapter 1, Part 3; Title 49, Chapter 4, Part 9 and Title 49, Chapter 7, Part 2.
