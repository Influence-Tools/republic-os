# Tennessee SB 1716 (110) — Garnishments and Executions - As enacted, requires that the form garnishment notice be changed to conform to existing law that specifies a garnishment creates a lien on earnings until the judgment is satisfied, or for six months, whichever occurs first. - Amends TCA Section 26-2-216.
