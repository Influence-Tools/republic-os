# Tennessee HB 1557 (110) — County Government - As enacted, authorizes a county legislative body to adopt a resolution by majority vote prohibiting term limits for persons appointed to boards or commissions by the county mayor if the appointee serves without compensation, not including reimbursement for travel and expenses. - Amends TCA Title 5; Title 6 and Title 7.
