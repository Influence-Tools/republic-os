# Tennessee HB 2105 (110) — Workers Compensation - As enacted, allows farm and agricultural employers to accept the Workers' Compensation Law by purchasing a workers' compensation insurance policy; allows a farm or agricultural employer to withdraw acceptance of the Law at any time by canceling or not renewing the policy and providing notice to its employees. - Amends TCA Section 50-6-106.
