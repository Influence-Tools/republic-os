# Michigan SB 1094 (2019-2020) — Health facilities: nursing homes; admittance of COVID-19-positive patients to nursing homes from another facility; prohibit, and develop centralized intake facilities.admittance of individuals who test positive for coronavirus in nursing homes; prohibit under certain circumstances, and require the department of health and human services to develop a process for the approval of certain centers and designated areas within nursing homes for individuals with coronavirus, implement certain visitation policies, and report certain information on coronavirus to the legislature. Amends sec. 21717 of 1978 PA 368 (MCL 333.21717) & adds sec. 5145.
