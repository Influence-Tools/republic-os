# Nevada SB 403 (80) — Revises provisions relating to data privacy for pupils. (BDR 34-309)

AN ACT relating to education; requiring each public and private school to provide certain information to a pupil or the parent or legal guardian of a pupil before providing technology to a pupil or allowing a pupil to use a school service; revising provisions relating to school service providers; and providing other matters properly relating thereto.
