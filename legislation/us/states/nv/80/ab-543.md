# Nevada AB 543 (80) — Makes various changes regarding state financial administration and makes appropriations for the support of the civil government of the State. (BDR S-1280)

AN ACT relating to state financial administration; making appropriations from the State General Fund and the State Highway Fund for the support of the civil government of the State of Nevada for the 2019-2021 biennium; providing for the use of the money so appropriated; making various other changes relating to the financial administration of the State; and providing other matters properly relating thereto.
