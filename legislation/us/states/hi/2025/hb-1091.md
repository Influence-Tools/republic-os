# Hawaii HB 1091 (2025) — RELATING TO SEWER TRANSMISSION LINES.

Requires a county to accept, within 60 days after receipt of a completed application for maintenance request, license or dedication of sewer transmission lines and related sewerage facilities servicing Hawaiian Home Lands that are brought into compliance by the Department of Hawaiian Home Lands. (CD1)
