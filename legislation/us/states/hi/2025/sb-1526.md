# Hawaii SB 1526 (2025) — RELATING TO THE DRUG AND ALCOHOL TOXICOLOGY TESTING LABORATORY.

Extends the lapse date for funds appropriated out of the Drug and Alcohol Toxicology Testing Laboratory Special Fund for fiscal years 2023-2024 and 2024-2025 for the establishment and maintenance of a drug and alcohol toxicology testing laboratory. (CD1)
