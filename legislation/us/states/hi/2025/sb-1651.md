# Hawaii SB 1651 (2025) — RELATING TO PUBLIC MEETINGS.

Requires board packets to be posted at least three full business days before a public meeting. Requires boards to provide notice to persons who have requested notification of meetings at the time the board packet is made available for public inspection. (CD1)
