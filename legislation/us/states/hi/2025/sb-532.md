# Hawaii SB 532 (2025) — RELATING TO THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION.

Authorizes school staff and agents trained by a health care professional to volunteer to administer medication to students at Department of Education public schools under certain conditions. Clarifies who may prescribe medication to be administered to students in schools. (CD1)
