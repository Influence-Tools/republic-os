# Hawaii SB 3191 (2024) — RELATING TO TECHNOLOGY TRANSFER AT THE UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII.

Permanently authorizes the University of Hawaiʻi to continue to regulate conflicts of interest and other ethical issues for technology transfer activities sponsored by the University of Hawaiʻi. Takes effect 6/29/2024. (CD1)
