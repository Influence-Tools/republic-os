# Hawaii SB 2845 (2024) — RELATING TO FIREARMS.

Prohibits a person from selling ammunition to a person under the age of twenty-one. Prohibits a person under the age of twenty-one from owning, possessing, or controlling ammunition, with exceptions. (CD1)
