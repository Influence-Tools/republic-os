# Hawaii SB 3365 (2024) — RELATING TO PLANT CARE COMPONENTS.

Requires the Department of Agriculture to certify treatments performed on wood chips, compost, and filter socks. Places certain restrictions on the distribution or transportation of plant care components without prior treatment certification from the Department. (CD1)
