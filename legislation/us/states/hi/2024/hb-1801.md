# Hawaii HB 1801 (2024) — RELATING TO STATE BONDS.

Authorizes issuance of general obligation bonds. Makes findings required by Article VII, Section 13, of the Hawaiʻi State Constitution to declare that the issuance of authorized bonds will not cause the debt limit to be exceeded. (CD1)
