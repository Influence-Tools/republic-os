# Hawaii SB 2476 (2024) — RELATING TO DENTAL HYGIENISTS.

Authorizes licensed dental hygienists to perform preventive dental sealant screenings and apply dental sealants on individuals, under certain conditions, in a school-based oral health program. (CD1)
