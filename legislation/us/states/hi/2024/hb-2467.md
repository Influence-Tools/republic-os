# Hawaii HB 2467 (2024) — RELATING TO RENT CREDITS FOR DEMOLITION AND INFRASTRUCTURE COSTS ON PUBLIC LAND LEASES.

Repeals the sunset provision of Act 222, Session Laws of Hawaiʻi 2021, to permanently allow the Board of Land and Natural Resources to approve rent credits for demolition and infrastructure costs for public land leases. (CD1)
