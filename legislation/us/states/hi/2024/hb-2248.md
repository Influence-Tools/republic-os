# Hawaii HB 2248 (2024) — RELATING TO BEACH MANAGEMENT ON THE NORTH SHORE OF OAHU.

Appropriates funds to the University of Hawaiʻi Sea Grant College Program to develop a North Shore Beach Management Plan for the North Shore of Oahu from Sunset Point to the Kapoʻo (Sharks Cove) area. Appropriates funds. (CD1)
