# Hawaii SB 2413 (2024) — RELATING TO AGRICULTURE.

Requires the Board of Agriculture to submit a report to the Legislature on the percentage of agricultural lands that are suitable for farming and actively farmed and certain dollar amounts relating to lease transfers between lessees. (CD1)
