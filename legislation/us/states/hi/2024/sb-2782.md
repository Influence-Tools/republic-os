# Hawaii SB 2782 (2024) — RELATED TO ELECTRONIC INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY.

Requires the Office of Enterprise Technology Services to develop multilingual accessibility standards. Appropriates funds to establish one full-time equivalent (1.0 FTE) position to develop multilingual accessibility standards within the Office of Enterprise Technology Services. (CD1)
