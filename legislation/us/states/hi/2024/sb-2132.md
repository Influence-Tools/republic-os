# Hawaii SB 2132 (2024) — RELATING TO THE RESIDENTIAL LANDLORD-TENANT CODE.

Amends the deadline related to the repair of conditions that constitute health or safety violations. Increases the amount deducted from a tenant's rent for the tenant's actual expenditures to correct health or safety violations and defective conditions. Takes effect 11/1/2024. (CD1)
