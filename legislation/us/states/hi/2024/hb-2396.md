# Hawaii HB 2396 (2024) — RELATING TO THE HAWAII NATIONAL GUARD.

Authorizes the payment of allowances for TRICARE Reserve Select coverages to junior officers, warrant officers, or enlisted personnel of the Hawaiʻi National Guard ordered to active duty for more than thirty days in the service of the State. (SD2)
