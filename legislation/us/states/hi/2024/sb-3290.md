# Hawaii SB 3290 (2024) — RELATING TO AMERICAN SIGN LANGUAGE.

Requires the Disability and Communication Access Board to convene a working group to study the state of American Sign Language interpretation services in Hawaiʻi. Requires a report to the Legislature, with the assistance of the Department of Health. (CD1)
