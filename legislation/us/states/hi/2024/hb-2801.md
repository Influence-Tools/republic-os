# Hawaii HB 2801 (2024) — RELATING TO COMMERCIAL PROPERTY ASSESSED FINANCING.

Repeals the authority of counties to administer commercial property assessed financing programs and delegates all existing administrative responsibilities of the counties under the commercial property assessed financing program to the Hawaiʻi Green Infrastructure Authority. Allows condominium associations to be eligible for commercial property assessed financing. (CD1)
