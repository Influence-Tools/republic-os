# Hawaii SB 2217 (2024) — RELATING TO REPORTING PERIODS.

Establishes a phased process for adjusting the reporting period for gift disclosures filed with the State Ethics Commission to conform with the fiscal year. Provides for electronic filing of documents required by the State Ethics Commission. (CD1)
