# Hawaii SB 2943 (2024) — RELATING TO WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT.

Establishes a commercial drivers workforce working group within the Department of Transportation to address the workforce shortage of commercial drivers in the State. Requires a report to the Legislature. (CD1)
