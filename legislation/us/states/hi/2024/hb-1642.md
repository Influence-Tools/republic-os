# Hawaii HB 1642 (2024) — RELATING TO GENERAL EMPLOYEE ORIENTATION.

Allows an employee or the exclusive representative, if applicable, to file a complaint with the Hawaiʻi Labor Relations Board if the employer fails to comply with section 78-64, Hawaiʻi Revised Statutes, relating to general employee orientation. (SD1)
