# Hawaii HB 2395 (2024) — RELATING TO THE HAWAII NATIONAL GUARD.

Makes Hawaiʻi National Guard service members activated by the Governor or the Governor's designated representative to state active duty ineligible for enrollment in the Employees' Retirement System based solely on the active duty service. (SD2)
