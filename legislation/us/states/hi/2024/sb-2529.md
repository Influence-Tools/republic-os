# Hawaii SB 2529 (2024) — RELATING TO PROVIDER ORDERS FOR LIFE SUSTAINING TREATMENT FORM.

Amends the definition of "patient's provider" in State law governing Provider Orders for Life-Sustaining Treatment by removing language that requires the provider to examine the patient. (CD1)
