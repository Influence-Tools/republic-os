# Hawaii HB 794 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO ENERGY AT THE UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII.

Establishes the University of Hawaii (UH) Green Special Fund to support energy efficiency, renewable energy, and sustainability projects and services, and planning, design, and implementation of sustainability projects for UH's benefit. Requires UH to submit an annual report to the Legislature. (HB794 CD1)
