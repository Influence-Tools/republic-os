# Hawaii HB 374 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO DENTAL ASSISTANTS.

Requires the auditor to conduct a study on the effects of regulation of dental assistants in the State via the certification requirements for dental assistants offered by Kapiolani community college. (SD2)
