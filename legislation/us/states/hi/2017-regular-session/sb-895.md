# Hawaii SB 895 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CRIMINAL TRESPASS.

Establishes the offense of criminal trespass onto state lands to the penal code. Amends the offense of criminal trespass in the second degree to apply to government agricultural property regardless of whether it is fenced, enclosed, or otherwise secured. (CD1)
