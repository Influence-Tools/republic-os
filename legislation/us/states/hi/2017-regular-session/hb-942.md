# Hawaii HB 942 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO FILIPINO VETERANS.

Authorizes the State Foundation on Culture and the Arts to commission an artist to design and build a monument to honor and commemorate Filipino veterans of World War II, with all costs to be expended from the Works of Art Special Fund. (HB942 CD1)
