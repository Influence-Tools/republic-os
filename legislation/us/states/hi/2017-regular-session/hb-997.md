# Hawaii HB 997 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CONCESSIONS.

Clarifies that the grant of concession or concession space shall not exceed fifteen years of a remaining term plus any agreed extension of the term. Removes the limitations on the types of airport concessions that are exempt from public bidding requirements. (HB997 CD1)
