# Hawaii SB 584 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO MORTGAGES.

Allows an extended or hanai family member to act as a co-mortgagor in assisting a qualified resident in securing a mortgage to purchase a dwelling unit from the Hawaii Housing Finance and Development Corporation. (CD1)
