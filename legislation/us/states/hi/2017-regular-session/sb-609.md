# Hawaii SB 609 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO ATTESTATION ENGAGEMENTS.

Requires that peer reviews performed on public accounting firms are performed in accordance with the entirety of the Statements on Standards for Attestation Engagements adopted by the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants. (CD1)
