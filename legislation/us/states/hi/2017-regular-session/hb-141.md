# Hawaii HB 141 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO NON-GENERAL FUNDS.

Repeals and abolishes various non-general funds of GOV, OHA, and DOE, as recommended by the Auditor in Auditor's Report Nos. 16-09, 16-10, and 16-11, and transfers unencumbered balances. (HB141 HD1)
