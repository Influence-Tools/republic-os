# Hawaii HB 599 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE HAWAII STATE PLAN.

Amends Hawaii State Planning Act to prioritize housing opportunities for extremely low- to above moderate- income households, require periodic updates to functional plans, and amend member nomination process for advisory committees for the functional plans. (SD2)
