# Hawaii SB 885 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE STATE RISK MANAGEMENT AND INSURANCE ADMINISTRATION.

Permits the Comptroller to acquire risk management, investigative, claims adjustment, actuarial, and other services on a fixed fee basis. Exempts a broker submitting a proposal in response to a fixed fee solicitation by the Comptroller and the broker's performance of the activities in accordance with the proposal from certain provisions of the Insurance Code. (CD1)
