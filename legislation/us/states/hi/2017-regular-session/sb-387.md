# Hawaii SB 387 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO HEALTH INSURANCE.

Requires a health carrier with a network plan to maintain a network that includes sufficient numbers of appropriate types of providers to ensure that covered persons have access to covered services. Specifies contract, disclosure, continuity of care, and directory publication requirements. (CD1)
