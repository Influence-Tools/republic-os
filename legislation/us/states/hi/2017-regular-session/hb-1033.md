# Hawaii HB 1033 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURE.

Allows state and county agencies to participate in administrative procedures under chapter 91, Hawaii Revised Statutes, by including "agencies" within the definition of "persons" in section 91-1, Hawaii Revised Statutes. (HB1033 CD1)
