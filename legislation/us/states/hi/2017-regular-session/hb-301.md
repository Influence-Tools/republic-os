# Hawaii HB 301 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO RESPONSE TO WRIT FOR CERTIORARI.

Requires the clerk of the court to grant a single extension of not more than fifteen days to file with the Supreme Court a response to an application for writ of certiorari upon a party's timely written request. (HB301 CD1)
