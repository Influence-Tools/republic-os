# Hawaii SB 396 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO SECURITY INTERESTS IN REAL PROPERTY.

Clarifies public records by amending the procedure for clearing titles by adding a reference to recorded assignments of leases and rents for which the underlying debt has been fully paid or satisfied. (CD1)
