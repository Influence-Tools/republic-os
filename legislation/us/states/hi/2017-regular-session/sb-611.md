# Hawaii SB 611 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO FIRE SPRINKLERS.

Extends the prohibition on county requirements for automatic sprinklers or sprinkler systems in one- and two-family detached dwelling units and non-residential agricultural and aquacultural structures located outside urban areas. (CD1)
