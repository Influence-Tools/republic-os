# Hawaii SB 119 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO PAYMENT OF RENT.

Establishes a cap of eight per cent on late rent payment fees, applicable to all new rental agreements and rental agreement renewals entered into on or after the effective date of this measure. Effective 11/1/2017. (CD1)
