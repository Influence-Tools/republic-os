# Hawaii HB 880 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION.

Establishes a Mark Licensing Agreement Program within the Department of Education and authorizes the Department of Education to register trademarks and service marks for department programs and individual department schools and enter into licensing agreements for their use. (HB880 CD1)
