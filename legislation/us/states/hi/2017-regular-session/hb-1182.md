# Hawaii HB 1182 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE EMPLOYEES' RETIREMENT SYSTEM.

Requires the ERS actuary to conduct annual stress tests of the system. Directs the ERS board to submit annual reports of the tests to the legislature. Takes effect 7/1/2017. (SD2)
