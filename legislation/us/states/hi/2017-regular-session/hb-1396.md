# Hawaii HB 1396 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO COMMUNITY CARE FOSTER FAMILY HOMES.

Authorizes the Department of Health, working in consultation with the Department of Human Services, to allow two private-pay individuals to be cared for in the same community care foster family home after consideration of specified relevant factors. (HB1396 CD1)
