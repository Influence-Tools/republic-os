# Hawaii SB 1006 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE ESTATE AND GENERATION-SKIPPING TRANSFER TAXES.

Clarifies the situs of property where the property is held by the decedent through a single member limited liability company that has not elected to be taxed as a corporation. (CD1)
