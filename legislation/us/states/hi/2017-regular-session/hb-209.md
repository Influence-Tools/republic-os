# Hawaii HB 209 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO TAXATION.

Establishes a state earned income tax credit. Changes income tax rates after 12/31/17. Repeals the sunset date for amendments made to the refundable food/excise tax credit by Act 223, SLH 2015. (HB209 CD1)
