# Hawaii HB 1231 (2017 Regular Session) — RELATING TO DISASTER RELIEF.

Specifies instances in which the Department of Education may retain disaster relief funds. Clarifies that the Department of Education may carry over funds received as disaster relief appropriation or reimbursement. (HB1231 CD1)
