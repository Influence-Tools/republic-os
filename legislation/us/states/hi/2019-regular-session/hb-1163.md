# Hawaii HB 1163 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO SAVINGS PROMOTION CONTESTS.

Authorizes a financial institution that is a depository institution to conduct savings promotion or prizelinked savings contests in which its account holders are contestants. (HB1163 CD1)
