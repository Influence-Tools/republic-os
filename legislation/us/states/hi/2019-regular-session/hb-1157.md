# Hawaii HB 1157 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO POST-SECONDARY EDUCATION STUDENT IMMUNIZATIONS.

Exempts students in post-secondary education programs from immunization requirements if they attend classes exclusively online or through distance learning, under certain conditions. (HB1157 CD1)
