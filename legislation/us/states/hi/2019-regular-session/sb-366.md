# Hawaii SB 366 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO HEALTH.

Appropriates funds to the Executive Office on Aging to fund the existing position of Alzheimer's Disease and Related Dementia Services Coordinator. Exempts the position from civil service and collective bargaining. (SB366 HD2)
