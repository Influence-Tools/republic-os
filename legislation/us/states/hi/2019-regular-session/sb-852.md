# Hawaii SB 852 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO RESTITUTION FOR VICTIMS OF CRIME.

Clarifies that lost earnings, mental health treatment, counseling, and therapy are eligible for reimbursement to a crime victim as a result of a defendant's offense. (CD1)
