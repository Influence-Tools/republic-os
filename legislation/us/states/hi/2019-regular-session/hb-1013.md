# Hawaii HB 1013 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO INVOLUNTARY HOSPITALIZATION.

Establishes an involuntary hospitalization task force to examine existing law and make recommendations to the legislature to reduce unnecessary emergency department admissions and improve access for MH-1 patients to the most appropriate level of care. (SD2)
