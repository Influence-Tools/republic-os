# Hawaii SB 812 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS.

Establishes the operating budget for certain programs of the Department of Commerce and Consumer Affairs. Establishes or amends certain special fund ceilings and provides for suspension or refund of fees if the ceiling is exceeded. (SB812 HD2)
