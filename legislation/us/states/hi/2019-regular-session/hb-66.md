# Hawaii HB 66 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO ATHLETE AGENTS.

Repeals the existing Uniform Athlete Agents Act and replaces it with the Revised Uniform Athlete Agents Act, which applies to certain financial advisers and makes other changes to the Uniform Act to increase effectiveness and enforceability. (HB66 CD1)
