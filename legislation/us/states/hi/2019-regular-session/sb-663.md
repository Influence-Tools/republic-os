# Hawaii SB 663 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO HIGHWAY SAFETY.

Establishes within the DOT a red light running committee to develop policy for pilot programs in the C&C of Honolulu, and Counties of Maui, Kauai, and Hawaii. (CD1)
