# Hawaii HB 845 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO EMPLOYEE TRAINING.

Requires all new employees within 20 days of being hired to be provided a general orientation on their benefits and rights by the employee's respective jurisdiction. (HB845 CD1)
