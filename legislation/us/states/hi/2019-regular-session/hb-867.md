# Hawaii HB 867 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN RESOURCES DEVELOPMENT.

Establishes the Administrative Assistant position exempt from Chapter 76, HRS, within the Department of Human Resources Development. Appropriates funds to DHRD for HRD102 expenditures – workforce attraction, selection, classification, and effectiveness. (HB867 CD1)
