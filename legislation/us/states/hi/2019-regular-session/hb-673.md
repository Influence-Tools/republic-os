# Hawaii HB 673 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO MEDICAL CANNABIS.

Provides a process for the voluntary or involuntary sale or transfer of an individual dispensary license. Eases restrictions on allowable operating hours and location of licensed facilities. Expands allowable interisland transportation of medical cannabis products for laboratory testing. (HB673 CD1)
