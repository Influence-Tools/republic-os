# Hawaii SB 770 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO REAL ESTATE LICENSES.

Requires a high school education or its equivalent as a condition for new applicants to obtain a license as a real estate broker or real estate salesperson. Effective 7/1/2020. (CD1)
