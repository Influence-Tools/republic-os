# Hawaii SB 330 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO AN EARNED INCOME DISREGARD PROGRAM.

Requires the Department of Human Services to implement an earned income disregard program as an intermediate step to implementing a medicaid buy-in program. Requires reports to the legislature. Takes effect upon approval by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services. (CD1)
