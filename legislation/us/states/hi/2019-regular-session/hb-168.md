# Hawaii HB 168 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE ELECTRONIC TRANSMISSION OF BALLOTS.

Authorizes a voter with special needs, including a disability, to request that a ballot be forwarded by electronic transmission at any time. (SD1)
