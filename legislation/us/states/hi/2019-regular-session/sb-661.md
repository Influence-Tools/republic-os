# Hawaii SB 661 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO FUEL CELL ELECTRIC VEHICLES.

Grants procurement priority for fuel cell electric vehicles for state and county vehicle purchases. Includes fuel cell electric vehicles in the definition of "electric vehicles" for purposes of parking fee exemption, high occupancy vehicle lane use, registration, and required parking spaces in places of public accommodation. (CD1)
