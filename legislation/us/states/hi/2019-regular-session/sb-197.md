# Hawaii SB 197 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CAMPAIGN FINANCE.

Exempts candidates who are deemed elected, running unopposed, or unsuccessful after a primary or special primary election from the prohibition on donating excess campaign funds for charitable or scholarship purposes before the general election date. (SB197 HD1)
