# Hawaii HB 420 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO HAWAIIAN CULTURE.

Removes a provision from the Transient Accommodations Tax law designating the Hawaii Convention Center as the location for operation of a Hawaiian Center and the Museum of Hawaiian Music and Dance. (HB420 CD1)
