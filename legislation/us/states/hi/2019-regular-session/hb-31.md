# Hawaii HB 31 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO ELECTION NOMINATION PAPERS.

Amends the voter information requirements for election nomination papers and the withdrawal of a nominating voter's signature by requiring only the month and date portions of a voter's date of birth. Repeals the social security number requirements. (HB31 HD1)
