# Hawaii SB 1237 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO HEALTH.

Requires health insurance providers that provide medicare advantage (medicare part C) health benefit plans to submit administrative data, including health care services claims and payment data, to the State Health Planning and Development Agency, or its designee. (CD1)
