# Hawaii SB 471 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO HOMELESSNESS.

Appropriates funds for core homelessness services, including the outreach program, rapid re-housing program, housing first program, family assessment centers, stored property and debris removal services, and the state rent supplemental program. (CD1)
