# Hawaii HB 1068 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO HEEIA STATE PARK.

Makes an appropriation for a Heeia State Park community-based long-range plan for the Heeia National Estuarine Research Reserve System. Requires the Hawaii Community Development Authority to consult with various entities in developing the plan. (HB1068 CD1)
