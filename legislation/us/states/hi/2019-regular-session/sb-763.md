# Hawaii SB 763 (2019 Regular Session) — RELATING TO ADVERTISING AND MARKETING.

Requires the Department of Business, Economic Development, and Tourism, in consultation with the Department of Agriculture, to conduct a study to assess the impact of companies selling products in the United States using place-based marketing without any material ties to the State. Appropriates funds for the study. (CD1)
