# Hawaii SB 2582 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO EXTENDED TERMS OF IMPRISONMENT.

Authorizes the courts to impose an extended term of imprisonment for an offender who is convicted of negligent homicide in the first degree and did not remain at the scene of the crime and render reasonable assistance to an injured person. (CD1)
