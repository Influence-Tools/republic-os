# Hawaii SB 2803 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO BOILER AND ELEVATOR SAFETY LAW.

Makes housekeeping amendments to the Boiler and Elevator Safety Law. Changes the name of the Boiler and Elevator Special Fund to the Boiler and Elevator Revolving Fund. Extends the time from five to ten years for the Director of Labor and Industrial Relations to reimburse the general fund from the Boiler and Elevator Revolving Fund. (CD1)
