# Hawaii HB 2396 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO TAX ADMINISTRATION.

Increases the limit on revenues collected by the Special Enforcement Section of the Department of Taxation that are deposited into the Tax Administration Special Fund. Authorizes the use of Tax Administration Special Fund moneys to fund support staff positions in the Special Enforcement Section. Authorizes the Department of Taxation to establish 5 full-time equivalent (5.0 FTE) positions within the Special Enforcement Section. (SD1)
