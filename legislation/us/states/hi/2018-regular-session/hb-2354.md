# Hawaii HB 2354 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE INCOME CHECK-OFF.

Changes from $2 to $5 of authorized income tax check-off that may be designated payable from an individual's state income tax refund to the libraries special fund. Changes from $4 to $10 permitted to joint filers in the designation of check-off to the libraries special fund. (HB2354 CD1)
