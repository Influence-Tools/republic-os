# Hawaii SB 2691 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO BOARD MEETINGS.

Allows boards, under Hawaii' Sunshine Law, to provide a copy of the public notice of a meeting to the Office of the Lieutenant Governor or appropriate county clerk's office via electronic mail. (CD1)
