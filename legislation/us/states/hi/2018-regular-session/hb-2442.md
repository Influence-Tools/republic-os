# Hawaii HB 2442 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO ABANDONED VEHICLES.

Requires the counties to take into custody any abandoned vehicle on a public road within ten business days. Amends the conditions that constitute a derelict vehicle. Allows, rather than requires, that abandoned vehicles be disposed of by public auction. (HB2442 CD1)
