# Hawaii SB 2174 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO MATERIAL WITNESS ORDERS.

Authorizes specified courts to issue material witness orders in cases initiated through felony complaint. Includes the family courts in the authorization to issue material witness orders. (CD1)
