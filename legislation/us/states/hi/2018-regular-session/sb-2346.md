# Hawaii SB 2346 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO ADDRESS CONFIDENTIALITY.

Establishes the Address Confidentiality Program in the Department of the Attorney General to help survivors of domestic abuse, sexual assault, and stalking to relocate and keep the location of their physical address confidential. (CD1)
