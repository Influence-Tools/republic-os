# Hawaii SB 2719 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO SEXUAL VIOLENCE.

Extends the period during which a victim of child sexual abuse may bring an otherwise time-barred civil action against the victim's abuser or an entity with a duty of care. Applies retroactively to April 24, 2012. (CD1)
