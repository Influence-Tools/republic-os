# Hawaii SB 2581 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE KING KAMEHAMEHA CELEBRATION COMMISSION.

Increases the number of members on the King Kamehameha celebration commission from thirteen to fifteen. Establishes an executive director to be appointed by the commission. (CD1)
