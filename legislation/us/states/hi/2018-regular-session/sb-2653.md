# Hawaii SB 2653 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CHAPTER 453, HAWAII REVISED STATUTES.

Establishes requirements for licensure by endorsement for physicians, surgeons, and osteopathic physicians beginning on 12/31/2018. Appropriates moneys. (CD1)
