# Hawaii HB 2161 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO VEHICLE INSPECTION STATIONS.

Changes the penalty for the first three instances of improper conduct of motor vehicle inspections at inspection stations from revocation or suspension of the station's operating permit to an administrative fine. (HB2161 CD1)
