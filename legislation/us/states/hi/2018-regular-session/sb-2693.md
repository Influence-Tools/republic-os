# Hawaii SB 2693 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE FESTIVAL OF PACIFIC ARTS.

Makes an appropriation to the temporary commission on the thirteenth Festival of Pacific Arts to plan for the Festival of Pacific Arts to be held in Honolulu from 6/11 - 6/27/2020. Effective 7/1/2018. (CD1)
