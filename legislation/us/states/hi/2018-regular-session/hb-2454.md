# Hawaii HB 2454 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO HAWAII CORRECTIONAL INDUSTRIES.

Establishes a three-year Correctional Industries Former Inmate Employment Pilot Project within the Department of Public Safety to assist former inmates as they reenter society. (HB2454 CD1)
