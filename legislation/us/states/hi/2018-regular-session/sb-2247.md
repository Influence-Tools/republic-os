# Hawaii SB 2247 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO OPIOID ANTAGONISTS.

Authorizes pharmacists to prescribe, dispense, and provide related education on opioid antagonists to individuals at risk of opioid overdose and to family members and caregivers of individuals at risk of opioid overdose without the need for a written, approved collaborative agreement; subject to certain conditions. (CD1)
