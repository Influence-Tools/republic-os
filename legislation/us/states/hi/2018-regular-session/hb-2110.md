# Hawaii HB 2110 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO RESILIENCY.

Directs the Public Utilities Commission to establish a microgrid services tariff to encourage and facilitate the development and use of energy resilient microgrids. Takes effect on 7/1/2018. (SD2)
