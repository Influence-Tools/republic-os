# Hawaii HB 1602 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO OPIOIDS.

Requires the inclusion of a label warning of the risks of addiction and death on the packaging of any opioid drug dispensed by a health care professional or pharmacist. (HB1602 CD1)
