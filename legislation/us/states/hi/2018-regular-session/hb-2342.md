# Hawaii HB 2342 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CONSUMER CREDIT REPORTING AGENCIES.

Expands the methods by which a consumer may request a security freeze. Specifies that a consumer credit reporting agency shall not charge a fee for placing, lifting, or removing a security freeze on a consumer's credit report or for placing or removing a security freeze on a protected consumer's credit report or records. (SD1)
