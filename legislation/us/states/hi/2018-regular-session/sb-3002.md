# Hawaii SB 3002 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO LIBRARIES.

Authorizes the State Librarian to designate one or more public library employees to be notaries public. Allows designated employees to perform as notaries public in government service for fees that will be deposited into the Library Fee for Enhanced Services Special Fund. (CD1)
