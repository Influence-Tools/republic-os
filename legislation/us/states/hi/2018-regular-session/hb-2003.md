# Hawaii HB 2003 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO DRIVING WHILE INTOXICATED.

Allows requests for permits that authorize the respondent or defendant to operate an employer's vehicle during the period of license revocation to be supported by the employer's sworn statement attesting to the period of the specified assigned hours of work that the defendant will drive the vehicle for purposes of employment. (HB2003 CD1)
