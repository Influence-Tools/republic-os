# Hawaii SB 2909 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO LAW ENFORCEMENT.

Requires the Legislative Reference Bureau to conduct a study that examines consolidating the law enforcement activities and responsibilities of various state divisions and agencies under a single, centralized state enforcement division or agency. (CD1)
