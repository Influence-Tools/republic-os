# Hawaii SB 2013 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CIVIL IDENTIFICATION.

Requires the Director of Transportation to adopt rules to allow an individual who has a letter from a licensed primary care provider certifying that a severe disability causes the individual to be homebound to renew a State identification card by means other than an in person appearance. (CD1)
