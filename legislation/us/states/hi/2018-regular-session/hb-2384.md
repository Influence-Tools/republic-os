# Hawaii HB 2384 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE UNIFORM CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES ACT.

Updates Uniform Controlled Substances Act for consistency with federal law. Allows prescription of drugs to patients undergoing medically managed withdrawal, also known as detoxification treatment and maintenance treatment, by practitioners who are properly registered. (SD1)
