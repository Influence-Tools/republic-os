# Hawaii SB 2861 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO PUBLIC SAFETY.

Requires the Department of Public Safety to establish performance indicators. Requires annual reports of the established performance indicators, criminal offender treatment programs, and programs established pursuant to the Community Safety Act. (CD1)
