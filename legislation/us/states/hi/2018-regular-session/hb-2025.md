# Hawaii HB 2025 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO RESOURCE RECOVERY.

Authorizes the Department of Education to issue grants to establish a composting grant pilot project in Department of Education schools. Authorizes a composting grant pilot project working group. Requires report to the Legislature. Appropriates funds. (HB2025 CD1)
