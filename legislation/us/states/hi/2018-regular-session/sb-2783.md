# Hawaii SB 2783 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE HAWAII PUBLIC HOUSING AUTHORITY.

Prohibits smoking in and increases the smoke-free zone around certain areas and buildings in public housing projects, state low-income housing projects, and elder or elderly housing from 20 to 25 feet. (CD1)
