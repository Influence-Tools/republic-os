# Hawaii HB 2416 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO TAX ON INTANGIBLE PROPERTY.

Clarifies that intangible property shall be exempt from the general excise tax used outside the State. Subjects intangible property acquired from an unlicensed seller and used in the State to the use tax at 4 percent of the value of the property. (HB2416 CD1)
