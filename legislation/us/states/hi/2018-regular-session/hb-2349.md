# Hawaii HB 2349 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CITATIONS FOR MOTOR VEHICLE REPAIR VIOLATIONS.

Authorizes the issuance of a citation for specific violations of the motor vehicle repair laws and rules by licensees. Sets forth a process for licensees to contest the citation and provides for the assessment of fines for each violation. (SD2)
