# Hawaii HB 2305 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO COFFEE BERRY BORER.

Extends the pesticide subsidy program manager position through the fiscal year immediately following the program sunset date. Extends the position's civil service and collective bargaining laws exemption until the expiration of the position. (HB2305 CD1)
