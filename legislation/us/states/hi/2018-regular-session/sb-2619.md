# Hawaii SB 2619 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO AERONAUTICS.

Replaces criminal penalties for certain airport offenses addressed in the State's aeronautics laws or in certain administrative rules or orders issued pursuant thereto with fines. (CD1)
