# Hawaii HB 1934 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION.

Prohibits the director of health from issuing permits for the construction of sewage wastewater injection wells unless alternative wastewater disposal options are not available, feasible, or practical. (SD2)
