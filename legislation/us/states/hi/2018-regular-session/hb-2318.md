# Hawaii HB 2318 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CONFIDENTIAL PERSONAL INFORMATION.

Specifies that certain government records maintained by the Department of Hawaiian Home Lands are not required to be publicly disclosed or made open to inspection pursuant to public records requests and may be discussed during an executive meeting of the Hawaiian Homes Commission. (HB2318 CD1)
