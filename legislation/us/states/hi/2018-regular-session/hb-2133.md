# Hawaii HB 2133 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO POLICE DEPARTMENTS.

Specifies that citizen administrative complaints against a police officer that involve allegations of domestic abuse by the police officer against a family or household member shall not be required to be filed as a notarized or sworn written statement. (HB2133 CD1)
