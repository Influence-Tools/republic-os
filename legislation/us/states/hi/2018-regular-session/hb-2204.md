# Hawaii HB 2204 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO HUMAN SERVICES.

Requires the Department of Human Services to reestablish the Exit and Retention Bonus Program to encourage welfare recipients to transition back into the workforce. Appropriates funds. (HB2204 CD1)
