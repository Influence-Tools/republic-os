# Hawaii SB 3077 (2018 Regular Session) — RELATING TO BIOFUELS.

Expands the renewable fuel tax credit cap by lowering the production threshold and expanding the types of renewable fuel eligible for the credit. Applies to taxable years after 12/31/2017. (CD1)
