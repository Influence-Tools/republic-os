# Hawaii HB 141 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO FINANCIAL DISCLOSURES.

Beginning 1/1/2025, requires each state legislator to include within the legislator's disclosure of financial interests the names of certain lobbyists with whom the legislator has a relationship. Effective 1/1/2025. (SD1)
