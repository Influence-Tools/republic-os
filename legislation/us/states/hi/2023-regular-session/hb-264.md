# Hawaii HB 264 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CRIMES AGAINST SPORTS OFFICIALS.

Makes intentionally or knowingly causing bodily injury to a sports official a class C felony. Authorizes a court to enjoin the offender from attending any sports event of the type at which the sports official was engaged in the lawful discharge of the sports official's duties. (SD1)
