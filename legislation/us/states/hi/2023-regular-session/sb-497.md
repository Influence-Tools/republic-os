# Hawaii SB 497 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO COMMERCIAL VEHICLES.

Prohibits certain oversized commercial vehicles from driving in the far left lane on roadways with three or more lanes for traffic moving in the same direction, subject to certain exceptions. Effective 1/1/2024. (CD1)
