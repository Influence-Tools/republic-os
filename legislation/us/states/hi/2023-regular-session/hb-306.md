# Hawaii HB 306 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO ORNAMENTAL GINGER.

Appropriates funds to the University of Hawaii for statewide research into ornamental ginger pathogens, prevention of the spread of ornamental ginger pathogens, production and distribution of pathogen-free ornamental ginger plants, and outreach to ornamental ginger producers. (CD1)
