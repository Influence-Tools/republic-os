# Hawaii SB 182 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO DISCLOSURES OF FINANCIAL INTERESTS.

Amends the filing deadline for disclosures of financial interests by certain candidates. Amends the penalty for the unauthorized release of confidential financial disclosure information. Amends the fines imposed and procedures relating to disclosures of financial interests. (CD1)
