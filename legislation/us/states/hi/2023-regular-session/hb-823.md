# Hawaii HB 823 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO DEATHS WITHIN THE CORRECTIONAL SYSTEM.

Expands the scope of reports on correctional facility and community correctional center deaths that are submitted to the governor and legislature. Requires certain reports to be published on the department of corrections and rehabilitation website. Mandates public access to other information reported. (HD2)
