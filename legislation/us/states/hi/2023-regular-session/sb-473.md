# Hawaii SB 473 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO THE PRACTICE OF PHARMACY.

Exempts manufacturers, wholesale distributors, manufacturers engaged in direct distribution to qualified persons, and third-party logistics providers of home dialysate drugs or devices from the license, registration, and permit requirements for pharmacies, under certain conditions. (CD1)
