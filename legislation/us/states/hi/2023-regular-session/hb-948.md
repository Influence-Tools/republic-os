# Hawaii HB 948 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CHILD AND ADOLESCENT MENTAL HEALTH.

Establishes a two-year child and adolescent crisis mobile outreach team pilot program on Oahu and one neighbor island site to expand existing crisis response services. Appropriates funds. Sunsets 12/31/2025. (CD1)
