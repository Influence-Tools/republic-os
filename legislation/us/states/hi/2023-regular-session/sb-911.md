# Hawaii SB 911 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO JURORS.

Clarifies that a prospective juror is disqualified to serve as a juror if they have been convicted of a felony and have not been finally discharged, in addition to not being pardoned. Effective 1/1/2024. (HD1)
