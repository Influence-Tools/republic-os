# Hawaii SB 712 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CORRECTIONS.

Establishes a trauma-informed certification program at the Windward Community College campus of the University of Hawaii for adult corrections officers. Requires the curriculum to include a level I trauma-informed care course designed for adult corrections officers. (CD1)
