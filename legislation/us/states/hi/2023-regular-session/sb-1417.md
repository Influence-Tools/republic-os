# Hawaii SB 1417 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CLIMATE CHANGE.

Requires the Hawaii Community Development Authority to consider the impacts of climate change, sea level rise, and climate-resilient development in the design and siting of buildings in the Kakaako and Kalaeloa community development districts. (CD1)
