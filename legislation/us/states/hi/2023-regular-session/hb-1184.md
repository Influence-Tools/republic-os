# Hawaii HB 1184 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO PROCUREMENT.

Allows agencies to rank fewer than three persons for professional services when fewer than three qualified persons respond to the solicitation or request to use alternative procurement procedures when no qualified person responds to the solicitation. (CD1)
