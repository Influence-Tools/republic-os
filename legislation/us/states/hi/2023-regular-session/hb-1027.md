# Hawaii HB 1027 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO MONEY TRANSMITTERS MODERNIZATION ACT.

Enables timely, coordinated, and efficient regulation of money transmission companies to achieve financial stability and economic growth, while providing consumer protection. Allows the State to share resources, data, and technology tools with other states to create a stronger multi-state system of financial regulation. (CD1)
