# Hawaii HB 983 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO TIME LIMITATIONS.

Defines the term "public servant" in section 701-108, Hawaii Revised Statutes, to clarify the scope and applicability of provisions related to the time limitations to bring prosecution based on misconduct in office. (SD2)
