# Hawaii SB 318 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO FETAL ALCOHOL SPECTRUM DISORDERS.

Requires the Department of Health to establish and administer a three-year pilot program to implement a co-management system of care for the diagnosis and treatment of persons with fetal alcohol spectrum disorders. Appropriates funds for the pilot program and an annual public awareness campaign. (CD1)
