# Hawaii HB 1058 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO ADOPTION.

Requires an adopted individual to be considered a child of both the adopted and natural parents for the sole purpose of determining familial relationships for purposes of the Hawaiian Homes Commission Act. (SD1)
