# Hawaii HB 97 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO PRELIMINARY DETERMINATION OF PROBABLE CAUSE.

Requires the Campaign Spending Commission to serve preliminary determinations of probable cause via first-class mail. Establishes a presumption of receipt when mailed to the address contained in the candidate or committee organizational report. (SD1)
