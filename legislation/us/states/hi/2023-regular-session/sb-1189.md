# Hawaii SB 1189 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO CAMPAIGN FINANCE.

Requires candidates, or the treasurer of their candidate committee, to file a preliminary campaign finance report on February 28 of the year of a general election. Effective 1/1/2026. (CD1)
