# Hawaii HB 978 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO PURCHASES OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES.

Transfers certain duties from the administrator of the State Procurement Office to the heads of health and human services purchasing agencies. Changes the small purchase threshold for health and human services. (CD1)
