# Hawaii HB 24 (2023 Regular Session) — RELATING TO WATER COMMON CARRIERS.

Clarifies that a common water carrier may enter into vessel leases longer than five years or enter into leverage leases for vessels upon prior approval of the Public Utilities Commission. (CD1)
