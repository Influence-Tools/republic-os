# Hawaii SB 2193 (2020 Regular Session) — RELATING TO EMPLOYMENT DISCRIMINATION.

Limits the convictions that may be used in employment decisions, from all convictions in the most recent ten years, to felony convictions that occurred in the most recent seven years and misdemeanor convictions that occurred in the most recent five years. (HD2)
