# Hawaii HB 1698 (2020 Regular Session) — RELATING TO COLLECTIVE BARGAINING.

Amends bargaining unit (14). Retains state law enforcement officers under bargaining unit (14). Creates a separate bargaining unit (15) for state and county ocean safety and water safety officers. Takes effect upon approval. (SD1)
