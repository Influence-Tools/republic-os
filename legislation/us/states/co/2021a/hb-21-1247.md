# Colorado HB 21-1247 (2021A) — Colorado Department Of Public Health And Environment Contract Pay To Grantees Up Front

The act allows the department of public health and environment, in contracting with certain grantees for the provision of services, to dispense up to 25% of the total value of the payments under the contract to the grantee immediately upon the execution or renewal of the contract.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
