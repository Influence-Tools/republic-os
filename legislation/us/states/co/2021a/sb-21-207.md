# Colorado SB 21-207 (2021A) — Public School Capital Construction Assistance Fund Transfer

On June 1, 2022, the state treasurer is required to transfer $100 million from the marijuana tax cash fund to the public school capital construction assistance fund (BEST fund).(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
