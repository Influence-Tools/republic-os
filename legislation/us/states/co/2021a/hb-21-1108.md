# Colorado HB 21-1108 (2021A) — Gender Identity Expression Anti-discrimination

The act adds the terms "gender expression" and "gender identity" to statutes prohibiting discrimination against members of a protected class, including statutes prohibiting discriminatory practices in the following areas:Membership of the Colorado civil rights commission;
