# Colorado SB 21-117 (2021A) — Foster Care Student Services Coordination

The act amends provisions concerning students in out-of-home placement that mandate cooperation between schools and county departments of human or social services (county departments) relating to education. Specifically, the act:Amends the definition of "student in out-of-home placement" to align with those students in custody of county departments;
