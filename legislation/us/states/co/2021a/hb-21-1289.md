# Colorado HB 21-1289 (2021A) — Funding For Broadband Deployment

Section 1 of the act declares the general assembly's intent to spend a portion of the money received by the state under the federal "American Rescue Plan Act" for broadband infrastructure and telehealth capabilities.Sections 2 and 3 extend the grant award distribution and reporting dates for the connecting Colorado students grant program.Section 6 requires the office of information technology (office) to:Enter into an agreement with a third-party vendor to develop and implement a strategic plan to expand and improve digital access to government services through the use of broadband;
