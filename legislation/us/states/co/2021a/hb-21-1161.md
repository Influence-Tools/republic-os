# Colorado HB 21-1161 (2021A) — Suspend Statewide Assessments For Select Grades

The act suspends the administration of state assessments, contingent on receiving a waiver of federal law from the federal department of education, for the following instructional areas and grade levels for the 2020-21 school year:Science for students enrolled in grades 5, 8, and 11;
