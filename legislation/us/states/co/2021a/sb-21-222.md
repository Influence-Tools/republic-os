# Colorado SB 21-222 (2021A) — Repeal Recovery Audit Program

The act repeals the state recovery audit program, effective July 1, 2022, and reduces the state fiscal year 2021-22 general fund appropriation to the department of personnel for use by financial operations and reporting for personal services by $64,714 and the related FTE by 1.0 FTE.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
