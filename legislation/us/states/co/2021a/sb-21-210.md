# Colorado SB 21-210 (2021A) — Remote Supports For Elderly, Blind, And Disabled Waiver

Joint Budget Committee. The bill expands the definition of "electronic monitoring services" to include other remote supports as the definition relates to the home- and community-based services waiver program for the elderly, blind, and disabled.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
