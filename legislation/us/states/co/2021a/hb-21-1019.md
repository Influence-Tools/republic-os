# Colorado HB 21-1019 (2021A) — Modification To Regulations Of Factory-built Structures

The act makes the following modifications to the regulations of factory-built structures, manufactured housing, and installers and sellers of manufactured housing:Clarifies that the division of housing (division) has enforcement powers over the installation and sale of manufactured homes and over the safety of hotels and multi-family structures where no other construction standards exist;
