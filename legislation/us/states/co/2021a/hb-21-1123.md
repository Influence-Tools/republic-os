# Colorado HB 21-1123 (2021A) — CAPS Checks For Substantiated Mistreatment Of Adult

The act authorizes the department of human services (state department) to disclose the results of a CAPS check without a court order to:A health oversight agency within the department of regulatory agencies (DORA), or a regulator within such a health oversight agency, for the purpose of a regulatory investigation; or
