# Colorado HB 21-1054 (2021A) — Housing Public Benefit Verification Requirement

The act creates, unless otherwise required by federal law, a public or assisted housing benefit exception to the requirement that an applicant for federal, state, or local public benefits verify lawful presence in the United States.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
