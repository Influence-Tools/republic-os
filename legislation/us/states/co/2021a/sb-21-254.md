# Colorado SB 21-254 (2021A) — Eliminate Obsolete Committee Child Care Licensing

The act eliminates the advisory committee that advised the state department of human services on the licensing of child care facilities prior to the creation of the early childhood leadership commission.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
