# Colorado SB 21-082 (2021A) — Alcohol Beverage Festival For Tastings And Sales

The act authorizes the following alcohol beverage licensees to obtain a festival permit, which would allow the licensees to hold festivals where they may conduct joint tastings and engage in any retail operations authorized by their licenses or permits:A beer and wine licensee;
