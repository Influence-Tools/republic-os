# Colorado SB 21-098 (2021A) — Sunset Prescription Drug Monitoring Program

The act continues the prescription drug monitoring program (program) indefinitely.Additionally, the act:Authorizes the state board of pharmacy (board) to promulgate rules that identify a list of prescription drugs that are not currently listed as controlled substances and require such drugs to be tracked through the program;
