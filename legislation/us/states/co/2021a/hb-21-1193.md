# Colorado HB 21-1193 (2021A) — Consumer Protection Supplemental Restraint Systems

The act makes it a deceptive trade practice for a person to knowingly or intentionally manufacture, import, distribute, sell, offer for sale, install, or reinstall a device intended to replace a supplemental restraint system component if the device is:A counterfeit supplemental restraint system component;
