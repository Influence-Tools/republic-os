# Colorado SB 21-112 (2021A) — General Fund Transfer To Capital Construction Fund State Parks

The act transfers a total of $20 million from the general fund to the capital construction fund in the 2020-21 state fiscal year for single-phase projects at 12 state parks.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
