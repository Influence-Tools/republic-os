# Colorado HB 21-1229 (2021A) — Home Owners' Associations Governance Funding Record Keeping

The act increases requirements for disclosure and transparency in the operations of unit owners' associations (HOAs) in common interest communities, including requiring an HOA to maintain and keep available to unit owners, as part of its official records:A list of the HOA's current fees chargeable upon sale of a home in the community; and
