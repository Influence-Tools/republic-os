# Colorado HB 21-1083 (2021A) — State Board Assessment Appeals Valuation Adjustment

Under current law, when a property owner appeals the valuation of property set by a county board of equalization, the valuation may not be increased on appeal. The act removes this restriction.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
