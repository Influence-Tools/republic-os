# Colorado HB 21-1152 (2021A) — Repeal Obsolete Capitol Dome Restoration Fund

The act repeals a provision of law that creates the capitol dome restoration fund, which is obsolete. The capitol dome restoration was a capital project that commenced in 2010 and has since been completed. The statutory sections regarding the capitol dome restoration were repealed in July 2015 but the statute establishing the fund and the necessary transfers of money to the fund were inadvertently left in the statutes. The act addresses that defect.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
