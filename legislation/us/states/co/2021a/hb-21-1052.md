# Colorado HB 21-1052 (2021A) — Define Pumped Hydroelectricity As Renewable Energy

The act removes the existing restriction on pumped hydroelectric facilities as a source of recycled energy, which is included in the definition of an eligible energy resource under the renewable energy standard statute, and instead includes any pumped hydroelectric facility under 15 megawatts that:Does not combust fossil fuel to pump water;
