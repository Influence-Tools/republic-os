# Colorado HB 21-1278 (2021A) — Special District Meeting Requirements

The act clarifies what qualifies as a meeting and a location for purposes of the meeting of a board of a special district. The act also prohibits a challenge to the method of conducting any special district board meeting that was held virtually before the effective date of the act.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
