# Colorado SB 21-229 (2021A) — Rural Jump-start Zone Grant Program

The act creates the rural jump-start zone grant program (grant program) and authorizes the Colorado economic development commission (commission) to issue grants, subject to available appropriations, as follows:Up to $20,000 to new businesses to establish operations;
