# Colorado SB 21-046 (2021A) — Department of Law Supplemental

The 2020 general appropriation act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the department of law.Amends House Bill 20-1379, concerning suspending the direct distribution to the public employees' retirement association for the 2020-21 state fiscal year, to decrease the amount decreased for the PERA direct distribution.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
