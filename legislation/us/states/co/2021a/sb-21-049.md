# Colorado SB 21-049 (2021A) — Department of Public Safety Supplemental

The 2020 general appropriation act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the department of public safety. The general fund, cash funds, and reappropriated funds portions of the appropriation are increased.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
