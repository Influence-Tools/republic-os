# Colorado SB 21-198 (2021A) — Repeal Capital Construction Education Fund Report Requirement

The act repeals a requirement that the state auditor annually report uses of state education fund money for school capital construction to the education committees of the senate and the house of representatives, the legislative audit committee, and the joint budget committee of the general assembly.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
