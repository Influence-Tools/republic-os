# Colorado HB 21-1153 (2021A) — Enter Zone Child Care Income Tax Credit

The act repeals the enterprise zone child care contributions income tax credit that was available for income tax years commencing prior to January 1, 1999.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
