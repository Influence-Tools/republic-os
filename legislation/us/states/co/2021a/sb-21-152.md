# Colorado SB 21-152 (2021A) — Rule Review Bill

Based on the findings and recommendations of the committee on legal services, the act extends all state agency rules that were adopted or amended on or after November 1, 2019, and before November 1, 2020, with the exception of certain rules of the following agencies, as specifically listed in the act:The state board of education concerning administration of the public school transportation fund;
