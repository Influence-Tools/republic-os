# Colorado SB 21-018 (2021A) — Continuation Of Necessary Document Program

The act continues the necessary document program (program) indefinitely. Beginning January 1, 2032, and each 5 years thereafter, the department of public health and environment shall report to the general assembly the total number of necessary documents acquired on an annual basis and any significant technological changes or other developments that affect the need for, or operation of, the program.The act appropriates $250,000 to the department of public health and environment for use by the office of health equity to implement the act.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
