# Colorado HB 21-1212 (2021A) — Diversity Of Governor's Appointments To Boards

The act requires the governor to make reasonable efforts to appoint members of diverse groups to statewide boards, commissions, committees, and task forces authorized by the general assembly.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
