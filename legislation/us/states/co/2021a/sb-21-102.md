# Colorado SB 21-102 (2021A) — Sunset Dental Hygienists Specialized Functions

The act continues the ability of dental hygienists to place interim therapeutic restorations (ITR) and apply silver diamine fluoride (SDF) until September 1, 2025, to align with the sunset review of the Colorado dental board (board).The act also:Relocates the statutory language granting dental hygienists the authority to apply SDF;
