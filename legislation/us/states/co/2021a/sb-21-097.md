# Colorado SB 21-097 (2021A) — Sunset Continue Medical Transparency Act

The act implements the recommendation of the department of regulatory agencies' sunset review and report concerning the "Michael Skolnik Medical Transparency Act of 2010" to continue the act for 7 years, until 2028.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
