# Colorado SB 21-196 (2021A) — Fiscal Year 2021-22 Legislative Appropriation Bill

The act appropriates $54,276,399 to the legislative department for the payment of expenses in the 2021-22 state fiscal year. Additionally, the act appropriates $25,000 to the youth advisory council cash fund within the legislative department.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
