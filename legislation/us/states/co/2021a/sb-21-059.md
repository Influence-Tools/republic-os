# Colorado SB 21-059 (2021A) — Juvenile Justice Code Reorganization

The act makes conforming amendments and includes a cleanup of the main definition section for title 19, Colorado Revised Statutes, to reflect changes made through the reorganization of article 2 of title 19, Colorado Revised Statutes.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
