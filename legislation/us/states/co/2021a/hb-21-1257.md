# Colorado HB 21-1257 (2021A) — Recognition Of Veterans In Capitol Complex Parks

On and after May 31, 2021, the act changes the name of Lincoln park and Liberty park in the capitol complex to "Lincoln veterans' memorial park".The act makes an appropriation.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
