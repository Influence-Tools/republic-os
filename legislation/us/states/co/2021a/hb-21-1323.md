# Colorado HB 21-1323 (2021A) — Special Olympics License Plate

The act creates the Special Olympics Colorado license plate for motor vehicles. A person qualifies for issuance of the plate if the person makes a donation to a designated nonprofit organization. The designated nonprofit organization must:Be headquartered in Colorado;
