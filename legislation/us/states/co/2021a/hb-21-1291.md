# Colorado HB 21-1291 (2021A) — Insurer Agent Branded Vehicle Title

Under Colorado law, an insurer who has declared a vehicle a total loss may obtain a salvage or nonrepairable title for the vehicle when the owner has failed to cooperate. The act authorizes an agent of an insurer to do the same.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
