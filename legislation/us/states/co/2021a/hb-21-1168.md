# Colorado HB 21-1168 (2021A) — Historically Underutilized Businesses Local Government Procurement

The act requires the department of local affairs (department), no later than August 13, 2021, to establish a pilot program to help local governments identify perceptual and substantial barriers to entry for historically underutilized businesses in local government procurement.The act requires local governments participating in the pilot program to consider a number of items, such as:Identifying implementation needs, such as labor and technology, for historically underutilized businesses preference programs for local government procurement (programs);
