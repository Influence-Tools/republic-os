# Colorado SB 21-220 (2021A) — Reverse Transfers From Severance Tax Operational Fund

To avoid a fund deficit in the severance tax operational fund, the act reverses 5 transfers made from the fund to other cash funds after the fiscal year 2019-20. Specifically, the state treasurer is required to transfer the following amounts to the fund:$1,998,205 from the species conservation trust fund;
