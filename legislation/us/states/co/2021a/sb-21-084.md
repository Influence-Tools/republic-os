# Colorado SB 21-084 (2021A) — Local Government Authority Roughed-in Roads

The act authorizes local governments to prohibit the operation of motor vehicles or off-highway vehicles on roughed-in roads, which are areas where the ground has been cut with the intention to make a road but has not been improved enough to qualify as a road.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
