# Colorado SB 21-257 (2021A) — Special Mobile Machinery Registration Exemption

The act allows an owner of special mobile machinery who regularly rents or leases the special mobile machinery and who pays specific ownership tax on a monthly basis in an amount equal to 2% of the rental or lease payments for the special mobile machinery to apply to the department of revenue for a registration exempt certificate. The department shall issue the certificate if:The department verifies that the owner regularly has 1,000 or more items of such special mobile machinery in the state;
