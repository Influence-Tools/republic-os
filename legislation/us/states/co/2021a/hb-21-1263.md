# Colorado HB 21-1263 (2021A) — Meeting And Events Incentive Program

The act creates the Colorado meeting and events incentive program (program) in the Colorado tourism office (office) to provide rebates and direct support to eligible events in Colorado to assist in the state's recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic.An eligible event means an event, including a meeting, conference, or festival, that:Takes place in Colorado between July 1, 2021, and December 31, 2022;
