# Colorado SB 21-290 (2021A) — Security For Colorado Seniors

The act creates the area agency on aging grant program (grant program) in the department of human service's state office on aging (state office). The purpose of the grant program is to assist and support the health, well-being, and security of older Coloradans. The act also creates the area on aging cash fund (cash fund), which is used to fund the grant program.The act requires the state office and the area agency on aging to collaborate and establish criteria for the following:Adopting the policies and procedures for the administration of the grant program;
