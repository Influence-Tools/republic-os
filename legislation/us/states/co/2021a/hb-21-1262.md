# Colorado HB 21-1262 (2021A) — Money Support Agricultural Events Organization

The act creates the agricultural events relief program in the department of agriculture to provide COVID-19 relief payments to agricultural events organizations, and appropriates $2 million from the general fund for the program. In addition, the act appropriates:$5 million for the Colorado state fair and industrial exhibition;
