# Colorado SB 17-060 (2017A) — Colorado Student Leaders Institute Relocation To Colorado Department Of Higher Education.

The Colorado student leaders institute currently exists as a pilot program in the lieutenant governor's office. The bill relocates the institute to the department of higher education without change.
