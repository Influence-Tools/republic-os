# Colorado SB 17-298 (2017A) — Motor Vehicle Dealers And Manufacturers

Current law prohibits a motor vehicle manufacturer (manufacturer) from requiring a motor vehicle dealer (dealer) to substantially alter a facility or premises if the manufacturer required it within the last 7 years at a cost set in statute based on the type of dealer.
