# Colorado HB 17-1297 (2017A) — Special District Meeting Compensation

Current law caps the compensation that a special district board member may receive at $1,600 per year and $100 per meeting attended. The bill increases this amount to $2,400 per year and specifies that special meetings include study sessions in specified circumstances.
