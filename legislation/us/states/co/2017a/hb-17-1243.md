# Colorado HB 17-1243 (2017A) — Relocate Title 12 Wholesale Sales Representatives

Committee on Legal Services. Current law directs the Office of Legislative Legal Services to study the organizational recodification of title 12 of the Colorado Revised Statutes, which relates to professions and occupations. To implement the initial recommendations of the study, section 1 of the bill relocates article 66 of title 12, which relates to wholesale sales representatives, to title 13. Section 2 repeals the article where this law was previously codified.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
