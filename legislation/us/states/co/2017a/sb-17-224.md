# Colorado SB 17-224 (2017A) — Relocate Title 12 Driving Schools

Committee on Legal Services. Current law directs the office of legislative legal services to study the organizational recodification of title 12 of the Colorado Revised Statutes, which relates to professions and occupations. To implement the initial recommendations of the study, Section 1 of the bill relocates the statutes governing commercial driving schools to part 6 of article 2 of title 42. Sections 2 and 3 update the citations of other statutes to harmonize with the new locations. Section 4 repeals the moved statutes.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
