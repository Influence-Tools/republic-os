# Colorado HB 17-1295 (2017A) — Repeal Governor's Office Of Marijuana Coordination

Joint Budget Committee. The general assembly created the governor's office of marijuana coordination in 2014 to coordinate the executive branch response to the legalization of retail marijuana as directed by the governor. The bill repeals the office of marijuana coordination, effective July 1, 2017.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
