# Colorado SB 17-291 (2017A) — Sunset School Safety Resource Center Advisory Board

Sunset Process - Senate Education Committee. The bill implements the recommendations of the sunset review and report on the school safety resource center advisory board (board) by eliminating the repeal date of the board and extending the board through September 1, 2022.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
