# Colorado HB 17-1077 (2017A) — Useful Public Service Cash Fund

The bill creates the useful public service cash fund (fund) in the judicial branch to facilitate the administration of programs that supervise the performance of useful public service by persons who are required to perform such service pursuant to a criminal sentence.
