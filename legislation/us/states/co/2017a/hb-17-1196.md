# Colorado HB 17-1196 (2017A) — Credit Hours Required For Barbers Cosmetologists

The bill requires the director of the division of professions and occupations in the department of regulatory agencies to promulgate rules for applicants for cosmetologist or barber licensure to furnish proof of training, not to exceed 50 credits or 1,500 contact hours.
