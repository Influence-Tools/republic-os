# Colorado HB 17-1216 (2017A) — Sales And Use Tax Simplification Task Force

The bill creates the sales and use tax simplification task force (task force) made up of legislative members and state and local sales and use tax experts. The bill requires the task force to study sales and use tax simplification between the state and local governments, and in particular between the state and home rule jurisdictions.
