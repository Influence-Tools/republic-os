# Colorado SB 17-214 (2017A) — Voluntary Firefighter Cancer Benefits Program

The bill allows an employer to participate in a voluntary firefighter cancer benefits program, as a multiple employer health trust to provide benefits to firefighters by paying contributions into the established trust.
