# Colorado SB 17-125 (2017A) — Lump-sum Compensation For Exonerated Persons

Current law requires the state to compensate persons who were wrongly convicted of crimes and subsequently incarcerated. Such compensation is paid in annual payments of $100,000 until the state's duty of compensation is satisfied.
