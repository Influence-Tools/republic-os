# Colorado SB 25-178 (2025A) — Colorado K-5 Social & Emotional Health Pilot Program

The act requires the state board of education to adopt rules that consider a number of factors in determining the amount of money each school participating in the K-5 social and emotional health pilot program will receive in order to carry out the pilot program.
