# Colorado SB 25-118 (2025A) — Health Insurance Prenatal Care No Cost Sharing

The act requires that, for health insurance policies providing maternity coverage, policies issued or renewed on or after January 1, 2027, must include prenatal care coverage without cost sharing for up to 3 office visits.
