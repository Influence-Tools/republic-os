# Colorado SB 25-254 (2025A) — Transfer Stationary Sources Control Fund

Section 1 of the act requires the state treasurer to transfer $5 million from the general fund to the stationary sources control fund on July 1, 2025, and requires the division of administration of the department of public health and environment to report on the division's implementation of efficiency improvement projects related to the stationary sources control fund.
