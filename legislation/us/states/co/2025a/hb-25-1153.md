# Colorado HB 25-1153 (2025A) — Statewide Government Language Access Assessment

The act requires the department of personnel (department), in partnership with the office of new Americans, to conduct or contract to conduct a statewide language access assessment of the readiness of principal departments to meet the language access standards outlined in the language access universal policy (assessment).
