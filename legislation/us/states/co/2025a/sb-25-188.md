# Colorado SB 25-188 (2025A) — Fiscal Year 2025-26 Legislative Appropriation Bill

The act appropriates $74,577,313 to the legislative department for the payment of expenses in the 2025-26 state fiscal year. Of this amount, $72,829,086 is from the general fund, $5,000 is from cash funds, and $1,743,227 is from reappropriated funds.
