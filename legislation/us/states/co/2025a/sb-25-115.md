# Colorado SB 25-115 (2025A) — Seedling Tree Nursery Spending Authority Extension

For money appropriated to the Colorado state university system for use by the Colorado state forest service to renovate and expand the seedling tree nursery, the act extends the spending authority through the 2026-27 state fiscal year.
