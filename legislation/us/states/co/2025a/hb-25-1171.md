# Colorado HB 25-1171 (2025A) — Possession of Weapon by Previous Offender Crimes

Under current law, it is illegal for a person to possess a firearm if the person was convicted of or adjudicated for certain felonies. The act adds motor vehicle theft in the first degree to the list of violations that prohibit a person from possessing a firearm.
