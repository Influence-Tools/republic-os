# Colorado HB 25-1132 (2025A) — Military Family Behavioral Health Grant Program

The act expands the veterans mental health services program to provide grants to local nonprofit organizations to establish and expand community behavioral health programs that provide behavioral health services to service members, veterans, and family members of service members and veterans.
