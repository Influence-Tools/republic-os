# Colorado SB 25-260 (2025A) — Repeal Colorado Household Financial Recovery Pilot Program

In 2022, the general assembly created the Colorado household financial recovery pilot program to facilitate lending to individuals and households impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic who face financial insecurity and who have difficulty accessing affordable loans to address the financial insecurity.
