# Colorado SB 25-177 (2025A) — Sunset Continue Early Childhood Leadership Commission

The act continues the early childhood leadership commission (commission) housed in the department of early childhood for 5 years, repealing on September 1, 2030. Before its repeal, the commission is subject to a sunset review.
