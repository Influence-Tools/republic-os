# Colorado SB 25-267 (2025A) — Eliminate Roll-forward Authority Utilities Line Item

The act repeals the roll-forward authority of a state department to use unexpended and unencumbered money from the department's utilities line item to purchase energy conservation equipment and services during the state fiscal year following the state fiscal year for which the money was appropriated without further appropriation.
