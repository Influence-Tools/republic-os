# Colorado SB 25-223 (2025A) — Mill Levy Equalization & Institute Charter Schools

Beginning with the 2025-26 budget year, and each budget year thereafter, the state charter school institute (institute) shall not distribute a portion of its appropriated mill levy equalization funds to multi-district online schools that are authorized by the institute.
