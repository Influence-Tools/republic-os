# Colorado SB 25-230 (2025A) — College Opportunity Fund Program

The act clarifies that the department of higher education may distribute financial assistance from an allocation authorized for student financial assistance programs to undergraduate students who attend participating private institutions of higher education and who participate in the college opportunity fund program.
