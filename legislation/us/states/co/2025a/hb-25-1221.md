# Colorado HB 25-1221 (2025A) — Emily Griffith Associate of Applied Science Degree

The act permits Emily Griffith technical college (college) to offer an associate of applied science degree program (degree program) with approval from the state board for community colleges and occupational education (board). The degree program must include a registered apprenticeship program and certain transferable general education courses.
