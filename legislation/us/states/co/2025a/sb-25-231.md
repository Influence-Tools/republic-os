# Colorado SB 25-231 (2025A) — Repeal Inclusive Higher Education Act

The act repeals the "Inclusive Higher Education Act". The inclusive higher education act provided grants to state institutions of higher education to establish or expand inclusive higher education programs for students with intellectual and developmental disabilities.
