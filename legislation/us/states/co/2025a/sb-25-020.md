# Colorado SB 25-020 (2025A) — Tenant and Landlord Law Enforcement

Section 1 of the act allows a person to access a suppressed court record if that person affirms that they are accessing the record on behalf of the attorney general for the purpose of investigating any violation of state law that the attorney general may enforce.
