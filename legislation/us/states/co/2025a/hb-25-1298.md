# Colorado HB 25-1298 (2025A) — Judicial Performance Commissions

The act establishes the twenty-third judicial district commission on judicial performance on December 1, 2025, and specifies the initial terms for members of the commission. The act repeals outdated provisions concerning the terms of members of judicial commissions.
