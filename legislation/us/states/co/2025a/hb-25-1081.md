# Colorado HB 25-1081 (2025A) — Reporting Statistics on Restitution

Beginning with the judicial department's 2026 "SMART Act" hearing, the act requires the state court administrator to report statistics concerning restitution payments received and owed during the previous 5 state fiscal years.
