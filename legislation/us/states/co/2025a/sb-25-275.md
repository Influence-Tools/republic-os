# Colorado SB 25-275 (2025A) — Nonsubstantive Relocation of Definitions in Colorado Revised Statutes

In the Colorado Revised Statutes, the act moves existing definitions into existing or new definition sections so that a reader may easily locate the definitions applicable to the relevant law. The act makes the changes without making substantive changes to the law.
