# Colorado SB 25-269 (2025A) — Transfer to Infrastructure Investment & Jobs Act Cash Fund

The act requires the state treasurer to transfer $4 million from the general fund to the "Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act" cash fund on July 1, 2025.
