# Colorado SB 25-088 (2025A) — Department of Agriculture Supplemental

The 2024 general appropriations act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the department of agriculture. The general fund and reappropriated funds portions of the appropriation are decreased and the cash funds and federal funds portions are increased.
