# Colorado HB 25-1203 (2025A) — Misbranding Cultivated Meat Products as Meat

The act prohibits food processing plants from selling or offering for sale cell-cultivated meat that is misbranded as a meat product. The act also requires food processing plants to clearly label cell-cultivated meat as cell-cultivated meat.
