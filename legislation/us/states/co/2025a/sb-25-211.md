# Colorado SB 25-211 (2025A) — Department of Corrections Budgeting Reports

The act requires the executive director (director) of the department of corrections (department) to report specified information on inmate population, bed capacity, and vacancy rates on a monthly basis. The act also requires the director to report to the joint budget committee and the office of state planning and budgeting:
