# Colorado SB 25-243 (2025A) — Revert Appropriated General Fund Money From Colorado Department of Labor & Employment

In state fiscal year 2023-24, the general assembly appropriated $1,400,000 from the general fund to the department of labor and employment to be used for the construction registered apprenticeship grant program. The act reverts $222,701 of that appropriation back to the general fund on or before June 30, 2025.
