# Colorado SB 25-114 (2025A) — Repeal of the FLEX Program

The act transfers all the unexpended and unencumbered money in the financial literacy and exchange (FLEX) fund to the general fund on June 30, 2025, and repeals the FLEX program on July 1, 2025.
