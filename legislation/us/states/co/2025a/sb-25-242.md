# Colorado SB 25-242 (2025A) — Division Unemployment Insurance Funding Mechanism

The act updates the funding mechanism for the division of unemployment insurance (division) by changing the name of the "employment and training technology fund" to the "unemployment insurance program support fund" and expanding the use of the fund to include the information technology and administrative costs of the division.
