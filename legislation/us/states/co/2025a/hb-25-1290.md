# Colorado HB 25-1290 (2025A) — Transit Worker Assault & Funding for Training

The act creates a specific criminal offense related to wrongfully interfering with a transit worker and adds it to the criminal harassment statute, as well as making the offense subject to the victim rights act. The act eliminates similar criminal conduct from the regional transportation district (district) statutory scheme.
