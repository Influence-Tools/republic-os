# Colorado SB 25-314 (2025A) — Recovery Audit Contractor Program

The act allows the department of health care policy and financing (department) to, on behalf of the department, contract with a recovery audit contractor (RAC) vendor to conduct RAC audits of medicaid providers (providers).
