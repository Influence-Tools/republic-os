# Colorado HB 25-1124 (2025A) — Universal Contracting Provision Requirements

Under current law, universal contracting provisions must address 16 requirements when a state agency contracts for the delivery of behavioral health services. The act repeals 13 of the existing requirements and adds a new requirement to facilitate connections between individuals and the statewide behavioral health safety net system.
