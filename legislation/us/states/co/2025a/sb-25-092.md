# Colorado SB 25-092 (2025A) — Department of Governor, Lt. Governor, and Office of State Planning & Budgeting Supplemental

The 2024 general appropriations act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the offices of the governor, lieutenant governor, and state planning and budgeting. The general fund portion of the appropriation is increased and reappropriated funds portion is decreased.
