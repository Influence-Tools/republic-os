# Colorado HB 25-1155 (2025A) — Modify Candidate Authority Watchers General Election

The act allows a candidate who is on the ballot for an election for the office of United States congress, state office, or district office of state concern and is subject to a recount to select one watcher in addition to any watchers otherwise selected for the recount.
