# Colorado SB 25-303 (2025A) — Repeal Natural Disaster Grant Fund

Effective July 1, 2025, the act repeals the natural disaster grant fund from which awards were granted to local governments for improvements to domestic wastewater treatment works or public drinking water systems that were impacted by a natural disaster.
