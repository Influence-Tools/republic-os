# Colorado SB 25-095 (2025A) — Department of Human Services Supplemental

The 2024 general appropriations act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the department of human services. The general fund and federal funds portions of the appropriation are decreased and the cash funds and reappropriated funds portions are increased.
