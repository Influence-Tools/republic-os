# Colorado SB 25-278 (2025A) — Epinephrine Administration in Schools

The act changes the term "epinephrine auto-injector" to "emergency-use epinephrine" in order to encompass alternatives to injecting epinephrine as a means to treat anaphylaxis in school settings.
