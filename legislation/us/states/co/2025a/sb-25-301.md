# Colorado SB 25-301 (2025A) — Remove Authorization Requirement Adjust Chronic Prescription

The act allows a health-care provider to, under certain circumstances, adjust the dose or frequency of a chronic maintenance drug without needing prior authorization from an insurance carrier.
