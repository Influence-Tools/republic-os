# Colorado SB 25-175 (2025A) — Sunset Bill Towing Task Force

The sunset law requires that new advisory committees and task forces be repealed and undergo a review before the repeal. The act changes the towing task force's scheduled repeal from September 1, 2025, to September 1, 2035.
