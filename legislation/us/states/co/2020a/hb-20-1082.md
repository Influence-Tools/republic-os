# Colorado HB 20-1082 (2020A) — State Historical Society Authority To Sell Property

The act grants the state historical society the authority to sell a vacant lot located in Georgetown, Colorado. The act specifies that the proceeds of the sale must be credited to the state museum cash fund to be used for capital outlay, capital construction, or controlled maintenance at museums statewide.
