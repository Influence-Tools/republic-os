# Colorado HB 20-1055 (2020A) — Vintner's Restaurant Alternating Proprietor

The act allows a vintner's restaurant licensee (licensee) to apply to the state licensing authority in the department of revenue for approval to manufacture vinous liquors on alternating proprietor licensed premises, which is a distinct and definite area that is owned by or in possession of the licensee and within which the licensee is authorized to manufacture and store vinous liquors. A licensee that is authorized to manufacture on alternating proprietor licensed premises is not permitted to conduct retail sales of vinous liquors on the alternating proprietor licensed premises.
