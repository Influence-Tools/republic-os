# Colorado HB 20-1417 (2020A) — Care Subfund In The General Fund

The state received $1.67 billion from the federal coronavirus relief fund created in the federal "Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act of 2020" (CARES Act), and the governor allocated $70 million of these federal funds to the general fund for further allocation by the general assembly for any permissible uses under the CARES Act. The state controller set aside this money in a special account, known as the care subfund.
