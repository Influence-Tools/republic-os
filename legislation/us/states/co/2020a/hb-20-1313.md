# Colorado HB 20-1313 (2020A) — Administration Of Late Ballots

In connection with the existing requirement that the county clerk and recorder (clerk) must ensure that any eligible applicant is registered to vote in an election in specified circumstances, the act adds a requirement that the applicant be mailed a ballot.
