# Colorado SB 20-032 (2020A) — Employee Age To Sell Alcohol Retail Liquor Store

The act removes the prohibition against an employee of a liquor-licensed drugstore who is under 21 years of age having contact with alcohol beverages at the liquor-licensed drugstore.
