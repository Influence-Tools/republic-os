# Colorado HB 20-1369 (2020A) — Reduce Prosecution Training Appropriation

The general assembly is required to annually appropriate $350,000 to the department of law for allocation to the Colorado district attorneys' council for prosecution training. The act reduces the annual amount to $200,000 for fiscal year 2020-21.
