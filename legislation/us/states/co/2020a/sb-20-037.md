# Colorado SB 20-037 (2020A) — Trusted Interoperability Platform Advisory Committee

The act creates the trusted interoperability platform advisory committee to develop a strategic plan to implement a trusted interoperability platform that securely exchanges information between criminal and juvenile justice systems and community health agencies.
