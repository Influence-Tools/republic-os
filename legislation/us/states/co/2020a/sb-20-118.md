# Colorado SB 20-118 (2020A) — Hazardous Materials Transportation Permit Issuance

Effective January 1, 2021, the act transfers the function of issuing permits for the transportation of hazardous materials and nuclear materials by motor vehicle from the public utilities commission to the department of transportation.
