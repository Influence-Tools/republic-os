# Colorado SB 20-134 (2020A) — Estimate Of Non-fee Sources Of Cash Fund Revenue

Current law limits the amount of uncommitted reserves that may remain in a cash fund at the end of any fiscal year. The definition of "uncommitted reserves" excludes revenue credited to a cash fund that is estimated to be derived from non-fee sources.
