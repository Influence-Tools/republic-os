# Colorado HB 20-1214 (2020A) — Sunset Home Warranty Service Contracts

The act implements the recommendations of the department of regulatory agencies in its sunset review and report on home warranty service contracts by continuing the statutes governing the contracts for 6 years, until 2026, and clarifying that home warranty service contracts are not insurance.
