# Colorado HB 20-1404 (2020A) — Sunset Delay Sex Offender Management Board Review

Due to the COVID-19 virus, the judiciary committee of the house of representatives (judiciary committee) was unable to hold a full hearing during the 2020 regular session on the sunset report on the sex offender management board (SOMB report) prepared by the department of regulatory agencies (DORA). The act continues the sex offender management board for one year and directs the judiciary committee to hold a hearing on the SOMB report during the 2021 regular session. DORA is not required to prepare an additional report prior to the 2021 session.
