# Colorado HB 20-1145 (2020A) — Move Over Or Slow Down For Official Vehicle

Preexisting law requires a driver who is overtaking an emergency vehicle, tow vehicle, or public utility vehicle that is parked on the side of the road to reduce and maintain a safe speed.
