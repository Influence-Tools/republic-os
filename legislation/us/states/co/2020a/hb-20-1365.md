# Colorado HB 20-1365 (2020A) — Sustain Funding History Colorado

Current law authorizes the general assembly to appropriate money to the state historical society from the museum and preservation operations account of the state historical fund to pay for history Colorado certificates of participation. The act allows money to also be appropriated from the general fund or any other available fund.
