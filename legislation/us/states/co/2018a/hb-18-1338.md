# Colorado HB 18-1338 (2018A) — Reduced Revenue Severance Tax Operational Fund Transfers

Joint Budget Committee. Under current law, money is transferred from the severance tax operational fund (operational fund) to certain cash funds to benefit programs that are commonly referred to as the tier 2 programs.
