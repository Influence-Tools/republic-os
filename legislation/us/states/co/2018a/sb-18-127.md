# Colorado SB 18-127 (2018A) — Repeal Publishing Requirement DOR Department Of Revenue Income Tax Rate Modifications

Statutory Revision Committee. The bill repeals the requirement that the executive director of the department of revenue publish an historical explanation of income tax rate modifications enacted in the state on every income tax return form.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
