# Colorado SB 18-231 (2018A) — Transition to Community-based Services Task Force

Joint Budget Committee. The bill establishes a task force for transition planning to make recommendations on improvements for the transition of individuals with disabilities who are receiving services and supports in an educational setting to receiving services and supports through home- and community-based services. It specifies membership on the task force and duties including making a report to specified committees of the general assembly.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
