# Colorado HB 18-1146 (2018A) — Sunset Measurement Standards Law

Sunset Process - House State, Veterans, and Military Affairs Committee. The bill implements the recommendations of the department of regulatory agencies in its sunset review and report on the measurement standards law by extending the law for 15 years.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
