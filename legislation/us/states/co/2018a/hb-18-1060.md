# Colorado HB 18-1060 (2018A) — Income Tax Deduction For Military Retirement Benefits

The starting point for determining state income tax liability is federal taxable income. This number is adjusted for additions and subtractions (deductions) that are used to determine Colorado taxable income, which amount is multiplied by the state's 4.63% income tax rate.
