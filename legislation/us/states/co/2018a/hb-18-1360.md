# Colorado HB 18-1360 (2018A) — Increase Number Historical Society Board Members

The state historical society is currently governed by a board of directors with 9 members appointed by the governor. The bill expands the number of directors to 13. The governor is prohibited from appointing more than 7 members from the same major or minor political party.
