# Colorado HB 18-1354 (2018A) — Powersports Vehicle Written Warranties

Statutory Revision Committee. Current law appears to forbid a powersports vehicle manufacturer or distributor from honoring written warranties. The bill clarifies that the powersports dealer is required to honor written warranties.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
