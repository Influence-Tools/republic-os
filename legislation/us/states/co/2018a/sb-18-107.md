# Colorado SB 18-107 (2018A) — Repeal Nomination Vacancy Filling In Municpal Elections

Statutory Revision Committee. The bill repeals the process by which a vacancy in nomination may be filled for an election conducted under the 'Colorado Municipal Code of 1965' and makes conforming amendments.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
