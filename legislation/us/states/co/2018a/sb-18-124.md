# Colorado SB 18-124 (2018A) — Imported Alcohol Beverages Waiting Period

Current law requires a manufacturer or importer of imported alcohol beverages to file a statement and notice of intent to import with the state licensing authority at least 30 days before the import or sale of the imported alcohol beverages. The bill removes the 30-day waiting period requirement.
