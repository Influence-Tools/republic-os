# Colorado SB 18-253 (2018A) — CSTARS Account Transfer To DRIVES Account Effective Date

Joint Budget Committee. The bill establishes a uniform date of July 1, 2019, to transition the department of revenue's Colorado state titling and registration (CSTARS) account to the department of revenue's DRIVES vehicle services account. The bill also delays for one year the corresponding statutory repeal dates.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
