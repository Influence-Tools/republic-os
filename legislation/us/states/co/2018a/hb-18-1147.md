# Colorado HB 18-1147 (2018A) — Sunset Process Weather Modification

Sunset Process - House Health, Insurance, and Environment Committee. Sections 1 and 2 of the bill continue the regulation of people who modify the weather until 2033. Section 3 repeals a provision that prohibits weather modification that benefits another state if the other state prohibits weather modification that benefits Colorado.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
