# Colorado HB 18-1372 (2018A) — Exempt Fund From Capital Construction Funding Mechanism

Capital Development Committee. The bill exempts the department of human services' regional center depreciation account in the capital construction fund from the definition of 'cash fund' for purposes of the requirements under the automatic cash fund funding mechanism for payment of future costs attributable to certain of the state's capital assets.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
