# Colorado SB 18-033 (2018A) — Animal Feeding Operation Permits Continuation

The bill replaces the July 1, 2018, repeal date for the department of public health and environment's animal feeding operation permit program with a repeal date of July 1, 2025. The bill also extends the fees associated with the program at their current levels.
