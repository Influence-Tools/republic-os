# Colorado HB 18-1293 (2018A) — Fiscal Year 18-19 Legislative Appropriation Bill

The bill makes appropriations for matters related to the legislative department for the 2018-19 state fiscal year. Additionally, the bill directs the state treasurer to transfer $850,000 from the preservation grant program account in the state historical fund to the legislative department cash fund for use in the project to restore the old supreme court chamber in the state capitol building.
