# Colorado HB 18-1336 (2018A) — Repeal Local Government Retail Marijuana Impact Grant Program

Joint Budget Committee. On July 1, 2019, the bill repeals the local government retail marijuana impact grant program, under which the department of local affairs (department) awards grants to eligible local governments for documented marijuana impacts. Any encumbered money from the fiscal year 2017-18 appropriation to the department remains available for expenditure in the next fiscal year. The bill also repeals a reporting requirement regarding the effectiveness of the grant program.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
