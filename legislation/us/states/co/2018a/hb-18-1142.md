# Colorado HB 18-1142 (2018A) — Remove Term "Pauper" From Colorado Revised Statutes

Statutory Revision Committee. The bill modernizes the language in statutory sections by replacing the terms 'pauper' and 'paupers' with 'indigent' or 'indigent persons'.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
