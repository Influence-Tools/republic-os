# Colorado HB 18-1329 (2018A) — Supplemental Payment Durable Medical Equipment

Joint Budget Committee. The bill authorizes a supplemental payment of state-only money to qualified providers of durable medical equipment who experienced a decrease in reimbursement in the 2017-18 state fiscal year as a result of the implementation of the federal '21st Century Cures Act'.
