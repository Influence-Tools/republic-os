# Colorado HB 18-1369 (2018A) — Obsolete References Proposition AA Refund Account

Statutory Revision Committee. The bill removes statutory references to section 39-28.8-604, Colorado Revised Statutes, the former proposition AA refund account that was repealed on July 1, 2017.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
