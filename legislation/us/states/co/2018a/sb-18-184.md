# Colorado SB 18-184 (2018A) — Permit Short-term Extraction Construction Material

Section 1 of the bill creates a new class of limited impact construction materials permits for one-time activities that produce construction materials as a by-product and are not intended to be ongoing mining operations. Section 2 authorizes an application fee of $400 for the permit and an annual fee of $200.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
