# Colorado SB 18-176 (2018A) — Board Meeting Dates Southwestern Water Conservation District

The board of the southwestern water conservation district (board) is currently required to meet on the fourth Tuesday of January, April, July, and October. Section 1 of the bill requires the board to meet once every 3 months and eliminates the specific date requirement. Section 2 makes conforming amendments to the provisions governing the terms of board members and of the board president.(Note: This summary applies to the reengrossed version of this bill as introduced in the second house.)
