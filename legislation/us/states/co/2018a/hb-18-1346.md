# Colorado HB 18-1346 (2018A) — Abuse Of Youth Under 21 In Care Of Institution

The bill directs the Colorado commission on criminal and juvenile justice to study the issue of institutional child abuse for children and youth in facilities operated by the department of human services. On or before July 1, 2019, the commission shall provide a report with its findings and recommendations to the general assembly.
