# Colorado HB 19-1209 (2019A) — Aeronautical Reporting Requirements

Intrastate air carriers - reports to aeronautics division. The act removes the requirement that air carriers providing intrastate air service within Colorado file semiannual reports with the aeronautics division regarding the on-time performance and the number of passengers denied boarding on intrastate flights by the air carrier.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
