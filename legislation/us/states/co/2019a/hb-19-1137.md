# Colorado HB 19-1137 (2019A) — Expand Teacher Cadet Program Include Early Childhood Education

Teacher cadet program - early childhood education. The act clarifies that high school students who are interested in early childhood education may participate in the teacher cadet program in high schools.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
