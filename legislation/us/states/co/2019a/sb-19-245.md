# Colorado SB 19-245 (2019A) — Time Requirements For Food Stamp Appeals

Food stamp program - rule-making - appeal deadline. The act grants the department of human services rule-making authority to require any party to file a notice of intent to file exceptions with the state department within five days after service of the initial decision upon the party, or otherwise forgo the ability to file exceptions.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
