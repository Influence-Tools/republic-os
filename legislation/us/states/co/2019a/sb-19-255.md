# Colorado SB 19-255 (2019A) — Gallagher Amendment Residential Assessment Rate

Property tax - residential assessment rate. Based on a residential target percentage that is equal to 45.69%, the act lowers the residential assessment rate from 7.2% to 7.15% for property tax years commencing on and after January 1, 2019, until the next property tax year that the general assembly adjusts the rate.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
