# Colorado HB 19-1256 (2019A) — Electronic Filing Of Certain Taxes

Returns - electronic filing and payment. The act requires taxpayers, not including individual income taxpayers, to both file tax returns and pay amounts due for specified taxes electronically.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
