# Colorado HB 19-1205 (2019A) — Reimburse Expenses Restorative Justice Council

Restorative justice coordinating council - expenses reimbursement. Under current law, members of the restorative justice coordinating council may not be reimbursed for expenses. The act allows reimbursement of expenses.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
