# Colorado SB 19-083 (2019A) — Colorado Department Of Public Health And Environment Air Quality Control

State board of health - supervision of air quality control programs - repeal. The act eliminates the requirement that the state board of health supervise certain air quality control programs and removes statutory provisions relating to the air pollution variance board and the air quality hearings board.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
