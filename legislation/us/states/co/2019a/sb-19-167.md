# Colorado SB 19-167 (2019A) — Honor Colorado Professional Fire Fighters

Registration - special license plates - professional fire fighters - appropriation. The act creates a Colorado professional fire fighters license plate. To be issued the plate, a qualified applicant must pay 2 one-time $25 fees and make a donation to a nonprofit organization selected by the department of revenue.
