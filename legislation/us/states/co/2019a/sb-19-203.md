# Colorado SB 19-203 (2019A) — Fiscal Year 2019-20 Legislative Appropriation Bill

Legislative appropriation - reappropriation from general assembly to legislative council. $51,308,908 is appropriated to the legislative department for the payment of expenses in the 2019-20 state fiscal year. Additionally, the act appropriates $25,000 to the youth advisory council cash fund within the legislative department.
