# Colorado HB 19-1070 (2019A) — Colorado Department Of Public Health And Environment Cancer Drug Testing

Substance testing by department - repeal. The act repeals language requiring the department of public health and environment to test substances that any individual, person, firm, association, or other entity has held out to have value in the diagnosis, treatment, alleviation, or cure of cancer.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
