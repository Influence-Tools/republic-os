# Colorado SB 19-194 (2019A) — National Guard Tuition Assistance Colorado State University Global

Colorado state university global campus - national guard tuition assistance. The act adds Colorado state university - global campus to the list of designated institutions of higher education for purposes of tuition assistance for members of the National Guard.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
