# Colorado SB 19-189 (2019A) — Sunset Concurrent Enrollment Advisory Board

Education - concurrent enrollment advisory board - continuation under sunset law. The act continues the concurrent enrollment advisory board (advisory board) until September 1, 2024. Prior to its repeal, the advisory board shall be reviewed.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
