# Colorado HB 19-1238 (2019A) — Clarification Of Manufactured Housing Standards

Certification of factory-built structures - insignias of approval. The act amends the state director of housing's authority to obtain injunctive relief to be consistent with the removal of the requirement that factory-built structures that are only substantially altered or repaired bear an insignia of approval issued by the division of housing.
