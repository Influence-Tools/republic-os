# Colorado SB 19-205 (2019A) — Honor Service Of Women Veterans

Motor vehicle registration - license plates - women veterans - appropriation. The act creates a license plate to honor women veterans of the United States armed forces. To be issued the plate, an applicant must pay 2 one-time $25 fees.
