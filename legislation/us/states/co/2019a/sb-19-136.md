# Colorado SB 19-136 (2019A) — Expand Division Youth Services Pilot Program

Youth services - pilot program - second location - appropriation. There is currently a pilot program to aid in the establishment of a division-wide therapeutic and rehabilitative culture (pilot program) in the division of youth services that operates in one location. The act expands the pilot program to a second location and requires a second evaluation and report on the pilot program.
