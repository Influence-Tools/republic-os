# Colorado SB 19-008 (2019A) — Substance Use Disorder Treatment In Criminal Justice System

Substance use disorders - alternatives to arrest and criminal charges for persons in need of substance use treatment - treatment in prisons and jails - record sealing - harm reduction program - appropriation. The act enacts policies related to the involvement of persons with substance use disorders in the criminal justice system.
