# Colorado HB 19-1284 (2019A) — Urban Drainage Flood Control District Board Directors

Urban drainage and flood control district - board of directors. The urban drainage and flood control district is a special district created in statute to design and construct flood control and warning measures within portions of the metropolitan Denver area. The district is governed by a board of directors (board).
