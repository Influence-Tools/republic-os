# Colorado HB 19-1332 (2019A) — Telephone Users Disabilities Fund Talking Book Library

Telecommunications - assistance to customers with disabilities - talking book library - appropriation. The act authorizes the use of money in the Colorado telephone users with disabilities fund (fund) to support talking book library services for persons who are blind and physically disabled.
