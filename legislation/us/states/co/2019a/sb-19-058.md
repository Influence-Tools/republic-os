# Colorado SB 19-058 (2019A) — Enactment of CRS 2018

Enactment of Colorado Revised Statutes 2018. The act enacts the softbound volumes of the Colorado Revised Statutes 2018 and the Special Supplement 2018 as the positive and statutory law of the state of Colorado and establishes the effective date of said publications.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
