# Colorado SB 19-162 (2019A) — Sunset River Outfitter Advisory Committee

River outfitter advisory committee - continuation under sunset law. The act implements the recommendation of the department of regulatory agencies in its sunset review and report on the river outfitter advisory committee by continuing the committee indefinitely.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
