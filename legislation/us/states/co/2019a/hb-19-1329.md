# Colorado HB 19-1329 (2019A) — Wholesale Sales Agricultural Fertilizer Tax Exempt

Sales and use tax - wholesale sales - agricultural commodities - fertilizer and spray adjuvants. Wholesale sales are not subject to sales and use taxes. The act includes sales of fertilizer and spray adjuvants used in the production of agricultural commodities in the definition of "wholesale sales" for sales and use tax purposes.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
