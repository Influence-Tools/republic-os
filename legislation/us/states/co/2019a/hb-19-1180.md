# Colorado HB 19-1180 (2019A) — Correcting The Definition Of Police Working Horse

Current law prohibits cruelty to certain animals, including a certified police working horse. However, there is no certification process for police working horses. The bill clarifies this by removing the term "certified" from the definition of police working horse and revises the definition accordingly.
