# Colorado SB 19-160 (2019A) — Sunset River Outfitters Licensing

Division of parks and wildlife - licensing of river outfitters - continuation under sunset law. The act implements the recommendation of the department of regulatory agencies' sunset review and report on the licensing of river outfitters by the division of parks and wildlife within the department of natural resources by continuing these functions until September 1, 2028.
