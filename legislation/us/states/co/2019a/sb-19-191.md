# Colorado SB 19-191 (2019A) — Prompt Pretrial Liberty And Fairness

Pretrial release - post bond within 2 hours - nominal processing fees - release 4 hours after posting bond - release even if costs or fees need to be paid - plan for bond hearing within 48 hours - application of bond toward fees, costs, fines, restitution, or surcharges.
