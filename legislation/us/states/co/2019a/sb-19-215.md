# Colorado SB 19-215 (2019A) — Parents Encouraging Parents Conference

Parents encouraging parents conference - appropriation. The act creates parents encouraging parents conferences for parents of children with disabilities. The department of education shall provide for the conferences and related lodging and food for attendees. The act requires a specified conference curriculum.
