# Colorado SB 19-213 (2019A) — Marijuana Cash Fund Transfer

Marijuana cash fund - marijuana tax cash fund - transfers. The act requires the state treasurer to make 2 transfers from the marijuana cash fund to the marijuana tax cash fund. On July 1, 2019, the state treasurer will transfer $914,416, and on July 1, 2020, the state treasurer will transfer $890,901.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
