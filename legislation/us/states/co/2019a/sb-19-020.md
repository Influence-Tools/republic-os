# Colorado SB 19-020 (2019A) — Wildland Fire Airspace Patrol System

Wildland fires - aerial firefighting - patrolling airspace - appropriation. The center of excellence for advanced technology aerial firefighting is required, subject to available appropriations, to study and, if feasible, implement a system to patrol the airspace above wildland fires.
