# Colorado SB 19-069 (2019A) — Nonpublic School Teacher Development Programs

Educator licensing - nonpublic school educator licensing programs. Under the act, nonpublic schools are permitted to operate induction programs for teachers, special services providers, principals, and administrators and alternative licensure programs for teachers and principals who do not hold professional licenses.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
