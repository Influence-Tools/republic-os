# Colorado SB 19-157 (2019A) — Sunset Fire Suppression Registration And Inspection

Fire suppression - registration of contractors - inspection and maintenance of fire suppression systems - continuation under sunset law. The act continues the fire suppression programs of the division of fire prevention and control in the department of public safety for 7 years, until 2026.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
