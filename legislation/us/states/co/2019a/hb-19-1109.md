# Colorado HB 19-1109 (2019A) — Convalescent Centers As Pharmacies

Pharmacies - authority of hospice or convalescent center to operate as a pharmacy. The act allows a licensed hospice or convalescent center to procure, store, order, dispense, and administer prescription medications.
