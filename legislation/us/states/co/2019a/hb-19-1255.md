# Colorado HB 19-1255 (2019A) — Mesa Verde National Park License Plate

Registration - special license plates - Mesa Verde National Park. The act creates the Mesa Verde National Park license plate. To be issued the plate, an applicant must pay 2 one-time $25 fees and make a donation to a nonprofit organization selected by the department of revenue.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
