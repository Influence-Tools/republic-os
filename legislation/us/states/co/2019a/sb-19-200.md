# Colorado SB 19-200 (2019A) — Alcohol Beverage Consumption National Western Center

Alcohol regulation - licensed premises - National Western Center. The act authorizes the city and county of Denver to promulgate an ordinance authorizing a person to remove an alcohol beverage from the licensed premises of a vendor within the National Western Center so long as the person does not leave the National Western Center.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
