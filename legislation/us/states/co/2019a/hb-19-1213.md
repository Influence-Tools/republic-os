# Colorado HB 19-1213 (2019A) — Urban Drainage Flood Control District Director Compensation

Urban drainage and flood control - director compensation. A member of a board of directors of an urban drainage and flood control district is currently limited to receiving $1,200 per year as compensation, not to exceed $75 per meeting attended. The act changes these maximum amounts to be the same as the amounts allowed for directors of special districts generally, which is currently specified in statute as $2,400 per year, not to exceed $100 per meeting.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
