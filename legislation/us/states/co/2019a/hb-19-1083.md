# Colorado HB 19-1083 (2019A) — Athletic Trainers License

Athletic trainers - regulation - change from registration to licensing. The act changes the terms describing the regulation of athletic trainers from "registration" to "license" and "licensure" and from "registrant" and "registered athletic trainer" to "licensee".(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
