# Colorado SB 19-082 (2019A) — Repeal Board Of Health Authority Over Colorado Department Of Public Health And Environment Funds

State board of health - repeal authority over money for state and local public works or public health functions. The act repeals the state board of health's authority to accept, use, disburse, and administer money allotted to the department of public health and environment for state and local public works or public health functions.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
