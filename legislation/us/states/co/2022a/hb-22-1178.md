# Colorado HB 22-1178 (2022A) — Department Of Personnel Supplemental

The 2021 general appropriations act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the department of personnel. The general fund, cash funds, and reappropriated funds portion of the appropriation are increased.
