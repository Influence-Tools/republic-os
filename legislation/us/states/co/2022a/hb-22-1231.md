# Colorado HB 22-1231 (2022A) — Foster Parent Bill Of Rights

The act creates certain rights for foster parents. The rights do not apply to a foster parent who jeopardizes the safety of a child or youth or a foster parent against whom criminal charges have been filed for child abuse, a sexual offense, or any felony.
