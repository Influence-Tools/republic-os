# Colorado SB 22-075 (2022A) — Removal Of Cemetery District Directors

The act allows a board of county commissioners, which appoints directors to a board of directors for a cemetery district, to remove any director from such a board for cause after giving the director notice and an opportunity to be heard at a public hearing.
