# Colorado SB 22-134 (2022A) — State Fair Master Plan Funding

The act requires the state treasurer to transfer $4 million from the general fund to the Colorado state fair authority cash fund within 3 days after the date the act takes effect to partly fund the implementation of the 2021 Colorado state fair master plan.
