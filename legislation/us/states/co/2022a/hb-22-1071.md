# Colorado HB 22-1071 (2022A) — Damages In Class Actions Consumer Protection Act

The act states that in a class action under the "Colorado Consumer Protection Act", a successful plaintiff may recover actual damages, injunctive relief allowed by law, and reasonable attorney fees and costs.
