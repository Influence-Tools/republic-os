# Colorado SB 22-069 (2022A) — Learning Disruption Effect On Teacher Evaluation

For the 2021-22 and 2022-23 school years, the act prohibits a school district or board of cooperative services from using measures of student academic growth derived from the Colorado growth model or from considering the performance plan type implemented by the school district or board of cooperative services or by a school in determining evaluation ratings for licensed personnel.
