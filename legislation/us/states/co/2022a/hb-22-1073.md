# Colorado HB 22-1073 (2022A) — Funeral Establishment And Crematory Inspection

The act authorizes the director of the division of professions and occupations to enter the premises of registered funeral establishments and crematories during business hours to conduct inspections. The director may contract with a third party to perform the inspection.
