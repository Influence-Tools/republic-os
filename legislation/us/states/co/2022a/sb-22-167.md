# Colorado SB 22-167 (2022A) — Affirm Greyhounds As Companion Pets

The act removes the exemption for greyhound breeders from the "Pet Animal Care and Facilities Act". By removing this language, greyhounds are elevated to a class of pet that is intended to be a companion pet.
