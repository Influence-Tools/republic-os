# Colorado SB 22-091 (2022A) — Nonsubstantive Changes To Rule Review Statutes

The act repeals a section of the "State Administrative Procedure Act" to remove obsolete or redundant provisions relating to the process for reviewing executive agency rules. The act also amends provisions in statute concerning executive agency rules to reflect current drafting practices relating to, among other practices, the use of gender-neutral language in statute.
