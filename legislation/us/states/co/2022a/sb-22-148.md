# Colorado SB 22-148 (2022A) — Colorado Land-based Tribe Behavioral Health Services Grant Program

The act creates the Colorado land-based tribe behavioral health services grant program (grant program) to provide funding to one or more Colorado land-based tribes to support capital expenditure for the renovation or building of a behavioral health facility to provide behavioral and mental health services. The grant program repeals on July 1, 2027.
