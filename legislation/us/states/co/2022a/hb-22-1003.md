# Colorado HB 22-1003 (2022A) — Youth Delinquency Prevention And Intervention Grants

The bill establishes the delinquency prevention and young offender intervention pilot grant program (program) in the division of criminal justice (division) within the department of public safety (department). The program awards 2-year grants to local governments, American Indian tribes, school districts and charter schools, and nonprofit organizations to fund projects to reduce crime among youth. Preference is given to applicants whose projects demonstrate a community-based response in which multiple agencies community-based partners coordinate to reduce crime among youth and those in areas with high rates of crime among youth involvement in the juvenile justice system .
