# Colorado HB 22-1246 (2022A) — Hospice Inpatient Unit Specialized Prescription Drug Outlet

The act allows a pharmacy located within a hospice inpatient unit to register as a specialized prescription drug outlet for the purposes of providing drugs, devices, and pharmacist services to the residents of the hospice inpatient unit.
