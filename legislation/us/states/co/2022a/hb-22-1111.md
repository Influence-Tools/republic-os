# Colorado HB 22-1111 (2022A) — Insurance Coverage For Loss Declared Fire Disaster

The act establishes new coverage requirements for homeowners insurance policies issued or renewed in Colorado, which requirements apply in the event of a total loss of an owner-occupied residence as a result of a wildfire disaster that is declared by the governor.
