# Colorado SB 22-021 (2022A) — Treatment Behavioral Health Disorders Justice System

The act updates provisions of the existing article 1.9 of title 18, Colorado Revised Statutes, concerning the treatment of persons with mental health disorders in the criminal and juvenile justice systems.
