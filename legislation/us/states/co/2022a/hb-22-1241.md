# Colorado HB 22-1241 (2022A) — Court Appointed Special Advocates Special License Plate

The act creates the CASA special license plate. In addition to the standard motor vehicle fees, the plate requires 2 one-time fees of $25. One of the fees is credited to the highway users tax fund and the other to the licensing services cash fund.
