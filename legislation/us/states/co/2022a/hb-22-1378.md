# Colorado HB 22-1378 (2022A) — Denver-metro Regional Navigation Campus Grant

The act directs the division of housing in the department of local affairs (division) to award a grant to a local government in the Denver metropolitan area or a community partner in conjunction with a local government in order to build or acquire, and then facilitate, a regional navigation campus to respond to and prevent homelessness.
