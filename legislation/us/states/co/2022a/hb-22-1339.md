# Colorado HB 22-1339 (2022A) — Merge Department Of Revenue Division Of Motor Vehicles Cash Funds

The licensing services cash fund (fund) consists mainly of fees charged for the issuance of drivers' licenses, driving permits, and state identification cards. The department of revenue (department) uses the fund primarily to fund the issuance of those documents.
