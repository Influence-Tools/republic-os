# Colorado HB 22-1208 (2022A) — Jail Data Collection Clean-up

Under existing law, the keeper of a jail is required to submit a quarterly report of inmate information to the division of criminal justice within the department of public safety (division), and the division is required to publish that information in a searchable and sortable format.
