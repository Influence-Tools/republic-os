# Colorado HB 22-1077 (2022A) — Colorado Nonprofit Security Grant Program

The act creates the Colorado nonprofit security grant program to provide money to qualified nonprofit organizations that are at high risk of a terrorist attack and that applied for, but did not receive, a grant from the federal nonprofit security grant program.
