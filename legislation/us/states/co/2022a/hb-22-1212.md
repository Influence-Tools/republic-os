# Colorado HB 22-1212 (2022A) — Sunset Process Conveyance Regulation

The act continues the regulation of conveyances and conveyance mechanics, contractors, and inspectors by the director of the division of oil and public safety within the department of labor and employment for 9 years, until September 1, 2031.
