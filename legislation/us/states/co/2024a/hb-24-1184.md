# Colorado HB 24-1184 (2024A) — Department of Governor, Lieutenant Governor, & Office of State Planning and Budgeting Supplemental

The 2023 general appropriations act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the offices of the governor, lieutenant governor, and state planning and budgeting. The general fund portion and reapproriated funds portions of the appropriation are decreased and the cash funds portion is increased.
