# Colorado HB 24-1194 (2024A) — Department of Natural Resources Supplemental

The 2023 general appropriations act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the department of natural resources. The general fund, the cash funds, the reappropriated funds, and the federal funds portions of the appropriation are increased.
