# Colorado HB 24-1207 (2024A) — Adjustments to School Funding Budget Year 2023-24

The general assembly recognizes that the actual funded pupil count and the at-risk pupil count for the 2023-24 budget year are higher than expected when the appropriation amount for the state share of total program funding was established during the 2023 legislative session, resulting in an increase in total program funding for the 2023-24 budget year.
