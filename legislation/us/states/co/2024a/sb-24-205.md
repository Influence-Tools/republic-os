# Colorado SB 24-205 (2024A) — Consumer Protections for Artificial Intelligence

On and after February 1, 2026, the act requires a developer of a high-risk artificial intelligence system (high-risk system) to use reasonable care to protect consumers from any known or reasonably foreseeable risks of algorithmic discrimination in the high-risk system.
