# Colorado HB 24-1414 (2024A) — Repeal COVID Heroes Collaboration Fund

The act repeals the COVID heroes collaboration fund on July 31, 2024. Prior to the repeal, on July 1, 2024, the state treasurer shall transfer the unexpended and unencumbered money in the COVID heroes collaboration fund to the general fund.
