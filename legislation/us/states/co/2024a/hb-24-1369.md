# Colorado HB 24-1369 (2024A) — Colorado Agriculture Special License Plate

The act creates the Colorado agriculture license plate. In addition to the normal fees for a license plate, a person must pay an additional one-time fee in the amount of $25, which money is credited to the highway users tax fund.
