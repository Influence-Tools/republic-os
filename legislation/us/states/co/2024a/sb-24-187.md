# Colorado SB 24-187 (2024A) — CSP Authority for Judicial Center & Judges

The act gives the Colorado state patrol authority to provide law enforcement services for the Ralph L. Carr Colorado judicial center and its grounds, subject to available appropriations, and to provide protection for Colorado judges and justices when they are present in the state capitol buildings group.
