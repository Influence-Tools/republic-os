# Colorado SB 24-206 (2024A) — Capitol Complex Renovation Fund

The act extends the deposit of the annual amount of depreciation of a capital asset acquired, repaired, replaced, improved, renovated, or constructed with money appropriated to a cash fund into the capitol complex renovation fund (fund) through July 1, 2029.
