# Colorado HB 24-1081 (2024A) — Regulate Sale Transfer Sodium Nitrite

The act limits the sale or transfer of a product containing sodium nitrite in a concentration greater than 10% of the mass or volume of the product (covered product) to commercial businesses that are verified to require a covered product.
