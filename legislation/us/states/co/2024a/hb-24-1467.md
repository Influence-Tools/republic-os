# Colorado HB 24-1467 (2024A) — Modifications to the State Personnel Total Compensation

The act requires the state personnel director to establish a "step pay" structure that provides consistent salary increases for employees instead of permitting merit pay. The act provides an exception for employees of the office of the state auditor.
