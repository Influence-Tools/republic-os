# Colorado HB 24-1013 (2024A) — Victim Programs in New Twenty-Third Judicial District

The new twenty-third judicial district exists beginning January 7, 2025. The act makes changes so that the crime victim compensation board and the victims and witnesses assistance and law enforcement board may provide compensation and services once the new judicial district exists.
