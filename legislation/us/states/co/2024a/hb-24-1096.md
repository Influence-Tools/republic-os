# Colorado HB 24-1096 (2024A) — School Psychologist Licensure Interstate Compact

The act enacts the "School Psychologists Licensure Interstate Compact" (compact). The purpose of the compact is to facilitate the interstate practice of school psychology in educational or school settings, thereby improving the availability of school psychological services (services) to the public.
