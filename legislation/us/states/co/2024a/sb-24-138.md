# Colorado SB 24-138 (2024A) — Modification of County Elected Officer Salary Categories

Each county in the state is categorized for purposes of establishing the salaries of elected officers in the county. The statutory salary amounts are adjusted every 2 years for inflation and take effect for terms commencing after any change is made.
