# Colorado SB 24-071 (2024A) — Seasonal Outdoor Adventure Day Camp Program

The act defines "seasonal outdoor adventure day camp program" (program) as a type of children's resident camp for licensing purposes or to ensure the programs are licensed. Programs serve children who are 5 years of age or older.
