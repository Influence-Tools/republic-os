# Colorado HB 24-1277 (2024A) — Sunset Youth Restraint & Seclusion Working Group

The youth restraint and seclusion working group (working group) is set to repeal September 1, 2024. The act extends the working group until September 1, 2034 and requires a sunset review prior to its repeal.
