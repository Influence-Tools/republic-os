# Colorado HB 24-1273 (2024A) — Sunset Continue Veterans Assistance Grant Program

The act implements the recommendation of the department of regulatory agencies' sunset review and report on the veterans assistance grant program (program) by continuing the program for 7 years, until September 1, 2031.
