# Colorado HB 24-1170 (2024A) — Rights for Youth Division of Youth Services Facilities

The act establishes in statute a bill of rights for youth who are the responsibility of the department of human services (department), whether the youth is detained or committed to the care and physical custody of a juvenile facility operated by the department.
