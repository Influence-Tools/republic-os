# Colorado HB 24-1278 (2024A) — Sunset Concurrent Enrollment Advisory Board

The act repeals the concurrent enrollment advisory board (advisory board) on September 1, 2027. Prior to the repeal, the department of regulatory agencies will conduct a sunset review and make recommendations concerning its continuation.
