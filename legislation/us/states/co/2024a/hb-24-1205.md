# Colorado HB 24-1205 (2024A) — Colorado Imagination Library Program

Current law requires the state librarian in the department of education to oversee the Colorado imagination library program (program) and to contract with a Colorado nonprofit organization (contractor) to operate the program. The act relocates the program to the department of early childhood (department).
