# Colorado HB 24-1285 (2024A) — Student Weight-Based Bullying Prevention

Current law identifies bullying behaviors that are subject to school district and charter school discipline policies and reporting requirements. The act adds a pattern of bullying based on a student's weight, height, or body size to the prohibited bullying behaviors.
