# Colorado HB 24-1470 (2024A) — Eliminate Premium Tax to Health Insurance Affordability Fund

Starting in the 2023-24 state fiscal year, the act discontinues the allocation of a portion of premium tax revenues to the health insurance affordability cash fund.
