# Colorado HB 24-1240 (2024A) — AmeriCorps Education Award Tax Subtraction

The act creates an income tax subtraction (deduction) for income tax years 2026 to 2033 for the amount of any Segal AmeriCorps Education Award received by a taxpayer for service in the AmeriCorps national service program, which is used by the taxpayer during the income tax year.
