# Colorado HB 24-1347 (2024A) — FY 2024-25 Legislative Appropriation Bill

The act appropriates $73,587,761 to the legislative department for the payment of expenses in the 2024-25 state fiscal year. Of this amount, $71,784,409 is from the general fund, $90,000 is from cash funds, and $1,713,352 is from reappropriated funds.
