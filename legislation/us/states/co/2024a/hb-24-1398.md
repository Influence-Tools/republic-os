# Colorado HB 24-1398 (2024A) — Transfers to Procurement Technical Assistance Fund

The act extends the period for which annual general fund transfers of $220,000 are made to the procurement technical assistance cash fund by 10 years so that the last transfer is made on July 1, 2034, rather than July 1, 2024.
