# Colorado HB 24-1116 (2024A) — Extend Contaminated Land Income Tax Credit

The act extends for an additional 5 years the availability of the state income tax credit allowed to a taxpayer for an approved environmental remediation of contaminated property, through income tax years commencing prior to January 1, 2030.
