# Colorado HB 24-1431 (2024A) — Stable Housing for Survivors of Abuse Program

The act creates the stable housing for survivors of domestic or sexual violence program (program) in the department of human services (state department). The act requires the state department to contract with community-based advocacy organizations to provide short-term assistance payments to survivors of domestic or sexual violence for stable housing.
