# Colorado HB 24-1458 (2024A) — Create Division of Animal Welfare in Department of Agriculture

The act creates the division of animal welfare (division) within the department of agriculture. The division is created to promote domestic animal welfare, including providing education and outreach, creating voluntary programs, and awarding grants.
