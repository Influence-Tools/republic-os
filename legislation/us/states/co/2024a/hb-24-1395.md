# Colorado HB 24-1395 (2024A) — Public School Capital Construction Assistance Fund Transfer Date

The act delays a $20 million transfer from the marijuana tax cash fund to the public school capital construction assistance fund from June 1, 2024, to June 1, 2026. The act reduces the appropriation to the department of education from the public school capital construction assistance fund for the 2024-25 state fiscal year by $20 million.
