# Colorado HB 24-1197 (2024A) — Department of Public Safety Supplemental

The 2023 general appropriations act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the department of public safety. The general fund and reappropriated funds portions of the appropriation are increased and the cash funds and federal funds portions are decreased.
