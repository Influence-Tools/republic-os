# Colorado HB 24-1389 (2024A) — School Funding 2023-24 for New Arrival Students

For the 2023-24 budget year, the act appropriates $24 million from the state education fund to the department of education (department) to distribute one-time funding to school districts and institute charter schools that enrolled new arrival students after the 2023-24 pupil enrollment count day.
