# Colorado HB 24-1256 (2024A) — Sunset Senior Dental Advisory Committee

The senior dental advisory committee created in the department of health care policy and financing is scheduled to repeal on September 1, 2024. The act continues the senior dental advisory committee until September 1, 2029.
