# Colorado HB 1416 (2026A) — Transfers to General Fund & Colorado Economic Development Fund

The act requires the state treasurer to transfer $1.2 million from the universal high school scholarship cash fund (fund) to the Colorado economic development fund and $2.3 million from the fund to the general fund on June 30, 2026.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
