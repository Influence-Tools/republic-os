# Colorado SB 173 (2026A) — Barre & Pilates Teacher Training Regulatory Exemptions

The act exempts pilates and barre teacher training courses, programs, and schools from regulation under the 'Private Occupational Education Act of 1981'.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
