# Colorado HB 1208 (2026A) — Sunset Compliance Advisory Panel Air Pollution

The act continues the compliance advisory panel to the air pollution control division in the department of public health and environment for 10 years, until September 1, 2036, pursuant to the provisions of the sunset law.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
