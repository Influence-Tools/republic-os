# Colorado HB 1369 (2026A) — Repeal of Contract for Online Platform Use

The act repeals the requirement for the department of higher education to contract for the use of an online platform by institutions of higher education in Colorado that assists students in accessing public benefits.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
