# Colorado HB 1358 (2026A) — Reduce Academic Accelerator Grant Program Appropriation

The act reduces by $5.2 million the appropriation for the Colorado academic accelerator grant program for the 2023-24 state fiscal year, which is available for expenditure through the 2026-27 state fiscal year.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
