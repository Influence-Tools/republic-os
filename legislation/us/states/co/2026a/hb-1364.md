# Colorado HB 1364 (2026A) — 2025 Consumer Price Index Calculation

For the purpose of calculating inflation based on the consumer price index for the 2025 calendar year, the act requires the index to be determined by averaging the 2 semiannual reports produced by the United States bureau of labor statistics for the 2025 calendar year.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
