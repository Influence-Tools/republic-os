# Colorado HB 1390 (2026A) — Health Disparities & Community Grant Evaluation

The act eliminates the third-party evaluation requirement for the health disparities and community grant program (program) and instead requires the department of public health and environment to evaluate the effectiveness of the program.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
