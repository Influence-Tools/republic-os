# Colorado HB 1380 (2026A) — Repeal Office of Judicial Discipline Ombudsman

The act repeals the office of the judicial discipline ombudsman, effective July 1, 2027, which was established in 2023 to act as an independent and confidential advisor on judicial discipline procedures and workplace culture.(Note: This summary applies to this bill as enacted.)
