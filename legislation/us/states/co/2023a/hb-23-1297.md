# Colorado HB 23-1297 (2023A) — Prohibit Corporation Issuing Scrip In Bearer Form

In conformance with federal law, the act repeals the allowance for a corporation to issue a scrip in bearer form upon the holder surrendering enough scrip to equal a full share.
