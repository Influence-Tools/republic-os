# Colorado SB 23-074 (2023A) — Sunset Modify Human Trafficking Prevention Training

The act implements the recommendations of the department of regulatory agencies (department), as contained in the department's 2022 sunset review of the human trafficking prevention training (training). The act continues the training for 7 years, until September 1, 2030.
