# Colorado SB 23-139 (2023A) — State Severance Tax Trust Fund Allocation

The act authorizes the general assembly to appropriate up to $10 million from the severance tax operational fund (operational fund) to the wildfire mitigation capacity development fund (wildfire fund) for state fiscal year 2022-23 and makes a corresponding appropriation.
