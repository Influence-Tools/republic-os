# Colorado HB 23-1062 (2023A) — Metropolitan District Tax For Parks And Recreation

The act allows a metropolitan district, which has existing authority to levy a sales tax to fund safety protection, street improvement, transportation, and fire protection services, to also levy a sales tax to provide parks or recreational facilities or programs within the district in which the tax is levied.
