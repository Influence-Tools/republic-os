# Colorado HB 23-1135 (2023A) — Penalty For Indecent Exposure In View Of Minors

The act makes indecent exposure a class 6 felony if committed when the person who commits indecent exposure knew there was a child under 15 years of age in view of the exposure and the person is more than 18 years of age and more than 4 years older than the child.
