# Colorado SB 23-228 (2023A) — Office Of Administrative Services For Independent Agencies

The act creates the office of administrative services for independent agencies (office) in the judicial department to provide administrative support services to the office of the child protection ombudsman, the independent ethics commission, the office of public guardianship, and the commission on judicial discipline.
