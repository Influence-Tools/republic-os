# Colorado SB 23-117 (2023A) — Department of Health Care Policy & Financing Supplemental

The 2022 general appropriations act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the department of health care policy and financing. The general fund, cash funds, and reappropriated funds of the appropriation are decreased and the federal funds are increased.
