# Colorado SB 23-138 (2023A) — Appropriation To Department of Health Care Policy And Financing For Denver Health

For the 2022-23 state fiscal year, the act appropriates $5 million from the general fund to the department of health care policy and financing to distribute to the Denver health and hospital authority.
