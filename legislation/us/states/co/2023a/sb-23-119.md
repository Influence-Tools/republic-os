# Colorado SB 23-119 (2023A) — Department of Human Services Supplemental

The 2022 general appropriations act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the department of human services. The general fund, cash funds, reapproriated funds, and federal funds portions of the appropriation are increased.
