# Colorado SB 23-140 (2023A) — Fentanyl Study Deadline And Appropriation

The act extends the deadline from January 1, 2023, to October 1, 2023, for when the department of public health and environment (department) must contract with an independent entity to conduct a study concerning House Bill 22-1326.
