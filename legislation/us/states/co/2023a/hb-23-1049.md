# Colorado HB 23-1049 (2023A) — Enactment Of CRS 2022

The act enacts the softbound volumes of the Colorado Revised Statutes 2022 and the subsequent changes approved by the voters at the general election on November 8, 2022, as the positive and statutory law of the state of Colorado and establishes the effective date of said publication.
