# Colorado SB 23-187 (2023A) — Public Utilities Commission Administrative Fee Setting Transportation Services

Under current law, various fees imposed on motor carriers are either specified in statute or set administratively by the public utilities commission (commission). The act removes the statutorily set fees and instead authorizes the commission to set the motor carrier fees administratively.
