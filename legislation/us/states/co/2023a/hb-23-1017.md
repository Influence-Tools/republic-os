# Colorado HB 23-1017 (2023A) — Electronic Sales And Use Tax Simplification System

As part of an effort to simplify the sales and use tax system, the department of revenue (department) created the electronic sales and use tax simplification system (SUTS), which is a one-stop portal designed to facilitate the collection and remittance of sales and use tax.
