# Colorado SB 23-197 (2023A) — Fiscal Year 2023-24 Legislative Appropriation Bill

The act appropriates $67,254,584 to the legislative department for the payment of expenses in the 2023-24 state fiscal year. Of this amount, $65,524,678 is from the general fund, $90,000 is from cash funds, and $1,639,906 is from reappropriated funds.
