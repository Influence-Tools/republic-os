# Colorado SB 23-261 (2023A) — Direct Care Workforce Stabilization Board

The act creates the direct care workforce stabilization board (board) in the department of labor and employment (department) to review the direct care industry, which is the industry of workers who provide home-based or community-based direct care to individuals who require assistance in accomplishing activities of daily living.
