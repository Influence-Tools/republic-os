# Colorado SB 23-019 (2023A) — Out-of-state Online Retail Vehicle Purchases

The act requires out-of-state online dealers and salespersons selling motor vehicles or powersports vehicles to submit to the jurisdiction of the motor vehicle dealer board (board) when selling to a Colorado consumer. The act does not require these online dealers and salespersons to obtain a Colorado dealer license or have a physical location in Colorado but requires them to comply with Colorado laws and the board's rules.
