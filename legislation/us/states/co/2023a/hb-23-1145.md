# Colorado HB 23-1145 (2023A) — Hearing Timelines Juveniles In Adult Facilities

The act aligns the timelines for hearings in Colorado law for a juvenile already ordered to be held in an adult facility while awaiting trial with the timelines in the federal "Juvenile Justice and Delinquency Prevention Act".
