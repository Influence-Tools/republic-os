# Colorado HB 23-1298 (2023A) — Earlier Funding For Middle School Lead Testing

The act changes the date upon which the department of public health and environment must begin providing reimbursements to eligible schools that serve students in sixth, seventh, or eighth grade for costs associated with the testing of drinking water from March 15, 2024, to June 1, 2023.
