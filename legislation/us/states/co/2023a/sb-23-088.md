# Colorado SB 23-088 (2023A) — Sentence Served Before Parole And Notify Victim

The act requires the Colorado commission on criminal and juvenile justice (commission) to report to the general assembly about its study of sentencing reform by June 30, 2023. The report must include a summary of the commission's work and an update on the status of the commission's and any task force's efforts to address clarity and certainty in the current criminal sentencing scheme regarding the amount of time that must be served on each sentence imposed by the court prior to a defendant's parole eligibility.
