# Colorado SB 23-116 (2023A) — Department of Governor, Lt. Governor, & OSPB Supplemental

The 2022 general appropriations act is amended to balance and make adjustments to the total amount appropriated to the department to the offices of the governor, lieutenant governor, and state planning and budgeting. The general fund and the reapproriated funds portion of the appropriation are increased.
