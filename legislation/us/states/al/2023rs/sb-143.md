# Alabama SB 143 (2023rs) — Relating to crimes and offenses; to add Article 12, commencing with Section 13A-6-260, to Chapter 6 of Title 13A to the Code of Alabama 1975, to identify criminal enterprise members, to enhance penalties for any criminal activity that benefits, promotes, or furthers the interest of a criminal enterprise, and to establish mandatory consecutive penalties for any firearm possession in certain circumstances; to amend Section 12-15-204, Code of Alabama 1975, to require any juvenile 16 years of age or older to be tried as an adult for any criminal enterprise related criminal activity; and in connection therewith would have as its purpose or effect the requirement of a new or increased expenditure of local funds within the meaning of Section 111.05 of the Constitution of Alabama of 2022.

Crimes and offenses, to identify criminal enterprise members, enhance penalties for any criminal activity that benefits, promotes, or furthers the interest of a criminal enterprise, create mandatory consecutive sentences for certain criminal enterprise crimes, and require certain juveniles to be tried as an adult in certain criminal enterprise related crimes.
