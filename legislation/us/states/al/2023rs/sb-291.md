# Alabama SB 291 (2023rs) — Relating to public contracts; to amend Section 41-16-51, Code of Alabama 1975, to further provide for contracts for which competitive bidding is not required; and in connection therewith would have as its purpose or effect the requirement of a new or increased expenditure of local funds within the meaning of Section 111.05 of the Constitution of Alabama of 2022.

Public contracts; contracts for which competitive bidding not required, further provided to include computer programs, software applications, and equipment and services for the prevention and detection of criminal activity by law enforcement agencies and community-oriented policing programs
