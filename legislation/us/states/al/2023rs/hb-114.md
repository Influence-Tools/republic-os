# Alabama HB 114 (2023rs) — Relating to fiduciary funds in the hands of officials; to amend Section 19-3-87, Code of Alabama 1975, to provide for disposition of unclaimed funds held in a county treasury after being unclaimed for a certain number of years; and to amend Section 35-12-73, Code of Alabama 1975, to exempt unclaimed funds under Section 19-3-87, Code of Alabama 1975.

Fiduciary funds held by counties, rights to unclaimed property in a county treasury forfeited after 10 years, Section 19-3-87 property exemption from Uniform Disposition of Unclaimed Property Act.
