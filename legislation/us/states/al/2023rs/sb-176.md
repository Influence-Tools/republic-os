# Alabama SB 176 (2023rs) — To create the Students' Right to Know Act of 2023; to require the Alabama Commission on Higher Education to collect data and create an interactive online tool for students for use in making informed decisions relating to education and professions; and to require the commission and Workforce Development Division of the Department of Commerce to share data and information as necessary to comply with this act.

Student Right to Know Act of 2023, ACHE required to collect and make available online data for students to plan for their educational and professional futures, Workforce Division of the Department of Commerce to share data and information with ACHE
