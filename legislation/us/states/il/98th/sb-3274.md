# Illinois SB 3274 (98th) — SCH CD-HIGHLY QUALIFIED TEACHR

Amends the School Code. Allows a teacher who teaches physical education or health education in the public schools to meet the requirements for highly qualified status that apply to teachers who teach in core academic subjects pursuant to the federal No Child Left Behind Act of 2001.
