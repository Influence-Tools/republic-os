# Illinois HB 4516 (98th) — PREDATORY CRIM SEX ASSAULT

Amends the Criminal Code of 2012 concerning predatory criminal sexual assault of a child. Provides that in order for the offense to be committed by the act of contact between the sex organ or anus of one person and the part of the body of another, the contact must be for the purpose of sexual gratification or arousal of the victim or the accused.
