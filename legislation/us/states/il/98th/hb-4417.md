# Illinois HB 4417 (98th) — PROBATION-PRETRIAL SERVICES

Amends the Probation and Probation Officers Act. Defines "pretrial services" as the services and programs established by a circuit court within a Probation and Court Services Department under the Pretrial Services Act.
