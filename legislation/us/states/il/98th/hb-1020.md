# Illinois HB 1020 (98th) — COUNTIES CD DEMOLITION

Amends the Counties Code. In provisions concerning the expedited removal of certain buildings that are a continuing hazard to the community in which they are located, deletes the requirement that the building be residential and 2 stories or less in height. Effective immediately.
