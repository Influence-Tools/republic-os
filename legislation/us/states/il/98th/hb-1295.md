# Illinois HB 1295 (98th) — IL URBAN DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY

Amends the Illinois Urban Development Authority Act. Provides that the Illinois Urban Development Authority may use funds for transportation projects. Defines "develop" or "development", "maintain" or "maintenance", "operate" or "operation", "transportation facility", and "transportation project".
