# Illinois SB 2922 (98th) — INS CD-PUBLIC ADJUSTER FEES

Amends the Illinois Insurance Code to provide that a public adjuster may not charge, agree to, or accept any compensation, payment, commissions, fee, or other valuable consideration in excess of 10% of the amount of the insurance settlement claim paid by the insurer.
