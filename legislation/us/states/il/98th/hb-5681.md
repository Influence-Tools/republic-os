# Illinois HB 5681 (98th) — WIU-BOARD OF TRUSTEES MEETING

Amends the Western Illinois University Law. Requires one of the Board of Trustees' quarterly meetings to be held on the Quad Cities campus of Western Illinois University. Effective immediately.
