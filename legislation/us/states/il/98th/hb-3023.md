# Illinois HB 3023 (98th) — CHILD SEX OFFENDER-PLAYGROUND

Amends the Criminal Code of 2012. Makes it unlawful for a child sex offender to knowingly be present in or loiter on a public way within 500 feet of any publicly accessible privately owned building with playground or recreation facilities or on real property comprising any publicly accessible privately owned building with playground or recreation facilities. Provides that a violation is a Class 4 felony.
