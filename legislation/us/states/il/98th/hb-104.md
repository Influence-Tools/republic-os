# Illinois HB 104 (98th) — DHFS-ALLKIDS-3RD PARTY INSURER

Amends the Illinois Insurance Code, the Covering ALL KIDS Health Insurance Act, and the Illinois Public Aid Code. Provides that to the extent authorized under federal law, the Department of Healthcare and Family Services shall identify individuals receiving services under medical assistance programs funded or partially funded by the State who may be or may have been covered by a third party health insurer, the period of coverage for such individuals, and the nature of the coverage. Requires a third party health insurer to provide the Department with eligibility information in a federally recommended or mutually agreed-upon format that includes, at a minimum, the names, addresses, dates, and sex of primary covered persons, the policy group numbers of the covered persons, and other identifiable information. Permits the Department to impose an administrative penalty on a third party health insurer that does not comply with the request for information. Provides that the Attorney General may bring suit on behalf of the Department to collect the penalty. Provides that recoveries made by the Department in connection with the imposition of this administrative penalty shall be deposited into the Public Aid Recoveries Trust Fund. Effective immediately.
