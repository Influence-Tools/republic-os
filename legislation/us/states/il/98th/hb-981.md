# Illinois HB 981 (98th) — INS CD-GUARANTY FUND-BOARD

Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. Provides that the board of directors of the Illinois Insurance Guaranty Fund shall consist of not less than 5 nor more than 11 persons, with 2 public members appointed by the Director of Insurance (instead of not less than 5 nor more than 9 persons).
