# Illinois SB 3437 (98th) — UTIL-ICC-NAT GAS MARKET REPORT

Amends the Public Utilities Act. Provides that an annual report prepared by the Illinois Commerce Commission's Office of Retail Market Development shall be filed by July 1 of each odd year beginning in 2015 (instead of July 1 of each odd year). Effective immediately.
