# Illinois SB 3009 (98th) — DCFS ANNUAL PROGRESS REPORT

Amends the Child Care Act of 1969. Provides that the Department of Children and Family Services shall provide the General Assembly with a comprehensive report on its progress in meeting performance measures and goals related to child care licensing no later than September 30th of each year (rather than September 30, 2013).
