# Illinois HB 129 (98th) — SCH CD-BRING PARENT TO SCH DAY

Amends the School Code. Requires all school boards to designate the first Monday in October of each year "Bring Your Parents to School Day" to promote parental involvement and student success. Provides that on this day, a school board shall permit the parents or guardians of students to attend class with their children and meet with teachers and administrators during the school day.
