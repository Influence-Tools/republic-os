# Illinois SB 1688 (98th) — COM COL-BASE OPERATING GRANTS

Amends the Public Community College Act. Removes a provision that requires a portion of a base operating grant to be allocated on the basis of non-residential gross square footage of space maintained by a community college district. Effective immediately.
