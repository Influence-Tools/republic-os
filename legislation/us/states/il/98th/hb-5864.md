# Illinois HB 5864 (98th) — PUBLIC SAFETY AGENCY NETWORK

Amends the Illinois Public Safety Agency Network Act. Redefines membership for the board of directors. Provides that the board may purchase or lease real estate or personal property for corporate purposes. Expands the power of the board of directors concerning the securing of funding. Makes changes concerning finances and reporting requirements. Effective immediately.
