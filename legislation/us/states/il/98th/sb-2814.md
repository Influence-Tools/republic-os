# Illinois SB 2814 (98th) — SANITARY DIST-BD OF TRUSTEES

Amends the Sanitary District Act of 1917. Provides that the district's board of trustees shall be selected from the district's corporate boundaries (currently, they are selected from the county in which the district is located). Effective immediately.
