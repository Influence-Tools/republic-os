# Illinois HB 167 (98th) — VEH CD-PRINCE HALL MASON PLATE

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides for the issuance of Prince Hall Freemasonry license plates. Provides that in addition to the appropriate registration fees, an applicant for the special plate shall be charged a fee of $25 at original issuance and renewal. Provides that $15 of the additional fee for original issuance and $2 of the additional fee for renewal shall be deposited into the Secretary of State Special License Plate Fund. Provides that $10 of the additional fee for original issuance and $23 of the additional fee for renewal shall be deposited into the Master Mason Fund.
