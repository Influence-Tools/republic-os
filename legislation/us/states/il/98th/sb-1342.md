# Illinois SB 1342 (98th) — MORTALITY REVIEW-MEMBERS

Amends the Department of Juvenile Justice Mortality Review Team Act. Provides that each mortality review team shall consist of at least one member who is a representative of a bar association, law school, or legal academic organization.
