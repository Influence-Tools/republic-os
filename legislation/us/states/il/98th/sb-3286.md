# Illinois SB 3286 (98th) — CIV PRO-SERVICE PROCESS-GATED

Amends the Civil Practice Law of the Code of Civil Procedure. Provides that a gated residential community, including a condominium association or a cooperative, shall grant unannounced entry into the community, including its common areas and common elements, to a person who is attempting to serve process on a defendant or witness who resides within or is known to be within the community.
