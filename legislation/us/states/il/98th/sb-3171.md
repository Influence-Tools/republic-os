# Illinois SB 3171 (98th) — MEDICAID-PODIATRY SERVICES

Amends the Medical Assistance Article of the Illinois Public Aid Code. Removes a provision requiring the Department of Healthcare and Family Services to limit adult podiatry services to individuals with diabetes. Effective immediately.
