# Illinois HB 2498 (98th) — COMPT-VOUCHERS FOR REFUNDS

Amends the State Comptroller Act. Provides that a provision requiring payment by electronic funds transfer of any itemized voucher for under $5 does not apply to vouchers presented by the Department of Revenue for the payment of refunds of taxes administered by the Department. Effective immediately.
