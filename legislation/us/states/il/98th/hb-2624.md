# Illinois HB 2624 (98th) — ITAP-LIBRARY DISTRICTS

Amends the Department of Central Management Services Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Provides that the full-time webmaster must compile and update the ITAP database with information received from library districts. Provides for a searchable database of both current employees and employees hired on or after the effective date of this amendatory Act, sorted separately by employing unit of government, employment position title, and current pay rate and year-to-date pay. Effective immediately.
