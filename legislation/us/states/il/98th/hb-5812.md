# Illinois HB 5812 (98th) — CTY CD-INTEREST IN CONTRACTS

Amends the Public Officer Prohibited Activities Act. Provides that a county officer may hold a position on the board of a not-for-profit corporation that is interested in a contract, work, or business of the county. Sets forth the limitations of participation if a county officer is appointed by the governing body of the county to represent the interests of the county on the board of a not-for-profit corporation. Sets forth the limitations of participation if a county officer is not appointed to the governing body of a not-for-profit corporation by the governing body of the county. Makes similar changes in the Counties Code.
