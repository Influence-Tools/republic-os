# Illinois HB 2641 (98th) — PERMANENT PLATES FOR FIRE VEH

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that motor vehicles used by fire departments or for fire protection services are eligible for $8 permanent registration plates, as long as those plates indicate the fire protection service that owns the vehicle. Provides that the Secretary of State shall put procedures in place to allow fire protection services in possession of these plates to transfer those plates at no additional charge.
