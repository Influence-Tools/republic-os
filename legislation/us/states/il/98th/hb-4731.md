# Illinois HB 4731 (98th) — INDEMNIFICATION-APP PROSECUTOR

Amends the State Employee Indemnification Act. Adds individuals or organizations who contract with the Office of the State's Attorneys Appellate Prosecutor to provide professional services to the definition of "employee". Effective immediately.
