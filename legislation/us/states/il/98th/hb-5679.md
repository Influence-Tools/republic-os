# Illinois HB 5679 (98th) — WIU-HIGH SCHOOL FEEDBACK SYSTM

Amends the Western Illinois University Law. With respect to the university's high school feedback system, requires the Board of Trustees to cooperate with the Board of Higher Education in submitting to each high school attendance center an annual report concerning the graduates of that high school attendance center. Effective immediately.
