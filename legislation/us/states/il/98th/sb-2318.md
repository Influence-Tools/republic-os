# Illinois SB 2318 (98th) — MUNI CD-POLICE-SERVICE SUMMONS

Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that retired police officers may serve summons for violations of ordinances occurring within their municipalities (currently, only active duty police officers may do so). Effective immediately.
