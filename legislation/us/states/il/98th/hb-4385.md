# Illinois HB 4385 (98th) — DOT VEHICLE SALE

Amends the Department of Transportation Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Provides that, in addition to passenger automobiles, light duty trucks, and heavy duty trucks, the Department of Transportation may sell any self-propelled motorized equipment in excess of 25 horsepower and attachments to units of local government upon a determination by the Department of Central Management Services that this equipment will not be re-allocated to other State agencies. Effective immediately.
