# Illinois HB 2943 (98th) — LOBBYISTS-CLIENT REGISTRANTS

Amends the Lobbyist Registration Act. Requires a registrant whose client is another registrant to disclose the name and address of the ultimate beneficiary of the registrant's services. Requires a registrant whose client is another registrant to include in expenditure reports the name and address of the ultimate beneficiary of the expenditure.
