# Illinois HB 4530 (98th) — MUNI CODE-VENDING

Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. Defines "vending machine" as a coin, currency, or debit card operated machine that dispenses goods or services, and includes machines that use utility services. Provides that "vending machine" includes those machines that provide an enclosed space or area for the use of a customer or for the delivery of the goods or services. Excludes machines operated by the United States Postal Service for the sale of postage stamps or other postal products.
