# Illinois HB 5619 (98th) — MUNI CD-SCH GROUND IMPROVMNTS

Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that for the purposes of implementation of ordinances by a plan commission or planning department regarding developer donations or impact fees, the definition of "school grounds" includes technological infrastructure.
