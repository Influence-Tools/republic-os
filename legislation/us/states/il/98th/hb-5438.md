# Illinois HB 5438 (98th) — LOCAL GOVT-AUDIT REPORTS

Amends the Governmental Account Audit Act, Counties Code, and Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that the amount of fees owed by a governmental unit for delinquent audits or reports may be reduced at the Comptroller's discretion. Effective immediately.
