# Illinois HB 2947 (98th) — BUDGET-PREVIOUS YEAR INFO

Amends the State Budget Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Provides that the budget shall contain a Section indicating whether there was a budget surplus or a budget deficit for the previous fiscal year, or whether the previous fiscal year's budget was balanced, based on estimates prepared by the Governor's Office of Management and Budget using actual figures available on the date the current fiscal year's budget is submitted. Provides that those estimates shall not include payments that are made from a future fiscal year's appropriations.
