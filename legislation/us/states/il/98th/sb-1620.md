# Illinois SB 1620 (98th) — WANTON WASTE OF GAME MEAT

Amends the Wildlife Code by making it illegal to wantonly waste or destroy usable meat from game animals. Defines "usable meat" and details locations where individuals may not dispose of harvested wildlife carcasses or parts.
