# Illinois HB 1404 (98th) — PUBLIC CONSTRUCTION BONDS

Amends the Public Construction Bond Act. Requires surety bonds on public construction projects by any political subdivision of the State costing over $50,000 (now, over $5,000). Makes surety bond requirements for any political subdivision of the State the same as those for the State. Effective immediately.
