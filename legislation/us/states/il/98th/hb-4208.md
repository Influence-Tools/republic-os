# Illinois HB 4208 (98th) — STATE OFFICIALS-COUNTY-ETHICS

Amends the State Officials and Employees Ethics Act. Provides that any member of a governmental entity appointed by the president or chairperson of the county board, or by any member or members of the county board, shall abide by certain ethics laws (current law provides for a member of a governmental entity appointed by the president or chairperson of the county board only).
