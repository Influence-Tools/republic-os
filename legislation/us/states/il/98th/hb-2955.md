# Illinois HB 2955 (98th) — ONLINE BUDGET PUBLICATION

Amends the State Budget Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Provides that within 30 days of its enactment into law, the Governor's Office of Management and Budget shall publish to its website the budget of the State of Illinois for the coming fiscal year in its entirety in specified formats.
