# Illinois HB 5485 (98th) — PUBLIC LABOR REL-MANNING

Amends the Illinois Public Labor Relations Act. Provides that in the case of fire fighter, and fire department or fire district paramedic matters, the arbitration decision shall be limited to wages, hours, and conditions of employment, which may include manning and residency requirements. Effective immediately.
