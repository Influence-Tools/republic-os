# Illinois SB 2980 (98th) — TWP CD-FINANCIAL STATEMENT

Amends the Township Code. Provides that a copy of the full statement of the financial affairs of the township may be provided to the electors at the annual township meeting in lieu of the township clerk reading the statement aloud.
