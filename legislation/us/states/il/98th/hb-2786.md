# Illinois HB 2786 (98th) — DHS-PUB AID-LEGAL GUARDIANS

Amends the Illinois Public Aid Code. Provides that whenever there is a change in legal guardianship of a minor child who receives benefits under this Code, the appropriate State agency shall immediately inform the Department of Human Services of the change in legal guardianship to ensure such benefits are sent directly to the minor child's legal guardian. Effective immediately.
