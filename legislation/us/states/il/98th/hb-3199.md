# Illinois HB 3199 (98th) — CMAP-ANNUAL HEARING-COGFA

Amends the Regional Planning Act. Requires the Commission on Government Forecasting and Accountability to hold a public hearing within 30 days after the Governor's Budget Address to the General Assembly to consider CMAP's annual report and its impact on the State budget. Sets forth notification requirements. Effective immediately.
