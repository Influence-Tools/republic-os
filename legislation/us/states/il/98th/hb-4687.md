# Illinois HB 4687 (98th) — NUCLEAR SAFETY-COBALT 60

Amends the Illinois Nuclear Safety Preparedness Act. Provides that specified shipping fees shall not apply to shipments consisting entirely of cobalt-60 or other medical isotopes, or both.
