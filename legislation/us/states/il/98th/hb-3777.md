# Illinois HB 3777 (98th) — SCH MAINTENANCE GRANT-SPEC ED

Amends the School Code and the School Construction Law. Authorizes the State Board of Education to make grants to special education cooperatives established by school districts for school maintenance projects; makes related changes. Effective immediately.
