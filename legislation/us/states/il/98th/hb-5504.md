# Illinois HB 5504 (98th) — TROOPER ROAD NAME-REPEAL

Amends the Illinois Highway Code. Repeals the provision allowing the Director of State Police to request the Department of Transportation to designate segments of highways in honor of Illinois State Troopers killed in the line of duty. Effective immediately.
