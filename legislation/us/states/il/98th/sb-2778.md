# Illinois SB 2778 (98th) — PROP TX-JUDGMENTS

Amends the Property Tax Code. Provides that, for tax year 2013 and subsequent tax years, all applications for judgment and order of sale for taxes and special assessments on delinquent properties shall be made by July 1 of the calendar year following the second installment due date. Effective immediately.
