# Illinois HB 4527 (98th) — SCH CD-CHARTER SCH-ENG LEARNER

Amends the Charter Schools Law of the School Code. Requires a charter school to comply with all federal and State laws and rules applicable to public schools that pertain to special education and the instruction of English language learners. Effective immediately.
