# Illinois HB 2820 (98th) — PUBLIC BUILDING COMM RENTING

Amends the Public Building Commission Act. Provides that the Public Building Commission may rent its buildings and facilities to not for profit corporations and non-profit organizations or associations. Provides definitions for these terms. Effective immediately.
