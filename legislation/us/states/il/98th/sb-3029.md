# Illinois SB 3029 (98th) — CHECK IDENTIFICATION NUMBERS

Amends the Consumer Deposit Account Act. Repeals a provision requiring all financial institutions that provide checks, drafts, or orders of withdrawal that are drawn against funds held by those financial institutions in a consumer-deposit account opened subsequent to January 1, 1982 to display the month and year in which the account was opened on the face of every order of withdrawal, and to display a unique number on the face of every order of withdrawal for accounts opened after January 1, 1982. Amends the Check Printer and Check Number Act. Repeals a provision requiring that any person who sells or distributes checks, drafts, or similar orders of withdrawal that are drawn against funds held in a consumer-deposit account opened subsequent to January 1, 1993 to display the month and year in which the account was opened on the face of every order of withdrawal, and to display a unique number on the face of every order of withdrawal for accounts opened after January 1, 1993. Effective immediately.
