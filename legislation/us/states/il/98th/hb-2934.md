# Illinois HB 2934 (98th) — INTERNET SAFETY-CHILD CARE

Amends the Internet Dating Safety Act and the Consumer Fraud and Deceptive Business Practices Act. Expands the scope of the Internet Dating Safety Act to cover Internet child care services, Internet senior care services, and Internet home care services. Changes the short title of the Internet Dating Safety Act to the Internet Dating, Internet Child Care, Internet Senior Care, and Internet Home Care Safety Act. Effective immediately.
