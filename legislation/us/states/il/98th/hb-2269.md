# Illinois HB 2269 (98th) — NOTARIAL RECORDS-EXTEND SUNSET

Amends the Illinois Notary Public Act. Extends the repeal date for certain provisions concerning Notarial Records from July 1, 2013 to July 1, 2018. Effective immediately.
