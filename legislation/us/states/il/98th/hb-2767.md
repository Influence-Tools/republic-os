# Illinois HB 2767 (98th) — PENCD-SERS-NO BOARD STIPENDS

Amends the State Employee Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Provides that, for service on or after July 1, 2013, "compensation" does not include any stipend payable to an employee for service on a board or commission. Effective immediately.
