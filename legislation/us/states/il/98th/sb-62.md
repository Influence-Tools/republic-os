# Illinois SB 62 (98th) — MHDD CD-CLINICAL PSYCHOLOGIST

Amends the Mental Health and Developmental Disabilities Code. Changes the definition of "clinical psychologist" to a person licensed by the Department of Financial and Professional Regulation under the Clinical Psychologist Licensing Act. Effective immediately.
