# Illinois SB 1245 (98th) — PUBLIC SAFETY EMPLOYEE BENEFIT

Amends the Public Safety Employee Benefits Act. Defines "catastrophic injury" to mean the consequences of an injury that permanently prevent an individual from performing any gainful work. Effective immediately.
