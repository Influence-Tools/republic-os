# Illinois SB 3057 (98th) — COMMON INT COMM ASSOC-LEASES

Amends the Common Interest Community Association Act. Provides that provisions requiring a unit owner leasing a unit to deliver a copy of a lease to the association apply only to leases that are greater than 30 days.
