# Illinois HB 2613 (98th) — FIN-CDB-UNUSED FEDERAL FUNDS

Amends the General Obligation Bond Act and the Capital Development Bond Act of 1972. Provides that the unused portion of federal funds received for a capital improvement project for which moneys from the Capital Development Fund have been expended shall remain in the Capital Development Board Contributory Trust Fund and shall be used for capital projects and for no other purpose, subject to appropriation and as directed by the Capital Development Board.
