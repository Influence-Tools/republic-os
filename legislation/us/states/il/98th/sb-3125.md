# Illinois SB 3125 (98th) — CARNIVAL & AMUSEMENT RIDES

Amends the Carnival and Amusement Rides Safety Act. Changes the Act's short title to "Amusement Ride and Attraction Safety Act". Changes the name of the Carnival-Amusement Safety Board to the Amusement Ride and Attraction Safety Board.
