# Illinois SB 3035 (98th) — NURS HOME-RECEIVER REIMBURSE

Amends the Nursing Home Care Act. In a provision concerning the reimbursement of receivers by the Department of Public Health adds that a monitor may also be reimbursed. Provides that the Department of Public Health may reimburse the receiver or monitor for its expenses from funds appropriated to the Department by the General Assembly after funds contained in the Long Term Care Monitor/Receiver Fund not allocated for the costs associated with hiring and maintaining of surveyors have been exhausted (currently, after funds contained in the Long Term Care Monitor/Receiver Fund have been exhausted). Effective immediately.
