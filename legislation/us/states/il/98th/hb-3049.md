# Illinois HB 3049 (98th) — LATINO FAMILY COMMISSION

Amends the Illinois Latino Family Commission Act. Replaces the Department of Public Aid with the Department of Healthcare and Family Services. Adds liaisons from the Illinois State Board of Education, the Illinois State Board of Higher Education, the Illinois Community College Board, the Illinois Department of Human Rights, and the Capital Development Board.
