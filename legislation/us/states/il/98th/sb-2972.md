# Illinois SB 2972 (98th) — SCH CD-LICENSE-SUPERINTENDENT

Amends the Educator Licensure Article of the School Code. Allows a superintendent endorsement to be affixed to the Professional Educator License of a holder who has had at least 2 years of experience employed full-time in a general administrative position, among other qualifications. Effective immediately.
