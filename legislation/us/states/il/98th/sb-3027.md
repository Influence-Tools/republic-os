# Illinois SB 3027 (98th) — MOBILE HOME-SALE IN ERROR

Amends the Mobile Home Local Services Tax Enforcement Act. Provides that the county collector may declare an administrative sale in error if: (1) the mobile home was not subject to taxation; (2) the taxes had been paid prior to the sale of the mobile home; (3) prior to the tax sale a voluntary or involuntary petition has been filed by or against the legal or beneficial owner of the mobile home requesting relief under the provisions of 11 U.S.C. Chapter 7, 11, 12, or 13; or (4) the mobile home is owned by the United States, the State of Illinois, a municipality, or a taxing district. Effective immediately.
