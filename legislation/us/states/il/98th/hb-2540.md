# Illinois HB 2540 (98th) — PREV WAGE-FILE DETERMINATIONS

Amends the Prevailing Wage Act. Deletes language requiring copies of determinations concerning the prevailing rate of wages to be filed with the Secretary of State. Effective January 1, 2014.
