# Illinois HB 2862 (98th) — CONVEYANCE-KANE COUNTY-APPLIC

Amends "An Act to authorize the Department of Mental Health to convey certain State-owned lands in Kane County", approved August 10, 1965, as amended. Provides that certain restrictions on the use of that land do not apply to a specified portion of that land. Effective immediately.
