# Illinois SB 1229 (98th) — DIETITIAN NUTRITIONIST-VARIOUS

Amends the Dietitian Nutritionist Practice Act. Provides that a "medically prescribed diet" may only be performed as initiated by or in consultation with a physician licensed under the Medical Practice Act of 1987 acting within the scope of his or her practice. Effective immediately.
