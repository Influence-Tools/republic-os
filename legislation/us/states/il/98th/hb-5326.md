# Illinois HB 5326 (98th) — LOCAL GOV'T RIDESHARING

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that permanent vehicle registration plates shall be issued for a one-time fee of $8 to vehicles owned by counties, townships, or municipal corporations used for the purpose of ridesharing.
