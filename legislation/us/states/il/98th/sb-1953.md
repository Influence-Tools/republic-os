# Illinois SB 1953 (98th) — PROP TX-PPV LEASES

Amends the Property Tax Code. In a Section concerning valuation of PPV leases, provides that the term "net income" means all revenues received minus actual expenses before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization (now, "net income" means all revenues received minus the lesser of (i) 42% of all revenue or (ii) actual expenses before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization). Effective January 1, 2014.
