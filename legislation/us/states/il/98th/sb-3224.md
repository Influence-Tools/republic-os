# Illinois SB 3224 (98th) — COMP-STATEWIDE MASTER CONTRACT

Amends the State Comptroller Act. Exempts contracts administered for statewide orders placed by agencies (commonly referred to as "statewide master contracts") from a Minority Contractor Opportunity Initiative fee. Effective immediately.
