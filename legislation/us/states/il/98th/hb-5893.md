# Illinois HB 5893 (98th) — PROP TX-NOTIFY FIRE PROTECTION

Amends the Property Tax Code. Provides that fire protection districts shall be notified of certain applications for exemption that would reduce the assessed valuation of any property by more than $100,000. Effective immediately.
