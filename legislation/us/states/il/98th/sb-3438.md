# Illinois SB 3438 (98th) — CO-OPERATIVE ACT SHARE LIMIT

Amends the Co-operative Act. Increases from 5 to 10 the number of shares for which a person may subscribe or control. Increases from $100 to $1,000 the maximum subscription price per share. Increase from $500 to $10,000 the maximum value of stock issued to any one shareholder. Effective immediately.
