# Illinois HB 2362 (98th) — DCFS RESD SRVCS CONSTRCTON PGM

Amends the DCFS Residential Services Construction Grant Program Act. Redefines the term "residential services" by removing language that provides that residential services are services for which the Department of Children and Family Services has rate setting authority. Effective immediately.
