# Illinois HB 4205 (98th) — PROC CD-RESPONSIVE BIDDER

Amends the Illinois Procurement Code. Provides that the requisite notice regarding every contract that is let shall include the number of unsuccessful bidders (currently unsuccessful responsive bidders). Effective immediately.
