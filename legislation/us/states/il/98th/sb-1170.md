# Illinois SB 1170 (98th) — UNLAWFUL POSS. OF WILDLIFE

Amends the Wildlife Code. Creates a prohibition on possession of wildlife or parts of wildlife that were taken in a manner that is illegal in the territory where the animal was taken. Additionally defines the statute of limitations on prosecution of violations.
