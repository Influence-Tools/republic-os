# Illinois HB 3 (98th) — SCH CD-INTERFUND TRANSFERS

Amends the School Code. Extends the time period during which a school district other than the Chicago school district may transfer moneys from specified funds for any purpose from June 30, 2013 to June 30, 2016. Effective immediately.
