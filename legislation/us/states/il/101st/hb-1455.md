# Illinois HB 1455 (101st) — DISPUTES OVER REMAINS

Amends the Disposition of Remains Act. Provides that any dispute concerning the right to control the disposition of a decedent's remains shall be resolved by a court within 30 days of the dispute being filed with the court.
