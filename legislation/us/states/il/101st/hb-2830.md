# Illinois HB 2830 (101st) — SCHOOL CONFERENCE NO TERMINATE

Amends the School Visitation Rights Act. Prohibits an employer from terminating an employee because of an absence from work due to employee's attendance at a school conference or activity. Effective immediately.
