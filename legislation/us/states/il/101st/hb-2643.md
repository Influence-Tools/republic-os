# Illinois HB 2643 (101st) — CANCEL CONTRACT SENIORS 15 DAY

Amends the Consumer Fraud and Deceptive Business Practices Act. Provides that a person age 65 and older may cancel certain contracts within 15, rather than 3, days after the day the contract was signed. Effective January 1, 2020.
