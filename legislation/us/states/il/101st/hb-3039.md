# Illinois HB 3039 (101st) — DHS-LONG-TERM CARE-NOTICE

Amends the Illinois Public Aid Code. Provides that, if a recipient resides at a long-term care facility, the Department of Healthcare and Family Services shall send the notice of its decision to terminate to the recipient and the long-term care facility.
