# Illinois SB 87 (101st) — VEH CD-TINTED LIGHTS

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that no person shall drive or move any motor vehicle or equipment upon any highway with any lighting or combination of lighting with a smoked or tinted lens or cover.
