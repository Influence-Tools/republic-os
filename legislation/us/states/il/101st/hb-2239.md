# Illinois HB 2239 (101st) — SIU-BOARD OF TRUSTEES-MEMBERS

Amends the Southern Illinois University Management Act. Provides that both student members (rather than only one student member) of the Board of Trustees of Southern Illinois University shall be voting student members; makes conforming changes.
