# Illinois HB 3471 (101st) — INS CD-CARDIOPULMONARY MONITOR

Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. Requires a group or individual policy of accident and health insurance to provide coverage for cardiopulmonary monitors for a person 18 years old or younger who has had a cardiopulmonary event. Effective January 1, 2020.
