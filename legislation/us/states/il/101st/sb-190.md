# Illinois SB 190 (101st) — PERSONNEL CD-ELIGIBILITY LISTS

Amends the Personnel Code. Provides that if an agency requests an open competitive eligible list from the Department of Central Management Services, the Director or Central Management Services shall also provide to the agency a Successful Disability Opportunities Program eligible candidate list. Effective immediately.
