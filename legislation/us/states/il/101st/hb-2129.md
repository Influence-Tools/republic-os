# Illinois HB 2129 (101st) — PARK DIST-EQUIP&MACHINE LEASES

Amends the Park District Code. Provides that a park district may enter into a lease for a period of not to exceed 8 years (rather than 5 years) for equipment and machinery after an affirmative vote of two-thirds of the governing board of the park district. Effective immediately.
