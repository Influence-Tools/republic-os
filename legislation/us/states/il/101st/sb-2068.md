# Illinois SB 2068 (101st) — COMMUNAL KITCHENS

Amends the Food Handling Regulation Enforcement Act. Prohibits a public health district from regulating the preparing and serving of food in a private residential leasehold that is prepared by or for the lessees and consumed by the lessees and their guests.
