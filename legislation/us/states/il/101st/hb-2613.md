# Illinois HB 2613 (101st) — PROF CORP SPEECH THERAPY

Amends the Professional Service Corporation Act. Includes within the scope of the term "related professions" and "related professional services" the practice of speech-language therapy by persons licensed under the Illinois Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology Practice Act. Effective immediately.
