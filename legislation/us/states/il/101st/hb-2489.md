# Illinois HB 2489 (101st) — MOBILE HOME-TRANSFER REPORT

Amends the Mobile Home Local Services Tax Act. Provides that the Secretary of State shall provide the county collector in each county a quarterly report of the transfer of title of mobile homes. Effective immediately.
