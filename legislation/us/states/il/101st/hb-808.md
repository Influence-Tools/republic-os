# Illinois HB 808 (101st) — SOS-ID CARDS UNDER AGE 18

Amends the Illinois Identification Card Act. Reduces the fee for original, renewal, and duplicate standard Illinois Identification Cards issued to persons under 18 years of age from $10 to $5.
