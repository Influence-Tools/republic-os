# Illinois HB 347 (101st) — PROBATE-ASSAULT OF ELDERLY

Amends the Probate Act of 1975. Provides that a person convicted of assault, aggravated assault, battery, or aggravated battery of an elderly person shall not receive any property, benefit, or other interest by reason of the death of that elderly person.
