# Illinois HB 925 (101st) — MOBILE HOME-PENALTY AMOUNT

Amends the Mobile Home Local Services Tax Act. Provides that the penalty for delinquent local services taxes shall not exceed the lesser of $100 or 50% of the original tax imposed (currently, $100). Effective immediately.
