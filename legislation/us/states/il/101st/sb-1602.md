# Illinois SB 1602 (101st) — VEH CD-MOBILE HOME DEALERS

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Changes the definition of "community-based manufactured home dealer" to include a person who buys a new manufactured home or park model that is located within the manufactured home community pursuant to a franchise agreement or similar agreement with a manufacturer, or a used manufactured home or park model located within the manufactured home community, with either the new or used manufactured home also able to be located in a different manufactured home community that is owned or managed by the community-based manufactured home dealer. Effective immediately.
