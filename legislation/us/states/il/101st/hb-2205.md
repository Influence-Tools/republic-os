# Illinois HB 2205 (101st) — SCH CD-CHI-SCHOOL CLOSING

Amends the Chicago School District Article of the School Code. With regard to a proposed school closure, requires the chief executive officer to publish on the school district's website a full financial report on the closure that includes an analysis of the closure's costs and benefits to the district. Effective immediately.
