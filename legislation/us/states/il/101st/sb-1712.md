# Illinois SB 1712 (101st) — FOIA-PUBLIC BODY CREDIT CARDS

Amends the Freedom of Information Act. Exempts from disclosure a public body's credit card numbers, bank account numbers, and other confidential account information that the disclosure of which could produce public loss.
