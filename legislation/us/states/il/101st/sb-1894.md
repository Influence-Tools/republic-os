# Illinois SB 1894 (101st) — SPEC PLATES-POLICE/FIREFIGHTER

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that children and stepchildren, in addition to the spouse and parents, of a police officer or firefighter who has died in the line of duty may be issued special license plates.
