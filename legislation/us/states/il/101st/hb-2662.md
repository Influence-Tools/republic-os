# Illinois HB 2662 (101st) — PEN CD-CHI FIRE-BOARD TERMS

Amends the Chicago Firefighter Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Provides that the board shall conduct regular elections for the election of a successor to the annuitant member of the board for a term of 3 (rather than 2) years. Makes a conforming change. Effective immediately.
