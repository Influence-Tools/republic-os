# Illinois SB 52 (94th) — FOIA-FINANCIAL INFORMATION

Amends the Freedom of Information Act. In the Act's existing exemption of certain trade secrets and commercial or financial information obtained by a public body from inspection and copying requirements, states that the exemption specifically includes certain information relating to private equity funds.
