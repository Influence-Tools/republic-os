# Illinois HB 1403 (94th) — PENSION FUND BDS-FIREFIGHTERS

Amends the Downstate Firefighter Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Provides for the creation of boards of trustees for fire protection districts. Provides for the termination of the terms of office of the existing boards and for the appointment and election of new 5-member boards. Effective immediately.
