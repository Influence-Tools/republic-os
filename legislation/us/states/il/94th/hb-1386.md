# Illinois HB 1386 (94th) — VEH CD-TRUCK WEIGHTS-PERMITS

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Sets fees of $250 quarterly and $1,000 annually for special permits for continuous limited operation of trucks that exceed wheel and axle load limits. Provides that all single axles, excluding the steer axle, and axles within a tandem are limited to 24,000 pounds or less, unless otherwise noted in the new language. Provides that the permits allow loads up to 12 feet wide and 110 feet in length. Provides that front tag axle and double tandem trailers are not eligible for the permits.
