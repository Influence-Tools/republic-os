# Illinois HB 603 (94th) — INC TX-HOUSING DONATION CREDIT

Amends the Illinois Income Tax Act to end the tax credit for donations to certain affordable housing projects with the taxable year ending on December 31, 2011 (now, 2006). Effective immediately.
