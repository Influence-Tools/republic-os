# Illinois SB 185 (94th) — MWRD-CIVIL SVC BD PAY

Amends the Metropolitan Water Reclamation District Act. Deletes a provision that establishes a salary for each member of the civil service board of the Metropolitan Water Reclamation District. Provides that compensation for each member of the board shall be established by the District's Board of Commissioners as part of the annual budget process. Effective immediately.
