# Illinois SB 2159 (94th) — PROCUREMENT-PUBLIC DOCUMENTS

Amends the Illinois Procurement Code. Provides that pre-award, post-award, administration, and close-out documents relating to a contract must be public after that contract is awarded. Effective immediately.
