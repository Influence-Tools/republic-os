# Illinois HB 4728 (94th) — VEH CD-CARRIER REGISTRATION

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Amends the Illinois Commercial Transportation Law to provide that any interstate carrier that is required to register under federal law is required to register with the Illinois Commerce Commission.
