# Illinois SB 272 (94th) — MUNI CD-USE & OCC TAX

Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that the rates of the Non-Home Rule Municipal Retailers' Occupation Tax Act, the Non-Home Rule Municipal Service Occupation Tax Act, and the Non-Home Rule Municipal Use Tax Act may not exceed 1% (now, the rates may not exceed 1/2 of 1%).
