# Illinois HB 4202 (94th) — INS-MENTAL HEALTH-OUTPT TRTMNT

Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. Provides that a group health benefit plan shall provide coverage for 60 (now 35) visits for outpatient treatment of mental illness. Effective immediately.
