# Illinois SB 1752 (94th) — CIV PRO-EXEMPTION-MINIMUM WAGE

Amends the Code of Civil Procedure. Provides that the amount of wages that may be applied toward a judgment is limited to the lesser of (1) 15% of gross weekly wages or (2) the amount by which the disposable earnings for the week exceed the greater of 45 times the federal minimum hourly wage or the minimum hourly wage prescribed by the Minimum Wage Law. Effective immediately.
