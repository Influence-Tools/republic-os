# Illinois HB 445 (94th) — BOAT SAFETY-PADDLE BOATS

Amends the Boat Registration and Safety Act. Reduces the registration fee for a non-motorized paddle boat to a uniform $6 (down from $15 or $45, depending on the length of the watercraft).
