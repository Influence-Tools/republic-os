# Illinois SB 2740 (94th) — PENCD-ART 4-TAX LEVY

Amends the Downstate Firefighter Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Provides that the annual property tax levied for pension purposes tax shall be forwarded directly to the treasurer of the board within 30 business days of receipt by the county (was, by the municipality). Effective immediately.
