# Illinois HB 48 (94th) — LIQUOR CONTROL-EXCEPTION REP

Amends the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Repeals provisions allowing a township board to authorize the local liquor control commissioner to issue a specific special event retailer's license in any township that has voted to prohibit the retail sale of alcoholic liquor.
