# Illinois HB 1384 (94th) — PEN CD-SURS-MEDICARE

Amends the State Universities Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Directs the System to conduct a divided medicare coverage referendum, open to employees continuously employed by the same employer since March 31, 1986. Allows eligible employees to irrevocably elect to participate in the federal medicare program. Effective immediately.
