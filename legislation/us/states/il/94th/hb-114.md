# Illinois HB 114 (94th) — ELEC CD-PRISON DETAINEES

Amends the Election Code. Specifies that confinement or detention in a jail or prison pending acquittal or conviction of a crime is not a disqualification for voting. Makes such confinement or detention a specified reason for absentee voting.
