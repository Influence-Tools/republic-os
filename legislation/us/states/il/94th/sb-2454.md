# Illinois SB 2454 (94th) — LIQUOR-SALE AT HIST SITES

Amends the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Provides that alcoholic liquors may be delivered to and sold at retail or dispensed at facilities, properties, and buildings under the jurisdiction of the Historic Sites and Preservation Division of the Historic Preservation Agency or the Abraham Lincoln Presidential Library and Museum by an individual or organization (now, a not-for-profit organization).
