# Illinois HB 1395 (94th) — INTERGOV COOP-SPECIAL DISTRICT

Amends the Intergovernmental Cooperation Act. Permits a special district that is coterminous with or entirely within the boundaries of a township to merge into the township (now, permitted only in counties with less than 1,000,000 population).
