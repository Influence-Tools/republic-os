# Illinois HB 415 (94th) — MILITARY-FUNERAL DUTY PAY

Amends the Military Code of Illinois. Requires that a member of the Army National Guard or the Air National Guard ordered to funeral honors duty receive an allowance of $100 (rather than $50).
