# Illinois SB 127 (94th) — VEH CD-BLUE LIGHTS-STOP LAMPS

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that an antique vehicle may display a blue light or lights of up to one inch in diameter as part of the vehicle's rear stop lamp or lamps. Effective immediately.
