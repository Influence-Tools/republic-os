# Illinois HB 748 (94th) — FIRE MARSHAL-DIVISIONS

Amends the State Fire Marshal Act. Provides that certain duties are assumed by unnamed divisions (rather than the Division of Fire Prevention and the Division of Personnel Standards and Education). Provides that the divisions are headed by directors (rather than Deputy State Fire Marshals).
