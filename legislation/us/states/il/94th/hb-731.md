# Illinois HB 731 (94th) — ST EMPLOYEE INS-CNTY HOSPITAL

Amends the State Employees Group Insurance Act of 1971. Redefines a unit of local government to include a hospital provider owned by a county that has 100 or fewer hospital beds and that has not already joined the insurance program.
