# Illinois SB 1751 (94th) — WAGE ASSIGNMENT-MINIMUM WAGE

Amends the Illinois Wage Assignment Act. Provides that the maximum wages, salary, commissions, and bonuses that may be collected by an assignee for any work week shall not exceed the lesser of (1) 15% of the gross amount paid for that week or (2) the amount by which the disposable earnings for the week exceed the greater of 45 times the federal minimum hourly wage or the minimum hourly wage prescribed by the Minimum Wage Law. Effective immediately.
