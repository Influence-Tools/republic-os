# Illinois SB 297 (94th) — SCH CD-DRIV ED-PRACTIC DRIVING

Amends the School Code. Removes a provision that allows a school district to adopt a policy to permit proficiency examinations for the practice driving part of the driver education course at any time after the completion of 3 hours of practice driving under direct individual instruction.
