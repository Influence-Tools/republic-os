# Illinois HB 4541 (94th) — PENCD-CHIC TCHRS-ADMINISTRATOR

Amends the Chicago Teacher Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Adds to the definition of "administrator", a member who is not on the Chicago teachers' salary schedule. Effective immediately.
