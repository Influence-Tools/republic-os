# Illinois SB 2772 (94th) — NOT FOR PROFIT-HOMEOWNR MEETNG

Amends the General Not For Profit Corporation Act of 1986. Provides for open meetings for the listed type of meetings of the board of directors of a residential not-for-profit homeowners association. Effective immediately.
