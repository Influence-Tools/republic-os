# Illinois SB 1669 (94th) — PEACE OFFICER INTERROGATION

Amends the Uniform Peace Officers' Disciplinary Act. Requires that a peace officer under investigation shall be informed in writing (now, not required to be written) of the name, rank, and unit or command of the officer in charge of the investigation, the interrogators, and all persons who will be present on the behalf of the employer (now, all persons present during any interrogation) during any interrogation except at a public administrative proceeding.
