# Illinois HB 2490 (94th) — MEDICAID-ALTERNATE PAYEE

Amends the Illinois Public Aid Code. Authorizes the Department of Public Aid to permit an individual practitioner to designate that Medicaid payments due to the practitioner be paid to an alternate payee, and requires that alternate payees register with the Department. Imposes certain requirements on alternate payees. Provides that the Department may seek revocation of an alternate payee under certain conditions and may permanently prohibit owners, officers, or persons with management responsibility for an alternate payee from participating in the Medicaid program.
