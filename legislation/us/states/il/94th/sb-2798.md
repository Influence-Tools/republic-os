# Illinois SB 2798 (94th) — TECH RESCUE TEAMS-FUNDING

Amends the Counties Code. Provides that a county board may charge a fee to a party requiring the use of or causing the use of a technical rescue team to recover personnel, expendables, and equipment costs.
