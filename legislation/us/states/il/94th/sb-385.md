# Illinois SB 385 (94th) — IL BANKING-BOARD VACANCIES

Amends the Illinois Banking Act. Provides that, unless prohibited by (now, if authorized by) the bank's by-laws, the directors of a State bank may properly fill a vacancy arising between shareholders' meetings by appointment. Provides that any director appointed to fill a vacancy arising between shareholders' meetings shall serve until the next meeting of shareholders at which directors are elected.
