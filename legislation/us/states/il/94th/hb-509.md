# Illinois HB 509 (94th) — SEIDA ADD IRVINGTON TOWNSHIP

Amends the Southeastern Illinois Economic Development Authority Act. Adds Irvington Township in Washington County to the Authority's territorial jurisdiction. Adds Irvington Township in Washington County as an entity which has the power to enter into intergovernmental agreements. Effective immediately.
