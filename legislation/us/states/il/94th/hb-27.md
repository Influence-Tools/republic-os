# Illinois HB 27 (94th) — LOCGOV-LIQUOR REGULATION

Amends the Counties Code. Provides that a county board may license or regulate any business (i) that is operating as a public accommodation or an adult entertainment facility; (ii) that is located in an unincorporated area of the county; (iii) that permits the consumption of alcoholic liquor on the premises; and (iv) that is not licensed under the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that the corporate authorities of each municipality may license or regulate any business (i) that is operating as a public accommodation or an adult entertainment facility; (ii) that permits the consumption of alcoholic liquor on the premises; and (iii) that is not licensed under the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Effective immediately.
