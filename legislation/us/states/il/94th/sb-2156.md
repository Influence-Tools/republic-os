# Illinois SB 2156 (94th) — CRIM CD-DANGEROUS PLACE

Amends the Criminal Code of 1961. In the statute concerning the offense of use of a dangerous place for the commission of a controlled substance or cannabis offense, changes a reference from "premise" to "premises" in the definition of "place". Effective immediately.
