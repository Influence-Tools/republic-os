# Illinois SB 1907 (94th) — CD CIV PRO-RECORDS

Amends the Code of Civil Procedure examination of patient records provisions. Permits a person, entity, or organization presenting a valid signed authorization for the release of records to examine a health care facility's records of a patient. Provides that a written request by a person, entity, or organization presenting a valid signed authorization for the release of records shall be satisfied within 30 days of receipt.
