# Illinois HB 832 (94th) — CNTY CD-BUDGET

Amends the Counties Code. Provides that, in its annual budget, a county may appropriate an amount not to exceed 5% (now, 3%) of the amount appropriated to the county's general corporate or operating fund, for the purpose of making specified capital improvements, repairs, or replacements with respect to real property or equipment or other tangible personal property of the county. Effective immediately.
