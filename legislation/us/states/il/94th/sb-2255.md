# Illinois SB 2255 (94th) — MWRD-PERSONNEL

Amends the Metropolitan Water Reclamation District Act. In a Section relating to the examination of applicants for classified civil service positions, provides that applicants for the position of assistant director of personnel are not subject to examination. Effective immediately.
