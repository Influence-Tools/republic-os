# Illinois HB 295 (94th) — EPA - PESTICIDE DEALER REGISTR

Amends the Illinois Pesticide Act. Provides that beginning July 1, 2005, any pesticide dealer selling non-restricted use pesticides for use in the production of an agricultural commodity in containers with a capacity of 2.5 gallons or greater or 10 pounds or greater must register with the Department on forms provided by the Director. Effective immediately.
