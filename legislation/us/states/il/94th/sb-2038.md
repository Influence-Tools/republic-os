# Illinois SB 2038 (94th) — CONVEYANCE-KANE CO-RESTRICTION

Amends "An Act to authorize the Department of Mental Health to convey certain State-owned lands in Kane County", as amended. Provides that certain restrictions on the use of that land do not apply to a portion of that land. Effective immediately.
