# Illinois SB 2054 (94th) — PK DIST CD REFUNDING BONDS

Amends the Park District Code. Provides that refunding bonds issued under the Park District Refunding Bond Act that refund or continue to refund bonds approved by referendum do not limit the right of a park district to issue non-referendum bonds in accordance with this Section.
