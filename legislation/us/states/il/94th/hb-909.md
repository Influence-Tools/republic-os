# Illinois HB 909 (94th) — COUNTY ECON DEV-GRUNDY

Amends the County Economic Development Project Area Property Tax Allocation Act. Provides that the corporate authorities of Grundy County may, by ordinance, propose the establishment of an economic development project and fix a time and place for a public hearing. Provides if an ordinance if adopted, Grundy County shall submit a certified copy of the ordinance, as adopted, to the Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity.
