# Illinois HB 4308 (94th) — SCH CD-TEACH EXCEL PROG-APPROP

Amends the School Code. Requires the State Board of Education's annual budget to set out by separate line item the appropriation for the Illinois Teaching Excellence Program. Effective immediately.
