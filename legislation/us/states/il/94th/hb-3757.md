# Illinois HB 3757 (94th) — IFA-FIRE TRUCK LOAN PROGRAM

Amends the Illinois Finance Authority Act. Provides for the continuation and re-enactment of the fire truck revolving loan program, formerly administered under the Rural Bond Bank Act (now repealed). Provides for joint administration of the program by the Illinois Finance Authority and the State Fire Marshal. Validates loans made and actions taken under the original enactment after its repeal. Effective immediately.
