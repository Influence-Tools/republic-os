# Illinois HB 2351 (94th) — VEH CD-TINTED WINDOWS-LUPUS

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that the prohibition against tinted windshields and tinted front side windows does not apply to vehicles owned and operated by persons afflicted with systemic lupus erythematosus or used to transport those persons. Provides that the Secretary of State shall issue distinctive registration stickers for the exempted vehicles. Effective immediately.
