# Illinois HB 3485 (94th) — ONE DAY REST-HOTEL ATTENDANT

Amends the One Day Rest In Seven Act. Provides that a hotel room attendant shall receive specified break periods. Provides that an employer may not require an employee to work during a break period. Provides that an employer of hotel room attendants shall make available a break room meeting specified requirements. Provides that an employer of hotel room attendants shall keep a complete and accurate record of the break periods. Provides for enforcement and penalties. Effective immediately.
