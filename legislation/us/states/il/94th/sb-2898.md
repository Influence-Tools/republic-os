# Illinois SB 2898 (94th) — SCH CD-ADMINISTER ALLERGY MED

Amends the School Code. In provisions that require a public or nonpublic school to permit the self-administration of medication by a pupil with asthma, adds the requirement that the school also permit the self-administration of medication by a pupil with allergies. Amends the State Mandates Act to require implementation without reimbursement. Effective immediately.
