# Illinois HB 1132 (94th) — VEH CD-DUI-5TH CONVICT CLSS X

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that any person convicted a sixth or subsequent time of driving under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or intoxicating compounds is guilty of a Class X felony.
