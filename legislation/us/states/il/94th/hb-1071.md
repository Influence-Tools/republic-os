# Illinois HB 1071 (94th) — PROC CD-SPAM FREE E-MAIL

Amends the Illinois Procurement Code. Requires that electronic mail service provided under State contracts must be provided reasonably free of unsolicited electronic mail advertisements (sometimes known as "spam").
