# Illinois HB 4196 (94th) — TWP CD BUILDING DEMOLITION

Amends the Township Code. Deletes the sunset provision that limits, as of January 1, 2006, the authority of a township to formally request that the county board commence specified proceedings concerning demolition, repair, or enclosure of dangerous and unsafe or uncompleted and abandoned buildings that are located outside of any municipality but within the township and, if the county declines the request, that the township may commence such proceedings. Effective immediately.
