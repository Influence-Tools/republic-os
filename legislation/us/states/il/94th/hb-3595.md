# Illinois HB 3595 (94th) — LOCAL GOVT TORT-PUBLIC ENTITY

Amends the Local Government and Governmental Employees Tort Immunity Act. Changes the definition of "local public entity" to include trustees of schools of townships and the treasurers of schools of townships. Effective immediately.
