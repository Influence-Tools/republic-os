# Illinois HB 601 (94th) — AG-SOYBEAN MARKETING BOARD

Amends the Soybean Marketing Act. Adds 6 at large members to the soybean marketing program operating board of directors. Provides for their election by producers at annual meetings from nominees selected by the operating board.
