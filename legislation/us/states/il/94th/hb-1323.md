# Illinois HB 1323 (94th) — CONSERVATION DIST BID LIMIT

Amends the Conservation District Act. Provides that contracts for supplies, material, or work in excess of $20,000 (now, $10,000) shall be let to lowest responsible bidder. Effective immediately.
