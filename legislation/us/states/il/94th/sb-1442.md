# Illinois SB 1442 (94th) — DCEO-FOOD BANK GRANTS

Amends the The Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Creates the Feed Illinois Fund as a special fund in the State treasury. Provides that, from appropriations from the Fund, the Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity must make grants to public or private food banks in Illinois for the purpose of making capital improvements and acquiring other non-food assets.
