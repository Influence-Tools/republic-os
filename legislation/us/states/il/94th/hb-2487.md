# Illinois HB 2487 (94th) — AGENCY REPORTS- ELECTRONIC

Amends the General Assembly Organization Act and the Legislative Commission Reorganization Act of 1984. Requires an electronic copy of all reports filed with the General Assembly to be provided to the Legislative Research Unit. Requires the Unit to post reports on the General Assembly's website.
