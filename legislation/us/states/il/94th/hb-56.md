# Illinois HB 56 (94th) — DORMITORY-FIRE EXTINGUISHER

Amends the Fire Sprinkler Dormitory Act. Provides that nothing in the Act may be construed to abrogate any statute, rule, or ordinance requiring that a fire extinguisher be present in a dormitory. Effective immediately.
