# Illinois HB 3532 (94th) — METH LAB CLEANUP PROTOCOL

Amends the Department of State Police Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois and the Environmental Protection Act. Provides that the Department of State Police and the Environmental Protection Agency shall jointly develop a protocol to be followed in determining whether a dwelling, structure, or other site at which methamphetamine had been illegally manufactured may again be safely put to its intended legal use. Provides that the Department of State Police and the Environmental Protection Agency must develop the protocol by January 1, 2006 and post the protocol on their official Web sites. Provides that the owner or lessor of a dwelling, structure, or other site at which methamphetamine had been illegally manufactured without the knowledge of the owner or lessor is immune from any civil liability that may result from conditions arising from the illegal manufacture of methamphetamine if the owner or lessor cleaned up the dwelling, structure, or other site in accordance with the protocol.
