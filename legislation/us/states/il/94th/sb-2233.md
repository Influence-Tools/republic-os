# Illinois SB 2233 (94th) — VEH CD&COLLISION REPAIR ACT

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that all automotive repairers and rebuilders shall provide proof that they comply with all the requirements of the Automotive Collision Repair Act. Amends the Automotive Collision Repair Act. Provides that, if a person is engaged in activities associated with automotive collision repair as covered in the Act, that person shall comply with the provisions of the Act.
