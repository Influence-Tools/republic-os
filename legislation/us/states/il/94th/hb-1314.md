# Illinois HB 1314 (94th) — WILDLIFE-NON-RESIDENT AUCTION

Amends the Wildlife Code. Provides that any person who does not possess a non-resident fur buyer permit must obtain a non-resident auction participation permit to deal in green hides at auctions. Sets fees. Makes other changes.
