# Illinois HB 3821 (94th) — EDU-COLLEGE STDNT IMMUNIZATION

Amends the College Student Immunization Act. Excludes private colleges and universities that do not provide on-campus housing from a definition of "post-secondary educational institution". Effective immediately.
