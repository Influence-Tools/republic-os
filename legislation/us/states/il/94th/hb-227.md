# Illinois HB 227 (94th) — PENCD-ART 14-VIS IMPAIRED VNDR

Amends the State Employees Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Provides that an employee can establish service credit for a period of up to 8 years during which he or she was employed by the Visually Handicapped Managers of Illinois in a vending program operated under a contractual agreement with the Department of Rehabilitation Services or its successor agency. Effective immediately.
