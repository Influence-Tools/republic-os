# Illinois SB 2277 (94th) — HORSERACING-PAYMNT TO PARKS

Amends the Illinois Horse Racing Act of 1975. Provides for redistribution of certain moneys paid to museums and aquariums located in park districts of over 500,000 population. Effective immediately.
