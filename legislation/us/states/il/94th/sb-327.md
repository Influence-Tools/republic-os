# Illinois SB 327 (94th) — LIQUOR-SALE- U OF I

Amends the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Allows a restaurant that is operated by a commercial tenant in the North Campus Parking Deck building that is located at 1201 West University Avenue, Urbana, Illinois and is owned by the Board of Trustees of the University of Illinois to serve and take deliveries of alcoholic liquor. Effective immediately.
