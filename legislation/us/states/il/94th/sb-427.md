# Illinois SB 427 (94th) — SCHL CD-PRINCIPALS-RECLASSIFY

Amends the School Code. In a Section regarding reclassification of principals in school districts other than the Chicago school district, provides that upon non-renewal of a principal's administrative contract, the principal shall be reclassified pursuant to the Section.
