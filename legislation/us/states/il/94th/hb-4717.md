# Illinois HB 4717 (94th) — DUI-LOCAL PROSECUTION

Amends the Illinois Municipal Code and the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that a municipality may charge an offender with a municipal misdemeanor offense for conduct that would constitute felony driving under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or intoxicating compounds, if the State's Attorney rejects or denies felony charges for that conduct. Effective immediately.
