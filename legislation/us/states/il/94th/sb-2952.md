# Illinois SB 2952 (94th) — GEOGRAPHIC INFO CNCL RE-ENACT

Re-enacts and amends the Illinois Geographic Information Council Act. The Act was created by Public Act 88-669, which has been held to be unconstitutional as a violation of the single subject clause of the Illinois Constitution. Includes validation provisions. Also makes revisory changes. Effective immediately.
