# Illinois SB 122 (94th) — LAND CONVEYANCE-TINLEY PARK

Authorizes the Secretary of Human Services to convey the specified property to the Village of Tinley Park for public roadway purposes and other non-profit uses. Provides that any part of the specified property that ceases to be used for public roadway purposes or non-profit uses shall revert back to the State. Effective immediately.
