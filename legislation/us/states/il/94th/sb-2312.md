# Illinois SB 2312 (94th) — BD HIGHER ED-NONTRAD STU MEMBR

Amends the Board of Higher Education Act. Adds an additional student member to the Board of Higher Education, which person must be a non-traditional undergraduate student who is at least 24 years old and represents the views of non-traditional students, such as a person who is employed or is a parent.
