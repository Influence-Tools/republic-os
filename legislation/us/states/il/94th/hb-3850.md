# Illinois HB 3850 (94th) — TIF EXTEND-OGLESBY

Amends the Tax Increment Allocation Redevelopment Act of the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that the redevelopment project in the TIF District created by an ordinance adopted on December 31, 1986 by the City of Oglesby must be completed by December 31 of the 35th year (now, the 23rd year) after the year in which the ordinance was adopted. Effective immediately.
