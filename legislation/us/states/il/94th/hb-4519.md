# Illinois HB 4519 (94th) — MORTGAGE ESCROW-TAX PAYMENTS

Amends the Mortgage Escrow Account Act. In a Section concerning a mortgage lender providing notice of tax payments, provides that notice to a borrower must be on at least an annual basis.
