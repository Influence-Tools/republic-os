# Illinois SB 2489 (94th) — VEH CD-TERMINAL SECURITY

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that the owner of a terminal is expressly authorized, within the terminal property, to construct and properly operate and maintain specified security measures to protect the terminal property. Effective immediately.
