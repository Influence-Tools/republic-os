# Illinois SB 1625 (94th) — BONDS-AGGREGATE PRINCIPAL-IFA

Amends the Illinois Finance Authority Act. Provides that the Authority may not have outstanding at any one time bonds for any of its corporate purposes in an aggregate principal amount exceeding $29,000,000,000 (now, $23,000,000,000). Effective immediately.
