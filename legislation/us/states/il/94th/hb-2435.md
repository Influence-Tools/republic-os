# Illinois HB 2435 (94th) — SIU-DEMUZIO LEGIS INTERN PROG

Amends the Southern Illinois University Management Act. Subject to appropriation, requires the president of the University to create the Vince Demuzio Legislative Internship Program for full-time undergraduate students. Sets forth goals for the program. Provides that participating students shall receive academic credit and be compensated financially. Effective immediately.
