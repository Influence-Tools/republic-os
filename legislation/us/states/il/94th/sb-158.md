# Illinois SB 158 (94th) — PODIATRY-DISCIPLINARY FUND

Amends the Podiatric Medical Practice Act of 1987. Provides that of the moneys deposited into the Illinois State Podiatric Disciplinary Fund, during each 2-year renewal period, $200,000 (rather than 15%) of the money received from the payment of renewal fees shall be used for certain scholarships, residency programs, and other expenses. Effective immediately.
