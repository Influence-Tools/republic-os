# Illinois HB 5249 (94th) — CRIM CD-SEX OFFENDER-DAY CARE

Amends the Criminal Code of 1961. Provides that it is a Class 4 felony for a child sex offender to knowingly operate, manage, be employed by, volunteer at, be associated with, or knowingly be present at a day care center, a part day child care facility, or a school providing before and after school programs for children under 18 years of age, with certain exceptions.
