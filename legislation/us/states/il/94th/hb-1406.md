# Illinois HB 1406 (94th) — PUB AID-TANF-RULES-REPEAL

Amends the Illinois Public Aid Code. In provisions concerning rules regulating the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF) program, eliminates provisions (i) repealing all rules regulating that program on July 1, 2006 and (ii) prohibiting the adoption of rules regulating that program after that date.
