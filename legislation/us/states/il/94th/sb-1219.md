# Illinois SB 1219 (94th) — CONDO ACT-APARTMENT CONVERSION

Amends the Condominium Property Act. Provides that, in the case of the conversion of an apartment building into condominium units, a municipality shall have the right to inspect the apartment building prior to the conversion to condominium units and may require that each new proposed condominium unit comply with the current life safety, building, and zoning codes of the municipality. Effective immediately.
