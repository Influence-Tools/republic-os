# Illinois HB 872 (94th) — PLUMBING CONTRACTORS-INSURANCE

Amends the Illinois Plumbing License Law. Makes changes concerning the amount of general liability, property damage, and workers compensation insurance coverage that a plumbing contractor must carry. Effective immediately.
