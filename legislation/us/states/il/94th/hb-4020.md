# Illinois HB 4020 (94th) — CRIM CD-VEH TRESPASS CIVIL

Amends the Criminal Code of 1961. Provides that a person may be liable in any civil action for money damages to the owner of the land he or she entered upon with a motor vehicle. Provides the measure of damages an owner may recover. Provides that if the person operating the vehicle is under the age of 16, the owner of the vehicle and the parent or legal guardian of the minor are jointly and severally liable. Defines "land", "owner", and "vehicle".
