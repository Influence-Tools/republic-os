# Illinois SB 2613 (94th) — CRIM CD-ELECTRONIC CONTRABAND

Amends the Criminal Code of 1961. Re-enacts the provisions of Public Act 89-688 relating to bringing contraband into a penal institution; possessing contraband in a penal institution; and unauthorized bringing of contraband into a penal institution by an employee. (Public Act 89-688 was held unconstitutional as violating the single subject clause of the Illinois Constitution). Effective immediately.
