# Illinois HB 360 (94th) — MARRIAGE ACT-CHILD COUNSELING

Amends the Illinois Marriage and Dissolution of Marriage Act. Requires the court to determine whether counseling is appropriate for a child who is involved in a custody proceeding. Authorizes the court to order counseling and apportion the cost between the parties.
