# Illinois SB 293 (94th) — SCHL CON-PROJECT LABOR AGREE

Amends the School Construction Law. Defines "project labor agreement". Provides that the State Board of Education and the Capital Development Board shall not require a project labor agreement for any school construction project grant or debt service grant provided under the Law.
