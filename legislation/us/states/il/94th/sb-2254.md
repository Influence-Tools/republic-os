# Illinois SB 2254 (94th) — DHS-BEHAVIORAL SUPPORT PLANS

Amends the Mental Health and Developmental Disabilities Administrative Act. Requires each individual in a community-based residential program for individuals with developmental disabilities who exhibits behavioral challenges to have an individualized behavioral support plan. Sets forth requirements for the plan. Prohibits certain types of interventions. Sets forth provisions concerning the application of other statutes. Effective immediately.
