# Illinois SB 21 (94th) — PENCD-CHI POLICE-CAPTAINS

Amends the Chicago Police Article of the Illinois Pension Code to provide that the provision prohibiting certain policemen who are assigned to non-civil service positions from making certain contributions does not apply to a policeman assigned to a non-civil service position with the title of Captain. Amends the State Mandates Act to require implementation without reimbursement. Effective immediately.
