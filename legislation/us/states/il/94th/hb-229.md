# Illinois HB 229 (94th) — AG-AG AREA DESIGNATION

Amends the Agricultural Areas Conservation and Protection Act. For purposes of designating an agricultural area, requires that any non-contiguous portion must be within 1.5 miles of the nearest portion of the area.
