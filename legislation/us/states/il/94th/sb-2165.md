# Illinois SB 2165 (94th) — CONDO PROP ACT-RELIGIOUS ITEMS

Amends the Condominium Property Act. Provides that the Board of Managers may not make a rule or regulation that shall prohibit any reasonable accommodation for religious practices, including the attachment of religiously-mandated objects to the front door area of a condominium unit.
