# Illinois HB 210 (94th) — NUTRITION - EARLY LEARNING

Amends the Illinois Early Learning Council Act. Provides that the Council shall act in coordination with the Interagency Nutrition Council when dealing with activities related to nutrition, nutrition education, and physical activity.
