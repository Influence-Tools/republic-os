# Illinois HB 4645 (99th) — CONSERV FOUND BD OF DIRECTORS

Amends the Illinois Conservation Foundation Act. Ends the terms of the members of the Illinois Conservation Foundation Board of Directors on the date upon which members of the new 12-member board have been appointed and can begin to take action as a Board. Establishes terms for the new members of the Illinois Conservation Foundation Board of Directors.
