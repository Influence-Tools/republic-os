# Illinois HB 3268 (99th) — DEPT OF HUMAN RIGHTS-POWERS

Amends the Illinois Human Rights Act. Provides that the Department of Human Rights has the power to coordinate with state (in addition to federal and local) agencies in conformity with the Act. Effective immediately.
