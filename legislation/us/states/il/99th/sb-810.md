# Illinois SB 810 (99th) — PUBLIC ADJUSTER RETESTING

Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. In provisions concerning the written examination for a public adjuster license, reduces the waiting period for retaking the examination for an individual who fails from 90 days to 7 days.
