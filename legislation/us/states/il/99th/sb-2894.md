# Illinois SB 2894 (99th) — PEN CD-IMRF-RETRO SURVIVOR BEN

Amends the Illinois Municipal Retirement Fund (IMRF) Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Deletes the one-year limit on backdating a survivor benefit. Effective immediately.
