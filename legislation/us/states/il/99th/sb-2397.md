# Illinois SB 2397 (99th) — LOTTERY-QUALITY OF LIFE

Amends the Illinois Lottery Law. Provides that the Quality of Life scratch-off game shall be discontinued on December 31, 2025 (rather than December 31, 2017). Effective immediately.
