# Illinois HB 3721 (99th) — NATIONAL GUARD-REEMPLOYMENT

Amends the Service Member's Employment Tenure Act. Expands the definition of "military service" to include any period of active duty by members of the National Guard who are called to active duty pursuant to an order of the Governor of this State or an order of a governor of any other state as provided by law. Effective immediately.
