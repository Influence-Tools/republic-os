# Illinois HB 4603 (99th) — CNTY CD-PUBLIC DEFENDER REPORT

Amends the Counties Code. Requires Public Defenders in counties with a population under 1,000,000 to report quarterly (currently, monthly) to the county board. Effective immediately.
