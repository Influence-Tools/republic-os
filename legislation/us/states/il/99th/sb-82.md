# Illinois SB 82 (99th) — SCH CD-BOARD ELECTIONS

Amends the School Code. Permits a congressional township comprising the territory of a school district to pass, by a 2/3 vote of all voters voting on the question, a proposition to elect board members at large and without restriction by area of residence within the district.
