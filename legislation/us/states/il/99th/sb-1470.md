# Illinois SB 1470 (99th) — MUNI CD-BUDGET DIRECTOR

Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that a budget officer of a municipality of less than 500,000 may hold another municipal office, either elected or appointed, including, but not limited to, the office of the mayor or president. Effective immediately
