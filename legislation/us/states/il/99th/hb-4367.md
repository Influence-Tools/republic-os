# Illinois HB 4367 (99th) — SCH CD-READING ADVISORY GROUP

Amends the School Code. Requires the State Board of Education to reestablish the reading instruction advisory group abolished on December 15, 2015 to complete the abolished group's work. Provides that the reestablished advisory group shall complete its work before December 31, 2016 and is abolished on December 31, 2016. Effective immediately.
