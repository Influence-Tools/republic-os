# Illinois HB 4112 (99th) — POLICE-CRISIS INTERVENTION

Amends the Illinois Police Training Act. Provides that the Illinois Law Enforcement Training and Standards Board shall create a standard curriculum for a certified training program in crisis intervention addressing specialized policing responses to people with mental illnesses. Further provides that officers who have successfully completed this program shall be issued a certificate attesting to their attendance of a Crisis Intervention Team (CIT) training program.
