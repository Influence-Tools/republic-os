# Illinois SB 1458 (99th) — OIL AND GAS REGULATORY FUND

Amends the State Finance Act. Renames a fund as the Oil and Gas Resource Management Fund (now, Mines and Minerals). Makes corresponding changes. Amends the Hydraulic Fracturing Regulatory Act. Provides that the Fund will be used to support the Division of Oil and Gas Resource Management (now, Office of Mines and Mineral). Effective immediately.
