# Illinois HB 200 (99th) — AED GRANTS TO PUBLIC LIBRARIES

Amends the Department of Public Health Powers and Duties Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Provides that public libraries may receive grants from the Heartsaver AED Fund to assist in the purchase of an Automated External Defibrillator. Effective immediately.
