# Illinois SB 3063 (99th) — IDOT & DNR LAND CONVEYANCES

Authorizes the Department of Transportation to convey certain parcels of land in the counties of Grundy and Madison. Authorizes the Department of Natural Resources to convey, for so long as the property is used for public purposes, certain parcels of land in the counties of Bureau and Stephenson. Effective immediately.
