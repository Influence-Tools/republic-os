# Illinois SB 1308 (99th) — PROBATE-CITATIONS TO RECOVER

Amends the Probate Act of 1975. Provides that the court shall order a citation to issue for the appearance before it of any person whom the petitioner believes may be liable to the estate of a ward pursuant to any civil cause of action. Makes other changes concerning persons who may be issued a citation.
