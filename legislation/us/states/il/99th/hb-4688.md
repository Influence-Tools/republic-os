# Illinois HB 4688 (99th) — PUBLIC WATER SUPPLY REGULATION

Amends the Public Water Supply Regulation Act. Provides that the owners or official custodians of public water supplies shall comply with the recommendations on optimal fluoridation for community water levels as proposed and adopted by the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services. Effective immediately.
