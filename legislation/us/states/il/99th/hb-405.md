# Illinois HB 405 (99th) — PARK DISTRICT LAND-SELLING

Amends the Park Commissioners Land Sale Act. Provides that notice of an application for the sale of park district land not exceeding 3 acres shall be given at least ten days before the day of the hearing on the application. Provides that all interested persons may appear at the hearing of the application in Circuit Court. Effective immediately.
