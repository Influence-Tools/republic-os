# Illinois HB 4115 (99th) — AGR/DHS-GAS STATION INFO

Amends the Motor Fuel Sales Act. Provides that: the Department of Agriculture shall maintain on its website a list of gasoline and service stations that are required to report to the Department of Agriculture's Bureau of Weights and Measures; the list shall include the addresses and telephone numbers of the gasoline and service stations; and the Department of Agriculture shall provide the Department of Human Services with a link to this website information. Provides that the Department of Human Services shall post on its website a link to the list of gasoline and service stations provided by the Department of Agriculture (rather than posting the addresses and telephone numbers of all gasoline and service stations in Illinois).
