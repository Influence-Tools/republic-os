# Illinois HB 2705 (99th) — DCFS-GRANDPARENT VISITATION

Amends the Children and Family Services Act. Requires the Department of Children and Family Services to establish rules granting visitation privileges to a non-custodial grandparent of a child who is in the care and custody of the Department. Effective immediately.
