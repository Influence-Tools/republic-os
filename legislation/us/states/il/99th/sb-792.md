# Illinois SB 792 (99th) — PROP TX-TRUTH IN TAXATION

Amends the truth in taxation Division of the Property Tax Code. Provides that if a taxing district maintains a website, the truth in taxation notice shall be published on the website in addition to a newspaper of general circulation.
