# Illinois HB 6325 (99th) — CD CORR-SPAC MEMBERS

Amends the Unified Code of Corrections. Deletes provision that 2 sitting judges shall serve on the Illinois Sentencing Policy Advisory Council. Provides that the retired judges on the Council shall be selected by certain other members of the Council (rather than by the Chief Justice of the Illinois Supreme Court). Effective immediately.
