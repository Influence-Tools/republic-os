# Illinois HB 5910 (99th) — FED LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES

Amends the Criminal Code of 2012. Changes references to various federal law enforcement agencies from their previous names to their current names. Includes in the definition of "peace officer" under the Criminal Code of 2012 for particular purposes: (1) Amtrak Police; (2) Federal Reserve Police; (3) the United States Department of Veterans Affairs Police; and (4) U.S. Probation and Pre-trial Services. Amends the Federal Law Enforcement Officer Immunity Act to make conforming changes.
