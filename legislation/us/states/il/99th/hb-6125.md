# Illinois HB 6125 (99th) — LIQUOR-LICENSE EXEMPTION

Amends the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Authorizes the issuance and renewal of a license to sell alcoholic liquor at premises located within 100 feet of a specific church and school located in the City of Chicago and a specific home for indigent persons or a specific church located in the City of Chicago. Effective immediately.
