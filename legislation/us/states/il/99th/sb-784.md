# Illinois SB 784 (99th) — CMS LAND CONVEYANCE-MARION

Authorizes the Department of Central Management Services to convey certain described real estate to the Rides Mass Transit District for $1. Provides that the property shall revert to the State if the property is no longer used for public purposes. Effective immediately.
