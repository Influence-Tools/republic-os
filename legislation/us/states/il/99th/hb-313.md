# Illinois HB 313 (99th) — OIL AND GAS RIGHTS-ANNUAL COMP

Amends the Oil and Gas Rights Act. Provides that payments may be remitted to payees annually for the aggregate of up to 12 months' accumulation of proceeds, if the total amount owed is $100 (instead of $25) or less.
