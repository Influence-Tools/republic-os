# Illinois HB 373 (99th) — GOV BDS&COMMS-MEETING NOTICES

Amends the Gubernatorial Boards and Commissions Act. Requires the Governor's Office of Boards and Commissions to establish and maintain on the Internet: (1) a centralized location for an electronic mail listserv for users to receive notices of the meetings of each board and commission and their agendas; and (2) a listing of the meetings times and agendas for each board and commission. Provides that the Office shall provide and post that information at least 48 hours before each meeting. Defines "electronic mail listserv".
