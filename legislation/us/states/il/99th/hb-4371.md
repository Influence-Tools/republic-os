# Illinois HB 4371 (99th) — WATER AUTHORITIES-DISSOLVE

Amends the Water Authorities Act. Provides that a water authority may be dissolved upon referendum of the electors of the water authority. Provides that the business affairs of the water authority shall be closed after approval of a referendum dissolving the water authority and for the disposition of property following dissolution. Further provides that any money remaining shall be paid to the school treasurer of the school district or districts in which the water authority was situated. Effective immediately.
