# Illinois SB 2186 (99th) — SCH CD-ZONING COMPLIANCE

Amends the School Code. Provides that a school district is subject to and its school board must comply with any valid local government zoning ordinance or resolution that applies where the pertinent part of the school district is located. Effective immediately.
