# Illinois SB 1806 (99th) — INS-GUARANTY FUND REPORT DATE

Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. Provides that the board of directors of the Insurance Guaranty Fund shall submit a financial report to the Director of Insurance no later than April 30 (previously March 30) of each year.
