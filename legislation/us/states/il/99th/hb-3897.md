# Illinois HB 3897 (99th) — ISAC-STUDENT MEMBER-SCHOLARSHP

Amends the Higher Education Student Assistance Act. Removes the prohibition on allowing the student member of the Illinois Student Assistance Commission to receive a scholarship or grant pursuant to the Act during his or her term of office with the Commission. Effective immediately.
