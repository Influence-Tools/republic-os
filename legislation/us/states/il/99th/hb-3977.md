# Illinois HB 3977 (99th) — CRIM PRO- HEARSAY EXCEP REPEAL

Amends the Code of Criminal Procedure of 1963. Repeals evidentiary provisions providing a hearsay exception for intentional murder of a witness and admissibility of prior statements of an unavailable witness whose absence was wrongfully procured. Effective immediately.
