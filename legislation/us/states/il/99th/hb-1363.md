# Illinois HB 1363 (99th) — PARK DISTRICT CD-JOINT PROGRAM

Amends the Park District Code. Provides that park districts may develop, operate, finance, and participate in joint recreational programs with one or more (instead of contiguous) park districts, cities, city recreation commissions, forest preserve districts, conservation districts, school districts, or other municipal or quasi-municipal governments. Effective immediately.
