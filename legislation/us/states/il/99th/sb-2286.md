# Illinois SB 2286 (99th) — TRAFFICKING NOTICE - HOTELS

Amends the Human Trafficking Resource Center Notice Act. Provides that the notice required to be posted under the Act shall be posted, among other places, in the public restrooms of motels and hotels in clear view of the public and employees where similar notices are customarily posted.
