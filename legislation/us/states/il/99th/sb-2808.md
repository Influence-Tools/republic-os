# Illinois SB 2808 (99th) — VEH CD-SALVAGE TITLE

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that if the cost of repairing the damage to a vehicle, including labor, would be greater than 70% (rather than 50%) of its fair market value without that damage, the vehicle shall be considered salvage.
