# Illinois HB 3503 (99th) — DHS-DHFS-LONG-TERM CARE-REPORT

Amends the Illinois Public Aid Code. In provisions concerning data jointly compiled by the Department of Human Services and the Department of Healthcare and Family Services on pending applications for long-term care eligibility, adds data on pending denials, appeals, and redeterminations to the list of data the Departments must compile into a monthly report which shall be posted on each Department's website. Effective immediately.
