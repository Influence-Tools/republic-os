# Illinois HB 6181 (99th) — SCH CD-PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMEN

Amends provisions concerning educator licensure in the School Code. Provides that the State Board of Education shall allow educators to earn professional development hours during the final 3 months of the school year and enter those professional development hours into the Educator Licensure Information System.
