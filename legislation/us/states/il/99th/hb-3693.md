# Illinois HB 3693 (99th) — TWP CD-DISSOLUTION INTO MUNI

Amends the Township Code. Provides that certain townships that are: (1) within a coterminous, or substantially coterminous, municipality; (2) in a county with a population of 270,000 or more; and (3) 23 square miles or more may adopt an ordinance to discontinue and abolish the township organization and transfer all the rights, powers, duties, assets, property, liabilities, obligations, and responsibilities of the township to the coterminous municipality. Further provides for procedures for the retention of township records and the disposition of any of the property belonging to the township. Amends the Property Tax Code, the Public Health District Act, the Illinois Public Aid Code, and the Illinois Highway Code making conforming changes. Effective immediately.
