# Illinois SB 2817 (99th) — PEN CD-COOK CO-PURCHASE CREDIT

Amends the Cook County Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Limits a provision allowing purchase of credit for certain former county service to a person who first becomes a contributor before the effective date of the amendatory Act. Defines the term "in service". Effective immediately.
