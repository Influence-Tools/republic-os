# Illinois HB 2814 (99th) — RESIDENTIAL MORTGAGE LICENSE

Amends the Residential Mortgage License Act of 1987. Provides that a mortgage loan brokered, funded, originated, serviced, or purchased by a party who is not licensed shall not be held to be invalid solely on the basis of specified violations of the Act. Effective immediately.
