# Illinois HB 4391 (99th) — TWP CD-CRIMINAL CONVICTIONS

Amends the Township Code. Provides that a person is not eligible to hold any office if that person, at the time required for taking the oath of office, has been convicted in any court located in the United States of any infamous crime, bribery, perjury, or other felony. Effective immediately.
