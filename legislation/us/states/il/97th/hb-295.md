# Illinois HB 295 (97th) — SEX OFFENDER REG-HIGHER ED

Amends the Sex Offender Registration Act. Provides that a sex offender shall also register with the public safety or security director of the institution of higher education which he or she is employed at or attends. Provides that the registration fees shall only apply to the municipality or county of primary registration, and not to campus registration.
