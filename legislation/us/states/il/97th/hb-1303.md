# Illinois HB 1303 (97th) — FIRE SAFETY-MUTUAL AID BOX SYS

Amends the Fire Investigation Act. Authorizes moneys in the Fire Prevention Fund to be used, subject to appropriation, for the maintenance, operation, and capital expenses of the Mutual Aid Box Alarm System (MABAS).
