# Illinois SB 3726 (97th) — CIV PRO-SUP CT RPT-ARBITRATION

Amends the Code of Civil Procedure. Repeals a provision requiring the Supreme Court to evaluate the effectiveness of mandatory court-annexed arbitration and report the results of the evaluation to the General Assembly annually. Effective immediately.
