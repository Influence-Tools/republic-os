# Illinois SB 3184 (97th) — PARK DIST-CAROL STREAM-BONDS

Amends the Park District Code. Provides that bonds issued by the Carol Stream Park District approved by referendum at the February 2, 2010 general primary election are not subject to the certain percentage limitations imposed, and shall not be considered as part of the existing principal indebtedness of that district.
