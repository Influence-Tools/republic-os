# Illinois HB 91 (97th) — DCFS-ABUSE PREVENTION PLAN

Amends the Children and Family Services Act. Provides that on or before the first Friday in October (rather than April) of each year, the Department of Children and Family Services shall submit to the Governor and the General Assembly a State comprehensive child abuse and neglect prevention plan. Effective immediately.
