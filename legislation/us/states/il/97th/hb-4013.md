# Illinois HB 4013 (97th) — RECYCLABLE METAL - COPPER

Amends the Recyclable Metal Purchase Registration Law. Provides that recyclable metal dealers in this State shall enter specified identifying information into forms provided by the Department of State Police and shall keep a copy of the form for a specified period for each transaction involving the purchase of recyclable metal containing copper in addition to purchases of recyclable metal valued at $100 or more and metal street signs.
