# Illinois HB 1215 (97th) — MUNI CD-TIF EXTENSION-MARKHAM

Amends the Tax Increment Allocation Redevelopment Act in the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that the redevelopment project in the TIF district created by an ordinance that was adopted on June 1, 1994 by the City of Markham must be completed by December 31 of the 35th year (now, the 23rd year) after the year in which the ordinance was adopted.
