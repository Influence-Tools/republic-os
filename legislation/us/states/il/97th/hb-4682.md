# Illinois HB 4682 (97th) — OPPORTUNITY TASK FORCE-SUPPORT

Amends the Employment and Economic Opportunity for Persons with Disabilities Task Force Act. Provides that the Employment and Economic Opportunity for Persons with Disabilities Task Force shall operate with administrative support from the Illinois Department of Employment Security (rather than operate under the auspices of the Governor's office). Effective immediately.
