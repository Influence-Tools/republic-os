# Illinois SB 2162 (97th) — VEH CD-SHERIFF PLTS:NO FEE

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that beginning with the 2012 registration year, county-owned vehicles operated by or for any county sheriff and designated deputy sheriffs shall be exempt from fees related to the transfer of registration plates from one vehicle to another. Provides that each county sheriff shall submit to the Secretary of State a list of all vehicles issued permanent vehicle registration plates containing (1) the Vehicle Identification Number of the Vehicle; and (2) the corresponding license plate number. Provides that each county sheriff shall submit to the Secretary an annual report advising the Secretary of (1) any vehicles previously reported to the Secretary which have been wrecked and no longer in use; (2) any additional vehicles acquired for use by the county sheriff and designated deputy sheriffs; (3) any additional registration plates issued; and (4) any transfer of registration plates from a wrecked vehicle to its replacement. Provides that the Secretary shall adopt rules to implement the provisions of this amendatory Act. Effective immediately.
