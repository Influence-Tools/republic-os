# Illinois HB 3334 (97th) — PEN CD-IMRF-CREDIT/INTEREST

Amends the Illinois Municipal Retirement Fund (IMRF) Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Removes a provision requiring an application for leave-of-absence credit to be filed within 2 years after termination of the leave. Specifies 2 additional criteria that must be met for a person to be eligible for either temporary disability benefits or total and permanent disability benefits. Amends the State Mandates Act to require implementation without reimbursement. Effective immediately.
