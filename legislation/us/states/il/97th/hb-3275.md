# Illinois HB 3275 (97th) — FINANCE-VET BRAIN INJURY FUND

Amends the State Finance Act. Creates the Veterans Traumatic Brain Injury Public Service Announcement Fund as a special fund in the State treasury. Provides that all donations to the Department of Veterans' Affairs for the purpose of providing public service announcements to inform veterans of benefits and services provided by the State to veterans suffering from traumatic brain injuries shall be deposited into the Veterans Traumatic Brain Injury Public Service Announcement Fund. Effective immediately.
