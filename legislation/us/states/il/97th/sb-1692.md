# Illinois SB 1692 (97th) — UCC - FRAUDULENT FINANCING

Amends the Uniform Commercial Code. Provides that no person shall file a fraudulent financing statement and assigns civil and criminal penalties for such filings. Provides a procedure for filing a form affidavit to provide notice of a fraudulent financing statement or amendment and provides guidance for when the Secretary of State may terminate the financing statement or amendment based on the affidavit. Provides that a filing office may terminate a record under specified circumstances with notice to the filer of the financing statement or amendment. Provides several circumstances where effective filing does not occur. Effective immediately.
