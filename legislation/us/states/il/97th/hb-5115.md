# Illinois HB 5115 (97th) — DNR-NUISANCE PERMITS-PASTURES

Amends the Wildlife Code. Provides that the Department of Natural Resources shall investigate a complaint by an authorized owner or tenant of lands or their agents when any wild bird or wild mammal is known to be destroying property, including cattle pastures. Effective immediately.
