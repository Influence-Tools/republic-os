# Illinois HB 1095 (97th) — FIRE MARSHAL-FLAME SAFEGUARD

Amends the State Fire Marshal Act. Provides that the Office of the State Fire Marshal shall adopt rules that, beginning July 1, 2012, prohibit the use of a rebuilt flame safeguard control in forced air heating equipment. Defines flame safeguard control.
