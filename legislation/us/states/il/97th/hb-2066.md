# Illinois HB 2066 (97th) — JURY COMMISSN-UNEMPLOYMNT LIST

Amends the Jury Act and the Jury Commission Act. Provides that lists of claimants for unemployment insurance shall be added to the other lists (Illinois driver's license, Identification Card, Disabled Person Identification Card, and registered voters lists) now used to create jury lists. Provides that the Director of the Department of Employment Security shall annually compile a list of persons who in the last 12 months filed a claim for unemployment insurance and shall send the list to the Secretary of State to be combined with lists of Illinois driver's license, Illinois Identification Card, and Illinois Disabled Person Identification Card holders to constitute the master list for jury lists.
