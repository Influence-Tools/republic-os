# Illinois HB 1542 (97th) — ETHICS-STATEMENTS-WEB FILING

Amends the Illinois Governmental Ethics Act. In provisions governing the Internet-based filing of statements of economic interests with county clerks, corrects a reference to an erroneous Section number. Replaces a provision that mandated the Internet-based filing of statements of economic interest with a provision that authorizes the filing of those statements by Internet or by standardized form. Effective immediately.
