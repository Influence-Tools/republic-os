# Illinois SB 2488 (97th) — VEH CD-WORK ZONES-CELL USE BAN

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that no person may use a wireless telephone at any time while operating a motor vehicle on any section of a highway that is undergoing a construction or maintenance project (instead of on a highway in a construction or maintenance speed zone).
