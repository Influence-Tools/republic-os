# Illinois HB 299 (97th) — HIV/AIDS REGISTRY-TEST RESULTS

Amends the HIV/AIDS Registry Act to provide that the information concerning those cases included in the HIV/AIDS Registry shall include all CD4 and HIV viral load test results.
