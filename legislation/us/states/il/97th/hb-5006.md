# Illinois HB 5006 (97th) — SOS MERIT CODE-VETERAN VISITS

Amends the Secretary of State Merit Employment Code. Grants veteran employees 4 (rather than 2) days per year to visit a veterans hospital for examination of a military service connected disability. Effective immediately.
