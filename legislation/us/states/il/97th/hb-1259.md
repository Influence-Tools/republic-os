# Illinois HB 1259 (97th) — VITAL RECORDS - HFS

Amends the Vital Records Act to provide that the electronic reporting system for death registrations shall be used to transfer information to the Department of Healthcare and Family Services not less often than once every 3-month period for the purpose of updating the roster of Medicaid recipients.
