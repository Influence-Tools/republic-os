# Illinois HB 4037 (97th) — MHDD CD-FACIILITY DIRECTOR

Amends the Mental Health and Developmental Disabilities Code. In the definition of "facility director", provides that a designee of the facility director may include a clinical professional counselor. Effective immediately.
