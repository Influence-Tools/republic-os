# Illinois SB 1952 (97th) — LABOR ARBITRATION SERV-REPEAL

Repeals the Labor Arbitration Services Act. Amends the Department of Labor Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois by providing that, if the Department of Labor began providing arbitration services with respect to a particular controversy or difference under the Labor Arbitration Services Act before its repeal, the Department may continue to provide arbitration services for the resolution of that controversy or difference in accordance with the Labor Arbitration Services Act as it existed immediately before its repeal. Effective immediately.
