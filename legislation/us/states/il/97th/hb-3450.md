# Illinois HB 3450 (97th) — LIQUOR - RENEWAL NOTIFICATION

Amends the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Provides that beginning on January 1, 2012, the State Commission shall send, through electronic mail, all licensees a reminder of the date each State license expires. Provides that the State Commission shall notify each licensee through electronic mail 30 days prior to the expiration date of his or her license. Provides that the State Commission is not required to comply with this notification requirement if the licensee does not provide the State Commission his or her electronic mail address. Effective immediately.
