# Illinois SB 3690 (97th) — BUDGET LAW-LONG-TERM CARE

Amends the State Budget Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Requires the long-term care rebalancing initiative to take into account the costs associated with caring for medically compromised, frail older adults who need institutional care, as well as the costs associated with providing support services for higher functioning, less medically compromised older adults who are able to live independently in the community. Effective immediately.
