# Illinois HB 3887 (97th) — ABUSED CHILD-MANDATED REPORTRS

Amends the Abused and Neglected Child Reporting Act. Adds personnel of institutions of higher education, athletic program personnel, and early intervention providers to the list of mandated reporters under the Act. Effective immediately.
