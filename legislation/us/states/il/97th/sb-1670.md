# Illinois SB 1670 (97th) — PARK DIST AQUARIUM & MUSEUM

Amends the Park District Aquarium and Museum Act. Provides that any aquarium or museum shall be open to persons who reside in this State (now, the public) without charge for a period equivalent to 52 days.
