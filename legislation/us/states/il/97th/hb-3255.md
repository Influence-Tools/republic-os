# Illinois HB 3255 (97th) — EMT LICENSURE-STATE TROOPER

Amends the Emergency Medical Services (EMS) Systems Act to provide that an EMT who is an Illinois State Trooper may submit an application to the Department of Public Health for a waiver of certain fees.
