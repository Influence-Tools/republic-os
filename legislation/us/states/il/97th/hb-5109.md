# Illinois HB 5109 (97th) — DENTAL SERVICE PLAN-TRUSTEES

Amends the Dental Service Plan Act in the provision concerning a dental service plan corporation board of trustees. Deletes the requirement that each trustee shall be a resident of Illinois. Provides that those designated as dental trustees shall comprise at least one-third (instead of a majority) of the trustees and shall be participating dentists of the corporation (instead of only dentists) licensed in Illinois to practice dentistry and adds the requirement that such trustees shall be residents of Illinois. Deletes the requirement that the remaining trustees, designated as public trustees, shall be representative of the subscribers to the dental service plan and that not more than one-third of the public trustees shall be licensed dentists. Makes a change in a provision concerning the expiration of the terms of dental trustees. Deletes a provision concerning meetings of subscribers for the election of public trustees.
