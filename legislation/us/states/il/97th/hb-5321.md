# Illinois HB 5321 (97th) — CBD-CONTRACT FEE SUNSET

Amends the Capital Development Board Act. Provides that a provision concerning the Capital Development Board's authority to charge contract administration fees is repealed on June 30, 2016 (now, June 30, 2012). Effective immediately.
