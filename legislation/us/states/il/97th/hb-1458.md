# Illinois HB 1458 (97th) — POWER AGENCY-RENEWABLE ENERGY

Amends the Illinois Power Agency Act. Changes the definition of "renewable energy resources" to include biogas and biosolids produced by municipal wastewater treatment plants in the State. Effective immediately.
