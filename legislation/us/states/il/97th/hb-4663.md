# Illinois HB 4663 (97th) — TRUSTS-DIRECTED TRUST-APPLIED

Amends the Trusts and Trustees Act. Adds provisions establishing a directed trust as a type of trust and establishes responsibilities among a distribution trust advisor, trust protector, and other parties to the trust. Provides that the changes to a trustee's powers in a directed trust apply to all trusts in existence on the effective date and all trusts created after that date. Provisions concerning the administration of a trust are available to any trust that is administered in Illinois or governed by Illinois law unless the trust instrument expressly prohibits the use of one or more those provisions by specific reference.
