# Illinois SB 2443 (97th) — $BHE FY12 OCE

Makes appropriations for the ordinary and contingent expenses of the Board of Higher Education for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2011, as follows: General Funds $30,493,400; Other State Funds $3,430,000; Federal Funds $5,500,000; Total $39,423,400.
