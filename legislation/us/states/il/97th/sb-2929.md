# Illinois SB 2929 (97th) — COM COL ACT-DOMESTIC PRODUCTS

Amends the Public Community College Act. Specifically includes the Procurement of Domestic Products Act as one of the laws (and related administrative requirements) that apply to the Public Community College Act. Removes a reference to the Public Works Preference Act, which has been repealed. Effective immediately.
