# Illinois HB 5478 (97th) — INS CD-DIRECTOR-PUBLIC RECORD

Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. Provides an exception from the provision concerning the public inspection of records, books, and papers of the office of the Director of Insurance for information that is exempt from disclosure under the Freedom of Information Act. Effective immediately.
