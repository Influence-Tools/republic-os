# Illinois HB 2099 (97th) — CHILD CARE ACT-SUID PREVENTION

Amends the Child Care Act of 1969. Provides that the Department shall require all licensed child care facility employees who care for newborns and infants to complete training every 3 years on the nature of sudden unexpected infant death (SUID), sudden infant death syndrome (SIDS), and the safe sleep recommendations of the American Academy of Pediatrics.
