# Illinois SB 2937 (97th) — NORTHWEST METRA DISTRICT

Amends the Local Mass Transit District Act. Creates the Northwest Metra Commuter Rail District. Provides that the Northwest Metra Commuter Rail District shall include all municipalities located within McHenry County.
