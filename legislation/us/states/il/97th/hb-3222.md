# Illinois HB 3222 (97th) — SCH CD-ADMIN CERT-PUBLIC ADMIN

Amends the School Code. Provides that a chief school business official endorsement shall be affixed to the administrative certificate of any holder who qualifies by having a Master's Degree in Public Administration from a regionally accredited higher education institution. Effective immediately.
