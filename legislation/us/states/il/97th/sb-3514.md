# Illinois SB 3514 (97th) — MEDICAL PRACTICE-SUNSET EXT

Amends the Regulatory Sunset Act. Extends the repeal date for the Medical Practice Act of 1987 from December 31, 2012 to December 31, 2022. Contains revisory changes. Effective immediately.
