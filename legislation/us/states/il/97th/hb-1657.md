# Illinois HB 1657 (97th) — LAKE MGT PROGRAM-TASK FORCE

Amends the Illinois Lake Management Program Act. Adds an Illinois congressional delegation member, or his or her designee, to be appointed by the Governor to the Task Force on the Conservation and Quality of the Great Lakes. Provides that no later than June 1 of each year, the Task Force shall submit a report to the General Assembly outlining its recommendations concerning legislative actions to protect the water quality and supply of the Great Lakes. Effective immediately.
