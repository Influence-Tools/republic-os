# Illinois HB 4566 (97th) — ABUSED KID-DEVELOPMNTAL SCREEN

Amends the Abused and Neglected Child Reporting Act. Provides that when there is a report of abuse or neglect involving a child under the age of 5, if the Child Protective Service Unit determines, following an investigation, that there is credible evidence that the child is abused or neglected, the Department of Children and Family Services shall refer the child to the appropriate State or local agency for a developmental screening within 60 days after the Unit's determination. Authorizes the Department to promulgate rules necessary for the implementation of this provision. Effective immediately.
