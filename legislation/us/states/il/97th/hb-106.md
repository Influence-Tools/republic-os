# Illinois HB 106 (97th) — SAFE HAVEN-INFANTS-POLICE DEPT

Amends the Abandoned Newborn Infant Protection Act. Expands the definition of the term "police station" to include a campus police department located on any college or university owned or controlled by the State or any private college or private university that is not owned or controlled by the State.
