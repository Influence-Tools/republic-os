# Illinois HB 5212 (97th) — PREVAIL WAGE REVISION NOTICE

Amends the Prevailing Wage Act. Provides that a public body may make required notifications regarding revisions in the prevailing rate of hourly wages by publishing an ordinance or resolution stating the public body's adherence to the prevailing wage requirements and posting a link with the revised rate schedule on the public body's official website.
