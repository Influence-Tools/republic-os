# Illinois SB 1835 (97th) — LIQUOR - MEMORIAL HALL

Amends the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Provides that alcoholic liquors may be delivered to and sold at Veterans Memorial Hall, located at 211 North Main Street, Rockford in Winnebago County. Effective immediately.
