# Illinois HB 1929 (97th) — CD CORR-MEDICAL-INMATE CO-PAY

Amends the Unified Code of Corrections. Increases from $2 to $5 the co-payment that a person committed to the Department of Corrections who is not otherwise exempt from the payment must pay to the Department for each visit for medical or dental services on a non-emergency basis.
