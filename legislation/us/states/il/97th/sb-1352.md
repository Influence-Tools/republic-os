# Illinois SB 1352 (97th) — PROCUREMENT-CBD CONSTRUCTION

Amends the Illinois Procurement Code. In a Section requiring building construction contracts to specify the 5 subdivisions of work to be performed, provides that, until a date 5 years (now, 4 years) after January 1, 2009, the requirements do not apply to certain construction projects for which the Capital Development Board is the construction agency and the project budget is at least $5,000,000 (now, $20,000,000). Provides that, with regard to these construction projects, the Capital Development Board may award in each year contracts with an aggregate total value of no more than $200,000,000 (now, $100,000,000). Deletes a sentence providing that, if certain conditions are met, the requirements do not apply to the Capitol Building HVAC upgrade project. Effective immediately.
