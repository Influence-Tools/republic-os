# Illinois SB 3380 (97th) — CARNIVAL SAFETY-BD MEMBERS

Amends the Carnival and Amusement Rides Safety Act. Provides that the Carnival-Amusement Safety Board shall consist of 9 (now, 8) members. Sets forth requirements concerning qualifications of the appointed members. Further provides that a majority of the 9 (now, 8) members of the Board constitutes a quorum. Effective immediately.
