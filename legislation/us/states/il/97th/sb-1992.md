# Illinois SB 1992 (97th) — CMS-ITAP GEN ASSEMBLY MEMBERS

Amends the Department of Central Management Services Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. In a Section concerning the Illinois Transparency and Accountability Portal, provides that ITAP shall provide direct access to a database of current members of the General Assembly and State employees of the executive, legislative, and judicial branches. Provides that this database shall be sorted by Rutan status, status of position as subject to collective bargaining, and, for General Assembly members, per diem allowances and mileage paid.
