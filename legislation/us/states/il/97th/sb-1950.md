# Illinois SB 1950 (97th) — ABUSED CHILD ACT-FALSE RPTS

Amends the Abused and Neglected Child Reporting Act. Enhances the criminal penalty for persons who knowingly transmit a false report of child abuse or neglect to the Department of Children and Family Services. Effective immediately.
