# Illinois HB 3441 (97th) — CHIP ACT-PLAN ADMINISTRATOR

Amends the Comprehensive Health Insurance Plan Act to provide that the Comprehensive Health Insurance Board at its option may extend the term of a plan administrator contract for a period not to exceed 3 years. Effective immediately.
