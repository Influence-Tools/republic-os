# Illinois HB 3923 (97th) — OPEN MTGS-PREPAID TUITION FUND

Amends the Open Meetings Act. Provides that the provision that authorizes closed meetings to be held to consider the sale or purchase of securities, investments, or investment contracts does not apply to meetings held to consider the investment of assets or income of funds deposited into the Illinois Prepaid Tuition Trust Fund. Effective immediately.
