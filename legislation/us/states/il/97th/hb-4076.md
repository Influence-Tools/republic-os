# Illinois HB 4076 (97th) — COSMETOLOGY-SCHOOL DEFINED

Amends the Barber, Cosmetology, Esthetics, Hair Braiding, and Nail Technology Act of 1985. Provides a definition for the term "school" used within the Act. Effective immediately.
