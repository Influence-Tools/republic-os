# Illinois SB 2822 (97th) — IDOT LAND CONVEYANCE

Authorizes the Department of Transportation to convey described parcels in Bond, Kendall, Sangamon, Tazewell, Vermilion, and Woodford Counties. Amends Public Act 97-27 by making changes to the legal description in a conveyance in Montgomery County. Effective immediately.
