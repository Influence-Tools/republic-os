# Illinois SB 2579 (97th) — VEH CD-KINGPIN-REAR AXLE LIMIT

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Removes length limits for the distance between the kingpin and the center of the rear axle of a semitrailer longer than 48 feet, in combination with a truck tractor.
