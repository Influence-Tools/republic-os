# Illinois HB 5114 (97th) — SCH CD-SAFETY ED-CPR VIDEO

Amends the School Code with respect to safety education. Requires all students enrolled in grades one through 8 in the public schools to watch a training video on cardio-pulmonary resuscitation and how to use an automated external defibrillator. Effective immediately.
