# Illinois SB 63 (97th) — ELEC CD-JUDGE ADDRESS

Amends the Election Code. Provides that the State Board of Elections shall not post the home address of any Supreme, Appellate, or Circuit Judge, or any candidate for Supreme, Appellate, or Circuit Judge, on its website.
