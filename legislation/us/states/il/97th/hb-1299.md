# Illinois HB 1299 (97th) — PTELL-AGGREGATE EXT-FORMATION

Amends the Property Tax Extension Limitation Law in the Property Tax Code. Provides that the question of establishing a maximum aggregate extension may be combined with the question of forming or establishing a new taxing district. Contains referendum language.
