# Illinois HB 3723 (96th) — TOLL HWY-ADVISORY COM-DISSOLVE

Amends the Toll Highway Act. Provides that a local advisory committee created by the Toll Highway Authority shall be dissolved when one year has elapsed since the opening of the tollway for which the committee was created. Effective immediately.
