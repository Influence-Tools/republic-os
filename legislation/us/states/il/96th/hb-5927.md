# Illinois HB 5927 (96th) — CHILD HEALTH-WAITING PERIOD

Amends the Children's Health Insurance Program Act and the Covering ALL KIDS Health Insurance Act to delete language mandating that if required monthly premiums are not paid, then the child shall be ineligible for re-enrollment for a minimum period of 3 months.
