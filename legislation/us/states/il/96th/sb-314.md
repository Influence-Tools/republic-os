# Illinois SB 314 (96th) — HEALTH FACILITIES PLANNING-BED

Amends the Illinois Health Facilities Planning Act. Requires that the Illinois Health Facilities Planning Board, in collaboration with a subcommittee of the Older Adult Services Advisory Committee, review and revise the Bed Need Methodology for Long Term Care and analyze the nursing home bed needs at the State's township level. Effective immediately.
