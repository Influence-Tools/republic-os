# Illinois HB 5998 (96th) — ASSISTED LIVING-ADVISORY BOARD

Amends the Assisted Living and Shared Housing Act. Deletes a provision providing that the Governor shall establish an Assisted Living and Shared Housing Standards and Quality of Life Advisory Board. Repeals a provision concerning the composition of the Board. Effective immediately.
