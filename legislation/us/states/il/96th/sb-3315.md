# Illinois SB 3315 (96th) — MED PRACT ACT-FEE SPLITTING

Amends the Medical Practice Act of 1987. In a provision prohibiting fee splitting, exempts from the provision an entity that is an Illinois not for profit corporation that is recognized as exempt from the payment of federal income taxes as an organization described in Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code and all of its members are full-time faculty members of a medical school that offers a M.D. degree program that is accredited by the Liaison Committee on Medical Education and a program of graduate medical education that is accredited by the Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education. Effective immediately.
