# Illinois HB 5923 (96th) — MUNI CD-INCORPORATION-VILLAGE

Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. In provisions concerning the incorporation of a village, provides that any contiguous territory not exceeding 0.7 square miles in a county of not less than 700,000 and not more than 750,000 inhabitants may be incorporated as a village under specified circumstances if the territory includes a contiguous lake of not less than 30 acres and not more than 40 acres. Provides for incorporation without the consent of any municipality located within 1.5 miles of the boundary line of the territory. Effective immediately.
