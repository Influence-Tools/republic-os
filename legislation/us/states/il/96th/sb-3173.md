# Illinois SB 3173 (96th) — CRIM CD-GAMBLING EXEMPTION

Amends the Criminal Code of 1961. Exempts from a gambling violation games of skill or chance where money or other things of value can be won but no payment or purchase is required to participate. Effective immediately.
