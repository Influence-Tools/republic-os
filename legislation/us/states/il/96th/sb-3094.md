# Illinois SB 3094 (96th) — INTERIOR DESIGN TITLE ACT

Amends the Interior Design Title Act. Removes requirement that residential interior designers be registered under the Act. Provides that the 2 additional Board members who are registered residential interior designers on the effective date of the amendatory Act shall serve out the full length of their current terms. Also makes technical changes. Effective immediately.
