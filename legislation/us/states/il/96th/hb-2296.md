# Illinois HB 2296 (96th) — METRA-EMERGENCY PROTOCOL

Amends the Metropolitan Transit Authority Act. Provides that the Board of the Authority must develop written protocols to respond to medical and sanitation emergencies and to other safety hazards. Effective immediately.
