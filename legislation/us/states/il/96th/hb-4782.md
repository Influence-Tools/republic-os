# Illinois HB 4782 (96th) — INS CD-SUPPLEMENTAL POLICIES

Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. Provides that the provisions of the Code concerning dependent coverage do not apply to short-term travel, disability income, long-term care, accident only, or limited or specified disease policies.
