# Illinois HB 210 (96th) — $COMPTROLLER-COURT REPORTING

Makes FY09 appropriations to the State Comptroller for expenses related to court reporting. The sum of $3,633,750 is appropriated for official court reporting. The sum of $760,000 is appropriated for State contributions to the State Employees Retirement System and $356,250 for State contributions to Social Security. Effective immediately.
