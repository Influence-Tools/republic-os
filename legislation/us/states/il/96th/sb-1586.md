# Illinois SB 1586 (96th) — VEH CD-SALVAGE VEH-PRIVATE OWN

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Requires applicants for a certificate of title for a rebuilt vehicle that have personally rebuilt or supervised the rebuild of the vehicle to sign a written affirmation stating that the applicant is a licensed rebuilder (rather than providing no requirement that the applicant to be a licensed rebuilder). Provides that if an applicant for certificate of title for a rebuilt vehicle has contracted with a licensed rebuilder for the rebuild of the vehicle, a copy of the contract and a statement from the rebuilder, in a form designated by the Secretary of State, certifying that all of the supporting documentation and the contents of the application for certificate of title are, to the best of the rebuilder's knowledge and belief, complete, true, and correct are required to complete the application. Effective January 1, 2010.
