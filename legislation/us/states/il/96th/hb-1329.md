# Illinois HB 1329 (96th) — ELECTROLOGST-EXEMPTIONS-GRNDFT

Amends the Electrologist Licensing Act. Removes the exemption allowing for a person functioning as an assistant to a person licensed to practice medicine in all its branches to provide electrology services. Provides that the Department of Financial and Professional Regulation may issue a license to an individual who failed to apply for licensure under the grandfathering provision of the Act within one year after the effective date of the rules adopted under the Act, but who otherwise meets the qualifications set forth in the provision, provided that the individual submits a completed application for licensure as required within 90 days after the effective date of the amendatory Act. Effective immediately.
