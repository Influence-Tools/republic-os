# Illinois HB 2644 (96th) — PEACE OFFICERS MEMORIAL DAY

Amends the State Commemorative Dates Act. Designates the first Thursday in May of each year as Peace Officers Memorial Day and the week including that day as Police Week. Recognizes each May 15th as National Peace Officers Memorial Day.
