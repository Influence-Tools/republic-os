# Illinois SB 1611 (96th) — PEN CD-IMRF-COUNTIES COUNCIL

Amends the IMRF Article of the Illinois Pension Code to add regional councils of government to the list of entities permitted to participate in the Fund as participating instrumentalities. Effective immediately.
