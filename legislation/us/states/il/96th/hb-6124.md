# Illinois HB 6124 (96th) — CIV PRO-CHILD SEX ABUSE-LIMITS

Amends the Code of Civil Procedure. Provides that an action for damages for personal injury based on childhood sexual abuse must be commenced within 20 (instead of 10) years of the date the limitation period begins to run or within 20 (instead of 5) years of the date the person abused discovers or through the use of reasonable diligence should discover that the act of childhood sexual abuse occurred and the injury was caused by that abuse. The changes made apply to actions pending on the effective date as well as actions commenced on or after that date.
