# Illinois SB 3088 (96th) — HUMAN SRVCS BUDGET&IMPACT NOTE

Creates the Human Services Budget and Impact Note Act. Requires the Department of Human Services and the Department of Healthcare and Family Services to prepare and file notes concerning a bill's impact. Requires the bill's sponsor to file a statement identifying a preferred means of funding the costs to be incurred because of the legislation. Contains provisions regarding committee appearances; amended bills; and confidentiality requirements.
