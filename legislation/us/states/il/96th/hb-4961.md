# Illinois HB 4961 (96th) — HISTORIC SITES-NO FUND SWEEPS

Amends the Illinois Historic Preservation Act and the State Finance Act. Provides that the Illinois Historic Sites Fund and the Presidential Library and Museum Operating Fund are not subject to sweeps, administrative charges, or charge-backs or any other fiscal or budgetary maneuver that would in any way transfer any amounts from those Funds into any other fund of the State. Effective immediately.
