# Illinois HB 4865 (96th) — CREDIT UNIONS-PROXY REVOCATION

Amends the Illinois Credit Union Act. In provisions concerning the meeting of members, provides that a proxy may be revoked by a written statement to that effect, by execution of a subsequently-dated proxy, or by attendance at a meeting and voting in person. Effective immediately.
