# Illinois HB 567 (96th) — ELECTIONS-COMMUNITY INTEGRATED

Amends the Election Code. Provides that a resident of a community-integrated living arrangement, as defined by rule of the State Board of Elections, is a resident, for voting purposes, of the election jurisdiction in which the arrangement is located.
