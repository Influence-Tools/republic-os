# Illinois HB 5410 (96th) — APP DEFENDER-FUNDS

Amends the State Appellate Defender Act. Provides that for each State fiscal year, the State Appellate Defender shall request a direct appropriation from the Capital Litigation Trust Fund for expenses incurred by the State Appellate Defender in providing assistance to trial attorneys in cases in which the death sentence is an authorized disposition. Makes other changes. Effective immediately.
