# Illinois HB 4928 (96th) — OPEN MEETINGS-ELDER ABUSE

Amends the Open Meetings Act. Permits a closed meeting for an elder abuse fatality review team's review of a death in which abuse or neglect is alleged, suspected, or substantiated. Effective immediately.
