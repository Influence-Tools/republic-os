# Illinois SB 1510 (96th) — PEN CD-ART 3-BENEFICIARY TRUST

Amends the Downstate Police Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Provides that any benefit to be received by or paid to a dependent beneficiary may be received by or paid to a trust established for such dependent beneficiary if the dependent beneficiary is living at the time such benefit would be received by or paid to such trust. Effective immediately.
