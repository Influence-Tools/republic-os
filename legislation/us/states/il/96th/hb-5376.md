# Illinois HB 5376 (96th) — NOT-FOR-PROFIT-UNEMPLOY-NOTICE

Amends the General Not For Profit Corporation Act of 1986. Requires a not-for-profit corporation that is excluded from the definition of "nonprofit organization" under the Unemployment Insurance Act because it does not have in employment 4 or more individuals within each of 20 or more calendar weeks to provide a written notice to each employee and each member of the board of directors that the employees of the not-for-profit corporation are not eligible to receive unemployment insurance benefits on the basis of their employment for the not-for-profit corporation.
