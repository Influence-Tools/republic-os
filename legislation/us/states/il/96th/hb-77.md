# Illinois HB 77 (96th) — FLAG DISPLAY-FIREFIGHTER DEATH

Amends the Flag Display Act. Requires that the U.S. national and Illinois State flags be displayed at half-staff when an Illinois firefighter is killed in the line of duty. Effective immediately.
