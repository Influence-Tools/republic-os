# Illinois SB 2052 (96th) — DCEO CRITICAL SKILLS GRANTS

Amends the Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Makes changes concerning the verification of the Illinois residency of participants in the Departments grant program for training in critically needed skills. Effective immediately.
