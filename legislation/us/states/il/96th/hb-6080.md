# Illinois HB 6080 (96th) — ADOPTION-CONSENT-SURRENDER

Amends the Adoption Act. Provides 2 new adoption forms: final and irrevocable designated surrender for purposes of adoption; and final and irrevocable consent to adoption by a specified person or persons in a non-DCFS case. Effective immediately.
