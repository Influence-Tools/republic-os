# Illinois HB 47 (96th) — FOIA-DISABILITY DATABASES

Amends the Freedom of Information Act. Exempts from the Act's copying and inspection requirements data of law enforcement agencies on the mental and physical disabilities, defects, and maladies of individuals maintained for the safety of responding law enforcement officers, the individuals, and the public. Contains a non-acceleration clause.
