# Illinois SB 1479 (96th) — PEN CD-SERS-FURLOUGH DAYS

Amends the State Employee Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Provides service credit for up to 5 days of voluntary or involuntary furlough used to address a State fiscal emergency. Effective immediately.
