# Illinois HB 3922 (96th) — DPH ACT - NUCLEAR AGENTS

Amends the Department of Public Health Act. In the provision concerning powers, mandates that the Department shall investigate the causes of dangerously contagious or infectious diseases and the health effects, health conditions, or health ailments related to a biological, chemical, radiological, or nuclear event (instead of dangerously contagious or infectious diseases). Sets forth the investigative powers of the Department concerning biological, chemical, radiological, and nuclear agents. Effective immediately.
