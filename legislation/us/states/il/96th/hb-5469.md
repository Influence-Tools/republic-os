# Illinois HB 5469 (96th) — PAY ON DEATH ACT-BENEFICIARY

Amends the Illinois Trust and Payable on Death Accounts Act. Provides that "beneficiary" includes, but is not limited to, a natural person, corporation, charitable organization, or any other organization.
