# Illinois SB 1434 (96th) — VEH CD-REPEAL COM DISTRIB FEE

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Repeals a provision providing for the collection of an annual commercial distribution fee on vehicles of the second division weighing more than 8,000 pounds. Effective July 1, 2010.
