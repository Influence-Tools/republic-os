# Illinois HB 6178 (96th) — COUNTY JAIL-MEDICAL EXPENSES

Amends the County Jail Act. Provides that an arresting authority shall be responsible for any qualified (rather than incurred) medical expenses relating to the arrestee until such time as the arrestee is placed in the custody of the sheriff. Effective immediately.
