# Illinois SB 3342 (96th) — BI-STATE/BONDS-40 YEARS

Amends the Bi-State Development Powers Act. Provides that bonds, notes, and other instruments issued by the Bi-State Development Authority must mature not later than 40 (instead of 30) years after the date of issuance. Effective immediately.
