# Illinois HB 6271 (96th) — CMS NEW EMPLOYEE WEBSITE

Amends the Department of Central Management Services Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Requires the Department to establish and maintain the Illinois New Employee Portal on its official website. Specifies the information to be included on the searchable database concerning State employees hired after the bill's effective date.
