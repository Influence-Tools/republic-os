# Illinois HB 4197 (96th) — DPT VET AFF-ADVERTISE POSITION

Amends the Department of Veterans Affairs Act. Provides that the Department of Veterans' Affairs may spend up to $30,000 per fiscal year for the purpose of advertising seeking employees to fill open positions at veterans medical facilities. Effective immediately.
