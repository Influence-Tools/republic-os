# Illinois SB 2986 (96th) — DOT-LAND CONVEYANCES

Amends Public Act 96-693 by providing that a described parcel in Christian County is conveyed by quitclaim deed to named persons (instead of an easement is released over and through described parcel) and by making changes in the description of the parcel. Authorizes the Department of Transportation to convey described parcels or release highway easements in Carroll, Monroe, Morgan, Pike, and Tazewell counties. Effective immediately.
