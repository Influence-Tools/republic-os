# Illinois HB 361 (96th) — COURT OF CLAIMS-DELIBERATE PER

Amends the Court of Claims Act. Provides that the Court must rule on a claim filed pursuant to the Line of Duty Compensation Act or Illinois National Guardsman's Compensation Act within one year after the application for that claim is filed. Effective immediately.
