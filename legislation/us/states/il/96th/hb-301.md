# Illinois HB 301 (96th) — SWIDA-BOARD

Amends the Southwestern Illinois Development Authority Act. Provides that the Secretary of Transportation (instead of the Director of Central Management Services) shall serve as an ex officio member of the governing body of the Authority. Effective immediately.
