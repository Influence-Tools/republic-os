# Illinois HB 5080 (96th) — LOCKSMITH AGENCY-LICENSURE

Amends the Private Detective, Private Alarm, Private Security, Fingerprint Vendor, and Locksmith Act of 2004. Requires an applicant for licensure as a locksmith agency to provide certain specified information to the Department. Requires a licensee who provides locksmith services to document certain information on the work order provided to the customer. Sets forth provisions concerning the address of record, additional grounds for discipline, physical or mental examinations, consent orders, restoration of licenses after disciplinary proceedings, administrative reviews, subpoenas, compelling testimony, powers and duties of the Department, fees, rosters, and the Illinois Administrative Procedure Act. Defines "address of record", "applicant", and "licensee". Makes other changes. Effective immediately.
