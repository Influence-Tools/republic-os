# Illinois HB 2270 (96th) — $DHS-JOLIET EASTER SEALS

Appropriates $750,000 from the General Revenue Fund to the Department of Human Services for a grant to Joliet Region Easter Seals for programs and for expansion and rehabilitation of facilities. Effective July 1, 2009.
