# Illinois HB 1335 (96th) — SCH CD-PRESS BOX-ACCESSIBILITY

Amends the School Code. Provides that a school board does not have to comply with the Illinois Accessibility Code with respect to accessibility to press boxes that are 1,000 square feet or less and that are on school property.
