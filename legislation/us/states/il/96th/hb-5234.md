# Illinois HB 5234 (96th) — HUMAN RTS-SEX HARASSMENT-EDUC

Amends provisions of the Illinois Human Rights Act prohibiting sexual harassment in higher education so that they also apply to sexual harassment in elementary and secondary schools. Effective immediately.
