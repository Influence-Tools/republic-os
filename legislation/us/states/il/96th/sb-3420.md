# Illinois SB 3420 (96th) — DCFS CHILDREN'S SERVICES FUND

Amends the State Finance Act. Provides that federal moneys paid to the State for certain child welfare services provided by described community mental health providers shall be deposited into the DCFS Children's Services Fund and used to provide child welfare services. Effective immediately.
