# Illinois HB 2536 (96th) — POW-NO MUSEUM FEES

Amends the Historic Preservation Agency Act. Provides that every veteran who is a resident of Illinois and a former prisoner of war is exempt from all admission fees in museums that receive State funds. Effective immediately.
