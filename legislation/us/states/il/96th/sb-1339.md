# Illinois SB 1339 (96th) — ROOFING LICENSE-EXAMINATION

Amends the Illinois Roofing Industry Licensing Act. Removes a provision that exempts a person who has a certain type of license from the examination requirement, so long as (1) the license continues to be valid and is renewed before expiration and (2) the person is not newly designated as a qualifying party after July 1, 2003. Effective immediately.
