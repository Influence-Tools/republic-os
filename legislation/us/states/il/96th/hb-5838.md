# Illinois HB 5838 (96th) — PHYSICAL FITNESS FCLTY-PUB SCH

Amends the Physical Fitness Facility Medical Emergency Preparedness Act. Adds facilities owned or operated by a public school organized under the School Code to the list of facilities that do not fall under the definition of "physical fitness facility". Effective immediately.
