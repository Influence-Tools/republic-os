# Illinois HB 680 (96th) — RENEWABLE FUELS-GRANT ADDITION

Amends the Illinois Renewable Fuels Development Program Act. Authorizes the Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity to award, in excess of the annual aggregate grant total, up to $4,000,000 to a grant applicant who installs advanced technologies for water usage, carbon footprint reduction, and other blending improvements designed to optimize processes at the applicants renewable fuels facility. Effective immediately.
