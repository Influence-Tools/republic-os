# Illinois SB 2520 (96th) — METRO-EAST SANITARY DIST-ANNEX

Amends the Metro-East Sanitary District Act of 1974. Provides that a sanitary district may, by ordinance, annex property within any unit of local government if the property is contiguous to the district and served by the sanitary district. Specifies the requirements of the ordinance. Sets forth the procedure for annexation. Makes other changes.
