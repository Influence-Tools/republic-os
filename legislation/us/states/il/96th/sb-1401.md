# Illinois SB 1401 (96th) — PEN CD-ART 3-END PARTICIPATION

Amends the Downstate Police Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Provides that a municipality having a population of less than 5,000 may terminate its participation under the Downstate Police Article and close its fund under specified circumstances. Effective immediately.
