# Illinois SB 1685 (96th) — PUB HLTH-SMK FREE IL RELIGIOUS

Amends the Smoke Free Illinois Act. In the provision concerning definitions, provides that "smoke" or "smoking" does not include smoking that is associated with a recognized religious ceremony, ritual, or activity.
