# Illinois SB 3183 (96th) — GOVT ETHICS-ECONOMIC INTERESTS

Amends the Illinois Governmental Ethics Act. With respect to required notification of certain persons who must file statements of economic interests with a county clerk, permits the county clerk to send the notices electronically (now, may send notices only by U.S. mail).
