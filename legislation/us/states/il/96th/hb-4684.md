# Illinois HB 4684 (96th) — CHILD SUPPORT-INC-FOSTER CARE

Amends the Illinois Marriage and Dissolution of Marriage Act. Provides that for purposes of determining an amount of child support, the list of items that are deducted from income (such as income tax and FICA withholding, pension contributions, union dues, health insurance, and specified expenditures) is expanded to include foster care payments paid by the Department of Children and Family Services for providing licensed foster care to a foster child. Effective immediately.
