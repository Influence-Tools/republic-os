# Illinois SB 125 (96th) — EPA--DEBRIS--POPULATION REQ.

Amends the Environmental Protection Act. Excludes the portion of a site or facility accepting exclusively general construction debris, located in a county with a population over 500,000 (now, 700,000) from regulation as a pollution control facility. Effective immediately.
