# Illinois HB 3255 (102nd) — VETERANS-SERVICE OFFICER

Amends the Department of Veterans' Affairs Act. Provides that all service officers and any supervisors, including the field manager, within the field division must be an honorably discharged veteran from service in the Armed Forces of the United States.
