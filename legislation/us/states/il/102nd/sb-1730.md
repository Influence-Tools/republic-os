# Illinois SB 1730 (102nd) — CORPORATIONS LGBTQ DIRECTORS

Amends the Business Corporation Act of 1983. Provides that public corporations must report the self-identified sexual orientation and self-identified gender identity of its directors. Effective January 1, 2022.
