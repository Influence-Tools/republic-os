# Illinois HB 3678 (102nd) — CRIM PRO-GRAND JUROR OATH

Amends the Code of Criminal Procedure of 1963. Changes the oath taken by grand jurors. Amends the Jury Act to delete a duplicative provision concerning the oath taken by the foreman of a Grand Jury. Effective immediately.
