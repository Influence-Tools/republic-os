# Illinois HB 2991 (102nd) — VETERANS' AFFAIRS-FIELD OFFICE

Amends the Department of Veterans' Affairs Act. Provides that the Department of Veterans' Affairs shall establish a field office in each legislative district (rather than such field offices as it shall find necessary to enable it to perform its duties).
