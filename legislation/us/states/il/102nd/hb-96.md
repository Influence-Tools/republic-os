# Illinois HB 96 (102nd) — VEH CD-TINTED WINDOWS

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Adds light sensitivity as a result of a traumatic brain injury to the list of medical conditions for which a person is exempt from certain restrictions concerning window tinting. Deletes language providing that no exemption shall be granted for any condition, such as light sensitivity, for which protection from the direct rays of the sun can be adequately obtained by the use of sunglasses or other eye protective devices.
