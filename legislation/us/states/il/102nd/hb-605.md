# Illinois HB 605 (102nd) — AMERICAN-MADE ILLINOIS FLAGS

Amends the Flag Display Act. Provides that no State institution or agency may purchase any American flags or Illinois State flags (currently, only American flags) except those manufactured in the United States of America.
