# Illinois SB 2486 (102nd) — PERSONNEL RECORD DISCIPLINE

Amends the Personnel Record Review Act. Provides that an individual aggrieved by a disclosure of a disciplinary report in violation of the Act may file a complaint with the Director of Labor or file an action in court within 7 years after the violation.
