# Illinois HB 4245 (102nd) — IRRIGATION CONTRACTORS-SUNSET

Amends the Regulatory Sunset Act. Extends the repeal date of provisions of the Illinois Plumbing License Law concerning irrigation contractors from January 1, 2024 to January 1, 2034. Effective immediately.
