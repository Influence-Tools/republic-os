# Illinois HB 1931 (102nd) — MUNI-INDUST PROJECT REVENUE

Amends the Industrial Project Revenue Bond Act in the Illinois Municipal Code. In the definition of "industrial project", includes use or disposal of surplus real estate owned by the municipality. Effective immediately.
