# Illinois SB 3082 (102nd) — LOCAL JOURNALISM TF-MEMBERS

Amends the Local Journalism Task Force Act. Adds additional members to be appointed by the Governor to the Local Journalism Task Force within 30 days after the effective date of the amendatory Act.
