# Illinois HB 3265 (102nd) — CIVIL EVIDENCE-RAPE CRISIS ORG

Amends the Evidence Article of the Code of Civil Procedure. Provides that "rape crisis organization" includes, but is not limited to, rape crisis centers certified by a statewide sexual assault coalition.
