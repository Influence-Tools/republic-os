# Illinois SB 505 (102nd) — MILITARY CD-ACTIVE DUTY DEATH

Amends the Military Code of Illinois. Provides that members of the Illinois National Guard serving on State Active Duty, in addition to those serving in a federal duty or training status, shall have a State flag presented to their next of kin upon their death.
