# Illinois SB 3833 (102nd) — ANCRA-MANDATED REPORTERS

Amends the Abused and Neglected Child Reporting Act. Expands the list of mandated reporters under the Act to include physical therapists, physical therapy assistants, occupational therapists, occupational therapy assistants, and athletic trainers.
