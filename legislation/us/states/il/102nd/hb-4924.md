# Illinois HB 4924 (102nd) — PENCD-DNST FIRE-TREASURER BOND

Amends the Downstate Firefighter Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Removes language requiring the treasurer of the board to execute a bond to the municipality conditioned for the faithful performance of the duties of the office and other conditions. Makes conforming changes. Effective immediately.
