# Illinois HB 209 (102nd) — FOOD SERVICE BAN LATEX GLOVES

Amends the Food Handling Regulation Enforcement Act. Provides that a food service establishment may not permit employees to use latex gloves in the preparation and handling of food. Provides that, to encourage compliance, a food service establishment shall receive a notification of warning for the first violation.
