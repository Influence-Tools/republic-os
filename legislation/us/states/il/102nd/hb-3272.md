# Illinois HB 3272 (102nd) — SCH CD-PUPIL ABSENCE-PREGNANCY

Amends the School Code. Requires a school board to adopt written policies related to absences and missed homework or classwork assignments as a result of or related to a student's pregnancy. Effective immediately.
