# Illinois HB 2394 (102nd) — MHDD-DATA COLLECTION FORMAT

Amends the Mental Health and Developmental Disabilities Administrative Act. Requires the Department of Human Services and the Department of Healthcare and Family Services to collaborate to develop a standardized format for specified data collection and registration no later than January 1, 2023 in collaboration with behavioral and mental health providers, stakeholders, and entities with expertise in federal requirements and form development. Provides that the Department of Human Service and the Department of Healthcare and Family Services must comply with the new standardized format within 6 months after its date of completion. Contains other provisions. Effective immediately.
