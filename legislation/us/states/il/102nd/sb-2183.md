# Illinois SB 2183 (102nd) — TRANSPORT NETWORK-REPEAL 2027

If and only if House Bill 156 of the 101st General Assembly becomes law, amends the Transportation Network Providers Act. Repeals the Act on January 1, 2027 (instead of June 1, 2021). Effective immediately.
