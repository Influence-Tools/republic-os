# Illinois SB 3189 (102nd) — PROP TX-SOUTHLAND REACTIVATION

Amends the Use Tax Act and the Retailers' Occupation Tax Act. Provides that, on and after January 1, 2023, returns for motor vehicles, watercraft, aircraft, and trailers that are required to be registered with an agency of the State are required to be filed electronically.
