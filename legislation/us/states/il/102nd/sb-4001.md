# Illinois SB 4001 (102nd) — DHS-DEMOGRAPHIC DATA-REPEAL

Amends the Department of Human Services Act. Repeals a provision requiring the Department of Human Services to collect and publicly report statistical data on the racial and ethnic demographics of program participants for each program administered by the Department.
