# Illinois SB 1767 (102nd) — PREV WAGE-PUBLIC SEARCH DATA

Amends the Prevailing Wage Act. Provides that the electronic database of certified payrolls must be searchable by the general public no later than January 1, 2022. Requires personal identifying information to be redacted. Effective immediately.
