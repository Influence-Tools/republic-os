# Illinois HB 202 (102nd) — FIREFIGHTER TRAINING-HISTORY

Amends the Illinois Fire Protection Training Act. In provisions requiring fire fighters to complete training relating to the history of the fire service labor movement, provides that "fire fighter" includes fire fighters hired by the Chicago Fire Department.
