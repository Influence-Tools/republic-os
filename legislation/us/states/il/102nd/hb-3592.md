# Illinois HB 3592 (102nd) — IDPH-MEDICAL EXAMINER OFFICES

Amends the Department of Public Health Powers and Duties Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Requires the Department of Public Health to ensure that coroners are included as part of medical examiner offices for the purposes of complying with and implementing specified federal provisions.
