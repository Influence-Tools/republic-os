# Illinois HB 4639 (102nd) — SOS-MANUFACTURED HOME DEALERS

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Repeals a Section concerning the licensing of manufactured home dealers. Creates a new Article concerning the regulation of manufactured home dealers within the Chapter of the Code concerning the licensing of dealers, transporters, wreckers, and rebuilders. Defines terms and provides that no person shall engage in the business of selling or dealing in manufactured homes of any make unless licensed to do so by the Secretary of State. Adds provisions governing: applications; fees; fees; bonding; insurance; business requirements; term of license; cancellation or revocation of licenses; and other matters. Effective July 1, 2023.
