# Illinois HB 4568 (102nd) — COAL MINING ACT-MINING BOARD

Amends the Coal Mining Act. Repeals provisions concerning the Miners' Examining Board. Provides that the Mining Board shall carry out the duties of the Miners' Examining Board. Makes changes in provisions concerning rulemaking. Makes conforming and other changes. Effective immediately.
