# Illinois SB 3097 (102nd) — PROP TX-NOTICE OF REDEMPTION

Amends the Property Tax Code. Provides that, after filing a petition to obtain a tax deed, the owner of a certificate of purchase must file with the clerk of the circuit court (currently, the county clerk) the names and addresses of persons who are entitled to service of notice. Effective January 1, 2022.
