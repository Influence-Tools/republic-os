# Illinois SB 1734 (102nd) — EMINENT DOMAIN-COOK COUNTY

Amends the Cemetery Protection Act. Requires a court order before a cemetery may disinter deceased human remains from a multiple-depth burial plot if the involved decedent is not located within a concrete outer burial container and if the involved decedent was interred 10 or more years ago.
