# Illinois HB 848 (102nd) — LOTTERY-SCRATCH-OFF

Amends the Illinois Lottery Law. Removes language that would require that sales of the special instant scratch-off game to benefit Alzheimer's care, support, education, and awareness be discontinued on January 1, 2022. Effective immediately.
