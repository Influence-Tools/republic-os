# Illinois SB 1905 (102nd) — FAMILY FERTILITY DISCLOSE INS

Creates the Family and Fertility Disclosure in Health Insurance Act. Requires employers that provide health insurance coverage to employees through policies written outside of this State to disclose to employees specified coverages required under the Illinois Insurance Code for policies written in this State and disclose the coverages that are not included in the coverage provided to the employees. Effective immediately.
