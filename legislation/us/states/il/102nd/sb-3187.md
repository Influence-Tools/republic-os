# Illinois SB 3187 (102nd) — COUNTIES CD-RECORDER STAMP

Amends the Counties Code. Provides that a physical or electronic image of the recorder's stamp satisfies the signature requirement for recorded instruments prior to, on, and after the effective date of the amendatory Act. Makes corresponding changes. Effective immediately.
