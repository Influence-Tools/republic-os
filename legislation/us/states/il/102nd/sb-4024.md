# Illinois SB 4024 (102nd) — AGING-LONG TERM CARE PROGRAM

Amends the Illinois Act on the Aging. In a provision requiring the Department on Aging to make certain long term care consumer choice information available to the public on the Internet, removes a reference to the name of the hyperlink "Resident's Right to Know".
