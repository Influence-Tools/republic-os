# Illinois HB 2568 (102nd) — PSEBA HLTH PLAN BARGAINED

Amends the Public Safety Employee Benefits Act. Provides that a health insurance plan, as defined, is limited to the insurance plan options codified in the employee collective bargaining agreement or bargained upon with an authorized agent and subject to the grievance process.
