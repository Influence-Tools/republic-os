# Illinois HB 590 (102nd) — SARCOIDOSIS AWARENESS MONTH

Amends the State Commemorative Dates Act. Provides that the month of April of each year is designated as Sarcoidosis Awareness Month, to be observed throughout the State as a month to promote the awareness of Sarcoidosis disease and treatment.
