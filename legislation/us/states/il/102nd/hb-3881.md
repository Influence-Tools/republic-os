# Illinois HB 3881 (102nd) — HAZARDOUS MATERIALS-LOCAL ROAD

Amends the Illinois Hazardous Materials Transportation Act. Defines "local road" as any State roadway, except for (i) a highway with 4 or more lanes, or (ii) an interstate highway. Effective immediately.
