# Illinois HB 2449 (102nd) — COUNTIES CODE-SPECIAL MEETINGS

Amends the Counties Code. Provides that special meetings of the board shall be held when requested by any chairman of the board (currently, restricted to chairmen of the board in counties where such chairmen are elected by the voters of the county). Effective immediately.
