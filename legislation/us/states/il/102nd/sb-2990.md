# Illinois SB 2990 (102nd) — TIF-VILLAGE OF PALATINE

Amends the Tax Increment Allocation Redevelopment Act of the Illinois Municipal Code. Extends the estimated date of completion of a redevelopment project and the retirement of obligations issued to finance redevelopment project costs for an ordinance adopted on December 13, 1999 by the Village of Palatine to create the Village of Palatine Downtown Area TIF District. Effective immediately.
