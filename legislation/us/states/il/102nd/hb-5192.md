# Illinois HB 5192 (102nd) — BUDGETING RESULTS COMMISSION

Amends the State Budget Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Provides that the commission established by the Governor concerning statewide prioritized goals as related to the State budget shall hold at least 2 in-person public meetings (currently, not required to be in-person). Provides that the commission may choose by a majority vote of its members to hold one virtual meeting, which is open to the public and over the Internet, in lieu of the required 2 in-person public meetings. Effective immediately.
