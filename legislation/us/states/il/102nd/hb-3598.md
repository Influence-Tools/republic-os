# Illinois HB 3598 (102nd) — INSURANCE-CHAMBERS OF COMMERCE

Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. Provides that companies that issue group policies of accident and health insurance must offer such policies to local chambers of commerce. Provides for enforcement by the Department of Insurance by rule.
