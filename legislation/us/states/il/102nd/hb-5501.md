# Illinois HB 5501 (102nd) — FIRST 2022 GENERAL REVISORY

Creates the First 2022 General Revisory Act. Combines multiple versions of Sections amended by more than one Public Act. Renumbers Sections of various Acts to eliminate duplication. Corrects obsolete cross-references and technical errors. Makes stylistic changes. Effective immediately.
