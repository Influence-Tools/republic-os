# Illinois HB 4218 (102nd) — CD CORR-MENSTRUAL HYGIENE PROD

Amends the Unified Code of Corrections. Provides that menstrual hygiene products shall be available, as needed, free of charge, at all institutions and facilities of the Department of Corrections for all committed persons and employees of the Department who menstruate.
