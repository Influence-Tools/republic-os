# Illinois SB 3498 (102nd) — RECORDS-PHYSICIAN ASSISTANT

Amends the Vital Records Act. Provides that "certifying health care professional" includes a physician assistant. Defines "physician assistant". Provides that in the absence of a certifying health care professional or with his or her approval, a medical certification may be completed and signed by a physician assistant.
