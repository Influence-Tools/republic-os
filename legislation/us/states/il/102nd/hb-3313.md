# Illinois HB 3313 (102nd) — TIF-CITY OF MORRIS-TIF DIST 1

Amends the Tax Increment Allocation Redevelopment Act of the Illinois Municipal Code. Creates a tax increment allocation financing extension to the 47th year (currently, the 35th year) after the adoption of the ordinance of December 29, 1986 by the City of Morris and that is known as the Morris TIF District 1. Requires adoption of an ordinance by the City of Morris extending the completion date of the redevelopment project area to 47 years and providing notice to the taxing bodies that would otherwise constitute the joint review board. Effective immediately.
