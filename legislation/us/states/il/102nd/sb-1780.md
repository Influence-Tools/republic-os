# Illinois SB 1780 (102nd) — MOBILE HOMES-WATER SUPPLY

Amends the Mobile Home Landlord and Tenant Rights Act. Provides that if exigent circumstances arise, the park owner is responsible for providing a water supply to each household following a 5-day period of no water.
