# Illinois HB 5441 (102nd) — CRIM CD-SEX OFFENSES-CONSENT

Amends the Criminal Code of 2012. Provides that for purposes of the Sex Offenses Article of the Code, "unable to give knowing consent" also includes when the victim is intoxicated, but the accused did not provide or administer the intoxicating substance.
