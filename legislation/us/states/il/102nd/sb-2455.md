# Illinois SB 2455 (102nd) — VEH CDREPEAL MOTORIST REPORT

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Repeals Sections of the Code requiring the driver of a vehicle that is involved in specified types of accidents to file a report of the accident with the Department of Transportation in its capacity as the Administrator of the Illinois Safety and Family Financial Responsibility Law. Effective immediately.
