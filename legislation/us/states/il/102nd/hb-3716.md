# Illinois HB 3716 (102nd) — VETS-IDOT-SEASONAL EMPLOYMENT

Amends the Veterans Preference Act. Provides that veterans shall be preferred for appointment to and employment with the Illinois Department of Transportation for the positions of snow removal operator and winter salaried highway maintainer under the Department's Winter Seasonal Employment Program.
