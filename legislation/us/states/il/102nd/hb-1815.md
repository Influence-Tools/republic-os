# Illinois HB 1815 (102nd) — VETERANS' AILMENTS TASK FORCE

Amends the Department of Veterans' Affairs Act. Provides that the Veterans' Service-Related Ailments Task Force shall meet at least once every 2 months beginning July 1, 2021 (rather than beginning July 1, 2019). Effective immediately.
