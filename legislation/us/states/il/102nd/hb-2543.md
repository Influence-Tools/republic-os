# Illinois HB 2543 (102nd) — WATER WELL INSTALLATION-SUNSET

Amends the Regulatory Sunset Act. Extends the repeal date of the Water Well and Pump Installation Contractor's License Act from January 1, 2022 to January 1, 2032. Effective immediately.
