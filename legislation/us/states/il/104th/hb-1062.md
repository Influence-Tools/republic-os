# Illinois HB 1062 (104th) — IL-CENTURY-NETWORK-PRIORITIES

Amends the Illinois Century Network Act. Provides that the connection of anchor institutions to the Illinois Century Network shall be prioritized according to the type of anchor institution, starting with schools and libraries.
