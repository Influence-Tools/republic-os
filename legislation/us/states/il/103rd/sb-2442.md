# Illinois SB 2442 (103rd) — FAIR PATIENT BILLING-INCOME

Amends the Fair Patient Billing Act. Provides that, notwithstanding any other provision of law, a hospital shall not charge or bill a patient whose household income is not greater than 138% of the federal poverty level.
