# Illinois HB 3768 (103rd) — UNIFORM RACIAL CLASSIFICATION

Amends the Uniform Racial Classification Act. Provides that when a State agency is required by law to compile or report statistical data using racial or ethnic classifications, that State agency shall also include people who are Middle Eastern or North African.
