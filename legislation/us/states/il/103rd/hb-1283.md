# Illinois HB 1283 (103rd) — TWP CD-SPECIAL SERVICE AREAS

Amends the Township Special Service Areas Article of the Township Code. Removes a provision in the definition of "township special service area" limiting special service areas to a township in a county with a population of more than 3,000,000. Effective immediately.
