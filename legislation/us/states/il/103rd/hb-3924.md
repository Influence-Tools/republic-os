# Illinois HB 3924 (103rd) — SCH CD-FENTANYL EDUCATION

Amends the Courses of Study Article of the School Code. Provides that school districts shall provide instruction on the dangers of fentanyl. Specifies the elements of the instruction. Provides that students shall be assessed on the fentanyl instruction.
