# Illinois HB 2972 (103rd) — RESCUE SQUAD DISTRICTS-FEES

Amends the Rescue Squad Districts Act. Provides that a rescue squad district may fix, charge, and collect fees for rescue squad services and ambulance services within or outside of the rescue squad district not exceeding the reasonable cost of the service. Effective immediately.
