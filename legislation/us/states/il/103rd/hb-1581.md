# Illinois HB 1581 (103rd) — VEH-SUBMARINE SERVICE PLATES

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Allows the Secretary of State to issue special registration plates designated as United States Submarine Veterans plates to each resident of this State who served in the United States Navy as a submariner.
