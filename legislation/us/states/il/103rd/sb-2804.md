# Illinois SB 2804 (103rd) — CIVIL ADMIN-CMS ADMIN HEARINGS

Amends the Department of Central Management Services Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Authorizes the Department of Central Management Services to provide coordination, support, and adjudication for State agencies' administrative hearing functions through its Bureau of Administrative Hearings. Authorizes the Department to enact rules as necessary to implement the changes. Effective immediately.
