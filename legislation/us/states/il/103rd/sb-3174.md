# Illinois SB 3174 (103rd) — UNDERGROUND RAILROAD TASK FORC

Amends the Illinois Underground Railroad Task Force Act. Requires the Task Force to submit a report of its findings and recommendations to the General Assembly and the Governor on or before December 31, 2024 (rather than July 1, 2024).
