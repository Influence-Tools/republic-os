# Illinois HB 3337 (103rd) — MUNI CD-INCORPORATED TOWNS

Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. In provisions about certain villages incorporated under special Acts, adds incorporated towns into the provisions to allow incorporated towns to elect 6 trustees. Effective immediately.
