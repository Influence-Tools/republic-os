# Illinois SB 3448 (103rd) — IEMA-ADDRESS VERIFICATION

Amends the Illinois Emergency Planning and Community Right to Know Act. Requires State agencies to verify addresses of locations of Tier II chemicals before providing Tier II chemical address information to local agencies.
