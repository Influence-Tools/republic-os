# Illinois HB 3707 (103rd) — ICC-TOW CONSUMER COMPLAINTS

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that the Illinois Commerce Commission shall publish all consumer complaints filed against any towing company on its website. Requires the Commission to update its website periodically to include information pertaining to the disposition of the complaint.
