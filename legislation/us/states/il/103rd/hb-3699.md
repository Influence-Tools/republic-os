# Illinois HB 3699 (103rd) — EMPLOY SECURITY-CHILD SUPPORT

Amends the Department of Employment Security Law. Directs the Department of Employment Security to work with the Department of Healthcare and Family Services to identify employment opportunities in the State for persons who are in arrears in child support obligations.
