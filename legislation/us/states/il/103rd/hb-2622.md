# Illinois HB 2622 (103rd) — DOWNSTATE FOREST PRESERVE-FUND

Amends the Downstate Forest Preserve District Act. Removes provisions requiring a forest preserve district's Landfill Expense Fund to be maintained for a period not to exceed 40 years from the date of closure of the facility.
