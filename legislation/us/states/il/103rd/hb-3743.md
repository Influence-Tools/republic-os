# Illinois HB 3743 (103rd) — ALOPECIA AWARENESS MONTH

Amends the State Commemorative Dates Act. Provides that the month of September of each year is designated as Alopecia Awareness Month to be observed throughout the State to bring awareness to the disease of alopecia.
