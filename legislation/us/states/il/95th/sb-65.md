# Illinois SB 65 (95th) — PEN CD-DOWNSTATE POLICE-TAXES

Amends the Downstate Police Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Provides that the annual property tax levied for pension purposes shall be forwarded directly to the treasurer of the board within 30 business days after receipt by the county. Effective immediately.
