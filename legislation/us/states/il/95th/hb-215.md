# Illinois HB 215 (95th) — LIVESTOCK MANAGEMENT-MANURE

Amends the Livestock Management Facilities Act. Provides that solid livestock waste stacking structures shall be sized to store not less than the amount of waste generated during 3 months of facility operation at design capacity (now 6 months).
