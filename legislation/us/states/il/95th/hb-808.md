# Illinois HB 808 (95th) — ASSISTD LIVNG-BACKGROUND CHECK

Amends the Assisted Living and Shared Housing Act. In connection with the issuance of a license under the Act, requires that all employees (instead of only direct care staff) meet the requirements of the Health Care Worker Background Check Act. Effective immediately.
