# Illinois HB 4548 (95th) — TIF EXTEND-LIBERTYVILLE

Amends the Tax Increment Allocation Redevelopment Act in the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that the redevelopment project in the TIF district created by an ordinance that was adopted on December 23, 1986 by the Village of Libertyville must be completed by December 31 of the 35th year (now, the 23rd year) after the year in which the ordinance was adopted. Effective immediately.
