# Illinois HB 4653 (95th) — DOC LAND TRANSFR-PEORIA COUNTY

Requires the Director of Corrections, on behalf of the State of Illinois and the Department of Corrections, to convey, by quit claim deed, all right, title, and interest of the State of Illinois and the Department of Corrections in and to certain real estate to Peoria County. Effective immediately.
