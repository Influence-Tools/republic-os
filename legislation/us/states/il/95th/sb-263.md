# Illinois SB 263 (95th) — AIRPORT AUTHORITY-PEORIA

Amends the Airport Authorities Act. Creates the Greater Metropolitan Airport Authority. Provides that the territory of the Authority shall be coterminous with the boundaries of Peoria County. Provides that the existing airport authority located within Peoria County is dissolved, and the Greater Metropolitan Airport Authority shall assume the rights to all property, assets, and liabilities of the dissolved authority. Provides for the appointment of the board members of the Greater Metropolitan Airport Authority.
