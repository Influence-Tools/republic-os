# Illinois HB 4216 (95th) — DCEO-SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

Amends the Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Creates the Illinois Science and Technology Commission. Sets forth the purpose of the Commission. Sets forth the appointing authority and terms of the members.
