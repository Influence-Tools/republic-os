# Illinois SB 1869 (95th) — MED PRAC-VISITING PROF-EXAM

Amends the Medical Practice Act of 1987. Removes the requirement that an applicant for initial renewal of a visiting professor permit successfully pass a general competency examination. Effective immediately.
