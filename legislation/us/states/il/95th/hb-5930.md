# Illinois HB 5930 (95th) — ST GREEN BUILDINGS-LED LIGHTS

Amends the Government Buildings Energy Cost Reduction Act of 1991. Requires the use of Energy Star lights or LED lights (now, Energy Star lights) in State owned or leased buildings of 1,000 square feet or larger.
