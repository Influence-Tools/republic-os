# Illinois HB 282 (95th) — PROP TX-TAX SALES

Amends the Property Tax Code. Authorizes, except in Cook County, the personal service of the notice of the expiration of the period of redemption to be made by licensed or registered private detectives. Provides that the amount of redemption includes the costs incurred in causing notices to be served by a licensed or registered private detective, but those costs may not exceed the amount that the sheriff would be authorized by law to charge if those notices had been served by the sheriff.
