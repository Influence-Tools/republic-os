# Illinois HB 3578 (95th) — PEN CD-COOK CO OFFCR ALT ANNTY

Amends the Cook County Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Provides that the plan of optional alternative benefits and contributions for Cook county officers elected by vote of the people only applies to county officers elected by vote of the people on or before the effective date of the amendatory Act. Amends the State Mandates Act to require implementation without reimbursement.
