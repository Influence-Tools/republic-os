# Illinois SB 1665 (95th) — INERT ANHYDROUS AMMONIA

Amends the Technology Advancement and Development Act. Provides that grants under the Act may be made to research and test products that render the use of anhydrous ammonia inert in the production of methamphetamine. Effective July 1, 2007.
