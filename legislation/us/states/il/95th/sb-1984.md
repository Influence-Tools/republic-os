# Illinois SB 1984 (95th) — SOYBEANS-NET MARKET PRICE

Amends the Soybean Marketing Act. Defines "net market price". Changes the assessment after the first 5 years from "2¢ per bushel" to "1/2 of 1% of the net market price".
