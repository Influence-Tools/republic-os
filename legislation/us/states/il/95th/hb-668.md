# Illinois HB 668 (95th) — VETERANS PREFERENCE-PENALTY

Amends the Veterans Preference Act. Increases the fine for a violation of the Act to a minimum of $75 (instead of $25) and a maximum of $300 (instead of $100). Effective immediately.
