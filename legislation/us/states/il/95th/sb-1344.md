# Illinois SB 1344 (95th) — MOTOR VEHICLE-RELEVANT MARKET

Amends the Motor Vehicle Franchise Act. Changes the definition of "relevant market area" to remove language allowing for the area of responsibility to be defined in the franchise agreement. Effective immediately.
