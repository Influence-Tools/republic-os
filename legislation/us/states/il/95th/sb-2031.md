# Illinois SB 2031 (95th) — EMERG TEL SYS BOARD-CNTY BOARD

Amends the Emergency Telephone System Act. Provides that, in counties with a population of more than 100,000 but less than 2,000,000, a member of the county board may serve on the Emergency Telephone System Board.
