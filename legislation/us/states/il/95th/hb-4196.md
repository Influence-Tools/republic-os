# Illinois HB 4196 (95th) — CONDOMINIUM ADV COUNCIL-REPORT

Amends the Condominium Advisory Council Act. Provides that the Condominium Advisory Council shall report its findings and recommendations to the Governor and General Assembly by January 31, 2009 (instead of January 31, 2008). Effective immediately.
