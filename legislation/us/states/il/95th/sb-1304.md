# Illinois SB 1304 (95th) — DCMS-COLLEGE FLEXIBLE SPENDING

Amends the Department of Central Management services Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Requires that, to the extent allowable by federal law and regulation, the Department must allow employees of State colleges and universities to participate in the Department's flexible spending programs for dependent care assistance and medical care assistance.
