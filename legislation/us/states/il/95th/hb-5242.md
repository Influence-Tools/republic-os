# Illinois HB 5242 (95th) — FD HANDLING REG-FOOD PANTRIES

Amends the Food Handling Regulation Enforcement Act. Provides that all food pantries shall register with the State-certified local public health department having jurisdiction in the area where the food pantry is located. Effective January 1, 2009.
