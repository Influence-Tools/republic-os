# Illinois HB 5230 (95th) — CRIM CD-SEXUAL BATTERY

Amends the Criminal Code of 1961 and the Unified Code of Corrections. Provides that the statutes concerning escape and aiding escape apply to a person adjudicated a delinquent minor for the commission of a felony or misdemeanor offense under the Juvenile Court Act of 1987 and to a person aiding the escape of a person adjudicated a delinquent minor for the commission of a felony or misdemeanor offense under the Juvenile Court Act of 1987. Provides that aiding an escape while armed with a dangerous weapon is a Class 1 non-probationable felony (rather than a Class 2 felony). Effective immediately.
