# Illinois HB 1864 (95th) — VEH CD-SNOWMOBILE-BOAT SAFETY

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code, the Snowmobile Registration and Safety Act, and the Boat Registration and Safety Act. Changes cross-references with regard to reimbursement by a DUI offender of the cost of an emergency response necessitated by the offender's violation. Effective immediately.
