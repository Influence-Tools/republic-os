# Illinois HB 4602 (95th) — INS CD-COVERAGE-SHINGLES VACC

Amends the State Employees Group Insurance Act of 1971, the Counties Code, the Illinois Municipal Code, the School Code, the Illinois Insurance Code, the Health Maintenance Organization Act, and the Voluntary Health Services Plans Act to provide coverage for a federally approved shingles vaccine.
