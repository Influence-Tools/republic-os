# Illinois HB 291 (95th) — JUV CT-DCFS-GUARDIANSHIP

Amends the Children and Family Services Act and the Juvenile Court Act of 1987. Provides that a delinquent minor under 15 years of age (rather than under 13 years of age) may be placed in the guardianship of the Department of Children and Family Services.
