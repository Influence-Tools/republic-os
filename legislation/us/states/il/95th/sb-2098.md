# Illinois SB 2098 (95th) — TREASURER-PROGRAMS

Amends the State Treasurer Act. Authorizes the State Treasurer to create the State Treasurer Financial Education and Savings Not-for-Profit Corporation for the purposes of promoting financial literacy and savings among the residents of the State of Illinois and to issue grants and scholarships. Sets forth requirements for the creation and operation of the Corporation. Effective immediately.
