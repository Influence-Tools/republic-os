# Illinois HB 682 (95th) — PROC CD-UNIVERSITY PRINTING

Amends the Illinois Procurement Code. Exempts public institutions of higher education from the requirement that a State agency include on materials it publishes certain information concerning State authorization, publication date, number of copies printed, and printing order number. Effective immediately.
