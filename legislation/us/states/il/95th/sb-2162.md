# Illinois SB 2162 (95th) — MUNI CD-ANNEXATIONS

Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that property that is subject to certain annexation agreements with a municipality that borders the Mississippi River is subject to the jurisdiction of that annexing municipality. Effective immediately.
