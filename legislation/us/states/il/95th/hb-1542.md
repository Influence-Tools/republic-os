# Illinois HB 1542 (95th) — MUNI CD-DISCHARGE BARGAINING

Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. In non-home rule units of government, effective June 1, 2007, makes collective bargaining mandatory with respect to removal or discharge of an officer or member of the fire or police department. Effective immediately.
