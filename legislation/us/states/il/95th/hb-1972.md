# Illinois HB 1972 (95th) — CD CORR-PROBATION -DNR FND

Amends the Unified Code of Corrections. Provides that as a condition of probation or supervision, the court may require the defendant to contribute a reasonable sum of money, not to exceed the maximum amount of the fine for which the defendant was sentenced, to the investigative cash fund established by the Department of Natural Resources for the purchase of evidence for investigation purposes and to conduct investigations of various conservation law violations.
