# Illinois HB 1334 (95th) — FUTURE TEACHER-GOLDEN APPLE

Amends the School Code. Requires the Chicago Board of Education to develop a plan for implementing a program that seeks to establish common bonds between youth of various backgrounds and ethnicities, which must be similar to that of the Challenge Day organization.
