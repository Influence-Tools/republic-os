# Illinois HB 1741 (95th) — AGRICULTURE-FERTILIZER-TONNAGE

Amends the Illinois Fertilizer Act of 1961. Provides that any person distributing fertilizer in this State shall provide the Director of the Illinois Department of Agriculture with a summary report of tonnage. Provides that persons engaged in the sale of ammonium nitrate shall obtain certain information upon its distribution, and keep that information for at least 2 years. Provides that any retailer of ammonium nitrate may refuse to sell ammonium nitrate to any person attempting to purchase ammonium nitrate (i) out of season, (ii) in unusual quantities, or (iii) under suspect purchase patterns. Effective July 1, 2007.
