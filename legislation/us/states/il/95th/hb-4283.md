# Illinois HB 4283 (95th) — TWP CD-BOARD OF TRUSTEES

Amends the Township Code. Provides that certain bond proceeds shall be expended under the direction of the board of trustees of the township (instead of the highway commissioners of the township). Provides that the board of trustees of the township (instead of the highway commissioners of the township) may designate certain parcels of land to be used for the purchase of parks. Effective immediately.
