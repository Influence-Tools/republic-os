# Illinois SB 345 (95th) — CNTY CD-SALES TAX

Amends the Counties Code. In a Section that authorizes a county to impose a sales tax for public safety or transportation purposes, revises the language of the referendum imposing the tax and the additional language that appears below the question. Allows the county to include a sunset provision for the tax.
