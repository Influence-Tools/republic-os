# Illinois SB 1627 (95th) — CD CORR-PRISONER LAWSUITS

Amends the Unified Code of Corrections. Includes in the definition of "lawsuit" for the purpose of the Department of Corrections making a determination as to whether to revoke good conduct credit of a prisoner who files a frivolous lawsuit, a second or subsequent petition for post-conviction relief under the Code of Criminal Procedure of 1963 whether filed with or without leave of court or a second or subsequent petition for relief from judgment under the Code of Civil Procedure.
