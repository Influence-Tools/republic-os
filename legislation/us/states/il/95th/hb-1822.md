# Illinois HB 1822 (95th) — HORSE RACING-MONEY TO MUSEUM

Amends the Illinois Horse Racing Act of 1975. Provides that the Children's Discovery Museum in Normal, Illinois shall receive payments from the General Revenue Fund at the funding level determined by the amounts paid to the Miller Park Zoo in Bloomington, Illinois in calendar year 2006. Effective immediately.
