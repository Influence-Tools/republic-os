# Illinois HB 3621 (95th) — JUV CT-APPEALABLE ORDER

Amends the Juvenile Court Act of 1987. Deletes provision that an order of the court relating to permanency goals following a permanency hearing shall be immediately appealable as a matter of right under Supreme Court Rule 304(b)(1). Effective immediately.
