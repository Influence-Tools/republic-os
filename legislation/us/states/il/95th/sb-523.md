# Illinois SB 523 (95th) — MWRD-APPOINTMENTS

Amends the Metropolitan Water Reclamation District Act. Provides that the Director of Personnel may not make a temporary appointment for more than 119 consecutive or non-consecutive working days (now, 120 days). Makes other changes.
