# Illinois HB 4251 (95th) — VEH CD-FARM TRACTOR-SHOULDER

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that the restrictions on driving a vehicle on the shoulder of a highway do not apply to any farm tractor or implement of husbandry. Effective immediately.
