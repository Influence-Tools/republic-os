# Illinois HB 1116 (95th) — VEH CD-FIRE DEPARTMENT PLATES

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides for the issuance of special license plates for vehicles owned by a fire department or fire protection district or by a chief officer of a fire department or fire protection district. Provides for the markings on these plates.
