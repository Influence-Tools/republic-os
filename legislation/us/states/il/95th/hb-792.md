# Illinois HB 792 (95th) — MWRD-ACCOUNTING

Amends the Metropolitan Water Reclamation District Act. Provides that the annual financial report for the District shall contain financial information required by generally accepted accounting principles for governments (instead of other specified information). Effective immediately.
