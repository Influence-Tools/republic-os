# Illinois SB 454 (95th) — MARRIAGE ACT-CHILD HEALTH TRST

Amends the Illinois Marriage and Dissolution of Marriage Act. Provides that the court in a dissolution judgment may also set aside a portion of the joint or separate estates of the parties in a fund or trust for the physical and mental health (at present, for the support, maintenance, education, and general welfare) of any minor, dependent, or incompetent child of the parties.
