# Illinois SB 255 (95th) — LOC GOV- COMMISSION REPORT

Amends the Local Government Consolidation Commission Act. Provides that the Commission shall render its final report to the General Assembly no later than December 31, 2007 (instead of December 31, 2006). Effective immediately.
