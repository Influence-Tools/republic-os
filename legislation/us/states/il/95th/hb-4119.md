# Illinois HB 4119 (95th) — CIV PRO-ATTY ISSUED SUBPOENA

Amends the Code of Civil Procedure. Provides that an attorney, as an officer of the court, may issue subpoenas on behalf of a clerk for witnesses and to counties in a pending action (instead of limiting the issuance of subpoenas to court clerks).
