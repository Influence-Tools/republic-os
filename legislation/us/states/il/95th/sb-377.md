# Illinois SB 377 (95th) — PENSION CD-IL CENTURY NETWORK

Amends the State Universities Article of the Illinois Pension Code. In the definitions of "employer" and "employee", updates certain references to the Illinois Century Network, which is now part of the Bureau of Communication and Computer Services. Effective immediately.
