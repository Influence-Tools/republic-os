# Illinois HB 4648 (95th) — VEH CD-DISTING SVC CROSS PLATE

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides for issuance of special license plates to a holder, or the surviving spouse of a holder, of the Distinguished Service Cross, at no additional cost to the applicant.
