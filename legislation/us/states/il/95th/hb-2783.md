# Illinois HB 2783 (95th) — VEH CD-KOREAN SERVICE PLATES

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides for issuance of Korean Service license plates to qualified applicants, at an additional initial charge of $15. Provides that $13 of the additional initial charge shall go to the Secretary of State Special License Plate Fund. Provides that $2 of the initial charge shall go to the Korean War Memorial Construction Fund.
