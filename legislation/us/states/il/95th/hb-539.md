# Illinois HB 539 (95th) — UNIFORM PEACE OFFICERS-SOS

Amends the Uniform Peace Officers' Disciplinary Act. Provides that Secretary of State sergeants, lieutenants, commanders, and investigator trainees are included in the definition of "officer".
