# Illinois HB 4583 (95th) — MIN WAGE-DAY CAMP COUNSELOR

Amends the Minimum Wage Law. In language providing that a day camp counselor is not subject to the adult minimum wage if specified requirements are met, deletes a phrase that limited the scope of the language to day camps of organized not-for-profit corporations.
