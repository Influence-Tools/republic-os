# Illinois HB 1639 (95th) — JUV CT-EXPUNGEMENT

Amends the Juvenile Court Act of 1987. Lessens from 90 days to 45 days after notification of the petition to expunge juvenile records the period of time in which the State's Attorney or prosecutor charged with the duty of prosecuting the offense, the Department of State Police, or the arresting agency or agencies may file an objection to the petition.
