# Illinois HB 3729 (95th) — PRIVATE SEWAGE DSP-NONCNFRM

Amends the Private Sewage Disposal Licensing Act. Provides that nonconforming private sewage disposal systems shall not be subject to prescribed design requirements of the private sewage disposal code, but must instead be designed individually by a licensed professional engineer or a licensed environmental health practitioner and approved by the Department of Public Health on a case-by-case basis. Effective immediately.
