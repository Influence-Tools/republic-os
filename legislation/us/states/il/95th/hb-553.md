# Illinois HB 553 (95th) — PARK DIST-PREQUALIFY FIRMS

Amends the Park District Code. Provides that the district shall prequalify firms seeking to provide architectural, engineering, and land surveying services. Provides that the district may use prequalification lists established by State agencies.
