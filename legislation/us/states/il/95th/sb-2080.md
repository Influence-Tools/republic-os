# Illinois SB 2080 (95th) — UCC-GENERAL,DOCUMENTS OF TITLE

Amends the Uniform Commercial Code. Makes changes to Article 1, which deals with general provisions, and Article 7, which deals with documents of title, in conformance with recommendations of the National Conference of Commissioners of Uniform State Laws. Makes conforming changes in other parts of the Uniform Commercial Code.
