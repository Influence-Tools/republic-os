# Illinois SB 438 (95th) — VEH CD-SALVAGE CERTIFICATE

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that a salvage certificate may be assigned to any municipal fire department (as well as to any licensed rebuilder, automotive parts recycler, scrap processor, or out-of-state salvage vehicle buyer).
