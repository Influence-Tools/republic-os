# Illinois HB 357 (95th) — SCH CD-INTERFUND TRANSFERS

Amends the School Code. Extends the time period during which a school district other than the Chicago school district may transfer moneys from specified funds for any purpose from June 30, 2007 to June 30, 2010. Effective immediately.
