# Illinois SB 2501 (95th) — QUICKTAKE-VILLAGE LAKE HILLS

Amends the Eminent Domain Act. Grants quick-take eminent domain powers for a period of one year to the Village of Lake in the Hills for the Lake in the Hills Airport runway. Effective immediately.
