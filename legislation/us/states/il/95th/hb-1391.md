# Illinois HB 1391 (95th) — CNTY CD-CHILD ADVOCACY FEE

Amends the Counties Code. Provides that, in each county in which a Children's Advocacy Center provides services, the county board may adopt a mandatory fee of up to $5 to be paid by a defendant on a judgment of guilty or a grant of supervision under certain Sections of the Unified Code of Corrections.
