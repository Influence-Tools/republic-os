# Illinois SB 336 (95th) — PROC CD-MORTGAGE INS

Amends the Illinois Procurement Code. Provides that a contract for bond or mortgage insurance relating to housing may be entered into for any period of time less than or equal to the maximum period of time that the subject bonds or mortgage may remain outstanding. Effective immediately.
