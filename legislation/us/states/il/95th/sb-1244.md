# Illinois SB 1244 (95th) — FIRE PROT DIST-MEDICAL SERVICE

Amends the Fire Protection District Act. Provides that, for purposes of a tax levied by a fire protection district to provide an ambulance service, "ambulance service" includes pre-hospital medical services. Provides that the amendatory changes are declarative of existing law.
