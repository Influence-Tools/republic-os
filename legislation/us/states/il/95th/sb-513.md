# Illinois SB 513 (95th) — CNTY CD-LAKE CNTY SEWER

Amends the Counties Code. In a Section concerning water and sanitary facilities, provides that, in an unincorporated area of Lake County, any property or subdivision zoned for single family residential use on May 4, 2006 and located within 500 feet of a public sanitary sewer system shall have access to and the use of the sanitary sewer system.
