# Illinois SB 2749 (95th) — FIRE PROTECTION-TECH RESCUE

Amends the Township Code, the Illinois Municipal Code, and the Fire Protection District Act. Provides that a township, municipality, or fire protection district may fix, charge, and collect reasonable fees for technical rescue services.
