# Illinois SB 436 (95th) — TWP CD-CONSTRUCTION CONTRACTS

Amends the Township Code. Provides that certain contracts for construction work with an estimated cost of more than $20,000 (now, $2,500) shall be let to the lowest responsible bidder after public notice. Effective immediately.
