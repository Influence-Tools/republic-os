# Illinois SB 715 (95th) — DHS-SCHOOL HEALTH CENTERS

Creates the School Health Center Act. Requires the Department of Human Services to initiate 20 new school health centers over a 5-year period beginning July 1, 2007, and build capacity with existing school health centers in the State. Effective immediately.
