# Illinois SB 2391 (95th) — VEH CD-ACCIDENT REPORTS-LOCAL

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that the Department of Transportation may furnish copies of its written accident reports to local agencies that are engaged in highway safety research and studies. Effective immediately.
