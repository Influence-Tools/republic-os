# Illinois HB 5069 (95th) — PROP TX-PAYMENT ADJUSTMENT

Amends the Property Tax Code concerning payments under specification. Provides that the collector shall (instead of "may") receive taxes on part of any property charged with taxes when a particular specification of the part is furnished. Effective immediately.
