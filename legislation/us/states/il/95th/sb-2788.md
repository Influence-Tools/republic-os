# Illinois SB 2788 (95th) — PARK DISTRICT-PROPERTY

Amends the Chicago Park District Act. Provides that the Chicago Park District may sell, lease, or otherwise convey all or any portion of District-owned property that is used solely and exclusively as office or administrative space.
