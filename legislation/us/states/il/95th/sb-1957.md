# Illinois SB 1957 (95th) — PEN CD-IMRF-ANNUITANT TRUSTEE

Amends the Illinois Municipal Retirement Fund Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Gives the annuitant trustee the right to vote on any and all actions before the board of trustees and increases from 4 to 5 the number of concurring votes necessary for every decision or action by the board of trustees. Effective immediately.
