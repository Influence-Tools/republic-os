# Illinois HB 1403 (95th) ‚Äî METH CONTROL-PENALTIES-CHURCH

Amends the Methamphetamine Control and Community Protection Act. Provides that if a person engages in participation in methamphetamine manufacturing and the methamphetamine manufacturing occurs within 1,000 feet of a place of worship, parsonage, or former church facility, the person commits aggravated participation in methamphetamine manufacturing.
