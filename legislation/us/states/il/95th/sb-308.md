# Illinois SB 308 (95th) — IDOT-SCHOOL ENTRANCE ROAD

Amends the Department of Transportation Law of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois. Provides that the Department of Transportation shall evaluate, fund, repair, and upgrade the entrances to public educational facilities that border State highways.
