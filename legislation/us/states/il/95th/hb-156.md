# Illinois HB 156 (95th) — CRIM CD-SEX OFFENDER-CARNIVAL

Amends the Criminal Code of 1961. Provides that it is unlawful for a child sex offender to knowingly operate, manage, be employed by, volunteer at, or be associated with any carnival, circus, street fair, or public festival when persons under the age of 18 are present.
