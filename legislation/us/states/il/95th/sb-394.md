# Illinois SB 394 (95th) — HIGHER ED-POLICE TRAINING INST

Amends the Police Training Institute Act. Provides that the training offered in the Police Training Institute shall be under the direction of the Office of the Provost and Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (rather than the Division of University Extension Services of the University). Effective immediately.
