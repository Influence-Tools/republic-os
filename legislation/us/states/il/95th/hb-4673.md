# Illinois HB 4673 (95th) — ASTC-HOSP LIC-PODIATRIST AUTH

Amends the Ambulatory Surgical Treatment Center Act and the Hospital Licensing Act. Allows for the administration of anesthesia under the Acts by a podiatrist with the authority to administer anesthesia under the Podiatric Medical Practice Act of 1987. Effective immediately.
