# Illinois HB 5768 (95th) — DOC LAND TRANSFER-PARIS

Authorizes the Director of Corrections, on behalf of the State of Illinois, to convey the Ed Jenison Work Camp by quit claim deed to the City of Paris for $1. Provides that the title to the property shall revert to the State if the property ceases to be used for public purposes. Effective immediately.
