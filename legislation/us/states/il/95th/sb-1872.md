# Illinois SB 1872 (95th) — ELEC CD-DUPAGE PETITIONS

If Senate Bill 662 of the 95th General Assembly becomes law, amends the Election Code to remove the greater nominating petition signature requirement for county offices in DuPage County (from 1.5% to 0.5% of qualified primary voters). Effective immediately.
