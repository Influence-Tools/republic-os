# Illinois HB 4 (95th) — AIRPORT AUTH-COMMISSIONERS

Amends the Airport Authorities Act. Provides that, for an airport authority containing 2 or more municipalities with a population of 5,000 or more that (i) is not located wholly within the corporate limits of the municipalities, and (ii) does not have physical facilities located wholly within a single county with a population between 600,000 and 3,000,000, 2 commissioners (instead of 1) shall be appointed by each municipality and 3 commissioners shall be appointed at large. Effective immediately.
