# Illinois SB 665 (95th) — CRIM CD-EAVESDROPPING EXEMPT

Amends the Criminal Code of 1961. Exempts from an eavesdropping violation, an electronic recording, including but not limited to, a motion picture, videotape, digital, or other visual or audio recording, made of the interior of a school bus while the school bus is being used in the transportation of students to and from school and school-sponsored activities, when the school board has adopted a policy authorizing such recording, notice of such recording policy is included in student handbooks and other documents including the policies of the school, notice of the policy regarding recording is provided to parents of students, and notice of such recording is clearly posted on the door of and inside the school bus. Effective immediately.
