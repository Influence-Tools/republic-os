# Illinois SB 424 (95th) — SCH CD-REQ HIGH SCH COURSES

Amends the School Code. Provides that if a pupil successfully completes one or more of the courses required as a prerequisite to receiving a high school diploma before entering high school, then the pupil shall be given high school credit for that course. Effective immediately.
