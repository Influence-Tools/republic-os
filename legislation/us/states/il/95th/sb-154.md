# Illinois SB 154 (95th) — EPA-NEW AND USED TIRE FEE

Amends the Environmental Protection Act. With respect to the additional 50-cent new or used tire fee collected from retail customers, eliminates a provision terminating the fee on January 1, 2008. Makes revisory changes. Effective July 1, 2007.
