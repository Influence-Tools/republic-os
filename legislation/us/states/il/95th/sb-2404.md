# Illinois SB 2404 (95th) — MINORITY BUSINESS ACT-EXTEND

Amends the Business Enterprise for Minorities, Females, and Persons with Disabilities Act. Extends the repeal date of the Act from September 6, 2008 to June 30, 2010. Effective immediately.
