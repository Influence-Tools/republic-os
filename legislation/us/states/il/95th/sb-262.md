# Illinois SB 262 (95th) — RIVERBOATS-ADMISSN TAX-REENTRY

Amends the Riverboat Gambling Act. Provides, as an exception to the requirement that the admission tax be paid for each admission, that a person who exits a riverboat gambling facility and reenters that riverboat gambling facility within the same gaming day shall be subject only to the initial admission tax. Effective immediately.
