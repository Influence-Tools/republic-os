# Illinois HB 724 (95th) — PRIV BUS/VOC SCHOOL-EXEMPTION

Amends the Private Business and Vocational Schools Act. Removes the dates on which an educational institution must have enrolled a majority of its students in degree programs and maintained an accredited status in order to not be considered to be a private business and vocational school under the Act. Effective immediately.
