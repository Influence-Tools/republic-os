# Illinois HB 841 (95th) — VEH CD-ABANDONED VEH-NOTICE

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that the clerk of the court shall (rather than may) notify the Secretary of State whenever a resident of this state fails to pay any fine, charge, or cost imposed for violating the provision of the Code regarding abandonment of vehicles, or a similar provision of a local ordinance.
