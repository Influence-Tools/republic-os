# Illinois HB 1643 (95th) — IL SUICIDE PREVENTION ALLIANCE

Amends the Illinois Suicide Prevention, Education, and Treatment Act. Deletes references to the "Illinois Suicide Prevention Strategic Planning Committee" and the "Committee" and replaces them with the "Illinois Suicide Prevention Alliance" and the "Alliance".
