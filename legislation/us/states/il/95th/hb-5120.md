# Illinois HB 5120 (95th) — PENCD-TRS-DISABLED ADULT CHILD

Amends the Downstate Teacher Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Removes language that renders individuals over the age of 18 who are adjudged to be disabled persons under specified provisions of the Probate Act of 1975 and who are receiving specified benefits under the Illinois Public Aid Code ineligible to receive benefits under the Article. Effective immediately.
