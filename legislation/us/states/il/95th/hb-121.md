# Illinois HB 121 (95th) — SPEECH PATHOLOGY-EXTEND SUNSET

Amends the Regulatory Sunset Act to change the repeal date of the Illinois Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology Practice Act from January 1, 2008 to January 1, 2018. Effective immediately.
