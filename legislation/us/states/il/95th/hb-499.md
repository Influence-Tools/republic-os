# Illinois HB 499 (95th) — LOCAL LIBRARY-TRUSTEE PETITION

Amends the Illinois Local Library Act. Makes the minimum signature requirement for a nominating petition for the office of library board trustee 2% of the number of votes cast at the last election for library trustee or 25, whichever is less (now, 50).
