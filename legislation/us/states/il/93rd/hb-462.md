# Illinois HB 462 (93rd) — MWRD-WORKING CASH FUND TRANSFR

Amends the Metropolitan Water Reclamation District Act. Provides that the Board, by a two-thirds vote of all its members, may transfer fund balances between its Working Cash Funds. Effective immediately.
