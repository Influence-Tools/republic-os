# Illinois HB 1250 (93rd) — SEWER FACLTY EXPANSN GUIDELNS

Creates the Facility Planning Area Rules Act. Requires the Illinois Environmental Protection Agency to propose rules and procedures for facility planning area amendments. Gives guidelines for the proposed rules and procedures. Repeals the Act on January 1, 2007. Effective immediately.
