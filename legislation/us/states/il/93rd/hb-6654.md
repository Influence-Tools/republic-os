# Illinois HB 6654 (93rd) — LIQ CNTRL-LICENSE REVOCATION

Amends the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Provides that the Illinois Liquor Control Commission shall refuse the issuance or renewal of a license if the licensee or license applicant has violated certain provisions of the Retailers' Occupation Tax Act concerning the filing of returns. Provides that the Illinois Liquor Control Commission shall suspend or revoke (now revoke) the license of a licensee who has violated certain provisions of the Retailers' Occupation Tax Act concerning the filing of returns.
