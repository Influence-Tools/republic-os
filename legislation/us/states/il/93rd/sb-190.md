# Illinois SB 190 (93rd) — DENTAL PRACTICE-DENTAL HYGIENE

Amends the Illinois Dental Practice Act. Provides that a dentist's written order to a hygienist of necessary services for patients in a long-term care facility or a mental health or developmental disability facility who are unable to travel to a dental office must be implemented within 120 (rather than 90) days of its issuance.
