# Illinois HB 3411 (93rd) — BI-STATE DEVELOPMENT AGENCY

Amends the Bi-State Development Agency Act. Provides that upon the expiration of a term or of a vacancy in the office of commissioner, the County Board Chairman of either Madison County or St. Clair County (instead of the Governor) shall appoint a replacement. Provides procedures for appointments.
