# Illinois SB 1124 (93rd) — SANITARY-DISSOLVE DISTRICTS

Amends the Sanitary District Act of 1917 to provide an alternative method to dissolve certain sanitary districts located in a county with a population not in excess of 1,000,000 persons and that meet stated criteria. Effective immediately.
