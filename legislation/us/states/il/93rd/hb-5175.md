# Illinois HB 5175 (93rd) — VEH CD-INSURANCE CARD RULES

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that the Secretary of State shall adopt rules requiring that reasonable measures be taken to prevent the fraudulent production of insurance cards. Effective January 1, 2005.
