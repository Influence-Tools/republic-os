# Illinois HB 2441 (93rd) — CONSUMER FRAUD ACT-WAIVER

Amends the Consumer Fraud and Deceptive Business Practices Act. Provides that any waiver by a consumer of the Protections in the Act is deemed contrary to public policy and is void and unenforceable.
