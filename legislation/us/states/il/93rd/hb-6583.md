# Illinois HB 6583 (93rd) — CNTY CD-TIF EXTEND

Amends the County Economic Development Project Area Tax Increment Allocation Act. Provides that a county may be authorized under the County Economic Development Project Area Tax Increment Allocation Act to establish economic development project areas and to adopt tax increment allocation financing for those areas within 20 months following the effective date of the amendatory Act (the previous authorization expired 20 months after May 7, 1991, the effective date of the Act). Effective immediately.
