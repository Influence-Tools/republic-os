# Illinois SB 1408 (93rd) — TRANSPORTATION RESOURCES

Creates the Interagency Coordinating Committee on Transportation Act. Creates the Illinois Coordinating Committee on Transportation to foster the coordination of public and private transportation services and establishes its membership, duties, and powers. Provides that the Governor or his or her designee shall serve as chairperson, and that the Secretary of Transportation and a representative of a community based organization in transportation, or their designees, shall serve as vice-chairpersons and be responsible for staff support for the Committee. Effective immediately.
