# Illinois HB 3285 (93rd) — GENDER-NEUTRAL STATUTES COMMSN

Creates the Gender-Neutral Statutes Commission Act. Creates the Gender-Neutral Statutes Commission, to consist of one member appointed by each of the Legislative Leaders. Directs the Commission to study the fiscal impact and other consequences of undertaking a comprehensive effort to make the Illinois Compiled Statutes gender neutral. Directs the Commission to report its findings by January 15, 2004. Effective immediately.
