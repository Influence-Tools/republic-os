# Illinois SB 2123 (93rd) — ELECTION CODE 2004 ELECTION

Amends the Election Code. Authorizes the State Board of Elections to take such action as necessary to give effect to directions of the national committee of an established political party regarding certification of the Presidential and Vice Presidential candidate selected at the national nominating convention in 2004.
