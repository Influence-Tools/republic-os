# Illinois HB 4374 (93rd) — PUBLIC LABOR REL-GRIEVANCE

Amends the Illinois Public Labor Relations Act. Provides that submission of all disciplinary matters to the grievance and arbitration procedure is a mandatory subject of bargaining notwithstanding the existence of any other applicable statute.
