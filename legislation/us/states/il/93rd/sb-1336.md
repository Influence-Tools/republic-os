# Illinois SB 1336 (93rd) — PUBLIC CONSTRUCTION BOND ACT

Amends the Public Construction Bond Act. Provides that a contractor shall not be required to post a cash bond or a letter of credit in addition to or as a substitute for the bond required of every contractor performing public work costing over $5,000.
