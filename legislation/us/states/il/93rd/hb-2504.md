# Illinois HB 2504 (93rd) — CLERKS OF CT-INTERMEDIARIES

Amends the Clerks of Court Act. Provides that no fees other than the filing fee for adoption may be charged to any person in connection with proceedings for the appointment of a confidential intermediary under the Adoption Act. Effective immediately.
