# Illinois SB 406 (93rd) — CRIM CD-SEXUAL ASSAULT-CONSENT

Amends the Criminal Code of 1961. Provides that a victim may not be deemed to have consented to an act of sexual penetration or sexual conduct if the victim initially consented to the sexual penetration or sexual conduct and later withdraws consent if the accused continues the act of sexual penetration or sexual conduct after the victim withdraws consent. Effective immediately.
