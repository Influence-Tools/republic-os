# Illinois HB 4239 (93rd) — CNTY CD-CHILD CUSTODY EXCHG

Amends the Counties Code. Provides that any county (now, only a county with a population of over 100,000 and less than 1,000,000) may establish a neutral site custody exchange fund by the passage of an ordinance by the county board. Effective immediately.
