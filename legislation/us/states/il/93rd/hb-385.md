# Illinois HB 385 (93rd) — VEHICLE CODE-EMERGENCY VEHICLE

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Codes. Sets a minimum fine for a violation of proper driving conduct when approaching a stationary authorized emergency vehicle. Effective immediately.
