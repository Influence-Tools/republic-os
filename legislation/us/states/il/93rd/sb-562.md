# Illinois SB 562 (93rd) — ATM REVERSE PIN SENDS ALARM

Amends the Electronic Fund Transfer Act. Provides that terminals must be designed and programmed so that the entry of a consumer's personal identification in reverse order causes an alarm to be sent to the local law enforcement agency.
