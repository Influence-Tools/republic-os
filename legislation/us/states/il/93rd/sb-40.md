# Illinois SB 40 (93rd) — EMS ACT-FEC LICENSE

Amends the Emergency Medical Services (EMS) Systems Act. Makes changes in provisions allowing the Department to issue a Freestanding Emergency Center license to a facility based on location. Deletes provisions making the Freestanding Emergency Center license part of a demonstration program.
