# Illinois SB 1352 (93rd) — CONDO ACT-PUBLIC HOUS AUTHORIT

Amends the Condominium Property Act. Adds the Public Housing Authority as a lessor under the definition of "leasehold condominium". Provides that the execution of a declaration as required by the Act does not make the lessor a developer for purposes of the Act. Effective immediately.
