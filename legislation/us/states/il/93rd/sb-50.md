# Illinois SB 50 (93rd) — VEH SEAT BELTS-STOPS-SEARCHES

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code and the Code of Criminal Procedure of 1963. Deletes language providing that law enforcement officers may not stop or search any vehicle solely on the basis of the failure of the occupants to wear seat belts.
