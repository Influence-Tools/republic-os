# Illinois HB 6983 (93rd) — PROC CD-RESIDENT PREFERENCE

Amends the Illinois Procurement Code. Provides that when the lowest responsible bidder on a contract is a non-resident, the State may award the contract to the lowest responsible resident bidder who rebids at or below the non-resident's bid.
