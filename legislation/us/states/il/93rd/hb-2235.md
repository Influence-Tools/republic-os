# Illinois HB 2235 (93rd) — EARLY CHILDHOOD ED BLOCK GRANT

Amends the School Code. Provides that 11% (instead of 8%) of the Early Childhood Education Block Grant shall be used to fund programs for children ages 0-3. Effective July 1, 2003.
