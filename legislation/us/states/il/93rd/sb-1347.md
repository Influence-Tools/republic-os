# Illinois SB 1347 (93rd) — PROBATE-SUMMARY ADMIN-$100,000

Amends the Probate Act of 1975. Provides that a decedent's estate qualifies for summary administration if the gross value of the estate does not exceed $100,000 (rather than $50,000).
