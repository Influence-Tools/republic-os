# Illinois HB 496 (93rd) — PERSONNEL APPNTMNT ELIGIB LIST

Amends the Secretary of State Merit Employment Code. Increases to 10 (now, 3) the number of persons ranking highest on the appropriate eligible list who must be considered for an appointment to fill a vacancy. Effective immediately.
