# Illinois SB 1383 (93rd) — CIVIC CENTER CD-INCREASE BID L

Amends the Civic Center Code. Provides that certain contracts with a value of more than $10,000 (now, $2,500 or $5,000) shall be awarded to the highest or lowest responsible bidder, as the case may be, after advertising for bids. Provides exceptions to the requirement that contracts be awarded to the lowest bidder in certain circumstances.
