# Illinois HB 3663 (93rd) — SAV BNK SAV LOAN EXAMS

Amends the Illinois Savings and Loan Act of 1985 and the Savings Bank Act. Makes provisions for the disclosure of information by the Commissioner of Banks and Real Estate in connection with examinations of savings banks and savings and loan associations. Effective immediately.
