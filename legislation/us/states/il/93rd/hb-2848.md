# Illinois HB 2848 (93rd) — EARLY INTRVENTN SRVCS-CONTRACT

Amends the Early Intervention Services System Act. Provides that contracts with or grants to regional intake entities must be made subject to public bid under a request for proposals process no later than July 1, 2005.
