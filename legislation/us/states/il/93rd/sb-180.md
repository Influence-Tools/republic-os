# Illinois SB 180 (93rd) — VITAL RECORDS ACT-INFO

Amends the Vital Records Act. Provides that an incomplete Record of Foreign Birth shall be completed only upon the receipt of a certified order from the circuit court in Illinois providing the additional information. Effective immediately.
