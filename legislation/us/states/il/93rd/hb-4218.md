# Illinois HB 4218 (93rd) — CLIN PSYCHOLOGISTS-BOARD

Amends the Clinical Psychologist Licensing Act. Provides that the members of the Clinical Psychologists Licensing and Disciplinary Board appointed to fill a vacancy shall be eligible for reappointment to 2 full terms (rather only one full term). Effective immediately.
