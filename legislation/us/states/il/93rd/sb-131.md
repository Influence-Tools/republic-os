# Illinois SB 131 (93rd) — HOSPICE PROGRAM LIC-TERMINL IL

Amends the Hospice Program Licensing Act. Changes the definition of terminally ill to include a patient that has a life expectancy of one year or less (rather than 6 months or less). Makes changes in the definition of "hospice service plan". Effective immediately.
