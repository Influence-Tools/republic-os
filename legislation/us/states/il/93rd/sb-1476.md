# Illinois SB 1476 (93rd) — PEN CD-GA-ACCELERATED 3% INCRS

Amends the General Assembly Article of the Illinois Pension Code. Disqualifies persons who first become participants after the effective date from the provision allowing members with more than 20 years of service to earn 3% annual increases before withdrawal from service. Effective immediately.
