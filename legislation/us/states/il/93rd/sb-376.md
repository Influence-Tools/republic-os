# Illinois SB 376 (93rd) — DEATH CERTIFICATE-DEMENTIA

Amends the Vital Records Act. Provides that the medical certification of cause of death shall expressly provide an opportunity for the person completing the certification to indicate that the death was caused in whole or in part by a dementia-related disease, Parkinson's Disease, or Parkinson-Dementia Complex. Effective immediately.
