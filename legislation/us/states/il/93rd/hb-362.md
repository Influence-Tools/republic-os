# Illinois HB 362 (93rd) — CAPITAL DEV BD-ENERGY CODE

Amends the Capital Development Board Act. Requires the Board by January 1, 2004, to adopt an energy code for the construction or repair of facilities funded by General Assembly appropriations. Effective immediately.
