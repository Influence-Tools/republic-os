# Illinois SB 2926 (93rd) — CHILD CARE-DEFINITIONS

Amends the Children and Family Services Act. Defines "youth transitional housing program". Amends the Child Care Act of 1969. Changes the definition of "child care center" and adds a definition for "partially exempt child care program".
