# Illinois HB 2525 (93rd) — CRIM CD-DOMESTIC BATTERY

Amends the Criminal Code of 1961. Includes a prior conviction under the laws of another jurisdiction that is similar to a violation under the laws of this State for which the defendant is guilty of a Class 4 felony for domestic battery.
