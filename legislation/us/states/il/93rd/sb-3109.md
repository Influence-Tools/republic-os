# Illinois SB 3109 (93rd) — SCH CD-REPORT STU STATISTICS

Amends the School Code. Requires a school board to report to the State Board of Education on a monthly basis the annual student dropout rate (now does not need to be reported every month) and the number of students who graduate, transfer, or otherwise leave.
