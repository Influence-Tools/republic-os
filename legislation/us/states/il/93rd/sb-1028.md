# Illinois SB 1028 (93rd) — COMMEMORATIVE DATES-JUNETEENTH

Amends the State Commemorative Dates Act. Designates the third Saturday in June of each year as Juneteenth National Freedom Day to commemorate the abolition of slavery throughout the United States and its territories in 1865. Effective immediately.
