# Illinois HB 3321 (93rd) — ICC-PUBLIC UTILITY PENALTY

Amends the Public Utilities Act. Establishes a procedure for the imposition and collection of penalties with respect to public utilities other than telecommunications carriers. Provides that civil penalties related to the late filing of reports, taxes, and other filings shall be paid into the Public Utility Fund. Establishes penalties for knowing misrepresentation in testimony before the Illinois Commerce Commission. Effective immediately.
