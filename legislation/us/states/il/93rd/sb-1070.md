# Illinois SB 1070 (93rd) — ED LABOR REL-EMPLOYEE-GRAD STU

Amends the Illinois Educational Labor Relations Act to provide that a graduate student employed by his or her institution of higher education is an "educational employee" within the meaning of the Act. Effective immediately.
