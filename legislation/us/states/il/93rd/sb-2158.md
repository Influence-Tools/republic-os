# Illinois SB 2158 (93rd) — MUNI CD-TIF-LIBRARY DISTRICTS

Amends the Tax Increment Allocation Redevelopment Act in the Illinois Municipal Code. Allows the distribution of a portion of the tax increment funds to library districts in the redevelopment project area that meet certain criteria.
