# Illinois SB 2401 (93rd) — VEH CD-SOS NOTICE-DONATION

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that the Secretary of State may include, with any notice that the Secretary is authorized or required to give under the Code or any other law regulating the operation of vehicles, any material, from any source, that pertains to the donation of vehicles to charitable organizations.
