# Illinois HB 4989 (93rd) — PEST CONTROL-CERT RENEWAL

Amends the Structural Pest Control Act. Provides that in order to have his or her certificate renewed, a certified technician must furnish evidence that he or she has attended a minimum of 9 classroom hours, in increments of 3 hours or more, of training at pest control training seminars approved by the Department of Public Health (now, at least one training seminar and any additional approved seminar on structural pest control required by any rule promulgated under the Act).
