# Illinois SB 291 (93rd) — SHERIFF WARRANT LIABILITY

Amends the Counties Code. Provides that no sheriff shall be civilly liable for serving any warrant, order, process, or judgment that, on its face, is apparently legally issued to him or her by a court of the State of Illinois. Effective immediately.
