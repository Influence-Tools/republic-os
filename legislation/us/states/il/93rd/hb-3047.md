# Illinois HB 3047 (93rd) — PHYS ASSTS-SUPERVISING PHYS

Amends the Medical Practice Act of 1987 and the Physician Assistant Practice Act of 1987 to allow supervising physicians to supervise more than 2 physician assistants. Effective immediately.
