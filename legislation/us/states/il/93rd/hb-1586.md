# Illinois HB 1586 (93rd) — UNINCORP PVT RDS-EXTND DATE

Amends the Property Tax Code. Changes to January 1, 1995 (now, January 1, 1959) the date by which a subdivision in an unincorporated area of a county under township organization that contains at least one mile (now, 2 miles) of streets or roadways situated entirely within a township and not owned by the county or any other unit of government must be platted in order for the highway commissioner to propose a special service area. Effective immediately.
