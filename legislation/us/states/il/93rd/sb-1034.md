# Illinois SB 1034 (93rd) — FOI-EXEMPT FACILITY PLANS

Amends the Freedom of Information Act. Provides that the exemption from inspection and copying requirements for architects' plans and engineers' technical submissions includes but is not limited to specified utility, transportation, public, and government facilities. Effective immediately.
