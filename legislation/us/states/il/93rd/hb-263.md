# Illinois HB 263 (93rd) — PORTS-POLICE POWERS & BENEFITS

Amends the Illinois International Port District Act. Provides that the District may police its property and exercise police powers, may enforce any rule or regulation provided by the ordinances of the District, including regulation of vehicular traffic and commercial maritime traffic, and may employ and commission police officers and other qualified persons. Provides that members of the Illinois International Port District Board shall not be prevented from receiving non-salary benefits of the type received by employees of the District. Effective immediately.
