# Illinois SB 2630 (93rd) — PROBATE-SMALL ESTATE-VALUE

Amends the Probate Act of 1975. In provisions concerning a small estate affidavit, increases the permissible maximum value of the decedent's personal estate from $50,000 to $100,000. Effective immediately.
