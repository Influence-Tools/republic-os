# Illinois HB 3082 (93rd) — PROCUREMENT-PREVAILING WAGE

Amends the Illinois Procurement Code. Applies the prevailing wage provisions to contracts for the following services: buildings and grounds, site technician, and natural resources. Removes the prohibition against considering State employee collective bargaining agreements when setting a prevailing wage rate.
