# Illinois SB 320 (93rd) — MWRD-BOND LIMIT INCREASE

Amends the Metropolitan Water Reclamation District Act. Increases the amount of debt that the District may issue during a budget year, from $100,000,000 to $150,000,000. Effective immediately.
