# Illinois HB 273 (93rd) — MUNICIPALITY-BOND INVESTMENT

Amends the Public Funds Investment Act and the Investment of Municipal Funds Act. Provides that a county, park district, sanitary district, or other municipal corporation may, by resolution or by ordinance (now, by ordinance only) use money in specified funds to purchase bonds and other interest bearing obligations of states other than the State of Illinois or of any political subdivision or agency of the State of Illinois or of any other state, whether the interest earned thereon is taxable or tax-exempt under federal law (now, the funds can only be used to purchase only certain municipal bonds or bonds and other interest bearing obligations of the United States and the State of Illinois). Effective immediately.
