# Illinois SB 3091 (93rd) — SCH CD-WAIVERS-ELIGIBILITY

Amends the School Code. Allows a joint agreement made up of school districts or a regional superintendent of schools on behalf of schools and programs operated by the regional office of education to apply for a waiver or modification of mandates within the School Code and State Board of Education rules. Effective immediately.
