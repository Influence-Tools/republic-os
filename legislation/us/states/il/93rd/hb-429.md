# Illinois HB 429 (93rd) — HUMAN SERVICES 211 COLLABOR

Creates the Human Services 211 Collaboration Board Act. Establishes a 16-member Human Services 211 Collaboration Board. Provides for the appointment of members to the Board by the Governor. Requires the Board to implement 211 as a statewide, non-emergency telephone number for information about governmental and non-profit services and referrals to human services agencies. Requires the Board to establish standards for 211 systems. Requires the Board to establish a pilot program for awarding grants to 211 providers. Establishes certain criteria for awarding the grants. Requires the Board to annually report to the Illinois Commerce Commission, the Governor, and the General Assembly on the use of 211 services in Illinois. Effective immediately.
