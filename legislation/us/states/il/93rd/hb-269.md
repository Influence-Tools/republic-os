# Illinois HB 269 (93rd) — COURT CLERKS-FEES

Amends the Clerks of Courts Act. Provides for a combined fee schedule for circuit clerks' fees in counties having a population of not more than 500,000 inhabitants. Repeals fee schedule for counties having a population of 180,000 inhabitants or less. Effective July 1, 2003.
