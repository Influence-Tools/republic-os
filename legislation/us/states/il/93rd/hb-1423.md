# Illinois HB 1423 (93rd) — DVET-LASALLE VET HOME CAPACITY

Amends the Department of Veterans Affairs Act. Provides that subject to appropriation, the Department shall increase by at least 80 beds the capacity of the Illinois Veterans Home at LaSalle and shall request and expend federal grants for this Veterans Home addition. Effective immediately.
