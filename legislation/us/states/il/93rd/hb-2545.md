# Illinois HB 2545 (93rd) — REDEPLOY ILLINOIS PROGRAM

Amends the Probation and Probation Officers Act. Creates the Redeploy Illinois Program. Permits pilot projects in which a county or group of counties may develop an agreement with the Supreme Court to reduce their number of commitments of juvenile offenders to the Department of Corrections, and then use the savings to develop local programming for youth who would otherwise have been committed to the Department of Corrections. Provides that the county or group of counties shall agree to limit their commitments to 75% of the level of commitments from the average number of juvenile commitments for the past 3 years, and shall receive the savings to redeploy for local programming for juveniles who would otherwise be held in confinement.
