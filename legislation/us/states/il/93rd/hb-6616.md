# Illinois HB 6616 (93rd) — PENCD-IMRF-CAIRO PUB UTIL

Amends the IMRF Article of the Illinois Pension Code to add the Cairo Public Utility Company to the list of entities permitted to participate in the Fund as participating instrumentalities. Effective immediately.
