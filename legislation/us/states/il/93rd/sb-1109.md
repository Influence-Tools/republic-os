# Illinois SB 1109 (93rd) — MEDICAID-RX DRUGS-NO COPAYMENT

Amends the Illinois Public Aid Code. Eliminates the current provision for a maximum $3 co-payment for brand-name drugs under the Medicaid program, and provides that no co-payment requirement may exist for prescription drugs. Effective immediately.
