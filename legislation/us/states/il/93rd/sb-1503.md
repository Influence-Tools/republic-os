# Illinois SB 1503 (93rd) — INCOME WITHHOLD SUPPORT-PENALT

Amends the Income Withholding for Support Act. Provides that a payor who knowingly fails to pay on time the amount designated in an income withholding notice to the State Disbursement Unit (whether or not the payor withheld the amount) must pay a $100 per day penalty for each day the payment is late.
