# Illinois HB 1150 (93rd) — ATM POINT OF SALE SURCHARGE

Amends the Electronic Fund Transfer Act in relation to notices required with respect to the imposition of surcharges. Removes the exemption from the notice requirements applicable to point-of-sale terminals.
