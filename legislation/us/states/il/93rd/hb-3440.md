# Illinois HB 3440 (93rd) — NURS HM-LIFE CR-VACCINATIONS

Amends the Life Care Facilities Act and the Nursing Home Care Act. Provides that the facilities licensed under the Acts must document evidence of vaccination for influenza and pneumococcal disease for each resident age 60 and over, unless the vaccination is medically contraindicated or the resident has refused the vaccination. Effective immediately.
