# Illinois SB 4 (93rd) — INCOME TX-EARNED INCOME CREDIT

Amends the Illinois Income Tax Act. Removes language that terminates the earned income tax credit with taxable years ending on or before December 31, 2002. Exempts the credit from the sunset requirements. Effective immediately.
