# Illinois HB 293 (93rd) — AGING-APPLY MEDICAL ASSIST

Amends the Illinois Act on the Aging. Requires all financially eligible applicants and recipients (now, all applicants and recipients) of services of the Department on Aging to apply for medical assistance under Article V of the Illinois Public Aid Code.
