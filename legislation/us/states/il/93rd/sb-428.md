# Illinois SB 428 (93rd) — ELEC CD-TOUCH SCREEN VOTING

Amends the Election Code. In the Article governing the use of in-precinct voting systems with optic scan technology, makes changes in terms and procedures to accommodate the use of touch-screen computers to display and mark ballots. Effective immediately.
