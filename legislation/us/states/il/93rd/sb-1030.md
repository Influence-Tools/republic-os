# Illinois SB 1030 (93rd) — TOBACCO-MINORS-LUNCH WAGON

Amends the Sale of Tobacco to Minors Act to provide that the sale or distribution at no charge of cigarettes from a lunch wagon engaging in any sales activity within 1,000 feet of any public or private elementary or secondary school grounds is prohibited.
