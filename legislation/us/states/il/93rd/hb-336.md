# Illinois HB 336 (93rd) — PUB LABOR-SMALL GOV UNITS

Amends the Illinois Public Labor Relations Act to provide that the Act is not applicable to units of local government employing less than 35 employees at the time the Petition for Certification or Representation is filed. Provides that bargaining units certified by the Board that fall below 35 employees after certification remain subject to the Act.
