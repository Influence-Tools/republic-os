# Illinois SB 2122 (93rd) — VEH CD-PROOF OF INSURANCE

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that every application for vehicle registration or renewal of vehicle registration must be accompanied by proof of insurance coverage. Specifies requirements for proof. Effective immediately.
