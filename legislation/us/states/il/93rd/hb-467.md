# Illinois HB 467 (93rd) — ATM LINK CARD NO FEE

Amends the Electronic Fund Transfer Act. Prohibits the imposition of fees upon a consumer for the use of a Link card or other access device used to obtain benefits under the Illinois Public Aid Code.
