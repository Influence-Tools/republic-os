# Illinois HB 7307 (93rd) — HEALTH FAC PLAN BD-REAPPOINT

Amends the Illinois Health Facilities Planning Act. Provides for the termination of the terms of office of the current members of the Health Facilities Planning Board and the appointment of new members by the Governor. Effective immediately.
