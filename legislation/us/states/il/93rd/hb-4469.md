# Illinois HB 4469 (93rd) — LOCGOV-PROCUREMENT CONTRACT

Amends the Downstate Forest Preserve District Act and the Park District Code. Provides that contracts for supplies, materials, and work that exceed $20,000 (instead of $10,000) must be let by competitive bidding.
