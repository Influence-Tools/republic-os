# Illinois SB 685 (93rd) — LOCAL MASS TRANSIT DIST ACT

Amends the Local Mass Transit District Act. Makes various changes to provisions concerning: definitions; the creation of a local mass transit district; annexation to a mass transit district; employees of a mass transit district; compensation of trustees; the powers of a local mass transit district and of the board of trustees of the district; and use and occupation taxes. Repeals a Section concerning the disconnection of territory.
