# Illinois SB 886 (93rd) — LOC GOV-CABLE TELEVISIONS

Amends the Counties Code and the Illinois Municipal Code concerning community antenna television systems. Provides that within 20 days after a county or municipality issues a permit or other authorization for the construction of residential buildings within any residentially-zoned subdivision created through a plat recorded after July 1, 2003, the county or municipality must notify any community antenna television system franchised to serve all or a portion of the subdivision of the issuance of the permit or other authorization. Provides that the notice must identify the owner of record of the property and the party to whom construction authorization has been granted.
