# Illinois SB 1368 (93rd) — SCH CD-CHI SCH FINANCE AUTH

Amends the School Finance Authority Act of the School Code. Provides that the powers and responsibilities granted to or imposed upon the Chicago School Finance Authority and the Chicago Board of Education under that Act concerning financial plans, budgets, contracts, staffing levels, expenditures, a chief financial officer, financial and managerial audits, cash accounts and bank accounts, financial, management, and budgetary systems, and reports are suspended until December 31, 2010 (instead of July 1, 2004). Effective immediately.
