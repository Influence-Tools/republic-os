# Illinois SB 1523 (93rd) — DEAF & HARD OF HEARING COMMN

Amends the Deaf and Hard of Hearing Commission Act. Provides that members of the Commission shall serve until their successors are appointed and qualified. Makes changes concerning the duties of the Commission. Provides that the Commission shall promulgate rules and regulations to implement this Act in accordance with the Illinois Administrative Procedure Act. Makes other changes.
