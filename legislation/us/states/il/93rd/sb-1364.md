# Illinois SB 1364 (93rd) — DHS-TANF-CIVIL RIGHTS IMPACT

Amends the Illinois Public Aid Code. Requires the Department of Human Services to file an annual report with the Governor and the General Assembly that details the disparate impact of various provisions of the TANF program on Whites, Blacks/African Americans, and Latinos. Sets forth matters to be included in the report. Effective immediately.
