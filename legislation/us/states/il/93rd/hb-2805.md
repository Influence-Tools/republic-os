# Illinois HB 2805 (93rd) — BD HIGH ED-FACULTY MEMBER

Amends the Board of Higher Education Act. Provides that one of the 10 members of the Board of Higher Education that is appointed by the Governor, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate, must be a faculty member at a public university.
