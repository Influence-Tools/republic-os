# Illinois HB 4351 (93rd) — PROP TX-APPORTIONMENT

Amends the Property Tax Code. Requires the county clerks of each county in which there was an under extension to proportionately increase the levy of that taxing district pursuant to a court order if a court, at any time (instead of prior to August 16, 2001, which was the effective date of P.A. 92-377), enters a final judgment that there was an over extension or under extension of taxes for an overlapping taxing district. Effective immediately.
