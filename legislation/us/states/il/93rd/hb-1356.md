# Illinois HB 1356 (93rd) — MEDICAL PRACTICE-DISC BOARD

Amends the Medical Practice Act of 1987. Gives voting rights to the 2 public members of the Medical Disciplinary Board and considers them for determining a quorum. Effective immediately.
