# Illinois SB 1414 (93rd) — HOSP LIC-PROT OF RECORDS

Amends the Hospital Licensing Act. Makes changes in provisions concerning the confidentiality of medical records and information. Removes provisions exempting persons from civil or criminal liability or professional discipline who act in good faith concerning the dissemination of information. Effective immediately.
