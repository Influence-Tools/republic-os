# Illinois SB 133 (93rd) — ENTPRS ZONE-ADDITIONAL ZONES

Amends the Illinois Enterprise Zone Act. Provides that beginning in the calendar year 2004 and until December 31, 2008, a total of 5 additional enterprise zones may be certified by the Department of Commerce and Community Affairs.
