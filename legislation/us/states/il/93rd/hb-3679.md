# Illinois HB 3679 (93rd) — PARK DIST-DESIGN BUILD CONTRCT

Amends the Park District Code. Allows a park district to use competitive selection and the prequalification of responsible bidders in connection with design/build projects. Sets the procedures that must be followed when awarding design/build contracts.
