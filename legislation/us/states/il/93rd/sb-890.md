# Illinois SB 890 (93rd) — SCH CD-REQUIRE ASIAN HISTORY

Amends the School Code. Requires every public elementary school and high school to include in its curriculum a unit of instruction studying the events of Asian History, including South Asia and the Pacific Islands. Amends the State Mandates Act to require implementation without reimbursement. Effective July 1, 2003.
