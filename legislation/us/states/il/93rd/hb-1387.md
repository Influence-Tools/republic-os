# Illinois HB 1387 (93rd) — COM COL-CHI-TREASURER BOND PEN

Amends the Public Community College Act. Makes the penalty of the bond of the treasurer of the Chicago community college district the same as the penalty of the bonds of the treasurers of all other community college districts in this State. Effective immediately.
