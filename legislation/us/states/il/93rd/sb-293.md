# Illinois SB 293 (93rd) — CIRCUT BREAKR-M SCLEROSIS DRUG

Amends the Senior Citizens and Disabled Persons Property Tax Relief and Pharmaceutical Assistance Act. Provides that beginning on January 1, 2004, the pharmaceutical assistance program covers prescription drugs used in the treatment of multiple sclerosis.
