# Illinois HB 4478 (93rd) — INS-SMALL EMPLYR INS POOL

Amends the Comprehensive Health Insurance Plan Act. Requires the Illinois Comprehensive Health Insurance Board to conduct a study pertaining to the feasibility of establishing a small employer health insurance pool in which employers may provide affordable health insurance coverage to their employees. Effective immediately.
