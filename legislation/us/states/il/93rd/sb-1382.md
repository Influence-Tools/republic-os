# Illinois SB 1382 (93rd) — BONDS-MUNI RISK MGMT POOL

Amends the Official Bond Payment Act. Provides that a municipality may obtain, from a risk management pool of municipalities, any official bonds required by law to be furnished by officers of the municipality. Effective January 1, 2004.
