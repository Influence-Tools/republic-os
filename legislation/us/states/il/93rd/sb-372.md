# Illinois SB 372 (93rd) — SCH CD-DRIVR ED-ORGAN DONATION

Amends the Driver Education Act of the School Code. Requires a driver education course to provide at least 30 minutes of classroom instruction relating to organ, tissue, and blood donation and organ, tissue, and blood donation procedures. Prohibits a student from being required to participate in this instruction if his or her parent or guardian objects. Amends the State Mandates Act to require implementation without reimbursement. Effective immediately.
