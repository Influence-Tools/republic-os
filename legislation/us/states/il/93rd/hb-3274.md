# Illinois HB 3274 (93rd) — GA SCHOLARSHIP-ANY STATE UNIV

Amends the School Code. Provides that each member of the General Assembly may annually nominate 2 students for scholarships at any State supported university (now one of the 2 must be to the University of Illinois). Makes related changes. Effective immediately.
