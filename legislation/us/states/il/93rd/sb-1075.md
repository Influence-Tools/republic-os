# Illinois SB 1075 (93rd) — RURAL BOND BANK-ISSUANCE

Amends the Rural Bond Bank Act. Increases the Bank's bond authorization to $500,000,000 outstanding at any time. Increases the maximum amount used to purchase securities issued by certain units of local government to $87,500,000. Effective immediately.
