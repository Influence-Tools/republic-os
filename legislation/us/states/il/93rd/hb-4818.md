# Illinois HB 4818 (93rd) — MEDICAID-SHELTRD CARE-MIN WAGE

Amends the Illinois Public Aid Code. Provides that the Department of Public Aid shall update the reimbursement rates paid to sheltered care facilities under the Medicaid program to provide for an increase in those rates sufficient to cover a sheltered care facility's expenses incurred in complying with the increase in the State minimum wage as provided in Public Act 93-581. Effective immediately.
