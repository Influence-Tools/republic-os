# Illinois HB 1284 (93rd) — CHILD ABUSE-REPORTS-COUNSELORS

Amends the Abused and Neglected Child Reporting Act. Adds licensed professional counselors and licensed clinical professional counselors to the list of persons required to report possible child abuse or neglect. Effective immediately.
