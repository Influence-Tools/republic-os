# Illinois HB 1425 (93rd) — FOIA-EXEMPTIONS

Amends the Freedom of Information Act. Exempts from inspection and copying the names, addresses, or other personal information of participants and registrants in park district, forest preserve district, or conservation district programs. Effective immediately.
