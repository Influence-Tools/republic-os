# Illinois HB 1437 (93rd) — VOLUNTARY PAYROLL DEDUCTIONS

Amends the Voluntary Payroll Deductions Act of 1983. Requires the solicitation of State and university employees hired after the official solicitation period, by providing them with the most recent campaign information. Effective immediately.
