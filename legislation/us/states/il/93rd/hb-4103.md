# Illinois HB 4103 (93rd) — VEH CD-FIRE FIGHTERS PLATES

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. In the provision regarding Illinois Fire Fighters' license plates, provides that the purposes for which moneys in the Illinois Fire Fighters' Memorial Fund shall be used include an annual Medal of Honor ceremony and related activities. Effective immediately.
