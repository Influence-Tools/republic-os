# Illinois SB 1205 (93rd) — MUNI CD-LNG TERM ENRGY CONTRCT

Amends the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that any municipality may enter into a long-term energy contract, even if the length of the contract would exceed the term of office of the corporate authorities that approved the contract. Effective July 1, 2003.
