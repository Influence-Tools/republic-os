# Illinois SB 2411 (93rd) — MUNI CD-EXTEND TIF-WAUKEGAN

Amends the Tax Increment Allocation Redevelopment Act in the Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that (i) the redevelopment project in the TIF District created by an ordinance adopted on January 19, 1988 and (ii) the redevelopment project in the TIF District created by an ordinance adopted on September 21, 1998 by the City of Waukegan must be completed by December 31 of the 35th year (now, the 23rd year) after the year in which the ordinance was adopted. Effective immediately.
