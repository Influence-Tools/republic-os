# Illinois SB 1375 (93rd) — ICCB FEDERAL TRUST FUND

Amends the Public Community College Act. Creates the ICCB Federal Trust Fund as a special fund in the State treasury. Provides that money recovered from federal programs for general administration that is received by the Illinois Community College Board shall be deposited into the Fund. Provides that all money in the Fund shall be used, subject to appropriation by the General Assembly, by the Board for the ordinary and contingent expenses of the Board. Effective immediately.
