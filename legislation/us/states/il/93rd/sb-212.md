# Illinois SB 212 (93rd) — CIV PRO-QUICK TAKE-SWIDA

Amends the Code of Civil Procedure. Authorizes quick-take proceedings by the Southwestern Illinois Development Authority from August 30, 2003 to August 30, 2005 (instead of May 22, 1998 to August 30, 2002). Effective immediately.
