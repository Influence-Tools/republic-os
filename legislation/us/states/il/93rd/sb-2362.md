# Illinois SB 2362 (93rd) — SCH CD-ST AID-LOCAL RESOURCES

Amends the School Code. In the State aid formula provisions, provides that calculation of Available Local Resources shall exclude tax amnesty funds received as a result of Public Act 93-26. Effective immediately.
