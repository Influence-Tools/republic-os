# Illinois SB 64 (93rd) — MEDICAID-DISEASE MANAGMT PILOT

Amends the Illinois Public Aid Code. As part of the Medicaid program, provides that the Department of Public Aid may undertake a pilot project to study patient outcomes, for patients with certain chronic diseases, associated with the use of disease management programs and services for chronic condition management. Provides that these amendatory provisions are repealed 10 years after the effective date of the amendatory Act.
