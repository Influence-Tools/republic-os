# Illinois HB 4402 (93rd) — WILDLIFE CD-DEER HUNT PERMITS

Amends the Wildlife Code. Defines bona fide equity partner. Provides that bona fide equity partners of a general partnership or limited partnership that owns at least 40 acres of land in a county in Illinois may obtain one free deer hunting permit for every 40 acres of land owned by the partnership in that county for the purpose of deer hunting on the partnership's land.
