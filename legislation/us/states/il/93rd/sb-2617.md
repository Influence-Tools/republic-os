# Illinois SB 2617 (93rd) — OPTOMETRIC PRACT-CIVIL PENALTY

Amends the Illinois Optometric Practice Act of 1987. Provides a civil penalty for any individual or entity that causes or attempts to cause a licensed optometrist or any other person under that individual's or entity's control to violate this Act or any other State or federal law or rule related to the practice of optometry. Effective immediately.
