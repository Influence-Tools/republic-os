# Illinois SB 1369 (93rd) — SCH CONSTUCTION-CAPITAL NEEDS

Amends the School Construction Law. Requires the State Board of Education and the Capital Development Board to file with the General Assembly a comprehensive assessment report of the capital needs of all school districts in this State before January 1, 2005 and every 2 years thereafter. Effective immediately.
