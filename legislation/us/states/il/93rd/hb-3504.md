# Illinois HB 3504 (93rd) — CRIM PRO-BAIL DEPOSIT

Amends the Code of Criminal Procedure of 1963. Provides that the court shall not order a bail bond deposited by or on behalf of a defendant in one case to be used to satisfy financial obligations of that same defendant in a different case until the bail bond is first used to satisfy any unpaid child support obligations as well as court costs in the case in which the bond has been deposited.
