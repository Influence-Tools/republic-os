# Illinois HB 3865 (93rd) — BINGO ACT-3 YEAR LICENSES

Amends the Bingo License and Tax Act. Provides that an applicant for a license to conduct bingo may apply for a 1-year or a 3-year license (now just a 1-year license). Provides that the fee for a 3-year license shall be 3 times the fee for a 1-year license. Effective July 1, 2004.
