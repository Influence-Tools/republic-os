# Illinois HB 2332 (93rd) — SCH CD-TEXTBK LOAN-SCIENCE KIT

Amends the School Code. In a Section concerning the loan of textbooks to students free of charge, provides that "textbook" includes science curriculum materials in a kit format that includes pre-packaged consumable materials if certain requirements are met. Effective immediately.
