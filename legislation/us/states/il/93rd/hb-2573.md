# Illinois HB 2573 (93rd) — LIQ CNTRL-LIABILITY INSURANCE

Amends the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Provides that the Illinois Liquor Control Commission shall not issue a license to a person who does not have a certain minimum amount of liquor liability insurance coverage.
