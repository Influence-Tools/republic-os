# Illinois HB 333 (93rd) — HORSE RACE-RAISE AGE OF MINOR

Amends the Illinois Horse Racing Act of 1975. Provides that, for purposes of the Act, the term "minor" means an individual under the age of 18 years rather than an individual under the age of 17 years.
