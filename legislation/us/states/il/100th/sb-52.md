# Illinois SB 52 (100th) — VEH CD-HORSEMEN COUNCIL PLATE

Amends the State Finance Act. Creates the Horsemen's Council of Illinois Fund as a special fund in the State treasury. Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code to allow for the issuance of decals for Universal special license plates by the Horsemen's Council of Illinois. Provides for the original fee, renewal fees, and fee distribution for Universal special license plates with decals issued by the Horsemen's Council of Illinois.
