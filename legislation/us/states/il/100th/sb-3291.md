# Illinois SB 3291 (100th) — UNMANNED AIRCRAFT SYSTEMS

Amends the Illinois Aeronautics Act. Defines "unmanned aircraft systems". Provides that the regulation of unmanned aircraft systems is an exclusive power and function of the State. Denies home rule powers. Effective immediately.
