# Illinois HB 3521 (100th) — TWP COLLECTORS-SANGAMON COUNTY

Amends the Township Code. Provides that each office of a township collector in Sangamon County shall cease upon the expiration of a township collector's term or, if vacant, on the effective date of the amendatory Act. Further provides that upon the cessation of an office of a township collector, the Sangamon County Treasurer assumes the duties of the township collector of that township. Effective immediately.
