# Illinois SB 3086 (100th) — INTERGOVTL-JOINT ACTION AGENCY

Amends the Intergovernmental Cooperation Act. Modifies the qualifications for Directors of the governing body of a Municipal Joint Action Water Agency to include an appointed official of a member municipality, public water district, township, State university, or county. Provides that for any Municipal Joint Sewage Treatment Agency established after the effective date of this amendatory Act, a Director sitting on the Board of Directors shall not be required to be an elected official of a member municipality or county, but may be an appointed official of a member municipality or county. Effective immediately.
