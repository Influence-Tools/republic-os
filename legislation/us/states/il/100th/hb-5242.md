# Illinois HB 5242 (100th) — BROWNFIELDS-VARIOUS

Amends the Brownfields Redevelopment and Intermodal Promotion Act. Makes a technical correction concerning the name of a Fund. Provides that certain powers and duties of the Managing Partner are subject to the laws and rules of the State and the government of Cook County. Effective immediately.
