# Illinois HB 3879 (100th) — CRIM JUSTICE INFO-PAPER SYSTEM

Amends the Illinois Criminal Justice Act. Provides that the Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority may conduct an inventory of law enforcement agencies, county sheriff's offices, clerks of the circuit court, or circuit clerks in this State that operate using a predominately paper system.
