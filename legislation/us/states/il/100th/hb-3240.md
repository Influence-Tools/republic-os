# Illinois HB 3240 (100th) — NOISE MONITORING-REPORTS

Amends the Permanent Noise Monitoring Act. Provides that on or before June 30, 2018 each airport shall upgrade its permanent noise monitoring system to be capable of producing the data necessary to meet the requirements of Public Act 99-202. Provides that on June 30, 2018 and thereafter an airport's permanent noise monitoring report and noise contour maps shall be produced using the criteria under Public Act 99-202. Effective immediately.
