# Illinois HB 4781 (100th) — HIGHER ED-VETERANS SERVICE ACT

Amends the Higher Education Veterans Service Act. Requires a public college or university to use its best efforts to hire a veteran of the armed services as the Coordinator of Veterans and Military Personnel Student Services. Effective immediately.
