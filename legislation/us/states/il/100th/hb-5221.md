# Illinois HB 5221 (100th) — DISABILITY BENEFIT-FIREFIGHTER

Amends the Public Employee Disability Act. Provides that the term "eligible employee" as used in the Act includes firefighters employed as full-time paramedics or firefighters who perform paramedic duties.
