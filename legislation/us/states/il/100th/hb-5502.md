# Illinois HB 5502 (100th) — REAL ESTATE TRAINEE APPRAISER

Amends the Real Estate Appraiser Licensing Act of 2002. Removes language providing that an associate real estate trainee appraiser license may not be renewed more than 2 times and language providing that an applicant for an associate real estate trainee appraiser license shall personally take and pass an examination authorized by the Department of Financial and Professional Regulation.
