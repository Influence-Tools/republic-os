# Illinois SB 2520 (100th) — DNR-PRAIRIE WIND TRAIL

Amends the Prairie Wind Trail Property Transfer Act. Provides that the Department of Natural Resources shall not accept any request received to transfer portions of the Prairie Wind Trail received after December 31, 2018 (rather than current August 18, 2017). Effective immediately.
