# Illinois HB 2762 (100th) — HOSPITAL-DO-NOT-ADMIT LISTS

Amends the University of Illinois Hospital Act and Hospital Licensing Act. Provides that no hospital may maintain a list of individuals that may not be admitted for treatment at the hospital.
