# Illinois SB 2306 (100th) — PROP TX-VETERANS-PRORATE

Amends the Property Tax Code. Provides that the homestead exemption for veterans with disabilities shall be prorated if the person qualifying for the exemption does not occupy the qualified residence as of January 1 of the taxable year. Effective immediately.
