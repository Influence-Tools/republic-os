# Illinois HB 3368 (100th) — SCH CD-ENTREPRENEUR CURRICULUM

Amends the School Code. Requires the State Board of Education to create model curriculum for use by school districts with secondary schools for the teaching of entrepreneur skills. Requires the State Board to encourage the input of business groups and universities in the creation of the model curriculum.
