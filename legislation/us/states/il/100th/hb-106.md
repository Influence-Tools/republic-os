# Illinois HB 106 (100th) — CHIEF SCHOOL BUSINESS OFFICIAL

Amends the Educator Licensure Article of the School Code. Provides that the holder of a master's degree in public administration who completes an additional 6 semester hours of internship in school business management from a regionally accredited institution of higher education and passes applicable State tests is eligible for a chief school business official endorsement on an Educator License with Stipulations. Requires the State Board of Education to adopt any rules necessary to implement the amendatory Act. Effective immediately.
