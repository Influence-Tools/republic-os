# Illinois HB 3130 (100th) — PESTICIDE REGISTRATION FEES

Amends the Illinois Pesticide Act. Changes the product registration fee to $600 per 2-year registration period (currently, $300 per product per year). Changes the business registration fee to $800 per 2-year registration period (currently, $400 per year). Changes the penalty for late registration to $100 per product in addition to the regular product registration fee (currently, $400 per product in lieu of the normal registration fee). Makes other changes. Effective immediately.
