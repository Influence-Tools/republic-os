# Illinois HB 4908 (100th) — SCH CD-DENTAL EXAMINATION DATA

Amends the School Code. Provides that all children in kindergarten and the second, sixth, and ninth grades (rather than all children in kindergarten and the second and sixth grades) of any public, private, or parochial school shall have a dental examination.
