# Illinois HB 3869 (100th) — SCH CD-IMPLICIT BIAS TRAINING

Amends the School Code. Requires in-service training for school personnel to include training on civil rights and in cultural diversity, including racial and ethnic sensitivity and implicit racial bias. Effective July 1, 2017.
