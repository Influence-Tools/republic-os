# Illinois SB 1758 (100th) — TREASURER-COLLEGE SAVINGS POOL

Amends the State Treasurer Act. Modifies the term "qualified expenses" for the purpose of participant requirements in the use of the College Savings Pool established under the Act. Effective immediately.
