# Illinois HB 4420 (100th) — CRIMINAL JUSTICE-TASK FORCE

Amends the Protection of Individuals with Disabilities in the Criminal Justice System Task Force Act. Changes the date by which the Task Force shall submit its report with findings and recommendations from March 31, 2018 to March 31, 2019. Changes the date on which the Act is repealed from June 30, 2018 to June 30, 2020. Effective immediately.
