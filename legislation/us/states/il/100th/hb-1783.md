# Illinois HB 1783 (100th) — ELECTRONIC TRANSFER-DEPOSITS

Amends the Electronic Fund Transfer Act. Requires a person who establishes or owns specified cash-dispensing terminals to post a telephone number on the terminal for consumers to call to report problems, along with the telephone number of the Department of Financial and Professional Regulation (rather than file notices of establishment or ownership with the Secretary of Financial and Professional Regulation). Repeals the Check Printer and Check Number Act and makes a conforming change in the Division of Banking Act. Effective immediately.
