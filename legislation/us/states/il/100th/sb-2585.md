# Illinois SB 2585 (100th) — VEH CD-REG PLATES-IL ST POLICE

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that permanent vehicle registration plates shall be issued to vehicles owned and used by the Illinois State Police at no charge. Effective immediately.
