# Illinois HB 539 (100th) — ELEC CD-NUMBER OF ELEC JUDGES

Amends the Election Code. Provides that county boards outside of the jurisdiction of boards of election commissioners may reduce the number of judges of election from 5 to 3 for primary elections. Effective immediately.
