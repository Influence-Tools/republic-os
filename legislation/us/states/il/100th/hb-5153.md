# Illinois HB 5153 (100th) — PERSONNEL CD-PARTIAL EXEMPTION

Amends the Personnel Code. Removes the sunset date for the partial personnel jurisdiction exemption for all positions within the Illinois School for the Deaf and the Illinois School for the Visually Impaired requiring licensure by the State Board of Education. Effective immediately.
