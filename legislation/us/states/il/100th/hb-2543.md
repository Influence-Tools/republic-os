# Illinois HB 2543 (100th) — VEH CD-CARRIER-AUTHRITY NUMBER

Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code. Provides that an interstate carrier operating intrastate may operate a motor vehicle upon the public roads of this State without the authority number of the carrier painted or affixed to any side of the cab or power unit. Effective immediately.
