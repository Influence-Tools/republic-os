# Illinois HB 2439 (100th) — ENERGY-COUNCIL-NO GRANTS

Amends the Energy Efficient Building Act. Provides that no member of an advisory council created as a result of the Act may receive State grants for teaching or administering continuing education concerning any recommendation or rule proposed by the advisory council. Effective immediately.
