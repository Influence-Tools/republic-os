# Illinois HB 4135 (100th) — FLUE GAS DESULFUR-TASK FORCE

Amends the Flue Gas Desulfurization (FGD) Task Force Act. Extends the date by which the Task Force shall report its findings and recommendations to the General Assembly from December 31, 2017 to December 31, 2018. Effective immediately.
