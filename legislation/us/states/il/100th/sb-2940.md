# Illinois SB 2940 (100th) — TWP BOARD-PURCHASING PROPERTY

Amends the Township Code. Provides that the electors may delegate the power to purchase, sell, or lease property to the township board for a period of up to 12 months and the township board may specify properties being considered. Makes a conforming change.
