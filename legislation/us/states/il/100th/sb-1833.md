# Illinois SB 1833 (100th) — INS CD-POLICY FORMS-EXEMPT

Amends the Illinois Insurance Code. In provisions concerning the filing of policy forms with the Director of Insurance, includes that policies issued to an exempt commercial purchaser, except for workers' compensation policies, are not subject to the provisions. Effective immediately.
