# Illinois HB 799 (100th) — HGHWY CD-DETOUR-WEB NOTICE

Amends the Illinois Highway Code. Provides that the Department of Transportation and a local governmental agency shall post notice of highway detour locations on their respective websites no later than 10 days before a detour becomes active.
