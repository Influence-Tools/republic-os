# Illinois SB 2541 (100th) — SCH CD-DEPT PUBLIC HEALTH-RULE

Amends the School Code. With respect to age-appropriate developmental screening and age-appropriate social and emotional screening, provides that the Department of Public Health must, no later than January 1, 2019, develop rules and appropriate revisions to the Child Health Examination form in conjunction with various organizations. Effective immediately.
