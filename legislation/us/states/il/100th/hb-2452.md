# Illinois HB 2452 (100th) — COMMUNITY-INTEGRATED LIVING

Amends the Community-Integrated Living Arrangements Licensure and Certification Act. Provides that the Department of Human Services shall conduct inspections of the records and premises of each community-integrated living arrangement certified under the Act at least once every 2 years. Effective immediately.
