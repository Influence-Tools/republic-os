# Illinois HB 370 (100th) — SCH CD-CHILD ABUSE HOTLINE

Amends the School Code. Requires each public school and charter school to post (1) the current toll-free telephone number of the Department of Children and Family Services' child abuse hotline; (2) instructions to call 9-1-1 for emergencies; and (3) directions for accessing the Department's website for more information on reporting abuse, neglect, and exploitation. Sets forth other requirements concerning the posting of this information.
