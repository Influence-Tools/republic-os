# Illinois HB 5155 (100th) — ADOPTION ACT-CONSENT FORMS

Amends the Adoption Act. Makes various changes to certain statutory adoption consent forms, including changes concerning: the inclusion of the names of persons granted consent to adopt; the provision of the email address, cell phone number, and land line phone number at which the person signing the consent can be provided with notice if the specified person does not adopt the child; procedural matters; and the effect of consent if the persons granted consent are granted a dissolution of marriage or dissolution of a civil union or one of the persons granted consent dies.
