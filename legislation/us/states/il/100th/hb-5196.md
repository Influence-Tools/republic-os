# Illinois HB 5196 (100th) — SCH CD-PARAPROFESSIONAL-FEE

Amends the Educator Licensure Article of the School Code. Provides that, beginning July 1, 2018, a licensee who holds a paraprofessional educator endorsement on an Educator License with Stipulations is not required to pay a license renewal fee. Effective immediately.
