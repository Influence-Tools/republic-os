# Illinois HB 2485 (100th) — VEH CD-PROSTATE CANCER DECAL

Amends the State Finance Act. Creates the Prostate Cancer Awareness Fund as a special fund in the State treasury. Amends the Illinois Vehicle Code to allow for the issuance of decals by the Illinois Department of Public Health. Provides for the original and renewal fees and fee distribution for Universal special license plates with decals issued by the Illinois Department of Public Health.
