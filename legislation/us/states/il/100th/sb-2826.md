# Illinois SB 2826 (100th) — HUMAN RIGHTS-PROTECTION STATUS

Amends the Illinois Human Rights Act. Provides that "order of protection status" includes status as a person protected under the Protective Orders Article of the Code of Criminal Procedure of 1963, the Stalking No Contact Order Act, and the Civil No Contact Order Act.
