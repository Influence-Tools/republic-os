# Illinois HB 4317 (100th) — SERVICE MEMBERS-LEASES

Amends the Service Member Residential Property Act. Provides that if a service member who has entered into certain residential leases is killed in action or while on active duty, then the immediate family or dependents of the service member may terminate the lease. Effective immediately.
