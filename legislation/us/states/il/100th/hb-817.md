# Illinois HB 817 (100th) — GROUP INSUR-ADOPTION PLACEMENT

Amends the State Employees Group Insurance Act of 1971. Defines the term "placement for adoption" under the Act, and for the purpose of "dependent" when that term is used in the context of the health and life plan. Makes conforming changes.
