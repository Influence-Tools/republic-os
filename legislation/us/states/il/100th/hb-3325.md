# Illinois HB 3325 (100th) — WATER DISTRICT-GENERAL MANAGER

Amends the Public Water District Act. Provides that a general manager of a public water district may be discharged at a meeting of the board of trustees upon a majority vote of the members present (currently, by a unanimous vote of the board of trustees). Effective immediately.
