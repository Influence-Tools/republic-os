# Illinois SB 941 (100th) — LIQUOR-SPECIAL EVENT RETAILERS

Amends the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Changes the definition of "special event retailer" to include the sale or offering of spirits by an educational, fraternal, political, civic, religious, or non-profit organization. Effective immediately.
