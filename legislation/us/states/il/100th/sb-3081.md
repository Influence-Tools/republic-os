# Illinois SB 3081 (100th) — PUBLIC HOUSING-WAITING LIST

Amends the Housing Authorities Act. Requires each Housing Authority to provide, upon request by the State, a list of all applicants waiting for admission to any public housing or housing project operated by the Housing Authority, including information on each applicant's position on the waiting list.
