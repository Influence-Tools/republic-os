# Illinois HB 3164 (100th) — LIQUOR-SALE NEAR CHURCH/SCHOOL

Amends the Liquor Control Act of 1934. Authorizes the issuance and renewal of a license to sell alcoholic liquor at premises located within 100 feet of specific places of worship or schools located in the City of Chicago. Effective immediately.
