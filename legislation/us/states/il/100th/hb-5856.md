# Illinois HB 5856 (100th) — PUBLIC TRANSIT TOLL EXEMPTION

Amends the Toll Highway Act. Provides that vehicles owned or operated by a Mass Transit District created under a provision of the Local Mass Transit District Act may use any toll highway without paying a toll.
