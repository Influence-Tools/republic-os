# Illinois HB 3010 (100th) — NORTH SHORE WATER RECLAMATION

Amends the North Shore Water Reclamation District Act. Provides that, for a trustee vacancy, the president of the water reclamation district board of trustees shall appoint an individual of the same political party of the trustee vacating the position. Adds barium, cadmium, mercury, selenium, and silver to the list of substances that may be toxic to the wastewater treatment processes. Provides that it is unlawful for any person to discharge effluent, gaseous wastes, sewage, industrial wastes, or other wastes into the sewage system and makes conforming changes. Provides that if a person has violated a board of trustees' final order, the trustees may authorize disconnection (currently, plugging) of the sewer or direct the water supplier to terminate service. Allows an owner or owners of non-contiguous territory to enter into an agreement requesting annexation of their non-continuous territory to the water reclamation district. Effective immediately.
