# Illinois HB 812 (100th) — STATE DESIGNATIONS-STATE PET

Amends the State Designations Act. Provides that shelter dogs and shelter cats that are residing in or have been adopted from a shelter or rescue facility in this State are designated as the official State pet of the State of Illinois. Effective immediately.
