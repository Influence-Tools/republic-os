# Illinois HB 685 (100th) — CLASSIFY MILKWEED-CNTY & MUNI

Amends the Counties Code and Illinois Municipal Code. Provides that counties and municipalities may not classify milkweed as a noxious or exotic weed. Limits home rule powers.
