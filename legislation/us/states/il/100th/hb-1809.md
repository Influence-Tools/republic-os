# Illinois HB 1809 (100th) — CORP FIDUCIARY-BANKS-OFFICE

Amends the Corporate Fiduciary Act. In provisions concerning office locations of corporate fiduciaries, removes the requirement that a corporate fiduciary of a bank, savings and loan association, or savings bank give notice of intent to establish a branch office to the Secretary of Financial and Professional Regulation 30 days prior to purchasing or leasing of land, building, or equipment. Replaces references to "Commissioner" with "Secretary". Effective immediately.
