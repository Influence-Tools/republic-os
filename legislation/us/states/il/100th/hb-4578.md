# Illinois HB 4578 (100th) — CHARITABLE ORG-LATE FILING FEE

Amends the Solicitation for Charity Act. Provides that a trustee, person, or organization who failed to timely file an annual report shall pay a $50 (rather than $100) late filing fee. Effective immediately.
