# Illinois SB 701 (100th) — PEN CD-IMRF-EARNINGS EXCLUSION

Amends the Illinois Municipal Retirement Fund (IMRF) Article of the Illinois Pension Code. For a person who first becomes a participating employee on or after the effective date of the amendatory Act, excludes vehicle allowances from the definition of "earnings". Effective immediately.
