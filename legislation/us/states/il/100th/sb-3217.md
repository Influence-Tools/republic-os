# Illinois SB 3217 (100th) — FOIA-DHS EXEMPTION

Amends the Freedom of Information Act. To the list of exemptions under the Act, adds certain records that relate to or affect the security of detention facilities requested by persons who are committed to the Department of Human Services Division of Mental Health. Effective immediately.
