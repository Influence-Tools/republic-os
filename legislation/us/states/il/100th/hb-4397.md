# Illinois HB 4397 (100th) — STUDENT LOAN SERVICER-DEBT

Amends the Student Loan Servicing Rights Act. Provides that, as used in the Act, "student loan servicer" does not include a law firm or licensed attorney that is collecting post-default debt. Effective December 31, 2018.
