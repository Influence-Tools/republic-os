# Illinois HB 2794 (100th) — VOCATIONAL ACADEMY-CHARTER SCH

Amends the School Code. Provides that a school board shall require the school district's high schools, if any, to inform all 11th and 12th grade students of dual enrollment and dual credit opportunities at public community colleges for qualified students.
