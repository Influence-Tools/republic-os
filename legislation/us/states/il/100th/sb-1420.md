# Illinois SB 1420 (100th) — DEAD ANIMAL DISPOSAL-EXEMPTION

Amends the Illinois Dead Animal Disposal Act. Provides that a non-profit organization or government entity that hosts a collection center to collect cooking grease or cooking oil from the public shall be exempt from licensure, fees, or reporting under the Act. Effective immediately.
