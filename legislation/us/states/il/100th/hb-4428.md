# Illinois HB 4428 (100th) — MILK INSPECTIONS-PAPER COPIES

Amends the Grade A Pasteurized Milk and Milk Products Act. Provides that the Department of Public Health or a unit of local government electing to administer and enforce the Act shall provide a dairy farm with a paper copy of the dairy farm's inspection report.
