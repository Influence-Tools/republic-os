# Illinois HB 4404 (100th) — RES MORTGAGE-LOAN PROCESSING

Amends the Residential Mortgage License Act of 1987. Provides that "soliciting, processing, placing, or negotiating a residential mortgage loan" excludes independent loan processing as permitted by the federal Secure and Fair Enforcement for Mortgage Licensing Act of 2008.
