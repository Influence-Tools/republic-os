# Indiana HB 1192 (2022) — Qualified providers and Medicaid school services.

Defines "qualified provider" and "school based nurse" for purposes of providing Medicaid covered services in a school setting. Specifies conditions that must be met in order for the school based Medicaid services to be provided.
