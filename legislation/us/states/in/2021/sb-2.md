# Indiana SB 2 (2021) — Funding for virtual instruction in public schools.

Establishes a definition of "virtual instruction" for purposes of determining a school corporation's basic tuition support using the: (1) 2020 fall count; and (2) 2021 spring count; of average daily membership.
