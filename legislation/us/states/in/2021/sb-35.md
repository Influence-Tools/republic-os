# Indiana SB 35 (2021) — Statutes applicable to Lake and St. Joseph counties.

Adds references to Lake County and St. Joseph County that were removed in P.L.278-2019. Specifies provisions for St. Joseph County with regard to division of the county into districts. Makes conforming amendments.
