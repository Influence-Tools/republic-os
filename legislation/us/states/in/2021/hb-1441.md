# Indiana HB 1441 (2021) — Full faith and credit for tribal court orders.

Requires that, under certain circumstances, a court of record in Indiana shall give full faith and credit to judgments and orders of a tribal court affiliated with the Pokagon Band of Potawatomi Indians.
