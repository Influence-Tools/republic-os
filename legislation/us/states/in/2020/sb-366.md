# Indiana SB 366 (2020) — Kankakee River and Yellow River development.

Provides that the auditor of state shall deduct amounts due from distributions of local income taxes allocated to (as opposed to payable to) the county when a county fails to pay direct support or special assessments to the Kankakee River basin and Yellow River basin development commission.
