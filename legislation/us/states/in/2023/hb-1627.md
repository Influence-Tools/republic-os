# Indiana HB 1627 (2023) — Sale of tax sale properties to nonprofits.

Provides that a tax sale statute concerning the sale of real property to eligible nonprofit entities for low or moderate income housing applies to all counties. (Current law provides that the provision only applies to a county having a consolidated city.)
