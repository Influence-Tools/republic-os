# Georgia HB 953 (2013_14) — Gwinnett Judicial Circuit; salary supplements for judges; change provisions

L. 1960, p. 110), as amended, so as to change provisions relating to the salary supplements for the judges of the Gwinnett Judicial Circuit; to provide an effective date; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
