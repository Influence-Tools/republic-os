# Georgia HB 1077 (2013_14) — Downtown Athens Development Authority; provide for powers, duties and responsibilities; provisions

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend an Act creating the Downtown Athens Development Authority, approved March 23, 1977 (Ga. L. 1977, p. 3533), as amended, which authority was created pursuant to an amendment to the Constitution as contained in Ga. L. 1975, p. 1698, and amended by Ga.
