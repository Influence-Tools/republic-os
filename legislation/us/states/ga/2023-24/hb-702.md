# Georgia HB 702 (2023_24) — Savannah, City of; Chatham County; Board of Education; modify compensation of members

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend an Act governing the school system of the City of Savannah and Chatham County, approved March 21, 1968 (Ga. L. 1968, p. 2636), as amended, particularly by an Act approved May 5, 2006 (Ga.
