# Georgia SB 503 (2021_22) — City of Macon, the City of Payne City, and Bibb County; new commissioner districts; provide

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend an Act to provide for the restructuring of the City of Macon, the City of Payne City, and Bibb County, approved April 20, 2012 (Ga.
