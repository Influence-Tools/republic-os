# Georgia HB 1014 (2021_22) — Montgomery County; abolish mode of compensating sheriff; repeal Act

L. 1966, p. 2120), as amended, particularly by an Act approved April 1, 1971 (Ga. L. 1971, p. 2856), an Act approved March 28, 1974 (Ga. L. 1974, p. 3648), and an Act approved March 13, 1978 (Ga.
