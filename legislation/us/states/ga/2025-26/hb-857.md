# Georgia HB 857 (2025_26) — Cobb County; State Court; chief deputy clerk and clerk; change salary

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend an Act creating the State Court of Cobb County, approved March 26, 1964 (Ga. L. 1964, p. 3211), as amended, particularly by an Act approved May 3, 2023 (Ga.
