# Georgia HB 619 (2025_26) — Donalsonville, City of; election schedule for mayor and councilmembers; revise

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend an Act providing a new charter for the City of Donalsonville, approved April 29, 1997 (Ga. L. 1997, p. 4524), as amended, so as to revise the election schedule for the mayor so as to better stagger elections of the mayor and councilmembers; to provide for an interim term of office; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
