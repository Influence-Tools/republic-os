# Georgia HB 749 (2025_26) — Carroll County; Board of Commissioners; chairperson and members; change annual salary

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend an Act amending, revising, superseding, and consolidating the laws pertaining to the Board of Commissioners of Carroll County, approved February 28, 1989 (Ga.
