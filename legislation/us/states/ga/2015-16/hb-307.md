# Georgia HB 307 (2015_16) — Sandersville, City of; election of members of city council; change provisions

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend an Act providing for a new charter for the City of Sandersville, approved March 28, 1990 (Ga. L. 1990, p. 4823), as amended, particularly by an Act approved April 29, 2014 (Ga.
