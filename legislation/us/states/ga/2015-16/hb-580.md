# Georgia HB 580 (2015_16) — Columbus, City of; Recorder's Court; increase amount of technology fee

L. 2013, p. 4414), so as to increase the amount of such fee; to repeal an automatic repeal of such Act; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
