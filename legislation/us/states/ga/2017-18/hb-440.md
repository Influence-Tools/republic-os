# Georgia HB 440 (2017_18) — Sandy Springs, City of; Fulton County; change corporate boundaries

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend an Act providing for a new charter for the City of Sandy Springs in Fulton County, approved April 15, 2005 (Ga.
