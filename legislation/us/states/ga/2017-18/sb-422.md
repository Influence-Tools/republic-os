# Georgia SB 422 (2017_18) — Clinical Laboratories; exempting pharmacists from provisions of said chapter when performing certain tests; provisions; change

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend Chapter 22 of Title 31 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to clinical laboratories, so as to provide for changes to provisions exempting pharmacists from provisions of said chapter when performing certain tests; to remove a cross-reference; to provide for reporting of tests and standards; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
