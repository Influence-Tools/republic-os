# Georgia HB 1035 (2017_18) — Muscogee County; Magistrate Court to impose and collect county law library fees; authorize

A BILL to be entitled an Act to authorize the Magistrate Court of Muscogee County to impose and collect county law library fees as part of the court costs in the magistrate court; to provide for practices and procedures connected therewith; to provide for related matters; to provide an effective date; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
