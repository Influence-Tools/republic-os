# Georgia SB 321 (2017_18) — Medicaid Claims; false or fraudulent; recover the maximum penalty authorized by federal law; increase the civil penalties

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend Code Section 49-4-168.1 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to civil penalties for false or fraudulent Medicaid claims, so as to increase the civil penalties that shall be imposed in order to allow this state to recover the maximum penalty authorized by federal law; to provide for related matters; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
