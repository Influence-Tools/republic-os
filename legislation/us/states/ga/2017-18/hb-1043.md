# Georgia HB 1043 (2017_18) — Jesup, City of; board of commissioners; provide staggered terms

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend an Act creating a new charter for the City of Jesup, approved December 16, 1937 (Ga. L. 1937-38 Ex.
