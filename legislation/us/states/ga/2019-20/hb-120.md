# Georgia HB 120 (2019_20) — Bell-Forsyth Judicial Circuit; Superior Court judges; increase salary supplement

L. 2006, p. 3680), so as to increase the amount of such supplement; to provide for related matters; to provide an effective date; to repeal conflicting laws; and for other purposes.
