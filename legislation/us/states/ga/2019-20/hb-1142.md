# Georgia HB 1142 (2019_20) — Forsyth County; creation or expansion of one or more community improvement districts in any municipality; authorize

A BILL to be entitled an Act to amend an Act to provide for the creation of one or more community improvement districts in Forsyth County, approved May 6, 2019 (Ga.
