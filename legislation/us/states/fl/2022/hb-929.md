# Florida HB 929 (2022) — City of West Palm Beach, Palm Beach County

City of West Palm Beach, Palm Beach County; Revises definition of "salary"; provides definition does not include certain persons in a collective bargaining agreement; provides for retroactive restoration of benefit accrual rate to 3 percent for all years of member's service within specified time period; provides eligible members receive lump-sum payment for accumulated leave payable upon retirement.
