# Florida HB 1107 (2022) — City of Inverness, Citrus County

City of Inverness, Citrus County; Creates special zone; provides boundaries & exception to general law; provides requirements for issuance of special permit for certain licensed vendors in described area for sale of alcoholic beverages for consumption off licensed premises & on public rights-of-way & public park property during city-approved special events; provides that special permits are in addition to certain other authorized temporary permits; requires bona fide licensed vendor to comply with all other statutory requirements.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2022-246.
