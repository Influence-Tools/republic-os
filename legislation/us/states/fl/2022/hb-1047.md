# Florida HB 1047 (2022) — Cedar Hammock Fire Control District, Manatee County

Cedar Hammock Fire Control District, Manatee County; Revises boundaries; provides for expansion of district; authorizes district to provide fire control & emergency medical services, levy & collect taxes, assessments, & fees, & administer fire rescue programs & services within district's expanded boundaries; requires referendum.
