# Florida SB 1078 (2022) — Soil and Water Conservation Districts

Soil and Water Conservation Districts; Providing for the subdivision of certain proposed soil and water conservation districts; requiring the supervisors of each soil and water conservation district to be elected from each of the district’s subdivisions; providing qualification requirements for supervisors to serve on the governing body of a soil and water conservation district; providing for the automatic dissolution of soil and water conservation districts under certain conditions, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2022-191.
