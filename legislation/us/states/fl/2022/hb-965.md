# Florida HB 965 (2022) — Environmental Management

Environmental Management; Provides for water quality enhancement areas & permits, water quality enhancement service areas, & enhancement credits; directs DEP & water management districts to authorize sale & use of enhancement credits for specified purposes; directs DEP to maintain specified ledgers; revises requirements for developers & homebuilders to qualify for graywater technology incentives; requires to modify specified rules.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2022-215.
