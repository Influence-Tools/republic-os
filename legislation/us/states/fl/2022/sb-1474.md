# Florida SB 1474 (2022) — Online Training for Private Security Officers

Online Training for Private Security Officers; Providing that certain required training criteria for Class “G” and Class “D” licenses, respectively, may be conducted online; providing requirements for such training; requiring the Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services to establish certain criteria and rules for the regulation of certain entities that provide online training; providing requirements for online training courses for a Class “D” license, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2022-79.
