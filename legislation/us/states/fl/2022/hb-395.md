# Florida HB 395 (2022) — "Victims of Communism Day"

"Victims of Communism Day"; Requires Governor to proclaim November 7 of each year as "Victims of Communism Day"; requires day to be observed in public schools & by public exercise; requires day other than November 7 to be observed by public schools under specified circumstance; requires high school students to receive specified instruction; requires SBE to adopt specified revised social studies standards.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2022-98.
