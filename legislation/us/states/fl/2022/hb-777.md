# Florida HB 777 (2022) — Local Tax Referenda Requirements

Local Tax Referenda Requirements; Requires referenda elections related to tourist development taxes, tourist impact taxes, children's services & independent special district property taxes, increases in county & municipal ad valorem tax millages, ninth-cent fuel tax, local option fuel taxes, & certain school district millage elections to be held on day of general elections.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2022-214.
