# Florida SB 58 (2022) — Relief of Yeilyn Quiroz Otero by Miami-Dade County

Relief of Yeilyn Quiroz Otero by Miami-Dade County; Providing for the relief of Yeilyn Quiroz Otero by Miami-Dade County; providing for the appropriation of funds to Heather Hasandras, as guardian of the property of Yeilyn Quiroz Otero, to compensate Miss Quiroz Otero for injuries sustained as a result of the negligence of an employee of Miami-Dade County; requiring that the funds, less certain fees and costs, be placed in a special needs trust for the exclusive use and benefit of Miss Quiroz Otero; providing a limitation on compensation and the payment of attorney fees; providing legislative intent regarding the waiver of certain liens, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2022-228.
