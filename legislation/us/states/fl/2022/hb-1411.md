# Florida HB 1411 (2022) — Floating Solar Facilities

Floating Solar Facilities; Requires floating solar facility to be permitted use in certain land use categories; requires local governments to promote expanded uses of floating solar facilities by taking specified actions; authorizes county or municipality to specify buffer & landscaping requirements; requires Office of Energy within DACS to submit recommendations to Legislature regarding floating solar facilities.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2022-83.
