# Florida HB 873 (2022) — Pub. Rec./Execution Information

Pub. Rec./Execution Information; Provides exemption from public records requirements for information or records that identify or could reasonably lead to identification of any person or entity that participates in execution; provides for retroactive application; provides for future legislative review & repeal of exemption; provides statement of public necessity.
