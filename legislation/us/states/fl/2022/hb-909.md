# Florida HB 909 (2022) — Pollution Control Standards and Liability

Pollution Control Standards and Liability; Provides that Secretary of Environmental Protection has exclusive jurisdiction in setting standards or procedures for evaluating environmental conditions & assessing potential liability for presence of contaminants on certain agricultural lands; prohibits secretary from delegating such authority to local governmental entity.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2022-127.
