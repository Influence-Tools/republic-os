# Florida HB 915 (2022) — Commercial Motor Vehicle Registration

Commercial Motor Vehicle Registration; Provides for future expiration of provision relating to vehicles with apportioned registrations; provides license plate & cab card requirements for vehicles registered in accordance with International Registration Plan; specifies fee for original or renewal cab card & trust fund where fee is deposited; provides for replacement at no charge of damaged or worn license plates.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2022-128.
