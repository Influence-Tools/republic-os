# Florida SB 754 (2022) — Mobile Home Registration Periods

Mobile Home Registration Periods; Revising the registration and registration renewal periods for a mobile home owned by a natural person; requiring the Department of Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles, beginning on a specified date, to give customers the option to renew their registrations on their dates of birth in certain years; specifying permissible renewal periods for such renewals, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2022-123.
