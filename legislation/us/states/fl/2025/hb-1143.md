# Florida HB 1143 (2025) — Permits for Drilling, Exploration, and Extraction of Oil and Gas Resources

Permits for Drilling, Exploration, and Extraction of Oil and Gas Resources; Prohibits drilling, exploration, or production of specified petroleum products within certain distance of national estuarine research reserves in certain counties; requires DEP to consider certain factors when determining whether natural resources of certain bodies of water & shore areas are adequately protected from potential accident or blowout; provides requirements for balancing test to make such determination.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2025-193.
