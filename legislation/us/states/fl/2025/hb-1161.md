# Florida HB 1161 (2025) — Removal of Altered Sexual Depictions Posted without Consent

Removal of Altered Sexual Depictions Posted without Consent ; Defines "covered platform"; requires covered platforms to establish process for removal of altered sexual depictions posted without consent of identifiable person; provides requirements for such process; requires notice; provides immunity for good faith compliance; prohibits unreasonable failure to comply; provides remedies; provides exceptions.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2025-133.
