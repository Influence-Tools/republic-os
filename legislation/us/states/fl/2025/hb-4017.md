# Florida HB 4017 (2025) — Bermont Drainage District, Charlotte County

Bermont Drainage District, Charlotte County; Creates Bermont Drainage District in Charlotte County as public corporation; provides that all subsequent proceedings concerning district are ratified, confirmed, & approved; creates district's charter; provides district's status & boundaries; provides minimum charter requirements; provides applicability of specified laws to district.
