# Florida HB 1607 (2025) — Cardiac Emergencies

Cardiac Emergencies; Requires, rather than authorizing, school districts to provide training in basic first aid to certain students; provides requirements for such training; requires each public school to develop plan for urgent life-saving emergencies (PULSE) for specified purposes; & provides requirements for school, staff, & defibrillator use & storage.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2025-67.
