# Florida HB 1545 (2025) — Parkinson's Disease

Parkinson's Disease; Establishes the Consortium for Parkinson's Disease Research within the University of South Florida; establishes the Parkinson's Disease Research Board; requires the board to direct the operations of the consortium and to annually adopt a plan for Parkinson's disease research; provides duties of the consortium director; provides research requirements for the plan.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2025-188.
