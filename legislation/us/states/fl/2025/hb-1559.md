# Florida HB 1559 (2025) — Vexatious Litigants

Vexatious Litigants; Expands actions subject to Florida Vexatious Litigant Law; revises eligibility for designation as vexatious litigant; revises sanctions & remedies for vexatious litigation; prohibits clerks of court from accepting certain filings from vexatious litigant; specifies duration of automatic stay imposed against vexatious litigation.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2025-128.
