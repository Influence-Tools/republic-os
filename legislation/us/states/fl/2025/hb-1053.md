# Florida HB 1053 (2025) — Department of Law Enforcement

Department of Law Enforcement; Repeals provisions relating to Florida Violent Crime & Drug Control Council & Violent Crime Investigative Emergency & Drug Control Strategy Implementation Account, respectively; changes name of Crimes Against Children Criminal Profiling Program to Child Exploitation & Crimes Against Children Program & expands scope of program; revises membership of Domestic Security Oversight Council; revises requirements for report by Chief of Domestic Security; increases maximum that may be spent for veterinary care of retired police dogs.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2025-83.
