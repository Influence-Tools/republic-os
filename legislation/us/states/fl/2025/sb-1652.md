# Florida SB 1652 (2025) — Public Records/Pleading, Request for Relief, or Other Document Stricken by a Court

Public Records/Pleading, Request for Relief, or Other Document Stricken by a Court; Providing an exemption from public records requirements for a matter in a pleading, a request for relief, or other document which has been stricken by the court in a noncriminal case if the court makes specific findings; providing a statement of public necessity, etc.
