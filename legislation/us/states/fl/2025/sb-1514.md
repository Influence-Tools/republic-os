# Florida SB 1514 (2025) — Anaphylaxis in Public and Charter Schools

Anaphylaxis in Public and Charter Schools; Requiring each district school board and charter school governing board to require that a school that serves students in kindergarten through grade 8 provide specified training to an adequate number of personnel; requiring each district school board and charter school governing board to require that a student’s emergency action plan remain effective at all times while the student is on school grounds, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2025-47.
