# Florida HB 989 (2025) — Licensure of Family Foster Homes

Licensure of Family Foster Homes ; Requires DCF to adopt rules to streamline licensure application process for licensed foster parents who relocate within this state; requires such rules to include priority review of applications, expedited home studies & background checks, & recognition of prior foster parent training coursework.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2025-63.
