# Florida HB 4067 (2025) — Special Beverage Licenses for Equestrian Sport Facilities in Marion County

Special Beverage Licenses for Equestrian Sport Facilities in Marion County; Provides for issuance of special beverage licenses to certain equestrian sport facilities located in Marion County; authorizes package sale of wine & malt beverages for off-premises consumption & sale of alcoholic beverages for on-premises consumption under such licenses; provides for conditions & restrictions; provides for compliance with Beverage Law, with certain exceptions.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2025-248.
