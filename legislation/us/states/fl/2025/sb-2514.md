# Florida SB 2514 (2025) — Health and Human Services

Health and Human Services; Authorizing certain dental and dental hygiene students to apply for the Dental Student Loan Repayment Program before obtaining active employment; creating the Cancer Connect Collaborative Research Incubator within the Department of Health, and overseen by the collaborative, to provide funding for a specified purpose over a specified timeframe; establishing the Bascom Palmer Eye Institute VisionGen Initiative within the William G. “Bill” Bankhead, Jr., and David Coley Cancer Research Program; requiring the department to revoke the medical marijuana use registry registration of qualified patients and caregivers who enter certain pleas or are found guilty of certain offenses; requiring the Agency for Health Care Administration to develop user-friendly consumer satisfaction surveys for nursing home facilities, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2025-204.
