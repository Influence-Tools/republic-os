# Florida HB 1405 (2023) — Biosolids

Biosolids; Establishes biosolids grant program within DEP; authorizes DEP to provide grants to counties, special districts, & municipalities for certain wastewater conversion projects; provides eligibility, prioritization, & funding requirements; authorizes waiver of matching fund requirement; requires DEP to develop specified reporting requirements for grants awarded to counties, special districts, & municipalities.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-293.
