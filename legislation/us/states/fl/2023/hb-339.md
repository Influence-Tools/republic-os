# Florida HB 339 (2023) — Education of Dependents of Deceased or Disabled Servicemembers, Prisoners of War, and Persons Missing in Action

Education of Dependents of Deceased or Disabled Servicemembers, Prisoners of War, and Persons Missing in Action; Revises eligibility requirements for educational benefits provided by state to spouse or dependent child of deceased or disabled servicemember; revises eligibility requirements for educational benefits provided by state to dependent child of prisoner of war, person missing in action, or deceased or disabled servicemember who participated in certain military operations.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-279.
