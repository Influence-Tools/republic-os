# Florida SB 180 (2023) — Regulation of Securities

Regulation of Securities; Requiring the Financial Services Commission to define the term “accredited investor” by rule; revising requirements for the registration of securities; revising grounds on which the office may revoke, suspend, or deny the registration of securities; specifying continuing education requirements for associated persons of investment advisers and federal covered advisers; authorizing the commission to establish rules of conduct and prohibited business practices for intermediaries, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-205.
