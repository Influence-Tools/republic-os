# Florida HB 621 (2023) — Death Benefits for Active Duty Servicemembers

Death Benefits for Active Duty Servicemembers; Revises amount & conditions of payment of death benefits; requires payment be made to beneficiary through process set out by DMA; removes provisions relating to payment when beneficiary is not designated; requires proof of residency or duty post to be provided to DMA; requires DMA to request CFO to draw warrant for payment of benefits from General Revenue Fund.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-163.
