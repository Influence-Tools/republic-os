# Florida HB 1565 (2023) — Town of Fort White, Columbia County

Town of Fort White, Columbia County; Revises boundaries, general powers to borrow money, & election & terms of members of Town Council; provides council districts; provides for presiding officer of council; provides for salaries of Mayor & members of Town Council, removes provisions relating to municipal court; revises provisions relating to police & fire departments; revises requirements for Town Clerk; removes provisions relating to town auditor, town tax assessor, town treasurer, tax administration, & canvass of election returns; revises requirements for election of candidates to office of Town Council; revises terms of office of councilmembers.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-344.
