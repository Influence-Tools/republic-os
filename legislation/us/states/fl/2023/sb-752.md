# Florida SB 752 (2023) — Temporary Commercial Kitchens

Temporary Commercial Kitchens; Requiring operators of public food service establishments who provide commissary services to maintain a temporary commercial kitchen registry; defining the term “temporary commercial kitchen”; preempting regulation of temporary commercial kitchens to the state; authorizing temporary commercial kitchens to be used in conjunction with licensed permanent food service establishments for specified purposes, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-48.
