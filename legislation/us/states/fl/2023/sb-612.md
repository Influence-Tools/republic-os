# Florida SB 612 (2023) — Blood Clot and Pulmonary Embolism Policy Workgroup

Blood Clot and Pulmonary Embolism Policy Workgroup; Citing this act as the “Emily Adkins Prevention Act”; requiring the Secretary of Health Care Administration, in conjunction with the State Surgeon General, to establish a blood clot and pulmonary embolism policy workgroup; requiring the secretary to submit a final report to the Governor and the Legislature by a specified date, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-192.
