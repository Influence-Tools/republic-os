# Florida HB 227 (2023) — Lee Memorial Health System, Lee County

Lee Memorial Health System, Lee County; Provides quorum requirements for system board meetings; removes requirement that health system’s annual audit & annual budget be filed with clerk of circuit court; revises provisions relating to purchasing real property, entering into financial agreements, & managing funds; provides process by which health system may convert to nonprofit entity; removes provisions relating to execution & enforcement of liens.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-326.
