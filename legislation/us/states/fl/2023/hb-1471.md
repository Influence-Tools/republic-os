# Florida HB 1471 (2023) — Health Care Provider Accountability

Health Care Provider Accountability; Revises rights of licensed nursing home facility residents; creates cause of action for ex parte temporary injunction against continued unlicensed activity; authorizes ACHA to provide records to local law enforcement & state attorneys' offices; provides standard of care requirements for office surgeries; prohibits certain office surgeries; provides physician, office, & procedure requirements.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-307.
