# Florida HB 49 (2023) — Abandoned and Historic Cemeteries

Abandoned and Historic Cemeteries; Creates Historic Cemeteries Program within Division of Historical Resources of DOS; creates Historic Cemeteries Program Advisory Council; authorizes certain entities to acquire conservation easements to preserve cemeteries; provides appropriations & authorizes positions.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-142.
