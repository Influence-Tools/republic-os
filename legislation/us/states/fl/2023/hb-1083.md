# Florida HB 1083 (2023) — Manatee County

Manatee County; Creates East River Ranch Stewardship District; provides definitions; establishes legal boundaries; provides for jurisdiction; provides for governing board; provides for membership, election, terms of office, & meetings; provides powers & duties; provides method for transition of board from landowner control to control by resident electors of district; provides for district manager & personnel; provides general & special powers of district; provides for bonds; provides for borrowing; provides for ad valorem taxation, special assessments, certificates of indebtedness & tax liens; provides for referendum.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-335.
