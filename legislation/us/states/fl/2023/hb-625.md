# Florida HB 625 (2023) — Children's Initiative Projects

Children's Initiative Projects; Revises objectives for specified working groups within Florida Children's Initiatives; provides that initiatives are administratively housed in DCF; exempts initiatives from control, supervision, or direction by DCF or any state department; requires initiatives to be managed by not-for-profit corporations.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-280.
