# Florida HB 537 (2023) — Custody and Supervision of Specified Offenders

Custody and Supervision of Specified Offenders; Excludes certain offenders from eligibility to receive basic gain-time & incentive gain-time; excludes certain offenders from eligibility for specified reductions to term of supervision; requires court to impose additional conditions of supervision on specified offenders.
