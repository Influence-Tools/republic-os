# Florida HB 1105 (2023) — Rapid DNA Grant Program

Rapid DNA Grant Program; Creates Rapid DNA Grant Program within FDLE for county jails or sheriffs' offices; requires department to annually award grant funds to county jails or sheriffs' offices; provides funding requirements; authorizes department to establish criteria & set specific time periods for acceptance of applications & selection process for awarding grant funds.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-234.
