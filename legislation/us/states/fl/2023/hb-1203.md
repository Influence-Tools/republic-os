# Florida HB 1203 (2023) — Registrations and Transfers of Heating, Ventilation, and Air-Conditioning System Manufacturer Warranties

Registrations and Transfers of Heating, Ventilation, and Air-Conditioning System Manufacturer Warranties; Requires manufacturer warranty for HVAC system to run with property; provides that warrantor continues to be obligated under terms of manufacturer warranty agreement for warranty transferred to new owner & may not charge transfer fee; provides that transfer of warranty to new owner does not extend warranty; provides warranty is deemed registered if contractor meets certain requirements.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2023-288.
