# Florida HB 4093 (2026) — Fellsmere Water Control District, Indian River County

Fellsmere Water Control District, Indian River County; Provides that the district is dependent district of Indian River County; provides that the Board of County Commissioners of Indian River County shall be governing board of district; revises boundaries of district; provides for maintenance & use of certain canals; provides for certain access to public records.
