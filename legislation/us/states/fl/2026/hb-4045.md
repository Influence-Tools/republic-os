# Florida HB 4045 (2026) — Jacksonville Aviation Authority, Consolidated City of Jacksonville, Duval County

Jacksonville Aviation Authority, Consolidated City of Jacksonville, Duval County; Provides that Jacksonville Aviation Authority is responsible for economic development of Cecil Airport as regional, state, & national aerospace & related-industry hub; requires annual presentation of economic development plan for Cecil Airport to council of City of Jacksonville for review; revises threshold amounts for certain purchases by authority.
