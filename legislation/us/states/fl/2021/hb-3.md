# Florida HB 3 (2021) — Home Book Delivery for Elementary Students

Home Book Delivery for Elementary Students; Provides credits against specified taxes under New Worlds Reading Initiative Tax Credit; revises order in which corporate income tax credit under the Initiative is applied; revises definition of "adjusted federal income;" revises specified corporate income tax calculation; provides Initiative, DOE, DOR, administrator, school district, & tax credit requirements; provides for a specified cooperative agreements.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-193.
