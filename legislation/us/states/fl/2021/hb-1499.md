# Florida HB 1499 (2021) — Pine Tree Water Control District, Broward County

Pine Tree Water Control District, Broward County; Requires members of board of supervisors be elected through general election; provides for staggered terms; provides requirements for elections of board members & candidates seeking election; requires Supervisor of Elections of Broward County to appoint certain persons, prepare & furnish ballots, designate polling places, & canvass returns; provides for declaration & certification of election results; provides requirements for referendum.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-254.
