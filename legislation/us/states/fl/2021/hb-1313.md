# Florida HB 1313 (2021) — Digital Driver Licenses and Identification Cards

Digital Driver Licenses and Identification Cards; Requires DHSMV to establish secure & uniform system for issuing optional digital proofs of driver licenses & identification cards; authorizes DHSMV to contract with one or more private entities to develop electronic credentialing system & other purposes; prohibits private entities from storing, selling, or sharing personal information collected from scanning digital proofs of driver licenses & identification cards.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-235.
