# Florida HB 1639 (2021) — Pub. Rec./Network Schematics, Hardware and Software Configurations, or Encryption/Supervisors of Elections

Pub. Rec./Network Schematics, Hardware and Software Configurations, or Encryption/Supervisors of Elections; Creates public records exemption for portions of records containing network schematics, hardware & software configurations, or encryption held by county supervisor of elections; provides for release of confidential & exempt information in certain instances to governmental entities; provides for retroactive application; provides for future legislative review & repeal of exemption; provides statement of public necessity.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-73.
