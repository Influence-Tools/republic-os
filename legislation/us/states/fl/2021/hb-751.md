# Florida HB 751 (2021) — City of Gainesville, Alachua County

City of Gainesville, Alachua County; Authorizes issuance of special license to mobile food dispensing vehicles to sell alcoholic beverages for consumption of alcoholic beverages within specified area; provides requirements; prohibits licensee from selling alcoholic beverages by package for consumption outside specified area; provides for issuance of an unlimited number of licenses within specified area; provides that operator is not exempt from meeting requirements to hold a license.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-245.
