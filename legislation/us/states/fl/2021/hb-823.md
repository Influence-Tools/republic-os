# Florida HB 823 (2021) — Alarm System Contractors

Alarm System Contractors; Provides that advertisements for alarm system contracting do not have to include contractor's registration or certification number; authorizes contractor to begin repairing specified fire alarm system under certain conditions; provides that repaired fire alarm system is not compliant with applicable codes & standards until certain conditions are met.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-110.
