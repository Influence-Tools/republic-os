# Florida HB 487 (2021) — Growth Management

Growth Management; Authorizing landowners with development orders existing before the incorporation of a municipality to elect to abandon such orders and develop the vested density and intensity contained therein under specified conditions; providing an exception to a prohibition against legal entities and their members exercising the power of eminent domain over or acquiring title to certain facilities or property; revising the required acreage thresholds for adopting an amendment using a small scale development amendment, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-206.
