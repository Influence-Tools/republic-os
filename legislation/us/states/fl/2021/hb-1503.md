# Florida HB 1503 (2021) — North Springs Improvement District, Broward County

North Springs Improvement District, Broward County; Revises number of board members; requires members to be residents of district; provides designated seats for supervisors; provides for repeal unless reviewed & saved from repeal by Legislature; provides exception to general law; requires board of supervisors be elected by qualified electors of district; provides requirements for referendum.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-256.
