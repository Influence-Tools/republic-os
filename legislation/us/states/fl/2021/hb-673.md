# Florida HB 673 (2021) — DNA Evidence Collected in Sexual Offense Investigations

DNA Evidence Collected in Sexual Offense Investigations; Requires FDLE to create & maintain statewide database for tracking sexual offense evidence kits; provides database requirements; requires FDLE to ensure that alleged sexual offense victims & certain other persons are notified of or have access to information regarding such kits & evidence; provides requirements for such notification.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-213.
