# Florida HB 77 (2021) — Diesel Exhaust Fluid

Diesel Exhaust Fluid; Requiring specified public airports to require a diesel exhaust fluid safety mitigation and exclusion plan for certain fixed-base operators; specifying plan requirements; requiring public airports to make such plans available for review during inspections by the Department of Transportation after a specified date; requiring the department to convene a workgroup of public airport representatives by a specified date to develop specified uniform industry standards; authorizing the department to adopt rules, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-61.
