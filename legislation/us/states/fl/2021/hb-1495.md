# Florida HB 1495 (2021) — Coral Springs Improvement District, Broward County

Coral Springs Improvement District, Broward County; Provides exception to general law; revises number of board members; requires members of Board of Supervisors of the Coral Springs Improvement District to be elected by qualified electors of district; provides for staggered terms of office for board; provides requirements for elections of board members & for candidates seeking election; provides duties of Supervisor of Elections of Broward County; provides for referendum.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-253.
