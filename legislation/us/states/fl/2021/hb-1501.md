# Florida HB 1501 (2021) — Sunshine Drainage District, Broward County

Sunshine Drainage District, Broward County; Revises number of members of board of supervisors; provides for members of board of supervisors to be elected by qualified electors of district; provides for staggered terms; requires nonpartisan elections; provides requirements for candidates & election duties of the supervisor of elections; provides requirements for referendum.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-255.
