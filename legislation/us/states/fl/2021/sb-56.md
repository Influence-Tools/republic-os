# Florida SB 56 (2021) — Community Association Assessment Notices

Community Association Assessment Notices; Requiring condominium associations to maintain specified affirmative acknowledgments as official records of the association; revising timeframes for foreclosure judgments; requiring condominium associations to deliver certain invoices for assessments or statements of account to unit owners in a specified manner; requiring cooperative associations to maintain specified affirmative acknowledgments as official records of the association, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-91.
