# Florida SB 1194 (2021) — Transportation

Transportation; Authorizing governing bodies of municipalities and counties to abandon and convey their interests in certain roads and rights-of-way dedicated in a recorded residential subdivision plat to community development districts under specified conditions; providing that certain governmental entities may not prohibit certain vendors from responding to competitive solicitations of certain contractual services; revising provisions authorizing vehicles and equipment to show or display flashing lights; prohibiting the Department of Transportation and contractors and subcontractors of the department from purchasing specified substances from a borrow pit unless specified conditions are satisfied, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-188.
