# Florida HB 1349 (2021) — Assistance Programs

Assistance Programs; Requires early learning coalition to give priority for participation in school readiness program to certain children; requires Office of Early Learning within DOE to coordinate with University of Florida Anita Zucker Center for Excellence in Early Childhood Studies to conduct analysis of certain assistance programs; requires DCF to provide certain assistance.
