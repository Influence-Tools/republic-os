# Florida HB 1137 (2021) — Information Technology Procurement

Information Technology Procurement; Requires DMS to establish project management & oversight standards for state agency compliance & perform project oversight on IT projects; requires agency to issue request for quote to certain vendors approved to provide commodities or services; requires DMS to prequalify firms & individuals to provide services on state term contract.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-227.
