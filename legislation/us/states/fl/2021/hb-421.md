# Florida HB 421 (2021) — Relief From Burdens on Real Property Rights

Relief From Burdens on Real Property Rights; Revises notice of claim requirements for property owners; creates presumption that settlement offers protect public interest; specifies property owners retain option to have court determine awards of compensation; authorizes property owners to bring claims against governmental entities in certain circumstances; provides property owners are not required to submit formal development applications or proceed through formal application processes to bring claims in specified circumstances.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-203.
