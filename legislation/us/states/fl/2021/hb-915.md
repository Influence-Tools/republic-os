# Florida HB 915 (2021) — Port of Palm Beach District, Palm Beach County

Port of Palm Beach District, Palm Beach County; Deletes provisions requiring certain persons to execute and deliver a bond within a specified time period after assuming office; revises annual salary of commissioners & revises the term "port manager" to "port director".
