# Florida HB 1645 (2021) — City of Freeport, Walton County

City of Freeport, Walton County; Provides exceptions to general law; provides requirements for specialty center designation; authorizes sale of alcoholic beverages for consumption on premises of specialty center under certain conditions; provides that applicant for alcoholic beverage license to be located in specialty center may not be denied licensure under certain conditions.
