# Florida HB 885 (2021) — Juvenile Justice Programs and Detention

Juvenile Justice Programs and Detention; Creates Accountability & Program Support Program within DJJ; requires court to consider specified information before ordering child into custody for failing to appear; requires each judicial circuit to develop plan in consultation with parties; requires DJJ to calculate annually & provide to each county that is not fiscally constrained & does not provide its own detention care for juveniles its annual percentage share of detention costs; authorizes DJJ to evaluate viability of alternative model for providing & funding education services for youth in detention & residential facilities.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-219.
