# Florida HB 1631 (2021) — Trailer Estates Park and Recreation District, Manatee County

Trailer Estates Park and Recreation District, Manatee County; Revises boundaries; revises powers & duties for trustees; provides for qualification of electors, annual election, & removal of trustees & appointment to fill vacancies; provides for assessment & collection of assessment; establishes fiscal year & provides for annual financial statements; authorizes trustees to issue bonds and other obligations; authorizing trustees to acquire & dispose of real & personal property & to adopt & enforce rules & regulations; provides for abolishment of district & conditions precedent to file suit against district or trustee; provides liability; requires referendum.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-261.
