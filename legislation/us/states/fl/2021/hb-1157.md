# Florida HB 1157 (2021) — Freestanding Emergency Departments

Freestanding Emergency Departments; Prohibits hospital-based off-campus emergency department from holding itself out to public as urgent care center; requires such departments to post signs containing specified statements; provides requirements for advertisement of such departments; requires AHCA & health insurers to post & annually update certain information on their websites.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-112.
