# Florida HB 597 (2021) — Homestead Exemption for Seniors 65 and Older

Homestead Exemption for Seniors 65 and Older; Revises provisions to require certain taxpayers to submit claim for homestead exemption only one time if certain conditions are met; deletes provision related to submission of supporting documentation for exemption renewal; requires property appraiser to provide specified information related to income limitations on annual basis.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-208.
