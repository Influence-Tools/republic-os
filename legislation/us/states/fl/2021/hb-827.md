# Florida HB 827 (2021) — School District Funding

School District Funding; Requires school districts allocate certain amount of specified funds to programs that prepare students to enroll in AICE courses; requires such funds be spent on specified costs; requires school districts to distribute specified bonuses to certain teachers providing pre-AICE instruction; requires bonuses based on student's score on AICE examination to be paid to all AICE teachers.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2021-84.
