# Florida HB 521 (2024) — Equitable Distribution of Marital Assets and Liabilities

Equitable Distribution of Marital Assets and Liabilities; Requires court to consider certain factors when determining if extraordinary circumstances exist; provides certain actions do not change whether real property is marital property; provides that business interests in closely held business is marital asset; requires court to consider certain factors when determining value of such interest; provides that certain real property is nonmarital asset.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-237.
