# Florida HB 691 (2024) — Town of Horseshoe Beach, Dixie County

Town of Horseshoe Beach, Dixie County; Provides exception to general law; requires Division of Alcoholic Beverages & Tobacco of DBPR to issue special alcoholic beverage license to certain restaurants which meet space, seating, & minimum gross revenue requirements; provides conditions for revocation of such license or denial of pending application for such license.
