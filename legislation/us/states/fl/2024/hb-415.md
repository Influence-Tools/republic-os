# Florida HB 415 (2024) — Pregnancy and Parenting Resources Website

Pregnancy and Parenting Resources Website; Requires DOH, in consultation with DCF & AHCA, to maintain website that provides pregnancy & parenting resources & provide clear & conspicuous link to such website on official website of respective departments & agency; requires DOH to contract with third party to develop such website by specified date.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-198.
