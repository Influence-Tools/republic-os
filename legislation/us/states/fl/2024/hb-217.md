# Florida HB 217 (2024) — College Campus Facilities in Areas of Critical State Concern

College Campus Facilities in Areas of Critical State Concern; Revises authorized uses of revenue from capital improvement fees to include certain college campus facilities & revises number of beds certain FCS institutions may provide to certain persons; revises which funds may be used for construction of dormitories; & requires Division of Bond Finance to conduct analysis of financing before issuance of specified bonds.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-50.
