# Florida HB 6007 (2024) — Relief/Julia Perez/St. Johns County Sheriff's Office

Relief/Julia Perez/St. Johns County Sheriff's Office; Provides for relief of Julia Perez by the St. Johns County Sheriff's Office; provides for appropriation of funds to compensate Julia Perez for personal injuries & damages sustained as result of negligence of employee of St. Johns County Sheriff's Office; provides legislative intent for waiver of certain lien interests; provides limitation on compensation & payment of attorney fees.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-299.
