# Florida HB 1117 (2024) — City of North Port, Sarasota County

City of North Port, Sarasota County; Creates Star Farms Village at North Port Stewardship District; establishes boundaries; provides for board of supervisors & election, membership, terms, meetings, & duties of board; provides method for transition of board from landowner control to control by resident electors; provides for district manager & treasurer; provides general & special powers of district; provides for bonds, borrowing, future ad valorem taxation, special assessments, issuance of certificates of indebtedness, & tax liens; provides for fees & charges; provides for referendum.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-292.
