# Florida HB 271 (2024) — Motor Vehicle Parking on Private Property

Motor Vehicle Parking on Private Property; Provides requirements for signage & invoices for parking facilities; authorizes entities to regulate such signage; prohibits late fees before certain period; requires such invoices to include dispute & appeal method; requires grace period before parking charges may be incurred; prohibits personal information from being sold, offered for sale, or transferred for sale by such owners or operators.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-64.
