# Florida HB 377 (2024) — License or Permit to Operate a Vehicle for Hire

License or Permit to Operate a Vehicle for Hire; Provides that person who holds license or permit issued by county or municipality to operate vehicle for hire may operate vehicle for hire in any other county or municipality without being subject to certain requirements or fees.
