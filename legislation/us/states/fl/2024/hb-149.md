# Florida HB 149 (2024) — Continuing Contracts

Continuing Contracts; Revises maximum estimated construction cost of construction projects for which governmental entity may enter into continuing contract; requires DMS to annually adjust maximum amount allowed under specified contracts using specified index & publish adjusted amount on DMS's website.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-204.
