# Florida HB 1569 (2024) — Exemption from Regulation for Bona Fide Nonprofit Organizations

Exemption from Regulation for Bona Fide Nonprofit Organizations; Authorizes FSC to adopt rules prescribing criteria & processes for determining whether organization is bona fide nonprofit organization for specified purpose; provides exemptions from certain regulation for bona fide nonprofit organizations & certain employees of bona fide nonprofit organization that meet specified criteria; requires Office of Financial Regulation to make specified determination; requires office to make such determination based on terms consistent with loan origination in public or charitable context; requires office to periodically examine books & activities of organization & to revoke its status as bona fide nonprofit organization.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-134.
