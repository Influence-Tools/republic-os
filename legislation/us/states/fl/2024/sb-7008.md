# Florida SB 7008 (2024) — OGSR/Department of the Lottery

OGSR/Department of the Lottery; Amending a provision relating to an exemption from public records requirements for certain information held by the Department of the Lottery, information about lottery games, personal identifying information of retailers and vendors for purposes of background checks, and certain financial information held by the department; providing for future legislative review and repeal of an exemption from public records requirements for information relating to the security of certain technologies, processes, and practices; removing the scheduled repeal of an exemption, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-49.
