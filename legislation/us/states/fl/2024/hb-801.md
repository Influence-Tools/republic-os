# Florida HB 801 (2024) — Alzheimer's Disease and Related Dementia Training for Law Enforcement and Correctional Officers

Alzheimer's Disease and Related Dementia Training for Law Enforcement and Correctional Officers; Requires FDLE to establish online, continued employment training component relating to Alzheimer's disease & related forms of dementia; requires training component be developed with DOEA; specifies instruction requirements for training; authorizes completion of such training to count toward certain requirement.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-52.
