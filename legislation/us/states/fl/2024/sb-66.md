# Florida SB 66 (2024) — Revive Awareness Day

Revive Awareness Day; Citing this act as “Victoria’s Law”; designating June 6 of each year as “Revive Awareness Day”; authorizing the Governor to issue an annual proclamation; encouraging the Department of Health to hold events to raise awareness of the dangers of opioid overdose and the availability and safe use of opioid antagonists as an effective way to rapidly reverse the effects of opioid overdose, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-67.
