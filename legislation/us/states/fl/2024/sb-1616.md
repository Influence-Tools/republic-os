# Florida SB 1616 (2024) — Electronic Access to Official Records

Electronic Access to Official Records; Requiring the county recorder or clerk of the court to make certain information publicly available through a searchable database on the county recorder’s or clerk of the court’s official website; authorizing such requirement to be satisfied by providing a stand-alone link to the official records index, etc.
