# Florida SB 544 (2024) — Swimming Lesson Voucher Program

Swimming Lesson Voucher Program; Creating the program within the Department of Health for a specified purpose; requiring the department to establish a network of swimming lesson vendors to participate in the program; requiring the department or a contracted nonprofit organization to attempt to secure a vendor in each county; specifying eligibility criteria for the program, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-89.
