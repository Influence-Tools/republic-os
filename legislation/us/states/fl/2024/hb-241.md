# Florida HB 241 (2024) — Coverage for Skin Cancer Screenings

Coverage for Skin Cancer Screenings; Requires DMS to require contracted state group health insurance plans to provide coverage & payment for annual skin cancer screenings performed by specified persons without imposing any cost-sharing requirement; specifies requirement for & restriction on payments for such screenings.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2024-63.
