# Florida HB 1D (2026D) — Establishing the Congressional Districts of the State

Establishing the Congressional Districts of the State; Redistricts state's congressional districts in accordance with U.S. Decennial Census of 2020 (plan EOGPCRP2026); reenacts provisions relating to election of representatives to Congress, electronic maps serving as the official maps of congressional districts, & severability; provides for applicability.
