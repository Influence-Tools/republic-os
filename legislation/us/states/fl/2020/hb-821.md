# Florida HB 821 (2020) — Pub. Rec. and Meetings/Information Technology Security Information

Pub. Rec. and Meetings/Information Technology Security Information; Provides exemptions from public records requirements for portions of records held by state agency that contain network schematics, hardware & software configurations, & encryption & certain recordings & transcripts; provides exemption from public meetings requirements for portions of meetings that would reveal such records; provides for future legislative review & repeal of exemptions; provides public necessity statement & for retroactive application of exemptions.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2020-25.
