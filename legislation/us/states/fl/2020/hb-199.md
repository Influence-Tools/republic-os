# Florida HB 199 (2020) — Sexual Battery Prosecution Time Limitation

Sexual Battery Prosecution Time Limitation; Designates act "Donna's Law"; creates exception to general time limitations allowing prosecution to be commenced at any time for specified sexual battery offenses against victims younger than a certain age.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2020-81.
