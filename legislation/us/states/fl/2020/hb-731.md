# Florida HB 731 (2020) — Agency for Health Care Administration

Agency for Health Care Administration; Revises certain duties of AHCA relating to reporting, oversight, licensure inspections, issuance of licenses, & Medicaid fraud investigations; removes annual assessment on health care entities; removes provisions relating to multiphasic testing centers.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2020-156.
