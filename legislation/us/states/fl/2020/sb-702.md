# Florida SB 702 (2020) — Petroleum Cleanup

Petroleum Cleanup; Authorizing the Department of Environmental Protection to use funds from the Inland Protection Trust Fund to pay for specified activities related to removal and replacement of petroleum storage systems; providing for petroleum storage system repair or replacement due to damage caused by ethanol or biodiesel and for preventive measures to reduce the potential for such damage; revising the contents of an advanced cleanup application to include a specified property owner or responsible party agreement, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2020-56.
