# Florida SB 886 (2020) — Errors in Deeds

Errors in Deeds; Providing that a deed containing a scrivener’s error conveys title as if there had been no such error if certain requirements are met; providing a form for a curative notice; requiring the clerks of the circuit court to accept and record curative notices; providing for the operation of a curative notice, etc.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2020-33.
