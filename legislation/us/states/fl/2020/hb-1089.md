# Florida HB 1089 (2020) — Trusts

Trusts; Authorizes trustees of certain trusts to reimburse persons being treated as owner of trust for specified amounts & specified manner; prohibits certain policies, values, & proceeds from being used for such reimbursement; requires specified powers be granted to certain persons if terms of trust require trustee to act at direction or with consent of such persons or that specified decisions be made directly by such persons.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2020-70.
