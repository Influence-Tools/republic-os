# Florida HB 1009 (2020) — Special Neighborhood Improvement Districts

Special Neighborhood Improvement Districts; Revises number of directors allowed for boards of special neighborhood improvement districts; requires local planning ordinances to specify number of directors & provide for 4-year staggered terms; requires that directors be landowners in proposed area & be subject to certain taxation.
