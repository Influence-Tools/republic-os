# Florida HB 59 (2020) — Automated Pharmacy Systems

Automated Pharmacy Systems; Authorizes community pharmacy to use automated pharmacy system; provides certain medicinal drugs stored in automated pharmacy system for outpatient dispensing are part of inventory of pharmacy providing services through such system; requires community pharmacies to adopt policies & procedures.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2020-124.
