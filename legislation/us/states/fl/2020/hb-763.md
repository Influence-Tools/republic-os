# Florida HB 763 (2020) — Patient Safety Culture Surveys

Patient Safety Culture Surveys; Requires licensed facilities to biennially conduct anonymous patient safety culture survey using applicable federal publication; requires facilities to biennially submit such survey to AHCA; requires agency to collect, compile, & publish survey data; revises requirements for submission of health care data to agency; provides appropriations.

**Enacted** — Laws of Florida, Chapter 2020-134.
