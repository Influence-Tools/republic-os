# Florida HB 1087 (2020) — Domestic Violence Services

Domestic Violence Services; Revises duties of DCF in relation to domestic violence program; removes duties & functions of Florida Coalition Against Domestic Violence with respect to domestic violence; revises requirements of domestic violence centers; removes coalition from capital improvement grant program process.
