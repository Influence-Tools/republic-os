# Delaware HB 67 (153) — AN ACT TO AMEND TITLE 21 OF THE DELAWARE CODE RELATING TO THE REMOVAL OF MOTOR

This Act creates a new chapter in Title 21 pertaining to the towing of vehicles without the consent of the owner or operator.
