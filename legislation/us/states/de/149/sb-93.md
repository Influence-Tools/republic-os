# Delaware SB 93 (149) — AN ACT TO AMEND TITLE 7 OF THE DELAWARE CODE RELATING TO FINFISHING.

This bill allows holders of gill net and hook and line permits to accept transfer of quotas from other commercial fishermen and catch the combined hook and line and gill net quota for striped bass using the equipment for which they hold a permit. The bill also allows commercial fishermen to use gill net tags during hook and line activities and hook and line tags during gill net activities. Additionally, the bill eliminates a specific reporting requirement.
