# Delaware SB 167 (149) — AN ACT TO AMEND THE CHARTER OF FENWICK ISLAND.

This Act amends the Charter of Fenwick Island to provide examples of documents that can be used to prove residency in the Town of Fenwick Island for purposes of voting in the annual municipal elections and change the limit on borrowing from $500,000 in any 1 year to 5% of the total assessed value of all non-tax-exempt real property.
