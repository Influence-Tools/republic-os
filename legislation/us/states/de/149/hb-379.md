# Delaware HB 379 (149) — AN ACT TO AMEND THE CHARTER OF THE TOWN OF WOODSIDE.

Section 1 clarifies the Town’s nuisance and dangerous building abatement powers and requires property owners to pay for the abatement costs. This section further clarifies the Town’s ability to grant franchises, licenses, and permits, and impose fees for the same. It authorizes the Town to impose a realty transfer tax, and it increases the maximum penalty for a code violation to $500.00.
