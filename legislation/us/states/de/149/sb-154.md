# Delaware SB 154 (149) — AN ACT TO AMEND THE CHARTER OF THE TOWN OF SOUTH BETHANY.

Section 1 eliminates the need to use an ordinance to appoint a Town Manager and specifies that all departments, including the Police Department, report directly to the Town Manager.
