# Delaware SB 42 (149) — AN ACT TO AMEND TITLE 18 OF THE DELAWARE CODE RELATING TO THE ADDITION OF MINOR LICENSED CHILDREN AND FOSTER CHILDREN TO EXISTING MOTOR VEHICLE POLICIES.

This Act permits individuals to add their resident minor child and foster child to their existing motor vehicle insurance policies as additional drivers.
