# Delaware SB 175 (149) — AN ACT TO AMEND TITLE 30 OF THE DELAWARE CODE RELATING TO WITHHOLDING OF INCOME TAX ON SALE OR EXCHANGE OF REAL ESTATE BY NONRESIDENTS.

This Act exempts the transfer of real estate acquired by foreclosure or deed in lieu of foreclosure from nonresident income tax withholding requirements.
