# Delaware HB 105 (149) — AN ACT TO CLARIFY TITLE 29, §6529 OF THE DELAWARE CODE RELATING TO THE AUTHORITY OF THE DIRECTOR OF THE OFFICE OF MANAGEMENT AND BUDGET.

This Act clarifies that the Director of the Office of Management and Budget has the authority to deposit Special Fund balances into the General Fund as a measure to control agency expenditures.
