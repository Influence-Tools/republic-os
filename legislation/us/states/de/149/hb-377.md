# Delaware HB 377 (149) — AN ACT TO AMEND TITLE 9 OF THE DELAWARE CODE RELATING TO TAXES.

This bill permits a County government to impose and collect a lodging tax of no more than 3 percent on hotel, motel, and tourist home rooms located in the unincorporated areas of the County.
