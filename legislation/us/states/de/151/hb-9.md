# Delaware HB 9 (151) — AN ACT TO AMEND TITLE 11 OF THE DELAWAE CODE RELATING TO CRIMES.

This Act adds the term “adjudication” to this section of the criminal code to enable juvenile defendants to petition the court for their adjudications of delinquency to be vacated and their juvenile criminal record to be expunged in regard to crimes, other than defined violent felonies, committed as a direct result of being victims of human trafficking.
