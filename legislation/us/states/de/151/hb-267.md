# Delaware HB 267 (151) — AN ACT TO AMEND CHAPTER 69 OF VOLUME 83 OF THE LAWS OF DELAWARE RELATING TO DECEDENTS' ESTATES AND TRUSTS.

This Act corrects an error in the drafting of Section 4, Chapter 69, Volume 83 of the Laws of Delaware, House Bill No. 164 of the 151st General Assembly, and makes the correction retroactive to the enactment of the law on June 30, 2021.
