# Delaware SB 298 (151) — AN ACT TO AMEND TITLE 26 OF THE DELAWARE CODE RELATING TO NET-METERING.

This bill amends provisions in Title 26 of the Delaware Code, Section 1014, related to rules and regulations promulgated by the Public Service Commission, municipal electric companies, and electric cooperatives on the subject of net energy metering.
