# Delaware HB 322 (151) — AN ACT TO AMEND TITLE 18 OF THE DELAWARE CODE RELATING TO PROCESSING OF REFUNDS OF OVERPAYMENTS.

This bill removes the requirement that the Director of the Office of Management and Budget approve Title 18-related refunds of overpayments.
