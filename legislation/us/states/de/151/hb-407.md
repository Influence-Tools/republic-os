# Delaware HB 407 (151) — AN ACT TO AMEND THE CHARTER OF THE TOWN OF FREDERICA.

This Act confers upon the Town Manager of the Town of Frederica the powers and authority conferred upon the Receiver of Taxes and County Treasurer for Kent County as set forth in Del.C. 87 specifically permitting the Town Manager to sell delinquent tax payer's real property through the monition process.
