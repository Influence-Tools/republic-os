# Delaware HB 112 (151) — AN ACT TO AMEND TITLE 25 OF THE DELAWARE CODE RELATING TO COMMON INTEREST OWNERSHIP.

This Act corrects several technical and typographic errors in the Delaware Common Interest Ownership Act (the “DUCIOA”) and makes a number of non-controversial changes agreed upon by the Common Interest Community Advisory Council and representatives of builders, real estate sales professionals, association managers, and owners.
