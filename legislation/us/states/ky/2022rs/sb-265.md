# Kentucky SB 265 (2022RS) — AN ACT relating to the Kentucky State University board of regents and declaring an emergency.

Direct the Governor to replace the Kentucky State University board of regents by April 1, 2022; direct the Governor's Postsecondary Education Nominating Committee to submit 16 nominations to the Governor by March 26, 2022; EMERGENCY.
