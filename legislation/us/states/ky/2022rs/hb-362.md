# Kentucky HB 362 (2022RS) — AN ACT relating to substance abuse intervention and declaring an emergency.

Amend KRS 222.433 to establish that health care professionals performing examinations may be subject to subpoena for purposes of cross-examination; establish that an order of treatment shall be issued upon a finding of proof beyond a reasonable doubt; EMERGENCY.
