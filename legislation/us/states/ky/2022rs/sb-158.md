# Kentucky SB 158 (2022RS) — AN ACT relating to reorganization.

Create a new section of KRS Chapter 42 to establish the Office of Fleet Management in the Finance and Administration Cabinet; establish the Division of Maintenance and the Division of Operations and Administration within the Office of Fleet Management; amend KRS 42.0145 to include the Office of Fleet Management within the Office of the Secretary of the Finance and Administration Cabinet; amend KRS 42.0171 to abolish the Division of Fleet Management within the Office of Administrative Services; amend KRS 12.020 and 45A.625 to conform.
