# Kentucky HB 238 (2023RS) — AN ACT relating to anatomical gift discrimination.

Create a new section in KRS Chapter 311 to prohibit a health care provider from discriminating against an individual who has a disability based solely on the individual's disability when providing health care and other services related to an anatomical gift donation.
