# Kentucky SB 247 (2023RS) — AN ACT relating to transient public school students.

Create a new section of KRS Chapter 158 requiring eligible students to have the option to remain in the same school when changing residence within the same school district; define "eligible student"; require the school district to provide transportation to the school from the student's new residence.
