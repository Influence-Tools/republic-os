# Kentucky HJR 53 (2025RS) — A JOINT RESOLUTION authorizing the release of funds.

Authorize the Office of State Budget Director to release capital construction funds for the Design Health Services Center project at Kentucky State University; require the university to submit a comprehensive business plan on or before November 1, 2025.
