# Kentucky SB 245 (2025RS) — AN ACT relating to the Department of Fish and Wildlife Resources Commission and declaring an emergency.

Amend KRS 150.022 to require that a Department of Fish and Wildlife Resources Commission member whose reappointment confirmation is declined by the Senate shall vacate his or her seat upon the date of sine die adjournment of the session in which the confirmation was declined.
