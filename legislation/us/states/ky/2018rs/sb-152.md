# Kentucky SB 152 (2018RS) — AN ACT relating to teacher compensation. Amend KRS 157.390 to allow school districts to provide compensation in addition to that provided in the single salary schedule for all classroom teachers in a school identified as being in targeted or comprehensive support and improvement status.
