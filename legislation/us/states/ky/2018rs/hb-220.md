# Kentucky HB 220 (2018RS) — AN ACT relating to marketplace contractors. Create a new section of KRS Chapter 342 defining "marketplace contractor" and "marketplace platform" and establishing the criteria in which a marketplace contractor shall not be deemed to be an employee of a marketplace platform.
