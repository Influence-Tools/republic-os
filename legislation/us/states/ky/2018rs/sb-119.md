# Kentucky SB 119 (2018RS) — AN ACT relating to cervid meat processors. Create a new section of KRS Chapter 150 to define "cervid meat processor"; require cervid meat processors to dispose of waste or unused material produced from processing cervid meat in the same manners as required for livestock, poultry, and fish carcasses under KRS 257.160; amend KRS 150.990 to establish penalties; amend KRS 257.160 to conform.
