# Kentucky SB 106 (2018RS) — AN ACT relating to blow drying services. Amend KRS 317A.010 to define "blow drying services" and "limited beauty salon"; amend KRS 317A.050 to establish who may be licensed to perform blow drying services in a licensed limited beauty salon; create accompanying fees; amend KRS 317A.062 to set various fees for a license to conduct blow drying services and a license to operate a limited beauty salon.
