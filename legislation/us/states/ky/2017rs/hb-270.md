# Kentucky HB 270 (2017RS) — AN ACT relating to manufactured housing. Create a new section of KRS Chapter 186A to require filing of an affidavit of severance with the county clerk when a manufactured home is to be removed from real estate located within the county; include requirements for information included on the affidavit of severance; require the county clerk to provide a copy of the affidavit of severance to the property valuation administrator.
