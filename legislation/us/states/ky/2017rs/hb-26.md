# Kentucky HB 26 (2017RS) — AN ACT relating to inspections by sheriffs. Repeal KRS 70.160, which requires monthly visits and inspections by the sheriff or deputies of certain public places, including but not limited to dance halls and roadhouses, along with written reporting to the county attorney and Circuit Court; amend KRS 70.170, 241.140, and 241.170 to conform.
