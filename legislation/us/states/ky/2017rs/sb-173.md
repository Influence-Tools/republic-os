# Kentucky SB 173 (2017RS) — AN ACT relating to Department of Juvenile Justice facilities and making an appropriation therefor. Provide authorization for the proceeds of the sale of state-owned real property and improvements in Owensboro, Kentucky operated by the Department of Juvenile Justice to be used to service debt relating to the Department of Juvenile Justice's Guaranteed Energy Savings Performance Contract loans.
