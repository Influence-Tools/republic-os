# Kentucky HB 78 (2017RS) — AN ACT relating to patient notification of mammogram results showing dense tissue. Create a new section of KRS 214.550 to 214.556 to require the provider of mammography services to provide information to a patient on breast density when appropriate; permit the Department for Public Health to provide updates to the definition of "dense breast tissue" if appropriate.
