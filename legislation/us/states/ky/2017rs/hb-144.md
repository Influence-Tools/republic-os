# Kentucky HB 144 (2017RS) — AN ACT relating to the operation of a motor vehicle. Create a new section of KRS Chapter 189 to define "solid waste collection service vehicle"; require motor vehicle operators to yield the right-of-way to these vehicles and the collection service's employees; provide that the section may be cited as the Slow Down to Get Around Law.
