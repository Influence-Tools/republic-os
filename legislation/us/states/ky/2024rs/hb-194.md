# Kentucky HB 194 (2024RS) — AN ACT relating to workplace violence against healthcare workers.

Amend KRS 508.025 to expand assault in the third degree to include causing or attempting to cause physical injury to a person employed by or under contract with a hospital if the event occurs in or on the premises of a hospital or hospital-owned outpatient facility.
