# Kentucky HB 371 (2024RS) — AN ACT relating to mine subsidence insurance.

Amend KRS 304.44-030 to require the administrator to establish the maximum total insured value reinsured per structure; increase the coverage amount for living expenses from $25,000 to $50,000; amend KRS 304.44-050 to conform; apply to policies issued or renewed on or after January 1, 2025; EFFECTIVE January 1, 2025.
