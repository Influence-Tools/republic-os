# Kentucky HB 513 (2024RS) — AN ACT relating to the New State Capitol campus.

Amend KRS 11.027 to create a process for submission of plans for installation or removal of statues, monuments, or objects of art on permanent display in the rotunda of the New State Capitol and require approval by the General Assembly.
