# Kentucky HB 122 (2024RS) — AN ACT relating to revenue and declaring an emergency.

Amend KRS 131.010 to define "administrative writings"; amend KRS 131.020 to require the department to publish administrative writings within 120 days of the issuance or finalization; amend KRS 131.030, 131.081, 131.130, and 131.190 to conform.
