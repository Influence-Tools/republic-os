# Kentucky HB 505 (2024RS) — AN ACT relating to alcohol and drug counselors.

Amend KRS 309.080, 309.0805, 309.083, 309.0813, 309.0831, 309.0841, 309.0842, and 309.088 to change references to "associate alcohol and drug counselor I" and "associate alcohol and drug counselor II"; amend KRS 309.081 to make changes to the Board of Alcohol and Drug Counselors; create a new Chapter 309 to establish criteria for registration as a temporary alcohol and drug peer support specialist; create a new section of KRS Chapter 222 to require the Cabinet for Health and Family Services to promulgate administrative regulations to establish requirements for providing alcohol and drug treatment and behavioral health service providers.
