# Kentucky SB 249 (2024RS) — AN ACT relating to sex offenders.

Create a new section of KRS 17.500 to 17.580 to define "social media platform"; require sex offenders who have committed a criminal offense against a victim who is a minor to display their full legal name on social media platforms; provide penalties.
