# Massachusetts H 4042 (191st) — An Act exempting certain positions in the city known as the town of West Springfield from the civil service law

Welch (with the approval of the mayor and town council) that the city known as the town of West Springfield be authorized to exempt certain positions in said town from the civil service law.
