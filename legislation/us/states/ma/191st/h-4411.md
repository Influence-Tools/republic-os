# Massachusetts H 4411 (191st) — An Act exempting all positions in the city known as the Town of Franklin's fire department from the civil service law

Roy (with the approval of the town council) that the city known as the town of Franklin be authorized to exempt all positions in the fire department of said town from the civil service law.
