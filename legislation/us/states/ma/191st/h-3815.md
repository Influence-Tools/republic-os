# Massachusetts H 3815 (191st) — An Act exempting the position of sealer of weights and measures within the town of Lynnfield from the Civil Service Law

By Representative Jones of North Reading and Senator Crighton, a joint petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3815) of Bradley H. Jones, Jr., and Brendan P. Crighton (by vote of the town) that the town of Lynnfield be authorized to exempt the position of sealer of weights and measures from the civil service law. Public Service.
