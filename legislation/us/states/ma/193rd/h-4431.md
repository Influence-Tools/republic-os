# Massachusetts H 4431 (193rd) — An Act directing the city of Boston Police Department to waive the maximum age requirement for police officers for Nuias Daveiga

Worrell (with the approval of the mayor and city council) that the city of Boston be authorized to appoint Nuias Daveiga a police officer in said city notwithstanding the maximum age requirement.
