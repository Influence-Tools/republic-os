# Massachusetts H 5062 (193rd) — An Act changing the Holyoke Treasurer position from elected to appointed

By Representative Duffy of Holyoke, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 5062) of Patricia A. Duffy (with the approval of the mayor and city council) that the city of Holyoke be authorized to change the treasurer in said city from an elected to an appointed position. Municipalities and Regional Government. [Local Approval Received.]
