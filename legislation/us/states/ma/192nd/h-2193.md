# Massachusetts H 2193 (192nd) — An Act to allow the town of Andover to transfer conservation land

By Ms. Nguyen of Andover, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2193) of Tram T. Nguyen and Frank A. Moran (by vote of the town) the town of Andover be authorized to transfer certain land to the conservation commission of the said town for conservation and passive recreation purposes. Municipalities and Regional Government. [Local Approval Received.]
