# Massachusetts H 4004 (192nd) — An Act relative to certain statutory funds and transfers of appropriations

Sections 7, 102 and 103 contained in the engrossed Bill making appropriations for the fiscal year 2022 for the maintenance of the departments, boards, commissions, institutions and certain activities of the Commonwealth, for interest, sinking fund and serial bond requirements and for certain permanent improvements (see House, No. 4002), which had been returned by His Excellency the Governor with recommendation of amendment (for message see Attachment D of House, No. 4019).
