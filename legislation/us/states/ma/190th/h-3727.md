# Massachusetts H 3727 (190th) — An Act directing the police department of the city of Boston to waive the maximum age requirement for police officers for James Anthony Verderico, Jr.

By Mr. Hunt of Boston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3727) of Daniel J. Hunt (with the approval of the mayor and city council) that the city of Boston be authorized to appoint Anthony Verderico, Jr., as police officer, notwithstanding the maximum age requirement. Public Service. [Local Approval Received.]
