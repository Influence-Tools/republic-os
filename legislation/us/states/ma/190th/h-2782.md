# Massachusetts H 2782 (190th) — An Act exempting certain uniformed positions in the fire department of the city of Marlborough from the civil service law

By Miss Gregoire of Marlborough, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2782) of Danielle W. Gregoire and Carmine L. Gentile (with the approval of the mayor and city council) that the city of Marlborough be authorized to exempt all positions in the fire department of said city from the civil service law. Public Service. [Local Approval Received.]
