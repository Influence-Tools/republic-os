# Massachusetts H 3784 (190th) — An Act authorizing the town of Milford to appoint water commissioners

By Mr. Murray of Milford, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3784) of Brian Murray and Ryan C. Fattman (by vote of the town) that the town of Milford be authorized to acquire the Milford Water Company, appoint a board of water commissioners and contract with a water department manager. Municipalities and Regional Government. [Local Approval Received.]
