# Massachusetts H 4488 (190th) — An Act authorizing the city of Beverly to employ Paul Cotter as Fire Chief beyond the statutory requirement age

Lovely (with the approval of the mayor and city council) that the city of Beverly be authorized to employ Paul Cotter as fire chief, notwithstanding the maximum age requirement. Public Service.
