# Virginia HB 377 (2018) — Virginia Water Protection Permit; exception for stormwater management facility on dry land.

Exempts from the requirement to obtain a Virginia Water Protection Permit any impact to a stormwater management facility on dry land. The bill directs the Department of Environmental Quality to adopt guidance to ensure that any project claiming this exemption creates no more than minimal ecological impact.
