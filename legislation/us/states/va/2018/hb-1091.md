# Virginia HB 1091 (2018) — Virginia Resources Authority; dredging projects.

Includes within the definition of the term "project" any dredging program or project undertaken to benefit the economic and community development goals of a local government, other than a dredging program or dredging project undertaken for or by the Virginia Port Authority.
