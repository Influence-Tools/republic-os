# Virginia SB 941 (2018) — Trooper Berke Bates Bridge; designating as bridge on Route 612 over I-64 in New Kent County.

Designates the bridge on Route 612 (Airport Road) over Interstate 64 at mile marker 209 in the County of New Kent the "Trooper Pilot Berke Bates Memorial Bridge."
