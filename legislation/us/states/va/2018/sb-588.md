# Virginia SB 588 (2018) — Alcoholic beverage control; annual mixed beverage special events licenses for museums.

Allows annual mixed beverage special events licenses to be issued to localities for special events conducted on the premises of a museum for historic interpretation that is owned and operated by the locality.
