# Virginia HB 1092 (2018) — Dredging projects; tax increment, financing.

Specifies that dredging projects are development projects eligible for tax increment financing. A dredging project by or for the Virginia Port Authority is not eligible for such financing unless the Authority has an agreement with a local governing body for local financial participation in the project.
