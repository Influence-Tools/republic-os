# Virginia HB 836 (2018) — Child day programs at public or private school facilities; exemptions.

Prohibits the Board of Social Services from adopting regulations governing child day programs located at public school facilities that require inspection or approval of the building, vehicles used to transport children attending the child day program that are owned by the school, or meals served to such children that are prepared by the school.
