# Virginia HB 1354 (2018) — Motor vehicles; covered & unlit lighting devices.

Provides that if certain lighting devices are unlit, have a clear lens, and have a clear reflector if the lighting device has a reflector, then a vehicle equipped with such lighting device may be operated on the highways without covering the lighting device.
