# Virginia HB 1024 (2018) — Attorney fees; repeals provision that allows only fee of one attorney to be taxed by court.

Repeals the provision that allows only the fee of one attorney to be taxed by the court, even if the recovering party had more than one attorney. This bill is a recommendation of the Boyd-Graves Conference.
