# Virginia HB 291 (2018) — Social Services, Commissioner of; storage and preservation of orders and adoption files.

Provides the Commissioner of Social Services with certain powers and duties related to the custody, storage, preservation, and duplication of adoption files. The bill contains an emergency clause.
