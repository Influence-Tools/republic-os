# Virginia HB 1212 (2018) — Pro se minors; signing of pleading, motion, or other paper by next friend.

Provides that a minor who is not represented by an attorney shall sign his pleading, motion, or other paper by his next friend. The bill further provides that either or both parents of such minor may sign on such minor's behalf as his next friend, unless such signature is otherwise prohibited by § 64.2-716 of the Uniform Trust Code.
