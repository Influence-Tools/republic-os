# Virginia HB 1377 (2018) — Epinephrine; possession and administration at outdoor educational programs.

Provides that an employee of an organization that provides outdoor educational experiences or programs for youth who is authorized by a prescriber and trained in the administration of epinephrine may possess and administer epinephrine and provides liability protection for such employees.
