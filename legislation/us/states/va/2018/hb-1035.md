# Virginia HB 1035 (2018) — Virginia Water Supply Revolving Fund; loans for regional projects.

Directs the Board of Health, when making loans, loan subsidies, or grants for regional water projects in the Eastern Virginia Groundwater Management Area, to give preference to projects that do not involve the withdrawal of groundwater from the coastal plain aquifer.
