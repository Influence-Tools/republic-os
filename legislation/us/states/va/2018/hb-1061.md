# Virginia HB 1061 (2018) — Blind or vision impaired; rehabilitative manufacturing and services industries.

Updates terminology regarding manufacturing and service industries operated by the Department for the Blind and Vision Impaired for the employment of individuals who are blind or vision impaired.
