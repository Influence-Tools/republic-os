# Virginia HB 1520 (2018) — Alcoholic beverage control; exemptions from licensure certain nonprofit swim clubs.

Allows any private swim club operated by a duly organized nonprofit corporation or association to allow its members to bring lawfully acquired alcoholic beverages onto the premises of such club and consume such alcoholic beverages on the premises of such club.
