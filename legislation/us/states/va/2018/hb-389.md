# Virginia HB 389 (2018) — Child abuse and neglect; notice of founded reports to Superintendent of Public Instruction.

Requires local departments of social services to notify the Superintendent of Public Instruction without delay (i) when an individual holding a license issued by the Board of Education is the subject of a founded complaint of child abuse or neglect and (ii) if the founded complaint of child abuse or neglect is dismissed on appeal.
