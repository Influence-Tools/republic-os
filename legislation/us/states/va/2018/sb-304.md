# Virginia SB 304 (2018) — Emergency medical services vehicles; temporary permit.

Provides that a temporary permit for an emergency medical services vehicle that does not meet required standards is valid for a period of 90 days from the end of the month of issue. Under current law, such permit is valid for a period not to exceed 60 days.
