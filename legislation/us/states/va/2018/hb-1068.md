# Virginia HB 1068 (2018) — License plates, special; veterans of certain military reserve organizations.

Provides that veterans of the Air Force Reserve, the Army Reserve, the Coast Guard Reserve, the Marine Reserve, and the Naval Reserve shall, upon application, be issued the special license plate issued under current law, upon application, to active members of those military reserve organizations.
