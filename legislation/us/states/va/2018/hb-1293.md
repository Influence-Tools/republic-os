# Virginia HB 1293 (2018) — Unemployment compensation; increases penalty for failure to file reports.

Increases the penalty, from $75 to $100, that the Virginia Employment Commission is required to assess an employer who fails to file a report required under Title 60.2 (Unemployment Compensation) with respect to wages or taxes.
