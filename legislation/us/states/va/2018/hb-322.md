# Virginia HB 322 (2018) — Naloxone or other opioid antagonist; possession & administration.

Adds employees of the Department of Corrections who are designated as probation and parole officers or correctional officers to the list of individuals who may possess and administer naloxone or other opioid antagonist, provided that they have completed a training program.
