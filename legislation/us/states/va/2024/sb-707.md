# Virginia SB 707 (2024) — Public middle schools and high schools; career and technical education organizations permitted.

Public middle schools and high schools; student organizations; career and technical education organizations permitted.Permits each public middle school and high school to establishcareer and technical education student organizations, regardlessof whether such school offers career and technical education courses.
