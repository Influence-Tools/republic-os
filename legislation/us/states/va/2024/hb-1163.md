# Virginia HB 1163 (2024) — Driver's license; standard for being denied or having license reviewed/revoked for medical reasons.

Department of Motor Vehicles; medical review. Changes the standard for being denied a driver's license or having a driver's license reviewed or revoked for medical reasons by removing language regarding the presence of a disability or disease and requiring the existence of&nbsp;an impairment that will prevent the driver from exercising reasonable and ordinary&nbsp;control over a motor vehicle or drive a motor vehicle safely.
