# Virginia HB 583 (2024) — Public elementary school teachers; length of daily lunch breaks.

Public school teachers; daily lunch breaks; data collection; report. Requires the Department&nbsp;of Education to&nbsp;annually collect and present in the Virginia School Survey of Climate and Working Conditions school-level and division-level data on the share of teachers that are provided each working day a lunch break of at least 30 minutes in length and unencumbered by any teaching or supervisory duties.
