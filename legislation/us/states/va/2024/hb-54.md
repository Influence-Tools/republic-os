# Virginia HB 54 (2024) — Falls Church, City of; amending charter, qualifications of members of boards and commissions.

Charter; City of Falls Church; qualificationsof members of boards and commissions. Removes the requirementin the City of Falls Church charter that board and commission membersbe qualified voters, replacing it with an age requirement of at least18 years of age while leaving the city residence requirement.
