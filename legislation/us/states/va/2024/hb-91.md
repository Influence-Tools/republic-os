# Virginia HB 91 (2024) — Smithfield, Town of; amending charter, municipal elections, repealing outdated provisions.

Charter; Town of Smithfield. Amends the charter for the Town of Smithfield in Isle of Wight County to reflect the town's shift from May to November municipal elections. The bill also updates or repeals outdated provisions of the town's charter. This bill is identical to SB 309.
