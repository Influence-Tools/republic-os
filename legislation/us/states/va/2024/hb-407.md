# Virginia HB 407 (2024) — Child Care Subsidy Program; categorical eligibility for certain families.

Child Care Subsidy Program; categorical eligibility for certain families. Provides that any family that receives public assistance through Medicaid or the Special Supplemental Nutrition Program for Women, Infants, and Children shall be deemed to categorically satisfy income eligibility requirements&nbsp;to receive assistance through the Child Care Subsidy Program.
