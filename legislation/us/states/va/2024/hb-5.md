# Virginia HB 5 (2024) — Town of Leesburg Parking Authority; created.

Town of Leesburg Parking Authority. Authorizesthe creation of the Town of Leesburg Parking Authority. The billgrants to the Authority various powers, including the authority to construct, operate, and lease parking facilities, contract with outside entities, issue revenue bonds and revenue refunding bonds, and acquire property. The bill also exempts the Authority from taxation.
