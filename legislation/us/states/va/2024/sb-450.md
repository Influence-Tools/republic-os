# Virginia SB 450 (2024) — Towing fees; State Corporation Commission shall examine existing regulation of fees.

Regulation of towing fees; State CorporationCommission; report. Directs the State Corporation Commission to examine the existing regulation of fees charged for the removal ofvehicles and identify policy options for the Commission to assumeall or part of such regulation. The bill requires the Commissionto report its findings to the General Assembly by November 30, 2024.
