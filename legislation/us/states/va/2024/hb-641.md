# Virginia HB 641 (2024) — Relief; Kingrea, David Wayne, compensation for wrongful inclusion on sex offender registry.

Claims; David Wayne Kingrea; compensation forwrongful inclusion on sex offender registry. Provides that anyindividual wrongfully incarcerated or wrongfully required to registeron the sex offender registry may be awarded compensation for everysuch year of wrongful incarceration or wrongful registration. Thebill grants relief in an amount to be appropriated to David WayneKingrea, who was wrongly convicted of indecent liberties with a minor,and as a result of such wrongful conviction, required to registeron the sex offender registry.
