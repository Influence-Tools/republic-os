# Virginia HB 1419 (2024) — Electronic sales by dealers; titling.

Electronic sales by dealers; titling. Authorizes certain&nbsp;motor vehicle dealers to choose to sell a motor vehicle electronically by obtaining a title in the dealer's name for resale. This bill has a contingent effective date of the earlier&nbsp;of (i) July 1, 2025, or (ii)&nbsp;receipt of proper notification&nbsp;that a secure power of attorney prescribed by federal law and in a form approved by the Department of Motor Vehicles is available to motor vehicle dealers to be electronically signed by a purchaser.
