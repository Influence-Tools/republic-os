# Virginia HB 425 (2024) — Motor Vehicles, Department of; release of privileged information.

Department of Motor Vehicles; release of privileged information. Permits the Department of Motor Vehicles to release to a party that is subject to an administrative proceeding conducted by the&nbsp;Department nonmedical privileged information necessary to participate in such administrative proceeding.&nbsp;The bill limits such&nbsp;information to matters of fact and law asserted or questioned by the Department and&nbsp;prohibits the dissemination of such information&nbsp;to any third party that is not a party to&nbsp;the administrative proceeding.
