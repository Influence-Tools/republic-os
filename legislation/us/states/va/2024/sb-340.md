# Virginia SB 340 (2024) — Virginia Freedom of Information Act; exclusions from mandatory disclosure, purchase card statement.

Virginia Freedom of Information Act; exclusionsfrom mandatory disclosure; purchase card statement. Clarifiesthat the name of a public employee, officer, or official as it appearson a purchase card statement or other payment record and the descriptionof individual purchases are not exempt from disclosure by the StateComptroller.
