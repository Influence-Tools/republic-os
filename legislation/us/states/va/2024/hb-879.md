# Virginia HB 879 (2024) — Land's End Wildlife Mgmt. Area; DWR to grant & convey an ingress-egress easement & right-of-way.

Conveyance of easement. Authorizes the Department of Wildlife Resources to grant and convey an easement and right-of-way at Land's End Wildlife Management Area to Joseph C. Frank III,&nbsp;Betty J. Frank, Jacob C. Ackerman, and Crystal F. Ackerman. The easement will allow ingress and egress from State Route 625 (Salem Church Road) to the grantees' properties.
