# Virginia HB 356 (2024) — Investment of public funds; qualified public entity allowed to invest in asset-backed securities.

Investment of public funds. Allows any qualified public entity of the Commonwealth to invest in asset-backed securities&nbsp;that are guaranteed by the United States or any agency thereof. This bill is identical to SB 510.
