# Virginia HB 59 (2024) — Virginia Regional Industrial Facilities Act; Planning Districts 13 and 14.

Virginia Regional Industrial Facilities Act; Planning Districts 13 and 14. Adds Planning Districts 13 (Southside) and 14 (Commonwealth Regional Commission) to the list of areas where localities may create a regional industrial facility authority that contains a town and only one city or county rather than at least two cities or counties.
