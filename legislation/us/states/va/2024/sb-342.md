# Virginia SB 342 (2024) — Virginia Waste Management Board; open burning of solid wastes, transportation of vegetative waste.

Virginia Waste Management Board; open burningof solid wastes; transportation of vegetative waste. Requiresthe Virginia Waste Management Board to amend regulations to allowfor vegetative waste to be transported to another location for destructionif it is impractical or unsafe to destroy such waste on the premisesof private property.
