# Virginia HB 1082 (2024) — Judgments, certain; debtor may petition court for restricted license.

Enforcement of certain judgments; restrictedlicense. Authorizes the court to order a restricted license tooperate a motor vehicle to a judgment debtor who has had his driver'slicense suspended for failure to satisfy certain judgments.
