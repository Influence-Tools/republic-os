# Virginia HB 738 (2024) — Space Force; extension of certain benefits and privileges for persons serving in Armed Forces.

Space Force; extension of certain benefits andprivileges for persons serving in a branch of the Armed Forces. Amends several provisions of law related to certain benefits andprivileges available to persons serving or having served in a branchof the Armed Forces to include the Space Force as an eligible branch.
