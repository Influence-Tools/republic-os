# Virginia HB 1538 (2024) — Manufactured home; conversion to real property.

Conversion of manufactured home to real property. Establishes a process whereby a manufactured home owner who is notlisted as the owner of such manufactured home on its title may detitlesuch manufactured home in order to convert the home to real property.
