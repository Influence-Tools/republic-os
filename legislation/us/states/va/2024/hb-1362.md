# Virginia HB 1362 (2024) — School bus video-monitoring system; citations.

School bus video-monitoring system; citations. Prohibits a contract between a&nbsp;private vendor&nbsp;and a school division for the operation of school bus video-monitoring systems to capture passing stopped school bus violations from requiring a minimum quota of violations captured or citations issued for the video-monitoring system to be deployed.
