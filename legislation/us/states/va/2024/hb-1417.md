# Virginia HB 1417 (2024) — Contractors; workers' compensation requirements.

Contractors; workers' compensation requirements.Removes the requirements that the governing body of a localityshall forward a signed certification to the Virginia Workers' CompensationCommission and the Commission shall conduct periodic audits of selectedcontractors to whom such body has issued business licenses, therebyeliminating the need for commissioners of the revenue to send theCommission 61A forms.
