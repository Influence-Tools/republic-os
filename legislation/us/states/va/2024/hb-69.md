# Virginia HB 69 (2024) — Vacancies in elected local offices; interim appointments, notice requirement.

Vacancies in elected local offices; interimappointments; notice requirement. Requires the local governingbody or elected school board making an interim appointment to filla vacancy in the membership of such body or board to hold a publicmeeting at least seven days prior to making such appointment. Thebill specifies that at such meeting, the body or board shall announcethe names of all persons being proposed for the interim appointmentand shall make available for inspection each person's resume andany other materials required by the body or board.
