# Virginia SB 510 (2024) — Investment of public funds; qualified public entity allowed to invest in asset-backed securities.

Investment of public funds. Allows any qualified public entity of the Commonwealth to invest in asset-backed securities that are guaranteed by the United States or any agency thereof. This bill is identical to HB 356.
