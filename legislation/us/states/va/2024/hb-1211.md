# Virginia HB 1211 (2024) — Tax assessment districts; establishes, petition by parcel owners.

Tax assessment districts; petition by parcelowners. Changes the threshold for petitioning a city or townfor establishment of a tax assessment district from not less thanthree-fourths of the landowners affected to the owners of not lessthan three-fourths of the parcels affected.
