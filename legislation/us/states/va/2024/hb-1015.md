# Virginia HB 1015 (2024) — Agricultural best management practices & agricultural equipment purchase tax credits; sunset date.

Agricultural best management practices tax credit;agricultural equipment purchase tax credit; sunset date. Extendsfrom January 1, 2025, to January 1, 2030, the sunset date of theindividual and corporate agricultural best management practices incometax credit and extends from January 1, 2026, to January 1, 2030, the sunset date of the individual and corporate income tax credit forthe purchase of conservation tillage and precision agricultural applicationequipment.
