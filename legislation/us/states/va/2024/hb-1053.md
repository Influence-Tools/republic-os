# Virginia HB 1053 (2024) — Abandoned watercraft; destruction and disposal by localities and state agencies.

Destruction and disposal of abandoned watercraftby localities and state agencies. Allows localities and stateagencies to apply, under certain conditions, to the Department ofWildlife Resources for an authorization to destroy and dispose ofan abandoned watercraft.
