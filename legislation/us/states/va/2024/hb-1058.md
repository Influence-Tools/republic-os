# Virginia HB 1058 (2024) — Chesapeake Bay Public Access Authorities; singular license for waterfowl blinds.

Department of Wildlife Resources; singular licensefor waterfowl blinds in Chesapeake Bay Public Access Authorities.Requires the Department of Wildlife Resources to develop a singularlicense and license plate supplied with such license for all stationaryblinds granted to and located on property owned by the Middle PeninsulaChesapeake Bay Public Access Authority and the Northern Neck ChesapeakeBay Public Access Authority.
