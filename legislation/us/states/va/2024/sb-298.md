# Virginia SB 298 (2024) — Conservation tillage and precision agricultural application equipment; tax credit for purchase.

Extends the sunset date of the individual and corporate tax creditfor purchase of conservation tillage and precision agricultural applicationequipment from January 1, 2026, to January 1, 2030.
