# Virginia HB 730 (2024) — Campaign finance; independent expenditure reports, electronic filing required.

Campaign finance; independent expenditure reports; electronic filing required. Requires independent expenditure reports to be filed electronically. Current law allows such reports to be filed either electronically or in writing. The bill&nbsp;has a delayed effective date of July 1, 2025. This bill is identical to SB 692.
