# Virginia HB 1002 (2024) — Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency, Division of; powers and duties.

Division of Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency;powers and duties. Requires the Department of Energy's Divisionof Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency to identify and monitorany federal grant programs, loan programs, or other opportunitiesfor federal funding to further the Commonwealth's energy efficiencygoals. The bill also requires the Division to aid businesses in theCommonwealth, industries, utilities, academic institutions, stateand local governments, and private institutions in securing federalfunding for energy efficiency efforts as part of its duty to encourage efforts to develop energy resources and energy conservation programs.
