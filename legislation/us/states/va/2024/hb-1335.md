# Virginia HB 1335 (2024) — J.D. Power Official Used Car Guide; added to list of publications for retail value of vehicles.

Motor vehicle value; J.D. Power OfficialUsed Car Guide. Adds the J.D. Power Official Used CarGuide to the list of publications from which the retail valueof an automobile is admissible as evidence of fair market value ofsuch automobile in any civil or criminal case in which the priceof an automobile is in issue.
