# Virginia HB 1487 (2024) — DHCD; translation of certain forms and documents into five non-English languages on website, etc.

Department of Housing and Community Development; forms and documents for landlords and tenants; translation into non-English languages. Directs the Department of Housing and Community Development to translate all forms and documents that the Department is mandated by law to create and that are posted on its website for use by residential landlords and tenants into the five non-English languages most commonly spoken in Virginia. The bill allows the Department to accept materials translated by volunteers but&nbsp;requires the Department to&nbsp;verify the accuracy of such translations prior to making such translations available on its website.
