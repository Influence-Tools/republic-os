# Virginia HB 682 (2024) — Bristol, City of; amending charter, increases voting precincts.

Charter; City of Bristol. Amends the City of Bristol's charter to provide for five voting precincts rather than four. The bill also replaces references to the personnel department with "human resources department" and makes changes related to the assignment of powers and duties. This bill is identical to SB 618.
