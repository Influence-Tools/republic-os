# Virginia SB 25 (2024) — Virginia Brownfield and Coal Mine Renewable Energy Grant Fund and Program; allocation of funds.

Virginia Brownfield and Coal Mine Renewable Energy Grant Fund and Program; allocation of funds. Removes the prohibition on the allocation of funds to the Virginia Brownfield and Coal Mine Renewable Energy Grant Fund and Program unless federal funds are available in an amount that would cover the entire cost of such an allocation. This bill is identical to HB 199.
