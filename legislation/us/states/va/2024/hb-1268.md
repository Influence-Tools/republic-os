# Virginia HB 1268 (2024) — Community corrections alternative program; evaluation and diagnosis to determine eligibility.

Eligibility for participation in community corrections alternative program; evaluation and diagnosis; referral to the Departmentof Corrections. Provides that a probation and parole officermay refer a defendant on probation to the Department of Correctionsfor evaluation and diagnosis to determine eligibility and suitabilityfor participation in the community corrections alternative programprior to the approval of the court. Under current law, such referralmay take place with the approval of the court. The bill also makestechnical amendments.
