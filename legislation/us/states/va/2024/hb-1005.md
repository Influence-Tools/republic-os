# Virginia HB 1005 (2024) — Asbestos, Lead, and Home Inspectors, Virginia Board for; dust sampling technicians, renovators.

Virginia Board for Asbestos, Lead, and Home Inspectors; dust sampling technicians, renovators, and accredited renovation training programs. Removes the responsibility of the Board for Asbestos, Lead, and Home Inspectors to promulgate regulations concerning dust sampling technicians, renovators, and accredited renovation training programs. This bill is identical to SB 560.
