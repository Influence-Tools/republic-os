# Virginia HB 1409 (2024) — Crash reports; contracted service providers.

Crash reports; contracted service providers. Authorizes law-enforcement agencies to utilize a contracted service provider to&nbsp;forward crash reports electronically to the Department of Motor Vehicles&nbsp;and manage or&nbsp;disseminate copies of certain crash reports as authorized by law. The bill authorizes the release of nonpersonally identifiable vehicle information from crash reports to a contracted service provider. This bill is identical to SB 732.
