# Virginia SB 19 (2024) — Recovery residences; death and serious injury reports.

Recovery residences; death and serious injuryreports. Requires the Department of Behavioral Health and DevelopmentalServices to promulgate regulations that require recovery residencesto report to the Department, in the same manner as licensed facilitiesand programs, any death that occurs in the recovery residence.
