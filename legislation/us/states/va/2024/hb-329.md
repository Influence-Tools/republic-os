# Virginia HB 329 (2024) — Marriage & family therapists; Bd. of Counseling to amend regulations related to licensure.

Board of Counseling; licensure by endorsementfor marriage and family therapists. Directs the Board of Counselingto amend its regulations related to the process for licensure byendorsement for marriage and family therapists. The bill removesrequirements that applicants for licensure by endorsement provideevidence that they meet certain educational and experience requirements.
