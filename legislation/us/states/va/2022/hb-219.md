# Virginia HB 219 (2022) — Kenbridge, Town of; amending charter, municipal elections.

Charter; Town of Kenbridge. Updates thetown's charter to reflect the upcoming shift in municipal electionsfrom May to November. In addition, the bill reduces the mayor's termfrom four years to two years, beginning with the election to be heldin November 2024.
