# Virginia HB 88 (2022) — Golf carts and utility vehicles; Town of Ivor authorized to allow operation on highways.

Golf carts and utility vehicles; Town of Ivor.Adds the Town of Ivor to the list of towns that may authorize theoperation of golf carts and utility vehicles on designated publichighways despite not having established their own police departments.
