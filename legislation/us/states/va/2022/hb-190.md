# Virginia HB 190 (2022) — Urbanna, Town of; amending charter, extends term for elected mayor and council members.

Charter; Town of Urbanna. Extends the termfor the elected mayor of the Town of Urbanna in Middlesex Countyfrom two years to four years and spells out specific responsibilitiesof the office. The bill also staggers and extends the terms for councilmembers from two years to four years.
