# Virginia HB 242 (2022) — Professional counselors, licensed; added to list of providers who can disclose or recommend records.

Practice of licensed professional counselors.Adds licensed professional counselors to the list of eligibleproviders who can disclose or recommend the withholding of patientrecords, face a malpractice review panel, and provide recommendationson involuntary temporary detention orders.
