# Virginia HB 238 (2022) — Land use assessment; forms used for revalidation of applications shall be prepared by TAX.

Land use assessment; forms. Provides that the forms used for revalidation of applications for land use assessment shall be prepared by the Department of Taxation. Under current law, such forms are prepared by the locality. The bill directs the Department to seek input from localities across the Commonwealth in developing such forms.
