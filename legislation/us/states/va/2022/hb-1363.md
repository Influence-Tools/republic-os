# Virginia HB 1363 (2022) — Norvel LaFallette Ray Lee Memorial Highway; designating as portion of Route 220 in Botetourt County.

Norvel LaFallette Ray Lee Memorial Highway.Designates the portion of U.S. Route 220 in Botetourt County betweenState Route 43 (Narrow Passage Road) and the boundary line between Botetourt and Alleghany Counties as the "Norvel LaFallette Ray LeeMemorial Highway."
