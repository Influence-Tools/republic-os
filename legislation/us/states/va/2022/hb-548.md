# Virginia HB 548 (2022) — Chesapeake, City of; local government authority to require analysis of water.

City of Chesapeake; local government authority to require analysis of water. Adds the City of Chesapeake to the list of localities that may establish reasonable testing requirements to determine compliance with existing federal or state drinking water quality standards and require that such testing be done prior to the issuance of building permits. This bill is identical to SB 53.
