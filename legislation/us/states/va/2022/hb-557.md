# Virginia HB 557 (2022) — Southwestern Va. Mental Health Inst.; Gov. to convey property to Smyth County.

Disposition of property previously used by theDepartment of Behavioral Health and Developmental Services as theSouthwestern Virginia Mental Health Institute. Authorizes theGovernor to convey a portion of property previously used by the Departmentof Behavioral Health and Developmental Services as the SouthwesternVirginia Mental Health Institute to Smyth County. The conveyanceshall be made without consideration and in as-is condition.
