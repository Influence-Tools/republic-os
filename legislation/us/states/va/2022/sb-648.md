# Virginia SB 648 (2022) — Real property tax; exemption for the elderly and handicapped.

Real property tax; exemption for the elderlyand handicapped. Provides that any locality may by ordinance accept documentation establishing eligibility for the real property taxexemption for the elderly and handicapped on a rolling basis throughoutthe year. Current law states that localities shall accept such documentationafter January 1 but before April 1 or a later date fixed by ordinance.
