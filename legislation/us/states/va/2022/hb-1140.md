# Virginia HB 1140 (2022) — Voter registration; cancellation of registration, notice requirement.

Voter registration; cancellation of registration; notice requirement. Requires general registrars to provide notice of the cancellation of a voter's registration to the voter by mail to the address listed in the voter's registration record and by email to the email address provided on the voter's registration application, if one was provided.
