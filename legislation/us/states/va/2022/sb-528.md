# Virginia SB 528 (2022) — Income tax, state; military benefits subtraction.

Income tax; military benefits subtraction;emergency. Establishes an income tax subtraction for up to $20,000of military benefits in taxable year 2021, up to $30,000 in taxable year 2022, and up to $40,000 in taxable year 2023 and each year thereafter.The bill defines military benefits to include military retirementincome and benefits paid to the surviving spouse of a veteran. Thebill contains an emergency clause.
