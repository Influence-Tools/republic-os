# Virginia SB 145 (2022) — Subaqueous beds; unlawful use, replacement of piers.

Unlawful use of subaqueous beds; replacement of piers. Excludes the replacement of certain private piers used for noncommercial purposes from the requirement that a permit be issued by the Virginia&nbsp;Marine Resources Commission prior to taking certain actions regarding the beds of the bays, ocean, rivers, streams, or creeks that are the property of the Commonwealth.
