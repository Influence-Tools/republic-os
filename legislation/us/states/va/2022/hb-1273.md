# Virginia HB 1273 (2022) — Hunting with dogs; dogs to wear tags.

Hunting with dogs; dogs to wear tags. Requiresthat any dog engaged in lawful hunting wear a substantial collarwith a tag attached that identifies the name of the owner or custodianof the dog and a current phone number.
