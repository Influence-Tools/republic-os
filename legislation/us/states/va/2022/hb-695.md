# Virginia HB 695 (2022) — Worker training tax credit; clarifies definition of 'eligible worker training,' extends sunset date.

Worker training tax credit. Extends from2022 to 2025 the sunset date for the worker training tax credit andremoves a requirement for such tax credit that direct costs incurredby a business primarily engaged in manufacturing in conducting orientation, instruction, and training in the Commonwealth be related to the manufacturing activities undertaken by the business.
