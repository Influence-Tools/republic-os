# Virginia HB 450 (2022) — Parking of vehicles; electric vehicle charging spaces, signage for penalty, civil penalties.

Parking of vehicles; electric vehicle chargingspots; civil penalties. Prohibits a person from parking a vehiclenot capable of receiving an electric charge in a space reserved forcharging electric vehicles. A violation is subject to a civil penaltyof not less than $100 nor more than $250, and the vehicle may betowed or impounded.
