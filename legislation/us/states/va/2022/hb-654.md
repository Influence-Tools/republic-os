# Virginia HB 654 (2022) — GO Virginia Grants; extends sunset provision.

Certain GO Virginia Grants; matching funds; sunset.&nbsp;Extends from&nbsp;July 1, 2022, to July 1, 2023, the sunset of the provision that allows a locality to use grant funds awarded by the Tobacco Region Revitalization Commission as matching funds for GO Virginia grants.
