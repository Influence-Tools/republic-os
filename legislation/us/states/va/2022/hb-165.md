# Virginia HB 165 (2022) — Commonwealth of Virginia Higher Educational Institutions Bond Act of 2022; created.

Bonds for public institutions of higher learning;emergency. Authorizes issuance of bonds in an amount up to $100,869,000for revenue-producing capital projects at James Madison Universityand Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University. The billcontains an emergency clause. This bill is identical to SB 93.
