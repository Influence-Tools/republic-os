# Virginia HB 3 (2022) — Gold, silver, and platinum bullion, etc.; extends sunset date for the sales tax exemption.

Sales tax exemption; gold, silver, and platinum bullion. Extends the sunset date for the sales tax exemption for gold, silver, and platinum bullion and legal tender coins whose sales price exceeds $1,000 to June 30, 2027. Under current law, the exemption will expire on June 30, 2022.
