# Virginia HB 229 (2022) — Social determinants of health; VDH shall collect & analyze information including demographic data.

Department of Health; social determinants of health. Directs the Department of Health to collect and analyze information, including demographic data,&nbsp;regarding social determinants of health, defined in the bill, and their impact on health risks and health outcomes of residents of the Commonwealth, and to make information regarding social determinants of health, their impact on health, and strategies for addressing social determinants of health to improve health outcomes available to the public on its website.
