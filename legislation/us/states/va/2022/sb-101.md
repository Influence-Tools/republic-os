# Virginia SB 101 (2022) — Retail Sales and Use Tax; extends media-related exemptions.

Retail sales and use tax; media-related exemptions. Extends from 2022 to 2027 the expiration of the retail sales anduse tax exemption for printing purchased by an advertising businessfrom a printer in the Commonwealth, so long as such material is distributedoutside of the Commonwealth.
