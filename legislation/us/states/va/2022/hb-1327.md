# Virginia HB 1327 (2022) — Civil cases; reimbursement of costs.

Civil cases; reimbursement of costs. Provides that the court shall not allow a defendant convicted of a crime from which a civil matter arose to recover costs for court reporting and recording of such trial from the victim of such crime.
