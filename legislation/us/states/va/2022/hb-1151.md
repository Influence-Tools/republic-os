# Virginia HB 1151 (2022) — Virginia Motor Vehicle Warranty Enforcement Act; adds autocycles to the vehicles protected by Act.

Virginia Motor Vehicle Warranty EnforcementAct. Adds autocycles to the vehicles protected by the VirginiaMotor Vehicle Warranty Enforcement Act (the Act), commonly knownas the "lemon law." The bill applies the Act to vehicles purchasedfor business purposes by a business entity that owns or leases no more than five motor vehicles. The bill contains technical amendments.
