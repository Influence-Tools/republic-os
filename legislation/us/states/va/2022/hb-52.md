# Virginia HB 52 (2022) — Tazewell, Town of; amending charter, increases term length for members of board of zoning appeals.

Charter; Town of Tazewell; board of zoning appeals. Increases the term length for members of the board of zoning appeals for the Town of Tazewell from two to five years. This bill is identical to SB 627.
