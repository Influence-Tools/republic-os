# Virginia SB 52 (2022) — Utilities, certain; pro rata reimbursements for installation.

Pro rata reimbursements for installation ofcertain utilities. Requires localities that adopt subdivisionordinances that permit subdividers or developers to install certain utilities to provide within that ordinance the subdivider's or developer'sentitlement to pro rata reimbursement for costs of such installations.Under current law, localities are permitted to provide such entitlementbut are not required to do so.
