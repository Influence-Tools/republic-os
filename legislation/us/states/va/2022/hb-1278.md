# Virginia HB 1278 (2022) — Wildlife Resources, Board of; conveyance of certain property.

Conveyance of certain property; conditions.Provides that if the Commonwealth conveys all or part of the parcelsdescribed in the deed of gift entered into on December 29, 2004,between Rabbit Creek, LP and the Commonwealth of Virginia, Departmentof Game and Inland Fisheries to the Shenandoah Valley BattlefieldsFoundation, any deed related to the conveyance (i) shall include acondition that the property be maintained and open to public useand (ii) shall provide that if such condition is not met, the propertyshall revert to the Commonwealth.
