# Virginia SB 161 (2022) — Heat-related illness; DOE to develop guidelines on policies to inform & educate coaches & athletes.

Department of Education; heat-related illness;guidelines. Directs the Department of Education, in conjunctionwith stakeholders, to develop guidelines on policies to inform andeducate coaches and student athletes and their parents or guardiansof the nature and risk of heat-related illness, how to recognizethe signs of heat-related illness, and how to prevent heat-relatedillness to be distributed to local school divisions by August 1, 2022.
