# Virginia SB 497 (2022) — Judge; Second Judicial Circuit, resident judge in Counties of Accomack and Northampton.

Judge; Second Judicial Circuit; resident judge in Counties of Accomack and Northampton. Requires that one judge from the Second Judicial Circuit be a resident and domiciliary&nbsp;of the County of Accomack or Northampton and preside regularly in the Counties of Accomack and Northampton.
