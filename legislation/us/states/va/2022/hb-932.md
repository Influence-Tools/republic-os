# Virginia HB 932 (2022) — Workers' compensation; COVID-19, health care providers.

Workers' compensation; COVID-19; health care providers. Extends from December 31, 2021, to December 31, 2022, the date by which COVID-19 causing the death or disability of a health care provider is presumed to be an occupational disease compensable under the Virginia&nbsp;Workers' Compensation Act.
