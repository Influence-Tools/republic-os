# Virginia HB 791 (2022) — Data centers; center fixtures are taxed as part of the real property where they are located, etc.

Property tax; data centers. Provides that if data center fixtures are taxed as part of the real property where they are located, they shall be valued based on depreciated reproduction or replacement cost, rather than based on the amount of income they generate. This bill is identical to SB 513.
