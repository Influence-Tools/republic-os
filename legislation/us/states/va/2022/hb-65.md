# Virginia HB 65 (2022) — Live nutria; prohibition against sale, etc., does not apply to Dept. of Wildlife Resources, etc.

Live nutria. Provides that the existing prohibitionagainst possession, sale, or liberation of nutria does not apply toemployees of the Department of Wildlife Resources or certain federalagencies, or any persons involved in research or management activitieswith such agencies.
