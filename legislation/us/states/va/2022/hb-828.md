# Virginia HB 828 (2022) — Dairy Producer Margin Coverage Premium Assistance Program; expands eligibility for participation.

Dairy Producer Margin Coverage Premium AssistanceProgram; emergency. Expands eligibility for participation inthe Dairy Producer Margin Coverage Premium Assistance Program toinclude dairy producers who have an approved Natural Resource ConservationService nutrient management or soil health plan developed by an approvedplanner. The bill also changes the application deadline from February1 to May 15 each year and includes an emergency clause.
