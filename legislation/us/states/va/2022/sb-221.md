# Virginia SB 221 (2022) — Index of wills; Rockingham Circuit Court to establish pilot program.

Indexing of wills; pilot program in Rockingham County. Permits the clerk of the Rockingham County Circuit Court to establish a pilot project for an index of wills lodged for safekeeping, with a searchable database available to the public.
