# Virginia HB 1188 (2022) — Student Advisory Board; established.

Board of Education; appointment of nonvotingstudent advisor. Requires the Governor to appoint to the Board of Education a high school student who will enter his senior yearduring the following school year as a nonvoting student advisor toserve for a term of one year. The bill provides that no such studentis eligible to be reappointed.
