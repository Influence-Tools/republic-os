# Virginia HB 1303 (2022) — Virginia Freedom of Information Act; Virginia Parole Board member votes.

Virginia Freedom of Information Act; Virginia Parole Board member votes. Provides that individual votes of the members of the Virginia Parole Board are public records and subject to the provisions of the Virginia Freedom of Information Act. This bill is identical to SB 5.
