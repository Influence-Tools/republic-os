# Virginia SB 96 (2022) — Gaming; use of the phrase 'Virginia is for Bettors', civil penalty.

Gaming; use of the name of Virginia or the Commonwealth;civil penalty. Prohibits sports betting and casino gaming operatorsand their affiliates from using the name of Virginia or the Commonwealth in an advertisement in association with their products or services.A violation is subject to a civil penalty of up to $50,000.
