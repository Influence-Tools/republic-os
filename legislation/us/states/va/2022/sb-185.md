# Virginia SB 185 (2022) — Income tax, state; extends sunset provision for major business facility job tax credit.

Income tax; major business facility job tax credit; sunset. Extends the sunset of the major business facility job tax credit from July 1, 2022, to July 1, 2025. This bill is identical to HB 269.
