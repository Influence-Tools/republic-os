# Virginia HB 678 (2022) — Person under a disability; parties unknown.

Person under a disability; parties unknown.Includes in the definition of "person under a disability" personsmade defendants by the general description of "parties unknown" insuits involving real property. This bill is a recommendation of theBoyd-Graves Conference.
