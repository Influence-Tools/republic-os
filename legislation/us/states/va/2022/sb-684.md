# Virginia SB 684 (2022) — Certified pollution control equipment; certification by subdivisions.

Certified pollution control equipment; certification by subdivisions. Provides that for pollution control equipment to be used as part of a political subdivision's water, wastewater, stormwater, or solid waste management facilities or systems, such equipment may be certified by the political subdivision itself instead of by the state certifying authority. This bill is identical to HB 148.
