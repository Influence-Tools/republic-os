# Virginia HB 1324 (2022) — Pharmacy, Board of; pharmacy work environment requirements.

Board of Pharmacy; pharmacy work environmentrequirements; emergency. Directs the Board of Pharmacy to promulgateregulations related to work environment requirements for pharmacypersonnel that protect the health, safety, and welfare of patients.The bill requires the Board to promulgate such regulations by September1, 2022. The bill contains an emergency clause.
