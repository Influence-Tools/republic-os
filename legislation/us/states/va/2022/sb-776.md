# Virginia SB 776 (2022) — Sale or auction of surplus property; General Assembly replacement project.

Sale or auction of surplus property; General Assembly replacement project. Provides for the sale or auction of surplus property from the Pocahontas Building as part of the General Assembly replacement project and for the transfer of all net proceeds of any such sale or auction to a special nonreverting fund for the restoration and ongoing preservation of Capitol Square.
