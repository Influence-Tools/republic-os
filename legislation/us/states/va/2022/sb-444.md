# Virginia SB 444 (2022) — Telecommunications companies; conveyance of easement and appurtenances.

Department of General Services; conveyance of easement and appurtenances thereto to telecommunications companies. Adds telecommunications companies to the list of entities to which a state department, agency, or institution may grant an easement. This bill is identical to HB 1019.
