# Virginia HB 1023 (2022) — Family life education curricula, certain; optional instruction on human trafficking of children.

High school&nbsp;family life education curricula; optional instruction on human trafficking. Permits any family life education curriculum offered by a local school division&nbsp;in high school to incorporate age-appropriate elements of effective and evidence-based programs on the prevention, recognition, and awareness of human trafficking of children.
