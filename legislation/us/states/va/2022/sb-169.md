# Virginia SB 169 (2022) — Practical nurses, licensed; authority to pronounce death for a patient in hospice, etc.

Licensed practical nurses; authority to pronounce death. Extends to licensed practical nurses the authority to pronounce the death of a patient in hospice, provided that certain conditions are met. Current law provides that physicians, registered nurses, and physician assistants may pronounce death.
