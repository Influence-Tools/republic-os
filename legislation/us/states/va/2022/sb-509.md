# Virginia SB 509 (2022) — Aquaculture; right to use and occupy the ground for the terms of a lease in Chesapeake Bay waters.

Right to aquaculture. Provides that the right to use and occupy the ground for the terms of a lease in Chesapeake Bay waters includes the right to propagate shellfish by whatever legal means necessary. This bill is identical to HB 189.
