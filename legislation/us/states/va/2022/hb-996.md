# Virginia HB 996 (2022) — Land use assessment; parcels with multiple owners.

Land use assessment; parcels with multiple owners. Allows the owner of a majority interest in an undivided parcel ofreal estate that is eligible for land use assessment to file theapplication on behalf of himself and for owners of any minority interest.The bill also provides that such majority owner shall be responsiblefor paying any roll-back taxes imposed due to a reclassificationof such property.
