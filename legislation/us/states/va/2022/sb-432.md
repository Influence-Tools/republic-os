# Virginia SB 432 (2022) — Sales tax; clarifies definition of 'accommodations,' effective dates.

Sales tax; taxable accommodations. Provides that, for purposes of the retail sales and use tax on accommodations, the term "accommodations" does not include rooms or space offered by a person in the business of providing conference rooms, meeting space, or event space if the person does not also offer rooms available for overnight sleeping. The substantive provisions of the bill are given retroactive effect to September 1, 2021, but no taxpayer shall be entitled to a refund for any taxes remitted prior to July 1, 2022.
