# Virginia SB 366 (2022) — Historical horse racing; electronic gaming terminals, age requirement, penalty.

Horse racing and pari-mutuel wagering; prohibitionon persons under 21 years of age; penalty. Raises from 18 to21 years of age the minimum age for a person to wager on or conduct any wagering on the outcome of a horse race or to be admitted intoa satellite facility without being accompanied by such person's parentor legal guardian.
