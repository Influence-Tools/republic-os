# Virginia HB 703 (2022) — License plates, special; localities to pay initial issuance fee costs for development and issuance.

Special license plates; localities. Authorizes localities to pay the initial issuance fee costs for the developmentand issuance of special license plates displaying the seal, symbol,emblem, or logotype of the locality in lieu of collecting 350 paidapplications for such license plates.
