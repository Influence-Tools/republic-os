# Virginia SB 404 (2022) — Search warrants; copy of search warrant and affidavit given to at least one adult occupant.

Search warrants; copy of search warrant and affidavit given to occupants. Clarifies that if the owner of the place to be searched is not present, a copy of the search warrant and affidavit shall be given to at least one adult occupant of the place to be searched.
