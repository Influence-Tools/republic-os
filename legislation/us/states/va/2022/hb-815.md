# Virginia HB 815 (2022) — Small Business and Supplier Diversity, Department of; mentorship program.

Department of Small Business and Supplier Diversity; mentorship program. Directs the Department of Small Business and Supplier Diversity to administer a mentorship pilot program under which established businesses or industry sector experts act as mentors to start-up women-owned and minority-owned businesses.
