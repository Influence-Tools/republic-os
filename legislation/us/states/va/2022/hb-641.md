# Virginia HB 641 (2022) — Transportation network companies; authorizes collection of cash fares.

Transportation network companies; cash fares. Authorizes the collection of cash fares by a transportation networkcompany partner if the ride is arranged through a transit systemfor an eligible paratransit passenger. The bill sets certain receiptand accounting requirements.
