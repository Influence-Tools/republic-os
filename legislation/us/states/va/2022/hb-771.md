# Virginia HB 771 (2022) — Chesapeake Bay Preservation Area information; local gov't in Tidewater Va. shall publish on website.

Department of Environmental Quality; publicationof local Chesapeake Bay Preservation Area information. Requireslocalities in Tidewater Virginia to submit, and the Department of Environmental Quality to publish on its website, criteria and elementsadopted by the locality concerning Chesapeake Bay Preservation Areas.
