# Virginia SB 395 (2022) — Roanoke Higher Education Authority; board of trustees, membership.

Roanoke Higher Education Authority; board oftrustees; membership. Adds the president of Virginia State Universityor his designee to the board of trustees of the Roanoke Higher EducationAuthority and removes the presidents of Averett University and MaryBaldwin College or their designees from the board of trustees.
