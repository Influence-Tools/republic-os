# Virginia HB 808 (2022) — Support orders; income withholding order, employer fees.

Support orders; income withholding order; employerfees. Clarifies that a fee of up to a maximum of $5 for eachreply or remittance on account of a support obligor may be chargedby an employer and withheld from the obligor's income in additionto the support amount to be withheld pursuant to an income withholdingorder. Currently, such amount is described only as a $5 fee.
