# Virginia SB 1350 (2021) — Transportation funding; statewide prioritization process, resiliency.

Transportation funding; statewide prioritizationprocess; resiliency. Adds resiliency, defined in the bill, tothe list of factors to be considered during the statewide transportationfunding prioritization process commonly known as SMART SCALE. Thebill also requires that the factors of congestion mitigation, economicdevelopment, accessibility, safety, resiliency, and environmentalquality be considered relative to the anticipated life-cycle costof the project or strategy under consideration.
