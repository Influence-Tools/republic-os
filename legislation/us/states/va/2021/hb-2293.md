# Virginia HB 2293 (2021) — Local gas severance tax; extends sunset date.

Local gas severance tax; sunset date. Extendsthe sunset date from January 1, 2022, to January 1, 2024, for authorityto impose an additional local gas severance tax that is dedicatedto (i) the local Coal and Gas Road Improvement Fund; (ii) the Virginia Coalfield Economic Development Fund; and (iii) water, sewer, andnatural gas systems and lines.
