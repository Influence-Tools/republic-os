# Virginia SB 1120 (2021) — County executive form of government; local budgets.

County executive form of government; local budgets. Provides that a county that has adopted the county executive formof government (Albemarle and Prince William Counties) may carry overunspent funds from year to year for multiyear capital projects andoutstanding grants without having to reappropriate the funds.
