# Virginia HB 1776 (2021) — Teachers; temporary extension of certain licenses by Board of Education.

Board of Education; temporary extension of certain licenses. Requires the Board of Education to grant a two-year extension of the license of any&nbsp;individual licensed by the Board&nbsp;whose license expires&nbsp;on June 30,&nbsp;2021, in order to provide&nbsp;the individual&nbsp;with sufficient additional time to complete the requirements for licensure.
