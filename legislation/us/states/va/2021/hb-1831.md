# Virginia HB 1831 (2021) — Home care organizations; personal care services by a nurse through use of audio or video technology.

Home care organizations; personal care services; supervision; regulations. Directs the Board of Health to includein regulations governing home care organizations a provision forsupervision of home care attendants providing personal care servicesby a licensed nurse through use of audio-only telephone communication.
