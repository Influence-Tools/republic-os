# Virginia HB 1854 (2021) — U.S. Route 29; county manager plan of government.

Naming U.S. Route 29; county manager plan ofgovernment. Authorizes the board of any locality that has adoptedthe county manager plan of government (Arlington County) to name any section of U.S. Route 29 located within the boundaries of thelocality. The bill provides that the Department of Transportation will place and maintain appropriate signs that will be paid for bythe locality.
