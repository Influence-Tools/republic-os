# Virginia HB 2323 (2021) — Library aid; former regional library system.

Library aid; former regional library system.Provides that former regional library systems that receive stateaid notwithstanding a loss of regional library status due to a cityreversion, shall receive such aid regardless of when a former cityreverted to town status.
