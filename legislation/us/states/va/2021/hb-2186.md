# Virginia HB 2186 (2021) — Mathews County; appointment of bd. of director to the Economic Development Authority of the County.

Industrial Development and Revenue Bond Act;directors; Mathews County. Provides that the Mathews County boardof supervisors may appoint one employee of the locality onto the board of directors for the Economic Development Authority of theCounty of Mathews. Under current law, no locality's economic developmentauthority director may be an officer or employee of the relevant locality with certain exceptions for towns with a population of lessthan 3,500 and Buchanan and Frederick Counties.
