# Virginia SB 1334 (2021) — Broadband capacity; expands existing pilot program, municipal broadband authorities.

Pilot program for broadband capacity; municipalbroadband authorities. Expands the existing broadband pilotprogram to allow for the participation of municipalities and government-ownedbroadband authorities in order to provide broadband service to unservedareas of the Commonwealth.
