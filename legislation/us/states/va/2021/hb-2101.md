# Virginia HB 2101 (2021) — GO Virginia Grants; matching funds, extends sunset provision.

GO Virginia Grants; matching funds; sunset.Repeals the July 1, 2021, sunset of the provision of the Code of Virginia allowing a locality to use grant funds awarded by the TobaccoRegion Revitalization Commission as matching funds for GO Virginiagrants.
