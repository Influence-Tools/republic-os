# Virginia HB 1796 (2021) — License plates, special; removes fee for issuance to Va. National Guard retirees.

Special license plates; Virginia National Guardretirees. Removes the fee for the issuance of a special licenseplate for retired members of the Virginia National Guard. Currently,such special license plates cost the same as the prescribed costfor a typical Virginia license plate.
