# Virginia HB 2287 (2021) — Economic development authorities; size of board in Powhatan County.

Economic development authorities; size of boardin Powhatan County; quorum. Reduces from seven to five the number of members that may be appointed to the economic development authorityboard by Powhatan County and reduces the quorum requirement to threemembers.
