# Virginia SB 1221 (2021) — Loudoun & Prince William Counties & Manassas & Manassas Park Cities; operation of local health dept.

Operation of the Loudoun County local healthdepartment. Allows Loudoun County to enter into a contract withthe Department of Health for the local administration of local healthservices. Currently, the local department of health is operated asa joint venture between the state and local governments.
