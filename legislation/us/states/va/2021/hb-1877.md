# Virginia HB 1877 (2021) — Legal service plans; seller registration.

Legal service plans; seller registration. Provides that a legal services&nbsp;plans seller may offer, advertise, or execute, or cause to be executed by the subscriber, any subscription contract in the Commonwealth if the seller has submitted the seller's information and fees&nbsp;to the legal services organization for which the seller offers subscription contracts. The bill requires the legal services&nbsp;organization to submit the information&nbsp;and fees to the Commissioner of the Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services within 30 days or receiving such information and fees.
