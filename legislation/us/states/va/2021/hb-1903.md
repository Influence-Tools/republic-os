# Virginia HB 1903 (2021) — Local government; authority to reduce the speed limit in a business district or residence district.

Local government authority; reduction of speedlimits. Authorizes local governing bodies to reduce the speedlimit to less than 25 miles per hour, but not less than 15 milesper hour, in a business district or residence district.
