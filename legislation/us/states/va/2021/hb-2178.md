# Virginia HB 2178 (2021) — Commonwealth of Virginia Higher Educational Institutions Bond Act of 2021; created.

Bonds for public institutions of higher learning;emergency. Authorizes issuance of bonds in an amount up to $34,136,000for revenue-producing capital projects at Virginia Polytechnic Instituteand State University. The bill contains an emergency clause.
