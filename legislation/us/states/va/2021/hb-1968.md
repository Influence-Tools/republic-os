# Virginia HB 1968 (2021) — Absentee voting; availability on Sundays in office of general registrar or voter satellite office.

Absentee voting; early in person; availabilityon Sundays. Permits the electoral board or general registrarof a county or city to provide absentee voting in person in the officeof the general registrar or voter satellite office on Sundays.
