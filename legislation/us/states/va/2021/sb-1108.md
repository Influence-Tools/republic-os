# Virginia SB 1108 (2021) — General district courts; jurisdictional limits.

General district courts; jurisdictional limits. Increases from $25,000 to $50,000 the maximum civil jurisdictional limit of general district courts for civil actions for&nbsp;personal injury and wrongful death. The bill contains an emergency clause.
