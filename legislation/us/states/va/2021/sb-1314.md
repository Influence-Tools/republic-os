# Virginia SB 1314 (2021) — Education and Labor Market Alignment, Office of; established.

Virginia Economic Development Partnership Authority;Office of Education and Labor Market Alignment established; workforceand higher education alignment. Directs the Virginia Economic Development Partnership Authority to establish an Office of Educationand Labor Market Alignment (the Office) to coordinate data analysison workforce and higher education alignment and translate data topartners. The Office shall provide a unified, consistent source ofinformation or analysis for policy development and implementationrelated to talent development and shall partner with the State Councilof Higher Education for Virginia, institutions of higher education,the Virginia Employment Commission, GO Virginia, and other relevantentities to offer resources and expertise related to education andlabor market alignment.
