# Virginia SB 1321 (2021) — Confirmatory adoption; expands the stepparent adoption provisions.

Confirmatory adoption. Expands the stepparentadoption provisions to allow a person who is not the child's stepparentbut has a legitimate interest in the child to file a joint petitionfor adoption with the child's birth parent or parent by adoption.
