# Virginia SB 1262 (2021) — Restricted permit; prepayment of fines and costs.

Restricted permit; prepayment of fines and costs.Provides that any person who is otherwise eligible to receive a restricted permit to operate a motor vehicle shall not be required to pay infull his fines and costs before being issued such restricted permit.
