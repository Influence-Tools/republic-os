# Virginia HB 1901 (2021) — Online Virginia Driver's Manual course; training school.

Online Virginia Driver's Manual course; trainingschool. Removes the requirement that a driver training schoolproviding the Virginia Driver's Manual course online be a computer-based driver education provider, as defined in the Code of Virginia.
