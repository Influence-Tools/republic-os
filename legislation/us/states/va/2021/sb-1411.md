# Virginia SB 1411 (2021) — Peanuts; extends sunset date of excise tax on all peanuts grown in Virginia.

Excise tax on peanuts. Extends from July1, 2021, to July 1, 2026, the sunset date of the excise tax on allpeanuts grown in Virginia and reduces from $0.30 per 100 pounds to$0.25 per 100 pounds the excise tax rate. The proceeds from thistax are used for promoting the sales and use of Virginia peanuts.
