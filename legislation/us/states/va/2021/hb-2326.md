# Virginia HB 2326 (2021) — Child-care services; regulation in localities.

Regulation of child-care services in localities.Expands to include all cities the authority related to the regulationof child-care services that is currently available to certain NorthernVirginia localities. This will have the effect of granting all citiesauthority by ordinance to regulate child-care facilities that provideregular care to one or more children not related by blood or marriage.
