# Virginia HB 2029 (2021) — Fire training activities; prohibition on the use of certain oriented strand board.

Department of Fire Programs; prohibition onthe use of certain oriented strand board in fire service trainingactivities. Prohibits the use by any person, local government,or agency of the Commonwealth of Class A fuel materials that containoriented strand board, defined in the bill, during fire service training activities.
