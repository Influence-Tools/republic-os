# Virginia HB 2202 (2021) — Elevator mechanic or accessibility mechanic, certain; exemption from certification.

Board for Contractors; exemption from certificationas an elevator mechanic or accessibility mechanic. Provides thatan individual is not required to be certified as an elevator mechanicor accessibility mechanic when working under the direct and immediatesupervision of an elevator mechanic or certified accessibility mechanicwho is certified in the specialty for which work is being performed.
