# Virginia HB 1928 (2021) — Historic resources; acquisition and lease of land.

Historic resources; acquisition and lease of land. Exempts additional land acquisition activities of the Department of Historic Resources (the Department) and the Board of Historic Resources (the Board) from review by the Department of General Services and authorizes the Department and Board to undertake additional leasing and property acquisition activities related to battlefield properties, designated landmarks, and other properties of historic significance.
