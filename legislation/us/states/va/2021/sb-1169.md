# Virginia SB 1169 (2021) — Student driver safety; driver education program shall include dangers of speeding.

High school student parking passes; valid driver'slicense or driver privilege card required. Requires each publichigh school to require any student who applies to obtain a pass topark a vehicle on school property to provide evidence that the studentpossesses a valid driver's license or driver privilege card.
