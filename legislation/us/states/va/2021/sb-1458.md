# Virginia SB 1458 (2021) — Identity Management Standards Advisory Council; transfers management of Council.

Secretary of Commerce and Trade; Identity Management Standards Advisory Council. Transfers the managementof the Identity Management Standards Advisory Council from the Secretaryof Administration to the Secretary of Commerce and Trade. The billalso adds to the membership of the Advisory Council a representativefrom the Commonwealth of Virginia Innovation Partnership Authorityand a representative of Virginia consumers.
