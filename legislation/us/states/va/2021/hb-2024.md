# Virginia HB 2024 (2021) — Bob White Covered Bridge; Dept. of Transportation to work w/ Patrick County in constructing replica.

Department of Transportation; replica of the Bob White Covered Bridge. Requires the Department of Transportation to work with the governing body of Patrick County and community groups interested in constructing a replica of the Bob White Covered Bridge to approve a construction plan for a replica of the Bob White Covered Bridge for tourism purposes open for pedestrian, non-motor vehicle traffic. The bill requires the Department to ensure that the replica bridge is sufficiently stable and does not create a hazard but exempts such replica bridge from all Code of Virginia provisions and Department of Transportation regulations regarding bridge standards and design.
