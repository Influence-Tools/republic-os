# Virginia HB 1642 (2019) — Restrictive housing; data collection and reporting, Department of Corrections to submit report.

Requires the Department of Corrections to report to the General Assembly and the Governor on or before October 1 of each year certain population statistics of persons incarcerated in state correctional institutions, including certain statistics regarding offenders placed in and released from restrictive housing and Shared Allied Management Units.
