# Virginia HB 2311 (2019) — Historical African American cemeteries; adds a cemetery in City of Suffolk.

Adds Oak Lawn Cemetery in Suffolk, with 468 eligible graves, to the list of cemeteries for which qualified organizations may receive funds from the Department of Historic Resources for the care of historical African American cemeteries and graves.
