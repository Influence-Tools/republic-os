# Virginia HB 1932 (2019) — Mineral mining; increases permit fees.

Provides a schedule for increases to fees a mining operator must pay when applying for a mining operation permit renewal, raising the current fee of $16 per disturbed acre by $2 each year from July 1, 2019, through July 1, 2022.
