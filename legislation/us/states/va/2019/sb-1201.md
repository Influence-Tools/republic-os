# Virginia SB 1201 (2019) — Transfer of venue; delinquency, adjudication.

. Provides that a transfer of venue in delinquency proceedings, which under current law may occur only after adjudication, may occur when such adjudication consists of a finding of facts sufficient to justify a finding of delinquency.
