# Virginia HB 2208 (2019) — Adoption by relative; expands the applicability of procedures.

Expands the applicability of adoption procedures for a child's close relatives to all of the child's adult relatives, including stepparents, stepbrothers or stepsisters, and all other adult relatives of the child by marriage or adoption.
