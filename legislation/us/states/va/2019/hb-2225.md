# Virginia HB 2225 (2019) — Financial exploitation of adults; reporting by financial institution staff.

Provides that any financial institution staff who suspects that an adult has been financially exploited may provide supporting information and records to the local department of social services or the adult protective services hotline, in addition to reporting such suspected exploitation to such local department or such hotline provided for under current law.
