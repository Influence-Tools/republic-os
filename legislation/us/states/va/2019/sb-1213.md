# Virginia SB 1213 (2019) — School and Campus Safety, Virginia Center for; threat assessment, case management tool.

Requires the Virginia Center for School and Campus Safety to develop a case management tool for use by public elementary and secondary school threat assessment teams and requires such threat assessment teams to use such tool to collect and report to the Center quantitative data on its activities.
