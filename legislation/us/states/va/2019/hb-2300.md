# Virginia HB 2300 (2019) — Motor vehicles, certain; operation by sex offenders.

Prohibits any person who is required to register on the Sex Offender and Crimes Against Minors Registry or the federal National Sex Offender Public Website from operating a taxicab for the transportation of passengers for hire.
