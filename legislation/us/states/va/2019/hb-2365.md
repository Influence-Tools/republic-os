# Virginia HB 2365 (2019) — Land preservation; special assessment, optional limit on annual increase in assessed value.

Authorizes localities that require use value assessment and taxation to provide by ordinance that the annual increase in the assessed value of eligible property shall not exceed a specified dollar amount per acre.
