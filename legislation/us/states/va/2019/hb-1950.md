# Virginia HB 1950 (2019) — Retail Sales and Use Tax; exemption, nonprofits, limited liability companies.

Provides that, for purposes of the retail sales and use tax exemption for nonprofit organizations, the exemption is available to a single member limited liability company whose sole member is a nonprofit organization.
