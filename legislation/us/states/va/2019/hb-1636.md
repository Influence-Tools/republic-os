# Virginia HB 1636 (2019) — Unmanned aircraft system; trespass, military airfield or military helicopter landing zone, penalty.

Provides that any person who knowingly and intentionally causes an unmanned aircraft system to take off or land in violation of current Federal Aviation Administration Special Security Instructions or UAS Security Sensitive Airspace Restrictions is guilty of a Class 1 misdemeanor.
