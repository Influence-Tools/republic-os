# Virginia HB 2405 (2019) — Real estate; delinquent taxes or liens, appointment of special commissioner, City of Martinsville.

Adds Martinsville to the list of cities with different requirements for the appointment of a special commissioner to execute the necessary deed to convey property with delinquent taxes or liens to the locality in lieu of a sale at public auction.
