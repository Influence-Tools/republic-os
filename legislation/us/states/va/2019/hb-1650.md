# Virginia HB 1650 (2019) — Lottery; disclosure of identity of winners.

Prohibits the Virginia Lottery from disclosing information about individual winners whose prize exceeds $10 million, and exempts such information from disclosure under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA), unless the winner consents to disclosure.
