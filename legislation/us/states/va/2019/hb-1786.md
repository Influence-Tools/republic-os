# Virginia HB 1786 (2019) — Vehicles on sidewalks; use of power-driven mobility devices.

Clarifies that any public entity may allow "other power-driven mobility devices," as defined in the bill, to be ridden or driven on sidewalks in accordance with the Americans with Disabilities Act and other state and federal laws.
