# Virginia HB 2305 (2019) — Localities; descriptions are replaced with locality names.

Sets out sections in Title 15.2 that are currently carried by reference only. Locality descriptions are replaced with locality names, and various technical amendments are made. This bill is a recommendation of the Virginia Code Commission.
