# Virginia HB 2425 (2019) — Health, Virginia Department of; monitoring of health care-associated infections.

Requires health care facilities that are required to report information about health care-associated infections (HAIs) to the National Healthcare Safety Network (NHSN) to release such data to the Board of Health through the NHSN.
