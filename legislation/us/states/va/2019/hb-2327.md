# Virginia HB 2327 (2019) — DPOR; unlicensed practice, cease and desist notices.

Permits the Director of the Department of Professional and Occupational Regulation, or his designee, to issue a notice to any person unlawfully engaging in unlicensed practice of an occupation to cease and desist such activity.
