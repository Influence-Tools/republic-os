# Virginia HB 2572 (2019) — Berryville, Town of; amending charter, reorganizes charter.

Reorganizes the charter of the Town of Berryville. Specific amendments include (i) an update to the boundary description, (ii) replacing the position of recorder with vice mayor, (iii) specifying certain town council appointments, and (iv) removing or updating obsolete provisions.
