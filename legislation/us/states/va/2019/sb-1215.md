# Virginia SB 1215 (2019) — School safety procedures; emergency situations, annual training.

Requires each school board to develop training on safety procedures in the event of an emergency situation on school property. The bill requires such training to be delivered to each student and employee in each school at least once each school year. This bill is identical to
