# Virginia HB 1952 (2019) — Patient care teams; podiatrists and physician assistants.

Establishes the role of "patient care team podiatrist" as a provider of management and leadership to physician assistants in the care of patients as part of a patient care team. The bill modifies the supervision requirements for physician assistants by establishing a patient care team model.
