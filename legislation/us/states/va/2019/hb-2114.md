# Virginia HB 2114 (2019) — License plates, special; members of the International Association of Fire Fighters.

Changes the special license plates for members of the International Association of Fire Fighters from nonrevenue sharing to revenue sharing and provides for the moneys to support the efforts of various charitable organizations supported by the firefighters throughout the Commonwealth.
