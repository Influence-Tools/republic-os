# Virginia HB 2621 (2019) — Rezoning and site plan approval; decommissioning solar energy equipment, etc., bonding provisions.

Requires a locality, as part of the local legislative approval process or as a condition of approval of a site plan, to require an owner, lessee, or developer of real property to enter into a written agreement to decommission solar energy equipment, facilities, or devices upon certain terms and conditions, including right of entry by the locality and financial assurance.
