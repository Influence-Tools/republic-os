# Virginia HB 1817 (2019) — Prostitution; promoting travel, penalty.

Makes it a Class 1 misdemeanor for any travel agent to knowingly promote travel services for the purposes of prostitution or certain offenses involving minors that require registration on the Sex Offender and Crimes Against Minors Registry.
