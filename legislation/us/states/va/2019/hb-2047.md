# Virginia HB 2047 (2019) — Oyster planting grounds; municipal dredging projects.

Extends the duration of an exception, for municipal dredging, to the right of a person to lease oyster-planting grounds, and establishes a procedure for determining compensation to such a leaseholder when his grounds are disturbed by such a dredging project.
