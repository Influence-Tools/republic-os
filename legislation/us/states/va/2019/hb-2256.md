# Virginia HB 2256 (2019) — Animals; remedies containing Cannabis plant, exemption.

Exempts animal remedies that contain any part of the Cannabis plant from certain provisions governing animal remedies, including the requirement that the manufacturer or distributor of an animal remedy obtain a registration for such remedy from the Commissioner of Agriculture and Consumer Services.
