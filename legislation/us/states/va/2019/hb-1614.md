# Virginia HB 1614 (2019) — Stormwater Management Fund, local; locality by ordinance authorized to create.

Authorizes a locality by ordinance to create a local Stormwater Management Fund consisting of appropriated local moneys for the purpose of granting funds to an owner of private property or a common interest community for stormwater management and erosion prevention on previously developed lands.
