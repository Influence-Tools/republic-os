# Virginia HB 2527 (2019) — Tolling; prohibited in Northern Virginia.

Prohibits the imposition and collection of tolls on any primary highway that is wholly located in Planning District 8 and that was previously classified as a secondary highway and is between 30 and 35 miles in length without prior approval by the General Assembly.
