# Virginia HB 1742 (2019) — Marsh Criminal-Traffic Division/Manchester General District Court, etc.; concurrent jurisdiction.

Clarifies that the Marsh Criminal-Traffic Division at Manchester General District Court has concurrent jurisdiction with the John Marshall Criminal-Traffic Division at the Richmond General District Court over all matters arising in the City of Richmond.
