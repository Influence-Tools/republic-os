# Virginia HB 1898 (2019) — Virginia Residential Landlord & Tenant Act; tenant's right of redemption.

Extends the amount of time that a tenant may have an unlawful detainer dismissed to two days before a writ of eviction is delivered to be executed if the tenant pays all amounts claimed on the summons in unlawful detainer to the landlord, the landlord's attorney, or the court.
