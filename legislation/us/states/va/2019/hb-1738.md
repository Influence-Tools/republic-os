# Virginia HB 1738 (2019) — School buildings; plans to be reviewed by a professional trained & experienced in crime prevention.

Requires the plans and specifications for new or remodeled public school building construction to be reviewed by an individual or entity with professional expertise in crime prevention through environmental design. All comments by such reviewer shall be submitted to the Superintendent of Public Instruction along with the final plans and specifications.
