# Virginia HB 2514 (2019) — Motor vehicle safety inspections; increases maximum charge from $16 to $20, $.70 transmitted to VSP.

Increases the maximum charge for a state safety inspection for a motor vehicle from $16 to $20 and increases the amount transmitted to the Department of State Police from $0.50 to $0.70.
