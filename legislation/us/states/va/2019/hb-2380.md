# Virginia HB 2380 (2019) — Higher educational institutions, public; online course catalogue, no-cost/low-cost course materials.

Requires the registrar or another appropriate employee of each public institution of higher education to identify conspicuously in the online course catalogue or registration system, as soon as practicable after the necessary information becomes available, each course for which the instructor exclusively uses no-cost course materials or low-cost course materials.
