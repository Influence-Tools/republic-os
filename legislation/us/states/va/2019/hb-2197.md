# Virginia HB 2197 (2019) — Summary judgment; limited use of discovery depositions and affidavits.

Allows for the limited use of discovery depositions and affidavits in support of or in opposition to a motion for summary judgment, provided that the only parties to the action are business entities and the amount at issue is $50,000 or more. This bill is identical to
