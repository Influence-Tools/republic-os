# Virginia SB 5017 (2020specialI) — Correctional facility, local; clarifies definition.

Local correctional facility; definition. Provides that, for purposes of minimum standards for correctional facilities and enforcement of those standards,&nbsp;the term "local correctional facility" shall also include any facility owned, maintained, or operated by any political subdivision or combination of political subdivisions of the Commonwealth that is used for the detention or incarceration of people pursuant to a contract or third-party contract with the federal government or any agency or contractor thereof.
