# Virginia SB 5106 (2020specialI) — Local land use approvals; extension of approvals to address the COVID-19 pandemic.

Local land use approvals; extension of approvals to address the COVID-19 pandemic. Extends until at least July 1, 2022, the sunset date for various local land use approvals that were valid and outstanding as of July 1, 2020. This bill incorporates SB 5044.
