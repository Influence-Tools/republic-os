# Virginia SB 1316 (2025) — Renewable energy portfolio standard eligible sources; geothermal electric generating resources.

Renewable energy portfolio standard eligible sources; geothermal electric generating resources. Provides that geothermal electric generating resources, as defined in the bill, located in the Commonwealth or physically located within the PJM region are eligible for compliance with renewable energy portfolio standard requirements.
