# Virginia SB 1318 (2025) — EMS agencies; Bd. of Pharmacy shall report on progress in response to changes in federal law.

Board of Pharmacy; emergency medical services providers; work group. Directs the Board of Pharmacy to convene a work group to advise the Board on issues related to emergency medical services providers. The bill requires the Board to staff the work group and directs the work group to meet at least quarterly. The bill has an expiration date of June 30, 2027.
