# Virginia SB 736 (2020) — Court fines and costs; community service work in lieu of payment during imprisonment.

Community service work in lieu of payment of fines and costs. Provides that a court may permit an inmate to earn credits against any fines and court costs imposed against him by performing community service. Under current law, credits may be earned only before or after imprisonment. This bill is identical to HB 277.
