# Virginia HB 1581 (2020) — Delinquent real property taxes; correction of tax records.

Tax delinquent real property; correction oftax records. Transfers from the local clerk of court to the localtreasurer the duties of maintaining records of delinquent real property taxes and sales of such property and of correcting records relating to such property.
