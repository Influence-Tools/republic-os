# Virginia SB 745 (2020) — Income tax, state; exclusion, student loan forgiveness, disabled veterans.

Income tax exclusion; student loan forgiveness; disabled veterans. Excludes from Virginia adjusted gross income any income received by a totally and permanently disabled veteran from student loan debt cancellation or discharge for taxable years 2020 through 2025.
