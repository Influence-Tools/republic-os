# Virginia SB 255 (2020) — Transient occupancy tax; authorizes Prince George County to impose.

Additional transient occupancy tax in Prince George County. Authorizes Prince George County to impose an additional transient occupancy tax at a rate of up to&nbsp;two percent, for a total maximum rate of seven percent. The revenue from the additional tax shall be used solely for tourism purposes.
