# Virginia HB 654 (2020) — Clean energy projects; authorizes DMME to sponsor a statewide financing program.

Financing clean energy projects. Authorizesthe Department of Mines, Minerals and Energy to sponsor a statewideclean energy financing program. The Department shall engage a privateentity through a competitive selection process to develop and administerthe program.
