# Virginia SB 968 (2020) — Relocated billboard signs; maintenance and repair.

Maintenance and repair of relocated billboardsigns. Provides that existing provisions related to the maintenanceand repair of nonconforming billboard signs do not apply to relocatedbillboard signs.
