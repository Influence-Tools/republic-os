# Virginia SB 430 (2020) — Minor's child-care records; access by parent.

Access to minor's child-care records by parent. Provides that, absent a court order, a minor's records from a child day center or family day home&nbsp;shall not be withheld from a parent of such minor, regardless of whether the parent has custody of such child.
