# Virginia HB 368 (2020) — Public schools; enrollment, certain children placed in foster care.

Public schools; enrollment; certain childrenplaced in foster care. Specifies, for the purpose of severalprovisions of law relating to the public school enrollment of childrenplaced in foster care, that a child or student placed in foster careincludes a pupil who was in foster care when he reached age 18 butsuch pupil has not yet reached age 22.
