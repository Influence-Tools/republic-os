# Virginia SB 940 (2020) — Circuit court clerk's fee; lodging, etc., of wills.

Circuit court clerk's fee; lodging of wills. Increases from $2 to $5 the fee that the circuit court clerk is required to charge for lodging, indexing, and preserving a will. This bill is identical to HB 305.
