# Virginia HB 1143 (2020) — Local school boards; support services positions, licensed behavior analysts.

Local school boards; support services positions; licensed behavior analysts and licensed assistant behavior analysts. Includes licensed behavior analysts and licensed assistant behavior analysts&nbsp;as support services positions in a local school division for the purposes of Title 22.1 (Education).
