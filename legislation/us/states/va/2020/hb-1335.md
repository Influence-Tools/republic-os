# Virginia HB 1335 (2020) — SCHEV; Director of Council to appoint student advisory committee.

State Council of Higher Education for Virginia; student advisory committee; Director of the Council. Changes the appointing authority for the State Council of Higher Education for Virginia's student advisory committee from the Council to the Director of the Council. The bill also directs the student advisory committee to report to the Director rather than to the Council.
