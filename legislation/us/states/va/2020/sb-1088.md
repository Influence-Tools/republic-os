# Virginia SB 1088 (2020) — Nongovernmental emergency medical services agencies; dissolution of agency.

Emergency medical services agency; dissolution; return of property purchased with public funds. Provides that upon dissolution of an emergency medical services agency, any property that was in the possession of such emergency medical services agency and that was purchased using public funds shall be offered to a city or county that was served by the emergency medical services agency to be used for life-saving purposes.
