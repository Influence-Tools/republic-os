# Virginia HB 1141 (2020) — Health benefit plans; renewal of plans, special exception.

Health benefit plans; special exception. Repeals a&nbsp;provision of the Code of Virginia authorizing health carriers to sell, issue, or offer for sale any health benefit plan that would otherwise not be permitted to be sold, issued, or offered for sale due to conflict with the requirements of the federal Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (PPACA), to the extent that the(a) the appropriate federal authority has suspended enforcement of provisions of the PPACA&nbsp;or (b) the requirements of the PPACA are amended by any federal law.
