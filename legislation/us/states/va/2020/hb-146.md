# Virginia HB 146 (2020) — Sample ballots; color of paper of unofficial sample ballots.

Sample ballots; color of paper of unofficialsample ballots. Removes the restriction on unofficial sampleballots being printed on yellow paper. With this bill, unofficialsample ballots are prohibited only from being printed on white paper.The bill contains technical amendments for clarity and readability.
