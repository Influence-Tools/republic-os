# Virginia HB 88 (2020) — Campaign finance; filing schedule for persons with multiple campaign committees.

Elections; campaign finance; filing schedulefor persons with multiple campaign committees. Requires any personwho is named as the candidate on the statement of organization formore than one campaign committee to file reports for all such committeeson the same schedule as any such committee.
