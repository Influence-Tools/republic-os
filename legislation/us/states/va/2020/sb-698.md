# Virginia SB 698 (2020) — Alcoholic beverage control; distiller licenses, monthly revenue transfers.

Alcoholic beverage control; distiller licenses;monthly revenue transfers. Allows the Board of Directors of theVirginia Alcoholic Beverage Control Authority to include in any agreementit enters into with a licensed distiller for the sale of alcoholicbeverages provisions that allow monthly revenue transfers from thedistiller to the Board to be submitted electronically and to be limitedto the amount due to the Board in applicable taxes and markups. Underlaw that is set to become effective on July 1, 2020, the Board isrequired to allow such electronic submission and to limit such monthlyrevenue transfers to the amount due to the Board in applicable taxes and markups. The bill also removes the provision, which would otherwisebecome effective on July 1, 2020, that requires the Board to waiveany case fee charged to a licensed distiller for moving spirits whensuch spirits are moved by employees of the distiller.
