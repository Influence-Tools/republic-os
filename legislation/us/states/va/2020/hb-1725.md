# Virginia HB 1725 (2020) — Administrative assistants; employees serving circuit court judges.

Judicial assistants. Provides that a judicialassistant serving a circuit court judge and whose position is fundedin whole or in part by a county or city shall serve at the pleasureand under the sole management and supervision of the presiding judgeof the circuit court.
