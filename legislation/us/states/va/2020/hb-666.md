# Virginia HB 666 (2020) — Vital records; expands the definition of 'dead body.'

Vital records; definitions.Expands the definitionof "dead body," for the purposes of the administration of vital recordsand the prohibition against disposal or concealment, beyond the recently deceased to include human bodies or body parts in any state of decomposition.
