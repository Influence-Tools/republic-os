# Virginia HB 653 (2020) — Condemned growing beds; electronic maps.

Condemned growing beds; electronic maps.Authorizes the Commissioner of Marine Resources to provide publicdesignation of condemned crustacea, finfish, or shellfish growingareas through the use of downloadable maps or digital interactiveonline maps. Current law requires that public notice be accomplishedonly with physical signs or markers.
