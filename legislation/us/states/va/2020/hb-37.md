# Virginia HB 37 (2020) — Alcoholic beverage control; stills or distilling apparatuses, permit requirement.

Alcoholic beverage control; stills or distilling apparatuses; permit requirement. Narrows the requirement that a permit be obtained from the Board of Directors of the Alcoholic Beverage Control Authority in order to keep, store, or possess any still or distilling apparatus to include only instances in which a still or distilling apparatus is kept, stored, or possessed for the&nbsp;purpose of distilling alcohol.
