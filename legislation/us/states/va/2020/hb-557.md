# Virginia HB 557 (2020) — Carnal knowledge of pretrial or posttrial offender; bail bondsmen, increases penalty.

Carnal knowledge of pretrial or posttrial offender;bail bondsmen; penalty. Increases to a Class 6 felony from aClass 1 misdemeanor the penalty for an owner or employee of a bailbond company that posted bond for a person to carnally know suchperson if the owner or employee has the authority to revoke the person'sbond.
