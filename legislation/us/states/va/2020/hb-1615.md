# Virginia HB 1615 (2020) — Recordation tax; supplemental writings.

Recordation tax; supplemental writings. Providesthat recordation of a supplemental writing for certain deeds of trustand mortgages shall not be taxable, even if the supplemental writingrecites an increase in the principal amount of bonds or obligations.The deeds of trust or mortgages that qualify for this exemption arethose that convey property within the Commonwealth to secure obligationsoutside the Commonwealth and do not state an increase in the amountof the principal obligation secured that was apportioned to propertywithin the Commonwealth.
