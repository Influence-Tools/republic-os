# Virginia HB 108 (2020) — Legal holidays; Election Day, removes Lee-Jackson Day as state holiday.

Legal holidays; Lee-Jackson Day; Election Day. Designates Election Day, the Tuesday after the first Monday in November, as a state holiday and removes Lee-Jackson Day as a state holiday. This bill is identical to SB 601.
