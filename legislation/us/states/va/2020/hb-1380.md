# Virginia HB 1380 (2020) — Uniform Directed Trust Act; codifies Act.

Uniform Directed Trust Act. Codifies theUniform Directed Trust Act, which expressly validates terms of a trustthat provide for a trust director, a term that is defined in theAct, and prescribes a set of rules for directed trusts, includingallocation of fiduciary duties.
