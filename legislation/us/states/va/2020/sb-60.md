# Virginia SB 60 (2020) — Capital outlay bill; timing of required submission.

Governor's required submission of capital outlayplan bills. Modifies the requirements of the Governor's submissionof capital outlay plan bills to require that the Governor ensurethat prefiled capital outlay plan bills are submitted to the Chairmenof the House Appropriations and Senate Finance Committees by theprefiling deadline established by a procedural resolution of theGeneral Assembly.
