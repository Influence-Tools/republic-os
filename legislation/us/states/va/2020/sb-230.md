# Virginia SB 230 (2020) — Grantor's tax and regional transportation improvement fee; payment of tax or fee.

Grantor's tax and regional transportation improvementfee. Provides that, for purposes of the grantor's tax and regionaltransportation improvement fee (the regional WMATA capital fee),the grantor and grantee may arrange that the grantee pay all or aportion of the tax or fee. Current law requires the grantor to paythe tax or fee.
