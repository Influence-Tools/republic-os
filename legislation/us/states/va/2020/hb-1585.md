# Virginia HB 1585 (2020) — Altavista, Town of; discounted water and sewer fees.

Discounted water and sewer fees; Town of Altavista. Adds the Town of Altavista to those localities that may develop criteriafor providing discounted water and sewer fees and charges for low-income,elderly, or disabled customers.
