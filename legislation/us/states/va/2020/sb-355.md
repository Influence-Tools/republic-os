# Virginia SB 355 (2020) — Assisted living facilities; regulations regarding audio-visual recording of residents, report.

Assisted living facilities; audio-visual recordingof residents. Directs the Board of Social Services (the Board)to promulgate regulations by July 1, 2021, governing audio-visual recording of residents in assisted living facilities and requiresthe Department of Social Services to convene a workgroup of stakeholdersto make recommendations on such regulations. The workgroup shall report its recommendations to the Board and the General Assembly byDecember 1, 2020.
