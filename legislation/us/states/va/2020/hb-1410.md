# Virginia HB 1410 (2020) — Food stamps; Restaurant Meals Program.

Food stamps; Restaurant Meals Program. Directsthe Department of Social Services to participate in the RestaurantMeals Program (RMP) of the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP). The bill requires the Department to develop and implementa plan to begin such participation no later than January 1, 2021.
