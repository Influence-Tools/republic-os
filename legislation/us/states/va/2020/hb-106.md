# Virginia HB 106 (2020) — Numbering on buildings; civil penalty.

Numbering on buildings; civil penalty. Providesthat an ordinance that requires buildings to have visible numberingmay include provisions for a civil penalty not to exceed $100 fora violation that has not been corrected within 15 days of noticeof such violation. Civil penalties assessed under this provisionshall be paid into the treasury of the locality where the violationoccurred.
