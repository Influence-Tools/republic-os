# Virginia SB 385 (2020) — Professional engineers; regulations, scope of exception.

Engineers; exemption from regulation. Excludes engineering services rendered in connection with gas pipeline facilitiesfrom the exemption from regulation by the Board for Architects, Professional Engineers, Land Surveyors, Certified Interior Designers and Landscape Architects. Currently, this exemption exists for the practice ofengineering solely as (i) an employee of a corporation engaged ininterstate commerce or (ii) an employee of a public service corporation,by rendering such corporation engineering service in connection withits facilities that are subject to regulation by the State CorporationCommission.
