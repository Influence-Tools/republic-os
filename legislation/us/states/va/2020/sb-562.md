# Virginia SB 562 (2020) — Rebuilt vehicles; issuance of title.

Rebuilt vehicles; issuance of title. Allowsa title to be issued for a rebuilt vehicle without an inspectionby the Department of Motor Vehicles if (i) the rebuilder has been licensed as an auto recycler, salvage pool, or vehicle removal operatorin good standing for at least 10 years, (ii) the rebuilt vehicleis at least 10 years old but is not an antique motor vehicle, and(iii) the resale value of the rebuilt vehicle is less than $10,000.
