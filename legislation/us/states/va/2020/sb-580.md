# Virginia SB 580 (2020) — Commonwealth of Virginia Higher Educational Institutions Bond Act of 2020; created.

Bonds for institutions of higher learning; emergency. Authorizes issuance of bonds in an amount up to $279,470,000 for revenue-producing capital projects at institutions of higher learning. The bill contains an emergency clause and is identical to HB 1246.
