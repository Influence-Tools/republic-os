# Virginia SB 557 (2020) — VDOT; electronic speed indicator signs on U.S. Route 17.

Department of Transportation; electronic speed indicator signs; U.S. Route 17. Requires the Commissioner of Highways to place at least six permanent electronic speed indicator signs on U.S. Route 17 near particular intersections in Fauquier County. This bill is identical to HB 941.
