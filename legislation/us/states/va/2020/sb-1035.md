# Virginia SB 1035 (2020) — Motor vehicle dealers; redefines 'relevant market area' for purposes of motorcycle franchises.

Motor vehicle dealers; motorcycle franchises.Redefines "relevant market area" for purposes of motorcycle franchises.The bill also removes the burden of proof from a motor vehicle dealerin a hearing related to a motorcycle franchise license.
