# Virginia SB 167 (2020) — Teachers, public school; grounds for dismissal.

Dismissal of teachers; grounds; incompetency.Removes receipt of one or more unsatisfactory performance evaluationsfrom the list of factors that "incompetency" may be construed toinclude for the purpose of establishing grounds for the dismissalof public school teachers.
