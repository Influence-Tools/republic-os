# Virginia SB 828 (2020) — Carbon-free energy and clean energy; definition.

Carbon-free energy and clean energy. Provides that "carbon-free energy" or "clean energy," as used in the Code of Virginia,&nbsp;includes electric energy generated from a source that does not emit carbon dioxide into the atmosphere during the process of generating the electric energy, including electric energy generated by the conversion of sunlight, wind, falling water, wave motion, tides, or&nbsp;geothermal or nuclear energy.
