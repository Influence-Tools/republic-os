# Virginia HB 1421 (2020) — Pollbooks; requirement for printed copies.

Pollbooks; requirement for printed copies ofpollbooks. Requires general registrars to produce and distributeprinted copies of pollbooks to each precinct for any primary andgeneral election. The bill also contains technical adjustments toreflect the current administrative responsibilities of the Departmentof Elections.
