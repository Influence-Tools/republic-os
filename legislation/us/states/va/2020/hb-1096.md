# Virginia HB 1096 (2020) — Dead animals; makes it a Class 1 misdemeanor for any person to place within or on any church.

Injuries to churches or church property; mutilateddead animals. Makes it a Class 1 misdemeanor for any person towillfully or maliciously place a mutilated dead animal within anychurch or on church property.
