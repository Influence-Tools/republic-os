# Virginia SB 434 (2020) — Child support; assignment of tax credits.

Child support; assignment of tax credits. Providesthat the court may assign a party in a child support proceeding theright to claim any credits resulting from the income tax dependencyexemption for any child or children of the parties for federal andstate income tax purposes.
