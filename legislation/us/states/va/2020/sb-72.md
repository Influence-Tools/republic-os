# Virginia SB 72 (2020) — Public defender offices; Cities of Manassas and Manassas Park and County of Prince William.

Public defender offices; Cities of Manassas and Manassas Park and County of Prince William. Establishes a public defender office for the Cities of Manassas and Manassas Park and the County of Prince William. This bill is identical to HB 366.
