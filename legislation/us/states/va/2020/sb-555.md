# Virginia SB 555 (2020) — Voting; restrictions on the basis of race.

Voting restrictions on the basis of race. Repeals several Acts of Assembly that implemented and enforced a state poll tax and provided for separate registration records on the basis of race. This bill is identical to HB 1086.
