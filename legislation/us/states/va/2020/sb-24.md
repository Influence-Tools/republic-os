# Virginia SB 24 (2020) — Agritourism activities; adds horseback riding to definition.

Agritourism activities; horseback riding andstabling. Adds horseback riding or stabling to the definitionof "agritourism activity." Agritourism activities have limited liabilityfor the inherent risks of the activity under certain conditions.
