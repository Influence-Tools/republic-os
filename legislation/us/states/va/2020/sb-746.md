# Virginia SB 746 (2020) — Comprehensive plan; adoption or disapproval by governing body.

Comprehensive plan. Extends the time by which a governing body is required to approve or disapprove a locality-initiated comprehensive plan amendment for more than 25 parcels from 90 to 150 days. This bill is identical to HB 726.
