# Virginia HB 211 (2020) — License plates, special; issuance for persons awarded the United States Air Medal, etc.

Special license plate; Air Medal. Creates a special license plate for persons awarded the Air Medal or for unremarried surviving spouses of such persons. An annual $10 fee, in addition to the prescribed cost of state license plates, will be charged for each set of such plates issued.
