# Virginia SB 257 (2020) — Brodnax, Town of; amending charter, reduces number of town councilmen.

Charter; Town of Brodnax; town council; membership; meetings. Reduces from seven to five the number of town councilmen for the Town of Brodnax. The bill also reduces from four to three the number of councilmen needed for a quorum and from&nbsp;four to two the number of councilmen needed to call a special meeting. The bill contains an emergency clause and is identical to HB 168.
