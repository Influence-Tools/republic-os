# Virginia SB 261 (2020) — Accounts filed by fiduciaries and reports filed by guardians; civil penalty.

Accounts filed by fiduciaries and reports filed by guardians; civil penalty. Provides that any account filed by a fiduciary with the commissioner of accounts and annual report filed by a guardian with the local department of social services shall be signed under oath and that the punishment for making a false statement or entry in such a filing is a&nbsp;civil penalty of not more than $500.&nbsp;This bill incorporates SB 308 and is identical to HB 1166.&nbsp;
