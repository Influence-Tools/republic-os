# Virginia HB 1608 (2020) — Fort Monroe Authority; exemption from the Virginia Personnel Act.

Fort Monroe Authority; exemption from the Virginia Personnel Act. Exempts the officers and employees of the Fort Monroe Authority from the provisions and requirements of the Virginia Personnel Act. This bill is identical to SB 980.
