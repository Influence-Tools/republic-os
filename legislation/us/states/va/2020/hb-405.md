# Virginia HB 405 (2020) — Menstrual supplies; school boards shall make available in each school building.

Menstrual supplies; availability; public elementary, middle, and high schools. Requires each school board to make tampons&nbsp;or pads available, at all times and at no cost to students, (i) in such accessible locations as it deems appropriate in each elementary school in the local school division and (ii)&nbsp;in the bathrooms of each middle school and high school in the local school division. This bill is identical to SB 232.
