# Virginia HB 449 (2020) — Unlawful hunting, fishing, or trapping; prohibition upon conviction.

Unlawful hunting, fishing, or trapping; prohibitionupon conviction. Provides that any person convicted of violatinga hunting, fishing, or trapping law may also be prohibited by thecourt from hunting, fishing, or trapping for a period of one to fiveyears.
