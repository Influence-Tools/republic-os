# Virginia HB 834 (2020) — Order of publication; electronic notice.

Order of publication; electronic notice. Providesthat a court may permit notice of an order of publication to be givenby electronic means in addition to or in lieu of publication in a newspaper, under such terms and conditions as the court may direct.This bill is a recommendation of the Boyd-Graves Conference.
