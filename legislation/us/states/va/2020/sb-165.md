# Virginia SB 165 (2020) — Life and annuities agents; report on licensure exam passage rate.

Life and annuities agents; report on examinationpassage rate. Eliminates the requirement that the State CorporationCommission provide an annual report to the General Assembly on the licensure exam passage rate of candidates for licensure as a lifeand annuities agent.
