# Virginia HB 1561 (2020) — Fort Monroe Authority; civil actions in general district court.

Fort Monroe Authority; civil actions in general district court. Authorizes the Fort Monroe Authority to prepare, execute, file, and have served certain documents in a civil proceeding in general district court without the intervention of an attorney. This bill is identical to SB 956.
