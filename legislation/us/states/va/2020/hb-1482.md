# Virginia HB 1482 (2020) — Involuntary admission or certification of eligibility order; clarifies provisions governing appeals.

Involuntary admission or certification of eligibilityorder; appeals. Clarifies provisions governing appeals of ordersfor involuntary admission or for certification as eligible for admissionto a training center and provides that in cases in which a personis released during the pendency of an appeal, the appeal shall bein accordance with provisions for testing the legality of detention.
