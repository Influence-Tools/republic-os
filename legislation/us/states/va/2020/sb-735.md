# Virginia SB 735 (2020) — Peer-to-peer vehicle sharing platforms; definitions, establishes requirements.

Peer-to-peer vehicle sharing platforms. Establishes insurance, taxation, recordkeeping, disclosure, and safety recall requirements for peer-to-peer vehicle sharing platforms, defined in the bill. The taxation provisions will become effective on October 1, 2020.
