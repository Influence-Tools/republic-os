# Virginia HB 949 (2020) — Alcoholic beverage control; privileges of local special events licensees.

Alcoholic beverage control; privileges of local special events licensees. Changes the provision limiting local special events licenses from 12 to 16 special events per year, and provides that the duration of any special event shall not exceed three consecutive days. This bill is identical to SB 689.
