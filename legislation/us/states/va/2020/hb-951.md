# Virginia HB 951 (2020) — Mutual assessment property and casualty insurers; notice by electronic delivery.

Mutual assessment property and casualty insurers;notice by electronic delivery. Authorizes a mutual assessmentproperty and casualty insurer to provide notice of assessment byelectronic delivery. Under current law, such notice may only be providedpersonally or by mail.
