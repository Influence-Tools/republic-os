# Virginia HB 505 (2020) — Zoning appeals, board of; writ of certiorari.

Board of zoning appeals; writ of certiorari.Provides that once the circuit court issues an allowance of a writof certiorari in response to a petition from a party aggrieved bya board of zoning appeals decision, the board of zoning appeals shallhave 21 days to respond.
