# Virginia HB 1431 (2020) — Art and Architectural Review Board; changes number of citizen members, staggered terms of members.

Art and Architectural Review Board; members; quorum. Increases from six to seven the number of members of the Art and Architectural Review Board by adding one citizen member appointed by the Governor from the Commonwealth at large and clarifies that all members appointed to the Board have voting privileges. The bill contains a technical amendment.
