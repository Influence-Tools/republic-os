# Virginia HB 938 (2020) — Courthouse; relocation or expansion.

Relocation or expansion of courthouse. Providesthat relocation or expansion of a courthouse to property within 1,000feet of the parcel upon which the courthouse is located shall nottrigger a referendum requirement. The existing statute exempts onlyrelocation or expansion to contiguous land.
