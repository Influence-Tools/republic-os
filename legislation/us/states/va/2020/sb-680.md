# Virginia SB 680 (2020) — Teachers in certain schools for students with disabilities; extension of provisional licenses.

Teachers in certain schools for students withdisabilities; provisional licenses; extension. Requires the Boardof Education to extend for at least one additional year, but forno more than two additional years, the three-year provisional licenseof a teacher employed in a school for students with disabilitiesthat is licensed by the Board upon receiving from the school administratorof such school (i) a recommendation for such extension and (ii) satisfactoryperformance evaluations for such teacher for each year of the originalthree-year provisional license. The provisions of the bill mirrorcurrent law relating to the extension of provisional licenses forpublic school teachers.
