# Virginia SB 1045 (2020) — Cannabidiol oil and THC-A oil; sample testing.

Cannabidiol oil and THC-A oil; sample testing.Directs the Board of Pharmacy to require that, after processingand before dispensing cannabidiol oil and THC-A oil, a pharmaceuticalprocessor make a homogenized sample available from each batch of product for testing at an independent laboratory meeting Board requirements.
