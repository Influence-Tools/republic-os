# Virginia HB 999 (2020) — School board policies; epinephrine, accessibility.

School board policies; epinephrine; accessibility.Requires each school board's policies on the possession and administration of epinephrine in every school in the local schooldivision to require that at least one school nurse, employee of theschool board, employee of a local governing body, or employee ofa local health department who is authorized by a prescriber and trainedin the administration of epinephrine has the means to access at alltimes during regular school hours any such epinephrine that is storedin a locked or otherwise generally inaccessible container or area.
