# Virginia HB 914 (2020) — Segregation in transportation; repeals certain Acts.

Segregation in transportation. Repeals acts of assembly chapters from 1901, 1906, and 1959 requiring and facilitating segregation on railcars, streetcars, and buses. This bill is identical to SB 896.
