# Virginia HB 1626 (2020) — Capitol Police; concurrent jurisdiction.

Capitol Police; concurrent jurisdiction. Provides that a Capitol Police officer who is a detector canine handler has concurrent jurisdiction with the law-enforcement officers of&nbsp;another jurisdiction&nbsp;that has requested the assistance of the Capitol Police in the detection of firearms, ammunition, explosives, propellants, or incendiaries. This bill is identical to SB 996.
