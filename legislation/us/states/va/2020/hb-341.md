# Virginia HB 341 (2020) — Taxation, Department of; sharing information with the Department of Social Services.

Department of Taxation sharing information withthe Department of Social Services. Authorizes the Departmentof Taxation to share tax information related to the federal earnedincome tax credit and the Virginia income tax credit for low-incometaxpayers with the Department of Social Services as necessary toadminister outreach and enrollment related to such credits.
