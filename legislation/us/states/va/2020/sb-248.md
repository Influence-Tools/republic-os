# Virginia SB 248 (2020) — Virginia Gun Violence Intervention and Prevention Fund; created, moneys accruing to Fund, etc.

Virginia Violence Intervention and Prevention Fund. Establishes the Virginia Violence Intervention and Prevention Fund to be administered by the Department of Criminal Justice Services for the purpose of supporting violence intervention and prevention programs, including street outreach, hospital-based violence intervention, and group violence intervention programs. The provisions of the bill are contingent on funding in a general appropriation act.
