# Virginia SB 996 (2020) — Capitol Police; concurrent jurisdiction.

Capitol Police; concurrent jurisdiction. Provides that a Capitol Police officer who is&nbsp;a detector canine&nbsp;handler has concurrent jurisdiction with the law-enforcement officers of another jurisdiction that has requested&nbsp;the assistance of the Capitol Police&nbsp;in the detection of firearms, ammunition, explosives, propellants, or incendiaries. This bill is identical to HB 1626.
