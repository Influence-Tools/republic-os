# Virginia SB 1046 (2020) — Clinical social workers; patient records, involuntary detention orders.

Clinical social workers; patient records; involuntary detention orders. Adds clinical social workers to the list ofeligible providers that includes treating physicians and clinicalpsychologists who can disclose or recommend the withholding of patientrecords, face a malpractice review panel, and provide recommendationson involuntary temporary detention orders.
