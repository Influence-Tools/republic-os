# Virginia SB 336 (2020) — Fishing permits; special permits for certain youth camps.

Special fishing permits for certain youth camps. Authorizes employees of an organized nonprofit tax-exempt youth campthat holds a special fishing permit for certain youth camps to fishwithout a license in public waters adjacent to property owned bythe camp. Current law only authorizes camp members under 18 yearsof age at such camp to fish without a license.
