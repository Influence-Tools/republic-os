# Virginia SB 690 (2020) — Dayton, Town of; amending charter, organizational and technical changes.

Charter; Town of Dayton. Makes numerous organizational and technical changes to the charter for the Town of Dayton in Rockingham County related to town council, powers, and officers. Such changes are consistent with powers generally granted to towns. This bill is identical to HB 1233.
