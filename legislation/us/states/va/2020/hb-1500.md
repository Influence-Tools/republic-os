# Virginia HB 1500 (2020) — Pendente lite spousal support; guidelines.

Pendente lite spousal support; guidelines.Makes current juvenile and domestic relations district court guidelinesfor the presumptive amount of temporary spousal support applicablein cases filed in circuit court. The bill also adjusts the guidelinesto account for changes to the federal tax code that became effectiveon January 1, 2019.
