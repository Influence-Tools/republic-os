# Virginia SB 256 (2020) — Alzheimer's Disease and Related Disorders Commission; extends sunset provision.

Alzheimer's Disease and Related Disorders Commission; sunset. Extends the sunset provision of the Alzheimer's Disease and Related Disorders Commission from July 1, 2020, to July 1, 2023. This bill is identical to HB 310.
