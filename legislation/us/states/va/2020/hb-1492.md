# Virginia HB 1492 (2020) — Parksley, Town of; amending charter, mayor and six members of council shall be elected in November.

Charter; Town of Parksley; November elections. Moves elections for the town council and mayor of the Town of Parksleyfrom May to November and decreases from four years to two years thelength of terms of the mayor and council members.
