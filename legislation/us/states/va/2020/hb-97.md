# Virginia HB 97 (2020) — Newborn screening; screening for Krabbe disease.

Newborn screening; Krabbe disease and otherlysosomal storage disorders. Requires that newborn screeningtests required to be performed on every infant born in the Commonwealthshall include screening for Krabbe disease and other lysosomal storagedisorders for which a screening test is available.
