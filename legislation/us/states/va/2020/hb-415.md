# Virginia HB 415 (2020) — Discipline; suspension, access to graded work during and after suspension.

Discipline; suspension; access to graded work. Requires school boards to adopt policies and procedures to ensure suspended students are able to access and complete graded work during and after&nbsp;the suspension.
