# Virginia HB 1681 (2020) — Virginia Charitable Gaming Board; electronic versions of instant bingo, pull tabs or seal cards.

Virginia Charitable Gaming Board; regulationsof the Board; electronic or mechanical equipment used in the conductof charitable gaming. Increases the number of electronic pulltabs that may be placed in the social quarters of a qualified organizationfrom nine, as currently provided by Virginia Charitable Gaming Boardregulations, to 30.
