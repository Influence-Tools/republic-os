# Virginia SB 528 (2020) — Richmond, City of; amending charter, residency of council members.

Charter; City of Richmond. Requires a council member to reside in the election district from which the member was elected throughout the member's term on the council. A council member may be removed from office for failure to comply with the residency requirement. This bill is identical to HB 1064.
