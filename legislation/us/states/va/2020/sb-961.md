# Virginia SB 961 (2020) — Hopewell, City of; amending charter, issuance of bonds.

Charter; City of Hopewell. Exempts from a referendum requirement bonds issued for the construction, improvement, expansion, or replacement of existing public buildings or facilities, and authorizes the City of&nbsp;Hopewell&nbsp;to replace existing buildings or facilities on an alternative site.
