# Virginia HB 256 (2020) — Disorderly conduct; students.

Disorderly conduct; students. Provides that an elementary or secondary&nbsp;school&nbsp;student&nbsp;is not guilty of disorderly conduct in a public place if the disorderly conduct occurred on the property of an elementary or secondary&nbsp;school, on a school bus, or at any activity conducted or sponsored by any elementary or secondary&nbsp;school. This bill incorporates HB 8 and is identical to SB 3.
