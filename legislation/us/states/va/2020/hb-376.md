# Virginia HB 376 (2020) — Teacher and support staff shortages; data, reporting.

Teachers, other instructional personnel, and support staff; shortages; data; reporting. Requires (i) each school board to report to the Department of Education annually the number and type of teacher, other instructional personnel, and&nbsp;support staff vacancies in the school division and (ii) each approved education preparation program to report to the Department of Education annually the number of individuals who&nbsp;completed the program by endorsement area. The bill requires the Department of Education to (a) establish deadlines for and the format of the reporting of such data and (b) aggregate and report such data annually on the Department's website.
