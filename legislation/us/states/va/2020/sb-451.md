# Virginia SB 451 (2020) — Juvenile and domestic relations district court; award of attorney fees and costs.

Juvenile and domestic relations district court;award of attorney fees. Permits a juvenile and domestic relationsdistrict court judge to take all relevant factors, in addition tothe relative financial ability of the parties, into considerationwhen awarding attorney fees and costs.
