# Virginia SB 87 (2020) — License plates, special; issuance for supporters of City of Va. Beach bearing the legend VB STRONG.

Special license plates; VB STRONG. Authorizes the issuance of special license plates for supporters of the City of Virginia Beach bearing the legend VB STRONG. The deadline for the receipt of 450 prepaid applications is November 1, 2020.
