# Virginia SB 1090 (2020) — Grays Creek; designating as component of Virginia Scenic Rivers System.

Virginia Scenic Rivers System; Grays Creek. Designates a six-mile portion of Grays Creek in Surry County as a component of the Virginia Scenic Rivers System. This bill is identical to HB 1612.
