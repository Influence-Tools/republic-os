# Virginia SB 284 (2020) — Charlottesville, City of; amending charter, city organization.

Charter; City of Charlottesville; city organization; council. Modernizes and reorganizes aspects of the charter of the City of Charlottesville. The bill updates and repeals various provisions to conform to state and federal law as well as current city organization. This bill is identical to HB 1107.
