# Virginia HB 998 (2020) — Flood plain; adoption of ordinances.

Adoption of flood plain ordinances. Providesthat any locality may by ordinance regulate the activity on, useof, or development of a flood plain in a manner consistent with anystate or federal flood plain management programs and requirements.
