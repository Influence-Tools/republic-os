# Virginia SB 957 (2020) — Civic Education, Commission on; increases membership, repeals sunset provision and general funding.

Commission on Civic Education; sunset and funding; membership. Repeals the sunset provision for the Commission on Civic Education and&nbsp;repeals a prohibition on the use of general funds to support the work of the Commission. The bill also increases and adjusts the makeup of the Commission's membership.
