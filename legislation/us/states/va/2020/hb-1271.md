# Virginia HB 1271 (2020) — Nonpublic service companies, certain; conveyance of right-of-way usage.

Conveyance of right-of-way usage to certain nonpublic service companies. Allows a 501(c)(4) social welfare organization to obtain a land use permit from the Department of Transportation to use rights-of-way to operate a wholesale open-access fiber network. This bill is identical to SB 792.
