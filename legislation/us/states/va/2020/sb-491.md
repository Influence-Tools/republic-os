# Virginia SB 491 (2020) — Inquiry and report of immigration status; persons charged with or convicted of certain crimes.

Inquiry and report of immigration status; personscharged with or convicted of certain crimes. Removes provisionsrequiring (i) jail officers to ascertain the citizenship of any inmatetaken into custody at a jail, (ii) probation and parole officersto inquire as to the citizenship status of an individual convictedof a felony in circuit court and referred to such officers, and (iii)officers in charge of correctional facilities to inquire as to thecitizenship of any person committed to a correctional facility, and therefore such information is not required to be reported to theCentral Criminal Records Exchange of the Department of State Police.The bill also removes the mandatory duty of the clerk of a courtcommitting a convicted alien to a correctional facility to furnishrelated court records to a United States immigration officer andthe requirement that an intake officer report to the Bureau of Immigrationand Customs Enforcement of the U.S. Department of Homeland Securityany juvenile detained on an allegation that the juvenile, believedto be in the United States illegally, committed a violent felony.
