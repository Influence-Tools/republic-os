# Virginia SB 646 (2020) — Tetrahydrocannabinol concentration; definition.

Tetrahydrocannabinol concentration; definition.Clarifies that "tetrahydrocannabinol concentration" refers to delta-9-tetrahydrocannabinoland is determined using post-decarboxylation testing or other equivalentmethod. The bill contains an emergency clause.
