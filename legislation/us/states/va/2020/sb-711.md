# Virginia SB 711 (2020) — Driving while license, permit, or privilege to drive suspended or revoked; mandatory minimum term.

Driving while license, permit, or privilegeto drive suspended or revoked; mandatory minimum. Eliminatesthe mandatory minimum term of confinement in jail of 10 days fora third or subsequent conviction of driving on a suspended license.
