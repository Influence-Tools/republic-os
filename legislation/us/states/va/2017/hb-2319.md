# Virginia HB 2319 (2017) — National Flood Insurance Program; participation by affected localities, report.

Requires the Secretary of Natural Resources to issue a report by November 1, 2018, listing any locality not participating in the Community Rating System of the National Flood Insurance Program and recommending any legislation necessary to encourage participation.
