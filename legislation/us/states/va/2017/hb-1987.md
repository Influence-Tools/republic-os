# Virginia HB 1987 (2017) — Alcoholic beverage control; new license for certain commercial lifestyle centers.

Defines "commercial lifestyle center" and creates a new nonretail license for commercial lifestyle centers. The bill sets out the privileges of the license and imposes a $300 annual state tax and a $60 annual local tax on the license. The bill contains technical amendments. This bill is identical to
