# Virginia HB 1477 (2017) — Cats and dogs; local government may, by ordinance, provide for lifetime licenses.

Authorizes the governing body of a county or city to provide for a lifetime dog or cat license. The bill removes the minimum annual tax for a dog or cat, sets the maximum tax for a lifetime license at $50, and limits the fee for a duplicate dog or cat tag to $1. This bill is identical to
