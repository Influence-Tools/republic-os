# Virginia HB 1842 (2017) — Alcoholic beverage control; neutral grain spirits or alcohol sold at government stores.

Increases from 101 to 151 the proof of neutral grain spirits or alcohol that is without distinctive character, aroma, taste, or color that may be sold at government stores. The bill has an expiration date of July 1, 2022.
