# Virginia SB 1044 (2017) — Nonconfidential court records; secure remote access, date of birth verification.

Provides that the Supreme Court and any other court clerk may provide online access to subscribers who have entered into an agreement with the clerk to have secure remote access to court records of nonconfidential criminal case information to confirm the complete date of birth of a defendant.
