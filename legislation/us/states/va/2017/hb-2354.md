# Virginia HB 2354 (2017) — Conflict of Interests Act, State and Local Government; school board employees, Planning District 4.

Provides that school divisions located in Planning District 4, New River Valley, are not subject to the prohibition against hiring a school division employee who is related to a member of the school board under certain circumstances.
