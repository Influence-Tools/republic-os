# Virginia SB 919 (2017) — Removal of blight; building collapse; emergency.

Allows a locality to charge owners of property the cost the locality incurred in removing or repairing a building or structure on such property if permitted under the Virginia Uniform Statewide Building Code in the event of an emergency.
