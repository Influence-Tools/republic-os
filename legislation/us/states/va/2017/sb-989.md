# Virginia SB 989 (2017) — Commonwealth's Twenty marksmanship award.

Provides for the Commonwealth's Twenty marksmanship award to recognize the top 20 marksmen in Virginia, chosen from the Virginia state residents who compete at the annual Virginia State Championship matches sanctioned by the Virginia Shooting Sports Association. Under current law, the award recognizes the top 20 competitors in each of the rifle and pistol Excellence-in-Competition matches at such Championships.
