# Virginia SB 1559 (2017) — Zoning appeals, board of; recipient to receive notice of zoning violation.

Requires that the recipient of certain notices from the board of zoning appeals receive notice via registered mail to the last known address or usual place of abode of the property owner or its registered agent, if any, prior to the commencement of the 30-day appeal period.
