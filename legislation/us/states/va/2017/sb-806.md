# Virginia SB 806 (2017) — Interstate 73 Corridor Development Fund and Program.

Establishes the Interstate 73 Corridor Development Fund and Program and reallocates to the I-73 Fund the $40 million from state recordation taxes that is currently allocated annually to the U.S. Route 58 Corridor Development Fund and Program. All provisions of the bill are contingent upon construction of and payments for Route 58 being completed. The bill contains a reenactment clause.
