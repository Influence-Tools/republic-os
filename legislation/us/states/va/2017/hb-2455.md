# Virginia HB 2455 (2017) — Personal property tax, tangible; list of uncollected balances.

Expands the uncollected personal property tax on vehicles for which the treasurer is required to compile a list to include the personal property tax on trailers, semitrailers, watercraft, and manufactured homes.
