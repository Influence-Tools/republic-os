# Virginia SB 944 (2017) — Institutions of higher education; possession and administration of epinephrine.

Authorizes and provides liability protection for employees of a public or private institution of higher education who are authorized by a prescriber and trained in the administration of epinephrine, insulin, or glucagon to possess and administer such epinephrine, insulin, or glucagon.
