# Virginia HB 2277 (2017) — Licensure of doctors of medicine, osteopathy, chiropractic, and podiatry; requirements.

Removes provisions related to licensure of graduates of an institution not approved by an accrediting agency recognized by the Board of Medicine. Under the bill, only graduates of institutions approved by an accrediting agency recognized by the Board of Medicine are eligible for licensure.
