# Virginia SB 804 (2017) — Retail Sales and Use Tax; media-related exemptions.

Extends from July 1, 2017, to July 1, 2022, the expiration of the retail sales and use tax exemption for printing purchased by an advertising business from a printer in the Commonwealth, so long as such material is distributed outside of the Commonwealth.
