# Virginia SB 1448 (2017) — Alcoholic beverage control; privileges of licensed distillers appointed as agents of ABC Board.

Allows a licensed distiller who has been appointed by the Alcoholic Beverage Control Board as an agent to sell spirits manufactured by the distiller at a site of an event licensed by the Board and conducted for the purpose of featuring and educating the consuming public about spirits products.
