# Virginia SB 1587 (2017) — Alcoholic beverage control; wine and beer licenses.

Includes Alleghany County in the list of wine and beer licenses that may be granted to persons operating food concessions at any outdoor performing arts amphitheater, arena, or similar facility that has seating for more than 20,000 persons.
