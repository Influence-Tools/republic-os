# Virginia HB 1982 (2017) — Graduation requirements; verified units of credit, satisfactory score on the PSAT examination.

Requires the Board of Education, in establishing graduation requirements, to provide for the award of verified units of credit for a satisfactory score, as determined by the Board, on the Preliminary ACT (PreACT) or the Preliminary SAT/National Merit Scholarship Qualifying Test (PSAT/NMSQT) examination.
