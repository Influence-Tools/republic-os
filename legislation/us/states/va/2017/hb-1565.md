# Virginia HB 1565 (2017) — Local tax and regulatory incentives; green development zones.

Authorizes localities to create green development zones that provide certain tax incentives and regulatory flexibility for up to 10 years to a business operating in an energy-efficient building or to a business that produces products used to reduce negative impact on the environment.
