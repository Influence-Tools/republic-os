# Virginia HB 2477 (2017) — Onsite sewage systems and private wells; VDH to take steps to eliminate site evaluation.

Directs the Department of Health to take certain steps to begin eliminating site evaluation and design services for onsite sewage systems and private wells provided by the Department.
