# Virginia HB 1457 (2017) — Part-time deputy sheriffs; compensation.

Includes like rank and experience as a factor in setting the maximum allowable hourly equivalent compensation paid to part-time deputy sheriffs performing like duties of full-time deputy sheriffs.
