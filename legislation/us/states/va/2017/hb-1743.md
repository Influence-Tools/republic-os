# Virginia HB 1743 (2017) — Alcoholic beverage control; retail on-premises license for nonprofit cinema houses.

Creates a new retail on-premises wine and beer license for nonprofit historic cinema houses. The bill defines "historic cinema house" and sets out the privileges of the license and imposes a $200 annual state license tax and a $20 annual local license tax.
