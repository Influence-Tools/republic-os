# Virginia HB 1858 (2017) — Small Business and Supplier Diversity, Department of; certification of certain businesses.

Authorizes the Director of the Department of Small Business and Supplier Diversity to adopt regulations that mandate certification without any additional paperwork of any small, women-owned, or minority-owned business that has obtained certification under any federal small, women-owned, and minority-owned business certification program.
