# Virginia HB 2229 (2017) — Electronic credentials; creates standards for DMV in issuing, reviewing, etc., report.

Creates standards for the Department of Motor Vehicles in issuing, reviewing, and displaying electronic credentials. The Electronic Credentials Act establishes fees for access to electronic credentials and standards for a verification system that would allow users to verify the authenticity of electronic credentials.
