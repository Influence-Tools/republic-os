# Virginia HB 2358 (2017) — Water utilities; suspension of proposed rate increases.

Extends the maximum period that the State Corporation Commission is authorized to suspend the implementation of a proposed rate increase rates from 150 to 180 days. The measure applies only to certain investor-owned water utilities.
