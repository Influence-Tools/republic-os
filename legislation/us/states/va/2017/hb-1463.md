# Virginia HB 1463 (2017) — Delinquent taxes; publication of list by governing body or treasurer.

Authorizes the governing body of a locality and the treasurer to publish lists of certain local delinquent taxes whether or not they are based on information as it exists at the end of the fiscal year.
