# Virginia HB 1785 (2017) — Emergency vehicles, privately owned volunteer; exemptions and requirements.

Clarifies that the current limit of no more than two flashing or steady-burning red or red and white combination warning lights applies to light units rather than individual lights on a vehicle owned by a member of a fire department, volunteer fire company, or volunteer EMS agency or a police chaplain.
