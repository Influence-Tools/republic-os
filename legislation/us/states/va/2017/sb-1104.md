# Virginia SB 1104 (2017) — Form of ballot; order of independent candidates, required paperwork.

Provides that when there is more than one independent candidate for an office, their names shall appear on the ballot in an order determined by the priority of time of filing all required paperwork for the office. In the event that two or more candidates file simultaneously, the order of filing is determined by lot by the electoral board.
