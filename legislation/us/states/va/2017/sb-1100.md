# Virginia SB 1100 (2017) — New Economy Workforce Credential Grant Program; reporting by SCHEV on Program.

Requires the State Council of Higher Education for Virginia to include in its annual report on the New Economy Workforce Credential Grant Program information on the wages of students who have completed noncredit workforce training programs, organized by credential name and relevant industry sector and including average wage and other relevant information.
