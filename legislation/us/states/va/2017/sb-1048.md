# Virginia SB 1048 (2017) — Death certificate; amendments other than correction of information.

Establishes a process for amending death certificates to change the name of the deceased, the deceased's parent or spouse, or the informant; the marital status of the deceased; or the place of residence of the deceased when the place of residence is outside the Commonwealth.
