# Virginia SB 904 (2017) — VWC; permits commissioners and deputy commissioners to carry a concealed weapon.

Provides an exception from the prohibition against carrying a weapon into courthouses in the Commonwealth for a commissioner or deputy commissioner of the Workers' Compensation Commission while in the conduct of official duties.
