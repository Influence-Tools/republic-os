# Virginia HB 2332 (2017) — Teachers; compensation at rate competitive with national average salary.

Defines, for the purpose of the stated goal of the Commonwealth for teacher compensation that its public school teachers be compensated at a rate that is competitive, the term "competitive" as, at a minimum, at or above the national average teacher compensation.
