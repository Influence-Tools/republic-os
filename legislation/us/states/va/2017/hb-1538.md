# Virginia HB 1538 (2017) — Revision and recodification of Title 23; corrections.

Corrects typographical errors and makes other technical amendments relating to the revision and recodification of Title 23 and declares such corrections and amendments to be effective retroactively to October 1, 2016. This bill is a recommendation of the Code Commission.
