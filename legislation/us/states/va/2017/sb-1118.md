# Virginia SB 1118 (2017) — Line of Duty Act; Act includes firefighter trainees.

Includes under the Line of Duty Act a person with a recognized membership status with a fire company or department who is enrolled in a Fire Service Training course offered by the Virginia Department of Fire Programs or required to become a certified firefighter.
