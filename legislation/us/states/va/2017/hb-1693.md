# Virginia HB 1693 (2017) — Virginia Public Procurement Act; contracts for architectural and engineering services.

Increases the maximum permissible fee for any single project encompassed in a contract for architectural or professional engineering services relating to multiple construction projects from $100,000 to $150,000.
