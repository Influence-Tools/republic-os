# Virginia SB 873 (2017) — Authority of fire chief over unmanned aircraft systems at a fire, etc.; civil liability.

Includes immediate airspace under the current authority of the fire chief or other officer in charge of maintaining order where there is imminent danger or the actual occurrence of fire or explosion or the uncontrolled release of hazardous materials that threaten life or property.
