# Virginia HB 1697 (2017) — Extension of approvals to address housing crisis.

Extends the sunset date for several measures related to various land use approvals from July 1, 2017, to July 1, 2020. The bill also expands the scope of such measures that will be subject to the extension to include those measures approved by January 1, 2017.
