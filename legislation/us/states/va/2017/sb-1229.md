# Virginia SB 1229 (2017) — Driver's license or learner's permit; issuance, minimum standards for vision tests.

Increases the minimum visual acuity an applicant must possess for issuance of a driver's license or learner's permit from a field of 100 degrees of horizontal vision in one or both eyes to a field of 110 degrees of horizontal vision in one or both eyes. This bill is identical to
