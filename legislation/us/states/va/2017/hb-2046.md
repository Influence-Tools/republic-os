# Virginia HB 2046 (2017) — Unused dispensed drugs; guidelines for provision of counseling and information on proper disposal.

Requires the Board of Pharmacy to develop guidelines for the provision of counseling and information regarding proper disposal of unused dispensed drugs, including information about pharmacy drug disposal programs in which the pharmacy may participate, by pharmacists to patients for whom a prescription is dispensed.
