# Virginia HB 1973 (2017) — Economic Development Access Program; bonded projects.

Imposes a 48-month moratorium on the repayment of funds allocated to a locality for a bonded project pursuant to the Economic Development Access Program, provided that the conditions of the Commonwealth Transportation Board's economic development access policy are met.
