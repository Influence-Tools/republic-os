# Virginia HB 1815 (2017) — Computer trespass; government computers and computers used for public utilities, penalty.

Increases the Class 1 misdemeanor computer trespass crimes to a Class 6 felony if the computer affected is one that is exclusively for the use of, or used by or for, the Commonwealth, a local government within the Commonwealth, or certain public utilities.
