# Virginia SB 1342 (2017) — District courts; jurisdictional limit does not include any attorney fees.

Provides that the jurisdictional limit for general district courts does not include any attorney fees. The bill requires that the amount of an appeal bond required for appeals from a district court include any amount awarded for attorney fees in the case.
