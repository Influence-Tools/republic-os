# Virginia SB 1003 (2017) — Wireless E-911 Fund; distribution percentages.

Postpones from July 1, 2017, to July 1, 2018, the date by which the Department of Taxation is required to conduct its first recalculation of the percentage of funds in the Wireless E-911 Fund that is required to be distributed to each public safety answering point (PSAP).
