# Virginia HB 1718 (2017) — Civics Education, Commission on; renamed Commission on Civic Education, extends sunset provision.

Renames the Commission on Civics Education as the Commission on Civic Education and extends from July 1, 2017, to July 1, 2019, the expiration of such commission. This bill is identical
