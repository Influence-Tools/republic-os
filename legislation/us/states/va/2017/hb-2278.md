# Virginia HB 2278 (2017) — Cooperative Marketing Fund; programs eligible for matching grant funds.

Provides that proposals for existing programs that have a measurable return on investment are eligible for matching grant funds under the Cooperative Marketing Fund. The bill also (i) removes the requirement that only private funds be matched and (ii) provides that guidelines established for the Fund may include a preference for proposals submitted by state agencies.
