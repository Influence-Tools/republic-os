# Virginia SB 1286 (2017) — Land preservation tax credits; withholding tax of nonresident owners.

Provides that the two percent transfer fee for land preservation tax credits shall not apply to a distribution of credits to a nonresident owner of a pass-through entity when such credits are applied by the pass-through entity to the withholding tax of the nonresident owner.
