# Virginia HB 2240 (2017) — Victims of crime; right to nondisclosure of certain information.

Requires that written consent provided by the victim's next of kin to law enforcement is necessary, if the victim is a minor, before a law-enforcement agency may disclose any information that identifies the victim of a crime that resulted in the victim's death.
