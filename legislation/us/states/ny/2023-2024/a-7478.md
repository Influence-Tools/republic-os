# New York A 7478 (2023-2024) — Relates to residency requirements for the village clerk/treasurer and the deputy clerk for the village of Cold Spring

Authorizes the village clerk/treasurer and the deputy clerk for the village of Cold Spring, Putnam county, to reside outside such village, but within the county in which such village is located or an adjoining county.
