# New York S 6919 (2023-2024) — Authorizes an occupancy tax in the town of Putnam Valley, in the county of Putnam

Authorizes the town of Putnam Valley, in Putnam county to adopt and amend local laws imposing a tax on persons occupying a room for hire in any hotel in an amount not to exceed 2% of the per diem rental rate for each room.
