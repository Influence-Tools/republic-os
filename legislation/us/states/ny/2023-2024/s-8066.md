# New York S 8066 (2023-2024) — Provides a funding source for the agrivoltaics research program; and places a sunset on such program

Provides that funding for the agrivoltaics research program shall come from appropriations made to the New York state college of agriculture and life sciences at Cornell university in chapter 53 of 2023 for the purpose of studying ecological impacts of agrivoltaics; places a sunset on such program of December 31, 2030.
