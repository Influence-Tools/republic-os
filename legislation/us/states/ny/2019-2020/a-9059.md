# New York A 9059 (2019-2020) — Relates to requiring motor carriers to identify each limousine such motor carrier operates and ensure that such information is update on their website

Provides that every motor carrier shall furnish the department of motor vehicles a list of each altered motor vehicle commonly referred to as a "stretch limousine" designed to carry 9 or more passengers; requires the commissioner of motor vehicles to annually review the bus driver files of each driver employed by a motor vehicle carrier who operates such stretch limousine; makes related provisions.
