# New York S 3115 (2019-2020) — Relates to the qualifications for holding the office of assistant district attorney or senior district attorney in the county of Cayuga

Permits a person performing the functions of assistant district attorney or senior assistant district attorney in Cayuga county to be a resident of either Cayuga county or an adjoining county.
