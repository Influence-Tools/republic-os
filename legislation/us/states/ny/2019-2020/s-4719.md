# New York S 4719 (2019-2020) — Authorizes Curt McDermott, a part-time town of Shawangunk police officer, to take the civil service exam for full-time police officer

Authorizes Curt McDermott, a part-time town of Shawangunk police officer in the county of Ulster, to take the civil service examination for full-time police officer, and to be placed on the eligible list for appointment if his score on the examination is high enough.
