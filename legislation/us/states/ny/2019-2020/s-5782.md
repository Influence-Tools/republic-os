# New York S 5782 (2019-2020) — Relates to commercial mobile radio and cellular telephone service providers

Relates to commercial mobile radio and cellular telephone service providers; permits the commission of public service to designate any commercial mobile radio service provider as an eligible telecommunications carrier for purposes of providing lifeline service.
