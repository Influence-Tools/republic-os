# New York S 3952 (2019-2020) — Relates to the merger of cemetery corporations

Provides that two cemeteries located in Suffolk county with contiguous or adjacent land dedicated for cemetery purposes and previously operating as public cemetery corporations may merge where their total acreage does not exceed three hundred twenty-five acres.
