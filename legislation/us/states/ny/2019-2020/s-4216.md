# New York S 4216 (2019-2020) — Relates to the Fairview Firemen's Benevolent Association's usage of moneys received from foreign fire insurance companies

Relates to the incorporation of the Fairview Firemen's Benevolent Association of the town of Greenburgh, county of Westchester, New York, and providing for the payment to such corporation of a portion of the taxes imposed by section five hundred fifty-three and five hundred fifty-four of the insurance law, in relation to the usage of moneys received from foreign fire insurance companies.
