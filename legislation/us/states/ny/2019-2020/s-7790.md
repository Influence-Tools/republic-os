# New York S 7790 (2019-2020) — Relates to qualifications for holding the office of corporation counsel in the city of Amsterdam

Relates to qualifications for holding the office of corporation counsel in the city of Amsterdam; provides that a person not living in the city of Amsterdam may hold the office of corporation counsel for such city provided he or she lives in Montgomery county or an adjoining county within the state of New York.
