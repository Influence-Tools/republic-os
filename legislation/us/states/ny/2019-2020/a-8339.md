# New York A 8339 (2019-2020) — Requires the Rye Country Day School to make the athletic field and track available for rental to public recreation groups for a certain amount of time per year

Requires the Rye Country Day School to make the athletic field and track available for rental to public recreation groups for a certain amount of time per year and to provide opportunity of public rental of recreation buildings constructed on the parcel transferred from the thruway authority.
