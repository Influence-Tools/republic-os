# New York S 6682 (2017-2018) — Relates to the taking of crabs

Relates to the taking of crabs; requires the department of environmental conservation to cooperate with any town, village or county that requests any municipal property be subject to a harvest closure when adopting regulation measures for horseshoe crab management.
