# New York A 5523 (2017-2018) — Relates to grant assurances or obligations for the airport and aviation projects in the town of East Hampton, county of Suffolk

Provides that the town of East Hampton, county of Suffolk may only enter into grant assurances or obligations where the term is ten years or more with the state of New York or the United States or any agency, or instrumentality thereof, for airport and aviation projects at the town airport or landing field upon the adoption of a resolution subject to permissive referendum.
