# New York A 7327 (2021-2022) — Relates to providing for discretionary buying thresholds

Allows the commissioner to purchase services and commodities for the office of general services or its customer agencies serviced by the office of general services business services center in an amount not exceeding eighty-five thousand dollars without a formal competitive process.
