# New York A 6426 (2021-2022) — Authorizes the district superintendent of the Eastport-South Manor central school district to adjust certain improper apportionments of school tax levy

Authorizes the district superintendent of the Eastport-South Manor central school district to adjust certain improper apportionments of school tax levy which occurred in the 2019-2020 tax year over the next two tax years (2020-2021 and 2021-2022) and validating certain acts by the Eastport-South Manor Central School District in connection with adjustment of the improper apportionment of the school tax levy in 2019-2020.
