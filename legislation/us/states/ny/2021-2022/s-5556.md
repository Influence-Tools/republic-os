# New York S 5556 (2021-2022) — Relates to local civil administrative enforcement procedures in the town of East Hampton

Authorizes the town of East Hampton to adopt a local law establishing an administrative adjudication hearing procedure for all code and ordinance violations relating to conditions which constitute a threat or danger to the public health, safety or welfare.
