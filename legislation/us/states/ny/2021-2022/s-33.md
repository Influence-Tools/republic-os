# New York S 33 (2021-2022) — Relates to designating a small business liaison

Provides that the departments of agriculture and markets, environmental conservation, labor, transportation and taxation and finance shall designate an existing employee to serve as its liaison to small businesses regulated by such agency; provides the duties of such small business liaison.
