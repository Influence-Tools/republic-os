# New York A 1251 (2021-2022) — Relates to the direct deposit of workers' compensation benefits

Requires an injured worker or person entitled to a death benefit to be provided with the option to have the compensation payment made through direct deposit, and requires such person be given notice of such option and the means necessary to enroll.
