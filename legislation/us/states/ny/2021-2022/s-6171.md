# New York S 6171 (2021-2022) — Relates to funeral entities and the operation of any crematory or crematorium

Relates to funeral entities and the operation of any crematory or crematorium; permits a crematory operated by a funeral entity in the town of Tonawanda, that has been subject to a consent order issued by the department of environmental conservation, to relocate existing operations subject to local authority approval.
