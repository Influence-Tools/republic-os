# New York A 7942 (2021-2022) — Relates to the transfer of unclaimed support collections and unidentified payments; repealer

Provides that any funds paid to a support collection unit which have not been disbursed after two years of diligent efforts to locate the person entitled to such funds, and funds which the remitter of such funds has not provided sufficient identifying information to associate the funds with an existing account shall be paid to the state comptroller.
