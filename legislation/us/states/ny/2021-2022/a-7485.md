# New York A 7485 (2021-2022) — Relates to exempting a certain parcel of land in the town of Islip from certain use restrictions

Exempts a certain parcel of land in the town of Islip from stated restrictions of use so that the Town of Islip may transfer a portion of the land for uses to be approved by the town board.
