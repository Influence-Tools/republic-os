# New York A 7576 (2021-2022) — Relates to the issuance of diplomas to youth who are placed, committed, supervised, detained or confined in certain facilities

Relates to the issuance of diplomas to youth who are placed, committed, supervised, detained or confined in certain facilities; provides that persons under twenty-one years of age who are placed with, committed to, under the supervision of, detained or otherwise confined in certain facilities, who participate in an educational program provided by such facilities, shall be issued a high school diploma upon completion of the minimum New York state diploma requirements.
