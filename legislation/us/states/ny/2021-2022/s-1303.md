# New York S 1303 (2021-2022) — Relates to pre-term labor care and providing pre-term labor patients with information regarding the potential health effects of pre-term labor and pre-term delivery; repealer

Provides for pre-term labor care and directing the commissioner of health to require hospitals to provide pre-term labor patients with information regarding the potential health effects of pre-term labor and pre-term delivery on an expectant mother and on her fetus.
