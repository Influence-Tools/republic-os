# New York S 874 (2021-2022) — Establishes state university of New York educational opportunity centers; repealer

Establishes the state university of New York educational opportunity centers which provide an integrated system of education, vocational training and student support services targeted to higher education access and the development of a quality workforce which supports the economic development of New York's distressed communities.
