# New York A 8724 (2021-2022) — Permits seaweed cultivation in underwater lands at Gardiner's and Peconic bays and commercial fishing licenses

Permits seaweed cultivation in underwater lands at Gardiner's and Peconic bays; permits Suffolk county to lease lands underwater ceded to it by the state for the purpose of seaweed cultivation; modifies certain provisions regarding commercial fishing licenses; makes related provisions.
