# New York S 7835 (2021-2022) — Relates to accreditation, approval, and operation of midwifery birth centers

Relates to accreditation, approval, and operation of midwifery birth centers; adds life safety codes or applicable other building standards to the considerations the commissioner shall consider when approving certificates of incorporation; extends the effective date to 180 days after it shall have become a law.
