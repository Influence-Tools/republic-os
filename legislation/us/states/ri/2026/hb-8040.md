# Rhode Island HB 8040 (2026) — AN ACT RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- ZONING ORDINANCES

Prohibits any city or town from enacting any zoning ordinance that would place restrictions on residential NARR-certified recovery residences that are not applicable to other residential homes.
