# California AB 11 (20092010 Special Session 8) — Transportation: general obligation bonds: letters of no prejudice.

Existing law, the Clean Air and Transportation Improvement Act of 1990, authorizes the issuance of $1.99 billion in general obligation bonds for rail and transit purposes. Existing law provides for the California Transportation Commission to allocate bond funds to eligible projects.
