# California AB 3 (20092010 Special Session 8) — Fines and forfeitures: forensic laboratories: Alcohol Beverage Control Fund: inmate education.

Under existing law, an additional state penalty of $1 is levied for each $10 or fraction thereof, upon every fine, penalty, or forfeiture collected by the courts for criminal offenses. Those funds are required to be deposited into the state's DNA Identification Fund to fund the operation of the DNA Fingerprint, Unsolved Crime and Innocence Protection Act, and to facilitate compliance with the requirement that DNA samples be included in the state summary criminal history information.
