# California AB 3 (20092010 Special Session 3) — Income taxes: sales and use taxes: vehicle license fees.

The Personal Income Tax Law imposes taxes based upon taxable income. That law also allows credits for personal exemptions, and imposes an alternative minimum tax, as specified.
