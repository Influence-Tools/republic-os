# California AB 7 (20092010 Special Session 3) — State funds: registered warrants.

Existing law provides that whenever the Controller draws a warrant upon the Treasurer payable out of the General Fund in an amount in excess of the balance remaining in the unapplied money in the General Fund, as specified, the Controller shall upon the same day present it to the Treasurer who shall endorse it in accordance with certain procedures, thereby creating registered warrants. The Controller, with the approval of the Pooled Money Investment Board, is authorized to fix a maturity date for registered warrants.
