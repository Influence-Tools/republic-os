# California AB 12 (20092010 Special Session 3) — California State Lottery.

(1) The California State Lottery Act, subject to the approval by the voters, appropriates moneys, commencing with the 2009â€“10 fiscal year, on an annual basis from the General Fund to various educational entities based on specified factors. The act provides that specified provisions regarding General Fund appropriations may only be amended by a vote of the people.
