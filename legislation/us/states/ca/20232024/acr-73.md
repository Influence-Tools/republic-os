# California ACR 73 (20232024) — Relative to State Scientist Day.

This measure would recognize May 3, 2023, as the 32nd Annual State Scientist Day and would declare that this day shall be a tribute to the dedication and professionalism of the state scientists who work on behalf of all the residents of California.
