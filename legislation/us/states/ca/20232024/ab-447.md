# California AB 447 (20232024) — Public postsecondary education: students with disabilities: inclusive college programs.

Existing law states the intent of the Legislature that public higher education in California strive to provide educationally equitable environments that give each Californian, regardless of age, economic circumstance, or certain specified characteristics, including mental disability, a reasonable opportunity to develop fully their potential.
