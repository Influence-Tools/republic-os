# California SB 739 (20232024) — Construction manager at-risk construction contracts: City of Elk Grove: zoo project.

Existing law authorizes a county, until January 1, 2029, with approval of the board of supervisors, to utilize construction manager at-risk construction contracts for the erection, construction, alteration, repair, or improvement of infrastructure, excluding roads, and including, among other things, buildings, subject to certain requirements and limitations.
