# California AB 1862 (20232024) — Engineering, land surveying, and architecture: limited liability partnerships.

Existing law, the Professional Engineers Act and the Professional Land Surveyors' Act, provide for the licensure and regulation of engineers and land surveyors by the Board for Professional Engineers, Land Surveyors, and Geologists. Existing law, the Architects Practice Act, provides for the licensure and regulation of architects by the California Architects Board. Existing law, the Uniform Partnership Act of 1994, authorizes the formation of registered limited liability partnerships and foreign limited liability partnerships, as specified.
