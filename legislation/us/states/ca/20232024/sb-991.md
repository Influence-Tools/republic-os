# California SB 991 (20232024) — School districts: Los Angeles Unified School District: inspector general.

(1) Existing law, until January 1, 2025, authorizes the Los Angeles Unified School District's Inspector General of the Office of the Inspector General to conduct audits and investigations, as specified, including administering oaths or affirmations. Existing law makes it a misdemeanor, punishable as specified, for a person, after the administration of an oath or affirmation by the inspector general, to state or affirm as true any material matter that they know to be false. Existing law requires the inspector general to submit to the Legislature annual interim reports and a final cumulative report.
