# California SB 564 (20232024) — Sheriffs and marshals: fees.

Existing law requires the Legislature to provide for an elected county sheriff and provides for the duties of the sheriff. Existing law prescribes fees for serving, executing, and processing required court notices, writs, orders, and other services provided by sheriffs and marshals.
