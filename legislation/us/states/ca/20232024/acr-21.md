# California ACR 21 (20232024) — Relative to California Kidney Day.

This measure would designate March 9, 2023, as California Kidney Day, and would urge Californians to familiarize themselves with the main causes and consequences of chronic kidney disease (CKD) and the importance of early intervention to conserve health care resources and to promote a better quality of life for CKD patients.
