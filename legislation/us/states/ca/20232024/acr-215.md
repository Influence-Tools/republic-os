# California ACR 215 (20232024) — Relative to the Payómkawish Highway.

This measure would designate a specified portion of State Highway Route 76 in the County of San Diego as the Payómkawish Highway. The measure would also request that the Department of Transportation determine the cost of appropriate signs showing this special designation and, upon receiving donations from nonstate sources sufficient to cover the cost, to erect those signs.
