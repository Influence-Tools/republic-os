# California SCR 161 (20232024) — Relative to the First Continental Congress.

This measure would commemorate the 250th anniversary of the First Continental Congress and encourage and all citizens to reflect on the legacy of the First Continental Congress and honor the sacrifices made by the delegates who stood up for the principles of liberty and self-governance.
