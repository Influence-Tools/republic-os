# California SCR 164 (20232024) — Relative to National Health Center Week.

This measure would proclaim the week of August 4, 2024, to August 10, 2024, inclusive, as National Health Center Week and would encourage Californians to take part in this week by visiting their local community health center and celebrating the important partnership between California's community health centers and the communities they serve.
