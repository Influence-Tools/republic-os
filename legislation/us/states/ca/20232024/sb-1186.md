# California SB 1186 (20232024) — Fairs: free admissions.

(1) Existing law governs expositions and fairs in this state, including the California ​Exposition and State Fair, county and district fairs, and citrus fruit fairs. Existing law defines courtesy pass admission as an admission without payment of the admission charge to any state, county, district, or citrus fruit fair, except, among other things, admission of any military personnel in uniform.
