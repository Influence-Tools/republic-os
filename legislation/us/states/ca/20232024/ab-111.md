# California AB 111 (20232024) — Personal Income Tax Law: exclusion: student loan debt: discharge of fees: higher education emergency grants.

(1) The Personal Income Tax Law, in modified conformity with federal income tax law, generally defines "gross income" as income from whatever source derived, except as specifically excluded, and provides various exclusions from gross income. Existing law authorizes the Chancellor of the California Community Colleges to discharge unpaid fees due or owed by a student to a community college district.
