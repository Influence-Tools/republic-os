# California SCR 33 (20232024) — Relative to Hereditary and Genetic Cancer Awareness Day.

This measure would declare May 3, 2023, and May 3 of each year thereafter, as Hereditary and Genetic Cancer Awareness Day, and encourage the residents of California to join together in raising awareness and heightening public knowledge of these hereditary and genetic mutations and resulting cancers.
