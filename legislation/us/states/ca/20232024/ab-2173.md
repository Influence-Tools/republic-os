# California AB 2173 (20232024) — Special education: emotional disability.

Existing federal law defines the term "emotional disturbance" for purposes of the federal Individuals with Disabilities Education Act. Existing state law uses the term "emotional disturbance" for certain purposes relating to the provision of special education services.
