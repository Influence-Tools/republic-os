# California SB 1405 (20232024) — Community service districts: Lake Arrowhead Community Services District: covenants, conditions, and restrictions.

The Community Services District Law authorizes the establishment of community services districts to provide various services to the geographic area within each district. That law further authorizes specified community services districts to enforce certain covenants, conditions, and restrictions within those districts, including authorizing the Lake Arrowhead Community Services District to enforce all or part of the covenants, conditions, and restrictions for a tract, and assume the duties of the Arrowhead Woods Architectural Committee, as provided.
