# California AB 476 (20232024) — Outdoor advertising displays: exemptions.

The Outdoor Advertising Act provides for the Department of Transportation to regulate advertising displays, as defined, within view of public highways. The act exempts from its provisions advertising displays used exclusively for specified purposes, including advertising displays to advertise the sale, lease, or exchange of real property upon which the advertising display is placed.
