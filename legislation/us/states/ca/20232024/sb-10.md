# California SB 10 (20232024) — Pupil health: opioid overdose prevention and treatment: Melanie's Law.

(1) Existing law authorizes a public or private elementary or secondary school to determine whether or not to make emergency naloxone hydrochloride or another opioid antagonist and trained personnel available at its school, and to designate one or more volunteers to receive related training to address an opioid overdose, as specified.
