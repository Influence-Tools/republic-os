# California SCR 64 (20232024) — Relative to Elder and Dependent Adult Abuse Awareness Month.

This measure would proclaim and acknowledge the month of June 2023 as Elder and Dependent Adult Abuse Awareness Month in California and would reiterate the importance of annually recognizing Elder and Dependent Adult Abuse Awareness Month in the state.
