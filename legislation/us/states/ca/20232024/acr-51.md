# California ACR 51 (20232024) — Relative to Donate Life/DMV Partnership Month.

This measure would proclaim the month of April 2023 as Donate Life/DMV Partnership Month in California and would encourage all Californians to register with the Donate Life California Organ & Tissue Donor Registry.
