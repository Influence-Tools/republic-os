# California SCR 80 (20232024) — Relative to Childhood Cancer Awareness Month.

This measure would declare the month of September 2024 as Childhood Cancer Awareness Month and state the commitment of the Legislature to support efforts to find cures, and less toxic treatments, for childhood cancer.
