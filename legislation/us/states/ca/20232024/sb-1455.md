# California SB 1455 (20232024) — Contractors: licensing.

(1) Existing law, the Contractors State License Law, establishes the Contractors State License Board within the Department of Consumer Affairs for the licensure and regulation of contractors. Existing law requires the board to appoint a registrar of contractors and fix the registrar's compensation, as specified.
