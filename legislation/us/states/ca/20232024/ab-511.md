# California AB 511 (20232024) — Personal income taxes: voluntary contributions: California ALS Research Network Voluntary Tax Contribution Fund.

Existing law authorizes an individual to contribute amounts in excess of personal income tax liability for the support of specified funds, including, for specified taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2008, and before January 1, 2013, an ALS/Lou Gehrig's Disease Research Fund to provide research grants to develop and advance the understanding, techniques, and modalities effective in the prevention, treatment, and cure of ALS.
