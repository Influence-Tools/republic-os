# California AB 1316 (20232024) — Emergency services: psychiatric emergency medical conditions.

Existing law, the Lanterman-Petris-Short Act, provides for the involuntary commitment and treatment of a person who is a danger to themselves or others or who is gravely disabled, as defined.
