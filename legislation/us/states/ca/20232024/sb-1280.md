# California SB 1280 (20232024) — Waste management: propane cylinders: reusable or refillable.

Existing law, the California Integrated Waste Management Act of 1989, establishes the Department of Resources Recycling and Recovery and requires the department to adopt rules and regulations, as necessary, to carry out the act.
