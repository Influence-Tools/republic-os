# California AJR 14 (20232024) — Federal homelessness funding.

This measure would request that the United States Secretary of Housing and Urban Development revisit the formula used to allocate federal homelessness dollars to local continuums of care and housing authorities to more equitably support communities with the highest rates of homelessness.
