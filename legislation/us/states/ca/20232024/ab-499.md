# California AB 499 (20232024) — Los Angeles County Metropolitan Transportation Authority: job order contracting: pilot program.

Existing law, the Local Agency Public Construction Act, sets forth procedures that a local agency is required to follow when procuring certain services or work. Existing law also sets forth specific public contracting requirements for certain transit districts. Existing law authorizes certain local agencies to engage in job order contracting, as prescribed.
