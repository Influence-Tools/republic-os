# California ACR 210 (20232024) — Conservation: Marine Protected Areas.

This measure would call upon the Natural Resources Agency, the Ocean Protection Council, the Fish and Game Commission, and the Department of Fish and Wildlife to prioritize, as supported by science, public process, and the adaptive management process, the expansion of California's Marine Protected Area Network following its first Decadal Management Review to achieve the state's 30x30 marine conservation goals.
