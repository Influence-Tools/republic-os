# California AB 507 (20232024) — Presidential electors.

Existing federal law requires a state's electors for the offices of President and Vice President of the United States to meet and vote on the first Tuesday after the second Wednesday in December immediately following their appointment at a location specified by the legislature of that state. Existing state law requires California's presidential electors to assemble at the State Capitol for this purpose.
