# California AB 1854 (20232024) — Service member protections.

Existing law authorizes a member of the United States Army Reserve or the National Guard who is called to active duty, as specified, to defer payments on certain obligations while serving on active duty. Existing law requires the reservist or the reservist's designee to deliver a written request for the deferral to the obligor in order to receive a deferral.
