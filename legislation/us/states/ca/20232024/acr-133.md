# California ACR 133 (20232024) — Relative to Missing and Murdered Indigenous People Awareness Month.

This measure would designate the month of May 2024 as Missing and Murdered Indigenous People Awareness Month in California and urge the Governor to declare a state of emergency due to the crisis of missing and murdered indigenous people.
