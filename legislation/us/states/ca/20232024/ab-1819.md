# California AB 1819 (20232024) — Enhanced infrastructure financing districts: public capital facilities: wildfires.

Existing law authorizes the legislative body of a city or a county to establish an enhanced infrastructure financing district to finance public capital facilities or other specified projects of communitywide significance. Existing law authorizes the district's governing board to issue, by majority vote, bonds, as specified.
