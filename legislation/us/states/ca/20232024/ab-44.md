# California AB 44 (20232024) — California Law Enforcement Telecommunications System: tribal police.

Existing law establishes the California Law Enforcement Telecommunications System (CLETS) within the Department of Justice to facilitate the exchange and dissemination of information between law enforcement agencies in the state.
