# California ACR 205 (20232024) — Portuguese Heritage Month: Day of Portugal: Day of the Azores.

This measure would declare the month of June 2024 to be Portuguese Heritage Month and would recognize June 10 as the Day of Portugal and May 20 as the Day of the Azores.
