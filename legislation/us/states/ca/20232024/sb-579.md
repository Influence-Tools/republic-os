# California SB 579 (20232024) — Fish: Annual Provisional Stocking Document.

Existing law establishes the Department of Fish and Wildlife and prescribes the functions and responsibilities of the department with regard to the implementation, administration, and enforcement of laws regulating fish and wildlife in the state.
