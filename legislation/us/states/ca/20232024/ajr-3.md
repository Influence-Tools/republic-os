# California AJR 3 (20232024) — Affordable Housing Credit Improvement Act of 2023.

This measure would declare that the Legislature supports a reduction to the threshold for tax-exempt bond financing and joins the Treasurer in urging the passage of the federal Affordable Housing Credit Improvement Act of 2023.
