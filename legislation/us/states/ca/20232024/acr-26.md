# California ACR 26 (20232024) — California Down Syndrome Awareness Week and Day.

This measure would proclaim March 19, 2023, to March 25, 2023, as California Down Syndrome Awareness Week and March 21, 2023, as California Down Syndrome Day, and would encourage all Californians to support and participate in related activities.
