# California ACR 1 (20232024 Special Session 1) — Relative to final adjournment of the 2023–24 First Extraordinary Session of the Legislature.

This measure would provide that the 2023–24 First Extraordinary Session of the Legislature shall stand adjourned sine die on the date that this measure has been adopted by both houses of the Legislature.
