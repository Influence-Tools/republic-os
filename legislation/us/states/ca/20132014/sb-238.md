# California SB 238 (20132014) — Santa Monica Mountains Conservancy members: voting.

Existing law, the Santa Monica Mountains Conservancy Act, establishes the Santa Monica Mountains Conservancy and prescribes the membership, functions, and duties of the conservancy with regard to the protection and preservation of lands within the Santa Monica Mountains Zone, as defined. Existing law requires the conservancy be composed of 9 voting members and 3 ex officio members, as specified, and requires the Supervisor of the Angeles National Forest to serve as an ex officio, nonvoting member of the conservancy.
