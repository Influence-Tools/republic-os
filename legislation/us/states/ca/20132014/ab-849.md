# California AB 849 (20132014) — Protection of victims: address confidentiality.

Existing law authorizes victims of domestic violence, sexual assault, or stalking to complete an application in person at a communityâ€‘based victims' assistance program to be approved by the Secretary of State for the purpose of enabling state and local agencies to respond to requests for public records without disclosing a program participant's residence address contained in any public record and otherwise provide for confidentiality of identity for that person, subject to specified conditions. Any person who makes a false statement in an application is guilty of a misdemeanor.
