# California SB 419 (20132014) — Restitution: collection of fines, fees, and orders.

(1) Existing law requires the court to order criminal defendants to pay restitution to the victim or victims as well as a fine. Existing law makes any portion of a restitution fine or restitution fee that remains unsatisfied after a defendant is no longer on probation, parole, postrelease community supervision, or mandatory supervision, or after the defendant has completed diversion enforceable by the California Victim Compensation and Government Claims Board, as specified, and authorizes a local collection program to continue to enforce restitution orders once a defendant is no longer on probation, postrelease community supervision, or mandatory supervision.
