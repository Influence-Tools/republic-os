# California AB 1405 (20132014) — Subversive Organization Registration Law: repeal.

Existing law, the Subversive Organization Registration Law, requires the registration of subversive organizations which are conceived and exist for the purpose of undermining and eventually destroying the democratic form of government in this state and in the United States.
