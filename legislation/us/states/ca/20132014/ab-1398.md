# California AB 1398 (20132014) — Solid waste: recycling: enforcement agencies.

(1) The California Integrated Waste Management Act of 1989 (act) requires a business, which is defined as a commercial or public entity, that generates more than 4 cubic yards of commercial solid waste per week or is a multifamily residential dwelling of 5 units or more, to arrange for recycling services. Existing law also requires jurisdictions to implement a commercial solid waste recycling program meeting specified elements. Existing law defines commercial solid waste by reference to a specified regulation.
