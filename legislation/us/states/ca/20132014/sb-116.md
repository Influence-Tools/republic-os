# California SB 116 (20132014) — Personal income taxes: voluntary contributions: Emergency Food Assistance Program.

The Personal Income Tax Law allows taxpayers, until January 1, 2014, to designate on their tax returns that a specified amount in excess of their tax liability be contributed to the Emergency Food Assistance Program, unless repealed earlier for failure to meet annual minimum contribution amounts.
