# California SB 960 (20132014) — Pharmacy.

Existing law, the Pharmacy Law, provides for the regulation and licensure of pharmacists by the California State Board of Pharmacy. Existing law authorizes the executive officer of the board, or his or her designee, to issue a letter of admonishment to a licensee for the failure to comply with specified requirements. Existing law requires a letter of admonishment to be in writing, and sets forth procedures by which the licensee may contest or comply with a letter of admonishment.
