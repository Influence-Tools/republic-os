# California AB 1382 (20132014) — Reporting.

Existing law requires certain health facilities and freestanding ambulatory surgery clinics to file, with the Office of Statewide Health Planning and Development, specified reports containing various patient and health data information, including principal language spoken by the patient and the external cause of injury.
