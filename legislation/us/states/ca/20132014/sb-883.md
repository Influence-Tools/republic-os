# California SB 883 (20132014) — West Contra Costa Healthcare District.

Existing law provides for the formation of local health care districts and specifies district powers. Existing law establishes the Major Risk Medical Insurance Fund in the State Treasury and continuously appropriates moneys in the fund, except as specified, to the State Department of Health Care Services for purposes of the California Major Risk Medical Insurance Program.
