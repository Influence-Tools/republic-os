# California SB 98 (20132014) — Public health.

Existing law imposes limits on the amount of income and personal and real property an individual or family may possess in order to be eligible for public aid, including under the CalFresh program, and specifies the allowable value of a licensed vehicle retained by an applicant for, or recipient of, that aid.
