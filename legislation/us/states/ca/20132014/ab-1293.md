# California AB 1293 (20132014) — Courts.

The Uniform Civil Fees and Standard Fee Schedule Act of 2005 establishes a uniform schedule of filing fees and other civil fees for the superior courts throughout the state. The act establishes certain uniform filing fees for probate proceedings, including, among others, the first petition for letters of administration or letters testamentary.
