# California SB 1462 (20132014) — Local government: omnibus bill.

(1) Existing law requires a fictitious business name statement to be filed with the clerk of the county in which a registrant has his or her principal place of business in this state or, if a registrant has no place of business in this state, with the Clerk of Sacramento County.
