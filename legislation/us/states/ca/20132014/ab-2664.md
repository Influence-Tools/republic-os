# California AB 2664 (20132014) — California National Guard.

Existing law authorizes the Adjutant General and the Military Department to solicit and accept funds or other donations for deposit in the California Military Department Support Fund. Money in the fund is available, upon appropriation, for specified purposes relating to the programs of the department.
