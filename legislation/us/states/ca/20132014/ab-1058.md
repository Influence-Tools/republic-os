# California AB 1058 (20132014) — San Diego County Regional Airport Authority.

(1) Existing law provides for the formation and functioning of city selection committees in any county in which 2 or more cities are incorporated, for the purpose of appointing city representatives to boards, commissions, and agencies as required by law.
