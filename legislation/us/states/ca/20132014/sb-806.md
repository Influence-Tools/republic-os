# California SB 806 (20132014) — Department of Motor Vehicles: license plate alternatives pilot program.

Existing law authorizes the Department of Motor Vehicles to issue license plates for vehicles, as specified, and to issue one or more stickers, tabs, or other suitable devices in lieu of the license plates, as specified.
