# California SB 1335 (20132014) — Income and corporation taxes: credits: information.

Existing law imposes various taxes and allows specified credits, deductions, exclusions, and exemptions in computing those taxes. Existing law limits the collection and use of taxpayer information and provides that any unauthorized use of this information is punishable as a misdemeanor.
