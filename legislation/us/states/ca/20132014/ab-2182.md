# California AB 2182 (20132014) — Alcoholic beverage control: winegrowers: wine sales event permits.

The Alcoholic Beverage Control Act authorizes the issuance of a wine sales event permit to any licensee under a winegrower's license, which authorizes the sale of bottled wine produced by the winegrowers at specified events, and is valid for a maximum of 5 consecutive days during the event period.
