# California AB 2755 (20132014) — Nonprofit corporations: directors.

The Nonprofit Corporation Law, among other things, regulates the organization and operation of nonprofit public benefit corporations, nonprofit mutual benefit corporations, and nonprofit religious corporations and defines terms for its purposes, including, but not limited to, the term "director."
