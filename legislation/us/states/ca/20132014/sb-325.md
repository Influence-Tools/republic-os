# California SB 325 (20132014) — Trustees of the California State University: student members.

Existing law establishes the California State University as a segment of the public postsecondary education system, establishes the Trustees of the California State University as the governing board of the university, and sets forth its membership, including, but not limited to, 2 student members who have at least junior year standing.
