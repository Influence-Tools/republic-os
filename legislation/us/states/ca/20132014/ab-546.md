# California AB 546 (20132014) — Local government: consolidation of offices.

Existing law authorizes the board of supervisors in Mendocino County, Sonoma County, Trinity County, and Tulare County to consolidate the duties of the offices of Auditor-Controller and Treasurer-Tax Collector into the elected office of Auditor-Controller-Treasurer-Tax Collector.
