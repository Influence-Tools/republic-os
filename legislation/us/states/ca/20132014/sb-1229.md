# California SB 1229 (20132014) — Week of the School Administrator.

Existing law provides that every first full week in the month of March of each year shall be designated as "Week of the School Administrator," and encourages schools, school districts, and county superintendents of schools to observe the week with public recognition of the contribution of school administrators, as specified.
