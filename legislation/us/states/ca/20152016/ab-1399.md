# California AB 1399 (20152016) — Income taxes: voluntary contributions: California Domestic Violence Victims Fund.

Existing law authorizes an individual to contribute amounts in excess of his or her tax liability for the support of specified funds. Existing law also has administrative provisions applicable to voluntary contributions.
