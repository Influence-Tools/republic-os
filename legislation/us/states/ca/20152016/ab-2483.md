# California AB 2483 (20152016) — Livestock Identification Advisory Board.

Existing law creates the Livestock Identification Advisory Board, and prescribes the membership, functions, and duties of the board. Existing law requires the board to meet at the call of its chairperson at least once in every 3 months.
