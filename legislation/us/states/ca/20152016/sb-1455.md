# California SB 1455 (20152016) — Pupil enrollment: military dependents.

Existing law requires persons between 6 and 18 years of age to attend a public school within the school district in which the pupil's parent or legal guardian resides, unless otherwise exempted. Existing law provides that a pupil complies with a school district's residency requirements for school attendance in that school district if the pupil meets one of the specified requirements.
