# California AB 346 (20152016) — Vehicle infractions and misdemeanors: arrests.

Existing law requires that whenever a person is arrested for a vehicle-related infraction or misdemeanor, he or she be taken immediately before a magistrate if he or she fails to present his or her driver's license or other satisfactory evidence of identity for examination.
