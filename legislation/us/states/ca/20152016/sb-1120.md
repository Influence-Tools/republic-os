# California SB 1120 (20152016) — Director of General Services: state medical facilities: leases.

(1) Existing law authorizes the Director of General Services, with the consent of the State Department of State Hospitals, to lease specified property not exceeding 10 acres located within the Napa State Hospital to a public governmental agency for the purpose of conducting its trainable mentally retarded program consistent with specified requirements.
