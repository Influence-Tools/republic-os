# California SB 822 (20152016) — Agricultural pest control: citrus disease prevention: monthly assessment.

Existing law creates the California Citrus Pest and Disease Prevention Committee in the Department of Food and Agriculture to, among other things, develop a statewide citrus specific pest and disease work plan.
