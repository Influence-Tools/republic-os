# California SB 1513 (20112012) — State Compensation Insurance Fund: investments.

Existing law requires the board of directors of the State Compensation Insurance Fund to invest and reinvest, from time to time, all moneys in the State Compensation Insurance Fund in excess of current requirements in the same manner as is authorized in certain provisions applicable to private insurance carriers. Existing law prohibits the board from investing or reinvesting in certain investments, including real estate and call options on common stock.
