# California SB 646 (20112012) — Toxics: enforcement: lead and cadmium jewelry.

(1) Existing law prohibits the manufacturing, shipping, selling, or offering for sale of jewelry, as defined, for retail sale in the state, unless the jewelry is made entirely from specified materials. Existing law also prohibits any person from taking those actions with regard to children's jewelry, as defined, unless the children's jewelry is made entirely of specified materials. Existing law, for purposes of those provisions, defines among other terms, "amended consent judgment" and "jewelry."
