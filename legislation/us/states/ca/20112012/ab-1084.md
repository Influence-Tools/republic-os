# California AB 1084 (20112012) — Veterans' farm and home purchases: shared equity cooperative housing.

Existing law provides for farm and home purchase benefits for qualifying veterans under the Veterans' Farm and Home Purchase Act of 1943, and subsequent acts, which are collectively referred to as the CalVet Home Loan program. Existing law defines "cooperative housing corporation" for purposes of this program to mean a real estate development in which membership in the corporation, by stock, is coupled with the exclusive right to possess a portion of the real property.
