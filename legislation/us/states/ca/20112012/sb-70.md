# California SB 70 (20112012) — Education finance: Budget Act of 2011.

(1) Existing law requires the county superintendent of schools of each county, among other specified duties, to make annual visits to each school in his or her county to observe its operation and to learn of its problems. Existing law requires that the priority objective of those visits is the determination of whether each school has sufficient textbooks, as defined. Existing law, until July 1, 2013, and for the 2008â€“09 to the 2012â€“13 fiscal years, inclusive, describes what is meant by sufficient textbooks or instructional materials for purposes of these visits by the county superintendent of schools.
