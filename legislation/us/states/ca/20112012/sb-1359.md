# California SB 1359 (20112012) — Personal income taxes: contributions: California Breast Cancer Research Fund: California Cancer Research Fund.

Personal Income Tax Law allows taxpayers, until January 1, 2013, to designate on their tax returns that a specified amount in excess of their tax liability be contributed to the California Breast Cancer Research Fund.
