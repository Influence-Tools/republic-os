# California AB 1222 (20112012) — California Housing Finance Agency.

Existing law establishes the California Housing Finance Authority, administered by a board of directors, whose primary purpose is to meet the housing needs of persons and families of low- or moderate-income. Existing law requires that the board establish the compensation of the executive director of the agency and other key exempt management, not to exceed a specified amount, and that the agency conduct salary surveys to determine the compensation.
