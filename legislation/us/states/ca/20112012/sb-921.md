# California SB 921 (20112012) — Military Department: Office of the Inspector General: California Military Whistleblower Protection Act.

Existing law establishes in state government the Military Department, which includes the office of the Adjutant General, the State Military Reserve, the California Cadet Corps, and the Naval Militia. There is also, within the Military Department, the inspector general, an office that inspects, audits, investigates, trains, and performs various duties necessary to support the mission of the Military Department.
