# California AB 2297 (20112012) — California Retail Food Code: skilled nursing facilities: intermediate care facilities for the developmentally disabled.

Existing law, the California Retail Food Code, provides for the regulation of health and sanitation standards for retail food facilities, as defined, by the State Department of Public Health and is primarily enforced by local health agencies. A violation of any provision of the code is a misdemeanor.
