# California SB 679 (20112012) — Energy: energy conservation projects: financial assistance: local governments and public institutions.

The Energy Conservation Assistance Act of 1979 requires, until January 1, 2013, the State Energy Resources Conservation and Development Commission to provide loans to eligible institutions, including local governments and public institutions, to finance all or a portion of the costs incurred in implementing projects related to energy conservation.
