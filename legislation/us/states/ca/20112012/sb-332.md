# California SB 332 (20112012) — Rental dwellings: smoking.

Existing law regulates the terms and conditions of residential tenancies. Existing law authorizes the creation of antitobacco use programs for school-age populations and prohibits any person from smoking a cigarette, cigar, or other tobacco-related product, or from disposing of cigarette butts, cigar butts, or any other tobacco-related waste, within a playground.
