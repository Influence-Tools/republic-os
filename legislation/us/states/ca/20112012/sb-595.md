# California SB 595 (20112012) — Tidelands and submerged lands: removal of vessels.

Existing law authorizes any peace officer, as described, any employee or officer of the State Lands Commission designated by the commission, or any lifeguard or marine safety officer employed by a county, city, or district, while engaged in the performance of official duties, to remove, and, if necessary, store a vessel, as defined, removed from a public waterway under specified circumstances.
