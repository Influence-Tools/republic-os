# California AB 1307 (20112012) — State Board of Equalization: administration: collections.

The Contractors' State License Law provides for the licensure and regulation of contractors by the Contractors' State License Board in the Department of Consumer Affairs. Existing law provides that the registrar of the Contractors' State License Board may refuse to issue, reinstate, reactivate, or renew a license or may suspend the license of a contractor for the failure of the licensee to resolve all outstanding final liabilities, which include taxes, additions to tax, penalties, interest, and any fees that may be assessed by the board, the Department of Industrial Relations, the Employment Development Department, or the Franchise Tax Board.
