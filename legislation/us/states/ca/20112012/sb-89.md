# California SB 89 (20112012) — Vehicles: vehicle license fee and registration fee.

(1) Existing law requires the amount appropriated by the Legislature for the use of the Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) and the Franchise Tax Board (FTB) for the enforcement of the Vehicle License Fee Law to be calculated as specified and transferred from the Motor Vehicle License Fee Account in the Transportation Tax Fund to the Motor Vehicle Account in the State Transportation Fund.
