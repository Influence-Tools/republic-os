# California AB 2548 (20112012) — California Veterans Board.

Existing law provides that the California Veterans Board consists of 7 members appointed by the Governor subject to Senate confirmation. All members of the board are required to be veterans, and certain members are required to have meet specific criteria with regard to their background, education, training, experience, or expertise.
