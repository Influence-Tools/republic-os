# California SB 532 (20112012) — Pupil instruction: high school curriculum: advanced placement courses: International Baccalaureate Diploma Program.

(1) Existing law contains legislative findings and declarations stating that advanced placement courses, among other things, help to improve the overall curriculum at schools where they are provided and provide a cost-effective means for high school pupils to obtain college-level coursework experience.
