# California SB 1075 (20112012) — Uniform Electronic Legal Material Act.

Existing law establishes the Legislative Counsel Bureau and provides that the chief of the bureau is the Legislative Counsel. Existing law requires the Legislative Counsel, with the advice of the Assembly Committee on Rules and the Senate Committee on Rules, to make specified legislative materials, bills, codes, and statutes available to the public in electronic form.
