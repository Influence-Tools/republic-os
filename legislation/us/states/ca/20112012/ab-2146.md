# California AB 2146 (20112012) — Political Reform Act of 1974: local campaign reform: County of San Bernardino.

The Political Reform Act of 1974 establishes the Fair Political Practices Commission, which has the primary responsibility for the impartial, effective administration and implementation of the act, including acting as the civil prosecutor responsible for the enforcement of the penalty provisions of the act.
