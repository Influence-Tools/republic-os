# California AB 2686 (20112012) — Franchise Tax Board: Taxpayers' Rights Advocate.

The Katz-Harris Taxpayers' Bill of Rights Act establishes the position of Taxpayers' Rights Advocate and provides specified protections for taxpayers for purposes of, among other things, determining their correct tax liability.
