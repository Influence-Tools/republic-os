# California SB 741 (20112012) — Alternative Protest Pilot Project.

Existing law provides for the Alternative Protest Pilot Project, which provides for alternative protest procedures in connection with state agency acquisition of goods and services, including the acquisition of information technology goods and services, and requires the Department of General Services to administer the project until December 31, 2011.
