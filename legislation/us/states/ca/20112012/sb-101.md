# California SB 101 (20112012) — Athlete agents: conflicts of interest.

Existing law, the Miller-Ayala Athlete Agents Act, the violation of which is a crime, regulates specified activities of an athlete agent in representing or seeking to represent student athletes and professional athletes. Under this act, an agent is prohibited from having an ownership or financial interest in any entity that is directly involved in the same sport as a person the agent represents, as specified.
