# California AB 2475 (20112012) — Military service protections: real and personal property rights.

Existing law provides legal rights, as specified, for service members in regard to court proceedings, interest liabilities, contracts, eviction proceedings, leases, tax and assessments, life insurance policies, and health insurance.
