# California AB 981 (20112012) — California Pollution Control Financing Authority: Capital Access Loan Program.

(1) Existing law establishes the Capital Access Loan Program for small businesses, administered by the California Pollution Control Financing Authority (authority) , which provides loans through participating financial institutions to qualifying small businesses.
