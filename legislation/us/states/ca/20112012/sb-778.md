# California SB 778 (20112012) — Alcoholic beverages licensees: contests and sweepstakes.

The Alcoholic Beverage Control Act prohibits any licensee from giving any premium, gift, or free goods in connection with the sale or distribution of any alcoholic beverage, except as provided.
