# California AB 29 (20112012) — Governor's Office of Business and Economic Development.

Existing law provides for various state programs and services for the purpose of attracting and retaining businesses in the state. Existing law creates the Office of Small Business Advocate in the Office of Planning and Research in the Governor's office.
