# California AB 1847 (20112012) — City of Long Beach: grant of public trust lands.

(1) Existing law grants to the City of Long Beach specified tide and submerged lands, bordering upon and under the Pacific Ocean or along the coastline within the territory of the City of Long Beach, subject to specified conditions.
