# California SB 12 (20112012) — Vehicles: aerodynamic devices.

Existing law provides that the total outside width of any vehicle or its load shall not exceed 102 inches, except door handles, hinges, cable cinchers, chain binders, and holders for the display of placards warning of hazardous materials may extend 3 inches on each side of the vehicle.
