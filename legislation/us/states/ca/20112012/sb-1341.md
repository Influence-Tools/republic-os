# California SB 1341 (20112012) — Corporation Tax Law: charitable corporations: exemptions: revocation.

Existing law provides that no exemption shall be allowed for a charitable corporation that fails to file required registration or periodic reports with the Attorney General. Existing law provides that after the Attorney General has notified the Franchise Tax Board in writing that a charitable corporation has failed to file required registration or periodic reports, the exemption will be disallowed and may only be reinstated when the registration or periodic reports are filed. Existing law further provides that a charitable corporation whose exemption is disallowed must pay the minimum tax, as provided, for any year or years its exemption was disallowed.
