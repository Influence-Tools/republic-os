# California SB 339 (20112012) — Alcoholic beverage control: on-sale beer and wine licenses: bona fide public eating place: public schoolhouses.

(1) The Alcoholic Beverage Control Act authorizes the issuance of an on-sale beer and wine eating place license, which authorizes the sale of beer and wine for consumption on or off the licensed premises, provided the licensee maintains the licensed premises as a bona fide public eating place, as defined, and meets other specified requirements.
