# California SB 874 (20112012) — School districts: community college districts: parcel taxes: exemptions.

Existing law authorizes any school district to impose qualified special taxes within the district pursuant to specified procedures. Existing law defines qualified special taxes as special taxes that apply uniformly to all taxpayers or all real property within the school district and may include taxes that exempt persons 65 years of age or older or for persons receiving Supplemental Security Income for a disability regardless of age.
