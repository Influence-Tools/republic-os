# California SB 760 (20112012) — Sexually violent predators: evaluations.

Existing law provides for the civil commitment of criminal offenders who have been determined to be sexually violent predators for treatment in a secure state hospital facility, as specified. Under existing law, persons to be evaluated for civil commitment are evaluated by 2 practicing psychiatrists or psychologists, or one practicing psychiatrist and one practicing psychologist, designated by the Director of State Hospitals. If the evaluators agree, then a petition for civil commitment may be filed.
