# California AB 2066 (20112012) — Residential care facilities for the elderly: revocation of licenses.

Existing law provides for the licensure and regulation of residential care facilities for the elderly. These provisions are administered by the State Department of Social Services. Violation of these provisions is a misdemeanor.
