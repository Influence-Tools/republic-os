# California SB 477 (20112012) — School district reorganization: bonded indebtedness: Wiseburn Unified School District: Centinela Valley Union High School District.

(1) Existing law specifies the manner in which bonded indebtedness of a school district on general obligation bonds is reallocated among school districts when school districts are reorganized.
