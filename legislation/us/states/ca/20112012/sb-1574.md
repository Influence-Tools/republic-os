# California SB 1574 (20112012) — Discovery: electronically stored information.

Existing law provides procedures for the production of evidence by subpoena. Existing law provides that a subpoena may require a witness to bring any books, documents, or other things under the witness's control that he or she is bound by law to produce in evidence.
