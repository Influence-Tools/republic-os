# California AB 673 (20112012) — Office of Multicultural Health: LGBT communities.

Existing law establishes the Office of Multicultural Health within the State Department of Public Health. Existing law requires the office to perform various duties on behalf of the State Department of Public Health and the State Department of Health Care Services relating to the state's racial and ethnic communities.
