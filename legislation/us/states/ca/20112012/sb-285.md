# California SB 285 (20112012) — Massage therapy instruction: fraud: criminal prosecutions.

(1) Existing law provides for the certification of massage practitioners and massage therapists by a massage therapy organization and authorizes the legislative body of a city or county to enact ordinances providing for the licensing and regulation of the business of massage when carried on within the city or county.
