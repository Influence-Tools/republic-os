# California AB 1024 (20112012) — Insurance: low-cost automobile insurance: sales.

(1) Existing law establishes within the California Automobile Assigned Risk Plan a low-cost automobile insurance program in order to provide a means for eligible consumers to buy automobile insurance, as specified.
