# California AB 339 (20112012) — Instructional materials: social content reviews: fees.

Existing law requires the State Board of Education in reviewing and adopting instructional materials to use specified criteria and ensure that, in its judgment, the submitted basic instructional materials meet all of the specified criteria, including, but not limited to, compliance with the specified requirements and guidelines for social content.
