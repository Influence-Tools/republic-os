# California AB 901 (20112012) — Economic development: small business.

(1) Existing law establishes the California Economic Development Fund in the State Treasury to receive economic development funds and loan repayment and grant proceeds and authorizes the Secretary of Business, Transportation and Housing, upon appropriation by the Legislature, to expend the fund's revenue to provide matching funds for economic development purposes. Existing law establishes certain definitions in this regard and defines a financial intermediary and a community development intermediary to include, among other things, microenterprise development organizations.
