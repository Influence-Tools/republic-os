# California AB 125 (20112012) — Insurance: guaranteed asset protection.

Existing law defines guaranteed automobile protection (GAP) insurance as insurance in which a person agrees to indemnify a vehicle purchaser or lessee for any of the difference between the actual cash value of the insured's vehicle at the time of an unrecovered theft or total loss and the amount owed on the vehicle pursuant to the terms of a loan, lease agreement, or installment sales contract used to purchase or lease the vehicle.
