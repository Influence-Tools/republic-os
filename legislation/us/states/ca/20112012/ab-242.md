# California AB 242 (20112012) — Taxation.

(1) Existing law requires the State Board of Equalization to reimburse a manufacturer for an amount equal to the sales tax which the manufacturer pays to or for the buyer when providing a replacement vehicle or making restitution pursuant to the Song-Beverly Consumer Warranty Act, subject to satisfactory proof, as specified.
