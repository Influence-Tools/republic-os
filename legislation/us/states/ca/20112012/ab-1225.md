# California AB 1225 (20112012) — Cemeteries: veteran's commemorative property.

Existing law prohibits a cemetery owned and operated by a city, county, or city and county from engaging in the business of selling monuments or markers, and also prohibits the cemetery's officers and employees who manage, operate, or otherwise maintain the cemetery on a day-to-day basis from engaging in the private business of selling monuments or markers.
