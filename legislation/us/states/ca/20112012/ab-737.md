# California AB 737 (20112012) — Boating and Waterways: Harbors and Watercraft Commission.

(1) Existing law establishes the Department of Boating and Waterways in the Natural Resources Agency and charges the department with duties related to beach erosion, harbors, and recreational boat trails. Existing law establishes the Boating and Waterways Commission to provide advice to that department.
