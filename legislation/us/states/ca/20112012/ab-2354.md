# California AB 2354 (20112012) — Travel insurance.

Existing law authorizes the issuance of limited licenses as travel insurance agents for employees of railroads, steamships, airlines, and other organizations engaged in transporting persons as common carriers and to individuals or employees of persons engaged in selling transportation on those common carriers.
