# California AB 571 (20112012) — Corporations: distributions.

Existing law provides for the formation and governance of corporations. Existing law allows a corporation to make a distribution of cash or property to shareholders, including a dividend or a repurchase or redemption of shares, only if either the corporation has retained earnings prior to the distribution equal to or exceeding the amount of the distribution or the corporation can satisfy specified balance sheet test and liquidity requirements after giving effect to the distribution, and the distribution will not render the corporation insolvent.
