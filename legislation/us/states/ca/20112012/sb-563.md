# California SB 563 (20112012) — Common interest developments: meetings.

(1) Existing law provides for the creation of common interest developments and requires that a common interest development be managed by an association that may or may not be incorporated. Existing law prescribes requirements for meetings of the board of directors of the association that manages the development, and requires notice of the time and place of a meeting of the board of directors to be given to the members of the association at least 4 days prior to the meeting, except as specified.
