# California SB 1309 (20112012) — Human resources.

The California Constitution establishes the State Personnel Board (board) and requires the board to, among other things, enforce the civil service statutes, prescribe probationary periods and classifications, adopt rules authorized by statute, and review disciplinary actions. The Constitution also requires the executive officer of the board to administer the civil service statutes under the rules of the board. Existing law established the Department of Personnel Administration for purposes of managing the nonmerit aspects of the state's personnel system.
