# California SB 56 (20112012) — Apprenticeship oversight.

Existing law requires the Division of Apprenticeship Standards within the Department of Industrial Relations to randomly audit all apprenticeship programs during each 5-year period commencing January 1, 2000, to ensure compliance with specified requirements, including any industry-specific training criteria established by the California Apprenticeship Council.
