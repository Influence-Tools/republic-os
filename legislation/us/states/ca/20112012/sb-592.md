# California SB 592 (20112012) — Dairy cattle supply liens.

(1) Existing law provides that a person who provides feed or materials to aid the raising or maintaining of dairy cattle has a lien upon the proceeds of the milk or milk products produced from the dairy cattle, for the reasonable or agreed charges for the feed or materials provided and for the costs of enforcing the lien, not to exceed an amount equal to charges for feed or material provided within a 45-day period.
