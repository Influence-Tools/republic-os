# California AB 1451 (20112012) — High school athletics: California High School Coaching Education and Training Program.

Existing law states the intent of the Legislature to establish a California High School Coaching Education and Training Program, administered by school districts, that emphasizes specified components, including, among other things, training, which is described as certification in cardiopulmonary resuscitation and first aid.
