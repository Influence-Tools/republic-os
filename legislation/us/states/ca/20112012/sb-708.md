# California SB 708 (20112012) — Funds transfers.

Existing provisions of the Commercial Code governing certain funds transfers, cited as the Uniform Commercial Codeâ€”Funds Transfers, provide, among other provisions, that a funds transfer is completed by acceptance by the beneficiary's bank of a payment order for the benefit of the beneficiary of the originator's payment order. Existing law excludes from the provisions of the Uniform Commercial Codeâ€”Funds Transfers those funds transfers governed by the federal Electronic Fund Transfer Act of 1978.
