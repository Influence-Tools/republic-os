# California AB 2269 (20112012) — Pupil instruction: Labor History Month.

Existing law deems the first week of April to be Labor History Week and encourages school districts to commemorate that week with appropriate educational exercises that make pupils aware of the role that the labor movement has played in shaping California and the United States.
