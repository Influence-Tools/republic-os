# California AB 2184 (20112012) — Alcoholic beverages: tied-house restrictions.

Existing law, known as tied-house restrictions, prohibits specified licensees from furnishing, giving, or lending money or other thing of value, directly or indirectly, to a person engaged in operating, owning, or maintaining an off-sale licensed premises.
