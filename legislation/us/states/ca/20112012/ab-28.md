# California AB 28 (20112012) — Governmental reorganization.

(1) Existing law establishes the Office for Citizen Initiative and Voluntary Action within the office of the Governor, and grants it certain powers and duties for the purpose of advocating and encouraging citizen initiative and volunteer action.
