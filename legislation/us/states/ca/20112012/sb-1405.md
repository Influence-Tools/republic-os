# California SB 1405 (20112012) — Accountancy: military service: practice privilege.

Existing law provides for the licensure and regulation of the practice of accountancy by the California Board of Accountancy within the Department of Consumer Affairs. The department is under the control of the Director of Consumer Affairs.
