# California SB 1210 (20112012) — Collection of criminal fines and penalties.

(1) Under existing law, in every case where a person is convicted of a crime and whose sentence includes parole, the court is required to assess a parole revocation fine, as specified, for deposit into the Restitution Fund in the State Treasury.
