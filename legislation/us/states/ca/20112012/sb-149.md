# California SB 149 (20112012) — Mobilehome and special occupancy parks: permit invoice: notice.

The Mobilehome Parks Act generally regulates various classifications of mobilehome and related vehicle parks. The Special Occupancy Parks Act generally regulates special occupancy parks. Those acts require any person prior to operating a manufactured housing community, mobilehome park, or special occupancy park to obtain a valid permit that is issued by the enforcement agency, as specified. Existing law requires that the permit be issued and invoiced according to a method and schedule established by the Department of Housing and Community Development.
