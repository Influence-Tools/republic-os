# California SB 1018 (20112012) — Public resources.

(1) Existing law establishes the Office of Education and the Environment in the California Environmental Protection Agency to implement the statewide environmental educational program and, in cooperation with the State Department of Education and the State Board of Education, develop and implement a unified education strategy on the environment for elementary and secondary schools in the state.
