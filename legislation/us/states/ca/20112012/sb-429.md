# California SB 429 (20112012) — Before and after school programs: After School Education and Safety Program: supplemental grants.

Existing law provides that every school that establishes a before school program component pursuant to the After School Education and Safety Program is eligible to receive a 3-year renewable direct grant, as specified, and is eligible to receive a supplemental grant to operate the program in excess of 180 schooldays during any combination of summer, intersession, or vacation periods, as specified.
