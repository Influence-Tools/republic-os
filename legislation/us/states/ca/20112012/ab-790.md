# California AB 790 (20112012) — Career technical education: Linked Learning Pilot Program.

Existing law requires the Superintendent of Public Instruction to develop, in conjunction with specified persons and entities, a report that explores the feasibility of expanding and establishing career multiple pathway programs, as defined, in California.
