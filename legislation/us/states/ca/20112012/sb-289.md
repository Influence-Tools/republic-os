# California SB 289 (20112012) — Clinical laboratory techniques: training and instruction.

Existing law provides for the licensure and regulation of clinical laboratories and various clinical laboratory health care professionals by the State Department of Public Health. Existing law authorizes the department to approve schools seeking to provide instruction in clinical laboratory techniques, as specified.
