# California SB 562 (20112012) — Housing omnibus bill.

(1) Existing law authorizes the California Debt Limit Allocation Committee to require any issuer making an application to the committee or the California Tax Credit Allocation Committee for allocation of a portion of the state ceiling, as defined, to make a deposit of up to 1% of the portion requested. If an allocation is given, the committee is required to keep the deposit, in proportion to the amount of allocation given, until bonds are issued. If bonds are not issued prior to the expiration of the allocation, the committee is required to keep the deposit, unless the committee determines there is good cause to return all or part of the deposit.
