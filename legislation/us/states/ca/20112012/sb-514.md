# California SB 514 (20112012) — Dextromethorphan: sale to minors prohibited.

Existing law prohibits a manufacturer, wholesaler, retailer, or other person from selling, transferring, or otherwise furnishing a specified substance, including ephedrine and pseudoephedrine, to a person under 18 years of age, except as specified. A first violation of this provision is a misdemeanor. Existing law further regulates the sale of nonprescription drugs, as specified.
