# California AB 619 (20112012) — Massage therapy.

(1) Existing law, until January 1, 2015, provides for the voluntary certification of massage practitioners and massage therapists by a nonprofit Massage Therapy Organization, as defined. Existing law requires the initial board of directors to, among other things, establish the organization.
