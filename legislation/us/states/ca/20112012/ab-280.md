# California AB 280 (20112012) — Horse racing: California-bred horses and parimutuel tickets.

Under existing law, the California Horse Racing Board is responsible for adopting rules and regulations for the protection of the public and the control of horse racing and parimutuel wagering, as well as enforcing all laws, rules, and regulations dealing with horse racing and parimutuel wagering.
