# California SB 718 (20112012) — Elder and dependent adult abuse: mandated reporting.

Existing law requires specified people, known as mandated reporters, to report cases of elder or dependent adult abuse, as defined. Existing law requires mandated reporters, after reporting the abuse by telephone, to send written reports to specified entities containing prescribed information.
