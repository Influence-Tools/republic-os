# California SB 220 (20112012) — Life insurance: group policies.

Existing law provides that coverage for unmarried dependent children under a group life insurance policy may continue through 20 years of age, or through 24 years of age if the dependent child is attending an educational institution, or may continue for a child 21 years of age or older who is both incapable of self-sustaining employment by reason of mental retardation or physical handicap.
