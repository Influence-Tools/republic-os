# California AB 1338 (20112012) — Redevelopment.

The Community Redevelopment Law authorizes the establishment of redevelopment agencies in communities to address the effects of blight, as defined, in blighted areas in those communities known as project areas. Existing law authorizes an agency to, within a survey area or for purposes of development, purchase, lease, obtain, or acquire any real or personal property and to acquire real property by eminent domain.
