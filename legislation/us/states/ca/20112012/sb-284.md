# California SB 284 (20112012) — Real property: marketable title.

Existing law permits a person to record an option to purchase real property in the chain of title for that property. Existing law provides that the option expires of record, within specified times, if no conveyance, contract, or other instrument giving notice of the exercise or extension of the option is recorded.
