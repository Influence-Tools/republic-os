# California SB 1577 (20112012) — Resources: public trust lands: City of Newport Beach.

(1) Existing law grants to the City of Newport Beach all tide and submerged lands, whether filled or unfilled, bordering upon and under the Pacific Ocean or Newport Bay, as described, subject to specified conditions.
