# California AB 561 (20112012) — Ventura County Watershed Protection District: indebtedness.

Existing law, the Ventura County Watershed Protection Act, creates the Ventura County Watershed Protection District and grants to that district authority that includes the control of floodwater and stormwater. The act authorizes the district to borrow money and incur indebtedness for various purposes, including financing flood control and conservation projects and repairing or removing flood control structures, in accordance with specified requirements.
