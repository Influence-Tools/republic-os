# California SB 1456 (20112012) — Community colleges: Seymour-Campbell Student Success Act of 2012.

(1) Existing law establishes the California Community Colleges, under the administration of the Board of Governors of the California Community Colleges, as one of the segments of public postsecondary education in this state.
