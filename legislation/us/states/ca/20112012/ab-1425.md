# California AB 1425 (20112012) — Insurance.

(1) Existing law requires the Insurance Commissioner to report to the Legislature by June 30, 2004, regarding the effectiveness of specified provisions regulating credit insurance agents in protecting consumers involved in credit insurance transactions.
