# California SB 117 (20112012) — Public contracts: prohibitions: discrimination based on gender or sexual orientation.

Existing law prohibits a state agency from entering into a contract for the acquisition of goods or services in the amount of $100,000 or more with a contractor who, in the provision of benefits, discriminates between employees with spouses and employees with domestic partners, or discriminates between the domestic partners and spouses of those employees.
