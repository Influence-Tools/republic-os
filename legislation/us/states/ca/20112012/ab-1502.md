# California AB 1502 (20112012) — Budget Act of 2012: augmentation.

(1) The Budget Act of 2012 appropriates specified amounts from the General Fund for public postsecondary education, including $51,500,000 to the University of California and $500,000 to Hastings College of the Law for purposes of addressing a portion of their employer pension contribution costs for the University of California Retirement Plan.
