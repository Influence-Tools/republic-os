# California AB 1027 (20112012) — Local publicly owned electric utilities: utility poles and support structures.

(1) Existing law provides for the creation of local publicly owned electric utilities, which include municipal corporations, municipal utility districts, public utility districts, and irrigation districts, that furnish electric services.
