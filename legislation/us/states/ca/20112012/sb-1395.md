# California SB 1395 (20112012) — State Auditor.

Existing law creates in state government the Bureau of State Audits under the direction of the Milton Marks "Little Hoover" Commission on California State Government Organization and Economy. Existing law provides that the bureau is headed by the State Auditor and independent of the executive branch and legislative control.
