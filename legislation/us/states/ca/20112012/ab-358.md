# California AB 358 (20112012) — Hazardous substances: underground storage tanks: releases: reports.

(1) Existing law generally regulates the storage of hazardous substances in underground storage tanks, including requiring underground storage tanks that are used to store hazardous substances to meet certain requirements. Existing law requires tank owners and operators to report unauthorized releases to local agencies, which includes specified information, and requires the State Water Resources Control Board to continuously post and update on its Internet Web site reports of information concerning unauthorized releases.
