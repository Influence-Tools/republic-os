Existing property tax law generally prohibits a local jurisdiction, in the 1985â€“86 fiscal year and each fiscal year thereafter, from imposing a property tax rate pursuant to a specified statutory provision in excess of the rate so imposed by that jurisdiction in specified fiscal years, unless it is imposed for specified purposes, including, among others, to make payments in support of certain pension programs.
