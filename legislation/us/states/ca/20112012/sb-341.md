# California SB 341 (20112012) — Vehicles: construction vehicles: backup alarms.

(1) Existing law requires a refuse or garbage truck to be equipped with an automatic backup audible alarm that sounds on backing and is capable of emitting an audible sound meeting specified requirements. A violation of this requirement is a crime.
