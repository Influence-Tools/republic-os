# California AB 2519 (20112012) — Real estate appraisers: licensing.

Existing law, the Real Estate Appraisers' Licensing and Certification Law, provides for the licensure and regulation of real estate appraisers and vests the duty of enforcing and administering that law in the Office of Real Estate Appraisers. Existing law authorizes the Director of the Office of Real Estate Appraisers to adopt regulations governing the process and procedure of licensing real estate appraisers, as specified.
