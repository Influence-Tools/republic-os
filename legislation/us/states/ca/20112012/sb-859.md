# California SB 859 (20112012) — Vehicles: records: confidentiality.

(1) Existing law requires the residence address in a record of the Department of Motor Vehicles to be kept confidential, with specified exceptions. One exception applies to a vehicle manufacturer licensed to do business in this state if the manufacturer, or its agent, under penalty of perjury, requests and uses the information only for the purpose of safety, warranty, or product recall if the manufacturer offers to make and makes any changes at no cost to the vehicle owner. Another exception applies to a dealer licensed to do business in this state if the dealer, or its agent, under penalty of perjury, requests and uses the information only for the purpose of completing registration transactions and documents. A violation of the Vehicle Code is a crime.
