# California AB 2643 (20112012) — Property taxation.

(1) Existing law authorizes the tax collector to apply any refund due a taxpayer, or a taxpayer's agent, to specified delinquent taxes, except when a refund is due to a taxpayer because the taxpayer or the agent for the taxpayer submitted a replicated tax payment, as defined.
