# California AB 657 (20112012) — Corporations: statement of information: Secretary of State.

Existing law requires corporations to register with the Secretary of State and file annual statements of information with specified information. Existing law requires the Secretary of State to annually mail a notice of compliance three months prior to the close of the applicable filing period.
