# California AB 384 (20112012) — State forest land: Jackson Demonstration State Forest.

Existing law authorizes the Department of Forestry and Fire Protection to engage in the management, protection, and reforestation of state forests and requires the Department of Parks and Recreation to have control of the state park system.
