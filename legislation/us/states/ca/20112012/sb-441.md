# California SB 441 (20112012) — Elections: contributions.

Existing law permits the county central committee of each qualified political party to supply county elections officials with party contribution envelopes or letters, as specified, to be included with the mailing of the sample ballot to each of the registered voters who disclosed preference for that same political party on the voter's affidavit of registration.
