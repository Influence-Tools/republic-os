# California SB 201 (20112012) — Flexible purpose corporations.

Existing law authorizes and regulates the formation and operation of corporations and nonprofit corporations and specifies the respective purposes for which they may lawfully be formed. Existing law specifies the duties of corporate directors and the rights of shareholders.
