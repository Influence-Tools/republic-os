# California AB 1498 (20112012) — Department of Technology: state contracts: information technology goods and services acquisition.

Existing law provides that there is in state government the California Technology Agency, which duties include establishing and enforcing state information technology strategic plans, policies, standards, and enterprise architecture. The agency is governed by the Secretary of California Technology.
