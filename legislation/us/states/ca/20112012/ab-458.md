# California AB 458 (20112012) — Guardianship.

Existing law, the Guardianship-Conservatorship Law, authorizes a court, upon hearing of a petition, to appoint a guardian of a person or estate of the proposed ward in accordance with specified provisions of law governing the custody of a minor child.
