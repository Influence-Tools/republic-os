# California AB 807 (20112012) — Vehicles: taxicab transportation services: periodic reports.

(1) Existing law requires the prospective employer of a driver who drives a specified vehicle, including a vehicle for which the driver is required to have a class A or B license, to obtain a report showing the driver's current public record as recorded by the Department of Motor Vehicles, and the employer of the driver to obtain an additional periodic report at least every 12 months. Existing law requires that those reports be presented upon request or upon demand, as applicable, to an authorized representative of the Department of the California Highway Patrol during regular business hours. A violation of the Vehicle Code is a crime.
