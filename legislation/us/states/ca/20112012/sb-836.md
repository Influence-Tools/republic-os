# California SB 836 (20112012) — Renewable energy resources: cost reporting.

Under existing law, the Public Utilities Commission has regulatory authority over public utilities, including electrical corporations, as defined. The California Renewables Portfolio Standard Program (RPS program) requires the commission to implement annual procurement targets for the procurement of eligible renewable energy resources, as defined, for all retail sellers, as defined, to achieve the targets and goals of the program.
