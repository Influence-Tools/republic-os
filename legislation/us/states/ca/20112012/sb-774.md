# California SB 774 (20112012) — Public postsecondary education: parking fees.

Existing law establishes the California Community Colleges under the administration of the Board of Governors of the California Community Colleges. Existing law authorizes the establishment of community college districts under the administration of community college governing boards, and authorizes these districts to provide instruction at community college campuses throughout the state. Existing law authorizes the governing board of each district to impose various fees, including maximum fees for parking and transportation services. Existing law requires the transportation services fee limit to be annually increased, as specified.
