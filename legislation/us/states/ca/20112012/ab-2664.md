# California AB 2664 (20112012) — County employees' retirement: electronic signatures.

The County Employees Retirement Law of 1937 provides a comprehensive set of benefits for county and district employees who are members of a retirement system subject to that law and establishes county retirement boards for the administration of benefits authorized under that law and authorizes each board to include specified provisions, by regulation, with respect to the administration of benefits.
