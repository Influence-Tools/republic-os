# California SB 806 (20112012) — State employees: military leave: salary overpayment.

Existing law requires the state, when it determines that an overpayment of compensation has been made to an employee, to notify the employee of the overpayment and afford the employee an opportunity to respond before commencing recoupment actions. Existing law requires that administrative action to recover an overpayment from a state employee be initiated within 3 years from the date of overpayment.
