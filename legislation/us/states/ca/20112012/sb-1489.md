# California SB 1489 (20112012) — Courts: management and destruction of trial court exhibits.

Existing law provides that, for purposes of specified provisions governing the management of trial court records, a "court record" consists of, among other things, administrative records filed in an action or proceeding, depositions, paper exhibits, transcripts, and recordings of electronically recorded proceedings, as specified.
