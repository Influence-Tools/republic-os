# California SB 493 (20112012) — State surplus property.

Existing law requires the Department of General Services to perform various functions and duties with respect to state property. Existing law authorizes the department to first offer appropriate state surplus personal property to school districts, and authorizes the department to offer it at less than fair market value, prior to offering that property to the public, as specified.
