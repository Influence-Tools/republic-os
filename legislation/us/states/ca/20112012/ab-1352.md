# California AB 1352 (20112012) — Taxation: vehicle license fees.

The Vehicle License Fee Law establishes, in lieu of any ad valorem property tax upon vehicles, an annual license fee for any vehicle subject to registration in this state in the amount of 0.65% of the market value of that vehicle, as provided. Existing law requires a portion of those revenues to be deposited into the Local Law Enforcement Services Account in the Local Revenue Fund 2011, as provided.
