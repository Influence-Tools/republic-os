# California SB 107 (20112012) — Physical education: California Interscholastic Federation.

Existing law describes the California Interscholastic Federation, provides the intent of the Legislature regarding its policies, and requires it to report to the Legislature and the Governor on its evaluation and accountability activities on or before January 1, 2010. Existing law repeals these provisions on January 1, 2012.
