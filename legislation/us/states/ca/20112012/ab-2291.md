# California AB 2291 (20112012) — Vehicles: advertising signs.

Existing law authorizes local authorities to adopt rules and regulations by ordinance or resolution regarding specified matters, including, among other things, regulating advertising signs on motor vehicles parked or left standing upon a public street. Existing law provides that the ordinance or resolution may establish a minimum distance that the advertising sign shall be moved after a specified time period.
