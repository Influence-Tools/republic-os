# California AB 307 (20112012) — Joint powers agreements: public agency: federally recognized Indian tribe.

Existing law authorizes 2 or more public agencies, as defined, to enter into an agreement to exercise common powers. Existing law also permits certain federally recognized Indian tribes to enter into joint powers agreements with particular parties and for limited purposes.
