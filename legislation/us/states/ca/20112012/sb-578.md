# California SB 578 (20112012) — Schools: pupils in foster care: course credit.

(1) Existing law requires a local educational agency to designate a staff person as the educational liaison for foster children and requires the liaison to assist foster children when transferring from one school to another or from one school district to another in ensuring proper transfer of credits, records, and grades.
