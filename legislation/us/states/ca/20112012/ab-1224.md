# California AB 1224 (20112012) — Veterans: veterans' farm and home purchases.

The Veterans' Farm and Home Purchase Act of 1974 authorizes the Department of Veterans Affairs to assist veterans in acquiring homes and farms, including cooperative dwelling units, by generally providing that the department may purchase a farm or home that the department then sells to a purchaser, as defined.
