# California SB 200 (20112012) — Delta levee maintenance.

Existing law, requires the Department of Water Resources to develop and submit to the Reclamation Board criteria for adoption by the board for the maintenance and improvement of nonproject levees, as prescribed. Existing law requires the department to inspect completed maintenance or improvement work, as specified, and requires the department to submit to the board a report upon completion of an inspection, as to its finding. Existing law also authorizes the department and the board to cooperate with the United States Army Corps of Engineers to develop and implement delta levee rehabilitation, improvement, and realignment, and to enhance the environment.
