# California AB 2432 (20092010) — Food facilities: prepackaged nonpotentially hazardous foods.

Existing law, the California Retail Food Code, provides for the regulation of health and sanitation standards for retail food facilities, including mobile food facilities and satellite food service, as defined, by the State Department of Public Health. Under existing law, local health agencies are primarily responsible for enforcing this code. A violation of these provisions is punishable as a misdemeanor.
