# California AB 2488 (20092010) — City and County of San Francisco: regional water system.

(1) Under existing law, the City and County of San Francisco operates the Hetch Hetchy Project as a regional water delivery system, supplying water to persons and entities in San Francisco and the Counties of Alameda, San Mateo, and Santa Clara.
