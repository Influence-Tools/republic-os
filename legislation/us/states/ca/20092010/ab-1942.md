# California AB 1942 (20092010) — Vehicles: video event recorder.

Existing law prohibits any person, except as specified, from driving any motor vehicle with any object or material placed, displayed, installed, affixed, or applied in or upon the vehicle that obstructs or reduces the driver's clear view through the windshield or side windows. A violation of the Vehicle Code is a crime.
