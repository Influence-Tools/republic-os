# California AB 2560 (20092010) — Education finance: federal tax credit bond volume cap.

Existing law assigns specified amounts of the state's 2009 federal tax credit bond volume cap to the State Department of Education and the California School Finance Authority, to be assigned and distributed to school districts, county offices of education, and charter schools, as specified.
