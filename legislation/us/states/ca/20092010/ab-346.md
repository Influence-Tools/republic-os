# California AB 346 (20092010) — Schools: noncertificated candidates: Activity Supervisor Clearance Certificate.

(1) Existing law allows school districts to agree among themselves to designate a single district, or a county superintendent to agree to act on behalf of participating school districts within the county or contiguous counties, for the purposes of performing specified duties regarding criminal background checks and maintaining employment lists if a person is an applicant for employment, or is employed on a part-time or substitute basis, in a position not requiring certification qualifications in multiple school districts within a county or within contiguous counties.
