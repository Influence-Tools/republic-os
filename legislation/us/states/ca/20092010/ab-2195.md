# California AB 2195 (20092010) — Taxation: State Board of Equalization:penalty: burden of proof.

Existing law imposes various taxes and fees that are administered and collected by the State Board of Equalization. Existing law provides that the taxpayer has the burden of proof in court proceedings for a refund of tax, while the state agency has the burden of proof with respect to its assertion of fraud or intent to evade penalties.
