# California AB 537 (20092010) — Farmers' markets: electronic benefit transfers.

Existing law, administered by the State Department of Social Services, provides for the establishment of a statewide electronic benefit transfer (EBT) system for the purpose of providing financial and food assistance benefits to needy Californians.
