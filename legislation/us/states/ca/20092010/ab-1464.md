# California AB 1464 (20092010) — Transportation: California Bicycle Routes of National, State, or Regional Significance Act.

Existing law requires the Department of Transportation, in cooperation with county and city governments, to establish minimum safety design criteria for the planning and construction of bikeways and roadways where bicycle travel is permitted.
