# California AB 2717 (20092010) — Insurance: agents and brokers: senior designation: use.

Existing law provides that a broker or agent may not use a senior designation unless he or she has met certain conditions, including, but not limited to, that the broker or agent has been granted the right and is currently authorized to use the senior designation by the organization that issues the designation, and the senior designation has been approved by the Insurance Commissioner for use by brokers and agents in the sale of insurance to seniors, as provided.
