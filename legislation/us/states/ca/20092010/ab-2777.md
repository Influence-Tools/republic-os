# California AB 2777 (20092010) — Transportation: omnibus bill.

(1) Existing law establishes the California Transportation Financing Authority, which consists of 7 members, with all powers reasonably necessary to carry out the powers and responsibilities expressly granted or imposed under the California Transportation Financing Authority Act. The act requires that 4 members of the authority constitute a quorum and that the affirmative vote of a quorum of the members present at a duly constituted meeting of the authority is necessary for any action taken by the authority.
