# California AB 1009 (20092010) — Bonds.

(1) Existing law establishes in state government the California Debt Limit Allocation Committee, with duties that include annually determining a state ceiling on the aggregate amount of private activity bonds that may be issued, and allocating that amount among state and local agencies. Existing law defines the term "state ceiling" for those purposes with regard to an amount specified in federal law.
