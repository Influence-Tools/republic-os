# California AB 138 (20092010) — Accounting firms: peer review.

Existing law provides for the licensing and regulation of certified public accountants and other licensees by the California Board of Accountancy. Existing law requires an accounting firm providing attest services, with certain exceptions, to complete a peer review every 3 years in order to renew its registration, if the board, prior to September 1, 2011, determines that a peer review program should be implemented and identifies the resources necessary for that implementation.
