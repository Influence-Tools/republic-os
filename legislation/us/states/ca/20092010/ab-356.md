# California AB 356 (20092010) — Radiologic technology: fluoroscopy.

Existing law sets forth the duties of various agencies relating to the protection of the public health and safety from the harmful effects of radiation, including, among others, the duties of the State Department of Public Health regarding the licensing and regulation of radiologic technology.
