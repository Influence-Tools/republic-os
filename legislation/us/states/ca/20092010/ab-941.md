# California AB 941 (20092010) — Adoption.

Existing law establishes the jurisdiction of the courts under the Uniform Parentage Act. The act specifies the counties in which an action may be brought, including among other counties as specified, the county in which a licensed California adoption agency maintains an office if that agency brings the action.
