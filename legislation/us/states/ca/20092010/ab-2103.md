# California AB 2103 (20092010) — San Francisco Bay Restoration Authority.

Existing law authorizes the San Francisco Bay Restoration Authority to levy a benefit assessment, special tax, or property-related fee consistent with Articles XIIIC and XIIID of the California Constitution, as specified.
