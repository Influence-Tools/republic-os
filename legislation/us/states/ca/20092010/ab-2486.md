# California AB 2486 (20092010) — Social host liability: furnishing alcohol to underage persons.

Under existing law, a social host who furnishes alcoholic beverages to any person may not be held legally accountable for damages suffered by that person, or for injury to the person or property of, or death of, any 3rd person, resulting from the consumption of those beverages.
