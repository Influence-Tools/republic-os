# California AB 1368 (20092010) — Child day care: safety.

Under existing law, the State Department of Public Health licenses and regulates child day care centers and family day care homes. Existing law requires day care center directors and licensees of large family day care homes to ensure that at least one staff member who has a current course completion in pediatric first aid and pediatric CPR, as specified, is onsite when children are present and is present with the children when they are offsite for facility activities. Willful or repeated violation of these provisions is a misdemeanor.
