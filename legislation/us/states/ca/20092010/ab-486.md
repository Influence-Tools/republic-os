# California AB 486 (20092010) — Local government: emergencies.

Existing law requires a governing body of a city, county, or city and county that declared a local emergency to review the need for continuing the local emergency at its regularly scheduled meetings, but at least every 21 days, or, if the governing body meets weekly, to review the need for continuing the local emergency at least every 14 days, until the local emergency is terminated.
