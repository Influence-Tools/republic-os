# California AB 121 (20092010) — Judgment liens: continuation.

Existing law provides that a judgment lien on specified personal property is created by filing a notice of judgment lien in the office of the Secretary of State. Existing law also provides that, unless the money judgment is satisfied or the judgment lien is terminated or released, the judgment lien continues for 5 years from the date of filing.
