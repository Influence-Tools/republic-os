# California AB 1071 (20092010) — Professions and vocations.

(1) Existing law provides for the licensure and regulation of various healing arts licensees by various boards within the Department of Consumer Affairs, including, but not limited to, the Medical Board of California, the California Board of Podiatric Medicine, the Board of Registered Nursing, the State Board of Optometry, the Respiratory Care Board of California, and the California State Board of Pharmacy. Existing law requires or authorizes these boards, with the exception of the California Board of Podiatric Medicine, to appoint an executive director or officer. Under existing law, these provisions will become inoperative on July 1, 2010, and will be repealed on January 1, 2011.
