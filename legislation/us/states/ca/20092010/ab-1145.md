# California AB 1145 (20092010) — Architects: fees.

Existing law provides for the licensure and regulation of architects by the California Architects Board. Existing law imposes various fees on licensees and applicants for a license, which are deposited in the California Architects Board Fund, a continuously appropriated fund. Under existing law, the fee for renewing a license may not exceed $200.
