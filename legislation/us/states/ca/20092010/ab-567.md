# California AB 567 (20092010) — Government practices.

(1) Existing law sets forth the duties and powers of the Bureau of State Audits and makes reference to audits and investigative audits. Existing law also prohibits the State Auditor or his or her employees from releasing to the public, among other things, any papers, correspondence, or any substantive information pertaining to any audit not completed.
