# California AB 76 (20092010) — Life and Annuity Consumer Protection Fund.

Existing law creates the Life and Annuity Consumer Protection Fund as a special account within the Insurance Fund and requires that the moneys deposited therein, from a $1 fee levied against insurers based upon each individual life insurance and annuity product worth $15,000 or more issued to a resident of this state, be dedicated to protecting consumers of insurance products. Under existing law, that provision is repealed as of January 1, 2010.
