# California AB 1045 (20092010) — HIV and AIDS reporting.

Existing law establishes various programs for the prevention of disease and the promotion of health to be administered by the State Department of Public Health, including, but not limited to, programs related to HIV and AIDS testing and for the reporting of information relating to HIV or AIDS infection.
