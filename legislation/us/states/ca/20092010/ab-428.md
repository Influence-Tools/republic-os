# California AB 428 (20092010) — Criminal history records.

Under existing law, the Department of Justice is required to maintain state summary criminal history information, as defined. Existing law authorizes the Attorney General to furnish state summary criminal history information and, when specifically authorized, federal criminal history information upon a showing of a compelling need, to various entities for a variety of purposes.
