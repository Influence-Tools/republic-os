# California AB 1547 (20092010) — State Board of Equalization: taxes and fees.

(1) The Sales and Use Tax Law requires a seller whose permit has been previously suspended or revoked to pay the State Board of Equalization a fee of $50 for the renewal or issuance of a permit.
