# California AB 2479 (20092010) — Stalking: surveillance.

Under existing law, a defendant who commits an assault against a plaintiff with the intent to capture any type of visual image, sound recording, or other physical impression of the plaintiff is liable for up to 3 times the amount of any general and special damages that are proximately caused by this act, as well as punitive damages and a civil fine of not less than $5,000 and not more than $50,000.
