# California AB 430 (20092010) — Corrections: citizens' advisory committees.

Existing law requires that each state prison under the jurisdiction of the Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation have a citizens' advisory committee, except that one committee may serve every prison located in the same city or community. Existing law specifies the membership of the committee.
