# California AB 890 (20092010) — City of Maywood: drinking water.

Existing law, the Calderon-Sher Safe Drinking Water Act of 1996, requires the State Department of Public Health to, among other things, adopt regulations relating to primary and secondary drinking water standards for contaminants in drinking water, which are based upon specified criteria. Violation of certain provisions relating to public water systems is a crime.
