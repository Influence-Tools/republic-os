# California AB 2768 (20092010) — Vehicles: charter-party carriers: busdrivers.

Existing law requires the clerk of a court in which a person was convicted of specified violations to prepare and forward to the Department of Motor Vehicles an abstract of record of the court covering the case in which the person was convicted.
