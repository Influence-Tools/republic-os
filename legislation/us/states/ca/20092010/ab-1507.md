# California AB 1507 (20092010) — Motor vehicle greenhouse gas emission reduction projects.

Existing law establishes the Carl Moyer Memorial Air Quality Standards Attainment Program, which provides grants to offset the incremental cost of projects that reduce covered emissions from covered sources.
