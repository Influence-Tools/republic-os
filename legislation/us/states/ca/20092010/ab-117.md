# California AB 117 (20092010) — Accountants.

Existing law provides for the licensure and regulation of accountants by the California Board of Accountancy in the Department of Consumer Affairs. Existing law prohibits a person or firm from using any title or designation in connection with the designation "certified public accountant" or "public accountant" that is false or misleading. A violation of specified provisions related to the practice of accountancy is a crime.
