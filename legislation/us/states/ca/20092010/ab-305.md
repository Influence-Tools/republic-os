# California AB 305 (20092010) — Hazardous materials: statute of limitations: penalties.

(1) Existing law of civil procedure relating to the time for commencing an action requires that actions for civil penalties or punitive damages under specified provisions relating to hazardous waste and hazardous substances be commenced within 5 years after the discovery by the agency bringing the action of the facts constituting the grounds for commencing the action.
