# California AB 1318 (20092010) — South Coast Air Quality Management District: emission reduction credits: California Environmental Quality Act.

(1) Under existing law, every air pollution control district or air quality management district governing board, except as specified, is required to establish by regulation a system by which all reductions in the emission of air contaminants that are to be used to offset certain future increases in the emission of air contaminants are required to be banked prior to use to offset future increases in emissions, as provided.
