# California AB 668 (20092010) — Trespass.

Existing law makes it unlawful for persons to engage in certain acts of trespass. In particular it is unlawful for a person who has been convicted of a violent felony committed upon a particular private property to enter upon that property after having been informed by a peace officer that the property is not open to the particular person; or to refuse or fail to leave the property upon being asked to leave the property, as specified.
