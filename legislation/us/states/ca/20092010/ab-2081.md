# California AB 2081 (20092010) — Education.

(1) Existing law requires the county superintendent of schools, upon the request of the governing board of a school district within his or her jurisdiction that elects to take advantage of specified provisions of law, to transfer a specified sum from the general fund of the school district to the school maintenance and repair fund.
