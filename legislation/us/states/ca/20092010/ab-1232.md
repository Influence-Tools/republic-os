# California AB 1232 (20092010) — Local agency formation commissions: powers and duties.

Existing law requires the local agency formation commission in each county to, among other things, review and approve or disapprove proposals for changes of organization, or reorganization, and authorizes the commission to initiate proposals for the formation, consolidation, or dissolution of a district, a merger, or the establishment of a subsidiary district, as specified.
