# California AB 292 (20092010) — Personal income taxes: contributions: Alzheimer's disease.

The Personal Income Tax Law allows taxpayers, until January 1, 2010, to contribute amounts in excess of their tax liability for the support of the California Alzheimer's Disease and Related Disorders Research Fund.
