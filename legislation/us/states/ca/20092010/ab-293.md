# California AB 293 (20092010) — Gambling regulation.

(1) The Gambling Control Act provides for the licensure of certain individuals and establishments involved in various gambling activities, and for the regulation of those activities, by the California Gambling Control Commission. Existing law provides for the enforcement of those activities by the Department of Justice. Any violation of these provisions is punishable as a misdemeanor, as specified.
