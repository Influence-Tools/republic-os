# California AB 1675 (20092010) — Trespassing: animal enclosures at zoos, circuses, and traveling animal exhibits.

Under existing law, every person who willfully enters and occupies real property without the consent of the owner, owner's agent, or person in lawful possession, is guilty of a misdemeanor.
