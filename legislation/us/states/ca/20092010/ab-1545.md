# California AB 1545 (20092010) — Uniform Principal and Income Act: trust administration: income and payments.

(1) Existing law, the Uniform Principal and Income Act, requires a trust to be administered, as specified, with due regard to the respective interests of defined income beneficiaries and remainder beneficiaries. For purposes of the act, "payment" is defined to mean a payment that a trustee may receive over a fixed number of years or during the life of one or more individuals because of services rendered or property transferred to the payer in exchange for future payments, or a payment that a trustee may receive pursuant to an income tax advantaged contractual, custodial, or trust arrangement, as prescribed.
