# California AB 347 (20092010) — Income and corporation taxes: charitable contributions: Haiti earthquake victims.

The Personal Income Tax Law and the Corporation Tax Law, in modified conformity to federal income tax laws, allow a charitable contribution deduction in computing tax liability.
