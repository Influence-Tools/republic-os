# California AB 1753 (20092010) — Slot machines.

Existing law, subject to exceptions, generally prohibits the possession and use of a "slot machine or device" as defined, and prohibits certain other acts and transactions pertaining to slot machines or devices. Existing law provides varying definitions of "slot machine or device" for these purposes. Violations of these provisions are punishable by varying misdemeanor penalties.
