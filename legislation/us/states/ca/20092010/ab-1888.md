# California AB 1888 (20092010) — Harbors and ports: Monterey Bay and the Bays of San Francisco, San Pablo, and Suisun: pilotage.

Existing law provides for the regulation and licensing of pilots for Monterey Bay and the Bays of San Francisco, San Pablo, and Suisun by the Board of Pilot Commissioners for the Bays of San Francisco, San Pablo, and Suisun. The board consists of 7 members, appointed by the Governor, with the consent of the Senate, with specified staggered terms.
