# California AB 2111 (20092010) — Service contracts.

(1) Existing law, the Electronic and Appliance Repair Dealer Registration Law, makes it unlawful for any person to act as a service contract administrator or a service contract seller without first registering with the Bureau of Electronic and Appliance Repair, Home Furnishings, and Thermal Insulation.
