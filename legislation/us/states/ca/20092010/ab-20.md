# California AB 20 (20092010) — University of California: California State University: contracts.

Existing law establishes the University of California (UC) as a public trust administered by the Regents of the University of California. Employees of the UC provide instruction to students and conduct research at each of the 10 campuses of the UC, which are located in Berkeley, Davis, Irvine, Los Angeles, Merced, Riverside, San Diego, San Francisco, Santa Barbara, and Santa Cruz. Existing law establishes the California State University (CSU) , under the administration of the Trustees of the California State University, as one of the other segments of public postsecondary education in this state.
