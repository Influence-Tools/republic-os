# California AB 2001 (20092010) — Building standards: State Department of Public Health: regulations.

Under existing law, the California Building Standards Law, the California Building Standards Commission is required to approve any building standard proposed by other agencies, as specified. Existing law transfers the responsibilities of certain agencies to adopt regulations relating to building standards to the commission.
