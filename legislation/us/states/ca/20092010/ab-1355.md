# California AB 1355 (20092010) — County employees' retirement: survivor's allowances.

The County Employees Retirement Law of 1937 (CERL) authorizes a county to provide specified optional survivor's allowances to members who receive retirement benefits pursuant to both CERL and federal social security on a nonintegrated basis. CERL provides that these survivor's allowances shall be calculated according to one of 2 alternative methods.
