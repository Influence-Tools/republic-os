# California AB 404 (20092010) — Income taxes: exemption: charitable organizations: administration.

The Corporation Tax Law, in modified conformity with federal income tax laws, exempts various types of organizations from state income taxes imposed by that law. Existing law allows organizations that have obtained a ruling or determination from the Internal Revenue Code that it is exempt from federal income taxes as an organization described in Section 501(c) (3) of the Internal Revenue Code to obtain exemption from state income taxes by submitting to the Franchise Tax Board a copy of the notification issued by the Internal Revenue Service approving the organization's tax-exempt status, as specified.
