# California AB 1179 (20092010) — Motor vehicle insurance: damage assessments.

Existing law generally regulates the conduct of motor vehicle insurers relative to insureds or claimants. Under existing law, insurers are required to provide each insured with an Auto Body Repair Consumer Bill of Rights containing specified information relating to the rights of insureds relative to covered auto body repairs.
