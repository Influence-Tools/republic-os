# California AB 2455 (20092010) — Military service: benefits.

Existing law authorizes members of the United States Military Reserve and National Guard who are called to active duty as a result of the Iraq or Afghanistan conflicts to defer payments on specified obligations for the period of active duty, as specified, including up to 2 loans subject to the Automobile Sales Finance Act.
