# California AB 2781 (20092010) — Insurance: Guarantee Association.

Existing law establishes the California Insurance Guarantee Association to provide coverage against losses arising from the failure of an insolvent property, casualty, or workers' compensation insurer to discharge its obligations under its insurance policies. The association is managed by a board of governors appointed by the commissioner, the President pro Tempore of the Senate, and the Speaker of the Assembly.
