# California AB 1582 (20092010) — Local agencies: spheres of influence.

(1) Existing law requires a Local Agency Formation Commission to develop and determine the sphere of influence of each local governmental agency within the county. A commission is authorized, at the time a commission approves a proposal for an incorporation or a reorganization which includes an incorporation, to determine the sphere of influence for the proposed new city. The commission is required to determine the sphere of influence for any newly incorporated city within one year of the effective date of incorporation.
