# California AB 2271 (20092010) — Probate: appeals.

Existing law establishes the right to appeal the making or denial of various orders or judgments under probate law. Existing law also provides that when an appeal is taken for the purpose of preventing injury or loss to a person or property the trial court may direct the exercise of the powers of the fiduciary, or may appoint a temporary guardian or conservator of the power, or a special administrator, to exercise the powers, as if no appeal were pending, as provided.
