# California AB 2215 (20092010) — Horse racing: advance deposit wagering: minisatellite wagering facilities.

Existing law authorizes advance deposit wagering to be conducted, with the approval of the California Horse Racing Board. Existing law requires the board to develop and adopt rules to license and regulate all phases of operation of advance deposit wagering for advance deposit wagering providers operating in California. Existing law authorizes a racing association, a fair, or a satellite wagering facility to enter into an agreement with an advance deposit wagering provider to accept and facilitate the placement of any wager at its facility that a California resident could make through that advance deposit wagering provider.
