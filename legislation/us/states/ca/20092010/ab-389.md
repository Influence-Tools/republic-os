# California AB 389 (20092010) — Long-term care insurance.

Existing law provides for regulation of insurers, including insurers issuing policies of long-term care insurance, by the Insurance Commissioner. Existing law requires benefits under individual long-term care insurance policies issued before new premium rate schedules are approved to be deemed reasonable in relation to premiums if the expected loss ratio is at least 60%, calculated in a manner that provides for adequate reserving of the risk.
