# California AB 506 (20092010) — State teachers' retirement: postretirement earnings.

(1) The State Teachers' Retirement Law limits the amount of postretirement compensation that may be earned in specified types of employment by a retired member of the Defined Benefit Program without a reduction in the retirement benefits of the member. That law, operative until June 30, 2010, provides exemptions from this limit and specifies that the limitation provisions do not apply to compensation earned by a member retired for service who has returned to work after retirement and, for at least 12 consecutive months, has not performed specified activities.
