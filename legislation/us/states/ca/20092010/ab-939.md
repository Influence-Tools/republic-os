# California AB 939 (20092010) — Family law proceedings.

Existing law prohibits after entry of judgment in specified family law proceedings in which there was at issue the visitation, custody, or support of a child, modification of the judgment or order, and prohibits a subsequent order in the proceedings, unless notice is served upon the party, as specified.
