# California AB 1414 (20092010) — Controlled substances: apomorphine: unscheduled.

Existing law, the California Uniform Controlled Substances Act, classifies controlled substances into 5 designated schedules, with the most restrictive limitations generally placed on controlled substances classified in Schedule I, and the least restrictive limitations generally placed on controlled substances classified in Schedule V. Existing law places apomorphine within Schedule II.
