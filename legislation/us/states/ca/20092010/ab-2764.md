# California AB 2764 (20092010) — The State Bar Act.

Existing law, the State Bar Act, provides for the licensure and regulation of attorneys by the State Bar of California, a public corporation. The State Bar is governed by a board known as the board of governors of the State Bar. The board of governors consists of 16 attorney members, 6 public members, and the President of the State Bar.
