# California AB 1441 (20092010) — Agricultural land: Williamson Act: lot lines.

Existing law, the Williamson Act, until January 1, 2010, authorizes a city or county and a landowner to agree to rescind a contract or contracts and simultaneously enter into a new contract or contracts to facilitate lot line adjustments.
