# California AB 905 (20092010) — Agricultural commodities.

Existing law provides that for lots over 200 pounds of any fruits, nuts, or vegetables that are the food product of any tree, vine, or plant, and for lots of over 200 pounds of walnut burl wood, every person who sells that commodity shall provide the buyer or transporter with a record of proof of ownership for each lot of the commodity. Existing law provides that every person who buys the commodity for resale shall obtain from the previous buyer or from the transporter a record of proof of ownership for each lot of the commodity. Other provisions of existing law provide that a violation of these provisions is a misdemeanor.
