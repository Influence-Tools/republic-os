# California AB 2761 (20092010) — Natural resources.

(1) Under existing law, the State Geologist is the head of the California Geological Survey in the Department of Conservation. The State Geologist advises the Director of Conservation regarding technical, scientific, and engineering issues, including the scientific quality of the division's products and activities. Existing law authorizes the State Geologist to perform various activities.
