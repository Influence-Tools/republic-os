# California AB 1632 (20092010) — Economic development.

(1) Existing law establishes various programs, including, but not limited to, the Capital Access Loan Program, to promote economic development throughout the state. Existing law also establishes the California Small Business Expansion Fund, the California Capital Access Fund, and the California Economic Development Fund.
