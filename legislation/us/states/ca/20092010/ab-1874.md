# California AB 1874 (20092010) — Education finance: Vallejo City Unified School District.

Existing law appropriates $60,000,000 from the General Fund to the Superintendent of Public Instruction for apportionment to the Vallejo City Unified School District as an emergency loan. Existing law authorizes the Vallejo City Unified School District, from June 30, 2004, to July 1, 2010, inclusive, to sell property owned by the district and to use the proceeds from the sale to reduce or retire that emergency loan.
