# California AB 1667 (20092010) — Public employment: County of Alameda.

The County Employees Retirement Law of 1937 authorizes, until January 1, 2011, the board of supervisors of the Counties of Solano and Santa Barbara to, by resolution, ordinance, contract, or contract amendment, provide different retirement benefits for some safety member bargaining units within the safety member classification of a county retirement system, as specified.
