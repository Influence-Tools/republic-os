# California AB 2260 (20092010) — State teachers' retirement: administration: benefits.

(1) The State Teachers' Retirement Law, which is administered by the Teachers' Retirement Board, prescribes a comprehensive system of rights and benefits for its members, including disability benefits, retirement, death benefits, and absences under the Family Medical Leave Act.
