# California AB 2618 (20092010) — Local government: county recorder.

Existing law authorizes the Los Angeles County Board of Supervisors and the Riverside County Board of Supervisors to adopt a resolution to authorize the county recorder to notify the party or parties executing a deed, quitclaim deed, or deed of trust.
