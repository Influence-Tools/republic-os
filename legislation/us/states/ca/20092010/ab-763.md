# California AB 763 (20092010) — Horse racing: Humboldt County Fair.

(1) Existing law provides for the operation of live horse racing by associations and fairs, and for wagering thereon, and for the operation of satellite wagering facilities, subject to regulation and oversight by the California Horse Racing Board, as specified.
