# California AB 1341 (20092010) — Property taxation: possessory interests: Long Beach Courthouse.

Existing property tax law requires that all property subject to tax be assessed at its full value, and includes certain possessory interests among those property interests subject to tax. Existing property tax law defines a taxable possessory interest to be a use that is independent, durable, and exclusive.
