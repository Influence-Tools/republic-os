# California AB 2632 (20092010) — Gang injunctions: violations: contempt of court.

Existing law provides for injunctive relief from the unlawful activities of criminal street gangs. Existing law provides that disobedience of the terms of any court order constitutes a contempt of court, and is punishable as a misdemeanor.
