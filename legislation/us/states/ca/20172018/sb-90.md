# California SB 90 (20172018) — Public social services: 1991 Realignment Legislation and IHSS Maintenance of Effort and collective bargaining.

(1) Existing law provides for the allocation of funds appropriated from the continuously appropriated Local Revenue Fund for the distribution of sales tax and motor vehicle license fee moneys to local agencies for the administration of various health, mental health, and public social service programs (1991 Realignment funds) .
