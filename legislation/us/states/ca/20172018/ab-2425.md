# California AB 2425 (20172018) — Property taxation: property records: transmission by mail or electronic format.

Existing property tax law requires, upon request of an assessee of property or designated representative, a county assessor to permit the assessee or representative to inspect or copy all information, documents, and records, other than market data, whether or not required to be kept or prepared by the assessor, relating to the appraisal and the assessment of the assessee's property, and any penalties and interest thereon.
