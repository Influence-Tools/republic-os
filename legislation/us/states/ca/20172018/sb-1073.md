# California SB 1073 (20172018) — Flood control: Napa River.

Existing law provides for state cooperation with the federal government in the construction of specified flood control projects. Existing law authorizes a plan of improvement for flood control and other purposes on the Napa River in Napa County, known as the Napa River Flood Control Project, in accordance with specified recommendations adopted by a specified federal act, at an estimated cost to the state of the sum that may be appropriated for state cooperation by the Legislature upon the recommendation and advice of the Department of Water Resources.
