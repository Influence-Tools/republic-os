# California AB 1706 (20172018) — Healing arts: chiropractic practice: speech-language pathology and audiology and hearing aid dispensing: occupational therapy: physical therapy.

(1) Under existing law, violations of specified provisions relating to the registration, licensure, certification, or authorization required to engage in certain businesses and professions, including, among others, physical therapy, are punishable as infractions under specified circumstances.
