# California SB 1208 (20172018) — Courts, judicial emergencies.

Existing law authorizes a presiding judge to request the Chairperson of the Judicial Council to authorize the court to transfer cases to an adjacent county, hold sessions in other parts of the county, extend specified time periods, and declare judicial holidays, among other emergency responses, when war, insurrection, pestilence, or other public calamity, among other things, threatens the orderly operation of a superior court location or locations within a county.
