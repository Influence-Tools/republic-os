# California AB 1360 (20172018) — Charter schools: pupil admissions, suspensions, and expulsions.

The Charter Schools Act of 1992 provides for the establishment and operation of charter schools and requires a petition for the establishment of a charter school to contain comprehensive descriptions of various procedures, including the charter school's admission, suspension, and expulsion procedures.
