# California SB 413 (20172018) — Dementia: major neurocognitive disorder.

Existing law regulates the licensure and operation of residential care facilities for the elderly, including the adoption of building standards to provide for locked and secured perimeters in residential care facilities for the elderly that care for persons with dementia.
