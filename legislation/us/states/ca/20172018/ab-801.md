# California AB 801 (20172018) — County of San Diego Citizens Redistricting Commission.

Existing law establishes the Independent Redistricting Commission in the County of San Diego, which consists of 5 members and 2 alternate members who are selected randomly, as prescribed. Existing law requires the commission to adjust the boundaries of the county's supervisorial districts and to adopt a redistricting plan, as specified.
