# California AB 1711 (20172018) — State military reserve personnel: leave benefits.

Existing law provides for leave for military service, rights and benefits accrued during that service, and reinstatement after that service for executive branch employees who are members of the Armed Forces, the National Guard, or the Naval Militia, as specified.
