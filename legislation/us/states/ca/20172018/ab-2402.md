# California AB 2402 (20172018) — Cannabis: personal information.

The Medicinal and Adult-Use Cannabis Regulation and Safety Act, among other things, provides for the licensure and regulation of commercial cannabis activity, including cultivation, manufacturing, distribution, and retail sale. Existing law requires licensees to maintain specified records of commercial cannabis transactions.
