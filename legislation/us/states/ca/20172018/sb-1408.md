# California SB 1408 (20172018) — Cigarettes: seizure.

Existing law, the California Cigarette Fire Safety and Firefighter Protection Act, prohibits a person from selling, offering, or possessing for sale in this state cigarettes that are not in compliance with certain requirements, including the requirement that the cigarettes are tested by the manufacturer in accordance with prescribed testing methods. Existing law provides an exception to the above prohibition for a person or entity that manufactures or sells cigarettes if the cigarettes are or will be stamped or metered for sale in another state or are packaged for sale outside of the United States.
