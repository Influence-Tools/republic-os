# California AB 2229 (20172018) — Residential property insurance: disclosures.

Existing law requires a named insured on a residential property insurance policy be provided with a copy of the California Residential Property Insurance Disclosure, which sets forth a description of certain types of insurance coverage, such as actual cash value coverage and guaranteed replacement cost coverage. Existing law also requires every California Residential Property Insurance Disclosure be accompanied by a California Residential Property Insurance Bill of Rights.
