# California SB 19 (20172018) — Public Utilities Commission: duties and responsibilities: governance.

(1) Existing law vests the Public Utilities Commission with regulatory authority over household goods carriers. Existing law imposes upon household goods carriers, and every person or corporation, owning or operating motor vehicles in the transportation of property for hire upon the public highways, under the jurisdiction of the commission, a license fee equal to 110 of 1% of the gross revenue, as defined.
