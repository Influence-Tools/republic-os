# California AB 880 (20172018) — Tribal Nation Grant Fund.

(1) Existing law establishes the Tribal Nation Grant Fund for the receipt and deposit of moneys received by the state from Indian tribes pursuant to the terms of tribal-state gaming compacts. Under existing law, moneys in the Tribal Nation Grant Fund are available, upon appropriation by the Legislature, for the discretionary distribution of funds to nongaming and limited-gaming tribes, as specified. An existing Executive Order establishes the position of the Governor's Tribal Advisor within the office of the Governor.
