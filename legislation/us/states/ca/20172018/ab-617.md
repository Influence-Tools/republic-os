# California AB 617 (20172018) — Nonvehicular air pollution: criteria air pollutants and toxic air contaminants.

(1) Existing law requires the State Air Resources Board to make available on its Internet Web site data concerning the emissions of greenhouse gases, criteria air pollutants, and toxic air contaminants, as specified.
