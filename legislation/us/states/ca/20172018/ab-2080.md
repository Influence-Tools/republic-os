# California AB 2080 (20172018) — Criminal offender record information: reporting.

Existing law requires criminal justice agencies to compile records and data, including a summary of arrests, pretrial proceedings, the nature and disposition of criminal charges, sentencing, incarceration, rehabilitation, and release, about criminal offenders. Existing law requires admissions or releases from detention facilities to be reported by the detention agency to the Department of Justice within 30 days of that action.
