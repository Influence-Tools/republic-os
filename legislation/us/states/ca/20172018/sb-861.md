# California SB 861 (20172018) — National Mortgage Settlement Fund: allocations.

Existing law creates the National Mortgage Special Deposit Fund, the moneys in which are continuously appropriated and to be allocated by the Department of Finance. Existing law authorizes the Director of Finance to allocate or otherwise use the funds in the National Mortgage Special Deposit Fund to offset General Fund expenditures in the 2011â€“12, 2012â€“13, and 2013â€“14 fiscal years.
