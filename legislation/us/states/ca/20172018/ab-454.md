# California AB 454 (20172018) — Personal income taxes: exclusion: wrongfully incarcerated individuals.

The Personal Income Tax Law imposes a tax on taxpayers measured by the taxpayer's taxable income for the taxable year, but excludes certain items of income from the computation of tax, including an exclusion for a specified amount received as compensation for having been the victim of a miscarriage of justice by a specific individual that was wrongfully convicted.
