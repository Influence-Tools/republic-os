# California SB 1183 (20172018) — Mortgages: deeds of trust: successors in interest.

Existing state law regulates reverse mortgages and defines a reverse mortgage to mean a nonrecourse loan secured by real property if the loan provides cash advances to a borrower based on the equity or the value in a borrower's owner-occupied principal residence, the loan requires no payment of principal or interest until the entire loan becomes due and payable, and the loan is made by a specified licensed or chartered lender.
