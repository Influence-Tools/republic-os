# California AB 3177 (20172018) — North County Transit District: contracting.

Existing law provides for creation of the North County Transit District, with various powers and duties relative to the planning and operation of a transit system in north San Diego County. Existing law authorizes the district to make contracts and to do all acts necessary and convenient for the full exercise of the district's powers in that regard.
