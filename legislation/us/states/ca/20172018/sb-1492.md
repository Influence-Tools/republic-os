# California SB 1492 (20172018) — The Department of Consumer Affairs.

(1) Existing law establishes the California Board of Accountancy in the Department of Consumer Affairs for the purpose of licensing and regulating the practice of accountancy. Existing law requires a licensee, in order to be authorized to sign reports on attest engagements, to have completed a minimum of 500 hours of experience in attest services, as specified.
