# California AB 2449 (20172018) — School districts, county offices of education, and community college districts: governing boards: elections.

Under existing law relating to the election of members of governing boards of school districts and governing boards of community college districts, the terms of office of certain board members commences on the first Friday in December. Under existing law relating to the election of members of county boards of education, the terms of office of certain board members commences on the last Friday in November.
