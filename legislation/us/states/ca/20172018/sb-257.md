# California SB 257 (20172018) — School admissions: pupil residency: pupils of departed parents: residents of adjoining state or foreign country: school district reimbursement.

(1) Existing law requires each person between 6 and 18 years of age not otherwise exempted to attend the public full-time day school or continuation school or classes in the school district where the person's parent or legal guardian is located. Existing law provides that a pupil is deemed to have complied with the residency requirements for school attendance in a school district if the pupil satisfies one of specified requirements.
