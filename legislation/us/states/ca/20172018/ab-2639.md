# California AB 2639 (20172018) — Pupil suicide prevention policies: reviews: updates.

Existing law requires the governing board or body of a local educational agency that serves pupils in grades 7 to 12, inclusive, to, before the beginning of the 2017â€“18 school year, adopt a policy on pupil suicide prevention, as specified, that specifically addresses the needs of high-risk groups.
