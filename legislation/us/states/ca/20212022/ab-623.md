# California AB 623 (20212022) — State-designated fairs: district agricultural associations: farmers' markets: California Apple Commission.

(1) Existing law defines state-designated fairs as the California Exposition and State Fair and other specified fairs that may receive financial support or are otherwise governed by specified state laws.
