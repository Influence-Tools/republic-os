# California AB 2163 (20212022) — San Gabriel Basin Water Quality Authority Act.

Existing law, the San Gabriel Basin Water Quality Authority Act, establishes the San Gabriel Basin Water Quality Authority and provides for its powers and duties. Existing law repeals the act on July 1, 2030. Upon the act's repeal, existing law prescribes various requirements for the administration of the authority's debts and assets.
