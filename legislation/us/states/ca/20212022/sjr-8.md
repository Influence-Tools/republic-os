# California SJR 8 (20212022) — Social Security Disability Insurance: disabled adult child benefit.

This measure would urge the President and the Congress of the United States to amend specified provisions of the federal Social Security Act to allow recipients of disabled adult child benefits under the act to continue to receive those benefits upon marriage.
