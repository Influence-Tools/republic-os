# California ACR 10 (20212022) — Relative to California Firefighter Appreciation Month and California Firefighters Memorial Day.

This measure would proclaim the month of September 2021 as California Firefighter Appreciation Month and September 25, 2021, as California Firefighters Memorial Day.
