# California AB 283 (20212022) — Corporate securities: exemption from requirements.

Existing law, the Corporate Securities Law of 1968, provides for the regulation of the issuance of corporate securities, requires the qualification of an offer or sale of securities, and provides for exemptions from qualification with the Commissioner of Financial Protection and Innovation.
