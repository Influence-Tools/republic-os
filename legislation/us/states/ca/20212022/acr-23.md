# California ACR 23 (20212022) — Relative to Construction Industry Suicide Prevention Awareness Day.

This measure would promote awareness of the problem of suicide facing the men and women within California's construction industry populations by proclaiming March 18, 2021, as Construction Industry Suicide Prevention Awareness Day in California.
