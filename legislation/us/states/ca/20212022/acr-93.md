# California ACR 93 (20212022) — Relative to Childhood Cancer Awareness Month.

This measure would proclaim the month of September 2021 as Childhood Cancer Awareness Month in California and state the commitment of the Legislature to support efforts to find cures for, and achieve prevention of, childhood cancer.
