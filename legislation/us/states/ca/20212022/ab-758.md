# California AB 758 (20212022) — Marks-Roos Local Bond Pooling Act of 1985: electric utilities: rate reduction bonds.

Existing law, the Marks-Roos Local Bond Pooling Act of 1985, authorizes certain joint powers authorities, upon application by a local agency that owns and operates a publicly owned utility, defined to mean certain utilities furnishing water or wastewater service to not less than 25,000 retail customers, to issue rate reduction bonds to finance utility projects, as defined, subject to certain requirements. Under the act, these rate reduction bonds are secured by a pledge of utility project property, and the joint powers authority issuing the bonds may impose on, and collect from, customers of the publicly owned utility a utility project charge to finance the bonds, as provided.
