# California SCR 98 (20212022) — Relative to the Fortuna Police Officer Raymond Quincy Mills Memorial Highway.

This measure would designate a specified portion of State Highway Route 101 in the City of Fortuna as the Fortuna Police Officer Raymond Quincy Mills Memorial Highway. The measure would request that the Department of Transportation determine the cost of appropriate signs showing this special designation and, upon receiving donations from nonstate sources covering that cost, erect those signs.
