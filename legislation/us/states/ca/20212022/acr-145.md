# California ACR 145 (20212022) — Triple-Negative Breast Cancer Awareness Day and Month.

This measure would proclaim March 3, 2022, as Triple-Negative Breast Cancer Awareness Day and the month of March 2022 as Triple-Negative Breast Cancer Awareness Month.
