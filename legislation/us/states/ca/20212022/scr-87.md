# California SCR 87 (20212022) — Relative to Enrolled Agents Day.

This measure would recognize April 15, 2022, as Enrolled Agents Day to commemorate the contributions that California's professional enrolled agents have made for 50 years to taxpayers, businesses, and tax agencies.
