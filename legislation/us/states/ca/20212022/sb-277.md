# California SB 277 (20212022) — Fireworks: dangerous fireworks: seizure: management.

Existing law authorizes the State Fire Marshal, among other entities, to seize certain fireworks. Existing law requires the State Fire Marshal to dispose of dangerous fireworks after specified proceedings. Under existing law, if no proceedings are commenced, the State Fire Marshal may dispose of the fireworks after satisfying certain specified requirements.
