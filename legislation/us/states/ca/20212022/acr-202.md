# California ACR 202 (20212022) — Relative to the PFC George Howell Memorial Highway.

This measure would designate a specified portion of State Route 101 in the County of Monterey as the PFC George Howell Memorial Highway. The measure would request the Department of Transportation to determine the cost of appropriate signs showing this special designation and, upon receiving donations from nonstate sources covering the cost, to erect those signs.
