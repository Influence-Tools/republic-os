# California SCR 86 (20212022) — Relative to Women's Military History Week.

This measure would recognize "Women Warriors" by proclaiming the week of March 14, 2022, to March 20, 2022, inclusive, as Women's Military History Week in California, recognize the hard-fought contributions of women to the military and freedom, and encourage Californians to honor the courageous sacrifices that women have made since the historic lifting of the ban on women in combat on January 24, 2013.
