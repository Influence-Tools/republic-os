# California AB 2342 (20212022) — Community Economic Resilience Fund Program.

Existing law establishes the Community Economic Resilience Fund Program within the Workforce Services Branch of the Employment Development Department within the Labor and Workforce Development Agency. Existing law requires the branch and the Inter-Agency Leadership Team, comprised of the Labor and Workforce Development Agency, the Office of Planning and Research, and the Governor's Office of Business and Economic Development, to administer the program. Existing law makes the team jointly responsible for planning, oversight, and decisionmaking, as specified, and sets forth the specifics of the team's composition and duties.
