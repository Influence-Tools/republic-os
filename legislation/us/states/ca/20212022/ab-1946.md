# California AB 1946 (20212022) — Electric bicycles: safety and training program.

Existing law, the Protected Bikeways Act of 2014, provides that the state's bicycle programs have not been fully developed or funded. Existing law establishes the Department of the California Highway Patrol within the Transportation Agency.
