# California SB 218 (20212022) — Corporations: ratification or validation of noncompliant corporate actions.

The General Corporation Law provides for the formation of domestic general corporations by the execution and filing of articles of incorporation with the Secretary of State. Under that law, the business and affairs of these corporations are generally managed by, and all corporate powers are exercised by or under, the direction of their boards of directors, and each director is elected by shareholder vote, with certain exceptions, as specified.
