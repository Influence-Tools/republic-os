# California ACR 203 (20212022) — Relative to the Army Specialist Roger Lee Memorial Highway.

This measure would designate a specified portion of State Highway Route 68 in the County of Monterey as the Army Specialist Roger Lee Memorial Highway. The measure would request that the Department of Transportation determine the cost of appropriate signs showing this special designation and, upon receiving donations from nonstate sources covering that cost, erect those signs.
