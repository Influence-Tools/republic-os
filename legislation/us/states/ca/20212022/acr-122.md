# California ACR 122 (20212022) — Relative to California Firefighter Appreciation Month and California Firefighters Memorial Day.

This measure would, among other things, proclaim the month of July 2022 as California Firefighter Appreciation Month and July 30, 2022, as California Firefighters Memorial Day.
