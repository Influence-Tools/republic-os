# California ACR 144 (20212022) — Relative to the Paul Vincent Rotondaro Memorial Highway.

This measure would designate a specified portion of State Route 140 in the County of Merced as the Paul Vincent Rotondaro Memorial Highway. The measure would request the Department of Transportation to determine the cost of appropriate signs showing this special designation and, upon receiving donations from nonstate sources covering that cost, to erect those signs.
