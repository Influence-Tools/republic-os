# California AB 934 (20212022) — Public buildings: shelter in place: guidelines.

Existing law establishes the Department of General Services, under the control of an executive officer known as the Director of General Services, in the Government Operations Agency and vests the department with specified powers and duties pertaining to state-owned real property and state buildings.
