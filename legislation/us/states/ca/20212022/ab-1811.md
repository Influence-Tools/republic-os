# California AB 1811 (20212022) — Local flood protection: planning: climate change.

Existing law authorizes a local agency to prepare a local plan of flood protection and prescribes that a plan include, among other components, a strategy to meet the urban level of flood protection, including planning for residual flood risk and system resiliency.
