# California ACR 195 (20212022) — Relative to the Officer Jimmy Arty Inn Memorial Highway.

This measure would designate a specified portion of State Route 4 in the City of Stockton as the Officer Jimmy Arty Inn Memorial Highway. The measure would request that the Department of Transportation determine the cost of appropriate signs showing this special designation and, upon receiving donations from nonstate sources covering that cost, erect those signs.
