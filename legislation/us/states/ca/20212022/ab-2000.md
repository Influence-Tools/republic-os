# California AB 2000 (20212022) — Motor vehicle speed contests and exhibitions of speed: offstreet parking facilities.

Existing law makes it a crime for a person to engage in a motor vehicle speed contest on a highway or an exhibition of speed on a highway, or to aid or abet therein.
