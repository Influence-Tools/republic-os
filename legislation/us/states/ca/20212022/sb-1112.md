# California SB 1112 (20212022) — Energy: building decarbonization: notice and recordation of a decarbonization charge.

(1) Existing law requires the State Energy Resources Conservation and Development Commission (Energy Commission) to undertake various actions in furtherance of meeting the state's clean energy and pollution reduction objectives.
