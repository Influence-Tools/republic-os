# California SCR 4 (20212022) — Relative to King Hùng Vương Commemorative Day.

This measure would proclaim April 21, 2021, as King Hùng Vương Commemorative Day and would recognize it as a day of cultural festivals commemorating the roots of Vietnamese Americans in California.
