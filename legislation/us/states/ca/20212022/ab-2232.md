# California AB 2232 (20212022) — School facilities: heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems.

Existing law establishes the California State University, which is administered by the Board of Trustees of the California State University, and the University of California, under the administration of the Regents of the University of California, as segments of public postsecondary education in this state.
