# California AB 662 (20212022) — State Fire Marshal and Emergency Medical Services Authority: peer-to-peer suicide prevention.

Under the California Fire Service Training and Education Program Act, the State Fire Marshal is authorized to, among other things, establish courses of study and curriculum relating to fire service training, as provided.
