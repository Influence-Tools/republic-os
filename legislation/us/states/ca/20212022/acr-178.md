# California ACR 178 (20212022) — Relative to Donate Life/DMV Partnership Month.

This measure would proclaim the month of April 2022 as Donate Life/DMV Partnership Month in California and would encourage all Californians to register with the Donate Life California Organ and Tissue Donor Registry.
