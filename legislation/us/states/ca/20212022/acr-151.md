# California ACR 151 (20212022) — Relative to the Captain Matthew Patrick Manoukian Memorial Highway.

This measure would designate a specified portion of State Route 280 in the County of Santa Clara as the Captain Matthew Patrick Manoukian Memorial Highway. The measure would request that the Department of Transportation determine the cost of appropriate signs showing this special designation and, upon receiving donations from nonstate sources covering that cost, erect those signs.
