# California AB 2661 (20212022) — Death benefits: tribal firefighters.

Existing law provides for the payment of a scholarship to a postsecondary educational institution, as provided, to a dependent of specified firefighters killed in the performance of duty. Existing law requires the scholarship to be paid out of funds annually appropriated in the Budget Act.
