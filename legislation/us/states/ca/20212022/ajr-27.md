# California AJR 27 (20212022) — Relative to the Armenian, Assyrian, and Greek Genocide.

This measure would, among other things, recognize April 24, 2022, as "State of California Day of Commemoration of the 107th Anniversary of the Armenian Genocide of 1915–1923," and would call upon the President of the United States and the United States Congress to formally and consistently reaffirm the historical truth that the atrocities committed against the Armenian people constituted genocide.
