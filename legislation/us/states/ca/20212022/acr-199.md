# California ACR 199 (20212022) — Relative to "Parks Make Life Better!®" Month.

This measure would recognize the importance of access to local parks, trails, open space, and facilities for the health, wellness, development, inspiration, and safety of all Californians and would declare the month of July 2022 as "Parks Make Life Better!®" Month.
