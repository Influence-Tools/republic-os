# California ACR 67 (20212022) — Relative to the Tommy Lasorda Memorial Highway.

This measure would designate a specified portion of Interstate 5 within the County of Orange as the Tommy Lasorda Memorial Highway. The measure would request the Department of Transportation to determine the cost of appropriate signs showing this special designation and, upon receiving donations from nonstate sources covering that cost, to erect those signs.
