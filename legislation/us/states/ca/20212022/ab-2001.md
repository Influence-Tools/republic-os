# California AB 2001 (20212022) — California Financing Law: remote work.

Existing law, the California Financing Law (CFL) , prohibits a finance lender, broker, mortgage loan originator, or program administrator licensee from transacting the business licensed or making a loan or administering a PACE program provided for by the CFL under another name or at another place of business than that named in the license except pursuant to a currently effective written order of the Commissioner of Financial Protection and Innovation authorizing the other name or other place of business.
