# California SB 1267 (20212022) — Clinical laboratories.

Existing law provides for the licensure, registration, and regulation of clinical laboratories and various clinical laboratory personnel by the State Department of Public Health. Existing law specifies what duties the various clinical laboratory personnel can perform. A violation of these provisions is a crime.
