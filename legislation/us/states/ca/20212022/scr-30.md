# California SCR 30 (20212022) — Relative to Cystinuria Awareness Day.

This measure would recognize and proclaim June 24, 2021, as Cystinuria Awareness Day to promote awareness of Cystinuria and to show support for California medical research centers that take an active role in the fight against the disease.
