# California SCR 99 (20212022) — Relative to Take Our Daughters And Sons To Work Day.

This measure would declare April 28, 2022, as Take Our Daughters and Sons to Work Day, and would recognize the goals of introducing our daughters and sons to the workplace and commend all participants of Take Our Daughters And Sons To Work Day.
