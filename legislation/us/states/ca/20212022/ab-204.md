# California AB 204 (20212022) — Budget Act of 2022: Health Omnibus.

Existing law establishes the Federal Trust Fund, consisting of all money received by the state from the United States. Existing law also establishes the California Health Trust Fund as a continuously appropriated fund to provide for the administration of the California Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act, as specified.
