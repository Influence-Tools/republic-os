# California AB 2503 (20212022) — Landlords and tenants: California Law Revision Commission: study.

Existing law generally regulates the relationship between parties to a lease of real property. Existing law refers to the lessor of real property variously as, among other terms, "landlord" or "lessor" and refers to the lessee of real property variously as, among other terms, "tenant" or "lessee." Existing law creates the California Law Revision Commission and requires the commission to, among other things, examine the common law and statutes of the state and judicial decisions for the purpose of discovering defects and anachronisms in the law and recommending needed reforms.
