# California ACR 164 (20212022) — Relative to the Lieutenant Colonel Seth "Jethro" Nehring Memorial Highway.

This measure would designate a specified portion of State Highway Route 168 in the County of Fresno as the Lieutenant Colonel Seth "Jethro" Nehring Memorial Highway. The measure would request that the Department of Transportation determine the cost of appropriate signs showing this special designation and, upon receiving donations from nonstate sources covering that cost, erect those signs.
