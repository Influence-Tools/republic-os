# California SCR 24 (20212022) — Relative to the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Deputy Joseph Solano Memorial Overcrossing.

This measure would designate the overcrossing on State Route 118 at Balboa Boulevard in the County of Los Angeles as the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Deputy Joseph Solano Memorial Overcrossing. The measure would request that the Department of Transportation determine the cost of appropriate signs showing this special designation and, upon receiving donations from nonstate sources covering that cost, erect those signs.
