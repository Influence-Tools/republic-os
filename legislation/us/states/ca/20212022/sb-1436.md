# California SB 1436 (20212022) — Respiratory therapy.

(1) Existing law, the Respiratory Care Practice Act, establishes the Respiratory Care Board of California for the licensure and regulation of respiratory therapy practitioners. Existing law makes a violation of that act a crime and repeals the act on January 1, 2023.
