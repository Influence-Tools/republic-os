# California SB 1174 (20212022) — Electricity: eligible renewable energy or energy storage resources: transmission and interconnection.

(1) The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC) has exclusive jurisdiction over the transmission of electricity in interstate commerce, over the sale of electricity at wholesale in interstate commerce, and over all facilities for the transmission or sale of electricity in interstate commerce.
