# California AB 319 (20212022) — Political Reform Act of 1974: contributions: foreign governments or principals.

Existing law, the Political Reform Act of 1974, provides for the comprehensive regulation of campaign financing, including requiring the reporting of campaign contributions. A violation of the act's provisions is punishable as a misdemeanor and subject to specified penalties.
