# California SB 1433 (20212022) — Private postsecondary education: California Private Postsecondary Education Act of 2009.

(1) Existing law establishes the California Private Postsecondary Education Act of 2009, which provides for student protections and regulatory oversight of private postsecondary educational institutions, as defined, in the state. The act is enforced by the Bureau for Private Postsecondary Education within the Department of Consumer Affairs.
