# California AB 2957 (20212022) — Local government: reorganization.

Existing law, the Cortese-Knox-Hertzberg Local Government Reorganization Act of 2000, provides the authority and procedure for the initiation, conduct, and completion of changes of organization, reorganization, and sphere of influence changes for cities and districts, as specified. Existing law requires an applicant seeking a change of organization or reorganization to submit a plan for providing services within the affected territory.
