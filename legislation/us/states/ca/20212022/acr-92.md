# California ACR 92 (20212022) — Relative to aromatic l-amino acid decarboxylase deficiency awareness.

This measure would proclaim October 23, 2022, as Aromatic L-Amino Acid Decarboxylase (AADC) Deficiency Awareness Day in the State of California.
