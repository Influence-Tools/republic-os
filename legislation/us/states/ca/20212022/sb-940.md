# California SB 940 (20212022) — Mobilehome parks: local ordinances.

Existing law, the Mobilehome Residency Law, prescribes various terms and conditions of tenancies in mobilehome parks. Existing law exempts new construction, defined as spaces initially held out for rent after January 1, 1990, from any ordinance, rule, regulation, or initiative measure adopted by a city or county, that establishes a maximum amount that a landlord may charge a tenant for rent.
