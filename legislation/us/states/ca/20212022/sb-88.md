# California SB 88 (20212022) — One-time stimulus payment: delinquent accounts: Earned Income Tax Credit: statements.

(1) Existing law authorizes various forms of relief for low-income Californians, including certain tax benefits and public assistance programs. Existing law also provides various forms of assistance to those Californians that have been impacted by the COVID-19 emergency.
