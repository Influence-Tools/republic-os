# California SB 821 (20212022) — Sacramento-San Joaquin Delta: Delta Independent Science Board.

Existing law establishes the Delta Stewardship Council. Existing law authorizes the council to contract for consultant services and architectural and engineering services to assist with preparation of the Delta Plan and performance of certain responsibilities of the council. Existing law exempts contracts for consultant services from specified provisions of law governing public contracting. Existing law requires the council to establish procedures for contracting for consultant services and architectural and engineering services.
