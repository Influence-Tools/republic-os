# California AB 1164 (20192020) — Surplus state real property: disposal.

(1) Existing law authorizes the Director of General Services to dispose of surplus state real property subject to a prescribed process and legislative authorization. Existing law requires that surplus state property not needed by a state agency be offered to local agencies and then to nonprofit affordable housing sponsors prior to being offered to private entities or individuals.
