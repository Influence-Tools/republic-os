# California AB 2606 (20192020) — Criminal justice: supervised release file.

Existing law requires the Department of Justice, in conjunction with the Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation, to update any supervised release file that is available to law enforcement on the California Law Enforcement Telecommunications System (CLETS) , as specified, to reflect newly paroled inmates.
