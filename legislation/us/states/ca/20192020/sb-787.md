# California SB 787 (20192020) — Animal welfare.

Existing law governs the seizure, rescue, adopting out, and euthanasia of abandoned and surrendered animals by animal control officers, law enforcement officers, animal shelters, and rescue organizations, and uses various terms, including "pound."
