# California AB 2192 (20192020) — Governor's Military Council.

Existing law establishes the Governor's Military Council that advises the Governor on efforts to retain military installations and operations within the state. Members of the council are appointed by the Governor and include bipartisan representatives from both houses of the Legislature, as specified. Existing law repeals the law establishing the council on January 1, 2021.
