# California AB 1340 (20192020) — Private postsecondary education: California Private Postsecondary Education Act of 2009: labor market outcome data reporting.

(1) Existing law, the California Private Postsecondary Education Act of 2009, provides, among other things, for student protections and regulatory oversight of private postsecondary schools in the state. The act is enforced by the Bureau for Private Postsecondary Education within the Department of Consumer Affairs.
