# California AB 593 (20192020) — Unemployment insurance: use of information: public workforce development programs.

Under existing law, the information obtained in the administration of the Unemployment Insurance Code is for the exclusive use and information of the Director of Employment Development in the discharge of the director's duties and is not open to the public. However, existing law permits the use of the information for specified purposes, including to enable the California Workforce Development Board and other entities to access any relevant quarterly wage data necessary for the evaluation and reporting of specified workforce program performance outcomes as required and permitted by various state and federal laws, as specified. Existing law makes it a crime for any person to knowingly access, use, or disclose this confidential information without authorization.
