# California AB 1428 (20192020) — Business practices: prepaid credit cards: refund methods.

Existing law requires a retail seller of goods to the public that has a policy of denying full cash or credit refunds for the exchange of merchandise for goods of equal value for at least 7 days following the purchase of the goods, to conspicuously display that store's refund and exchange policy.
