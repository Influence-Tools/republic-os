# California AB 1803 (20192020) — Pharmacy: health care coverage: claims for prescription drugs sold for retail price.

Existing law, the Pharmacy Law, requires a pharmacy to inform a customer at the point of sale for a covered prescription drug whether the retail price is lower than the applicable cost-sharing amount for the prescription drug, except as specified, and, if the customer pays the retail price, requires the pharmacy to submit the claim to the customer's health care service plan or health insurer.
