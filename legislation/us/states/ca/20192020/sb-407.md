# California SB 407 (20192020) — Medicare supplement benefit coverage.

Existing federal law provides for the Medicare Program, which is a public health insurance program for persons 65 years of age and older and specified persons with disabilities who are under 65 years of age. Under the Medicare Program, eligible persons receive various health care services, including medically necessary services and supplies and preventive services.
