# California AB 1099 (20192020) — Insurance: California Organized Investment Network.

Until January 1, 2020, existing law requires an admitted insurer with annual premiums written in California equal to or in excess of $100,000,000 to provide information to the Insurance Commissioner by July 1, 2016, on all of its community development investments, including investments through California Organized Investment Network (COIN) -certified community development financial institutions, community development infrastructure investments, and green investments, in California for the 2013, 2014, and 2015 calendar years. Existing law requires the commissioner, by December 31, 2016, to provide specified information on the Department of Insurance's internet website.
