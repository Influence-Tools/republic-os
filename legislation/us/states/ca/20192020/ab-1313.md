# California AB 1313 (20192020) — Higher education: prohibited debt collection practices.

Existing law, the Donahoe Higher Education Act, requires public higher education entities to adopt regulations to withhold institutional services, including the withholding of transcripts, upon notice to students that they are in default of their loans.
