# California SB 389 (20192020) — Mental Health Services Act.

Existing law, the Mental Health Services Act (MHSA) , an initiative statute enacted by the voters as Proposition 63 at the November 2, 2004, statewide general election, establishes the continuously appropriated Mental Health Services Fund to fund various county mental health programs. Existing law prohibits MHSA moneys from being used to pay for persons incarcerated in state prison or parolees from state prisons.
