# California SB 180 (20192020) — Gene therapy kits: advisory notice and labels.

Existing federal law establishes the United States Food and Drug Administration to, among other duties, promote the public health by taking appropriate action on the marketing of regulated drugs, devices, and biological products in a timely manner.
