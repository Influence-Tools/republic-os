# California AB 605 (20192020) — Special education: assistive technology devices.

(1) Existing law establishes a right of individuals with exceptional needs to receive free appropriate public education, and ensures the right to special instruction and related services needed to meet their unique needs, in conformity with federal law. Under existing law, a local educational agency, as defined, may be responsible for providing an assistive technology device, as defined, for the use of a pupil with exceptional needs when that device is needed to implement that pupil's individualized education program.
