# California AB 356 (20192020) — Los Angeles Community College District: best value procurement: pilot program.

The Local Agency Public Construction Act requires the governing board of any community college district to let any contract for a public project, as defined, involving an expenditure of $15,000 or more, to the lowest responsible bidder that gives security as the board requires, or else reject all bids.
