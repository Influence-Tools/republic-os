# California AB 212 (20192020) — Counties: recording fees.

Existing law requires the recorder of each county, upon payment of proper fees and taxes, to accept for recordation any instrument, paper, or notice that is authorized or required by law to be recorded, as specified. Existing law establishes a fee for recording documents with the county recorder at $10 for the first page and $3 for each additional page and authorizes a county recorder to assess additional specified fees, including a fee of $1 for each document filed in order to defray the cost of converting the county recorder's document storage system to micrographics.
