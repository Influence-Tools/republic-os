# California AB 3362 (20192020) — State Bar: open meetings: discipline: attorneys: foreign legal consultants: annual license fees.

Existing law, the State Bar Act, provides for the licensure and regulation of attorneys by the State Bar of California, a public corporation. The State Bar is governed by a board of trustees.
