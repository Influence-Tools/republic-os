# California AB 27 (20112012 Special Session 1) — Voluntary Alternative Redevelopment Program.

The Community Redevelopment Law authorizes the establishment of redevelopment agencies in communities to address the effects of blight, as defined, in those communities and requires agencies to prepare, or cause to be prepared, and to approve a redevelopment plan for each project area.
