# California AB 16 (20112012 Special Session 1) — Local Revenue Fund 2011.

Existing law establishes the Local Revenue Fund 2011, a continuously appropriated fund, and creates various accounts and subaccounts within that fund, for purposes of funding Public Safety Services, as defined.
