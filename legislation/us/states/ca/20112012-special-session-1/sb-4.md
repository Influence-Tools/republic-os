# California SB 4 (20112012 Special Session 1) — Supplemental Law Enforcement Services Account.

(1) Existing law requires in each county treasury a Supplemental Law Enforcement Services Account (SLESA) , to receive all amounts allocated to a county for purposes of specified law enforcement services. Existing law also requires that in any fiscal year for which a county receives moneys to be expended for those law enforcement purposes, that the county auditor allocate the moneys in the county's SLESA, as specified, including any interest or other return earned on the investment of those moneys.
