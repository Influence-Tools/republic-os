# California ACR 109 (20252026) — Relative to Officer Gonzalo Carrasco, Jr., Memorial Highway.

This measure would designate a specified portion of State Highway Route 43 in the City of Selma as the Officer Gonzalo Carrasco, Jr., Memorial Highway. The measure would request the Department of Transportation to determine the cost of appropriate signs showing this special designation and, upon receiving donations from nonstate sources sufficient to cover the cost, to erect those signs.
