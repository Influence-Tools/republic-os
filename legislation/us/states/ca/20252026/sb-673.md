# California SB 673 (20252026) — Courthouse construction: filing fee surcharges.

Existing law, until January 1, 2026, authorizes the Board of Supervisors of San Bernardino County to impose a surcharge, not to exceed $35, on certain filing fees for purposes of funding its Courthouse Construction Fund.
