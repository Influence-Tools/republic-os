# California SCR 47 (20252026) — 250th anniversary of the Battle of Lexington and Concord.

This measure would commemorate the 250th anniversary of the "shot heard round the world" and the Battle of Lexington and Concord, and would urge citizens across the country to participate in educational programs, reenactments, and commemorative events to reflect upon the enduring importance of liberty and justice.
