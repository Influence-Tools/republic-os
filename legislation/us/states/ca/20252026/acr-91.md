# California ACR 91 (20252026) — Portuguese Heritage Month: Day of Portugal: Day of the Azores.

This measure would declare the month of June 2025 to be Portuguese Heritage Month and would recognize June 10 as the Day of Portugal and June 9 as the Day of the Azores.
