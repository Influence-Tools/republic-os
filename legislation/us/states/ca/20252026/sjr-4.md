# California SJR 4 (20252026) — Research and education: federal funding cuts: the National Institutes of Health and postsecondary educational institutions.

This measure would urge the administration of President Donald J. Trump and the Congress of the United States to rescind the National Institutes of Health funding cuts. This measure also affirms the Legislature's commitment to postsecondary educational institutions.
