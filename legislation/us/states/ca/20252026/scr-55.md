# California SCR 55 (20252026) — Relative to Apprenticeship Week.

This measure would declare the week of April 27, 2025, to May 3, 2025, inclusive, as "Apprenticeship Week" in the State of California and would request that the Governor issue a proclamation calling on the people of the great State of California to observe the week with appropriate programs and educational activities.
