# California SCR 102 (20252026) — Relative to California Firefighter Appreciation Month and California Firefighters Memorial Day.

This measure would proclaim the month of October 2025 as California Firefighter Appreciation Month and October 4, 2025, as California Firefighters Memorial Day.
