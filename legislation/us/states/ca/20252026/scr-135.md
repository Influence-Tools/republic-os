# California SCR 135 (20252026) — Relative to Peace Corps Week.

This measure would proclaim March 1, 2026, to March 7, 2026, inclusive, as Peace Corps Week, in recognition of the 65th anniversary of the establishment of the United States Peace Corps, as provided. The measure would also support awarding Peace Corps volunteers a Congressional Gold Medal to honor their distinguished service to the United States.
