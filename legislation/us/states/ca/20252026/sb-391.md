# California SB 391 (20252026) — Community colleges: Chancellor of the California Community Colleges: data request fee policy.

Existing law establishes the California Community Colleges under the administration of the Board of Governors of the California Community Colleges as one segment of public postsecondary education in the state. Under existing law, the board of governors appoints a chief executive officer, who is known as the Chancellor of the California Community Colleges.
