# California ACR 128 (20252026) — Relative to Day of Solidarity with Ukraine.

This measure would recognize February 24, 2026, as a Day of Solidarity with Ukraine, honoring the resilience of the Ukrainian people and acknowledging the ongoing impact of the war on youth, families, and communities.
