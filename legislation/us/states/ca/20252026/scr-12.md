# California SCR 12 (20252026) — Relative to certified registered nurse anesthetists.

This measure would commend certified registered nurse anesthetists for their expertise, dedication, and service to the people of California, acknowledge their critical role in promoting opioid-free, opioid-sparing, and non-opioid pain management strategies, and celebrate National CRNA Week 2025.
