# California ACR 63 (20252026) — Relative to California Fairgrounds Appreciation Month.

This measure would designate the month of May 2025 as California Fairgrounds Appreciation Month and would extend the Legislature's warmest regards and appreciation to the people and organizations who work to keep the network of California state fairgrounds strong, vibrant, relevant, and successful.
