# California SCR 46 (20252026) — Relative to California Holocaust Memorial Day.

This measure would proclaim April 24, 2025, as California Holocaust Memorial Day and would urge all Californians to observe this day of remembrance for the victims of the Holocaust in an appropriate manner.
