# California ACR 66 (20252026) — Relative to Skin Cancer Awareness Month.

This measure would proclaim the month of May 2025, as Skin Cancer Awareness Month, and would encourage all Californians to make themselves and their families aware of the risk of skin cancer and the measures that can be taken to prevent it.
